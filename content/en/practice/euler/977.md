A key simplification is obtained by setting $x=1$:

$$f(y)=f^{(y)}(1).$$

Therefore every valid function is completely determined by the orbit of $1$. Writing

$$a_n=f(n),$$

the defining condition becomes

$$a_{n+1}=a_{a_n}.$$

So the entire problem reduces to counting sequences satisfying this self-referential recurrence.
