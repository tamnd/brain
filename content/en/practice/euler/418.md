$$a \approx b \approx c \approx \sqrt[3]{43!},$$

$$(a,b,c)= (392094800867753984,\, 392227980963120315,\, 392841187200000000)$$

$$abc = 43!, \qquad a\le b\le c,$$

$$f(43!) = a+b+c$$
