[Project Euler Problem 449](https://projecteuler.net/problem=449)

## Solution

Let the candy centre be the ellipsoid

$$b^2x^2+b^2y^2+a^2z^2=a^2b^2.$$

Dividing by $a^2b^2$,

$$\frac{x^2}{a^2}+\frac{y^2}{a^2}+\frac{z^2}{b^2}=1.$$

So the semi-axes are

$$(a,a,b).$$

For this problem:

$$a=3,\qquad b=1.$$

We want the volume of a uniform $1$ mm coating around the ellipsoid.

---

# 1. Geometric insight: Steiner’s formula

For any smooth convex body $K$, the volume of its outer parallel body at distance $r$ is

$$V(r)=V_0 + Sr + Mr^2 + \frac{4\pi}{3}r^3,$$

where

- $V_0$ = original volume,
- $S$ = surface area,
- $M=\displaystyle\int H\,dA$ = integrated mean curvature.

Therefore the chocolate volume (the shell only) is

$$V_{\text{choc}}
=
V(1)-V_0
=
S+M+\frac{4\pi}{3}.$$

So we only need:

1. surface area $S$,
2. integrated mean curvature $M$.

---

# 2. Surface area of an oblate spheroid

Because $a>b$, the ellipsoid is an oblate spheroid.

Define eccentricity

$$e=\sqrt{1-\frac{b^2}{a^2}}.$$

The surface area is

$$S
=
2\pi a^2
\left(
1+\frac{1-e^2}{e}\operatorname{artanh}(e)
\right).$$

For $a=3,b=1$,

$$e=\sqrt{1-\frac19}=\sqrt{\frac89}=\frac{2\sqrt2}{3}.$$

---

# 3. Integrated mean curvature

Parameterize the generating curve:

$$r(z)=a\sqrt{1-\frac{z^2}{b^2}},\qquad -b\le z\le b.$$

For a surface of revolution,

$$\int H\,dA
=
\pi\int_{-b}^{b}
\left(
1-\frac{r r''}{1+r'^2}
\right)\,dz.$$

Substituting the ellipsoid profile and simplifying gives

$$M
=
2\pi b
+
\frac{2\pi a^2}{\sqrt{a^2-b^2}}
\arcsin\!\left(\frac{\sqrt{a^2-b^2}}{a}\right).$$

---

# 4. Compute for $a=3,\ b=1$

## Surface area

$$S
=
2\pi(9)
\left(
1+\frac{1/9}{(2\sqrt2/3)}
\operatorname{artanh}\!\left(\frac{2\sqrt2}{3}\right)
\right)$$

Numerically,

$$S \approx 68.17956397.$$

---

## Integrated mean curvature

$$M
=
2\pi
+
\frac{18\pi}{\sqrt8}
\arcsin\!\left(\frac{\sqrt8}{3}\right).$$

Numerically,

$$M \approx 31.01034638.$$

---

## Chocolate volume

$$V_{\text{choc}}
=
S+M+\frac{4\pi}{3}.$$

Thus

$$V_{\text{choc}}
\approx
68.17956397
+
31.01034638
+
4.18879020
=
103.37870096.$$

---

# 5. Python implementation

```
from math import pi, sqrt, atanh, asin

def chocolate_volume(a, b):
    # eccentricity
    e = sqrt(1.0 - (b*b)/(a*a))

    # surface area of oblate spheroid
    S = 2*pi*a*a * (1 + ((1 - e*e)/e) * atanh(e))

    # integrated mean curvature
    M = 2*pi*b + (2*pi*a*a / sqrt(a*a - b*b)) * asin(sqrt(a*a - b*b)/a)

    # Steiner formula for 1 mm shell
    return S + M + 4*pi/3

# test cases
print(chocolate_volume(1, 1))  # 28*pi/3 = 29.321531...
print(chocolate_volume(2, 1))  # 60.35475635...

# target
ans = chocolate_volume(3, 1)
print(f"{ans:.8f}")
```

# 6. Verification against the examples

### Sphere $a=b=1$

The formula reduces to

$$4\pi + 4\pi + \frac{4\pi}{3}
=

exactly matching the problem statement.

---

### $a=2,\ b=1$
