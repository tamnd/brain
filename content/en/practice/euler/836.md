[Project Euler Problem 836](https://projecteuler.net/problem=836)

## Solution

**Answer:** `aprilfoolsjoke`

This Project Euler problem is a deliberate joke/problem trap.

The instruction says:

> “Give as your answer the concatenation of the first letters of each **bolded** word.”

The bolded words are:

- **affine**
- **plane**
- **radically**
- **integral**
- **local**
- **field**
- **open**
- **oriented**
- **line**
- **section**
- **jacobian**
- **orthogonal**
- **kernel**
- **embedding**

Taking the first letter of each:

**A P R I L F O O L S J O K E**

which concatenates to:

**APRILFOOLSJOKE**

**Answer: APRILFOOLSJOKE**
