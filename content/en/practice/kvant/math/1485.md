## Problem

Prove that for all sets $x_1$, $x_2$, $\ldots$, $x_n$, $0\lt x_1\le x_2\le\ldots\le x_n$, the expression $$x_2^{k}(x_1-x_3)+x_3^{k}(x_2-x_4)+\ldots+x_1^{k}(x_n-x_2)$$ is nonnegative when $k\gt 1$ and nonpositive when $0\lt k\lt 1$.
