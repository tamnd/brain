# Kvant

[Kvant](https://kvant.digital) (Квант) is a popular science magazine covering mathematics and physics, published in the Soviet Union and Russia since 1970. This site collects solutions to problems from the magazine's problem section.

- [Mathematics](/math/) — algebra, geometry, combinatorics
- [Physics](/physics/) — classical mechanics, thermodynamics, optics, electromagnetism
