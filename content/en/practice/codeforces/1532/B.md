## Problem Understanding

We are asked to track a frog moving along a one-dimensional axis starting at position 0. The frog alternates between two types of jumps: a jump of length `a` to the right and a jump of length `b` to the left. After `k` jumps, we need the frog's final position. The input consists of multiple independent queries, each specifying values for `a`, `b`, and `k`. The output should be the final position for each query.
