# Kvant Physics

[Kvant](https://kvant.digital) (Квант) is a popular science magazine covering mathematics and physics, published in the Soviet Union and Russia since 1970. This page collects solutions to **10 physics problems** from the magazine's problem section, covering the years 1970 to 1970. 10 solutions have been independently verified.
