# IMO 1971 SL 4

**Origin:** GBR

## Problem

We are given two mutually tangent circles in the plane, with
radii r1, r2. A line intersects these circles in four points, determining three
segments of equal length. Find this length as a function of r1 and r2 and
the condition for the solvability of the problem.
