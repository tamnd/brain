# IMO 1976 SL 6

**Origin:** NET

## Problem

A rectangular box can be ﬁlled completely with unit cubes.
If one places cubes with volume 2 in the box such that their edges are
parallel to the edges of the box, one can ﬁll exactly 40% of the box.
Determine all possible (interior) sizes of the box.
