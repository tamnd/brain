# IMO 1976 SL 3

**Origin:** CZS

## Problem

In a convex quadrangle with area 32 cm2, the sum of the
lengths of two nonadjacent edges and of the length of one diagonal is equal
to 16 cm.
(a) What is the length of the other diagonal?
(b) What are the lengths of the edges of the quadrangle if the perimeter
is a minimum?
(c) Is it possible to choose the edges in such a way that the perimeter is
a maximum?
