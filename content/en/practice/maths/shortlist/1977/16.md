# IMO 1977 SL 16

**Origin:** VIE

## Problem

Let E be a set of n points in the plane (n \geq3) whose co-
ordinates are integers such that any three points from E are vertices of
a nondegenerate triangle whose centroid doesn’t have both coordinates
integers. Determine the maximal n.
