# IMO 1977 SL 12

**Origin:** NET

## Problem

On the sides of a square ABCD one constructs inwardly
equilateral triangles ABK, BCL, CDM, DAN. Prove that the midpoints
of the four segments KL, LM, MN, NK, together with the midpoints of
the eight segments AK, BK, BL, CL, CM, DM, DN, AN, are the 12
vertices of a regular dodecagon.
