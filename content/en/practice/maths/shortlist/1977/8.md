# IMO 1977 SL 8

**Origin:** GBR

## Problem

Let S be a convex quadrilateral ABCD and O a point inside
it. The feet of the perpendiculars from O to AB, BC, CD, DA are A1, B1,
C1, D1 respectively. The feet of the perpendiculars from O to the sides of
Si, the quadrilateral AiBiCiDi, are Ai+1Bi+1Ci+1Di+1, where i = 1, 2, 3.
Prove that S4 is similar to S.
