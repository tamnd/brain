# IMO 1986 SL 6

**Origin:** NET

## Problem

Find four positive integers each not exceeding 70000 and each
having more than 100 divisors.

## Solution

There are ﬁve such numbers:
69300 = 22 \cdot 32 \cdot 52 \cdot 7 \cdot 11 :
3 \cdot 3 \cdot 3 \cdot 2 \cdot 2 = 108 divisors;
50400 = 25 \cdot 32 \cdot 52 \cdot 7 :
6 \cdot 3 \cdot 3 \cdot 2 = 108 divisors;
60480 = 26 \cdot 33 \cdot 5 \cdot 7 :
7 \cdot 4 \cdot 2 \cdot 2 = 112 divisors;
55440 = 24 \cdot 32 \cdot 5 \cdot 7 \cdot 11 :
5 \cdot 3 \cdot 2 \cdot 2 \cdot 2 = 120 divisors;
65520 = 24 \cdot 32 \cdot 5 \cdot 7 \cdot 13 :
5 \cdot 3 \cdot 2 \cdot 2 \cdot 2 = 120 divisors.
