(b) Suppose that the required numbering exists. Consider a part of the
chessboard as in Figure 2. By the stated condition, all the numbers

p + q + r + s, q + r + s + t, p + q + r + t, p + r + s + t give the same
remainder modulo 4, and so do p, q, r, s. We deduce that all numbers
on black cells of the board, except possibly the two corner cells, give
the same remainder, which is impossible.
