# IMO 1984 SL 1

**Origin:** FRA

## Problem

Find all solutions of the following system of n equations in n
variables:
x1|x1| −(x1 −a)|x1 −a| = x2|x2|,
x2|x2| −(x2 −a)|x2 −a| = x3|x3|,
\cdot \cdot \cdot
xn|xn| −(xn −a)|xn −a| = x1|x1|,
where a is a given number.
