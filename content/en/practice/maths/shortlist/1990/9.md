# IMO 1990 SL 9

**Origin:** HUN

## Problem

The incenter of the triangle ABC is K. The midpoint of AB
is C1 and that of AC is B1. The lines C1K and AC meet at B2, the lines
B1K and AB at C2. If the areas of the triangles AB2C2 and ABC are
equal, what is the measure of angle \angleCAB?
