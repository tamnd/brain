# IMO 1990 SL 26

**Origin:** USA

## Problem

Let P be a cubic polynomial with rational coeﬃcients, and let
q1, q2, q3, . . . be a sequence of rational numbers such that qn = P(qn+1) for
all n \geq1. Prove that there exists k \geq1 such that for all n \geq1, qn+k = qn.
