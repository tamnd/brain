# IMO 1992 SL 19

**Origin:** IRE

## Problem

and suppose there exists an integer z such that p divides f(z). Prove that
there exist integers z1, z2, . . . , z8 such that if
g(x) = (x −z1)(x −z2) \cdot \cdot \cdot (x −z8),
then all coeﬃcients of f(x) −g(x) are divisible by p.
