# IMO 1992 SL 20

**Origin:** FRA

## Problem

In the plane, let there be given a circle C, a line l tangent
to C, and a point M on l. Find the locus of points P that have the
following property: There exist two points Q and R on l such that M is
the midpoint of QR and C is the incircle of PQR.
