# IMO 1973 SL 7

**Origin:** POL

## Problem

Given a tetrahedron ABCD, let x = AB \cdot CD, y = AC \cdot BD,
and z = AD \cdot BC. Prove that there exists a triangle with edges x, y, z.

## Solution

The result follows immediately from Ptolemy’s inequality.
