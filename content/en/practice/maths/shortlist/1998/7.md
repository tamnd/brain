# IMO 1998 SL 7

**Origin:** GBR

## Problem

Let ABC be a triangle such that \angleACB = 2\angleABC. Let D be
the point on the side BC such that CD = 2BD. The segment AD is
extended to E so that AD = DE. Prove that
\angleECB + 180◦= 2\angleEBC.
