# IMO 1998 SL 5

**Origin:** FRA

## Problem

Let ABC be a triangle, H its orthocenter, O its circumcenter,
and R its circumradius. Let D be the reﬂection of A across BC, E that
of B across CA, and F that of C across AB. Prove that D, E, and F are
collinear if and only if OH = 2R.
