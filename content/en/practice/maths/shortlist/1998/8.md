# IMO 1998 SL 8

**Origin:** IND

## Problem

Let ABC be a triangle such that \angleA = 90◦and \angleB < \angleC. The
tangent at A to its circumcircle \omega meets the line BC at D. Let E be the
reﬂection of A across BC, X the foot of the perpendicular from A to BE,
and Y the midpoint of AX. Let the line BY meet \omega again at Z. Prove
that the line BD is tangent to the circumcircle of triangle ADZ.

## Solution
