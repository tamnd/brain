# IMO 1997 SL 11

**Origin:** NET

## Problem

Let P(x) be a polynomial with real coeﬃcients such that P(x) >
0 for all x \geq0. Prove that there exists a positive integer n such that
(1 + x)nP(x) is a polynomial with nonnegative coeﬃcients.
