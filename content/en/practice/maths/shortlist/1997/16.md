# IMO 1997 SL 16

**Origin:** BLR

## Problem

In an acute-angled triangle ABC, let AD, BE be altitudes and
AP, BQ internal bisectors. Denote by I and O the incenter and the cir-
cumcenter of the triangle, respectively. Prove that the points D, E, and
I are collinear if and only if the points P, Q, and O are collinear.
