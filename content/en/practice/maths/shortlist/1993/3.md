# IMO 1993 SL 3

**Origin:** SPA

## Problem

Consider the triangle ABC, its circumcircle k with center O
and radius R, and its incircle with center I and radius r. Another circle kc
is tangent to the sides CA, CB at D, E, respectively, and it is internally
tangent to k.
Show that the incenter I is the midpoint of DE.
