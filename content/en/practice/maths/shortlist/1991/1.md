# IMO 1991 SL 1

**Origin:** PHI

## Problem

Let ABC be any triangle and P any point in its interior. Let
P1, P2 be the feet of the perpendiculars from P to the two sides AC and
BC. Draw AP and BP, and from C drop perpendiculars to AP and BP.
Let Q1 and Q2 be the feet of these perpendiculars. Prove that the lines
Q1P2, Q2P1, and AB are concurrent.
