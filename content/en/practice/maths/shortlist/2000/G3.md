# IMO 2000 SL G3

**Origin:** IND  |  **Category:** Geometry

## Problem

Let O be the circumcenter and H the orthocenter of an acute
triangle ABC. Show that there exist points D, E, and F on sides BC,
CA, and AB respectively such that OD + DH = OE + EH = OF + FH
and the lines AD, BE, and CF are concurrent.
