# IMO 1978 SL 7

**Origin:** FRA

## Problem

We consider three distinct half-lines Ox, Oy, Oz in a plane.
Prove the existence and uniqueness of three points A \inOx, B \inOy,
C \inOz such that the perimeters of the triangles OAB, OBC, OCA are
all equal to a given number 2p > 0.
