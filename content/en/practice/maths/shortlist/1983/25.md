# IMO 1983 SL 25

**Origin:** USS

## Problem

Prove that every partition of 3-dimensional space into three
disjoint subsets has the following property: One of these subsets contains
all possible distances; i.e., for every a \inR+, there are points M and N
inside that subset such that distance between M and N is exactly a.
