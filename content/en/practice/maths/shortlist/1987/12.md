# IMO 1987 SL 12

**Origin:** POL

## Problem

Given a nonequilateral triangle ABC, the vertices listed coun-
terclockwise, ﬁnd the locus of the centroids of the equilateral triangles
A′B′C′ (the vertices listed counterclockwise) for which the triples of points
A, B′, C′; A′, B, C′; and A′, B′, C are collinear.
