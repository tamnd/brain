# IMO 2003 SL C3

**Origin:** LIT  |  **Category:** Combinatorics

## Problem

Let n \geq5 be a given integer. Determine the largest integer
k for which there exists a polygon with n vertices (convex or not, with
non-self-intersecting boundary) having k internal right angles.
