# IMO 1979 SL 2

**Origin:** BEL

## Problem

From a bag containing 5 pairs of socks, each pair a diﬀerent
color, a random sample of 4 single socks is drawn. Any complete pairs
in the sample are discarded and replaced by a new pair draw from the
bag. The process continues until the bag is empty or there are 4 socks of
diﬀerent colors held outside the bag. What is the probability of the latter
alternative?
