# IMO 1996 SL G1

**Origin:** GBR  |  **Category:** Geometry

## Problem

Let triangle ABC have orthocenter H, and let P be a point
on its circumcircle, distinct from A, B, C. Let E be the foot of the altitude
BH, let PAQB and PARC be parallelograms, and let AQ meet HR in
X. Prove that EX is parallel to AP.
