# IMO 1996 SL G7

**Origin:** GBR  |  **Category:** Geometry

## Problem

Let $ABC$ be an acute-angled triangle with circumcenter $O$ and circumradius $R$. Let $AO$ meet the circle $BOC$ again in $A'$, let $BO$ meet the circle $COA$ again in $B'$, and let $CO$ meet the circle $AOB$ again in $C'$. Prove that

$$OA' \cdot OB' \cdot OC' \geq 8R^3.$$

When does equality hold?
