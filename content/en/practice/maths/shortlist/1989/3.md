# IMO 1989 SL 3

**Origin:** AUS

## Problem

Ali Barber, the carpet merchant, has a rectangular piece of
carpet whose dimensions are unknown. Unfortunately, his tape measure is
broken and he has no other measuring instruments. However, he ﬁnds that
if he lays it ﬂat on the ﬂoor of either of his storerooms, then each corner of
the carpet touches a diﬀerent wall of that room. He knows that the sides of
the carpet are integral numbers of feet and that his two storerooms have
the same (unknown) length, but widths of 38 feet and 50 feet respectively.
What are the carpet dimensions?
