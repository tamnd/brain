# IMO 1994 SL G1

**Origin:** FRA  |  **Category:** Geometry

## Problem

A semicircle \Gamma is drawn on one side of a straight line l. C
and D are points on \Gamma. The tangents to \Gamma at C and D meet l at B and
A respectively, with the center of the semicircle between them. Let E be
the point of intersection of AC and BD, and F the point on l such that
EF is perpendicular to l. Prove that EF bisects \angleCFD.

## Solution

ular from P to AB. Denote by O the center of \Gamma. Since \trianglePAQ ∼\triangleOAD
and \trianglePBQ ∼\triangleOBC, we obtain

are concyclic, we have \angleDFP = \angleDOP = \anglePOC = \anglePFC.
