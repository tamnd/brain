# IMO 1994 SL G4

**Origin:** AUS-ARM  |  **Category:** Geometry

## Problem

N is an arbitrary point on the bisector of \angleBAC.
P and O are points on the lines AB and AN, respectively, such that
∡ANP = 90◦= ∡APO. Q is an arbitrary point on NP, and an arbitrary
line through Q meets the lines AB and AC at E and F respectively. Prove
that ∡OQE = 90◦if and only if QE = QF.
