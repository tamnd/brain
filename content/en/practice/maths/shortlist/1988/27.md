# IMO 1988 SL 27

**Origin:** GBR

## Problem

The triangle ABC is acute-angled. Let L be any line in the
plane of the triangle and let u, v, w be the lengths of the perpendiculars
from A, B, C respectively to L. Prove that
u2 tan A + v2 tan B + w2 tan C \geq2∆,
where ∆is the area of the triangle, and determine the lines L for which
equality holds.
