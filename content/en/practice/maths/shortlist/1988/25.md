**Origin:** GBR

## Problem

A positive integer is called a double number if its decimal rep-
resentation consists of a block of digits, not commencing with 0, followed
immediately by an identical block. For instance, 360360 is a double num-
ber, but 36036 is not. Show that there are inﬁnitely many double numbers
that are perfect squares.
