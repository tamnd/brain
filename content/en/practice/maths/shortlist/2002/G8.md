# IMO 2002 SL G8

**Origin:** ARM  |  **Category:** Geometry

## Problem

Let S1 and S2 be circles meeting at the points A and B. A
line through A meets S1 at C and S2 at D. Points M, N, K lie on the line
segments CD, BC, BD respectively, with MN parallel to BD and MK
parallel to BC. Let E and F be points on those arcs BC of S1 and BD
of S2 respectively that do not contain A. Given that EN is perpendicular
to BC and FK is perpendicular to BD, prove that ∡EMF = 90◦.
