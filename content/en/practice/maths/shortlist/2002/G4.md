# IMO 2002 SL G4

**Origin:** RUS  |  **Category:** Geometry

## Problem

Circles S1 and S2 intersect at points P and Q. Distinct points
A1 and B1 (not at P or Q) are selected on S1. The lines A1P and B1P
meet S2 again at A2 and B2 respectively, and the lines A1B1 and A2B2
meet at C. Prove that as A1 and B1 vary, the circumcenters of triangles
A1A2C all lie on one ﬁxed circle.
