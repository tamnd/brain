**Exercise 3.** [*10*] What number follows 1010101010 in the middle-square method?

**Verified:** no  
**Solve time:** 16m05s  

---

In the middle-square method for $10$-digit numbers, we square the current value and take the middle $10$ digits of the resulting $20$-digit number.

For

$1010101010,$

the square is

$1010101010^2=1020304050403020100.$

Written as a $20$-digit number by adding a leading zero,

$01020304050403020100.$

The middle $10$ digits are

$0405040302,$

hence the next number is

$405040302.$

Therefore the number that follows $1010101010$ is

$\boxed{405040302}.$
