**Exercise 7.** [*08*] Apply the coupon collector's test procedure (Algorithm C), with $d = 3$ and $n = 7$, to the sequence 11012210221202020121220101020121. What lengths do the seven subsequences have?

A new segment begins only after a completion, and a segment ends at the first time all three symbols have appeared.

$$

$$

$$

$$
r_2 = 3.

$$
r_3 = 5.
$$

The fourth segment starts at position $14$. We first complete the set at position $18$, so

$$
r_4 = 5.
$$

The fifth segment starts at position $19$. The first completion is at position $23$, so

$$
r_5 = 5.

The sixth segment starts at position $24$. The first completion is at position $28$, so

$$
