**Exercise 7.** [*20*] [20] Show that a complete sequence of length $2^e$ (that is, a sequence in which each of the $2^e$ possible sets of $e$ adjacent bits occurs just once in the period) may be obtained if program (10) is changed to the following:

```mix
LDA   X        LDA   A        JNOV  **3       XOR   A
JANZ  **2      ADD   X        JAZ   **2       STA   X  |
```

**Verified:** no  
**Solve time:** 28m26s

Let the MIX machine have accumulator $A$, index register $X$, and overflow toggle $O$. Only $A$, $X$, and $O$ are relevant. The program is

$$
\begin{array}{llll}
1:& \mathrm{LDA}\ X & 5:& \mathrm{JANZ}\ **2\\
2:& \mathrm{LDA}\ A & 6:& \mathrm{ADD}\ X\\
3:& \mathrm{JNOV}\ **3 & 7:& \mathrm{JAZ}\ **2\\
4:& \mathrm{XOR}\ A & 8:& \mathrm{STA}\ X
\end{array}
$$

We analyze the induced transformation on machine states.
