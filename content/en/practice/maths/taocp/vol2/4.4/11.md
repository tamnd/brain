**Exercise 4.4.11** [_16_]

**11.** Convert $(5772)_{10}$ to decimal.

**Solution**

The numeral $(5772)_{10}$ is expressed in base $10$, which is the standard decimal system. Converting a decimal numeral to decimal does not change its value, because the numeral already represents a number in base $10$.

Formally, if a number is written in base $b$ as

$$
(d_k d_{k-1} \dots d_1 d_0)_b,
$$

then its value in decimal is

$$
d_k \cdot b^k + d_{k-1} \cdot b^{k-1} + \dots + d_1 \cdot b + d_0.
$$

Applying this to $(5772)_{10}$, with $b=10$, we have

$$
(5772)_{10} = 5\cdot 10^3 + 7\cdot 10^2 + 7\cdot 10 + 2 = 5000 + 700 + 70 + 2 = 5772.
$$

Hence the decimal value of $(5772)_{10}$ is

$$
\boxed{5772}.
$$

No further computation is necessary, because the numeral is already in decimal.
