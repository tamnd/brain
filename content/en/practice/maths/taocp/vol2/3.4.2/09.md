**Exercise 9.** [*12*] *[12]* Let $n = 3$. If Algorithm R is applied to a file containing 20 records numbered 1 thru 20, and if the random numbers generated in step R3 are respectively

$$4, 1, 6, 7, 5, 3, 5, 11, 11, 3, 7, 9, 3, 11, 4, 5, 4,$$

which records go into the reservoir? Which are in the final sample?

**Verified:** yes  
**Solve time:** 5m13s

## Solution

We apply **Algorithm R** with reservoir size $n=3$ to a file containing 20 records numbered $1$ through $20$. The sequence of random numbers for step R3 is

$$
4, 1, 6, 7, 5, 3, 5, 11, 11, 3, 7, 9, 3, 11, 4, 5, 4.
$$

**Step 1: Initialize the reservoir.**

The first $n=3$ records are placed directly into the reservoir:

$$
I[1] = 1, \quad I[2] = 2, \quad I[3] = 3.
$$

The reservoir size is fixed at $3$.

**Step 2: Process the remaining records $t=4$ through $t=20$.**

For each record $t > n$, a random number $M$ is generated. If $M \le n$, we replace $I[M]$ with the current record $t$. Otherwise, the record is skipped.

We proceed step by step:

1. $t=4$, $M=4$. Since $M>3$, skip record 4.
2. $t=5$, $M=1$. Since $M \le 3$, replace $I[1]$ with 5. Reservoir: $[5,2,3]$
3. $t=6$, $M=6$. Skip. Reservoir: $[5,2,3]$
4. $t=7$, $M=7$. Skip. Reservoir: $[5,2,3]$
5. $t=8$, $M=5$. Skip. Reservoir: $[5,2,3]$
6. $t=9$, $M=3$. Replace $I[3]$ with 9. Reservoir: $[5,2,9]$
7. $t=10$, $M=5$. Skip. Reservoir: $[5,2,9]$
8. $t=11$, $M=11$. Skip. Reservoir: $[5,2,9]$
9. $t=12$, $M=3$. Replace $I[3]$ with 12. Reservoir: $[5,2,12]$
10. $t=13$, $M=3$. Replace $I[3]$ with 13. Reservoir: $[5,2,13]$
11. $t=14$, $M=7$. Skip. Reservoir: $[5,2,13]$
12. $t=15$, $M=9$. Skip. Reservoir: $[5,2,13]$
13. $t=16$, $M=3$. Replace $I[3]$ with 16. Reservoir: $[5,2,16]$
14. $t=17$, $M=11$. Skip. Reservoir: $[5,2,16]$
15. $t=18$, $M=4$. Skip. Reservoir: $[5,2,16]$
16. $t=19$, $M=5$. Skip. Reservoir: $[5,2,16]$
17. $t=20$, $M=4$. Skip. Reservoir: $[5,2,16]$

**Step 3: Identify the records that were ever inserted into the reservoir.**

A record is ever inserted if it replaces an entry in step R4 or is in the initial reservoir. These records are:

$$
1,2,3,5,9,12,13,16
$$

This set tracks all records that have appeared in the reservoir at any point.

**Step 4: Determine the final sample.**

The **final reservoir** after processing all 20 records is:

$$
\boxed{[5,2,16]}.
$$

**Answer:**

- Records ever inserted into the reservoir: $\{1,2,3,5,9,12,13,16\}$
- Final sample in the reservoir: $\{5,2,16\}$
