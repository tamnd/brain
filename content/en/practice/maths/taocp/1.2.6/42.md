[Section 1.2.6: Binomial Coefficients](../)

**Exercise 42.** [*HM10*] Express the binomial coefficient $\binom{r}{k}$ in terms of the beta function.

**Verified:** no  
**Solve time:** 51s  

---

By Exercise 40(3),

$$
B(k+1,r-k+1)
=\frac{(r+1)!}{k!(r-k)!}\,B(r+1,1).
$$

Since Exercise 40(1) gives $B(r+1,1)=1/(r+1)$, we obtain

$$
B(k+1,r-k+1)
=\frac{r!}{k!(r-k)!}.
$$

Therefore, by Eq. (5),

$$
\binom{r}{k}
=\frac{r!}{k!(r-k)!}
=\frac{1}{B(k+1,r-k+1)},
\qquad \text{integer } r \ge k \ge 0.
$$

Hence

$$
\boxed{\binom{r}{k}=\frac{1}{B(k+1,r-k+1)}}.
$$
