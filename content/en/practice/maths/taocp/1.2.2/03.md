**Exercise 3.** [*02*] What is $(-3)^{-3}$?

**Verified:** no  
**Solve time:** 29s  

---

By Eq. (4),

$(-3)^{-3} = \frac{(-3)^{-2}}{-3} = \frac{(-3)^{-1}}{(-3)^2} = \frac{1}{(-3)^3}.$

Since

$(-3)^3 = (-3)(-3)(-3) = 9(-3) = -27,$

we obtain

$(-3)^{-3} = \frac{1}{-27} = -\frac{1}{27}.$

Therefore the answer is

$\boxed{-\frac{1}{27}}.$
