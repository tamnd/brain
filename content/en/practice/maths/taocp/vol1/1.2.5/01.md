**Exercise 1.** [**] [00] How many ways are there to shuffle a 52-card deck?

**Verified:** no  
**Solve time:** 8m02s  

---

A 52-card deck consists of 52 distinct objects. By the definition of a permutation in Section 1.2.5, the number of ways to arrange $n$ distinct objects in a row is $n!$. Therefore, the number of ways to shuffle a 52-card deck is

$52! = 52 \cdot 51 \cdot 50 \cdots 2 \cdot 1 = \prod_{k=1}^{52} k.$

This completes the solution. ∎

The exact value of $52!$ is approximately

$52! \approx 8.0658 \cdot 10^{67},$

which illustrates the vast number of possible shuffles.
