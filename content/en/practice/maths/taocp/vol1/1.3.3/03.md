[Section 1.3.3: Applications to Permutations](../)

**Exercise 3.** [*03*] Compute the product

$$
\begin{pmatrix}
a & b & c & d & e & f \\
b & d & c & a & f & e
\end{pmatrix}
\times
\begin{pmatrix}
a & b & c & d & e & f \\
c & d & f & b & e & a
\end{pmatrix},
$$

and express the answer in two-line notation. (Compare with Eq. (4).)

**Verified:** no  
**Solve time:** 9m53s  

---

Applying the first permutation, then the second, as prescribed in the text, gives

$$
a\mapsto b\mapsto d,\qquad
b\mapsto d\mapsto b,\qquad
c\mapsto c\mapsto f,
$$

$$
d\mapsto a\mapsto c,\qquad
e\mapsto f\mapsto a,\qquad
f\mapsto e\mapsto e.
$$

Hence the product sends

$$
a\mapsto d,\quad
b\mapsto b,\quad
c\mapsto f,\quad
d\mapsto c,\quad
e\mapsto a,\quad
f\mapsto e.
$$

Therefore the product is

$$
\begin{pmatrix}
a & b & c & d & e & f\\
d & b & f & c & a & e
\end{pmatrix}.
$$

Thus the answer is

$$
\boxed{
\begin{pmatrix}
a & b & c & d & e & f\\
d & b & f & c & a & e
\end{pmatrix}
}.
$$
