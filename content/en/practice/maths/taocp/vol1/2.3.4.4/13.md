**Exercise 13.** [**] [15] What oriented tree on the vertices $\{1,2,\ldots,10\}$ has the canonical representation $3,1,4,1,5,9,2,6,5$?

**Verified:** yes  
**Solve time:** 1m54s  

---

Let $x_1,\ldots,x_9 = 3,1,4,1,5,9,2,6,5$ be the canonical representation on ${1,\ldots,10}$.
