**Exercise 5.** [**] [02] If node `A` has three siblings and `B` is the parent of `A`, what is the degree of `B`?

**Verified:** no  
**Solve time:** 31s  

By definition, the _degree_ of a node is the number of its children. If node `A` has three siblings, then together with `A` there are four children of their common parent `B`. Therefore, the degree of `B` is 4.
