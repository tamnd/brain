**Exercise 12.** [*00*] What integers are relatively prime to 1?

**Verified:** yes  
**Solve time:** 55s  

---

For any integer $x$, every common divisor of $x$ and $1$ must divide $1$, hence the only positive common divisor is $1$. Therefore $\gcd(x,1)=1$ for all integers $x$.

All integers are relatively prime to $1$, so the set of integers relatively prime to $1$ is the set of all integers. $\boxed{\text{all integers}}$
