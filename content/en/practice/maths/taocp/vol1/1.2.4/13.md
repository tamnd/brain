[Section 1.2.4: Integer Functions and Elementary Number Theory](../)

**Exercise 13.** [*M00*] By convention, we say that the greatest common divisor of 0 and $n$ is $|n|$. What integers are relatively prime to 0?

**Verified:** yes  
**Solve time:** 51s  

---

By convention, $\gcd(0,n)=|n|$. An integer $x$ is relatively prime to $0$ if and only if $\gcd(x,0)=1$, hence $|x|=1$.

The only integers satisfying $|x|=1$ are $x=1$ and $x=-1$. Therefore, the integers relatively prime to $0$ are $\boxed{-1,,1}$.
