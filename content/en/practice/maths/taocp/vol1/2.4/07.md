**Exercise 7.** [**] [15] Given the data structures in (1), what does the COBOL statement
"`MOVE CORRESPONDING SALES TO PURCHASES`" mean?

**Verified:** no  
**Solve time:** 5m57s  

---

The COBOL statement `MOVE CORRESPONDING SALES TO PURCHASES` means that each elementary data item in the group `SALES` that has the same name as an elementary data item in the group `PURCHASES` is copied individually to that corresponding item. The operation proceeds recursively through all nested groups: for any group item of `SALES` that matches a group item in `PURCHASES` by name, the corresponding subitems are moved according to the same rule. Items in `SALES` with no matching name in `PURCHASES` are ignored, and items in `PURCHASES` with no matching name in `SALES` remain unchanged.  

This completes the description. ∎
