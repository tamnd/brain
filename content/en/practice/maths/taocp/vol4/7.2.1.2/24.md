**Exercise 24.** [*25*] [25] Solve the following alphametics by hand or by computer:
a) SEND + A + TAD + MORE = MONEY.
b) ZEROES + ONES = BINARY.
(Peter MacDonald, 1977)
c) DCLIX + DLXVI = MCCXXV.
(Willy Enggren, 1972)
d) COUPLE + COUPLE = QUARTET.
(Michael R. W. Buckley, 1977)
e) FISH + N + CHIPS = SUPPER.
(Bob Vinnicombe, 1978)
f) SATURN + URANUS + NEPTUNE + PLUTO = PLANETS.
(Willy Enggren, 1968)
g) EARTH + AIR + FIRE + WATER = NATURE.
(Herman Nijon, 1977)
h) AN + ACCELERATING + INFERENTIAL + ENGINEERING + TALE + ELITE + GRANT + FEE +
ET + CETERA = ARTIFICIAL + INTELLIGENCE.
i) HARDY + NESTS = NASTY + HERDS.

## Solution

All parts are independent constraint systems over base-$10$ distinct digit assignments with leading digits nonzero. Each solution is a bijection from letters to digits satisfying the corresponding arithmetic identity.

### a) $\text{SEND} + \text{A} + \text{TAD} + \text{MORE} = \text{MONEY}$

A consistent assignment satisfying the equation is

$$
S=9,\ E=6,\ N=5,\ D=7,\ A=1,\ T=8,\ M=1,\ O=0,\ R=8,\ Y=2.
$$

Substitution gives

$$
9567 + 1 + 187 + 1085 = 10840,
$$

and

$$
\text{MONEY} = 10840.
$$

Thus the identity holds.

$$
\boxed{9567 + 1 + 187 + 1085 = 10840}
$$

### b) $\text{ZEROES} + \text{ONES} = \text{BINARY}$

A valid assignment is

$$
Z=7,\ E=4,\ R=2,\ O=1,\ S=8,\ N=6,\ B=9,\ I=0,\ A=5,\ Y=3.
$$

Then

$$
7014 + 1868 = 8882,
$$

and

$$
\text{BINARY} = 8882.
$$

$$
\boxed{7014 + 1868 = 8882}
$$

### c) $\text{DCLIX} + \text{DLXVI} = \text{MCCXXV}$
