We fix variable order $x_1 < x_2$. A ZDD node labeled $x_i$ has two outgoing edges: the 0-edge (low) and the 1-edge (high). Terminal nodes are $0$ and $1$. Reduction rules identify isomorphic subgraphs and eliminate nodes whose 1-edge points to $0$, replacing them by their 0-child.

We enumerate all Boolean functions $f(x_1,x_2)$ via their truth tables in lexicographic order

$$
(x_1,x_2) = 00,01,10,11.
$$

Each function is then given by its ZDD.

$$
f \equiv 0.
$$
