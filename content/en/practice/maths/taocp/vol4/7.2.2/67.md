\text{top } t_i & \text{right } r_i\\
\text{left } \ell_i & \text{bottom } b_i

A $180^\circ$ rotation swaps opposite edges:

$$
(t_i,r_i,\ell_i,b_i)\;\mapsto\;(b_i,\ell_i,r_i,t_i).
$$

We place each card into a $3\times 3$ grid. Let the placement be a bijection

$$
\pi:\{1,\dots,9\}\to \{1,2,3\}^2.

## 2. Coupled constraints (the key correction)

### Vertical (black-letter) sums

\sum_{r=1}^3 V(\pi^{-1}(r,c)) = 13,

where $V(i)\in\{t_i,b_i\}$ depends similarly on placement and rotation.

\sum_{r,c} x_{i,r,c} = 1.

\sum_{r,i} V_{i}(r,c,\varepsilon_i)x_{i,r,c} = 13.
