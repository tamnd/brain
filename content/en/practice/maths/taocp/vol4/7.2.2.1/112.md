A torto word is traced by a simple noncrossing king path, that is, successive letters occupy horizontally, vertically, or diagonally adjacent cells.

Let $(r,c)$ denote row $r$, column $c$.

#### TORTO

Use the path

$$
(1,1)\rightarrow(1,2)\rightarrow(1,3)\rightarrow(2,2)\rightarrow(3,2).
$$

$$
T,O,R,T,O.
$$

#### WORDS

(2,1)\rightarrow(2,2)\rightarrow(1,3)\rightarrow(2,3)\rightarrow(3,1).

W,O,R,D,S.

#### SOLVER
