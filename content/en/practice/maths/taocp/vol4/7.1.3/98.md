[Section 7.1.3: Bitwise Tricks and Techniques](../)

**Exercise 98.** [*20*] Given $x = (x_7 \ldots x_0)_{256}$ and $y = (y_7 \ldots y_0)_{256}$, compute $z = (z_7 \ldots z_0)_{256}$ and $w = (w_7 \ldots w_0)_{256}$, where $z_j = \max(x_j, y_j)$ and $w_j = \min(x_j, y_j)$ for $0 \le j < 8$.

## Solution

For each byte position $0 \le j < 8$, treat $x_j$ and $y_j$ as independent integers in ${0,\ldots,255}$ and perform all arithmetic componentwise.

Define

$$
d_j = x_j - y_j.
$$

Then $|d_j| = |x_j - y_j|$ satisfies

$$
x_j = \frac{x_j + y_j + d_j}{2}, \qquad y_j = \frac{x_j + y_j - d_j}{2}
$$

whenever $d_j = x_j - y_j$, but replacing $d_j$ by $|d_j|$ selects max and min via symmetry.

Indeed, consider the two cases.

If $x_j \ge y_j$, then $|x_j - y_j| = x_j - y_j$, hence

$$
\frac{x_j + y_j + |x_j - y_j|}{2}
= \frac{x_j + y_j + x_j - y_j}{2}
= x_j,
$$

and

$$
\frac{x_j + y_j - |x_j - y_j|}{2}
= \frac{x_j + y_j - (x_j - y_j)}{2}
= y_j.
$$

If $x_j < y_j$, then $|x_j - y_j| = y_j - x_j$, hence

$$
\frac{x_j + y_j + |x_j - y_j|}{2}
= \frac{x_j + y_j + y_j - x_j}{2}
= y_j,
$$

and

$$
\frac{x_j + y_j - |x_j - y_j|}{2}
= \frac{x_j + y_j - (y_j - x_j)}{2}
= x_j.
$$

Thus the larger value is always selected by the “plus” expression and the smaller by the “minus” expression.

Define bytewise results

$$
z_j = \frac{x_j + y_j + |x_j - y_j|}{2}, \qquad
w_j = \frac{x_j + y_j - |x_j - y_j|}{2}.
$$

Each expression depends only on $x_j$ and $y_j$, so assembling the bytes gives

$$
z = (z_7 \ldots z_0)_{256}, \qquad w = (w_7 \ldots w_0)_{256}.
$$

This completes the construction of $z_j = \max(x_j,y_j)$ and $w_j = \min(x_j,y_j)$ for all $j$. ∎
