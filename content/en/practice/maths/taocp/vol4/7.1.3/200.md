**Exercise 200.** [*20*] [20] Interpret the contents of register $3 after the following three MMIX instruc-
tions have been executed: MOR $1,$0,#94; MXOR $2,$0,#94; SUBU $3,$1,$2.

## Solution

In MMIX, register $0$ is the constant zero register, so its contents are $0$.

The instruction

$\text{MOR } $1,$0,#94$ computes bitwise OR of the contents of $$
0$ and the immediate constant $#94$, hence
$\$1 \leftarrow 0 \mid \#94 = \#94.$
The instruction
$\text{MXOR } $2,$0,#94$ computes bitwise XOR of
$$0$ and $#94$, hence

$\$2 \leftarrow 0 \oplus \#94 = \#94.$

The instruction

$\text{SUBU } $3,$1,$2$ performs unsigned subtraction modulo $2^{64}$, so

$\$3 \leftarrow \#94 - \#94 = 0.$

Therefore the final contents of register $$3$ are

$\boxed{0}.$
