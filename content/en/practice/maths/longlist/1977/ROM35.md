# IMO 1977 LL ROM35

**Origin:** ROM

## Problem

Find all numbers N = a1a2 . . . an for which 9 \times a1a2 . . . an =
an . . . a2a1 such that at most one of the digits a1, a2, . . . , an is zero.

Remark. If we omit the condition that at most one of the digits is zero,
the solutions are numbers of the form Nk1Nk2 . . . Nkr, where k1 = kr,
k2 = kr−1 etc.

for k = 9 and for k = 4 (namely 0, 2199 . . .978 and combinations as
