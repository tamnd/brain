# IMO 1977 LL FRG12

**Origin:** FRG

## Problem

Let z be an integer > 1 and let M be the set of all numbers
of the form zk = 1 + z + \cdot \cdot \cdot + zk, k = 0, 1, . . . . Determine the set T of
divisors of at least one of the numbers zk from M.

## Solution

According to part (a) of the previous problem we can conclude that T =
{n \inN | (n, z) = 1}.
