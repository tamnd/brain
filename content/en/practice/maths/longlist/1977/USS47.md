# IMO 1977 LL USS47

**Origin:** USS

## Problem

A square ABCD is given. A line passing through A intersects
CD at Q. Draw a line parallel to AQ that intersects the boundary of the
square at points M and N such that the area of the quadrilateral AMNQ
is maximal.

## Solution

M lies on AB and N lies on BC. If CQ \leq2CD/3, then BM = CQ/2. If
CQ > 2CD/3, then N coincides with C.
