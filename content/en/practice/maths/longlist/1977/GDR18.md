# IMO 1977 LL GDR18

**Origin:** GDR

## Problem

Given an isosceles triangle ABC with a right angle at C,
construct the center M and radius r of a circle cutting on segments
AB, BC, CA the segments DE, FG, and HK, respectively, such that
\angleDME + \angleFMG + \angleHMK = 180◦and DE : FG : HK = AB : BC :
CA.
