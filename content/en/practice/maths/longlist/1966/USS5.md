# IMO 1966 LL USS5

**Origin:** USS

## Problem

Prove the inequality
tan \pi sin x
4 sin \alpha + tan \pi cos x
4 cos \alpha > 1
for any x, \alpha with 0 \leqx \leq\pi/2 and \pi/6 < y < \pi/3.
