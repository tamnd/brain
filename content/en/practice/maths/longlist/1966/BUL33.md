# IMO 1966 LL BUL33

**Origin:** BUL

## Problem

Two circles touch each other from inside, and an equilateral
triangle is inscribed in the larger circle. From the vertices of the triangle
one draws segments tangent to the smaller circle. Prove that the length
of one of these segments equals the sum of the lengths of the other two.
