# IMO 1966 LL USS6

**Origin:** USS

## Problem

A convex planar polygon M with perimeter l and area S is given.
Let M(R) be the set of all points in space that lie a distance at most R
from a point of M. Show that the volume V (R) of this set equals
V (R) = 4
3\piR3 + \pi
2 lR2 + 2SR.
