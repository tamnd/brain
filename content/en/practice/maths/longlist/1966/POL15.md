# IMO 1966 LL POL15

**Origin:** POL

## Problem

Points A, B, C, D lie on a circle such that AB is a diameter and
CD is not. If the tangents at C and D meet at P while AC and BD meet
at Q, show that PQ is perpendicular to AB.
