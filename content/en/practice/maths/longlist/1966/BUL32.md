# IMO 1966 LL BUL32

**Origin:** BUL

## Problem

The sides a, b, c of a triangle ABC form an arithmetic progression;
the sides of another triangle A1B1C1 also form an arithmetic progression.
Suppose that \angleA = \angleA1. Prove that the triangles ABC and A1B1C1 are
similar.
