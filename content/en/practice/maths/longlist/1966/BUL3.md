# IMO 1966 LL BUL3

**Origin:** BUL

## Problem

A regular triangular prism has height h and a base of side length
a. Both bases have small holes in the centers, and the inside of the three
vertical walls has a mirror surface. Light enters through the small hole in
the top base, strikes each vertical wall once and leaves through the hole
in the bottom. Find the angle at which the light enters and the length of
its path inside the prism.
