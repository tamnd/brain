# IMO 1966 LL HUN20

**Origin:** HUN

## Problem

We are given three equal rectangles with the same center in
three mutually perpendicular planes, with the long sides also mutually
perpendicular. Consider the polyhedron with vertices at the vertices of
these rectangles.
(a) Find the volume of this polyhedron;
(b) can this polyhedron be regular, and under what conditions?
