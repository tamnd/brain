# IMO 1966 LL ROM47

**Origin:** ROM

## Problem

Find the number of lines dividing a given triangle into two parts
of equal area which determine the segment of minimum possible length
inside the triangle. Compute this minimum length in terms of the sides
a, b, c of the triangle.
