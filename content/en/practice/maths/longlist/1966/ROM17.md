# IMO 1966 LL ROM17

**Origin:** ROM

## Problem

Suppose ABCD and A′B′C′D′ are two parallelograms arbi-
trarily arranged in space, and let points M, N, P, Q divide the segments
AA′, BB′, CC′, DD′ respectively in equal ratios.
(a) Show that MNPQ is a parallelogram;
(b) Find the locus of MNPQ as M varies along the segment AA′.
