# IMO 1966 LL GDR2

**Origin:** GDR

## Problem

Given n positive real numbers a1, a2, . . . , an such that a1a2 \cdot \cdot \cdot an
= 1, prove that
(1 + a1)(1 + a2) \cdot \cdot \cdot (1 + an) \geq2n.
