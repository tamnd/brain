# IMO 1966 LL ROM39

**Origin:** ROM

## Problem

In a plane, a circle with center O and radius R and two points
A, B are given.
(a) Draw a chord CD parallel to AB so that AC and BD intersect at a
point P on the circle.
(b) Prove that there are two possible positions of point P, say P1, P2, and
ﬁnd the distance between them if OA = a, OB = b, AB = d.
