# IMO 1966 LL CZS42

**Origin:** CZS

## Problem

there is a subsequence ak1, ak2, . . . , akm, where 1 \leqk1 < k2 < \cdot \cdot \cdot < km \leq
n, such that a2
k1 + a2
k2 + \cdot \cdot \cdot + a2
km is divisible by n.
