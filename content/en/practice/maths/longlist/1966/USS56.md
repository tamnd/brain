# IMO 1966 LL USS56

**Origin:** USS

## Problem

Let ABCD be a tetrahedron such that AB \perpCD,
AC \perpBD, and AD \perpBC. Prove that the midpoints of the edges of
the tetrahedron lie on a sphere.
