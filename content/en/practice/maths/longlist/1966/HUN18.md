# IMO 1966 LL HUN18

**Origin:** HUN

## Problem

Solve the equation
sin x +
cos x = 1
p, where p is a real parameter.
Discuss for which values of p the equation has at least one real solution
and determine the number of solutions in [0, 2\pi) for a given p.
