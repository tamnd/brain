# IMO 1992 LL AUS1

**Origin:** AUS

## Problem

Points D and E are chosen on the sides AB and AC of the
triangle ABC in such a way that if F is the intersection point of BE and
CD, then AE + EF = AD + DF. Prove that AC + CF = AB + BF.
