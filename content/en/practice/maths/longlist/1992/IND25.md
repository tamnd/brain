# IMO 1992 LL IND25

**Origin:** IND

## Problem

(a) Show that the set N of all natural numbers can be parti-
tioned into three disjoint subsets A, B, and C satisfying the following
conditions:
A2 = A,
B2 = C,
C2 = B,
AB = B,
AC = C,
BC = A,
where HK stands for {hk | h \inH, k \inK} for any two subsets H, K
of N, and H2 denotes HH.
(b) Show that for every such partition of N, min{n \inN | n \inA and n +
1 \inA} is less than or equal to 77.
