# IMO 1992 LL USA80

**Origin:** USA

## Problem

Given a graph with n vertices and a positive integer m that is
less than n, prove that the graph contains a set of m + 1 vertices in which
the diﬀerence between the largest degree of any vertex in the set and the
smallest degree of any vertex in the set is at most m −1.
