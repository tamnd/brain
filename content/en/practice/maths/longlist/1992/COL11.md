# IMO 1992 LL COL11

**Origin:** COL

## Problem

Let \varphi(n, m), m ̸= 1, be the number of positive integers less
than or equal to n that are coprime with m. Clearly, \varphi(m, m) = \varphi(m),
where \varphi(m) is Euler’s phi function. Find all integers m that satisfy the
following inequality:
\varphi(n, m)
n
\geq\varphi(m)
m
for every positive integer n.
