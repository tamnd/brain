# IMO 1992 LL ROM64

**Origin:** ROM

## Problem

For any positive integer n consider all representations n =
a1 + \cdot \cdot \cdot + ak, where a1 > a2 > \cdot \cdot \cdot > ak > 0 are integers such that for all
i \in{1, 2, . . ., k −1}, the number ai is divisible by ai+1. Find the longest
such representation of the number 1992.
