**Origin:** SPA

## Problem

symmetric to the median with the respect to the internal bisector (all
relative to the same vertex). In the triangle ABC, the median ma meets
BC at A′ and the circumcircle again at A1. The symmedian sa meets BC
at M and the circumcircle again at A2. Given that the line A1A2 contains
the circumcenter O of the triangle, prove that:
(a) AA′
AM = b2 + c2
2bc
;
(b) 1 + 4b2c2 = a2(b2 + c2).
