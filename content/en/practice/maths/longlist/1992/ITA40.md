# IMO 1992 LL ITA40

**Origin:** ITA

## Problem

The colonizers of a spherical planet have decided to build N
towns, each having area 1/1000 of the total area of the planet. They also
decided that any two points belonging to diﬀerent towns will have diﬀerent
latitude and diﬀerent longitude. What is the maximal value of N?
