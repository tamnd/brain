# IMO 1992 LL TWN76

**Origin:** TWN

## Problem

Given any triangle ABC and any positive integer n, we say
that n is a decomposable number for triangle ABC if there exists a de-
composition of the triangle ABC into n subtriangles with each subtriangle
similar to \triangleABC. Determine the positive integers that are decomposable
numbers for every triangle.
