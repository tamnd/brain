# IMO 1992 LL AUS3

**Origin:** AUS

## Problem

Let ABC be a triangle, O its circumcenter, S its centroid, and
H its orthocenter. Denote by A1, B1, and C1 the centers of the circles
circumscribed about the triangles CHB, CHA, and AHB, respectively.
Prove that the triangle ABC is congruent to the triangle A1B1C1 and that
the nine-point circle of \triangleABC is also the nine-point circle of \triangleA1B1C1.
