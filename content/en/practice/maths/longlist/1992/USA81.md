# IMO 1992 LL USA81

**Origin:** USA

## Problem

Suppose that points X, Y, Z are located on sides BC, CA,
and AB, respectively, of \triangleABC in such a way that \triangleXY Z is similar
to \triangleABC. Prove that the orthocenter of \triangleXY Z is the circumcenter of
\triangleABC.
