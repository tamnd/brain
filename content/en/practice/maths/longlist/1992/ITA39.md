# IMO 1992 LL ITA39

**Origin:** ITA

## Problem

Let n \geq2 be an integer. Find the minimum k for which there
exists a partition of {1, 2, . . ., k} into n subsets X1, X2, . . . , Xn such that
the following condition holds: for any i, j, 1 \leqi < j \leqn, there exist
x1 \inX1, x2 \inX2 such that |xi −xj| = 1.
