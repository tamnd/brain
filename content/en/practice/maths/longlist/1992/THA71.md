# IMO 1992 LL THA71

**Origin:** THA

## Problem

Let P1(x, y) and P2(x, y) be two relatively prime polynomials
with complex coeﬃcients. Let Q(x, y) and R(x, y) be polynomials with
complex coeﬃcients and each of degree not exceeding d. Prove that there
exist two integers A1, A2 not simultaneously zero with |Ai| \leqd + 1 (i =
1, 2) and such that the polynomial A1P1(x, y) + A2P2(x, y) is coprime to
Q(x, y) and R(x, y).
