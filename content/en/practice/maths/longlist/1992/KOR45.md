# IMO 1992 LL KOR45

**Origin:** KOR

## Problem

Let n be a positive integer. Prove that the number of ways
to express n as a sum of distinct positive integers (up to order) and the
number of ways to express n as a sum of odd positive integers (up to
order) are the same.
