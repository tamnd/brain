# IMO 1992 LL IND30

**Origin:** IND

## Problem

Let Pn = (19 + 92)(192 + 922) \cdot \cdot \cdot (19n + 92n) for each positive
integer n. Determine, with proof, the least positive integer m, if it exists,
for which Pm is divisible by 3333.
