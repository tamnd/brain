# IMO 1992 LL FRG18

**Origin:** FRG

## Problem

Fibonacci numbers are deﬁned as follows: F1 = F2 = 1, Fn+2 =
Fn+1+Fn, n \geq1. Let an be the number of words that consist of n letters 0
or 1 and contain no two letters 1 at distance two from each other. Express
an in terms of Fibonacci numbers.
