# IMO 1992 LL TUR72

**Origin:** TUR

## Problem

In a school six diﬀerent courses are taught: mathematics,
physics, biology, music, history, geography. The students were required to
rank these courses according to their preferences, where equal preferences
were allowed. It turned out that:
(i) mathematics was ranked among the most preferred courses by all stu-
dents;
(ii) no student ranked music among the least preferred ones;
(iii) all students preferred history to geography and physics to biology; and
(iv) no two rankings were the same.
Find the greatest possible value for the number of students in this school.
