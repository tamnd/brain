# IMO 1992 LL IRE33

**Origin:** IRE

## Problem

Let a, b, c be positive real numbers and p, q, r complex numbers.
Let S be the set of all solutions (x, y, z) in C of the system of simultaneous
equations
ax + by + cz = p,
ax2 + by2 + cz2 = q,
ax3 + bx3 + cx3 = r.
Prove that S has at most six elements.
