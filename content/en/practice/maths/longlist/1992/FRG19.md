# IMO 1992 LL FRG19

**Origin:** FRG

## Problem

Denote by an the greatest number that is not divisible by 3
and that divides n. Consider the sequence s0 = 0, sn = a1 + a2 + \cdot \cdot \cdot+ an,
n \inN. Denote by A(n) the number of all sums sk (0 \leqk \leq3n, k \inN0)
that are divisible by 3. Prove the formula
A(n) = 3n−1 + 2 \cdot 3(n/2)−1 cos(n\pi/6),
n \inN0.
