# IMO 1986 LL TUR72

**Origin:** TUR

## Problem

A one-person game with two possible outcomes is played as
follows: After each play, the player receives either a or b points, where a
and b are integers with 0 < b < a < 1986. The game is played as many
times as one wishes and the total score of the game is deﬁned as the sum
of points received after successive plays. It is observed that every integer
x \geq1986 can be obtained as the total score whereas 1985 and 663 cannot.
Determine a and b.
