# IMO 1986 LL ROM63

**Origin:** ROM

## Problem

Let AA′, BB′, CC′ be the bisectors of the angles of a triangle
ABC (A′ \inBC, B′ \inCA, C′ \inAB). Prove that each of the lines A′B′,
B′C′, C′A′ intersects the incircle in two points.
