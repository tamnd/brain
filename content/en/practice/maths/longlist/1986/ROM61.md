# IMO 1986 LL ROM61

**Origin:** ROM

## Problem

Given a positive integer n, ﬁnd the greatest integer p with the
property that for any function f : P(X) \toC, where X and C are sets of
cardinality n and p, respectively, there exist two distinct sets A, B \inP(X)
such that f(A) = f(B) = f(A \cupB). (P(X) is the family of all subsets of
X.)
