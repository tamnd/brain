# IMO 1986 LL FRA21

**Origin:** FRA

## Problem

Let AB be a segment of unit length and let C, D be variable
points of this segment. Find the maximum value of the product of the
lengths of the six distinct segments with endpoints in the set {A, B, C, D}.
