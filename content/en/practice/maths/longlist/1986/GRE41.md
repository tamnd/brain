# IMO 1986 LL GRE41

**Origin:** GRE

## Problem

Let M, N, P be the midpoints of the sides BC, CA, AB of a
triangle ABC. The lines AM, BN, CP intersect the circumcircle of ABC
at points A′, B′, C′, respectively. Show that if A′B′C′ is an equilateral
triangle, then so is ABC.
