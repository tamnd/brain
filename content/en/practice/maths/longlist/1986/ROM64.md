# IMO 1986 LL ROM64

**Origin:** ROM

## Problem

Let (an)n\inN be the sequence of integers deﬁned recursively by
a1 = a2 = 1, an+2 = 7an+1 −an −2 for n \geq1. Prove that an is a perfect
square for every n.
