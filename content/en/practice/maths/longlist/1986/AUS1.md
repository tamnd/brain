# IMO 1986 LL AUS1

**Origin:** AUS

## Problem

Let k be one of the integers 2, 3, 4 and let n = 2k −1. Prove
the inequality
1 + bk + b2k + \cdot \cdot \cdot + bnk \geq(1 + bn)k
for all real b \geq0.
