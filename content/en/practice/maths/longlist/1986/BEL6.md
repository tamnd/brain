# IMO 1986 LL BEL6

**Origin:** BEL

## Problem

In an urn there are one ball marked 1, two balls marked 2, and
so on, up to n balls marked n. Two balls are randomly drawn without
replacement. Find the probability that the two balls are assigned the same
number.
