# IMO 1986 LL IRE46

**Origin:** IRE

## Problem

We wish to construct a matrix with 19 rows and 86 columns,
with entries xij \in{0, 1, 2} (1 \leqi \leq19, 1 \leqj \leq86), such that:
(i) in each column there are exactly k terms equal to 0;
(ii) for any distinct j, k \in{1, . . . , 86} there is i \in{1, . . . , 19} with xij +
xik = 3.
For what values of k is this possible?
