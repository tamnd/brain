# IMO 1986 LL FRA23

**Origin:** FRA

## Problem

Let I and J be the centers of the incircle and the excircle in
the angle BAC of the triangle ABC. For any point M in the plane of
the triangle, not on the line BC, denote by IM and JM the centers of the
incircle and the excircle (touching BC) of the triangle BCM. Find the
locus of points M for which IIMJJM is a rectangle.
