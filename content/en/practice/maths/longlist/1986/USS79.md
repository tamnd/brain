# IMO 1986 LL USS79

**Origin:** USS

## Problem

Let AA1, BB1, CC1 be the altitudes in an acute-angled triangle
ABC. K and M are points on the line segments A1C1 and B1C1 respec-
tively. Prove that if the angles MAK and CAA1 are equal, then the angle
C1KM is bisected by AK.
