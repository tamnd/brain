# IMO 1986 LL GBR31

**Origin:** GBR

## Problem

Let P and Q be distinct points in the plane of a triangle ABC
such that AP : AQ = BP : BQ = CP : CQ. Prove that the line PQ
passes through the circumcenter of the triangle.
