# IMO 1986 LL TUR73

**Origin:** TUR

## Problem

Let (ai)i\inN be a strictly increasing sequence of positive real
numbers such that limi\to\inftyai = +\inftyand ai+1/ai \leq10 for each i. Prove
that for every positive integer k there are inﬁnitely many pairs (i, j) with
10k \leqai/aj \leq10k+1.
