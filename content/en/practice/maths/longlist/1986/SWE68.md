# IMO 1986 LL SWE68

**Origin:** SWE

## Problem

Consider the equation x4 + ax3 + bx2 + ax + 1 = 0 with real
coeﬃcients a, b. Determine the number of distinct real roots and their
multiplicities for various values of a and b. Display your result graphically
in the (a, b) plane.
