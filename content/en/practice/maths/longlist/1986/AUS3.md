# IMO 1986 LL AUS3

**Origin:** AUS

## Problem

A line parallel to the side BC of a triangle ABC meets AB
in F and AC in E. Prove that the circles on BE and CF as diameters
intersect in a point lying on the altitude of the triangle ABC dropped
from A to BC.
