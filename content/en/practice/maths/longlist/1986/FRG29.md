# IMO 1986 LL FRG29

**Origin:** FRG

## Problem

We deﬁne a binary operation ⋆in the plane as follows: Given
two points A and B in the plane, C = A ⋆B is the third vertex of the
equilateral triangle ABC oriented positively. What is the relative position
of three points I, M, O in the plane if I ⋆(M ⋆O) = (O ⋆I) ⋆M holds?
