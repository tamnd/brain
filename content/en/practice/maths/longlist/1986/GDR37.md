# IMO 1986 LL GDR37

**Origin:** GDR

## Problem

Prove that the set {1, 2, . . ., 1986} can be partitioned into 27
disjoint sets so that no one of these sets contains an arithmetic triple (i.e.,
three distinct numbers in an arithmetic progression).
