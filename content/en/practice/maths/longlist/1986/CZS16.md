# IMO 1986 LL CZS16

**Origin:** CZS

## Problem

Given a positive integer k, ﬁnd the least integer nk for which
there exist ﬁve sets S1, S2, S3, S4, S5 with the following properties:
|Sj| = k
for j = 1, . . . , 5,
j=1
Sj
= nk;
|Si \capSi+1| = 0 = |S5 \capS1|,
for i = 1, . . . , 4.
