# IMO 1971 LL GBR14

**Origin:** GBR

## Problem

Note that 83 −73 = 169 = 132 and 13 = 22 + 32. Prove that
if the diﬀerence between two consecutive cubes is a square, then it is the
square of the sum of two consecutive squares.
