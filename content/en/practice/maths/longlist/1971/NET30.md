# IMO 1971 LL NET30

**Origin:** NET

## Problem

Prove that the system of equations
2yz + x −y −z = a,
2xz −x + y −z = a,
2xy −x −y + z = a,
a being a parameter, cannot have ﬁve distinct solutions. For what values
of a does this system have four distinct integer solutions?
