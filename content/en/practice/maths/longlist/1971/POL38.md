# IMO 1971 LL POL38

**Origin:** POL

## Problem

Let A, B, C be three points with integer coordinates in the
plane and K a circle with radius R passing through A, B, C. Show that
AB\cdotBC\cdotCA \geq2R, and if the center of K is in the origin of the coordinates,
show that AB \cdot BC \cdot CA \geq4R.
