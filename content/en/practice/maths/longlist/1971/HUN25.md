# IMO 1971 LL HUN25

**Origin:** HUN

## Problem

Let ABC, AA1A2, BB1B2, CC1C2 be four equilateral triangles
in the plane satisfying only that they are all positively oriented (i.e., in
the counterclockwise direction). Denote the midpoints of the segments
A2B1, B2C1, C2A1 by P, Q, R in this order. Prove that the triangle PQR
is equilateral.
