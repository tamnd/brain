# IMO 1971 LL HUN22

**Origin:** HUN

## Problem

We are given an n \times n board, where n is an odd number. In
each cell of the board either +1 or −1 is written. Let ak and bk denote the
products of numbers in the kth row and in the kth column respectively.
Prove that the sum a1 + a2 + \cdot \cdot \cdot + an + b1 + b2 + \cdot \cdot \cdot + bn cannot be equal
to zero.
