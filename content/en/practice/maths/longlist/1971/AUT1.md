# IMO 1971 LL AUT1

**Origin:** AUT

## Problem

The points S(i, j) with integer Cartesian coordinates 0 < i \leqn,
0 < j \leqm, m \leqn, form a lattice. Find the number of:
(a) rectangles with vertices on the lattice and sides parallel to the coor-
dinate axes;
(b) squares with vertices on the lattice and sides parallel to the coordinate
axes;
(c) squares in total, with vertices on the lattice.
