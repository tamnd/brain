# IMO 1971 LL GDR18

**Origin:** GDR

## Problem

Let a1, a2, . . . , an be positive numbers, mg = (a1a2 \cdot \cdot \cdot an)1/n
their geometric mean, and ma = (a1 + a2 + \cdot \cdot \cdot + an)/n their arithmetic
mean. Prove that
(1 + mg)n \leq(1 + a1) \cdot \cdot \cdot (1 + an) \leq(1 + ma)n.
