# IMO 1971 LL SWE45

**Origin:** SWE

## Problem

Let m and n denote integers greater than 1, and let \nu(n) be
the number of primes less than or equal to n. Show that if the equation
n
\nu(n) = m has a solution, then so does the equation
n
\nu(n) = m −1.
