# IMO 1971 LL USS49

**Origin:** USS

## Problem

Diagonals of a convex quadrilateral ABCD intersect at a
point O. Find all angles of this quadrilateral if ∡OBA = 30◦, ∡OCB =
45◦, ∡ODC = 45◦, and ∡OAD = 30◦.
