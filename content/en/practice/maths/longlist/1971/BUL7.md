# IMO 1971 LL BUL7

**Origin:** BUL

## Problem

In a triangle ABC, let H be its orthocenter, O its circumcenter,
and R its circumradius. Prove that:
(a) |OH| = R\sqrt1 −8 cos \alpha cos \beta cos \gamma, where \alpha, \beta, \gamma are angles of the tri-
angle ABC;
(b) O \equivH if and only if ABC is equilateral.
