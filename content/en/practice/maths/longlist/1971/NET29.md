# IMO 1971 LL NET29

**Origin:** NET

## Problem

A rhombus with its incircle is given. At each vertex of the
rhombus a circle is constructed that touches the incircle and two edges of
the rhombus. These circles have radii r1, r2, while the incircle has radius
r. Given that r1 and r2 are natural numbers and that r1r2 = r, ﬁnd r1, r2,
and r.
