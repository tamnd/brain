# IMO 1971 LL USS51

**Origin:** USS

## Problem

Suppose that the sides AB and DC of a convex quadrilateral
ABCD are not parallel. On the sides BC and AD, pairs of points (M, N)
and (K, L) are chosen such that BM = MN = NC and AK = KL = LD.
Prove that the areas of triangles OKM and OLN are diﬀerent, where O
is the intersection point of AB and CD.
