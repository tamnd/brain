# IMO 1983 LL CAN17

**Origin:** CAN

## Problem

In how many ways can 1, 2, . . . , 2n be arranged in a 2 \times n
rectangular array
 a1 a2 \cdot \cdot \cdot an
b1 b2 \cdot \cdot \cdot bn

for which:
(i) a1 < a2 < \cdot \cdot \cdot < an,
(ii) b1 < b2 < \cdot \cdot \cdot < bn,
(iii) a1 < b1, a2 < b2, . . . , an < bn?
