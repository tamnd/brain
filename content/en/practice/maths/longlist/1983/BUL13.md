# IMO 1983 LL BUL13

**Origin:** BUL

## Problem

Let p be a prime number and a1, a2, . . . , a(p+1)/2 diﬀerent nat-
ural numbers less than or equal to p. Prove that for each natural number
r less than or equal to p, there exist two numbers (perhaps equal) ai and
aj such that
p \equivaiaj(mod r).
