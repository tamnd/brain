# IMO 1983 LL VIE73

**Origin:** VIE

## Problem

Let ABC be a nonequilateral triangle. Prove that there exist
two points P and Q in the plane of the triangle, one in the interior and
one in the exterior of the circumcircle of ABC, such that the orthogonal
projections of any of these two points on the sides of the triangle are
vertices of an equilateral triangle.
