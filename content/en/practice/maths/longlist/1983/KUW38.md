# IMO 1983 LL KUW38

**Origin:** KUW

## Problem

Let {un} be the sequence deﬁned by its ﬁrst two terms u0, u1
and the recursion formula
un+2 = un −un+1.
(a) Show that un can be written in the form un = \alphaan + \betabn, where
a, b, \alpha, \beta are constants independent of n that have to be determined.
(b) If Sn = u0 + u1 + \cdot \cdot \cdot + un, prove that Sn + un−1 is a constant inde-
pendent of n. Determine this constant.
