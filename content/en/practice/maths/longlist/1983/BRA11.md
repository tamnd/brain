# IMO 1983 LL BRA11

**Origin:** BRA

## Problem

A boy at point A wants to get water at a circular lake and
carry it to point B. Find the point C on the lake such that the distance
walked by the boy is the shortest possible given that the line AB and the
lake are exterior to each other.
