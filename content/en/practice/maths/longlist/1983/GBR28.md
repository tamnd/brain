# IMO 1983 LL GBR28

**Origin:** GBR

## Problem

Show that if the sides a, b, c of a triangle satisfy the equation
2(ab2 + bc2 + ca2) = a2b + b2c + c2a + 3abc,
then the triangle is equilateral. Show also that the equation can be satisﬁed
by positive real numbers that are not the sides of a triangle.
