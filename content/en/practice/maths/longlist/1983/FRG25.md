# IMO 1983 LL FRG25

**Origin:** FRG

## Problem

How many permutations a1, a2, . . . , an of {1, 2, . . ., n} are
sorted into increasing order by at most three repetitions of the following
operation: Move from left to right and interchange ai and ai+1 whenever
ai > ai+1 for i running from 1 up to n −1?
