# IMO 1983 LL BEL5

**Origin:** BEL

## Problem

Consider the set Q2 of points in R2, both of whose coordinates
are rational.
(a) Prove that the union of segments with vertices from Q2 is the entire
set R2.
(b) Is the convex hull of Q2 (i.e., the smallest convex set in R2 that con-
tains Q2) equal to R2?
