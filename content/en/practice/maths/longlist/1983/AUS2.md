# IMO 1983 LL AUS2

**Origin:** AUS

## Problem

Seventeen cities are served by four airlines. It is noted that
there is direct service (without stops) between any two cities and that
all airline schedules oﬀer round-trip ﬂights. Prove that at least one of the
airlines can oﬀer a round trip with an odd number of landings.
