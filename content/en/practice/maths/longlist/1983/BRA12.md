# IMO 1983 LL BRA12

**Origin:** BRA

## Problem

The number 0 or 1 is to be assigned to each of the n vertices
of a regular polygon. In how many diﬀerent ways can this be done (if we
consider two assignments that can be obtained one from the other through
rotation in the plane of the polygon to be identical)?
