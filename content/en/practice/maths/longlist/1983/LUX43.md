# IMO 1983 LL LUX43

**Origin:** LUX

## Problem

Given a square ABCD, let P, Q, R, and S be four variable
points on the sides AB, BC, CD, and DA, respectively. Determine the
positions of the points P, Q, R, and S for which the quadrilateral PQRS
is a parallelogram, a rectangle, a square, or a trapezoid.
