# IMO 1983 LL LUX42

**Origin:** LUX

## Problem

Consider the square ABCD in which a segment is drawn
between each vertex and the midpoints of both opposite sides. Find the
ratio of the area of the octagon determined by these segments and the
area of the square ABCD.
