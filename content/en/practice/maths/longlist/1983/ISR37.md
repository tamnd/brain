# IMO 1983 LL ISR37

**Origin:** ISR

## Problem

The points A1, A2, . . . , A1983 are set on the circumference of a
circle and each is given one of the values \pm1. Show that if the number of
points with the value +1 is greater than 1789, then at least 1207 of the
points will have the property that the partial sums that can be formed by
taking the numbers from them to any other point, in either direction, are
strictly positive.
