# IMO 1979 LL FIN19

**Origin:** FIN

## Problem

For k = 1, 2, . . . consider the k-tuples (a1, a2, . . . , ak) of positive
integers such that
a1 + 2a2 + \cdot \cdot \cdot + kak = 1979.
Show that there are as many such k-tuples with odd k as there are with
even k.
