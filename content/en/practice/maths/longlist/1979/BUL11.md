# IMO 1979 LL BUL11

**Origin:** BUL

## Problem

Prove that a pyramid A1A2 . . . A2k+1S with equal lateral edges
and equal space angles between adjacent lateral walls is regular.
Variant.
Prove that a pyramid A1 . . . A2k+1S with equal space angles
between adjacent lateral walls is regular if there exists a sphere tangent
to all its edges.
