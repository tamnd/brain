# IMO 1979 LL HUN42

**Origin:** HUN

## Problem

Let a quadratic polynomial g(x) = ax2 + bx + c be given and
an integer n \geq1. Prove that there exists at most one polynomial f(x) of
nth degree such that f(g(x)) = g(f(x)).
