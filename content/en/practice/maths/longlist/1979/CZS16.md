# IMO 1979 LL CZS16

**Origin:** CZS

## Problem

Let Q be a square with side length 6. Find the smallest integer
n such that in Q there exists a set S of n points with the property that
any square with side 1 completely contained in Q contains in its interior
at least one point from S.
