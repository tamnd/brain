# IMO 1979 LL YUG81

**Origin:** YUG

## Problem

Let P be the set of rectangular parallelepipeds that have at
least one edge of integer length. If a rectangular parallelepiped P0 can be
decomposed into parallelepipeds P1, P2, . . . , Pn \inP, prove that P0 \inP.
