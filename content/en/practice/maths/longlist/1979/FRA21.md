# IMO 1979 LL FRA21

**Origin:** FRA

## Problem

Let E be the set of all bijective mappings from R to R satisfying
(\forallt \inR)
f(t) + f −1(t) = 2t,
where f −1 is the mapping inverse to f. Find all elements of E that are
monotonic mappings.
