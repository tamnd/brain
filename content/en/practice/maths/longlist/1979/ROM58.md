# IMO 1979 LL ROM58

**Origin:** ROM

## Problem

Prove that there exists a natural number k0 such that for
every natural number k > k0 we may ﬁnd a ﬁnite number of lines in the
plane, not all parallel to one of them, that divide the plane exactly in k
regions. Find k0.
