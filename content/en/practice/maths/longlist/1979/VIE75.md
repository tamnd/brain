# IMO 1979 LL VIE75

**Origin:** VIE

## Problem

Given an equilateral triangle ABC, let M be an arbitrary point
in space.
(a) Prove that one can construct a triangle from the segments MA, MB,
MC.
(b) Suppose that P and Q are two points symmetric with respect to the
center O of ABC. Prove that the two triangles constructed from the
segments PA, PB, PC and QA, QB, QC are of equal area.
