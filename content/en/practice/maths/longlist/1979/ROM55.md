# IMO 1979 LL ROM55

**Origin:** ROM

## Problem

Let a, b be coprime integers. Show that the equation ax2 +
by2 = z3 has an inﬁnite set of solutions (x, y, z) with x, y, z \inZ and x, y
mutually coprime (in each solution).
