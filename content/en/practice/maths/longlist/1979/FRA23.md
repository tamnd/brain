# IMO 1979 LL FRA23

**Origin:** FRA

## Problem

Consider the set E consisting of pairs of integers (a, b), with a \geq
1 and b \geq1, that satisfy in the decimal system the following properties:
(i) b is written with three digits, as \alpha2\alpha1\alpha0, \alpha2 ̸= 0;
(ii) a is written as \betap . . . \beta1\beta0 for some p;
(iii) (a + b)2 is written as \betap . . . \beta1\beta0\alpha2\alpha1\alpha0.
Find the elements of E.
