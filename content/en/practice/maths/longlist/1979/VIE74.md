# IMO 1979 LL VIE74

**Origin:** VIE

## Problem

Given an equilateral triangle ABC of side a in a plane, let
M be a point on the circumcircle of the triangle. Prove that the sum
s = MA4 + MB4 + MC4 is independent of the position of the point M
on the circle, and determine that constant value as a function of a.
