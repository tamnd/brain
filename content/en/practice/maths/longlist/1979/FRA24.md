# IMO 1979 LL FRA24

**Origin:** FRA

## Problem

Let a and b be coprime integers, greater than or equal to 1.
Prove that all integers n greater than or equal to (a −1)(b −1) can be
written in the form:
n = ua + vb,
with (u, v) \inN \times N.
