# IMO 1979 LL VIE72

**Origin:** VIE

## Problem

Let f(x) be a polynomial with integer coeﬃcients. Prove that
if f(x) equals 1979 for four diﬀerent integer values of x, then f(x) cannot
be equal to 2 \times 1979 for any integral value of x.
