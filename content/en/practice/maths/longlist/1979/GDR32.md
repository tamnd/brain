# IMO 1979 LL GDR32

**Origin:** GDR

## Problem

Let n, k \geq1 be natural numbers. Find the number A(n, k) of
solutions in integers of the equation
|x1| + |x2| + \cdot \cdot \cdot + |xk| = n.
