# IMO 1979 LL ISR43

**Origin:** ISR

## Problem

Let a, b, c denote the lengths of the sides BC, CA, AB, respec-
tively, of a triangle ABC. If P is any point on the circumference of the
circle inscribed in the triangle, show that aPA2+bPB2+cPC2 is constant.
