# IMO 1982 LL VIE56

**Origin:** VIE

## Problem

Let f(x) = ax2 + bx + c and g(x) = cx2 + bx + a. If |f(0)| \leq1,
|f(1)| \leq1, |f(−1)| \leq1, prove that for |x| \leq1,
(a) |f(x)| \leq5/4,
(b) |g(x)| \leq2.
