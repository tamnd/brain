# IMO 1982 LL BEL6

**Origin:** BEL

## Problem

On the three distinct lines a, b, and c three points A, B, and
C are given, respectively. Construct three collinear points X, Y, Z on lines
a, b, c, respectively, such that BY
AX = 2 and CZ
AX = 3.
