# IMO 1982 LL TUN44

**Origin:** TUN

## Problem

Let A and B be positions of two ships M and N, respectively,
at the moment when N saw M moving with constant speed v following
the line Ax. In search of help, N moves with speed kv (k < 1) along the
line By in order to meet M as soon as possible. Denote by C the point of
meeting of the two ships, and set
AB = d, \angleBAC = \alpha, 0 \leq\alpha < \pi
2 .
Determine the angle \angleABC = \beta and time t that N needs in order to meet
M.
