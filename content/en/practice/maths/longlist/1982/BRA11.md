# IMO 1982 LL BRA11

**Origin:** BRA

## Problem

A rectangular pool table has a hole at each of three of its
corners. The lengths of sides of the table are the real numbers a and b. A
billiard ball is shot from the fourth corner along its angle bisector. The
ball falls in one of the holes. What should the relation between a and b
be for this to happen?
