# IMO 1982 LL TUN43

**Origin:** TUN

## Problem

(a) What is the maximal number of acute angles in a convex
polygon?
(b) Consider m points in the interior of a convex n-gon. The n-gon is
partitioned into triangles whose vertices are among the n + m given
points (the vertices of the n-gon and the given points). Each of the
m points in the interior is a vertex of at least one triangle. Find the
number of triangles obtained.
