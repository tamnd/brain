# IMO 1982 LL FRA26

**Origin:** FRA

## Problem

Let (an)n\geq0 and (bn)n\geq0 be two sequences of natural numbers.
Determine whether there exists a pair (p, q) of natural numbers that satisfy
p < q
and
ap \leqaq, bp \leqbq.
