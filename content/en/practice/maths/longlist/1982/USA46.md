# IMO 1982 LL USA46

**Origin:** USA

## Problem

Prove that if a diagonal is drawn in a quadrilateral inscribed
in a circle, the sum of the radii of the circles inscribed in the two triangles
thus formed is the same, no matter which diagonal is drawn.
