# IMO 1982 LL USS50

**Origin:** USS

## Problem

Let O be the midpoint of the axis of a right circular cylinder.
Let A and B be diametrically opposite points of one base, and C a point
of the other base circle that does not belong to the plane OAB. Prove
that the sum of dihedral angles of the trihedral OABC is equal to 2\pi.
