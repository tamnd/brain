# IMO 1982 LL USS52

**Origin:** USS

## Problem

We are given 2n natural numbers
1, 1, 2, 2, 3, 3, . . ., n −1, n −1, n, n.
Find all n for which these numbers can be arranged in a row such that
for each k \leqn, there are exactly k numbers between the two numbers k.
