# IMO 1982 LL BUL13

**Origin:** BUL

## Problem

A regular n-gonal truncated pyramid is circumscribed around
a sphere. Denote the areas of the base and the lateral surfaces of the
pyramid by S1, S2, and S, respectively. Let \sigma be the area of the polygon
whose vertices are the tangential points of the sphere and the lateral faces
of the pyramid. Prove that
\sigmaS = 4S1S2 cos2 \pi
n.
