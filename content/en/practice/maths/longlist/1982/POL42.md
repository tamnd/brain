# IMO 1982 LL POL42

**Origin:** POL

## Problem

Let F be the family of all k-element subsets of the set
{1, 2, . . ., 2k + 1}. Prove that there exists a bijective function f : F \toF
such that for every A \inF, the sets A and f(A) are disjoint.
