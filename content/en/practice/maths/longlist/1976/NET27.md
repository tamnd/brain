# IMO 1976 LL NET27

**Origin:** NET

## Problem

In a plane three points P, Q, R, not on a line, are given. Let
k, l, m be positive numbers. Construct a triangle ABC whose sides pass
through P, Q, and R such that
P divides the segment AB in the ratio 1 : k,
Q divides the segment BC in the ratio 1 : l, and
R divides the segment CA in the ratio 1 : m.
