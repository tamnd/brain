# IMO 1976 LL GDR22

**Origin:** GDR

## Problem

A regular pentagon A1A2A3A4A5 with side length s is given.
At each point Ai a sphere Ki of radius s/2 is constructed. There are two
spheres K1′ and K2′ eah of radius s/2 touching all the ﬁve spheres Ki.
Decide whether K1′ and K2′ intersect each other, touch each other, or
have no common points.
