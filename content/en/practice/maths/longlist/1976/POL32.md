# IMO 1976 LL POL32

**Origin:** POL

## Problem

We consider the inﬁnite chessboard covering the whole plane.
In every ﬁeld of the chessboard there is a nonnegative real number. Every
number is the arithmetic mean of the numbers in the four adjacent ﬁelds
of the chessboard. Prove that the numbers occurring in the ﬁelds of the
chessboard are all equal.
