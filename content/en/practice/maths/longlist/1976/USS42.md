# IMO 1976 LL USS42

**Origin:** USS

## Problem

For a point O inside a triangle ABC, denote by A1, B1, C1
the respective intersection points of AO, BO, CO with the corresponding
sides. Let n1 =
AO
A1O, n2 =
BO
B1O, n3 =
CO
C1O . What possible values of
n1, n2, n3 can all be positive integers?
