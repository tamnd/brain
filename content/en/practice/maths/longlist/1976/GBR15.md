# IMO 1976 LL GBR15

**Origin:** GBR

## Problem

Let ABC and A′B′C′ be any two coplanar triangles. Let L be
a point such that AL\parallelBC, A′L\parallelB′C′, and M, N similarly deﬁned. The
line BC meets B′C′ at P, and similarly deﬁned are Q and R. Prove that
PL, QM, RN are concurrent.
