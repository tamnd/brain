# IMO 1976 LL VIE49

**Origin:** VIE

## Problem

Determine whether there exist 1976 nonsimilar triangles with
angles \alpha, \beta, \gamma, each of them satisfying the relations
sin \alpha + sin \beta + sin \gamma
cos \alpha + cos \beta + cos \gamma = 12
and
sin \alpha sin \beta sin \gamma = 12
25.
