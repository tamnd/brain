# IMO 1976 LL YUG51

**Origin:** YUG

## Problem

Four swallows are catching a ﬂy. At ﬁrst, the swallows are
at the four vertices of a tetrahedron, and the ﬂy is in its interior. Their
maximal speeds are equal. Prove that the swallows can catch the ﬂy.
