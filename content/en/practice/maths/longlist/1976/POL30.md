# IMO 1976 LL POL30

**Origin:** POL

## Problem

Prove that if P(x) = (x−a)kQ(x), where k is a positive integer,
a is a nonzero real number, Q(x) is a nonzero polynomial, then P(x) has
at least k + 1 nonzero coeﬃcients.
