# IMO 1976 LL BUL5

**Origin:** BUL

## Problem

Let ABCDS be a pyramid with four faces and with ABCD
as a base, and let a plane \alpha through the vertex A meet its edges SB and
SD at points M and N, respectively. Prove that if the intersection of the
plane \alpha with the pyramid ABCDS is a parallelogram, then
SM \cdot SN > BM \cdot DN.
