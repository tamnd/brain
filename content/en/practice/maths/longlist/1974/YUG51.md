# IMO 1974 LL YUG51

**Origin:** YUG

## Problem

There are n points on a ﬂat piece of paper, any two of them
at a distance of at least 2 from each other. An inattentive pupil spills
ink on a part of the paper such that the total area of the damaged part
equals 3/2. Prove that there exist two vectors of equal length less than 1
and with their sum having a given direction, such that after a translation
by either of these two vectors no points of the given set remain in the
damaged area.
