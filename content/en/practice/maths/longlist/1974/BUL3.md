# IMO 1974 LL BUL3

**Origin:** BUL

## Problem

Let ABCD be an arbitrary quadrilateral. Let squares ABB1A2,
BCC1B2, CDD1C2, DAA1D2 be constructed in the exterior of the
quadrilateral. Furthermore, let AA1PA2 and CC1QC2 be parallelograms.
For any arbitrary point P in the interior of ABCD, parallelograms RASC
and RPTQ are constructed. Prove that these two parallelograms have two
vertices in common.
