# IMO 1974 LL USA41

**Origin:** USA

## Problem

Through the circumcenter O of an arbitrary acute-angled trian-
gle, chords A1A2, B1B2, C1C2 are drawn parallel to the sides BC, CA, AB
of the triangle respectively. If R is the radius of the circumcircle, prove
that
A1O \cdot OA2 + B1O \cdot OB2 + C1O \cdot OC2 = R2.
