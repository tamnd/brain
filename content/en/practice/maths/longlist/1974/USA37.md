# IMO 1974 LL USA37

**Origin:** USA

## Problem

Let a, b, and c denote the three sides of a billiard table in the
shape of an equilateral triangle. A ball is placed at the midpoint of side
a and then propelled toward side b with direction deﬁned by the angle \theta.
For what values of \theta will the ball strike the sides b, c, a in that order?
