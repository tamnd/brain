# IMO 1974 LL USS44

**Origin:** USS

## Problem

We are given n mass points of equal mass in space. We deﬁne
a sequence of points O1, O2, O3, . . . as follows: O1 is an arbitrary point
(within the unit distance of at least one of the n points); O2 is the center
of gravity of all the n given points that are inside the unit sphere centered
at O1; O3 is the center of gravity of all of the n given points that are
inside the unit sphere centered at O2; etc. Prove that starting from some
m, all points Om, Om+1, Om+2, . . . coincide.
