# IMO 1974 LL USA39

**Origin:** USA

## Problem

Let n be a positive integer, n \geq2, and consider the polynomial
equation
xn −xn−2 −x + 2 = 0.
For each n, determine all complex numbers x that satisfy the equation
and have modulus |x| = 1.
