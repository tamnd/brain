# IMO 1974 LL GBR17

**Origin:** GBR

## Problem

Show that there exists a set S of 15 distinct circles on the
surface of a sphere, all having the same radius and such that 5 touch
exactly 5 others, 5 touch exactly 4 others, and 5 touch exactly 3 others.
