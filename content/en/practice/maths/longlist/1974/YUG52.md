# IMO 1974 LL YUG52

**Origin:** YUG

## Problem

A fox stands in the center of the ﬁeld which has the form of an
equilateral triangle, and a rabbit stands at one of its vertices. The fox can
move through the whole ﬁeld, while the rabbit can move only along the
border of the ﬁeld. The maximal speeds of the fox and rabbit are equal
to u and v, respectively. Prove that:
(a) If 2u > v, the fox can catch the rabbit, no matter how the rabbit
moves.
(b) If 2u \leqv, the rabbit can always run away from the fox.
