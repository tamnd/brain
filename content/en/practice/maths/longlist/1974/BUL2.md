# IMO 1974 LL BUL2

**Origin:** BUL

## Problem

Let {un} be the Fibonacci sequence, i.e., u0 = 0, u1 = 1,
un = un−1 + un−2 for n > 1. Prove that there exist inﬁnitely many prime
numbers p that divide up−1.
