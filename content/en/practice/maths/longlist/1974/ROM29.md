# IMO 1974 LL ROM29

**Origin:** ROM

## Problem

Let A, B, C, D be points in space. If for every point M on the
segment AB the sum
area(AMC)+area(CMD)+area(DMB)
is constant show that the points A, B, C, D lie in the same plane.
