# IMO 1974 LL ROM27

**Origin:** ROM

## Problem

Let C1 and C2 be circles in the same plane, P1 and P2 arbitrary
points on C1 and C2 respectively, and Q the midpoint of segment P1P2.
Find the locus of points Q as P1 and P2 go through all possible positions.
Alternative version. Let C1, C2, C3 be three circles in the same plane. Find
the locus of the centroid of triangle P1P2P3 as P1, P2, and P3 go through
all possible positions on C1, C2, and C3 respectively.
