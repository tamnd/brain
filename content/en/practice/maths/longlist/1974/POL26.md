# IMO 1974 LL POL26

**Origin:** POL

## Problem

Let g(k) be the number of partitions of a k-element set M, i.e.,
the number of families {A1, A2, . . . , As} of nonempty subsets of M such
that Ai \capAj = \emptysetfor i ̸= j and %n
i=1 Ai = M. Prove that
nn \leqg(2n) \leq(2n)2n
for every n.
