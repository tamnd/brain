# IMO 1974 LL CZS12

**Origin:** CZS

## Problem

A circle K with radius r, a point D on K, and a convex
angle with vertex S and rays a and b are given in the plane. Construct
a parallelogram ABCD such that A and B lie on a and b respectively,
SA + SB = r, and C lies on K.
