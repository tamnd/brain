# IMO 1974 LL USS46

**Origin:** USS

## Problem

Outside an arbitrary triangle ABC, triangles ADB and BCE
are constructed such that \angleADB = \angleBEC = 90◦and \angleDAB =
\angleEBC = 30◦. On the segment AC the point F with AF = 3FC is
chosen. Prove that
\angleDFE = 90◦
and
\angleFDE = 30◦.
