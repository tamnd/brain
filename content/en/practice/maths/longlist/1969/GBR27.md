# IMO 1969 LL GBR27

**Origin:** GBR

## Problem

The segment AB perpendicularly bisects CD at X. Show that,
subject to restrictions, there is a right circular cone whose axis passes
through X and on whose surface lie the points A, B, C, D. What are the
restrictions?
