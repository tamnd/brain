# IMO 1969 LL BUL8

**Origin:** BUL

## Problem

Find all functions f deﬁned for all x that satisfy the condition
xf(y) + yf(x) = (x + y)f(x)f(y),
for all x and y. Prove that exactly two of them are continuous.
