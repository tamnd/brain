# IMO 1969 LL GBR28

**Origin:** GBR

## Problem

Let us deﬁne u0 = 0, u1 = 1 and for n \geq0, un+2 = aun+1+bun,
a and b being positive integers. Express un as a polynomial in a and b.
Prove the result. Given that b is prime, prove that b divides a(ub −1).
