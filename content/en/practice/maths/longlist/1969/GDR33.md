# IMO 1969 LL GDR33

**Origin:** GDR

## Problem

Given a ring G in the plane bounded by two concentric circles
with radii R and R/2, prove that we can cover this region with 8 disks of
radius 2R/5. (A region is covered if each of its points is inside or on the
border of some disk.)
