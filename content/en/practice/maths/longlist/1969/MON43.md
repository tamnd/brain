# IMO 1969 LL MON43

**Origin:** MON

## Problem

Let p and q be two prime numbers greater than 3. Prove that
if their diﬀerence is 2n, then for any two integers m and n, the number
S = p2m+1 + q2m+1 is divisible by 3.
