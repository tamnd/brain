# IMO 1969 LL BEL1

**Origin:** BEL

## Problem

A parabola P1 with equation x2 −2py = 0 and parabola P2
with equation x2 + 2py = 0, p > 0, are given. A line t is tangent to P2.
Find the locus of pole M of the line t with respect to P1.
