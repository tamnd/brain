# IMO 1969 LL FRA20

**Origin:** FRA

## Problem

A polygon (not necessarily convex) with vertices in the lattice
points of a rectangular grid is given. The area of the polygon is S. If I is
the number of lattice points that are strictly in the interior of the polygon
and B the number of lattice points on the border of the polygon, ﬁnd the
number T = 2S −B −2I + 2.
