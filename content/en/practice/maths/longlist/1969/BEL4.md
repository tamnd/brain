# IMO 1969 LL BEL4

**Origin:** BEL

## Problem

Let O be a point on a nondegenerate conic. A right angle with
vertex O intersects the conic at points A and B. Prove that the line AB
passes through a ﬁxed point located on the normal to the conic through
the point O.
