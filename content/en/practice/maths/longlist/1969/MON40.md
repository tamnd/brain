# IMO 1969 LL MON40

**Origin:** MON

## Problem

Find the number of ﬁve-digit numbers with the following
properties: there are two pairs of digits such that digits from each pair are
equal and are next to each other, digits from diﬀerent pairs are diﬀerent,
and the remaining digit (which does not belong to any of the pairs) is
diﬀerent from the other digits.
