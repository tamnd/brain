# IMO 1969 LL BEL2

**Origin:** BEL

## Problem

(a) Find the equations of regular hyperbolas passing through
the points A(\alpha, 0), B(\beta, 0), and C(0, \gamma).
(b) Prove that all such hyperbolas pass through the orthocenter H of the
triangle ABC.
(c) Find the locus of the centers of these hyperbolas.
(d) Check whether this locus coincides with the nine-point circle of the
triangle ABC.
