# IMO 1969 LL YUG71

**Origin:** YUG

## Problem

Let four points Ai (i = 1, 2, 3, 4) in the plane determine four
triangles. In each of these triangles we choose the smallest angle. The sum
of these angles is denoted by S. What is the exact placement of the points
Ai if S = 180◦?
