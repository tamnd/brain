# IMO 1969 LL POL54

**Origin:** POL

## Problem

Given a polynomial f(x) with integer coeﬃcients whose value
is divisible by 3 for three integers k, k + 1, and k + 2, prove that f(m) is
divisible by 3 for all integers m.
