# IMO 1969 LL GDR29

**Origin:** GDR

## Problem

Find all real numbers \lambda such that the equation
sin4 x −cos4 x = \lambda(tan4 x −cot4 x)
(a) has no solution,
(b) has exactly one solution,
(c) has exactly two solutions,
(d) has more than two solutions (in the interval (0, \pi/4)).
