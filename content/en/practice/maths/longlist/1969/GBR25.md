# IMO 1969 LL GBR25

**Origin:** GBR

## Problem

Let a, b, x, y be positive integers such that a and b have no
common divisor greater than 1. Prove that the largest number not ex-
pressible in the form ax + by is ab −a −b. If N(k) is the largest number
not expressible in the form ax + by in only k ways, ﬁnd N(k).
