# IMO 1969 LL NET50

**Origin:** NET

## Problem

The bisectors of the exterior angles of a pentagon B1B2B3B4B5
form another pentagon A1A2A3A4A5. Construct B1B2B3B4B5 from the
given pentagon A1A2A3A4A5.
