# IMO 1969 LL GDR31

**Origin:** GDR

## Problem

Find the number of permutations a1, . . . , an of the set
{1, 2, . . ., n} such that |ai −ai+1| ̸= 1 for all i = 1, 2, . . ., n −1. Find
a recurrence formula and evaluate the number of such permutations for
n \leq6.
