# IMO 1969 LL CZS15

**Origin:** CZS

## Problem

Let K1, . . . , Kn be nonnegative integers. Prove that
K1!K2! \cdot \cdot \cdot Kn! \geq[K/n]!n,
where K = K1 + \cdot \cdot \cdot + Kn.
