# IMO 1969 LL SWE58

**Origin:** SWE

## Problem

Six points P1, . . . , P6 are given in 3-dimensional space such that
no four of them lie in the same plane. Each of the line segments PjPk is
colored black or white. Prove that there exists one triangle PjPkPl whose
edges are of the same color.
