# IMO 1969 LL NET48

**Origin:** NET

## Problem

Let x1, x2, x3, x4, and x5 be positive integers satisfying
x1 +x2 +x3 +x4 +x5 = 1000,
x1 −x2 +x3 −x4 +x5 > 0,
x1 +x2 −x3 +x4 −x5 > 0,
−x1 +x2 +x3 −x4 +x5 > 0,
x1 −x2 +x3 +x4 −x5 > 0,
−x1 +x2 −x3 +x4 +x5 > 0.
(a) Find the maximum of (x1 + x3)x2+x4.
(b) In how many diﬀerent ways can we choose x1, . . . , x5 to obtain the
desired maximum?
