# IMO 1969 LL NET46

**Origin:** NET

## Problem

The vertices of an (n + 1)-gon are placed on the edges of a
regular n-gon so that the perimeter of the n-gon is divided into equal
parts. How does one choose these n + 1 points in order to obtain the
(n + 1)gon with
(a) maximal area;
(b) minimal area?
