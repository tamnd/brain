# IMO 1969 LL MON41

**Origin:** MON

## Problem

Given two numbers x0 and x1, let \alpha and \beta be coeﬃcients
of the equation 1 −\alphay −\betay2 = 0. Under the given conditions, ﬁnd an
expression for the solution of the system
xn+2 −\alphaxn+1 −\betaxn = 0,
n = 0, 1, 2, . . . .
