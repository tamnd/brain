# IMO 1969 LL BUL10

**Origin:** BUL

## Problem

Let M be the point inside the right-angled triangle ABC
(\angleC = 90◦) such that
\angleMAB = \angleMBC = \angleMCA = ϕ.
Let \psi be the acute angle between the medians of AC and BC. Prove that
sin(ϕ+\psi)
sin(ϕ−\psi) = 5.
