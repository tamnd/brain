# IMO 1972 LL SWE38

**Origin:** SWE

## Problem

Congruent rectangles with sides m (cm) and n (cm) are
given (m, n positive integers). Characterize the rectangles that can be
constructed from these rectangles (in the fashion of a jigsaw puzzle). (The
number of rectangles is unbounded.)
