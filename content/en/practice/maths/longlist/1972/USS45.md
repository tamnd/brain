# IMO 1972 LL USS45

**Origin:** USS

## Problem

Let ABCD be a convex quadrilateral whose diagonals AC and
BD intersect at point O. Let a line through O intersect segment AB at
M and segment CD at N. Prove that the segment MN is not longer than
at least one of the segments AC and BD.
