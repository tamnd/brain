# IMO 1972 LL MON24

**Origin:** MON

## Problem

The diagonals of a convex 18-gon are colored in 5 diﬀerent
colors, each color appearing on an equal number of diagonals. The diag-
onals of one color are numbered 1, 2, . . .. One randomly chooses one-ﬁfth
of all the diagonals. Find the number of possibilities for which among the
chosen diagonals there exist exactly n pairs of diagonals of the same color
and with ﬁxed indices i, j.
