# IMO 1972 LL CZS9

**Origin:** CZS

## Problem

Given natural numbers k and n, k \leqn, n \geq3, ﬁnd the set
of all values in the interval (0, \pi) that the kth-largest among the interior
angles of a convex ngon can take.
