# IMO 1972 LL USS46

**Origin:** USS

## Problem

Numbers 1, 2, . . . , 16 are written in a 4\times4 square matrix so that
the sum of the numbers in every row, every column, and every diagonal
is the same and furthermore that the numbers 1 and 16 lie in opposite
corners. Prove that the sum of any two numbers symmetric with respect
to the center of the square equals 17.
