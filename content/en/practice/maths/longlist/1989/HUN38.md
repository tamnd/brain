# IMO 1989 LL HUN38

**Origin:** HUN

## Problem

Connecting the vertices of a regular n-gon we obtain a closed
(not necessarily convex) n-gon. Show that if n is even, then there are two
parallel segments among the connecting segments and if n is odd then
there cannot be exactly two parallel segments.
