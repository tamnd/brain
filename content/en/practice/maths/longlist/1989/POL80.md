# IMO 1989 LL POL80

**Origin:** POL

## Problem

We are given a ﬁnite collection of segments in the plane, of
total length 1. Prove that there exists a line ℓsuch that the sum of the
lengths of the projections of the given segments to the line ℓis less than
2/\pi.
