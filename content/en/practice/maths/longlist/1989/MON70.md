# IMO 1989 LL MON70

**Origin:** MON

## Problem

Three mutually nonparallel lines li (i = 1, 2, 3) are given
in a plane. The lines li determine a triangle and reﬂections fi with axes
on lines li. Prove that for every point of the plane, there exists a ﬁnite
composition of the reﬂections fi that maps that point to a point interior
to the triangle.
