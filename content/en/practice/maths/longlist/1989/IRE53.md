# IMO 1989 LL IRE53

**Origin:** IRE

## Problem

Let f(x) = (x −a1)(x −a2) \cdot \cdot \cdot (x −an) −2, where n \geq3
and a1, a2, . . . , an are distinct integers. Suppose that f(x) = g(x)h(x),
where g(x), h(x) are both nonconstant polynomials with integer coeﬃ-
cients. Prove that n = 3.
