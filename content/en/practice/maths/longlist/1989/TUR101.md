# IMO 1989 LL TUR101

**Origin:** TUR

## Problem

Let ABC be an equilateral triangle and \Gamma the semicircle
drawn exteriorly to the triangle, having BC as diameter. Show that if a
line passing through A trisects BC, it also trisects the arc \Gamma.
