# IMO 1989 LL TUR102

**Origin:** TUR

## Problem

If in a convex quadrilateral ABCD, E and F are the midpoints
of the sides BC and DA respectively. Show that the sum of the areas of
the triangles EDA and FBC is equal to the area of the quadrangle.
