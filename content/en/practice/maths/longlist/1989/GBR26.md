# IMO 1989 LL GBR26

**Origin:** GBR

## Problem

Let a, b, c, d be positive integers such that ab = cd and a + b =
c −d.
Prove that there exists a right-angled triangle the measures of whose sides
(in some unit) are integers and whose area measure is ab square units.
