# IMO 1989 LL POR85

**Origin:** POR

## Problem

Let P(x) be a polynomial with integer coeﬃcients such that
P(m1) = P(m2) = P(m3) = P(m4) = 7 for given distinct integers
m1, m2, m3, and m4. Show that there is no integer m such that P(m) = 14.
