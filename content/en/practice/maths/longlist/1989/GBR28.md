# IMO 1989 LL GBR28

**Origin:** GBR

## Problem

Let b1, b2, . . . , b1989 be positive real numbers such that the
equations
xr−1 −2xr + xr+1 + brxr = 0
(1 \leqr \leq1989)
have a solution with x0 = x1990 = 0 but not all of x1, . . . , x1989 are equal
to zero. Prove that
b1 + b2 + \cdot \cdot \cdot + b1989 \geq
995.
