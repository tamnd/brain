# IMO 1989 LL COL9

**Origin:** COL

## Problem

Let m be a positive integer and deﬁne f(m) to be the number
of factors of 2 in m! (that is, the greatest positive integer k such that
2k | m!). Prove that there are inﬁnitely many positive integers m such
that m −f(m) = 1989.
