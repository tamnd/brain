# IMO 1989 LL IRE54

**Origin:** IRE

## Problem

Let f be a function from the real numbers to the real numbers
such that f(1) = 1, f(a+b) = f(a)+f(b) for all a, b, and f(x)f(1/x) = 1
for all x ̸= 0.
Prove that f(x) = x for all real numbers x.
