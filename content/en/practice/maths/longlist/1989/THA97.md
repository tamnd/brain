# IMO 1989 LL THA97

**Origin:** THA

## Problem

Let n be a positive integer, X = {1, 2, . . ., n}, and k a positive
integer such that n/2 \leqk \leqn. Determine, with proof, the number of all
functions f : X \toX that satisfy the following conditions:
(i) f 2 = f;
(ii) the number of elements in the image of f is k;
(iii) for each y in the image of f, the number of all points x in X such that
f(x)=y is at most 2.
