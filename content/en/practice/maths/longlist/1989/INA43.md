# IMO 1989 LL INA43

**Origin:** INA

## Problem

Let f(x) = a sin2 x+b sin x+c, where a, b, and c are real num-
bers. Find all values of a, b, and c such that the following three conditions
are satisﬁed simultaneously:
(i) f(x) = 381 if sin x = 1/2.
(ii) The absolute maximum of f(x) is 444.
(iii) The absolute minimum of f(x) is 364.
