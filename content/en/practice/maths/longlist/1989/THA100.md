# IMO 1989 LL THA100

**Origin:** THA

## Problem

Let A be an n\timesn matrix whose elements are nonnegative real
numbers. Assume that A is a nonsingular matrix and all elements of A−1
are nonnegative real numbers. Prove that every row and every column of
A has exactly one nonzero element.
