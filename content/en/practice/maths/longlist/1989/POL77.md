# IMO 1989 LL POL77

**Origin:** POL

## Problem

Given that
cos x + cos y + cos z
cos(x + y + z)
= sin x + sin y + sin z
sin(x + y + z)
= a,
show that
cos(y + z) + cos(z + x) + cos(x + y) = a.
