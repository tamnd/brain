# IMO 1989 LL VIE107

**Origin:** VIE

## Problem

Let E be the set of all triangles whose only points with integer
coordinates (in the Cartesian coordinate system in space), in its interior
or on its sides, are its three vertices, and let f be the function of area of
a triangle. Determine the set of values f(E) of f.
