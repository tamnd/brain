# IMO 1989 LL INA48

**Origin:** INA

## Problem

Let S be the point of intersection of the two lines l1 : 7x−5y +
8 = 0 and l2 : 3x + 4y −13 = 0. Let P = (3, 7), Q = (11, 13), and let A
and B be points on the line PQ such that P is between A and Q, and B
is between P and Q, and such that PA/AQ = PB/BQ = 2/3. Without
ﬁnding the coordinates of B ﬁnd the equations of the lines SA and SB.
