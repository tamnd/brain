# IMO 1989 LL KOR61

**Origin:** KOR

## Problem

Let A be a set of positive integers such that no positive integer
greater than 1 divides all the elements of A. Prove that any suﬃciently
large positive integer can be written as a sum of elements of A. (Elements
may occur several times in the sum.)
