# IMO 1989 LL CUB12

**Origin:** CUB

## Problem

Let P(x) be a polynomial such that the following inequalities
are satisﬁed:
P(0) > 0;
P(1) > P(0);
P(2) > 2P(1) −P(0);
P(3) > 3P(2) −3P(1) + P(0);
and also for every natural number n, P(n + 4) > 4P(n + 3) −6P(n + 2) +
4P(n + 1) −P(n). Prove that for every positive natural number n, P(n)
is positive.
