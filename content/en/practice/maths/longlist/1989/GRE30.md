# IMO 1989 LL GRE30

**Origin:** GRE

## Problem

a point M on the inscribed circle and the projections D, E, F of M on
the sides BC, AC, and AB respectively. Let S, S1 denote the areas of the
triangles ABC and DEF respectively. Find the maximum and minimum
values of the quotient
S
S1 (here r denotes the inradius of ABC and, as
usual, a = BC, b = AC, c = AB).
