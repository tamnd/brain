# IMO 1978 LL CZS8

**Origin:** CZS

## Problem

For two given triangles A1A2A3 and B1B2B3 with areas ∆A
and ∆B, respectively, AiAk \geqBiBk, i, k = 1, 2, 3. Prove that ∆A \geq∆B
if the triangle A1A2A3 is not obtuse-angled.
