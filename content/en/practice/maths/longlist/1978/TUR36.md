# IMO 1978 LL TUR36

**Origin:** TUR

## Problem

The integers 1 through 1000 are located on the circumference
of a circle in natural order. Starting with 1, every ﬁfteenth number (i.e.,
1, 16, 31, . . .) is marked. The marking is continued until an already marked
number is reached. How many of the numbers will be left unmarked?
