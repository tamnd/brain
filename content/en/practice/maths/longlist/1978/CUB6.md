# IMO 1978 LL CUB6

**Origin:** CUB

## Problem

Prove that for all X > 1 there exists a triangle whose sides
have lengths P1(X) = X4+X3+2X2+X+1, P2(X) = 2X3+X2+2X+1,
and P3(X) = X4−1. Prove that all these triangles have the same greatest
angle and calculate it.
