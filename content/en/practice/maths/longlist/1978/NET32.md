# IMO 1978 LL NET32

**Origin:** NET

## Problem

Let C be the circumcircle of the square with vertices (0, 0),
(0, 1978), (1978, 0), (1978, 1978) in the Cartesian plane. Prove that C con-
tains no other point for which both coordinates are integers.
