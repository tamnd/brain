# IMO 1978 LL FIN13

**Origin:** FIN

## Problem

The satellites A and B circle the Earth in the equatorial plane
at altitude h. They are separated by distance 2r, where r is the radius
of the Earth. For which h can they be seen in mutually perpendicular
directions from some point on the equator?
