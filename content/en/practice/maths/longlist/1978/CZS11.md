# IMO 1978 LL CZS11

**Origin:** CZS

## Problem

Find all natural numbers n < 1978 with the following property:
If m is a natural number, 1 < m < n, and (m, n) = 1 (i.e., m and n
are relatively prime), then m is a prime number.
