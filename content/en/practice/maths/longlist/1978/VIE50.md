# IMO 1978 LL VIE50

**Origin:** VIE

## Problem

A variable tetrahedron ABCD has the following properties:
Its edge lengths can change as well as its vertices, but the opposite edges
remain equal (BC = DA, CA = DB, AB = DC); and the vertices A, B, C
lie respectively on three ﬁxed spheres with the same center P and radii
3, 4, 12. What is the maximal length of PD?
