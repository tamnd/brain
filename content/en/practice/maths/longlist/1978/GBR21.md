# IMO 1978 LL GBR21

**Origin:** GBR

## Problem

A circle touches the sides AB, BC, CD, DA of a square at
points K, L, M, N respectively, and BU, KV are parallel lines such that
U is on DM and V on DN. Prove that UV touches the circle.
