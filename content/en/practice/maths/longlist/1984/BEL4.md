# IMO 1984 LL BEL4

**Origin:** BEL

## Problem

Given a triangle ABC, three equilateral triangles AEB, BFC,
and CGA are constructed in the exterior of ABC. Prove that:
(a) CE = AF = BG;
(b) CE, AF, and BG have a common point.
