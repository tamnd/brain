# IMO 1984 LL POL45

**Origin:** POL

## Problem

Let X be an arbitrary nonempty set contained in the plane and
let sets A1, A2, . . . , Am and B1, B2, . . . , Bn be its images under parallel
translations. Let us suppose that
A1 \cupA2 \cup\cdot \cdot \cdot \cupAm \subsetB1 \cupB2 \cup\cdot \cdot \cdot \cupBn
and that the sets A1, A2, . . . , Am are disjoint. Prove that m \leqn.
