# IMO 1984 LL NET41

**Origin:** NET

## Problem

Determine positive integers p, q, and r such that the diagonal
of a block consisting of p\timesq \timesr unit cubes passes through exactly 1984 of
the unit cubes, while its length is minimal. (The diagonal is said to pass
through a unit cube if it has more than one point in common with the
unit cube.)
