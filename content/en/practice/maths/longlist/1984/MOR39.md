# IMO 1984 LL MOR39

**Origin:** MOR

## Problem

Let ABC be an isosceles triangle, AB = AC, \angleA = 20◦. Let
D be a point on AB, and E a point on AC such that \angleACD = 20◦and
\angleABE = 30◦. What is the measure of the angle \angleCDE?
