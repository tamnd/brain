# IMO 1984 LL ROM48

**Origin:** ROM

## Problem

Let ABC be a triangle with interior angle bisectors AA1,
BB1, CC1 and incenter I. If \sigma[IA1B] + \sigma[IB1C] + \sigma[IC1A] = 1
2\sigma[ABC],
where \sigma[ABC] denotes the area of ABC, show that ABC is isosceles.
