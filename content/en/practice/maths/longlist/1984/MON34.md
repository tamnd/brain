# IMO 1984 LL MON34

**Origin:** MON

## Problem

One country has n cities and every two of them are linked by a
railroad. A railway worker should travel by train exactly once through the
entire railroad system (reaching each city exactly once). If it is impossible
for worker to travel by train between two cities, he can travel by plane.
What is the minimal number of ﬂights that the worker will have to use?
