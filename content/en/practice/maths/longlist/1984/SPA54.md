# IMO 1984 LL SPA54

**Origin:** SPA

## Problem

Let P be a convex planar polygon with equal angles. Let
l1, . . . , ln be its sides. Show that a necessary and suﬃcient condition for
P to be regular is that the sum of the ratios
li
li+1 (i = 1, . . . , n; ln+1 = l1)
equals the number of sides.
