# IMO 1984 LL SPA55

**Origin:** SPA

## Problem

Let a, b, c be natural numbers such that a+b+c = 2pq(p30+q30),
p > q being two given positive integers.
(a) Prove that k = a3 + b3 + c3 is not a prime number.
(b) Prove that if a \cdot b \cdot c is maximum, then 1984 divides k.
