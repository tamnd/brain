# IMO 1984 LL USS65

**Origin:** USS

## Problem

A tetrahedron is inscribed in a sphere of radius 1 such that the
center of the sphere is inside the tetrahedron.
Prove that the sum of lengths of all edges of the tetrahedron is greater
than 6.
