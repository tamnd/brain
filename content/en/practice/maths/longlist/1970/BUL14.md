# IMO 1970 LL BUL14

**Origin:** BUL

## Problem

sin 2\alpha + sin 2\beta + sin 2\gamma = 2(sin \alpha + sin \beta + sin \gamma)(cos \alpha + cos \beta + cos \gamma)
−2(sin \alpha + sin \beta + sin \gamma).
