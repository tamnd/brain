# IMO 1970 LL USS57

**Origin:** USS

## Problem

Let the numbers 1, 2, . . . , n2 be written in the cells of an n \times n
square board so that the entries in each column are arranged increasingly.
What are the smallest and greatest possible sums of the numbers in the
kth row? (k a positive integer, 1 \leqk \leqn.)
