# IMO 1970 LL POL41

**Origin:** POL

## Problem

Let a cube of side 1 be given. Prove that there exists a point
A on the surface S of the cube such that every point of S can be joined
to A by a path on S of length not exceeding 2. Also prove that there is
a point of S that cannot be joined with A by a path on S of length less
than 2.
