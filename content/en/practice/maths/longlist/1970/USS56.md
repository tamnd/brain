# IMO 1970 LL USS56

**Origin:** USS

## Problem

A square hole of depth h whose base is of length a is given.
A dog is tied to the center of the square at the bottom of the hole by a
rope of length L >
\sqrt
2a2 + h2, and walks on the ground around the hole.
The edges of the hole are smooth, so that the rope can freely slide along
it. Find the shape and area of the territory accessible to the dog (whose
size is neglected).
