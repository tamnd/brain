# IMO 1970 LL NET34

**Origin:** NET

## Problem

In connection with a convex pentagon ABCDE we consider
the set of ten circles, each of which contains three of the vertices of the
pentagon on its circumference. Is it possible that none of these circles
contains the pentagon? Prove your answer.
