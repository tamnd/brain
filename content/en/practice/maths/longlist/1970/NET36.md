# IMO 1970 LL NET36

**Origin:** NET

## Problem

Let x, y, z be nonnegative real numbers satisfying
x2 + y2 + z2 = 5
and
yz + zx + xy = 2.
Which values can the greatest of the numbers x2 −yz, y2 −xz, z2 −xy
have?
