# IMO 1970 LL ROM46

**Origin:** ROM

## Problem

Given a triangle ABC and a plane \pi having no common points
with the triangle, ﬁnd a point M such that the triangle determined by
the points of intersection of the lines MA, MB, MC with \pi is congruent
to the triangle ABC.
