# IMO 1970 LL BEL7

**Origin:** BEL

## Problem

Let ABCD be any quadrilateral. A square is constructed on
each side of the quadrilateral, all in the same manner (i.e., outward or
inward). Denote the centers of the squares by M1, M2, M3, and M4.
Prove:
(a) M1M3 = M2M4;
(b) M1M3 is perpendicular to M2M4.
