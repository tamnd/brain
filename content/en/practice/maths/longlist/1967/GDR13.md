# IMO 1967 LL GDR13

**Origin:** GDR

## Problem

Find whether among all quadrilaterals whose interiors lie inside
a semicircle of radius r there exists one (or more) with maximal area. If
so, determine their shape and area.

## Solution

The maximum area is 3
\sqrt
3r2/4 (where r is the radius of the semicircle)
and is attained in the case of a trapezoid with two vertices at the endpoints
of the diameter of the semicircle and the other two vertices dividing the
semicircle into three equal arcs.
