# IMO 1988 LL INA41

**Origin:** INA

## Problem

(a) Let ABC be a triangle with AB = 12 and AC = 16. Suppose M is the
midpoint of side BC and points E and F are chosen on sides AC and
AB respectively, and suppose that the lines EF and AM intersect at
G. If AE = 2AF then ﬁnd the ratio EG/GF.
(b) Let E be a point external to a circle and suppose that two chords
EAB and ECD meet at an angle of 40◦. If AB = BC = CD, ﬁnd
the size of \angleACD.
