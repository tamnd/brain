# IMO 1988 LL GBR20

**Origin:** GBR

## Problem

It is proposed to partition the set of positive integers into two
disjoint subsets A and B subject to the following conditions:
(i) 1 is in A;
(ii) no two distinct members of A have a sum of the form 2k + 2 (k =
0, 1, 2, . . .); and
(iii) no two distinct members of B have a sum of that form.
Show that this partitioning can be carried out in a unique manner and
determine the subsets to which 1987, 1988, and 1989 belong.
