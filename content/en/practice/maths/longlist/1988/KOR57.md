# IMO 1988 LL KOR57

**Origin:** KOR

## Problem

Let C be a cube with edges of length 2. Construct a solid with
fourteen faces by cutting oﬀall eight corners of C, keeping the new faces
perpendicular to the diagonals of the cube and keeping the newly formed
faces identical. If at the conclusion of this process the fourteen faces so
formed have the same area, ﬁnd the area of each face of the new solid.
