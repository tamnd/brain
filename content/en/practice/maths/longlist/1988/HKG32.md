# IMO 1988 LL HKG32

**Origin:** HKG

## Problem

Assuming that the roots of x3+px2+qx+r = 0 are all real and
positive, ﬁnd a relation between p, q, and r that gives a necessary condition
for the roots to be exactly the cosines of three angles of a triangle.
