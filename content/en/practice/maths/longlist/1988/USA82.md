# IMO 1988 LL USA82

**Origin:** USA

## Problem

The triangle ABC has a right angle at C. The point P is
located on segment AC such that triangles PBA and PBC have congruent
inscribed circles. Express the length x = PC in terms of a = BC, b = CA,
and c = AB.
