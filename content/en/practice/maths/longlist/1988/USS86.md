# IMO 1988 LL USS86

**Origin:** USS

## Problem

Let a, b, c be integers diﬀerent from zero. It is known that the
equation ax2 + by2 + cz2 = 0 has a solution (x, y, z) in integers diﬀerent
from the solution x = y = z = 0. Prove that the equation ax2+by2+cz2 =
1 has a solution in rational numbers.
