# IMO 1988 LL FRA13

**Origin:** FRA

## Problem

Let T be a triangle with inscribed circle C. A square with sides
of length a is circumscribed about the same circle C. Show that the total
length of the parts of the edges of the square interior to the triangle T is
at least 2a.
