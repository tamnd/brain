# IMO 1988 LL HKG35

**Origin:** HKG

## Problem

In the triangle ABC, let D, E, and F be the midpoints of the
three sides, X, Y , and Z the feet of the three altitudes, H the orthocenter,
and P, Q, and R the midpoints of the line segments joining H to the three
vertices. Show that the nine points D, E, F, P, Q, R, X, Y, Z lie on a circle.
