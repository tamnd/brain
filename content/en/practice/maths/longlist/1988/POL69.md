# IMO 1988 LL POL69

**Origin:** POL

## Problem

For a convex polygon P in the plane let P ′ denote the convex
polygon with vertices at the midpoints of the sides of P. Given an integer
n \geq3, determine sharp bounds for the ratio area(P ′)
area(P) over all convex
n-gons P.
