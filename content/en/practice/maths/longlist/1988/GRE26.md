# IMO 1988 LL GRE26

**Origin:** GRE

## Problem

Let AB and CD be two perpendicular chords of a circle with
center O and radius r, and let X, Y, Z, W denote in cyclical order the
four parts into which the disk is thus divided. Find the maximum and
minimum of the quantity
A(Z)
A(Y )+A(W), where A(U) denotes the area of U.
