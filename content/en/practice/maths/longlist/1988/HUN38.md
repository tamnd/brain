# IMO 1988 LL HUN38

**Origin:** HUN

## Problem

In a multiple choice test there were 4 questions and 3 possible
answers for each question. A group of students was tested and it turned
out that for any 3 of them there was a question that the three students
answered diﬀerently. What is the maximal possible number of students
tested?
