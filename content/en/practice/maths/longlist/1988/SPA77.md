# IMO 1988 LL SPA77

**Origin:** SPA

## Problem

Consider h + 1 chessboards. Number the squares of each board
from 1 to 64 in such a way that when the perimeters of any two boards
of the collection are brought into coincidence in any possible manner, no
two squares in the same position have the same number. What is the
maximum value of h?
