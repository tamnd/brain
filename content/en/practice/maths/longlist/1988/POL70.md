# IMO 1988 LL POL70

**Origin:** POL

## Problem

In 3-dimensional space a point O is given and a ﬁnite set A
of segments with the sum of the lengths equal to 1988. Prove that there
exists a plane disjoint from A such that the distance from it to O does
not exceed 574.
