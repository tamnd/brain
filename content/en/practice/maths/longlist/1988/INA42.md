# IMO 1988 LL INA42

**Origin:** INA

## Problem

(a) Four balls of radius 1 are mutually tangent, three resting an the ﬂoor
and the fourth resting on the others. A tetrahedron, each of whose
edges has length s, is circumscribed around the balls. Find the value
of s.
(b) Suppose that ABCD and EFGH are opposite faces of a rectangu-
lar solid, with \angleDHC = 45◦and \angleFHB = 60◦. Find the cosine of
\angleBHD.
