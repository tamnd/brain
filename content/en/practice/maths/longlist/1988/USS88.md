# IMO 1988 LL USS88

**Origin:** USS

## Problem

There are six circles inside a ﬁxed circle, each tangent to
the ﬁxed circle and tangent to the two adjacent smaller circles. If the
points of contact between the six circles and the larger circle are, in order,
A1, A2, A3, A4, A5, and A6, prove that
A1A2 \cdot A3A4 \cdot A5A6 = A2A3 \cdot A4A5 \cdot A6A1.
