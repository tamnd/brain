# IMO 1985 LL GBR31

**Origin:** GBR

## Problem

Let E1, E2, and E3 be three mutually intersecting ellipses, all
in the same plane. Their foci are respectively F2, F3; F3, F1; and F1, F2.
The three foci are not on a straight line. Prove that the common chords
of each pair of ellipses are concurrent.
