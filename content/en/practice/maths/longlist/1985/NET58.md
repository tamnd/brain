# IMO 1985 LL NET58

**Origin:** NET

## Problem

Prove that there are inﬁnitely many pairs (k, N) of positive
integers such that 1 + 2 + \cdot \cdot \cdot + k = (k + 1) + (k + 2) + \cdot \cdot \cdot + N.
