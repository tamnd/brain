# IMO 1985 LL FRG28

**Origin:** FRG

## Problem

Let M be the set of the lengths of an octahedron whose sides
are congruent quadrangles. Prove that M has at most three elements.
(FRG 1a) Let an octahedron whose sides are congruent quadrangles be
given. Prove that each of these quadrangles has two equal sides meeting
at a common vertex.
