# IMO 1985 LL IRE39

**Origin:** IRE

## Problem

Given a triangle ABC and external points X, Y , and Z such
that ∡BAZ = ∡CAY , ∡CBX = ∡ABZ, and ∡ACY = ∡BCX, prove
that AX, BY , and CZ are concurrent.
