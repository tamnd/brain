# IMO 1985 LL ITA48

**Origin:** ITA

## Problem

In a given country, all inhabitants are knights or knaves. A
knight never lies; a knave always lies. We meet three persons, A, B, and
C. Person A says, “If C is a knight, B is a knave.” Person C says, “A
and I are diﬀerent; one is a knight and the other is a knave.” Who are the
knights, and who are the knaves?
