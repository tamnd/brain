# IMO 1985 LL ISR43

**Origin:** ISR

## Problem

Suppose that 1985 points are given inside a unit cube. Show
that one can always choose 32 of them in such a way that every (possibly
degenerate) closed polygon with these points as vertices has a total length
of less than 8
\sqrt
3.
