## Problem

A “large” circular disk is attached to a vertical wall. It rotates
clockwise with one revolution per minute. An insect lands on the disk and
immediately starts to climb vertically upward with constant speed \pi
3 cm
per second (relative to the disk). Describe the path of the insect
(a) relative to the disk;
(b) relative to the wall.
