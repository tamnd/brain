# IMO 1985 LL BRA8

**Origin:** BRA

## Problem

Let K be a convex set in the xy-plane, symmetric with respect
to the origin and having area greater than 4. Prove that there exists a point
(m, n) ̸= (0, 0) in K such that m and n are integers.
