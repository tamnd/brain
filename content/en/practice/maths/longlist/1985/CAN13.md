# IMO 1985 LL CAN13

**Origin:** CAN

## Problem

Find the average of the quantity
(a1 −a2)2 + (a2 −a3)2 + \cdot \cdot \cdot + (an−1 −an)2
taken over all permutations (a1, a2, . . . , an) of (1, 2, . . . , n).
