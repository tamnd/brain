# IMO 1985 LL SPA75

**Origin:** SPA

## Problem

Let ABCD be a rectangle, AB = a, BC = b. Consider the
family of parallel and equidistant straight lines (the distance between two
consecutive lines being d) that are at an the angle \varphi, 0 \leq\varphi \leq90◦,
with respect to AB. Let L be the sum of the lengths of all the segments
intersecting the rectangle. Find:
(a) how L varies,
(b) a necessary and suﬃcient condition for L to be a constant, and
(c) the value of this constant.
