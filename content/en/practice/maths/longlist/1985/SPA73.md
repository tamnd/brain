# IMO 1985 LL SPA73

**Origin:** SPA

## Problem

Let A1A2, B1B2, C1C2 be three equal segments on the three
sides of an equilateral triangle. Prove that in the triangle formed by the
lines B2C1, C2A1, A2B1, the segments B2C1, C2A1, A2B1 are proportional
to the sides in which they are contained.
