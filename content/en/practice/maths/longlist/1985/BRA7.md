# IMO 1985 LL BRA7

**Origin:** BRA

## Problem

A convex quadrilateral is inscribed in a circle of radius 1. Prove
that the diﬀerence between its perimeter and the sum of the lengths of its
diagonals is greater than zero and less than 2.
