# IMO 1985 LL AUS2

**Origin:** AUS

## Problem

We are given a triangle ABC and three rectangles R1, R2, R3
with sides parallel to two ﬁxed perpendicular directions and such that their
union covers the sides AB, BC, and CA; i.e., each point on the perimeter
of ABC is contained in or on at least one of the rectangles. Prove that all
points inside the triangle are also covered by the union of R1, R2, R3.
