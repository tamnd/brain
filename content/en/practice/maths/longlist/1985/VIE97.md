# IMO 1985 LL VIE97

**Origin:** VIE

## Problem

In a plane a circle with radius R and center w and a line \Lambda
are given. The distance between w and \Lambda is d, d > R. The points M and
N are chosen on \Lambda in such a way that the circle with diameter MN is
externally tangent to the given circle. Show that there exists a point A
in the plane such that all the segments MN are seen in a constant angle
from A.
