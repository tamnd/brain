# IMO 1985 LL NOR61

**Origin:** NOR

## Problem

Consider the set A = {0, 1, 2, . . ., 9} and let (B1, B2, . . . , Bk)
be a collection of nonempty subsets of A such that Bi \capBj has at most
two elements for i ̸= j. What is the maximal value of k?
