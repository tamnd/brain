# IMO 1985 LL MON50

**Origin:** MON

## Problem

From each of the vertices of a regular n-gon a car starts to
move with constant speed along the perimeter of the n-gon in the same
direction. Prove that if all the cars end up at a vertex A at the same time,
then they never again meet at any other vertex of the n-gon. Can they
meet again at A?
