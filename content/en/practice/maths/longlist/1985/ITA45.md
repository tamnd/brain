# IMO 1985 LL ITA45

**Origin:** ITA

## Problem

Two persons, X and Y , play with a die. X wins a game if the
outcome is 1 or 2; Y wins in the other cases. A player wins a match if he
wins two consecutive games. For each player determine the probability of
winning a match within 5 games. Determine the probabilities of winning
in an unlimited number of games. If X bets 1, how much must Y bet for
the game to be fair?
