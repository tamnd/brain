# IMO 1985 LL GDR35

**Origin:** GDR

## Problem

We call a coloring f of the elements in the set M = {(x, y) |
x = 0, 1, . . . , kn −1; y = 0, 1, . . ., ln −1} with n colors allowable if every
color appears exactly k and l times in each row and column and there are
no rectangles with sides parallel to the coordinate axes such that all the
vertices in M have the same color. Prove that every allowable coloring f
satisﬁes kl \leqn(n + 1).
