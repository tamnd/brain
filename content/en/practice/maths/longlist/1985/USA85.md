# IMO 1985 LL USA85

**Origin:** USA

## Problem

Let CD be a diameter of circle K. Let AB be a chord that is
parallel to CD. The line segment AE, with E on K, is parallel to CB; F
is the point of intersection of line segments AB and DE. The line segment
FG, with G on DC, extended is parallel to CB. Is GA tangent to K at
point A?
