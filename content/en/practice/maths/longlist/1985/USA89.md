# IMO 1985 LL USA89

**Origin:** USA

## Problem

Given that n elements a1, a2, . . . , an are organized into n pairs
P1, P2, . . . , Pn in such a way that two pairs Pi, Pj share exactly one el-
ement when (ai, aj) is one of the pairs, prove that every element is in
exactly two of the pairs.
