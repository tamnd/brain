# IMO 1985 LL MOR56

**Origin:** MOR

## Problem

Let ABCD be a rhombus with angle \angleA = 60◦. Let E be a
point, diﬀerent from D, on the line AD. The lines CE and AB intersect
at F. The lines DF and BE intersect at M. Determine the angle ∡BMD
as a function of the position of E on AD.
