# IMO 1985 LL ITA46

**Origin:** ITA

## Problem

Let C be the curve determined by the equation y = x3 in the
rectangular coordinate system. Let t be the tangent to C at a point P of
C; t intersects C at another point Q. Find the equation of the set L of the
midpoints M of PQ as P describes C. Is the correspondence associating
P and M a bijection of C on L? Find a similarity that transforms C into
L.
