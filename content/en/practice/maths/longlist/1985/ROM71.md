# IMO 1985 LL ROM71

**Origin:** ROM

## Problem

For every integer r > 1 ﬁnd the smallest integer h(r) > 1
having the following property: For any partition of the set {1, 2, . . ., h(r)}
into r classes, there exist integers a \geq0, 1 \leqx \leqy such that the numbers
a + x, a + y, a + x + y are contained in the same class of the partition.
