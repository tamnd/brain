# IMO 1985 LL ROM67

**Origin:** ROM

## Problem

Let k \geq2 and n1, n2, . . . , nk \geq1 natural numbers having the
property n2 | 2n1 −1, n3 | 2n2 −1, . . . , nk | 2nk−1 −1, and n1 | 2nk −1.
Show that n1 = n2 = \cdot \cdot \cdot = nk = 1.
