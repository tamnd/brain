# IMO 1985 LL TUR81

**Origin:** TUR

## Problem

Given the side a and the corresponding altitude ha of a triangle
ABC, ﬁnd a relation between a and ha such that it is possible to construct,
with straightedge and compass, triangle ABC such that the altitudes of
ABC form a right triangle admitting ha as hypotenuse.
