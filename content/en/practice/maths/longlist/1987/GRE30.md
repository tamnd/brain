# IMO 1987 LL GRE30

**Origin:** GRE

## Problem

Consider the regular 1987-gon A1A2 . . . A1987 with center O.
Show that the sum of vectors belonging to any proper subset of M =
{OAj | j = 1, 2, . . . , 1987} is nonzero.
