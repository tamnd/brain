# IMO 1987 LL MOR45

**Origin:** MOR

## Problem

Let us consider a variable polygon with 2n sides (n \inN) in a
ﬁxed circle such that 2n −1 of its sides pass through 2n −1 ﬁxed points
lying on a straight line ∆. Prove that the last side also passes through a
ﬁxed point lying on ∆.
