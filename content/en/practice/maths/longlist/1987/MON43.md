# IMO 1987 LL MON43

**Origin:** MON

## Problem

Let 2n + 3 points be given in the plane in such a way that
no three lie on a line and no four lie on a circle. Prove that the number
of circles that pass through three of these points and contain exactly n
interior points is not less than 1
