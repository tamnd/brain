# IMO 1987 LL TUR62

**Origin:** TUR

## Problem

Let l, l′ be two lines in 3-space and let A, B, C be three points
taken on l with B as midpoint of the segment AC. If a, b, c are the distances
of A, B, C from l′, respectively, show that b \leq
a2+c2
, equality holding
if l, l′ are parallel.
