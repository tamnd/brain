# IMO 1987 LL AUS4

**Origin:** AUS

## Problem

Let a1, a2, a3, b1, b2, b3 be positive real numbers. Prove that
(a1b2 + a2b1 + a1b3 + a3b1 + a2b3 + a3b2)2
\geq4(a1a2 + a2a3 + a3a1)(b1b2 + b2b3 + b3b1)
and show that the two sides of the inequality are equal if and only if
a1/b1 = a2/b2 = a3/b3.
