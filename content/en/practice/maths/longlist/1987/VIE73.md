# IMO 1987 LL VIE73

**Origin:** VIE

## Problem

Let f(x) be a periodic function of period T > 0 deﬁned over R.
Its ﬁrst derivative is continuous on R. Prove that there exist x, y \in[0, T )
such that x ̸= y and
f(x)f ′(y) = f(y)f ′(x).
