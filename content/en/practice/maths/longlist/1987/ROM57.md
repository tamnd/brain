# IMO 1987 LL ROM57

**Origin:** ROM

## Problem

The bisectors of the angles B, C of a triangle ABC intersect
the opposite sides in B′, C′ respectively. Prove that the straight line B′C′
intersects the inscribed circle in two diﬀerent points.
