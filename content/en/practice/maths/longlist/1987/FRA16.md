# IMO 1987 LL FRA16

**Origin:** FRA

## Problem

Let ABC be a triangle. For every point M belonging to segment
BC we denote by B′ and c′ the orthogonal projections of M on the straight
lines AC and BC. Find points M for which the length of segment B′C′
is a minimum.
