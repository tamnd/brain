# IMO 1987 LL VIE76

**Origin:** VIE

## Problem

Given two sequences of positive numbers {ak} and {bk} (k \inN)
such that
(i) ak < bk,
(ii) cos akx + cos bkx \geq−1
k for all k \inN and x \inR,
prove the existence of limk\to\inftyak
bk and ﬁnd this limit.
