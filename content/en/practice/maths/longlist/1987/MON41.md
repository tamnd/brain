# IMO 1987 LL MON41

**Origin:** MON

## Problem

Let n points be given arbitrarily in the plane, no three of
them collinear. Let us draw segments between pairs of these points. What
is the minimum number of segments that can be colored red in such a way
that among any four points, three of them are connected by segments that
form a red triangle?
