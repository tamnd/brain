# IMO 1987 LL FIN13

**Origin:** FIN

## Problem

A be an inﬁnite set of positive integers such that every n \inA is
the product of at most 1987 prime numbers. Prove that there is an inﬁnite
set B \subsetA and a number p such that the greatest common divisor of any
two distinct numbers in B is b.
