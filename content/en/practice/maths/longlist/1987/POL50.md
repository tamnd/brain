# IMO 1987 LL POL50

**Origin:** POL

## Problem

Let P, Q, R be polynomials with real coeﬃcients, satisfying
P 4+Q4 = R2. Prove that there exist real numbers p, q, r and a polynomial
S such that P = pS, Q = qS and R = rS2.
Variants: (1) P 4 + Q4 = R4; (2) gcd(P, Q) = 1; (3) \pmP 4 + Q4 = R2 or
R4.
