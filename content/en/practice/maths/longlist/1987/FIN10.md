# IMO 1987 LL FIN10

**Origin:** FIN

## Problem

In a Cartesian coordinate system, the circle C1 has center
O1(−2, 0) and radius 3. Denote the point (1, 0) by A and the origin by O.
Prove that there is a constant c > 0 such that for every X that is exterior
to C1,
OX −1 \geqc min{AX, AX2}.
Find the largest possible c.
