## Problem

On the plane, two points $A$ and $B$ are given. Find the locus of the third vertices $C$ of a triangle $ABC$ such that:

1. the altitude $AA'$ is equal to the side $BC$;
2. the median $AA_1$ is equal to the side $AC$;
3. the median $AA_1$ is equal to the side $BC$;
4. the altitude $CC'$ is equal to the median $BB_1$;
5. the altitude $BB'$ is equal to the median $CC_1$.

M. A. Kvantov
