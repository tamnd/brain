**Category:** Geometry

## Problem

The vertices X, Y , Z of an equilateral triangle XY Z lie respectively on the sides BC,
CA, AB of an acute-angled triangle ABC. Prove that the incenter of triangle ABC lies inside
triangle XY Z.
