**Category:** Geometry

## Problem

The incircle ω of acute-angled scalene triangle ABC has centre I and meets sides BC,
CA, and AB at D, E, and F, respectively. The line through D perpendicular to EF meets ω
again at R. Line AR meets ω again at P. The circumcircles of triangles PCE and PBF meet
again at Q ‰ P. Prove that lines DI and PQ meet on the external bisector of angle BAC.
(India)
