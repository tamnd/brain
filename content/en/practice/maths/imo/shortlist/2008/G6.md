**Category:** Geometry

## Problem

There is given a convex quadrilateral ABCD. Prove that there exists a point P inside
the quadrilateral such that
∠PAB + ∠PDC = ∠PBC + ∠PAD = ∠PCD + ∠PBA = ∠PDA + ∠PCB = 90◦
(1)
if and only if the diagonals AC and BD are perpendicular.
