**Category:** Geometry

## Problem

Let ABCD be a convex quadrilateral and let P and Q be points in ABCD such that
PQDA and QPBC are cyclic quadrilaterals. Suppose that there exists a point E on the line
segment PQ such that ∠PAE = ∠QDE and ∠PBE = ∠QCE. Show that the quadrilateral
ABCD is cyclic.
