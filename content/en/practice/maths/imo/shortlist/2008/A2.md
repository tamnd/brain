**Category:** Algebra

## Problem

(a) Prove the inequality
x2
(x − 1)2
+
y2
(y − 1)2
+
z2
(z − 1)2
≥ 1
for real numbers x,y,z 6= 1 satisfying the condition xyz = 1.
(b) Show that there are infinitely many triples of rational numbers x, y, z for which this
inequality turns into equality.
