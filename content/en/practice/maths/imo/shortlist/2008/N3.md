**Category:** Number Theory

## Problem

Let a0,a1,a2,... be a sequence of positive integers such that the greatest common divisor
of any two consecutive terms is greater than the preceding term; in symbols, gcd(ai,ai+1) > ai−1.
Prove that an ≥ 2n
for all n ≥ 0.
