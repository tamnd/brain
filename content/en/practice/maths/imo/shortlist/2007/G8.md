**Category:** Geometry

## Problem

Point P lies on side AB of a convex quadrilateral ABCD. Let ω be the incircle
of triangle CPD, and let I be its incenter. Suppose that ω is tangent to the incircles of
triangles APD and BPC at points K and L, respectively. Let lines AC and BD meet at E,
and let lines AK and BL meet at F. Prove that points E, I, and F are collinear.
(Poland)
