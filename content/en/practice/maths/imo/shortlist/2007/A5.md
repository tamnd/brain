## Problem

Let c > 2, and let a(1),a(2),... be a sequence of nonnegative real numbers such that
a(m + n) ≤ 2a(m) + 2a(n) for all m,n ≥ 1, (1)
and
a(2k
) ≤
(k + 1)c
for all k ≥ 0. (2)
Prove that the sequence a(n) is bounded.
(Croatia)
