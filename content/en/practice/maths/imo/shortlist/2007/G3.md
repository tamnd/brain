**Category:** Geometry

## Problem

The diagonals of a trapezoid ABCD intersect at point P. Point Q lies between the
parallel lines BC and AD such that ∠AQD = ∠CQB, and line CD separates points P and Q.
Prove that ∠BQP = ∠DAQ.
(Ukraine)
