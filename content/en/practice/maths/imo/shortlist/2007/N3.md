**Category:** Number Theory

## Problem

Let X be a set of 10000 integers, none of them is divisible by 47. Prove that there
exists a 2007-element subset Y of X such that a − b + c − d + e is not divisible by 47 for any
a,b,c,d,e ∈ Y .
(Netherlands)
