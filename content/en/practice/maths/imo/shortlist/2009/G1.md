## Problem

Let ABC be a triangle with AB = AC. The angle bisectors of A and B meet the sides BC
and AC in D and E, respectively. Let K be the incenter of triangle ADC. Suppose that
∠BEK = 45◦
. Find all possible values of ∠BAC.
