**Category:** Geometry

## Problem

Let ABCDE be a convex pentagon such that
∠BAC = ∠CAD = ∠DAE and ∠ABC = ∠ACD = ∠ADE.
The diagonals BD and CE meet at P. Prove that the line AP bisects the side CD.
(USA)
