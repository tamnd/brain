**Category:** Geometry

## Problem

Let ABC be a triangle with incentre I. A point P in the interior of the triangle satisfies
∠PBA + ∠PCA = ∠PBC + ∠PCB.
Show that AP ≥ AI and that equality holds if and only if P coincides with I.
(Korea)
