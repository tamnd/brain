[IMO 2006 Shortlist G10](https://www.imo-official.org/problems/IMO2006SL.pdf)

**Category:** Geometry

## Problem

To each side a of a convex polygon we assign the maximum area of a triangle contained
in the polygon and having a as one of its sides. Show that the sum of the areas assigned to all
sides of the polygon is not less than twice the area of the polygon.
(Serbia)
