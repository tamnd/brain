[IMO 2006 Shortlist G9](https://www.imo-official.org/problems/IMO2006SL.pdf)

**Category:** Geometry

## Problem

Points A1,B1,C1 are chosen on the sides BC, CA, AB of a triangle ABC, respectively.
The circumcircles of triangles AB1C1, BC1A1, CA1B1 intersect the circumcircle of triangle
ABC again at points A2,B2,C2, respectively (A2 6= A, B2 6= B, C2 6= C). Points A3,B3,C3 are
symmetric to A1,B1,C1 with respect to the midpoints of the sides BC, CA, AB respectively.
Prove that the triangles A2B2C2 and A3B3C3 are similar.
(Russia)
