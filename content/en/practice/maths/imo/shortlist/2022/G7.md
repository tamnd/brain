## Problem

Let ABC and A1
B1
C1
be two triangles having the same circumcircle ω, and the same
orthocentre H. Let Ω be the circumcircle of the triangle determined by the lines AA1
, BB1
and CC1
. Prove that H, the centre of ω, and the centre of Ω are collinear.
