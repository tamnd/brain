[IMO 2022 Shortlist N5](https://www.imo-official.org/problems/IMO2022SL.pdf)

**Category:** Number Theory

## Problem

For each 1 ď i ď 9 and T P N, define dipTq to be the total number of times the digit
i appears when all the multiples of 1829 between 1 and T inclusive are written out in base 10.
Show that there are infinitely many T P N such that there are precisely two distinct values
among d1pTq,d2pTq,...,d9pTq.
(United Kingdom)
