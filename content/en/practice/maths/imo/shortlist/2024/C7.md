[IMO 2024 Shortlist C7](https://www.imo-official.org/problems/IMO2024SL.pdf)

**Category:** Combinatorics

## Problem

Let N be a positive integer and let a1, a2, ... be an infinite sequence of positive
integers. Suppose that, for each n ą N, an is equal to the number of times an´1 appears in the
list a1, a2, ..., an´1.
Prove that at least one of the sequences a1, a3, a5, ... and a2, a4, a6, ... is eventually
periodic.
(Australia)
