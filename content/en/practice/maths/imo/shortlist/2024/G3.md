## Problem

that segment AB is tangent to the circumcircle of triangle CME at M and that D lies on the
circumcircles of triangles AME and BMC. Lines AD and ME intersect at K, and lines BD
and MC intersect at L. Points P and Q lie on line EC so that =PDC “ =EDQ “ =ADB.
Prove that lines KP, LQ, and MD are concurrent.
