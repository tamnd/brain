## Problem

Let ABC be a triangle with incentre I, and let Ω be the circumcircle of triangle BIC.
Let K be a point in the interior of segment BC such that =BAK ă =KAC. The angle bisector
of =BKA intersects Ω at points W and X such that A and W lie on the same side of BC, and
the angle bisector of =CKA intersects Ω at points Y and Z such that A and Y lie on the same
side of BC.
Prove that =WAY “ =ZAX.
