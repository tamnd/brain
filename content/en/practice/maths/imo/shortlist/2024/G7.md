[IMO 2024 Shortlist G7](https://www.imo-official.org/problems/IMO2024SL.pdf)

**Category:** Geometry

## Problem

Let ABC be a triangle with incentre I such that AB ă AC ă BC. The second
intersections of AI, BI, and CI with the circumcircle of triangle ABC are MA, MB, and MC,
respectively. Lines AI and BC intersect at D and lines BMC and CMB intersect at X. Suppose
the circumcircles of triangles XMBMC and XBC intersect again at S ‰ X. Lines BX and CX
intersect the circumcircle of triangle SXMA again at P ‰ X and Q ‰ X, respectively.
Prove that the circumcentre of triangle SID lies on PQ.
(Thailand)
