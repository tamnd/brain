**Category:** Combinatorics

## Problem

Let n be a positive integer. There are 2n knights sitting at a round table. They
consist of n pairs of partners, each pair of which wishes to shake hands. A pair can shake hands
only when next to each other. Every minute, one pair of adjacent knights swaps places.
Find the minimum number of exchanges of adjacent knights such that, regardless of the
initial arrangement, every knight can meet her partner and shake hands at some time.
(Belarus)
