## Problem

The points P and Q are chosen on the side BC of an acute-angled triangle ABC so
that =PAB “ =ACB and =QAC “ =CBA. The points M and N are taken on the rays AP
and AQ, respectively, so that AP “ PM and AQ “ QN. Prove that the lines BM and CN
intersect on the circumcircle of the triangle ABC.
(Georgia)
