**Category:** Number Theory

## Problem

Let τ(n) be the number of positive divisors of n. Let τ1(n) be the number of positive
divisors of n which have remainders 1 when divided by 3. Find all possible integral values of
the fraction τ(10n)
τ1(10n)
.
