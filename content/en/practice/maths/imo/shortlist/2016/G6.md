**Category:** Geometry

## Problem

Let ABCD be a convex quadrilateral with ∠ABC = ∠ADC < 90◦
. The internal
angle bisectors of ∠ABC and ∠ADC meet AC at E and F respectively, and meet each
other at point P. Let M be the midpoint of AC and let ω be the circumcircle of triangle
BPD. Segments BM and DM intersect ω again at X and Y respectively. Denote by Q the
intersection point of lines XE and Y F. Prove that PQ ⊥ AC.
