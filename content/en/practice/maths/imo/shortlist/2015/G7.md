[IMO 2015 Shortlist G7](https://www.imo-official.org/problems/IMO2015SL.pdf)

**Category:** Geometry

## Problem

Let ABCD be a convex quadrilateral, and let P, Q, R, and S be points on the sides
AB, BC, CD, and DA, respectively. Let the line segments PR and QS meet at O. Suppose
that each of the quadrilaterals APOS, BQOP, CROQ, and DSOR has an incircle. Prove that
the lines AC, PQ, and RS are either concurrent or parallel to each other.
(Bulgaria)
