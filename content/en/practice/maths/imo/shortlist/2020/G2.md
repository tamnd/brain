## Problem

Let ABCD be a convex quadrilateral. Suppose that P is a point in the interior of
ABCD such that
=PAD : =PBA : =DPA “ 1 : 2 : 3 “ =CBP : =BAP : =BPC.
The internal bisectors of angles ADP and PCB meet at a point Q inside the triangle ABP.
Prove that AQ “ BQ.
(Poland)
