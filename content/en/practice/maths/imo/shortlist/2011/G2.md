**Category:** Geometry

## Problem

Let A1A2A3A4 be a non-cyclic quadrilateral. Let O1 and r1 be the circumcenter and the
circumradius of the triangle A2A3A4. Define O2, O3, O4 and r2, r3, r4 in a similar way. Prove
that
O1A2
1 − r2
+
O2A2
2 − r2
+
O3A2
3 − r2
+
O4A2
4 − r2
= 0.
