## How to Use These Lessons

Each lesson contains a grammar explanation with full examples, a vocabulary table of 20–25 items, a 10–14 line dialogue illustrating the target structures in use, and three practice exercises with answer keys. Work through the lessons in order for best results, but experienced learners may jump to specific topics using the table above.

After completing all twelve lessons, proceed to the [B1 overview page](../) for reading and listening milestones, then move to [B2](../../b2/) for your next targets.
