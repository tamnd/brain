## What You Can Do at A2

At A2 you can:
- Discuss everyday topics: family, hobbies, work, daily routine, travel
- Narrate simple past events and plans for the future
- Handle most travel situations (hotels, restaurants, transport, shopping)
- Use the full correlatives table in context
- Form compound words using common affixes
- Read simple Esperanto texts (graded readers, simple articles)
- Write short descriptive texts and messages
- Understand Esperanto when spoken slowly and clearly

## Completing the Correlatives Table

A major A2 milestone is mastering all **45 correlatives** (the ki-, ti-, ƒâi-, neni-, i- table). See the [correlatives reference](../../grammar/correlatives/) for the full table.

Key correlatives to prioritize at A2:
- **iu/iom/iel** ‚Äî someone/some amount/somehow
- **ƒâiu/ƒâiom/ƒâiam** ‚Äî everyone/all/always
- **neniu/neniom/neniam** ‚Äî nobody/nothing/never
- **tiu/tiom/tiam** ‚Äî that one/that amount/then
