## What You Can Do at A1

At A1 you can:
- Introduce yourself and ask about others (name, age, nationality, occupation)
- Use basic greetings and farewells (Saluton, Ĝis, Bonan matenon)
- Count to 1000 and use numbers in context
- Name common objects, colors, and everyday items
- Form simple present-tense sentences
- Ask and answer yes/no questions
- Express basic needs (I want, I have, I need)
- Understand very slow, clearly articulated Esperanto

## Core Grammar at A1

| Feature | Rule | Example |
|---------|------|---------|
| Nouns | end in **-o** | libro (book), hundo (dog) |
| Adjectives | end in **-a** | bona (good), granda (big) |
| Adverbs | end in **-e** | bone (well), rapide (quickly) |
| Plurals | add **-j** | libroj (books), bonaj (good [pl.]) |
| Accusative | add **-n** | Mi havas libron (I have a book) |
| Definite article | **la** | la libro (the book) |
| Present tense | verb + **-as** | Mi parolas (I speak) |
| Past tense | verb + **-is** | Mi parolis (I spoke) |
| Future tense | verb + **-os** | Mi parolos (I will speak) |
| Infinitive | verb + **-i** | paroli (to speak) |
| Personal pronouns | mi, vi, li, ŝi, ĝi, ni, vi, ili, oni | Mi estas studento |

## Example Sentences at A1

- **Saluton! Mi nomiĝas Ana.** — Hello! My name is Ana.
- **Mi estas studento el Francio.** — I am a student from France.
- **Ĉu vi parolas Esperanton?** — Do you speak Esperanto?
- **Jes, mi parolas iomete.** — Yes, I speak a little.
- **Kiom kostas ĉi tio?** — How much does this cost?
- **Mi volas du kafojn, bonvolu.** — I would like two coffees, please.
- **Kie estas la stacidomo?** — Where is the train station?
- **Mi ne komprenas. Ĉu vi povas ripeti?** — I don't understand. Can you repeat?
- **La libro estas sur la tablo.** — The book is on the table.
- **Hodiaŭ estas varma tago.** — Today is a warm day.

## Next Steps → A2

Once you can handle the sentences above comfortably:
1. Learn the **45 correlatives** table (ki-, ti-, ĉi-, neni-, i- × -o, -a, -e, -el, -al, -am, -es, -om, -u)
2. Expand to **past and future tenses** in conversation
3. Learn the most common **prefixes and suffixes** (mal-, re-, -ist-, -in-, -ej-, -aĵ-)
4. Start **Gerda Malaperis! chapters 6–15**
5. Read the [A2 guide](../a2/) for your next targets
