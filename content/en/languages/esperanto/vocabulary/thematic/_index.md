## How to Use These Lists

1. **Don't memorize lists** — use them as a reference while reading, listening, or speaking
2. **Add to Anki** — create cards for words you want to actively know
3. **Use in sentences** — for each word, try forming 2–3 sentences
4. **Group by word family** — note derivatives and affixed forms
5. **Prioritize active vocabulary** — aim to actively use 30–50% of each list; passive recognition of the rest comes naturally

## Esperanto Word Family Pattern

For each thematic root, remember the four forms:

| Form | Ending | Example (from "food" theme) |
|------|--------|-----------------------------|
| Noun | -o | manĝo (meal), pomo (apple) |
| Adjective | -a | manĝa (food-related), poma (apple-) |
| Adverb | -e | manĝe (food-wise) |
| Verb | -i | manĝi (to eat), pomi (to pick apples — creative) |

Plus affixed forms like: manĝaĵo (food, stuff to eat), manĝejo (dining room), manĝema (gluttonous).
