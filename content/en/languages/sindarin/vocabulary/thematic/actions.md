## Negation with Verbs

Negative commands use *avo* + infinitive:
- *Avo garo!* — Do not do it!
- *Avo meno!* — Do not go!

Negative statements use *ú-* prefix (triggers soft mutation):
- *Ú-cenin* — I do not see
- *Ú-melon* — I do not love
