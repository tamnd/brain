These are words directly attested in Tolkien's Sindarin writings from the LotR period (1951–1973), marked **S.** All appear in *The Lord of the Rings*, its appendices, or in linguistic journals (*Vinyar Tengwar*, *Parma Eldalamberon*) published with Tolkien's authority.

*N.* words are from the Noldorin period (1930–1950) and are usable for Neo-Sindarin. They are marked clearly.
