## The Essential Toolkit

These five resources cover 90% of what you need:

### 1. Sindarin Crash Course
**URL**: sindarincrashcourse.neocities.org
**Cost**: Free
**Best for**: Beginners; structured 6-unit grammar course with interactive tools (mutation tool, verb conjugator, noun pluralizer)

### 2. Parf Edhellen
**URL**: elfdict.com
**Cost**: Free
**Best for**: Daily dictionary lookups; 135,773 words across all Elvish languages; open-source; user-friendly

### 3. Eldamo
**URL**: eldamo.org
**Cost**: Free
**Best for**: Deep research; every Tolkien Elvish word with source period labels (S./N./G./ᴺS.); maintained by Paul Strack; updated 2026

### 4. RealElvish Academy (Fiona Jallings)
**URL**: academy.realelvish.net
**Cost**: Free courses
**Best for**: Comprehensive structured learning; 4 tracks (pronunciation, writing, grammar, practice); 45+ grammar lessons

### 5. *A Fan's Guide to Neo-Sindarin* (Fiona Jallings)
**URL**: store.realelvish.net / Amazon
**Cost**: ~$20–30
**Best for**: Intermediate-advanced textbook; most current learner-focused print resource
