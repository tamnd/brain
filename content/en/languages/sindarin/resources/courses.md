## Essential Courses

### Sindarin Crash Course
**URL**: [sindarincrashcourse.neocities.org](https://sindarincrashcourse.neocities.org)
**Level**: Beginner → Intermediate
**Cost**: Free

The most systematically organized free Sindarin course. Built around 6 grammar units, each covering a part of speech in depth. Also includes interactive tools that are unavailable elsewhere.

**6 Core Grammar Units:**

| Unit | Sindarin Name | Topics Covered |
|------|---------------|----------------|
| 1 | *Emith* (Nouns) | Plurals, i-affection, definite articles, direct objects |
| 2 | *Ceraith* (Verbs) | Aorist, suffixes, present continuous, past tense, future |
| 3 | *Pemith* (Pronouns) | Object forms, subject suffixes, possessive forms |
| 4 | *Cephith a Mebith* (Adjectives & Adverbs) | Plural adj., "to be" copula, adverb formation |
| 5 | *Febith* (Prepositions) | Dative, genitive, directional/locational prepositions |
| 6 | *Limith* (Conjunctions) | Conjunctions, subordinate clauses |

**Supplementary Sections:**
- *Prestennith a Gwimith* — Mutation patterns and Neo-Sindarin neologisms
- *Nern* (Tales) — Reading practice through translated stories

**Interactive Tools (unique feature):**
- Mutation tool — enter any word, see all 5 mutations applied
- Verb conjugator — conjugate any verb in all tenses
- Noun pluralizer — pluralize any noun automatically
- Frequency word list — most common Sindarin words by frequency

### Sindarin Hub Lessons
**URL**: [sindarinlessons.weebly.com](https://sindarinlessons.weebly.com)
**Level**: Beginner
**Cost**: Free

A set of 20+ lessons that take you from the very basics to complex sentence construction. Notably attested-first: all information comes from Tolkien's own writings; any reconstructions are clearly marked.

**Lesson Structure (approximate):**
1. Pronunciation
2. Introduction to mutations
3. Nouns and plurals
4. The definite article
5. Basic verbs (present)
6. Adjectives
7. Pronouns
8. Soft mutation — full coverage
9. Nasal mutation
10. Mixed mutation
11. Past tense
12. Future tense
13. Prepositions
14. Conjunctions
15. Name construction
16–20+: Complex sentences, translation practice, name creation

Also includes a searchable dictionary and a separate PDF dictionary (3rd edition).

### RealElvish Academy — 4-Track System
**URL**: [academy.realelvish.net](https://academy.realelvish.net)
**Level**: Beginner → Advanced
**Cost**: Free (courses); books available for purchase

Fiona Jallings' comprehensive Academy offers four parallel tracks that you can follow independently or together:

| Track | Sindarin Name | Focus | Lessons |
|-------|---------------|-------|---------|
| 1 | **Carpho Edhellen!** | Pronunciation | Vowels, consonants, stress, diphthongs, IPA |
| 2 | **Teitho Edhellen!** | Writing | Tengwar Standard Mode, Mode of Beleriand, Cirth |
| 3 | **Gelio Edhellen!** | Grammar | Full grammar; 45+ posts covering all topics |
| 4 | **Nevio Edhellen!** | Practical use | Composition, names, translation, real application |

The Grammar track (*Gelio Edhellen!*) is the most comprehensive free Sindarin grammar course available, with individual posts for each grammatical topic:
- P4: Basic Grammar
- P9: Consonant Mutations overview
- P10: Soft Mutation detailed
- P45: Passive Participle
- (and many more)

### Ardalambion — Helge Fauskanger's Analysis
**URL**: [ardalambion.net](https://ardalambion.net)
**Level**: Intermediate → Advanced
**Cost**: Free

Helge Fauskanger (Norwegian linguist, born 1971) runs Ardalambion ("Of the Languages of Arda") — one of the most authoritative and comprehensive Tolkien linguistics sites since 1997.

For Sindarin specifically:
- **Sindarin articles**: detailed phonological and grammatical analysis
- **Verb conjugation**: [ardalambion.net/sindverb.htm](https://ardalambion.net/sindverb.htm) — the definitive verb conjugation reference
- **Reconstructing the verb system**: ardalambion.net/sverb-rec.htm
- **Word analyses**: word-by-word analysis of Tolkien's Sindarin texts
- **"Speak Elf Yourself"**: video series (primarily Quenya-focused but some Sindarin)

Note: Ardalambion went offline in December 2022 but reopened at a new domain in early 2023. Always verify the current URL.

## Which Course Should I Start With?

| If you are... | Start with... |
|---------------|---------------|
| Absolute beginner, never studied linguistics | **Sindarin Hub** or **Cuio Edhellen! PDF** |
| Want the most organized/systematic approach | **Sindarin Crash Course** |
| Want to go deep with a comprehensive course | **RealElvish Academy (Gelio Edhellen!)** |
| Intermediate and want to fill grammar gaps | **RealElvish Academy + Ambar-Eldaron** |
| Advanced and want primary-source-level detail | **Ardalambion + *A Gateway to Sindarin*** |

You don't have to choose just one — many learners work through multiple courses in parallel (one as primary, one as supplementary reference).
