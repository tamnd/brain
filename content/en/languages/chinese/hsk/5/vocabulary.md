HSK 5 (B2) introduces approximately 1,600 new words, bringing the cumulative total to around 2,500. At this level learners can understand extended speech and texts on abstract, complex, or unfamiliar topics, read newspaper articles and academic texts with relative ease, and express themselves fluently and spontaneously on a wide range of subjects. The vocabulary below is organized by semantic category and covers the first ~600 words of the HSK 5 list; additional words are being added in follow-up sessions.
