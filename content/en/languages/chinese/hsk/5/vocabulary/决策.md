## 决策 (juécè)

决策 refers to the process of making significant decisions, particularly in organizational, governmental, or strategic contexts, as well as the decisions themselves.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** decision-making; a policy decision, strategic decision
2. **[verb]** to make decisions, to determine a course of action

## Example Sentences

**科学的决策需要充分的数据支撑和理性分析。** Kēxué de juécè xūyào chōngfèn de shùjù zhīchēng hé lǐxìng fēnxī. Scientific decision-making requires sufficient data support and rational analysis.

**管理层的错误决策导致公司蒙受了巨大损失。** Guǎnlǐ céng de cuòwù juécè dǎozhì gōngsī méng shòu le jùdà sǔnshī. The management's erroneous decisions caused the company to suffer enormous losses.

**在危机时刻，领导者必须迅速而果断地决策。** Zài wēijī shíkè, lǐngdǎo zhě bìxū xùnsù ér guǒduàn de juécè. In a crisis moment, leaders must make decisions swiftly and decisively.

**民主化的决策过程有助于提高决策的公正性和可接受性。** Mínzhǔ huà de juécè guòchéng yǒuzhù yú tígāo juécè de gōngzhèngxìng hé kě jiēshòu xìng. A democratized decision-making process helps improve the fairness and acceptability of decisions.

**这家公司采用大数据分析来优化决策过程。** Zhè jiā gōngsī cǎiyòng dà shùjù fēnxī lái yōuhuà juécè guòchéng. This company uses big data analysis to optimize its decision-making process.

**政府在重大项目上的决策应该公开透明，接受社会监督。** Zhèngfǔ zài zhòngdà xiàngmù shàng de juécè yīnggāi gōngkāi tòumíng, jiēshòu shèhuì jiāndū. The government's decisions on major projects should be open and transparent and subject to public oversight.

## Usage Notes

决策 is a formal and professional term, primarily used in organizational, governmental, and business contexts. It is stronger and more formal than 决定 (to decide/a decision), which can be used for everyday personal choices. 决策 implies a weightier, more complex process involving analysis and strategy.

The verb form is common in formal writing: 决策层 (decision-making level/management), 决策者 (decision-maker). In business Chinese, 战略决策 (strategic decision) and 决策支持系统 (decision support system) are standard terms.

## Memory Hook

决 means "to decide/determine" and 策 means "strategy/plan" -- 决策 is deciding on a strategy, the moment when analysis and deliberation crystallize into a chosen course of action.
