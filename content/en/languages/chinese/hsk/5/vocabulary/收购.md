## 收购 (shōugòu)

Used in business and agriculture to describe the purchase of a company, brand, or large batch of goods, often implying a transfer of ownership or control.

## Meanings
1. **[verb]** to acquire, to buy out, to purchase in bulk
2. **[noun]** acquisition, takeover

## Example Sentences
**这家科技公司宣布收购一家人工智能初创企业。** Zhè jiā kējì gōngsī xuānbù shōugòu yī jiā réngōng zhìnéng chūchuàng qǐyè. The tech company announced the acquisition of an artificial intelligence startup.

**农业合作社负责收购农民的粮食。** Nóngyè hézuòshè fùzé shōugòu nóngmín de liángshi. The agricultural cooperative is responsible for purchasing grain from farmers.

**此次收购涉及金额高达数十亿美元。** Cǐ cì shōugòu shèjí jīn'é gāodá shùshí yì měiyuán. This acquisition involves an amount of several billion US dollars.

**外资企业试图收购这家国有银行的股份。** Wàizī qǐyè shìtú shōugòu zhè jiā guóyǒu yínháng de gǔfèn. A foreign-invested enterprise attempted to acquire shares in this state-owned bank.

**政府对恶意收购行为出台了新的监管规定。** Zhèngfǔ duì èyì shōugòu xíngwéi chūtāile xīn de jiānguǎn guīdìng. The government introduced new regulatory rules targeting hostile takeovers.

**这次并购是近年来最大规模的行业收购之一。** Zhè cì bìnggòu shì jìnnián lái zuì dà guīmó de hángyè shōugòu zhī yī. This merger is one of the largest industry acquisitions in recent years.

## Usage Notes
收购 appears in two distinct contexts. In business, it refers to corporate mergers and acquisitions (M&A), where one company buys another or a controlling stake. In agriculture and trade, it describes a state or cooperative purchasing farm produce from individual producers at a set price.

The word implies a significant, often official or large-scale purchase. It differs from 购买 (gòumǎi), which is neutral and everyday; 收购 always carries a sense of scale and strategic intent.

## Memory Hook
收 means "to receive/collect" and 购 means "to purchase." Imagine a large hand (企业) reaching out to collect and purchase an entire smaller company -- that is a 收购.
