## 审核 (shěnhé)

To review or audit something means to carefully examine it against standards or criteria to verify its accuracy, completeness, or compliance.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to review; to audit; to verify; to scrutinize; to approve after examination

## Example Sentences

**所有申请材料都需要经过严格的审核。** Suǒyǒu shēnqǐng cáiliào dōu xūyào jīngguò yángé de shěnhé. All application materials need to go through strict review.

**财务部门每季度对账目进行一次审核。** Cáiwù bùmén měi jìdù duì zhàngmù jìnxíng yīcì shěnhé. The finance department reviews the accounts once every quarter.

**该网站的内容在发布前经过了专业团队的审核。** Gāi wǎngzhàn de nèiróng zài fābù qián jīngguòle zhuānyè tuánduì de shěnhé. The website's content was reviewed by a professional team before publication.

**审核通过后，合同才可以正式签署。** Shěnhé tōngguò hòu, hétong cái kěyǐ zhèngshì qiānshǔ. Only after the review is passed can the contract be formally signed.

**平台对用户上传的视频进行自动审核。** Píngtái duì yònghù shàngyǎo de shìpín jìnxíng zìdòng shěnhé. The platform automatically reviews videos uploaded by users.

**我的申请还在审核中，预计下周出结果。** Wǒ de shēnqǐng hái zài shěnhé zhōng, yùjì xià zhōu chū jiéguǒ. My application is still under review; the result is expected next week.

## Usage Notes

审核 combines careful examination (审) with verification/checking (核). It is used in formal and institutional contexts -- applying for permits, reviewing financial accounts, moderating online content, or vetting documents. It is more systematic and process-oriented than a casual 检查 (to check).

审核 often implies that approval or rejection follows the review. Common phrases include 审核通过 (approved) and 审核未通过 or 审核失败 (not approved/failed review). In the digital age, 内容审核 (content moderation) has become an extremely common use of this word.

## Memory Hook

审 (to examine carefully/trial) + 核 (to verify/nucleus) -- checking right down to the core, like a judge examining evidence at trial.
