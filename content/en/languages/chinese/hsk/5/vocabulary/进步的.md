## 进步的 (jìnbù de)

进步的 describes views, policies, or people that advocate for social reform, change, and improvement. In political contexts, it refers to left-leaning or reform-oriented positions. In general use, it simply means "forward-looking" or "showing improvement."

## Meanings
1. **[adj]** progressive; favoring change and reform; forward-thinking; showing advancement

## Example Sentences
**进步的教育理念强调批判性思维和创新能力。** Jìnbù de jiàoyù lǐniàn qiángdiào pīpànxìng sīwéi hé chuàngxīn nénglì. Progressive educational ideas emphasize critical thinking and innovation.

**这位政治家以其进步的立场赢得了年轻选民的支持。** Zhè wèi zhèngzhìjiā yǐ qí jìnbù de lìchǎng yíngdéle niánqīng xuǎnmín de zhīchí. This politician won the support of young voters with his progressive stance.

**进步的社会政策应关注弱势群体的权益保障。** Jìnbù de shèhuì zhèngcè yīng guānzhù ruòshì qúntǐ de quányì bǎozhàng. Progressive social policies should focus on protecting the rights of vulnerable groups.

**他的思想在当时被认为是非常进步的。** Tā de sīxiǎng zài dāngshí bèi rènwéi shì fēicháng jìnbù de. His ideas were considered very progressive at the time.

**进步的环境政策需要政府和企业的共同努力。** Jìnbù de huánjìng zhèngcè xūyào zhèngfǔ hé qǐyè de gòngtóng nǔlì. Progressive environmental policies require joint efforts from both government and businesses.

**学生在本学期的学习中取得了令人欣慰的进步。** Xuéshēng zài běn xuéqī de xuéxí zhōng qǔdéle lìng rén xīnwèi de jìnbù. Students made encouraging progress in their studies this semester.

## Usage Notes
进步的 has two main registers: in political discourse, it means "progressive" in the left-leaning/reformist sense; in everyday contexts, it means "showing improvement or advancement" (e.g., 学习很进步 -- much improved in studies).

The noun/verb 进步 (to make progress; progress) is extremely common: 取得进步 (to make progress), 不断进步 (to continuously improve). The adjective 进步的 is used to modify nouns.

## Memory Hook
进 means "to advance/move forward" and 步 means "step" -- 进步 is taking steps forward, so 进步的 means progressive or forward-moving.
