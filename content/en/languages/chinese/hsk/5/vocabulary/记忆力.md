## 记忆力 (jìyìlì)

记忆力 refers specifically to the mental capacity or faculty to retain and recall information, as opposed to 记忆 (jìyì) which can mean a specific memory or the act of memorizing.

## Meanings
1. **[noun]** memory; the ability to remember; retentive power

## Example Sentences
**他的记忆力非常好，能记住很多细节。** Tā de jìyìlì fēicháng hǎo, néng jìzhù hěn duō xìjié. His memory is excellent; he can remember many details.

**随着年龄增长，记忆力往往会有所下降。** Suízhe niánlíng zēngzhǎng, jìyìlì wǎngwǎng huì yǒusuǒ xiàjiàng. Memory tends to decline somewhat as one gets older.

**充足的睡眠有助于提高记忆力。** Chōngzú de shuìmián yǒuzhù yú tígāo jìyìlì. Adequate sleep helps to improve memory.

**学习一门新语言可以锻炼记忆力。** Xuéxí yī mén xīn yǔyán kěyǐ duànliàn jìyìlì. Learning a new language can train and strengthen memory.

**这种疾病会损害患者的记忆力。** Zhè zhǒng jíbìng huì sǔnhài huànzhě de jìyìlì. This disease can damage a patient's memory.

**科学家正在研究如何增强人类的记忆力。** Kēxuéjiā zhèngzài yánjiū rúhé zēngqiáng rénlèi de jìyìlì. Scientists are studying how to enhance human memory.

## Usage Notes
The suffix 力 (lì) denotes an ability or power, so 记忆力 specifically means the capacity for memory, not a particular memory. Compare: 记忆 (jìyì) = a memory / the act of memorizing; 记忆力 (jìyìlì) = the power of memory / memory as a faculty.

This distinction is similar in English to "memory" (specific recollection) vs. "memory capacity/ability." At B2 level it is important to use 记忆力 when discussing the mental faculty and 记忆 when referring to specific memories or the act of memorizing.

## Memory Hook
记 (to record) + 忆 (to recollect) + 力 (power/ability): the power to record and recollect -- your memory ability.
