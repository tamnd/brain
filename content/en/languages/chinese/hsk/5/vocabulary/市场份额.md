## 市场份额 (shìchǎng fèn'é)

Market share refers to the percentage of a market's total sales or revenue that is earned by a particular company, product, or brand over a specified period.

## Meanings

1. **[noun phrase]** market share; the portion of market controlled by a company or product

## Example Sentences

**该公司在智能手机市场的份额已超过30%。** Gāi gōngsī zài zhìnéng shǒujī shìchǎng de fèn'é yǐ chāoguò 30%. The company's share of the smartphone market has exceeded 30%.

**两家企业激烈竞争，争夺国内市场份额。** Liǎng jiā qǐyè jīliè jìngzhēng, zhēngduó guónèi shìchǎng fèn'é. The two companies are competing fiercely for domestic market share.

**通过降低价格，他们成功扩大了市场份额。** Tōngguò jiàngdī jiàgé, tāmen chénggōng kuòdàle shìchǎng fèn'é. By lowering prices, they successfully expanded their market share.

**市场份额的下降表明公司需要调整营销策略。** Shìchǎng fèn'é de xiàjiàng biǎomíng gōngsī xūyào tiáozhěng yíngxiāo cèlüè. The decline in market share indicates the company needs to adjust its marketing strategy.

**新产品的推出帮助公司抢占了更大的市场份额。** Xīn chǎnpǐn de tuīchū bāngzhù gōngsī qiǎngzhànle gèng dà de shìchǎng fèn'é. The launch of new products helped the company seize a larger market share.

**分析市场份额的变化对制定商业战略至关重要。** Fēnxī shìchǎng fèn'é de biànhuà duì zhìdìng shāngyè zhànlüè zhìguān zhòngyào. Analyzing changes in market share is crucial for developing business strategy.

## Usage Notes

市场份额 is a standard business and economics term used in corporate reporting, market analysis, and business news. It is always used in formal contexts -- business meetings, financial reports, academic papers on economics.

份额 alone means "share" or "portion" and can appear in other compound terms. The measure for market share is typically expressed as a percentage (百分比) or described with verbs like 占 (to account for/occupy) and 超过 (to exceed).

## Memory Hook

市场 (market) + 份额 (one's share/portion) -- your slice of the market pie. How big is your piece?
