## 繁荣 (fánróng)

繁荣 describes a state of great activity, growth, and prosperity, typically applied to economies, cultures, societies, or markets. It can function as an adjective (thriving, prosperous) or a verb (to make prosper, to flourish).

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** prosperous; thriving; flourishing; booming
2. **[verb]** to prosper; to flourish; to make something thrive

## Example Sentences

**改革开放政策推动了中国经济的持续繁荣。** Gǎigé kāifàng zhèngcè tuīdòngle Zhōngguó jīngjì de chíxù fánróng. The reform and opening-up policy has driven the sustained prosperity of China's economy.

**这座城市的文化产业日趋繁荣，各类艺术节活动层出不穷。** Zhè zuò chéngshì de wénhuà chǎnyè rì qū fánróng, gè lèi yìshùjié huódòng céng chū bù qióng. The city's cultural industries are becoming increasingly flourishing, with all kinds of arts festival events appearing one after another.

**国际贸易的自由化有助于繁荣全球市场，惠及各国消费者。** Guójì màoyì de zìyóuhuà yǒuzhù yú fánróng quánqiú shìchǎng, huì jí gè guó xiāofèizhě. The liberalisation of international trade helps to make global markets flourish, benefiting consumers in all countries.

**夜市经济的兴起让这片老城区重新焕发了繁荣的活力。** Yèshì jīngjì de xīngqǐ ràng zhè piàn lǎo chénggū chóngxīn huànfāle fánróng de huólì. The rise of the night market economy has reinvigorated this old urban district with a new vitality and prosperity.

**社会稳定是经济繁荣的前提条件，没有稳定就没有发展。** Shèhuì wěndìng shì jīngjì fánróng de qiántí tiáojiàn, méiyǒu wěndìng jiù méiyǒu fāzhǎn. Social stability is a precondition for economic prosperity; without stability there is no development.

**多元文化共存促进了这个地区的文化繁荣与创新。** Duōyuán wénhuà gòngcún cùjìnle zhège dìqū de wénhuà fánróng yǔ chuàngxīn. The coexistence of diverse cultures has promoted cultural prosperity and innovation in this region.

## Usage Notes

繁荣 is a formal word used in political speeches, economic reports, and literary description. It conveys scale and vitality, not just financial wealth. 繁荣昌盛 is a common chengyu-like phrase meaning "flourishing and prosperous," often used in official rhetoric.

As a verb, 繁荣 takes a following object: 繁荣经济 (make the economy prosper), 繁荣文化 (make culture flourish). This verb use is more formal and is common in policy documents.

## Memory Hook

繁 (numerous/abundant -- many threads) + 荣 (glory/flourish -- like a blooming flower) -- abundant and gloriously blooming: prosperity.
