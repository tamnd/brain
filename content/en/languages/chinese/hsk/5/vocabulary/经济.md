## 经济 (jīngjì)

经济 is the primary Chinese word for economy and economics. As an adjective it can also mean economical or cost-effective. It is one of the most frequent words in modern Chinese media and public discourse.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** economy; economics; economic activity
2. **[adjective]** economical; affordable; cost-effective

## Example Sentences

**全球经济在新冠疫情后经历了一段艰难的复苏期。** Quánqiú jīngjì zài xīnguān yìqíng hòu jīnglìle yī duàn jiānnán de fùsū qī. The global economy went through a difficult recovery period after the COVID-19 pandemic.

**中国已成为世界第二大经济体，对全球贸易影响深远。** Zhōngguó yǐ chénwéi shìjiè dì èr dà jīngjì tǐ, duì quánqiú màoyì yǐngxiǎng shēnyuǎn. China has become the world's second-largest economy and has a far-reaching impact on global trade.

**发展绿色经济是实现可持续发展目标的重要路径。** Fāzhǎn lǜsè jīngjì shì shíxiàn kě chíxù fāzhǎn mùbiāo de zhòngyào lùjìng. Developing a green economy is an important path to achieving sustainable development goals.

**这款经济型轿车以低油耗和实惠的售价受到工薪阶层的青睐。** Zhè kuǎn jīngjì xíng jiàochē yǐ dī yóuhào hé shíhuì de shòujià shòudào gōngxīn jiēcéng de qīnglài. This economy-class sedan is popular among wage earners for its low fuel consumption and affordable price.

**市场经济的核心是价格机制，由供需关系决定资源配置。** Shìchǎng jīngjì de héxīn shì jiàgé jīzhì, yóu gōng xū guānxi juédìng zīyuán pèizhì. The core of a market economy is the price mechanism, with supply and demand determining resource allocation.

**数字经济的崛起为许多传统行业带来了新的挑战与机遇。** Shùzì jīngjì de juéqǐ wèi xǔduō chuántǒng hángyè dàiláile xīn de tiǎozhàn yǔ jīyù. The rise of the digital economy has brought new challenges and opportunities to many traditional industries.

## Usage Notes

经济 is both a standalone noun and a very productive modifier. As a standalone noun it means the economy (the economy grew; the economy contracted). As a modifier before another noun it means economic: 经济危机 (economic crisis), 经济体制 (economic system).

The adjective sense "economical/affordable" is used in product descriptions (经济实惠 -- economical and affordable) and travel (经济舱 -- economy class on a plane). Context makes the two senses clear.

## Memory Hook

经 (manage/pass through) + 济 (aid/benefit/cross a river) -- managing the flow of resources to benefit society; that is an economy.
