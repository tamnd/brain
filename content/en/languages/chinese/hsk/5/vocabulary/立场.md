## 立场 (lìchǎng)

立场 refers to a person's or organization's fundamental viewpoint or position on an issue, especially in political, diplomatic, or ideological debates.

## Meanings
1. **[noun]** standpoint; position; stance; point of view

## Example Sentences
**政府在这一问题上的立场十分明确，不会做出妥协。** Zhèngfǔ zài zhè yī wèntí shàng de lìchǎng shífēn míngquè, bù huì zuò chū tuǒxié. The government's position on this issue is very clear and will not compromise.

**双方在谈判中坚守各自的立场，导致协议迟迟未能达成。** Shuāngfāng zài tánpàn zhōng jiānshǒu gèzì de lìchǎng, dǎozhì xiéyì chíchí wèinéng dáchéng. Both sides stuck to their respective positions during negotiations, causing the agreement to be long delayed.

**一个成熟的学者应该能够从不同立场分析同一个问题。** Yī gè chéngshú de xuézhě yīnggāi néng gòu cóng bùtóng lìchǎng fēnxī tóngyī gè wèntí. A mature scholar should be able to analyze the same issue from different standpoints.

**他的立场在压力下发生了明显转变，让支持者感到失望。** Tā de lìchǎng zài yālì xià fāshēng le míngxiǎn zhuǎnbiàn, ràng zhīchízhě gǎndào shīwàng. His position clearly shifted under pressure, disappointing his supporters.

**我们坚持独立自主的立场，不接受外部势力的干涉。** Wǒmen jiānchí dúlì zìzhǔ de lìchǎng, bù jiēshòu wàibù shìlì de gānshè. We adhere to an independent and self-reliant position and do not accept interference from external forces.

**企业在回应媒体质疑时需要明确表达自己的立场。** Qǐyè zài huíyìng méitǐ zhìyí shí xūyào míngquè biǎodá zìjǐ de lìchǎng. Enterprises need to clearly express their position when responding to media questioning.

## Collocations
| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 坚守立场 (jiānshǒu lìchǎng) | to stick to one's position |
| 立场明确 (lìchǎng míngquè) | clear position |
| 转变立场 (zhuǎnbiàn lìchǎng) | to change one's position |
| 不同立场 (bùtóng lìchǎng) | different standpoints |
| 政治立场 (zhèngzhì lìchǎng) | political stance |
| 表明立场 (biǎomíng lìchǎng) | to state one's position |

## Usage Notes
立场 specifically refers to a fundamental position or stance rooted in principles or interests, often in political, diplomatic, or ideological discourse. It is stronger and more value-laden than 看法 (kànfǎ, opinion/view) or 观点 (guāndiǎn, viewpoint).

立场 implies that a position is held with commitment and may be defended or maintained under pressure. Common collocations include 坚守立场 (hold firm to one's stance) and 表明立场 (make one's position clear).

## Memory Hook
立 (to stand/establish) + 场 (field/place) -- the place where you stand -- your standpoint; position.
