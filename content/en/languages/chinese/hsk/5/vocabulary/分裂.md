## 分裂 (fēnliè)

分裂 describes the act of splitting, dividing, or breaking apart -- whether a physical substance, a political entity, a social group, or a personal relationship.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to split, to divide, to break apart
2. **[noun]** division, schism, split

## Example Sentences

**党内的分裂严重削弱了这个政党的执政能力。** Dǎng nèi de fēnliè yánzhòng xuēruò le zhège zhèngdǎng de zhízhèng nénglì. The split within the party seriously weakened this political party's governing capacity.

**他们担心这场争论会导致组织内部的分裂。** Tāmen dānxīn zhè chǎng zhēnglùn huì dǎozhì zǔzhī nèibù de fēnliè. They worried that this debate would lead to a split within the organization.

**细胞分裂是生物体生长和繁殖的基本过程。** Xìbāo fēnliè shì shēngwùtǐ shēngzhǎng hé fánzhí de jīběn guòchéng. Cell division is a basic process of the growth and reproduction of organisms.

**外部势力试图分裂这个国家，破坏其统一。** Wàibù shìlì shìtú fēnliè zhège guójiā, pòhuài qí tǒngyī. External forces are attempting to split this country and undermine its unity.

**家族内部的财产纠纷最终导致了家庭的分裂。** Jiāzú nèibù de cáichǎn jiūfēn zuìzhōng dǎozhì le jiātíng de fēnliè. Property disputes within the family ultimately led to the family's split.

**思想上的分裂往往比物理上的分裂更难弥合。** Sīxiǎng shàng de fēnliè wǎngwǎng bǐ wùlǐ shàng de fēnliè gèng nán míhé. Ideological divisions are often harder to bridge than physical ones.

## Usage Notes

分裂 is used across many domains: biology (细胞分裂, cell division), politics (国家分裂, national division), and social/organizational contexts (团队分裂, team split). The word carries a strongly negative connotation in political Chinese, where 分裂国家 (splitting the nation) is one of the most serious charges.

分裂 differs from 分开 (to separate) in that 分裂 implies a break or rupture that was previously unified, often with violence, conflict, or serious damage. 分开 is neutral and can be used for any separation.

## Memory Hook

分 means "to divide" and 裂 means "to crack/split" -- 分裂 is not a gentle parting but a forceful cracking apart, like a log splitting under an axe.
