## 汇报 (huìbào)

汇报 means to give a formal report or briefing, typically to a superior or organization. It conveys the act of summarizing information and presenting it upward in a hierarchy.

## Meanings
1. **[verb]** to report; to give a briefing; to present a summary to a superior
2. **[noun]** report; briefing; summary presentation

## Example Sentences
**他每周向经理汇报工作进展情况。** Tā měi zhōu xiàng jīnglǐ huìbào gōngzuò jìnzhǎn qíngkuàng. He reports the progress of his work to the manager every week.

**明天我需要向董事会汇报本季度的销售数据。** Míngtiān wǒ xūyào xiàng dǒngshìhuì huìbào běn jìdù de xiāoshòu shùjù. Tomorrow I need to report this quarter's sales data to the board of directors.

**调查小组就最新发现向上级进行了详细汇报。** Diàochá xiǎozǔ jiù zuìxīn fāxiàn xiàng shàngjí jìnxíngle xiángxì huìbào. The investigation team gave a detailed briefing to their superiors on the latest findings.

**她准备了一份完整的汇报材料，包括数据图表。** Tā zhǔnbèile yī fèn wánzhěng de huìbào cáiliào, bāokuò shùjù túbiǎo. She prepared a complete set of report materials, including data charts.

**请在会议前把你的汇报发给我看一下。** Qǐng zài huìyì qián bǎ nǐ de huìbào fā gěi wǒ kàn yīxià. Please send me your report to review before the meeting.

**这位官员亲自向市长汇报了灾区的最新情况。** Zhè wèi guānyuán qīnzì xiàng shìzhǎng huìbàole zāiqū de zuìxīn qíngkuàng. The official personally reported the latest situation in the disaster area to the mayor.

## Usage Notes
汇报 implies a formal, hierarchical communication where information flows upward -- from employee to manager, from official to leader. It differs from 报告 (bàogào), which is a broader term for any report or announcement. While 报告 can be horizontal (e.g., a research report shared with peers), 汇报 specifically suggests reporting upward to someone with authority.

The verb 汇报 is commonly followed by 给 + person or 向 + person to indicate the recipient of the report. In workplace Chinese, this is a high-frequency word for office and organizational communication.

## Memory Hook
汇 means "to converge/compile" and 报 means "to report/inform" -- 汇报 is compiling information and sending it up as a report.
