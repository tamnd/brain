## 敬佩 (jìngpèi)

Expresses a deep, heartfelt combination of respect and admiration, typically directed at a person's moral character, achievements, or perseverance.

## Meanings
1. **[verb]** to admire deeply, to hold in high esteem, to have profound respect for

## Example Sentences
**我非常敬佩她在逆境中坚持不懈的精神。** Wǒ fēicháng jìngpèi tā zài nìjìng zhōng jiānchí bùxiè de jīngshén. I deeply admire her unwavering spirit in the face of adversity.

**同学们对这位老教授的学识和品格深感敬佩。** Tóngxuémen duì zhè wèi lǎo jiàoshòu de xuéshí hé pǐngé shēn gǎn jìngpèi. The students feel deep admiration for this elderly professor's knowledge and character.

**他的无私奉献让所有同事对他肃然起敬，深感敬佩。** Tā de wúsī fèngxiàn ràng suǒyǒu tóngshì duì tā sùrán qǐjìng, shēn gǎn jìngpèi. His selfless dedication filled all his colleagues with deep respect and admiration.

**我敬佩那些在艰难条件下仍坚守岗位的医护人员。** Wǒ jìngpèi nàxiē zài jiānnán tiáojiàn xià réng jiānshǒu gǎngwèi de yīhù rényuán. I admire the medical workers who remain at their posts despite difficult conditions.

**这位科学家的一生令人由衷敬佩。** Zhè wèi kēxuéjiā de yīshēng lìng rén yóuzhōng jìngpèi. This scientist's life inspires genuine admiration.

**她表示十分敬佩这位作家敢于说真话的勇气。** Tā biǎoshì shífēn jìngpèi zhè wèi zuòjiā gǎnyú shuō zhēnhuà de yǒngqì. She expressed deep admiration for the writer's courage in speaking the truth.

## Usage Notes
敬佩 is a formal, elevated expression of admiration. Unlike 佩服 (pèifú), which can be used casually or even sarcastically, 敬佩 always implies sincere, serious respect with a moral dimension. It is typically used to praise someone for integrity, sacrifice, perseverance, or exceptional achievement.

In written Chinese -- speeches, essays, and formal letters -- 敬佩 appears more frequently than in casual speech. B2 learners should use it when they want to sound genuinely respectful rather than flippant.

## Memory Hook
敬 (jìng) means respect/reverence (as in 尊敬) and 佩 means to wear as an ornament or to admire. When you 敬佩 someone, you wear their example like a badge of honor -- you revere and carry their spirit with you.
