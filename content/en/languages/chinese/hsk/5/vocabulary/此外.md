## 此外 (cǐ wài)

This conjunction is used to introduce additional points beyond what has already been stated, functioning as a formal connective in written and spoken Chinese.

## Meanings
1. **[conj]** in addition; besides; moreover; apart from this

## Example Sentences
**他擅长数学和物理。此外，他还会弹钢琴。** Tā shàncháng shùxué hé wùlǐ. Cǐ wài, tā hái huì tán gāngqín. He is good at math and physics. In addition, he can play the piano.

**我们需要更多资金。此外，还需要专业人才。** Wǒmen xūyào gèng duō zījīn. Cǐ wài, hái xūyào zhuānyè réncái. We need more funding. Besides, we also need professional talent.

**这家餐厅环境很好。此外，价格也很实惠。** Zhè jiā cāntīng huánjìng hěn hǎo. Cǐ wài, jiàgé yě hěn shíhuì. This restaurant has a great atmosphere. In addition, the prices are also reasonable.

**报告涵盖了市场分析。此外，还包括竞争对手研究。** Bàogào hányùle shìchǎng fēnxī. Cǐ wài, hái bāokuò jìngzhēng duìshǒu yánjiū. The report covers market analysis. Besides, it also includes competitor research.

**她会说中文和英文。此外，她正在学习法语。** Tā huì shuō zhōngwén hé yīngwén. Cǐ wài, tā zhèngzài xuéxí fǎyǔ. She can speak Chinese and English. In addition, she is learning French.

**这项政策有助于减少污染。此外，它还能促进经济发展。** Zhè xiàng zhèngcè yǒuzhù yú jiǎnshǎo wūrǎn. Cǐ wài, tā hái néng cùjìn jīngjì fāzhǎn. This policy helps reduce pollution. Moreover, it can also promote economic development.

## Usage Notes
此外 is a formal connective typically used in written Chinese or formal speech. It appears at the beginning of a new sentence or clause to introduce an additional point. Unlike 而且 (érgqiě), which is more commonly used mid-sentence, 此外 tends to begin a new sentence and carries a slightly more formal register.

Note the difference between 此外 and 另外 (lìngwài): both mean "in addition," but 另外 can also mean "another" as an adjective, while 此外 is purely conjunctive. In formal writing, 此外 is often preferred for its concise, literary quality.

## Memory Hook
Think of 此 (this/here) + 外 (outside/beyond) -- "beyond this point" -- there is something more to add.
