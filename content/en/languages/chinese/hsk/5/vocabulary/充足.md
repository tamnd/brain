## 充足 (chōngzú)

充足 describes a quantity or amount that is fully sufficient, adequate, or more than enough for a particular purpose or need.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** sufficient; adequate; ample; plentiful (fully meeting needs or requirements)

## Example Sentences

**充足的睡眠对于维持身体健康和提高工作效率都非常重要。** Chōngzú de shuìmián duìyú wéichí shēntǐ jiànkāng hé tígāo gōngzuò xiàolǜ dōu fēicháng zhòngyào. Adequate sleep is very important for maintaining physical health and improving work efficiency.

**这个项目的成功实施需要充足的资金支持和专业团队的协作。** Zhège xiàngmù de chénggōng shíshī xūyào chōngzú de zījīn zhīchí hé zhuānyè tuánduì de xiézuò. The successful implementation of this project requires sufficient funding and the collaboration of a professional team.

**政府已准备了充足的医疗物资，以应对可能发生的公共卫生紧急情况。** Zhèngfǔ yǐ zhǔnbèile chōngzú de yīliáo wùzī, yǐ yìngduì kěnéng fāshēng de gōnggòng wèishēng jǐnjí qíngkuàng. The government has prepared sufficient medical supplies to respond to possible public health emergencies.

**只有掌握了充足的证据，检察官才能提起诉讼，确保指控的可信度。** Zhǐyǒu zhǎngwòle chōngzú de zhèngjù, jiǎncháguān cái néng tíqǐ sùsòng, quèbǎo zhǐkòng de kěxìndù. Only when prosecutors have gathered sufficient evidence can they file a lawsuit to ensure the credibility of the charges.

**这一地区阳光充足，非常适合发展太阳能发电产业。** Zhè yī dìqū yángguāng chōngzú, fēicháng shìhé fāzhǎn tàiyáng néng fādiàn chǎnyè. This region has ample sunshine and is very suitable for developing the solar power generation industry.

**研究人员认为，充足的经费和人才投入是推动科研突破的两大关键因素。** Yánjiūrényuán rènwéi, chōngzú de jīngfèi hé réncái tóurù shì tuīdòng kēyán tūpò de liǎng dà guānjiàn yīnsù. Researchers believe that sufficient funding and talent investment are the two key factors driving scientific breakthroughs.

## Usage Notes

充足 describes an amount that is fully satisfactory and meets or exceeds requirements. It is generally a positive descriptor. It differs from 足够 (enough; sufficient), which is more neutral and conversational. 充足 tends to be used in formal contexts and carries a slightly stronger connotation of being amply sufficient rather than just barely enough.

充足 is commonly used to describe resources, evidence, preparation, sleep, sunlight, and funding. It is not typically used to describe people (you wouldn't say a person is 充足), but rather the quantities or resources associated with activities and situations.

## Memory Hook

充 (full/to fill) + 足 (sufficient/foot) — 充足 is to be "fully filled up to the foot/base," i.e., completely sufficient, with nothing lacking.
