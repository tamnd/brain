## 贷款 (dàikuǎn)

贷款 refers to a loan from a bank or financial institution, and is central vocabulary for discussions of personal finance, housing, business operations, and economic policy in China.

## Meanings
1. **[noun]** loan; credit (from a bank)
2. **[verb]** to take out a loan; to lend money (from a bank's perspective)

## Example Sentences
**他向银行申请了一笔住房贷款。** Tā xiàng yínháng shēnqǐng le yī bǐ zhùfáng dàikuǎn. He applied to the bank for a housing loan.

**贷款利率的高低直接影响借款人的还款压力。** Dàikuǎn lìlǜ de gāodī zhíjiē yǐngxiǎng jiè kuǎn rén de huánkuǎn yālì. The level of loan interest rates directly affects borrowers' repayment pressure.

**政府为小微企业提供了低息贷款支持。** Zhèngfǔ wèi xiǎo wēi qǐyè tígōng le dīxī dàikuǎn zhīchí. The government provided low-interest loan support for small and micro enterprises.

**他每月需要偿还贷款三千元。** Tā měi yuè xūyào chánghuán dàikuǎn sān qiān yuán. He needs to repay 3,000 yuan in loan repayments every month.

**由于信用记录不佳，他的贷款申请被拒绝了。** Yóuyú xìnyòng jìlù bù jiā, tā de dàikuǎn shēnqǐng bèi jùjué le. Due to a poor credit record, his loan application was rejected.

**学生可以申请助学贷款，减轻家庭负担。** Xuéshēng kěyǐ shēnqǐng zhùxué dàikuǎn, jiǎnqīng jiātíng fùdān. Students can apply for student loans to reduce the family's financial burden.

## Usage Notes
贷款 is the standard term for loans from financial institutions. As a noun it refers to the loan itself (申请一笔贷款, to apply for a loan). As a verb it means to lend or to take a loan: 银行贷款给企业 (the bank lends to enterprises) or 向银行贷款 (to take a loan from a bank).

Distinguish 贷款 (bank/institutional loan) from 借款 (jiè kuǎn, to borrow/lend money informally between individuals). 贷款 always implies a formal financial institution.

## Memory Hook
贷 (to lend/credit) + 款 (funds/money): funds provided on credit -- a loan.
