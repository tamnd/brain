## 领导 (lǐngdǎo)

领导 functions as both a verb meaning to lead or guide an organisation, and as a noun referring to a leader or the leadership of an organisation, and is one of the most common words in Chinese organisational and political vocabulary.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to lead; to guide; to head (an organisation or group)
2. **[noun]** leader; leaders; leadership; those in charge

## Example Sentences

**她被任命为新部门的负责人，领导一支由三十人组成的团队。** Tā bèi rènmìng wéi xīn bùmén de fùzérén, lǐngdǎo yī zhī yóu sānshí rén zǔchéng de tuánduì. She was appointed as the head of the new department, leading a team of thirty people.

**公司领导层决定进行全面的战略转型以应对市场变化。** Gōngsī lǐngdǎo céng juédìng jìnxíng quánmiàn de zhànlüè zhuǎnxíng yǐ yìngduì shìchǎng biànhuà. The company's leadership decided to carry out a comprehensive strategic transformation in response to market changes.

**卓越的领导力是推动组织持续发展的核心力量。** Zhuóyuè de lǐngdǎolì shì tuīdòng zǔzhī chíxù fāzhǎn de héxīn lìliàng. Outstanding leadership is the core force driving the continuous development of an organisation.

**各国领导人在峰会上就气候变化问题达成了重要共识。** Gèguó lǐngdǎorén zài fēnghuì shàng jiù qìhòu biànhuà wèntí dáchéngle zhòngyào gòngshì. Leaders of various countries reached important consensus on climate change at the summit.

**他在危机时期展现出了果断而冷静的领导风格。** Tā zài wēijī shíqī zhǎnxiàn chūle guǒduàn ér lěngjìng de lǐngdǎo fēnggé. He displayed a decisive and calm leadership style during the crisis.

**强有力的政治领导是实现国家稳定和发展的关键。** Qiáng yǒulì de zhèngzhì lǐngdǎo shì shíxiàn guójiā wěndìng hé fāzhǎn de guānjiàn. Strong political leadership is the key to achieving national stability and development.

## Usage Notes

领导 is extremely versatile. As a noun it can refer to any superior or leader: 你的领导 can mean "your boss/leader." In everyday Chinese, 领导 (with neutral tone) is also informally used to address or refer to one's superior, much like "boss" in English. As a verb, it governs a following noun: 领导团队 (to lead a team), 领导国家 (to lead the nation).

The derived forms 领导力 (leadership ability), 领导层 (leadership level), and 领导人 (leader/statesman) are essential for formal contexts.

## Memory Hook

领 (to lead/receive) + 导 (to guide) = to lead and guide people forward.
