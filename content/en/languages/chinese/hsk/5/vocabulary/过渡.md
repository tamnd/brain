## 过渡 (guòdù)

过渡 describes a transitional phase or the process of moving from one state, system, or condition to another, and is widely used in political, economic, and social discourse at B2 level.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** transition; transitional period; changeover
2. **[verb]** to transition; to shift from one state to another

## Example Sentences

**这个国家正处于从计划经济向市场经济过渡的阶段。** Zhège guójiā zhèng chǔyú cóng jìhuà jīngjì xiàng shìchǎng jīngjì guòdù de jiēduàn. This country is in the stage of transitioning from a planned economy to a market economy.

**领导层的过渡必须平稳进行，以维护社会稳定。** Lǐngdǎo céng de guòdù bìxū píngwěn jìnxíng, yǐ wéihù shèhuì wěndìng. The leadership transition must proceed smoothly to maintain social stability.

**过渡期间，旧制度和新制度将并行运作。** Guòdù qījiān, jiù zhìdù hé xīn zhìdù jiāng bìngxíng yùnzuò. During the transition period, the old and new systems will operate in parallel.

**他用一段幽默的话作为两个话题之间的过渡。** Tā yòng yī duàn yōumò de huà zuòwéi liǎng gè huàtí zhījiān de guòdù. He used a humorous remark as a transition between the two topics.

**从学生身份过渡到职场人士需要一段适应时间。** Cóng xuéshēng shēnfèn guòdù dào zhíchǎng rénshì xūyào yī duàn shìyìng shíjiān. Transitioning from a student to a working professional requires an adjustment period.

**能源结构的过渡是实现碳中和的关键步骤。** Néngyuán jiégòu de guòdù shì shíxiàn tàn zhōnghé de guānjiàn bùzhòu. The transition in energy structure is a key step toward achieving carbon neutrality.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 过渡期 (guòdù qī) | transition period |
| 平稳过渡 (píngwěn guòdù) | smooth transition |
| 过渡阶段 (guòdù jiēduàn) | transitional stage |
| 过渡政府 (guòdù zhèngfǔ) | transitional government |
| 顺利过渡 (shùnlì guòdù) | successful/smooth transition |
| 权力过渡 (quánlì guòdù) | power transition |

## Usage Notes

过渡 emphasises the in-between nature of change -- the crossing over from one state to another -- rather than the end result. The compound 过渡期 (transition period) is extremely common in official language. In writing and speaking, 过渡 can also serve as a structural signal, e.g. in a speech, a 过渡 sentence connects one section to the next.

Compare with 转变 (zhuǎnbiàn, to transform/change), which focuses more on the resulting change rather than the process of crossing over.

## Memory Hook

过 (to pass/cross over) + 渡 (to ferry across water) = to ferry yourself across from one side to another.
