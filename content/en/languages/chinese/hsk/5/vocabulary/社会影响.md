## 社会影响 (shèhuì yǐngxiǎng)

社会影响 refers to the ways in which the presence, behavior, or attitudes of other people affect an individual's own thoughts, feelings, and actions. It is a fundamental concept in social psychology and sociology. 社会影响 can be direct, such as peer pressure, or indirect, such as cultural norms.

## Meanings
1. **noun** social influence, the impact of society or social groups on individual behavior

## Example Sentences
**社会影响对青少年的行为有重要作用。** Shèhuì yǐngxiǎng duì qīngshàonián de xíngwéi yǒu zhòngyào zuòyòng. Social influence plays an important role in adolescent behavior.

**媒体是传递社会影响的重要渠道。** Méitǐ shì chuándì shèhuì yǐngxiǎng de zhòngyào qúdào. Media is an important channel for transmitting social influence.

**他研究社会影响如何改变人的决策。** Tā yánjiū shèhuì yǐngxiǎng rúhé gǎibiàn rén de juécè. He studies how social influence changes people's decisions.

**正面的社会影响能促进个人成长。** Zhèngmiàn de shèhuì yǐngxiǎng néng cùjìn gèrén chéngzhǎng. Positive social influence can promote personal growth.

**她意识到自己的行为受到了社会影响。** Tā yìshí dào zìjǐ de xíngwéi shòudào le shèhuì yǐngxiǎng. She realized that her behavior was affected by social influence.

**社会影响的研究有助于理解群体行为。** Shèhuì yǐngxiǎng de yánjiū yǒuzhù yú lǐjiě qúntǐ xíngwéi. Research on social influence helps us understand group behavior.

## Usage Notes
社会影响 can function as a standalone noun phrase or as a modifier in compound phrases. It is commonly used in academic writing, psychology, sociology, and social commentary. Note that 影响 alone means "influence/impact" and can be used as both a noun and verb.

When used as a verb phrase, 社会影响 requires a verb: for example, 社会影响了他的决定 is incorrect; say 社会因素影响了他的决定 (social factors influenced his decision).

## Memory Hook
社会 means "society" and 影响 means "influence/impact" — social influence is society casting its shadow (影) on you.
