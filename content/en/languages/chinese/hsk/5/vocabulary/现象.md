## 现象 (xiànxiàng)

现象 refers to an observable phenomenon, event, or occurrence, especially one that is noteworthy, widespread, or in need of explanation.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** phenomenon, occurrence (something observable)
2. **[noun]** symptom, manifestation (of a deeper underlying cause)

## Example Sentences

**全球气候变暖是当今世界最受关注的环境现象之一。** Quánqiú qìhòu biànnuǎn shì dāngjīn shìjiè zuì shòu guānzhù de huánjìng xiànxiàng zhī yī. Global warming is one of the most widely discussed environmental phenomena in the world today.

**社会上存在的贫富差距现象需要引起高度重视。** Shèhuì shàng cúnzài de pínfù chājù xiànxiàng xūyào yǐnqǐ gāodù zhòngshì. The phenomenon of wealth inequality in society needs to be given a high degree of attention.

**这种文化现象的背后反映了深层次的社会变迁。** Zhè zhǒng wénhuà xiànxiàng de bèihòu fǎnyìng le shēn céngcì de shèhuì biànqiān. Behind this cultural phenomenon lies a reflection of deep-seated social change.

**科学家尝试解释这种奇特的自然现象。** Kēxuéjiā chángshì jiěshì zhè zhǒng qítè de zìrán xiànxiàng. Scientists are trying to explain this peculiar natural phenomenon.

**网络购物已经成为一种普遍的社会现象。** Wǎnglù gòuwù yǐjīng chéngwéi yī zhǒng pǔbiàn de shèhuì xiànxiàng. Online shopping has already become a widespread social phenomenon.

**表面现象往往会掩盖事物的本质。** Biǎomiàn xiànxiàng wǎngwǎng huì yǎngài shìwù de běnzhì. Surface phenomena often conceal the essence of things.

## Usage Notes

现象 is a neutral, descriptive term used to identify and label observable occurrences. In philosophical usage (especially in discussions of Marxism or phenomenology), 现象 contrasts with 本质 (essence): phenomena are the observable surface manifestations, while 本质 is the underlying truth. This 现象/本质 distinction is important in academic Chinese writing at B2 level and above.

At B2 level, 现象 is essential for discussing social trends, scientific observations, and cultural analysis.

## Memory Hook

现 means to appear or be visible, and 象 means image or form -- 现象 is what appears and takes visible form before your eyes, the observable face of a deeper reality.
