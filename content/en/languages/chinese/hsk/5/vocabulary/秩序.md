## 秩序 (zhìxù)

秩序 refers to a state of orderly arrangement and proper functioning, whether in social conduct, public spaces, legal systems, or international relations.

## Meanings
1. **[noun]** order; orderliness; social order; discipline

## Example Sentences
**维护公共秩序是每个公民的基本义务。** Wéihù gōnggòng zhìxù shì měi gè gōngmín de jīběn yìwù. Maintaining public order is a basic obligation of every citizen.

**法律是保障社会秩序的重要工具。** Fǎlǜ shì bǎozhàng shèhuì zhìxù de zhòngyào gōngjù. Law is an important tool for ensuring social order.

**演唱会现场的观众秩序井然，没有发生任何混乱。** Yǎnchànghui xiànchǎng de guānzhòng zhìxù jǐngrán, méiyǒu fāshēng rènhé hùnluàn. The audience at the concert site was in perfect order with no disorder occurring.

**战争严重破坏了该地区的社会经济秩序。** Zhànzhēng yánzhòng pòhuài le gāi dìqū de shèhuì jīngjì zhìxù. The war seriously disrupted the socio-economic order of the region.

**金融市场需要有效监管来维持正常的交易秩序。** Jīnróng shìchǎng xūyào yǒuxiào jiānguǎn lái wéichí zhèngcháng de jiāoyì zhìxù. Financial markets need effective regulation to maintain normal trading order.

**国际社会正在努力构建更公正合理的世界秩序。** Guójì shèhuì zhèngzài nǔlì gòujiàn gèng gōngzhèng hélǐ de shìjiè zhìxù. The international community is working to build a more just and rational world order.

## Usage Notes
秩序 denotes orderliness as a state or quality, not as an individual act. It is a formal noun used in political, legal, and social contexts. The opposite is 混乱 (hùnluàn, chaos/disorder).

The phrase 秩序井然 (in perfect order) is a common four-character idiom expressing a scene that is orderly and disciplined. In formal writing, 秩序 often appears in contexts discussing governance, rule of law, and social stability.

## Memory Hook
秩 (orderly/graded) + 序 (sequence/order) -- an orderly sequence -- order; social order.
