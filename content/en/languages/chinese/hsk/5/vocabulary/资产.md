## 资产 (zīchǎn)

资产 refers to anything of value owned by a person, company, or government -- including financial investments, property, and equipment -- and is a foundational term in economics, accounting, and business journalism.

## Meanings
1. **[noun]** assets; property; holdings (financial or physical)

## Example Sentences
**这家公司的总资产超过五百亿元。** Zhè jiā gōngsī de zǒng zīchǎn chāoguò wǔ bǎi yì yuán. The company's total assets exceed 50 billion yuan.

**资产与负债的平衡是企业财务管理的核心。** Zīchǎn yǔ fùzhài de pínghéng shì qǐyè cáiwù guǎnlǐ de héxīn. Balancing assets and liabilities is at the core of corporate financial management.

**房地产是许多家庭最主要的资产。** Fángdìchǎn shì xǔduō jiātíng zuì zhǔyào de zīchǎn. Real estate is the most important asset for many families.

**政府决定出售部分国有资产，引进私人投资。** Zhèngfǔ juédìng chūshòu bùfen guóyǒu zīchǎn, yǐnjìn sīrén tóuzī. The government decided to sell some state-owned assets and attract private investment.

**知识产权也是企业的重要无形资产。** Zhīshí chǎnquán yě shì qǐyè de zhòngyào wúxíng zīchǎn. Intellectual property is also an important intangible asset of enterprises.

**在经济危机期间，资产价值大幅缩水。** Zài jīngjì wéijī qījiān, zīchǎn jiàzhí dàfú suōshuǐ. During the economic crisis, asset values shrank dramatically.

## Collocations

## Usage Notes
资产 is the technical term for assets in accounting and finance, and contrasts directly with 负债 (fùzhài, liabilities). The balance sheet (资产负债表, zīchǎn fùzhài biǎo) is a key financial document pairing these two concepts.

资产 can also be used metaphorically: 人才是国家的宝贵资产 (talented people are the nation's precious asset). In everyday speech 财产 (cáichǎn) is sometimes used similarly but is more general and informal.

## Memory Hook
资 (resources/capital) + 产 (to produce/property): capital that produces property -- assets.
