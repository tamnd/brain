## 谋划 (móuhuà)

谋划 means to plan carefully and strategically, often implying calculated, deliberate thought about how to achieve a significant goal -- it can be neutral (strategic planning) or subtly negative (scheming).

## Meanings
1. **[verb]** to plan strategically; to scheme; to devise

## Example Sentences
**领导层正在谋划公司下一阶段的发展战略。** Lǐngdǎocéng zhèngzài móuhuà gōngsī xià yī jiēduàn de fāzhǎn zhànlüè. The leadership is strategically planning the company's development strategy for the next phase.

**他精心谋划了这次商业并购行动。** Tā jīngxīn móuhuà le zhècì shāngyè bìnggòu xíngdòng. He carefully devised this commercial acquisition move.

**据报道，犯罪团伙早已开始谋划这次抢劫。** Jù bàodào, fànzuì tuánhuǒ zǎo yǐ kāishǐ móuhuà zhècì qiǎngjié. According to reports, the criminal gang had already begun plotting this robbery long ago.

**她花了数年时间谋划自己的创业之路。** Tā huā le shù nián shíjiān móuhuà zìjǐ de chuàngyè zhī lù. She spent several years strategically planning her entrepreneurial journey.

**国家层面的改革需要周密谋划，不能仓促行事。** Guójiā céngmiàn de gǎigé xūyào zhōumì móuhuà, bù néng cāngcù xíngshì. Reform at the national level requires careful planning and cannot be rushed.

**他被指控谋划推翻公司现任管理层。** Tā bèi zhǐkòng móuhuà tuīfān gōngsī xiànrèn guǎnlǐcéng. He was accused of plotting to overthrow the company's current management.

## Collocations
| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 精心谋划 (jīngxīn móuhuà) | to carefully scheme/plan |
| 周密谋划 (zhōumì móuhuà) | meticulous planning |
| 谋划发展 (móuhuà fāzhǎn) | to plan for development |
| 谋划布局 (móuhuà bùjú) | to plan and position strategically |
| 谋划已久 (móuhuà yǐ jiǔ) | having planned for a long time |

## Usage Notes
谋划 sits between strategic planning (positive) and scheming (neutral to negative). The context and collocates determine the tone: 谋划发展战略 (planning a development strategy) is entirely positive, while 谋划犯罪 (plotting a crime) is negative.

It is more intense and deliberate than 计划 (jìhuà) or 规划 (guīhuà), implying cleverness, calculation, and foresight. At B2 level, use it in formal writing about strategy or in literary/news contexts where long-term plotting is described.

## Memory Hook
谋 (to scheme/plan cleverly) + 划 (to draw/plan): drawing up a clever plan -- to scheme or strategize.
