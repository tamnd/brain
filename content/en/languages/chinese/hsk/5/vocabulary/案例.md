## 案例 (ànlì)

A specific, documented instance or example -- used in legal, medical, academic, or business contexts for analysis, learning, or illustration.

## Meanings
1. **[noun]** case, case study, example instance

## Example Sentences
**法学院学生通过分析经典案例来理解法律原则。** Fǎxuéyuàn xuéshēng tōngguò fēnxī jīngdiǎn ànlì lái lǐjiě fǎlǜ yuánzé. Law school students understand legal principles by analyzing classic cases.

**这个公司的成功案例被写入了多所商学院的教材。** Zhège gōngsī de chénggōng ànlì bèi xiě rùle duō suǒ shāngxuéyuàn de jiàocái. This company's success story has been written into the teaching materials of several business schools.

**医生在病例讨论会上分享了一个罕见的临床案例。** Yīshēng zài bìnglì tǎolùn huì shàng fēnxiāngle yī gè hǎnjiàn de línchuáng ànlì. The doctor shared a rare clinical case at the case discussion meeting.

**这起网络诈骗案例提醒我们要提高信息安全意识。** Zhè qǐ wǎngluò zhàpiàn ànlì tíxǐng wǒmen yào tígāo xìnxī ānquán yìshí. This online fraud case reminds us to raise our information security awareness.

**研究人员分析了数百个案例后得出了初步结论。** Yánjiū rényuán fēnxīle shù bǎi gè ànlì hòu déchūle chūbù jiélùn. The researchers drew preliminary conclusions after analyzing hundreds of cases.

**这项政策的实施效果可以从多个省份的案例中得到印证。** Zhè xiàng zhèngcè de shíshī xiàoguǒ kěyǐ cóng duō gè shěngfèn de ànlì zhōng dédào yìnzhèng. The effectiveness of this policy can be corroborated by cases from multiple provinces.

## Usage Notes
案例 is used in professional and academic contexts. In law, it refers to a specific legal case (similar to 案件, ànjiàn, but 案例 emphasizes its use as a model or teaching example). In business education, 案例教学 (case-based teaching) is a widely used pedagogical method.

案例 differs from 例子 (lìzi, example) in register: 例子 is general and informal, while 案例 is formal and always refers to a real or documented instance rather than a hypothetical illustration.

## Memory Hook
案 = case (criminal or legal case, also a desk/table). 例 = example/precedent. A 案例 is a case that serves as an example -- a real instance documented at the desk of a professional for others to learn from.
