## 有效 (yǒuxiào)

Describes something that works as intended and produces real results -- or something that is legally or formally in force.

## Meanings
1. **[adjective]** effective, efficacious (producing results)
2. **[adjective]** valid, in force (legally or formally binding)

## Example Sentences
**这种药物对治疗失眠非常有效。** Zhè zhǒng yàowù duì zhìliáo shīmián fēicháng yǒuxiào. This medication is very effective for treating insomnia.

**请在有效期内使用您的优惠券。** Qǐng zài yǒuxiào qī nèi shǐyòng nín de yōuhuìquàn. Please use your coupon within its validity period.

**有效沟通是解决团队矛盾的最佳方式。** Yǒuxiào gōutōng shì jiějué tuánduì máodùn de zuìjiā fāngshì. Effective communication is the best way to resolve team conflicts.

**护照是出境时唯一有效的身份证明文件。** Hùzhào shì chūjìng shí wéiyī yǒuxiào de shēnfèn zhèngmíng wénjiàn. A passport is the only valid identity document when leaving the country.

**研究表明，定期锻炼是预防心脏病的有效手段。** Yánjiū biǎomíng, dìngqī duànliàn shì yùfáng xīnzàngbìng de yǒuxiào shǒuduàn. Research shows that regular exercise is an effective means of preventing heart disease.

**这份协议在双方签署后立即生效，为期三年有效。** Zhè fèn xiéyì zài shuāngfāng qiānshǔ hòu lìjí shēngxiào, wéiqī sānnián yǒuxiào. This agreement takes effect immediately upon signing by both parties and is valid for three years.

## Usage Notes
有效 covers two distinct English meanings: "effective" (producing desired results) and "valid" (legally or temporally in force). Context determines which sense applies. 有效期 (validity period) relates to the legal/temporal sense, while 有效方法 (effective method) relates to efficacy.

The antonym is 无效 (wúxiào), meaning "ineffective" or "invalid/null and void." In legal and contractual Chinese, 无效合同 (null and void contract) and 有效期 are standard phrases B2 learners need to know.

## Memory Hook
有 = to have. 效 = effect/result. Something 有效 is something that "has effect" -- it delivers what it promises.
