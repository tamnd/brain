## 机器学习 (jīqì xuéxí)

A subfield of artificial intelligence in which computer systems are trained on data to recognize patterns and make decisions without being explicitly programmed for each task.

## Meanings
1. **[noun]** machine learning

## Example Sentences
**机器学习技术正在彻底改变医学影像诊断的方式。** Jīqì xuéxí jìshù zhèngzài chèdǐ gǎibiàn yīxué yǐngxiàng zhěnduàn de fāngshì. Machine learning technology is fundamentally changing the way medical imaging diagnosis works.

**这款推荐系统基于机器学习算法分析用户的行为数据。** Zhè kuǎn tuījiàn xìtǒng jīyú jīqì xuéxí suànfǎ fēnxī yònghù de xíngwéi shùjù. This recommendation system uses machine learning algorithms to analyze user behavior data.

**越来越多的高校开设了机器学习相关课程。** Yuè lái yuè duō de gāoxiào kāishèle jīqì xuéxí xiāngguān kèchéng. More and more universities have opened machine learning-related courses.

**机器学习模型的准确性在很大程度上取决于训练数据的质量。** Jīqì xuéxí móxíng de zhǔnquèxìng zài hěn dà chéngdù shàng qǔjué yú xùnliàn shùjù de zhìliàng. The accuracy of machine learning models depends to a large extent on the quality of the training data.

**监督学习和无监督学习是机器学习的两种主要方法。** Jiāndū xuéxí hé wú jiāndū xuéxí shì jīqì xuéxí de liǎng zhǒng zhǔyào fāngfǎ. Supervised learning and unsupervised learning are the two main approaches in machine learning.

**在欺诈检测领域，机器学习的表现远优于传统规则系统。** Zài qīzhà jiǎncè lǐngyù, jīqì xuéxí de biǎoxiàn yuǎn yōuyú chuántǒng guīzé xìtǒng. In the field of fraud detection, machine learning far outperforms traditional rule-based systems.

## Usage Notes
机器学习 is a direct calque of the English term "machine learning." It is closely related to 人工智能 (artificial intelligence) and 深度学习 (deep learning) -- with 机器学习 being the broader category and 深度学习 being a specific subtype using neural networks.

In Chinese tech media and academic papers, 机器学习 appears frequently alongside 数据科学 (data science), 算法 (algorithm), and 神经网络 (neural network). B2 learners in STEM or technology fields should treat this as a fixed compound.

## Memory Hook
机器 = machine. 学习 = learning/to study. Machine learning is exactly what it says -- machines that learn. Picture a robot sitting at a school desk, studying endless data to get smarter.
