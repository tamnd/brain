## 相反 (xiāngfǎn)

相反 is used to express opposition or contradiction between two things, either as an adjective describing opposite states or as a discourse marker introducing a contrasting statement.

## Meanings
1. **[adjective]** opposite; contrary; reverse
2. **[adverb/conjunction]** on the contrary; conversely

## Example Sentences
**他们两人的性格截然相反，一个内敛，一个开朗。** Tāmen liǎng rén de xìnggé jiérán xiāngfǎn, yī gè nèilǎn, yī gè kāilǎng. The two of them have completely opposite personalities -- one introverted, one outgoing.

**我以为他会反对，相反，他非常支持这个计划。** Wǒ yǐwéi tā huì fǎnduì, xiāngfǎn, tā fēicháng zhīchí zhège jìhuà. I thought he would object; on the contrary, he strongly supported the plan.

**这两种方法的效果恰好相反。** Zhè liǎng zhǒng fāngfǎ de xiàoguǒ qiàhǎo xiāngfǎn. The effects of these two methods are exactly opposite.

**减少休息时间并没有提高效率，相反，还导致了工作质量下降。** Jiǎnshǎo xiūxi shíjiān bìng méiyǒu tígāo xiàolǜ, xiāngfǎn, hái dǎozhì le gōngzuò zhìliàng xiàjiàng. Reducing rest time did not improve efficiency; on the contrary, it led to a decline in work quality.

**他们两人的观点完全相反，根本无法达成共识。** Tāmen liǎng rén de guāndiǎn wánquán xiāngfǎn, gēnběn wúfǎ dáchéng gòngshí. Their views are completely opposite and they simply cannot reach a consensus.

**事情的发展与预期的恰好相反。** Shìqíng de fāzhǎn yǔ yùqī de qiàhǎo xiāngfǎn. Events developed in exactly the opposite way from what was expected.

## Collocations
| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 截然相反 (jiérán xiāngfǎn) | completely/diametrically opposite |
| 恰好相反 (qiàhǎo xiāngfǎn) | exactly the opposite |
| 方向相反 (fāngxiàng xiāngfǎn) | opposite direction |
| 相反的意见 (xiāngfǎn de yìjiàn) | contrary opinion |
| 与此相反 (yǔ cǐ xiāngfǎn) | in contrast to this |
| 相反地 (xiāngfǎn de) | conversely; on the contrary |

## Usage Notes
相反 serves double duty as both an adjective and a discourse connector. When used as an adjective, it typically follows the noun it modifies or appears in predicate position: 方向相反 (opposite directions), 结果相反 (opposite results).

As a discourse marker, 相反 introduces a statement that contradicts the previous one and is equivalent to "on the contrary" or "conversely." It is slightly more formal than 反而 (fǎn'ér), which focuses on an unexpected reversal.

## Memory Hook
相 (mutually/each other) + 反 (reverse/opposite) -- mutually reversed -- opposite; on the contrary.
