## 合作 (hézuò)

合作 means to work together toward a shared goal, and as a noun it refers to the cooperation or collaboration itself, used widely in business, international relations, and everyday contexts.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to cooperate; to collaborate; to work together
2. **[noun]** cooperation; collaboration; partnership

## Example Sentences

**两国决定在科技领域加强合作。** Liǎng guó juédìng zài kējì lǐngyù jiāqiáng hézuò. The two countries decided to strengthen cooperation in the field of science and technology.

**成功的项目需要团队成员之间的密切合作。** Chénggōng de xiàngmù xūyào tuánduì chéngyuán zhījiān de mìqiè hézuò. A successful project requires close cooperation among team members.

**他们公司与多家国际机构建立了合作关系。** Tāmen gōngsī yǔ duōjiā guójì jīgòu jiànlì le hézuò guānxi. Their company established cooperative relationships with several international organizations.

**我们期待与贵公司展开合作。** Wǒmen qīdài yǔ guì gōngsī zhǎnkāi hézuò. We look forward to beginning cooperation with your company.

**良好的合作精神是职场成功的关键。** Liánghǎo de hézuò jīngshén shì zhíchǎng chénggōng de guānjiàn. A good spirit of cooperation is the key to workplace success.

**这次合作为双方带来了丰厚的利润。** Zhè cì hézuò wèi shuāngfāng dàilái le fēnghòu de lìrùn. This cooperation brought substantial profits for both parties.

## Usage Notes

合作 is a highly versatile word used in nearly every domain -- business, diplomacy, science, education, and daily life. 合 means "to combine/join" and 作 means "to do/work," so 合作 literally means "to join and work together."

It is commonly followed by a complement indicating the domain: 在某领域合作 (cooperate in a field) or 就某问题合作 (cooperate on an issue). In business Chinese, 合作伙伴 (partner) and 建立合作关系 (establish a cooperative relationship) are standard phrases.

## Memory Hook

合 means "join/combine" and 作 means "work/do" -- 合作 is to "join and work," which is exactly what cooperation means.
