## 观点 (guāndiǎn)

观点 is one of the most essential nouns for academic and argumentative writing in Chinese, referring to a person's point of view, perspective, or opinion on a particular matter.

## Meanings
1. **[noun]** viewpoint; point of view; perspective; standpoint

## Example Sentences
**请分享一下你对这个问题的观点。** Qǐng fēnxiǎng yīxià nǐ duì zhège wèntí de guāndiǎn. Please share your viewpoint on this issue.

**双方的观点截然不同，很难达成共识。** Shuāngfāng de guāndiǎn jiérán bùtóng, hěn nán dáchéng gòngshí. The two sides' perspectives are completely different, making it hard to reach consensus.

**他在文章中清楚地阐述了自己的观点。** Tā zài wénzhāng zhōng qīngchǔ de chǎnshùle zìjǐ de guāndiǎn. He clearly expounded his viewpoint in the article.

**从经济学的观点来看，这项政策是合理的。** Cóng jīngjì xué de guāndiǎn lái kàn, zhè xiàng zhèngcè shì hélǐ de. From an economic viewpoint, this policy is reasonable.

**我尊重你的观点，但我有不同的看法。** Wǒ zūnzhòng nǐ de guāndiǎn, dàn wǒ yǒu bùtóng de kànfǎ. I respect your viewpoint, but I have a different opinion.

**这篇论文提出了一个新颖的观点。** Zhè piān lùnwén tíchū le yīgè xīnyǐng de guāndiǎn. This paper puts forward a novel viewpoint.

## Collocations
| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|

| 从...观点看 (cóng...guāndiǎn kàn) | from the perspective of... |

## Usage Notes
观点 is a neutral, formal noun suitable for essays, debates, and academic writing. It is essentially interchangeable with 看法 (kànfǎ) in casual speech, but 观点 is more formal and often used in written or professional contexts. 立场 (lìchǎng, stance/position) emphasizes a committed position, while 观点 can be more tentative or analytical.

The pattern 从...的观点来看 (from the perspective of...) is extremely useful for structuring arguments in Chinese.

## Memory Hook
观 (to observe/view) + 点 (point): the specific point from which you observe -- your viewpoint.
