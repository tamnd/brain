## 洪水 (hóngshuǐ)

洪水 refers to an overflow of a large amount of water beyond its normal confines, especially onto dry land -- a natural disaster that can cause widespread damage to communities and ecosystems.

## Meanings
1. **[noun]** flood; floodwater; inundation

## Example Sentences
**连日暴雨导致河流决堤，洪水淹没了整个村庄。** Lián rì bàoyǔ dǎozhì héliú juédī, hóngshuǐ yānmòle zhěnggè cūnzhuāng. Continuous heavy rain caused the river to burst its banks, and the flood submerged the entire village.

**气候变化使洪水发生的频率和强度都在增加。** Qìhòu biànhuà shǐ hóngshuǐ fāshēng de pínlǜ hé qiángdù dōu zài zēngjiā. Climate change is increasing both the frequency and intensity of floods.

**政府提前发布预警，帮助居民应对即将到来的洪水。** Zhèngfǔ tíqián fābù yùjǐng, bāngzhù jūmín yìngduì jí jiāng dàolái de hóngshuǐ. The government issued early warnings to help residents cope with the approaching flood.

**洪水过后，许多家庭失去了住所，急需救援。** Hóngshuǐ guòhòu, xǔduō jiātíng shīqùle zhùsuǒ, jí xū jiùyuán. After the flood, many families lost their homes and urgently needed relief.

**防洪工程的建设可以有效减少洪水带来的损失。** Fánghóng gōngchéng de jiànshè kěyǐ yǒuxiào jiǎnshǎo hóngshuǐ dàilái de sǔnshī. The construction of flood control projects can effectively reduce flood losses.

**他们用沙袋堆起屏障，试图阻挡汹涌的洪水。** Tāmen yòng shābāo duī qǐ píngzhàng, shìtú zǔdǎng xiōngyǒng de hóngshuǐ. They piled up sandbags to form a barrier, trying to block the surging floodwaters.

## Usage Notes
洪水 is the general term for flood or floodwater. A related compound is 洪涝 (hónglào), which specifically refers to flooding caused by excessive rainfall. In news and disaster-reporting contexts, 洪涝灾害 (flood disaster) is a common phrase.

Note that 洪水 can be used metaphorically in Chinese, as in 信息的洪水 (a flood of information) or 洪水猛兽 (a fierce flood and wild beast -- used idiomatically to mean something extremely dangerous).

## Memory Hook
洪 = flood/vast (water radical + 共 together) -- vast amounts of water coming together -- floodwater.
