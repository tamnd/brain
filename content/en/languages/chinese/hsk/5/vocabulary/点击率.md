## 点击率 (diǎnjī lǜ)

点击率 is a digital marketing and internet term referring to the ratio of users who click on a specific link, advertisement, or piece of content compared to the total number of users who view it.

## Meanings
1. **[noun]** click-through rate (CTR); click rate; number of clicks (as a measure of popularity)

## Example Sentences
**这篇文章的点击率在发布后几小时内就突破了一百万。** Zhè piān wénzhāng de diǎnjī lǜ zài fābù hòu jǐ xiǎoshí nèi jiù tūpòle yī bǎi wàn. This article's click count broke one million within a few hours of publication.

**提高广告的点击率是数字营销团队的重要目标之一。** Tígāo guǎnggào de diǎnjī lǜ shì shùzì yíngyāo tuánduì de zhòngyào mùbiāo zhī yī. Increasing the advertisement click-through rate is one of the important goals of the digital marketing team.

**这个视频标题非常吸引人，因此获得了极高的点击率。** Zhège shìpín biāotí fēicháng xīyǐn rén, yīncǐ huòdéle jí gāo de diǎnjī lǜ. This video's title is very engaging, and it therefore received an extremely high click rate.

**内容质量和标题吸引力是影响文章点击率的两大因素。** Nèiróng zhìliàng hé biāotí xīyǐn lì shì yǐngxiǎng wénzhāng diǎnjī lǜ de liǎng dà yīnsù. Content quality and headline appeal are two major factors influencing an article's click-through rate.

**该平台通过算法推荐高点击率的内容给更多用户。** Gāi píngtái tōngguò suànfǎ tuījiàn gāo diǎnjī lǜ de nèiróng gěi gèng duō yònghù. The platform uses algorithms to recommend high-click content to more users.

**为了提升点击率，运营团队专门优化了文章的封面图和标题。** Wèile tíshēng diǎnjī lǜ, yùnyíng tuánduì zhuānmén yōuhuà le wénzhāng de fēngmiàn tú hé biāotí. To boost click-through rates, the operations team specifically optimized the article's cover image and title.

## Usage Notes
点击率 is a digital age term directly related to internet content. It combines 点击 (to click) and 率 (rate/ratio). In Chinese internet and media contexts, 点击率 can refer both to click-through rate (CTR -- the ratio of clicks to views) and to raw view/click count (sometimes used loosely to mean "how many times something was clicked").

This word is essential vocabulary for anyone working in digital media, e-commerce, or content marketing in China. Related terms include 转化率 (conversion rate), 曝光量 (exposure/impressions), and 流量 (traffic/flow of users).

## Memory Hook
点击 = click (to point and click), 率 = rate -- click rate -- how often people are clicking.
