## 干旱 (gānhàn)

A drought is a prolonged period of abnormally low rainfall that causes a serious shortage of water, threatening agriculture, ecosystems, and communities.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** drought; prolonged dry period with water shortage
2. **[adjective]** arid; dry; drought-affected

## Example Sentences

**严重的干旱导致农作物大量减产。** Yánzhòng de gānhàn dǎozhì nóngzuòwù dàliàng jiǎnchǎn. The severe drought caused a large reduction in crop yields.

**这个地区长期干旱，水资源极度匮乏。** Zhège dìqū chángqī gānhàn, shuǐ zīyuán jídù kuìfá. This region is long affected by drought, with extremely scarce water resources.

**气候变化导致全球许多地区干旱频率增加。** Qìhòu biànhuà dǎozhì quánqiú xǔduō dìqū gānhàn pínlǜ zēngjiā. Climate change is causing the frequency of droughts to increase in many regions around the world.

**政府紧急调拨资金，帮助干旱地区的农民渡过难关。** Zhèngfǔ jǐnjí diàobō zījīn, bāngzhù gānhàn dìqū de nóngmín dùguò nán guān. The government urgently allocated funds to help farmers in drought-affected areas overcome the crisis.

**科学家们正在研发更耐干旱的农作物品种。** Kēxuéjiāmen zhèngzài yánfā gèng nài gānhàn de nóngzuòwù pǐnzhǒng. Scientists are developing more drought-resistant crop varieties.

**为了应对干旱，当地居民建造了更多蓄水设施。** Wèile yìngduì gānhàn, dāngdì jūmín jiànzàole gèng duō xùshuǐ shèshī. To cope with drought, local residents built more water storage facilities.

## Usage Notes

干旱 refers specifically to drought conditions caused by lack of rainfall, distinct from general dryness. It is used in meteorological, agricultural, and environmental contexts. The character 旱 specifically means "drought/dry land," while 干 means "dry."

In news and official reports, you will see 抗旱 (to fight drought) and 旱情 (drought situation/conditions) as common shortened forms. 干旱 can also function attributively: 干旱地区 (drought-affected/arid region), 干旱气候 (arid climate).

## Memory Hook

干 (dry) + 旱 (drought; the character has 日 sun at the top shining down) -- the sun drying everything out, creating a dry, thirsty land.
