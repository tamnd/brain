## 素养 (sùyǎng)

素养 refers to the cultural, moral, or professional cultivation that a person develops through education and practice over time. It implies a deep, internalised quality rather than a mere skill. In modern educational discourse it is often translated as "literacy" or "competency."

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** cultivation; literacy; competency; quality (developed through sustained learning and practice)

## Example Sentences

**信息素养是现代公民在数字时代必须具备的基本能力。** Xìnxī sùyǎng shì xiàndài gōngmín zài shùzì shídài bìxū jùbèi de jīběn nénglì. Information literacy is a basic competency that modern citizens must possess in the digital age.

**教育的目标不仅是传授知识，更是培养学生的综合素养。** Jiàoyù de mùbiāo bùjǐn shì chuánshòu zhīshì, gèng shì péiyǎng xuéshēng de zōnghé sùyǎng. The goal of education is not only to impart knowledge but also to cultivate students' overall competencies.

**一名优秀的医生需要具备扎实的专业知识和良好的职业素养。** Yī míng yōuxiù de yīshēng xūyào jùbèi zhāshí de zhuānyè zhīshì hé liánghǎo de zhíyè sùyǎng. An excellent doctor needs to have solid professional knowledge and good professional ethics and cultivation.

**他多年来坚持阅读，文学素养远超同龄人。** Tā duō nián lái jiānchí yuèdú, wénxué sùyǎng yuǎn chāo tónglíng rén. Having persisted in reading for many years, his literary cultivation far surpasses that of his peers.

**学校将科学素养纳入课程体系，从小培养学生的探究能力。** Xuéxiào jiāng kēxué sùyǎng nàrù kèchéng tǐxì, cóng xiǎo péiyǎng xuéshēng de tànjiū nénglì. Schools have incorporated scientific literacy into the curriculum to cultivate students' inquiry skills from an early age.

**员工的职业素养直接影响企业的服务质量和社会形象。** Yuángōng de zhíyè sùyǎng zhíjiē yǐngxiǎng qǐyè de fúwù zhìliàng hé shèhuì xíngxiàng. Employees' professional cultivation directly affects the quality of a company's services and its public image.

## Usage Notes

素养 is a formal, elevating term. It differs from 能力 (ability, skill -- more task-specific and measurable) in that 素养 implies something deeper: a cultivated quality that is part of one's character. The phrase 核心素养 (core competencies/literacies) is central to current Chinese educational policy discourse.

素养 is almost always used with a domain modifier: 文化素养, 信息素养, etc. Using 素养 alone can sound abstract; context or a modifier is usually provided.

## Memory Hook

素 (plain/original -- one's natural self) + 养 (nourish/cultivate) -- what you have nourished in your natural self through years of cultivation.
