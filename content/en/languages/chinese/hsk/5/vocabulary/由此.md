## 由此 (yóucǐ)

由此 is a formal connective used to draw a conclusion or logical inference from information that has just been presented, common in academic writing and formal speech.

## Meanings
1. **[adverb]** from this; thus; hence; therefore; as a result of this

## Example Sentences
**数据显示销量持续下降，由此可以看出市场需求正在萎缩。** Shùjù xiǎnshì xiāoliàng chíxù xiàjiàng, yóucǐ kěyǐ kàn chū shìchǎng xūqiú zhèngzài wěisuō. Data shows sales continue to fall; hence it can be seen that market demand is shrinking.

**这项研究证明了两者之间存在联系，由此引发了广泛讨论。** Zhè xiàng yánjiū zhèngmíng le liǎng zhě zhījiān cúnzài liánxì, yóucǐ yǐnfā le guǎngfàn tǎolùn. This research proved a connection between the two, thus triggering widespread discussion.

**由此可见，教育投入与经济增长密切相关。** Yóucǐ kě jiàn, jiàoyù tóurù yǔ jīngjì zēngzhǎng mìqiē xiāngguān. From this it can be seen that investment in education is closely related to economic growth.

**他的论证逻辑清晰，由此得出了令人信服的结论。** Tā de lùnzhèng luójí qīngxī, yóucǐ dé chū le lìng rén xìnfú de jiélùn. His argument was logically clear, thus arriving at a convincing conclusion.

**气温持续升高，由此导致冰川加速融化。** Qìwēn chíxù shēnggāo, yóucǐ dǎozhì bīngchuān jiāsù rónghuà. Temperatures continue to rise, hence causing glaciers to melt at an accelerated rate.

**双方签署了协议，由此开启了新一轮合作。** Shuāngfāng qiānshǔ le xiéyì, yóucǐ kāiqǐ le xīn yī lún hézuò. Both sides signed the agreement, thus initiating a new round of cooperation.

## Usage Notes
由此 is a formal and literary connective. It is primarily used in written Chinese -- academic papers, news editorials, and formal reports. In spoken Chinese, 因此 (yīncǐ) or 所以 (suǒyǐ) are more natural alternatives.

The phrase 由此可见 (from this it can be seen) is one of the most common fixed expressions using 由此 and frequently appears in concluding sentences of paragraphs in argumentative writing. Unlike 因此, which simply means "therefore," 由此 places emphasis on the specific evidence or situation just mentioned as the basis for the conclusion.

## Memory Hook
由 (from/due to) + 此 (this) -- from this specific point -- hence, thus.
