## 就此而言 (jiù cǐ ér yán)

This phrase is used to signal that the following statement concerns or elaborates on the point just mentioned, making explicit the scope of the discussion.

## Meanings

1. **[phrase/discourse marker]** in this regard; speaking of this; with respect to this point; as far as this is concerned

## Example Sentences

**就此而言，我们的政策还需要进一步完善。** Jiù cǐ ér yán, wǒmen de zhèngcè hái xūyào jìnyībù wánshàn. In this regard, our policies still need further improvement.

**就此而言，东西方文化之间的差异是显而易见的。** Jiù cǐ ér yán, dōng xīfāng wénhuà zhījiān de chāyì shì xiǎn ér yìjiàn de. In this regard, the differences between Eastern and Western cultures are obvious.

**就此而言，加强国际合作是解决全球问题的必要途径。** Jiù cǐ ér yán, jiāqiáng guójì hézuò shì jiějué quánqiú wèntí de bìyào tújìng. In this regard, strengthening international cooperation is a necessary path to solving global problems.

**就此而言，我们不能忽视教育公平的重要性。** Jiù cǐ ér yán, wǒmen bù néng hūshì jiàoyù gōngpíng de zhòng yào xìng. In this regard, we cannot ignore the importance of educational equity.

**就此而言，技术进步与社会责任必须同步推进。** Jiù cǐ ér yán, jìshù jìnbù yǔ shèhuì zérèn bìxū tóngbù tuījìn. In this regard, technological progress and social responsibility must be advanced in tandem.

**就此而言，个人的选择也会对社会产生深远影响。** Jiù cǐ ér yán, gèrén de xuǎnzé yě huì duì shèhuì chǎnshēng shēnyuǎn yǐngxiǎng. In this regard, individual choices can also have a profound impact on society.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 就此而言，... (jiù cǐ ér yán,...) | in this regard, ... (sentence opener) |
| 就某点而言 (jiù mǒu diǎn ér yán) | with regard to a certain point |
| 就整体而言 (jiù zhěngtǐ ér yán) | overall; on the whole |
| 就目前而言 (jiù mùqián ér yán) | as far as the current situation is concerned |
| 就个人而言 (jiù gèrén ér yán) | as far as an individual is concerned |
| 就长远而言 (jiù chángyuǎn ér yán) | in the long run |

## Usage Notes

就此而言 is a formal discourse connector used to restrict or focus the scope of a statement to the previously mentioned topic. It is most common in academic writing, formal speeches, and analytical essays. It signals to the reader or listener: "within the context of what was just said, the following applies."

The structure 就...而言 is a productive pattern in Chinese: 就目前情况而言 (as far as the current situation is concerned), 就个人而言 (personally speaking), 就整体而言 (overall). Learners should practice using this flexible frame. 就此而言 is simply 就此 (regarding this) + 而言 (speaking of), a fixed but expandable pattern.

## Memory Hook

就 (with regard to) + 此 (this) + 而言 (speaking/saying) -- "Speaking with regard to this very point..." -- a formal way to stay precisely on topic.
