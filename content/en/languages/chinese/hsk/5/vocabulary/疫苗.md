## 疫苗 (yìmiáo)

疫苗 refers to a medical preparation introduced into the body to stimulate immunity against a specific disease, a term that became widely used in everyday Chinese during the COVID-19 pandemic.

## Meanings
1. **[noun]** vaccine; a preparation that provides immunity against a disease

## Example Sentences
**科学家用不到一年的时间就研发出了新冠疫苗。** Kēxuéjiā yòng bù dào yī nián de shíjiān jiù yánfā chū le xīnguān yìmiáo. Scientists developed a COVID-19 vaccine in less than a year.

**接种疫苗是预防传染病最有效的手段之一。** Jiēzhǒng yìmiáo shì yùfáng chuánrǎnbìng zuì yǒuxiào de shǒuduàn zhī yī. Getting vaccinated is one of the most effective means of preventing infectious diseases.

**世界卫生组织呼吁各国加快疫苗分发速度。** Shìjiè Wèishēng Zǔzhī hūyù gèguó jiākuài yìmiáo fēnfā sùdù. The World Health Organization called on countries to speed up vaccine distribution.

**儿童应按时接受规定的疫苗接种计划。** Értóng yīng ànshí jiēshòu guīdìng de yìmiáo jiēzhǒng jìhuà. Children should receive the prescribed vaccination schedule on time.

**疫苗的研发需要经历严格的临床试验阶段。** Yìmiáo de yánfā xūyào jīnglì yángé de línchuáng shìyàn jiēduàn. Vaccine development requires going through rigorous clinical trial stages.

**部分人群对疫苗存在犹豫情绪，影响了整体免疫效果。** Bùfèn rénqún duì yìmiáo cúnzài yóuyù qíngxù, yǐngxiǎng le zhěngtǐ miǎnyì xiàoguǒ. Vaccine hesitancy among some groups has affected overall immunity effectiveness.

## Usage Notes
疫苗 is a formal medical term used in health policy, news, and scientific contexts. The verb most commonly paired with it is 接种 (jiēzhǒng, to vaccinate/inoculate). In informal speech, 打疫苗 (dǎ yìmiáo, to get a vaccine shot) is also widely used.

疫 means epidemic/plague and 苗 means seedling/sprout. The metaphor is of planting a small amount of disease to grow immunity -- a vivid classical coinage that has proven apt for modern immunology.

## Memory Hook
疫 (epidemic) + 苗 (seedling) -- a tiny seedling of the epidemic planted in your body to grow immunity -- a vaccine.
