## 硬件 (yìngjiàn)

硬件 refers to the physical components of a computer system or electronic device, as opposed to software, and is also used figuratively to describe physical infrastructure or tangible resources.

## Meanings
1. **[noun]** hardware (computer/electronic components)
2. **[noun]** physical infrastructure; tangible equipment (figurative)

## Example Sentences
**这台电脑的硬件配置已经无法满足现代游戏的要求。** Zhè tái diànnǎo de yìngjiàn pèizhì yǐjīng wúfǎ mǎnzú xiàndài yóuxì de yāoqiú. The hardware configuration of this computer can no longer meet the requirements of modern games.

**芯片是计算机硬件中最核心的组成部分。** Xīnpiàn shì jìsuànjī yìngjiàn zhōng zuì héxīn de zǔchéng bùfèn. Chips are the most core component of computer hardware.

**该工厂斥资数亿元升级了生产线的硬件设施。** Gāi gōngchǎng chīzī shù yì yuán shēngjí le shēngchǎnxiàn de yìngjiàn shèshī. The factory invested hundreds of millions to upgrade the hardware facilities of its production line.

**硬件的性能瓶颈制约了软件功能的进一步开发。** Yìngjiàn de xìngnéng píngjǐng zhìyuē le ruǎnjiàn gōngnéng de jìnyībù kāifā. Hardware performance bottlenecks constrain the further development of software features.

**这家公司同时开展硬件研发和软件开发业务。** Zhè jiā gōngsī tóngshí kāizhǎn yìngjiàn yánfā hé ruǎnjiàn kāifā yèwù. This company carries out both hardware research and development and software development.

**高校的实验室硬件条件直接影响科研成果的产出。** Gāoxiào de shíyànshì yìngjiàn tiáojiàn zhíjiē yǐngxiǎng kēyán chéngguǒ de chǎnchū. The hardware conditions of university laboratories directly affect research output.

## Collocations

## Usage Notes
硬件 is the direct translation of "hardware" in computing. It always contrasts with 软件 (ruǎnjiàn, software). The pairing 软硬件 (software and hardware) is a very common compound in tech discourse.

In a figurative sense, 硬件 can refer to physical infrastructure and tangible resources in non-tech contexts, such as 教学硬件 (teaching hardware/facilities) or 基础硬件 (basic physical infrastructure).

## Memory Hook
硬 (hard/solid) + 件 (item/piece) -- the solid, physical pieces of a system -- hardware.
