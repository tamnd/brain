## 落后 (luòhòu)

To fall behind in development, progress, or competition is the core sense of 落后, a word used widely in economic, educational, and social contexts.

## Meanings
1. **[verb]** to fall behind; to lag behind
2. **[adjective]** backward; underdeveloped; outdated

## Example Sentences
**他在比赛中落后了五分钟。** Tā zài bǐsài zhōng luòhòu le wǔ fēnzhōng. He fell five minutes behind in the race.

**这个地区的经济发展相对落后。** Zhège dìqū de jīngjì fāzhǎn xiāngduì luòhòu. The economic development of this region is relatively backward.

**如果不学习新技术，企业很快就会落后。** Rúguǒ bù xuéxí xīn jìshù, qǐyè hěn kuài jiù huì luòhòu. If a company does not learn new technologies, it will quickly fall behind.

**落后的基础设施阻碍了当地的发展。** Luòhòu de jīchǔ shèshī zǔ'àile dāngdì de fāzhǎn. Backward infrastructure has hindered local development.

**他努力学习，不想在同学中落后。** Tā nǔlì xuéxí, bù xiǎng zài tóngxué zhōng luòhòu. He studies hard because he does not want to fall behind his classmates.

**改革的目的是消除落后的生产方式。** Gǎigé de mùdì shì xiāochú luòhòu de shēngchǎn fāngshì. The purpose of the reform is to eliminate backward modes of production.

## Collocations
| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 落后地区 (luòhòu dìqū) | underdeveloped region |
| 经济落后 (jīngjì luòhòu) | economically backward |
| 落后于 (luòhòu yú) | to fall behind (something/someone) |
| 技术落后 (jìshù luòhòu) | technologically outdated |
| 甩掉落后 (shuǎidiào luòhòu) | to shed backwardness |

## Usage Notes
落后 can function as both a verb and an attributive adjective. As a verb it often takes a time or degree complement (落后了三分钟, 落后很多). As an adjective it precedes a noun (落后的技术). It carries a mildly negative connotation and is common in formal writing about development and competition.

At the B2 level, pay attention to the structure 落后于 + noun, which means "to lag behind" a specific benchmark, e.g., 落后于世界水平 (to lag behind world standards).

## Memory Hook
Imagine a runner whose foot (落, to fall) is stuck at the back (后) of the track -- always falling behind.
