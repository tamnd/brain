## 认知 (rènzhī)

认知 covers both the process of knowing and understanding (cognition) and the resulting knowledge or awareness one holds, making it a central term in psychology, education, and social commentary.

## Meanings
1. **[noun]** cognition; understanding; awareness
2. **[verb]** to recognize; to perceive; to understand

## Example Sentences
**人工智能研究离不开对人类认知的深入理解。** Réngōng zhìnéng yánjiū lí bù kāi duì rénlèi rènzhī de shēnrù lǐjiě. AI research cannot be separated from a deep understanding of human cognition.

**他对这个问题的认知还比较浅显。** Tā duì zhège wèntí de rènzhī hái bǐjiào qiǎnxiǎn. His understanding of this issue is still relatively shallow.

**认知障碍是阿尔茨海默病的主要症状之一。** Rènzhī zhàng'ài shì ā'ěrcíhǎimò bìng de zhǔyào zhèngzhuàng zhī yī. Cognitive impairment is one of the main symptoms of Alzheimer's disease.

**通过学习，我们可以不断提升自己的认知水平。** Tōngguò xuéxí, wǒmen kěyǐ bùduàn tíshēng zìjǐ de rènzhī shuǐpíng. Through learning, we can continuously raise our level of understanding.

**社会对心理健康问题的认知正在逐步改变。** Shèhuì duì xīnlǐ jiànkāng wèntí de rènzhī zhèngzài zhúbù gǎibiàn. Society's awareness of mental health issues is gradually changing.

**儿童的认知发展分为几个不同阶段。** Értóng de rènzhī fāzhǎn fēn wéi jǐ gè bùtóng jiēduàn. Children's cognitive development is divided into several distinct stages.

## Usage Notes
认知 is a formal, academic-register term used widely in psychology, education policy, and social analysis. As a noun it refers to one's cognitive understanding or awareness of something. As a verb it means to come to know or recognize, though the noun usage is far more common in modern Chinese.

Distinguish 认知 from 了解 (liǎojiě, to understand/know about): 认知 implies a deeper cognitive or psychological process, while 了解 is a more neutral, everyday term for being informed about something.

## Memory Hook
认 (to recognize) + 知 (to know): recognizing and knowing together -- the full process of cognition.
