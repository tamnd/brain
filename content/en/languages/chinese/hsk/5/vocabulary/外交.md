## 外交 (wàijiāo)

外交 refers to diplomacy -- the management of relations between countries through negotiation and communication -- and is a core term in international relations and political science.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** diplomacy; foreign affairs; diplomatic relations
2. **[adjective]** diplomatic; pertaining to foreign affairs

## Example Sentences

**两国之间的外交关系已经正常化。** Liǎng guó zhījiān de wàijiāo guānxi yǐjīng zhèngchánghuà. Diplomatic relations between the two countries have been normalized.

**外交部发言人就此事发表了声明。** Wàijiāo bù fāyánrén jiù cǐ shì fābiǎo le shēngmíng. The Foreign Ministry spokesperson issued a statement on this matter.

**这位总统擅长以外交手段解决争端。** Zhè wèi zǒngtǒng shāncháng yǐ wàijiāo shǒuduàn jiějué zhēngduān. This president is skilled at resolving disputes through diplomatic means.

**该国宣布与邻国断绝外交关系。** Gāi guó xuānbù yǔ lín guó duànjué wàijiāo guānxi. The country announced the severance of diplomatic relations with its neighbor.

**经济外交越来越成为大国竞争的重要工具。** Jīngjì wàijiāo yuè lái yuè chéngwéi dàguó jìngzhēng de zhòngyào gōngjù. Economic diplomacy is increasingly becoming an important tool in great power competition.

**这位大使在任期间为促进双边外交做出了重要贡献。** Zhè wèi dàshǐ zài rènqī jiān wèi cùjìn shuāngbiān wàijiāo zuòchū le zhòngyào gòngxiàn. This ambassador made important contributions to promoting bilateral diplomacy during his tenure.

## Usage Notes

外交 is a formal political term used in government, journalism, and academic discussion of international relations. 外 means "foreign/outside" and 交 means "to exchange/interact," so 外交 literally means "foreign exchange" in the sense of international interaction.

外交部 (Ministry of Foreign Affairs) is the standard name for the foreign ministry in Chinese-speaking countries. The adjective 外交 can modify many nouns: 外交官 (diplomat), 外交途径 (diplomatic channel), 外交豁免权 (diplomatic immunity).

## Memory Hook

外 means "outside/foreign" and 交 means "to exchange/interact." 外交 is exchanging and interacting with foreign countries -- literally what diplomacy does.
