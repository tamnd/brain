## 质疑 (zhìyí)

质疑 means to question or challenge the truth, validity, or authority of something, implying a critical skepticism that goes beyond simple curiosity.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to question; to challenge; to express doubt about
2. **[noun]** a challenge; a critical question; skepticism

## Example Sentences

**研究人员对该实验的方法论提出了质疑。** Yánjiū rényuán duì gāi shíyàn de fānglùn lùn tíchūle zhìyí. Researchers raised questions about the methodology of the experiment.

**公众对政府的处理方式表示质疑。** Gōngzhòng duì zhèngfǔ de chǔlǐ fāngshì biǎoshì zhìyí. The public expressed doubts about the government's handling of the matter.

**他的结论遭到了同行的强烈质疑。** Tā de jiélùn zāodàole tóngháng de qiángliè zhìyí. His conclusions were strongly challenged by peers.

**科学的发展离不开对既有理论的大胆质疑。** Kēxué de fāzhǎn lí bù kāi duì jìyǒu lǐlùn de dǎdǎn zhìyí. Scientific progress cannot be separated from bold questioning of existing theories.

**媒体对这项调查的公正性提出了质疑。** Méitǐ duì zhè xiàng diàochá de gōngzhèngxìng tíchūle zhìyí. The media raised questions about the impartiality of the survey.

**质疑权威需要勇气，但这是独立思考的体现。** Zhìyí quánwēi xūyào yǒngqì, dàn zhè shì dúlì sīkǎo de tǐxiàn. Questioning authority requires courage, but it is a reflection of independent thinking.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |

## Usage Notes

质疑 carries a stronger, more critical connotation than 怀疑 (huáiyí, to suspect/doubt). While 怀疑 can refer to personal suspicion or uncertainty, 质疑 implies a public or formal challenge directed at a claim, authority, or method. It is a key term in academic, journalistic, and democratic discourse, associated with critical thinking.

提出质疑 (to raise a challenge) is the most natural verbal structure. 遭到质疑 (to come under scrutiny/challenge) is common in passive constructions. At B2, learners should use 质疑 in contexts involving institutional or intellectual challenges.

## Memory Hook

ZHI (质, to question/interrogate) + YI (疑, to doubt) = to interrogate something you doubt.
