## 合理 (hélǐ)

合理 means reasonable, rational, or sensible -- describing something that conforms to logic, fairness, or common sense and can be justified.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** reasonable; rational; sensible; justified; appropriate

## Example Sentences

**这个价格非常合理，我们决定接受。** Zhège jiàgé fēicháng hélǐ, wǒmen juédìng jiēshòu. This price is very reasonable, so we decided to accept it.

**他的要求是合理的，管理层应该认真考虑。** Tā de yāoqiú shì hélǐ de, guǎnlǐ céng yīnggāi rènzhēn kǎolǜ. His request is reasonable, and management should consider it seriously.

**合理的饮食结构对健康至关重要。** Hélǐ de yǐnshí jiégòu duì jiànkāng zhìguān zhòngyào. A rational diet structure is crucial to health.

**我们需要制定一个合理的工作计划。** Wǒmen xūyào zhìdìng yī gè hélǐ de gōngzuò jìhuà. We need to formulate a reasonable work plan.

**法律应当在合理范围内保护个人隐私。** Fǎlǜ yīngdāng zài hélǐ fànwéi nèi bǎohù gèrén yǐnsī. The law should protect personal privacy within a reasonable scope.

**资源的合理分配可以减少社会不平等。** Zīyuán de hélǐ fēnpèi kěyǐ jiǎnshǎo shèhuì bù píngděng. Rational allocation of resources can reduce social inequality.

## Usage Notes

合理 (in accordance with reason) contrasts with 合法 (in accordance with the law) -- something can be legal but not reasonable, or reasonable but not legal. 合 means "in accordance with" and 理 means "reason/logic/principle."

合理 is widely used across formal and informal registers to justify decisions, evaluations, and expectations. Its negative form 不合理 (unreasonable) is equally common and is often used in complaints or criticisms.

## Memory Hook

合 means "to conform" and 理 means "reason/logic" -- 合理 means to conform to reason, i.e., to be reasonable.
