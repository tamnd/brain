## 虚拟现实 (xūnǐ xiànshí)

虚拟现实 refers to computer-generated immersive environments that simulate the real world or create entirely fictional spaces, commonly abbreviated as VR in both Chinese and English contexts.

## Meanings
1. **[noun]** virtual reality (VR)

## Example Sentences
**虚拟现实技术正在改变游戏行业。** Xūnǐ xiànshí jìshù zhèngzài gǎibiàn yóuxì hángyè. Virtual reality technology is transforming the gaming industry.

**这家医院用虚拟现实帮助患者进行康复训练。** Zhè jiā yīyuàn yòng xūnǐ xiànshí bāngzhù huànzhě jìnxíng kāngfù xùnliàn. This hospital uses virtual reality to help patients with rehabilitation training.

**佩戴虚拟现实头盔，你可以身临其境地参观博物馆。** Pèidài xūnǐ xiànshí tóukuī, nǐ kěyǐ shēn lín qí jìng de cānguān bówùguǎn. Wearing a virtual reality headset, you can visit a museum as if you were really there.

**虚拟现实与增强现实的界限越来越模糊。** Xūnǐ xiànshí yǔ zēngqiáng xiànshí de jièxiàn yuè lái yuè móhu. The boundary between virtual reality and augmented reality is becoming increasingly blurred.

**许多学校开始引入虚拟现实教学工具。** Xǔduō xuéxiào kāishǐ yǐnrù xūnǐ xiànshí jiāoxué gōngjù. Many schools have begun introducing virtual reality teaching tools.

**他的论文研究的是虚拟现实对社交行为的影响。** Tā de lùnwén yánjiū de shì xūnǐ xiànshí duì shèjiāo xíngwéi de yǐngxiǎng. His thesis studies the impact of virtual reality on social behavior.

## Usage Notes
虚拟现实 is the full formal term; in casual speech and tech writing it is often shortened to VR (spoken as "VR" in Chinese). The modifier 虚拟 (xūnǐ) means "virtual/simulated," while 现实 (xiànshí) means "reality." This compound structure makes the term transparent and easy to remember.

Contrast with 增强现实 (zēngqiáng xiànshí, augmented reality / AR), which overlays digital content onto the real world rather than replacing it entirely.

## Memory Hook
虚 (empty/hollow) + 拟 (to imitate) + 现实 (reality): a hollow imitation of reality -- virtual reality.
