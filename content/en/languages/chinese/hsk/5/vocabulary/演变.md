## 演变 (yǎnbiàn)

演变 describes the gradual evolution or development of something over time, typically used for historical, cultural, or social processes that unfold across extended periods.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to evolve, to develop gradually, to change over time
2. **[noun]** evolution, gradual development, historical change

## Example Sentences

**汉字经过几千年的演变，形成了今天的样貌。** Hànzì jīngguò jǐ qiān nián de yǎnbiàn, xíngchéng le jīntiān de yànmào. Chinese characters evolved over thousands of years to take on their present appearance.

**这场局部冲突有可能演变为更大规模的战争。** Zhè chǎng júbù chōngtū yǒu kěnéng yǎnbiàn wéi gèng dà guīmó de zhànzhēng. This local conflict could potentially evolve into a larger-scale war.

**语言随着时代的发展不断演变。** Yǔyán suízhe shídài de fāzhǎn bùduàn yǎnbiàn. Language evolves continuously as the times develop.

**研究人类社会的演变规律有助于预测未来发展。** Yánjiū rénlèi shèhuì de yǎnbiàn guīlǜ yǒuzhù yú yùcè wèilái fāzhǎn. Studying the laws of human social evolution helps predict future development.

**这一思想运动的演变过程相当复杂。** Zhè yī sīxiǎng yùndòng de yǎnbiàn guòchéng xiāngdāng fùzá. The evolutionary process of this intellectual movement is quite complex.

**小矛盾如果不加以处理，往往会演变成大问题。** Xiǎo máodùn rúguǒ bù jiāyǐ chǔlǐ, wǎngwǎng huì yǎnbiàn chéng dà wèntí. If minor contradictions are not addressed, they tend to evolve into major problems.

## Usage Notes

演变 emphasizes gradual, organic change over time, often on a historical or generational scale. It is closely related to 演化 (evolutionary change in the biological sense) but 演变 is more commonly used for cultural, linguistic, social, and political processes. The pattern 演变为 (to evolve into) is frequently used to describe how one thing develops into another, sometimes in a warning context (小问题演变为大危机).

At B2 level, 演变 is essential for historical analysis and academic writing about long-term processes.

## Memory Hook

演 means to perform or unfold (like a drama unfolding on stage), and 变 means change -- 演变 is change unfolding like a long historical drama, scene by scene.
