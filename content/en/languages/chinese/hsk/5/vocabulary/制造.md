## 制造 (zhìzào)

制造 primarily means to manufacture or produce physical goods on a large or industrial scale, and figuratively can mean to fabricate or create (a false situation, tension, or problem).

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to manufacture; to produce; to make (especially industrially)
2. **[verb]** to fabricate; to engineer (a situation, conflict, or false impression)

## Example Sentences

**这家工厂专门制造汽车零部件。** Zhè jiā gōngchǎng zhuānmén zhìzào qìchē líng bùjiàn. This factory specializes in manufacturing automobile parts.

**"中国制造"已成为全球市场中举足轻重的标签。** "Zhōngguó zhìzào" yǐ chéngwéi quánqiú shìchǎng zhōng jǔzú qīngzhòng de biāoqiān. "Made in China" has become a label of significant weight in the global market.

**有媒体被指责制造虚假新闻，误导公众。** Yǒu méitǐ bèi zhǐzé zhìzào xūjiǎ xīnwén, wùdǎo gōngzhòng. Some media outlets were accused of fabricating false news and misleading the public.

**该事件是有人蓄意制造的政治危机。** Gāi shìjiàn shì yǒurén xùyì zhìzào de zhèngzhì wéijī. The incident was a political crisis deliberately engineered by someone.

**现代工业可以制造出精度极高的机械部件。** Xiàndài gōngyè kěyǐ zhìzào chū jīngdù jí gāo de jīxiè bùjiàn. Modern industry can manufacture mechanical components with extremely high precision.

**战争贩子总是试图制造紧张局势以牟取利益。** Zhànzhēng fànzǐ zǒngshì shìtú zhìzào jǐnzhāng júshì yǐ móuqǔ lìyì. Warmongers always try to engineer tense situations to reap profits.

## Usage Notes

制造 is the standard term for industrial manufacturing and is at the heart of China's economic vocabulary (制造业, the manufacturing industry). In its figurative use, 制造 takes on a negative connotation, implying deliberate fabrication of something false or harmful: 制造谣言 (to spread/fabricate rumors), 制造麻烦 (to cause trouble), 制造危机 (to engineer a crisis).

The distinction between 制造 and 创造 (chuàngzào) is important: 制造 is about production (often industrial, sometimes deceptive), while 创造 implies original creation and inventiveness.

## Memory Hook

ZHI (制, to regulate/control the process) + ZAO (造, to make/build) = to control a process of making things — manufacturing.
