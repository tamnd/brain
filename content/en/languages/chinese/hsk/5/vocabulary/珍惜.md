## 珍惜 (zhēnxī)

珍惜 means to cherish or treasure something -- to value it highly, use it wisely, and not take it for granted. It implies a conscious appreciation of something precious.

## Meanings
1. **[verb]** to cherish; to treasure; to value; to make good use of (something precious)

## Example Sentences
**我们应该珍惜每一段友谊，真正的朋友是人生的宝贵财富。** Wǒmen yīnggāi zhēnxī měi yī duàn yǒuyì, zhēnzhèng de péngyǒu shì rénshēng de bǎoguì cáifù. We should cherish every friendship; true friends are precious treasures in life.

**珍惜时间，合理安排日程，是成功人士的共同特点。** Zhēnxī shíjiān, hélǐ ānpái rìchéng, shì chénggōng rénshì de gòngtóng tèdiǎn. Cherishing time and managing schedules wisely are common traits of successful people.

**我们要珍惜现有的自然资源，不能为了短期利益而过度开发。** Wǒmen yào zhēnxī xiànyǒu de zìrán zīyuán, bù néng wèile duǎnqī lìyì ér guòdù kāifā. We must cherish existing natural resources and not over-exploit them for short-term gains.

**她非常珍惜这次难得的学习机会，每天都刻苦用功。** Tā fēicháng zhēnxī zhè cì nándé de xuéxí jīhuì, měitiān dōu kèkǔ yòng gōng. She cherishes this rare learning opportunity very much and studies hard every day.

**只有经历过失去，才会真正懂得珍惜拥有的一切。** Zhǐyǒu jīnglìguò shīqù, cái huì zhēnzhèng dǒngde zhēnxī yōngyǒu de yīqiè. Only after experiencing loss will one truly understand how to cherish everything one has.

**我们都应该珍惜与家人在一起的每一刻。** Wǒmen dōu yīnggāi zhēnxī yǔ jiārén zài yīqǐ de měi yī kè. We should all cherish every moment spent together with family.

## Usage Notes
珍惜 implies both valuing something and taking good care of it or making good use of it -- it is not just an emotional feeling but also an active behavior. The character 珍 means "precious/treasure" and 惜 means "to value/be sparing with," so 珍惜 captures both the preciousness of the thing and the conscious effort to make the most of it.

Compare 珍惜 with 爱护 (àihù, to take care of/protect) -- 爱护 focuses on physical care and protection, while 珍惜 focuses on truly valuing and not wasting. 珍惜时间 is a very common phrase encouraging people not to waste their time.

## Memory Hook
珍 = precious/treasure (like a jewel), 惜 = be sparing with/value -- treating something like a precious treasure and being careful not to waste it -- to cherish.
