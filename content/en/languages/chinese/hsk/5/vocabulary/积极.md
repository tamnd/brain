## 积极 (jījí)

积极 describes a proactive, enthusiastic, or constructive attitude toward something, as well as the positive effect or impact of an action or change.

## Meanings
1. **[adjective]** positive; proactive; active; enthusiastic
2. **[adjective]** constructive; beneficial (referring to effects or influences)

## Example Sentences
**她对新项目表现出极大的积极性，主动承担了很多任务。** Tā duì xīn xiàngmù biǎoxiàn chū jídà de jījíxìng, zhǔdòng chéngdān le hěn duō rènwù. She showed great enthusiasm for the new project and proactively took on many tasks.

**政府采取了积极的财政政策来刺激经济增长。** Zhèngfǔ cǎiqǔ le jījí de cáizhèng zhèngcè lái cìjī jīngjì zēngzhǎng. The government adopted proactive fiscal policies to stimulate economic growth.

**积极参与课堂讨论有助于加深对知识的理解。** Jījí cānyù kètáng tǎolùn yǒuzhù yú jiāshēn duì zhīshì de lǐjiě. Actively participating in classroom discussions helps deepen understanding of knowledge.

**这项政策对改善民众生活水平起到了积极作用。** Zhè xiàng zhèngcè duì gǎishàn mínzhòng shēnghuó shuǐpíng qǐdào le jījí zuòyòng. This policy has played a positive role in improving people's living standards.

**面对困难，他始终保持积极乐观的心态。** Miànduì kùnnan, tā shǐzhōng bǎochí jījí lèguān de xīntài. Facing difficulties, he always maintained a positive and optimistic mindset.

**积极配合监管要求是企业合规经营的重要体现。** Jījí pèihé jiānguǎn yāoqiú shì qǐyè héguī jīngyíng de zhòngyào tǐxiàn. Actively cooperating with regulatory requirements is an important expression of compliant business operations.

## Usage Notes
积极 has two related senses. As a description of attitude, it means proactive, enthusiastic, or willing to take action. As a description of effects, it means positive or constructive (e.g., 积极作用, 积极影响).

Its antonym is 消极 (xiāojí, negative/passive). Note that 积极 in the sense of "positive effects" is slightly more formal; in everyday speech, 好的影响 (good influence) might be used instead.

## Memory Hook
积 (to accumulate) + 极 (extreme/utmost) -- accumulating to the utmost -- actively pushing forward with full enthusiasm.
