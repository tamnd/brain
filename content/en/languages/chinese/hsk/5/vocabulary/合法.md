## 合法 (héfǎ)

合法 means legal or lawful, describing actions, activities, organizations, or rights that conform to the law and are permitted or recognized by legal authority.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** legal; lawful; legitimate; in accordance with the law

## Example Sentences

**这家公司是经过工商注册的合法企业。** Zhè jiā gōngsī shì jīngguò gōngshāng zhùcè de héfǎ qǐyè. This company is a legally registered business.

**公民有权通过合法途径维护自己的权益。** Gōngmín yǒuquán tōngguò héfǎ tújìng wéihù zìjǐ de quányì. Citizens have the right to protect their interests through legal channels.

**该行为已超出合法范围，将受到法律处罚。** Gāi xíngwéi yǐ chāochū héfǎ fànwéi, jiāng shòudào fǎlǜ chǔfá. This behavior has exceeded legal boundaries and will be subject to legal punishment.

**我们应该确保所有商业活动都是合法的。** Wǒmen yīnggāi quèbǎo suǒyǒu shāngyè huódòng dōu shì héfǎ de. We should ensure that all business activities are legal.

**合法移民享有与公民相近的权利。** Héfǎ yímín xiǎngyǒu yǔ gōngmín xiāngjìn de quánlì. Legal immigrants enjoy rights similar to those of citizens.

**法院裁定这份合同是合法有效的。** Fǎyuàn cáidìng zhè fèn hétong shì héfǎ yǒuxiào de. The court ruled that this contract is legally valid.

## Usage Notes

合法 (legal) and its antonym 非法 (illegal) are essential terms in legal, administrative, and political discourse. 合 means "in accordance with" and 法 means "law," so 合法 literally means "in accordance with the law."

合法 can modify nouns directly (合法权益, lawful rights) or function as a predicate (这是合法的, this is legal). It is more formal than 正当 (proper/legitimate) and specifically refers to conformity with written law rather than general moral rightness.

## Memory Hook

合 means "to conform" and 法 means "law" -- 合法 is to conform to the law, which is what "legal" means in any language.
