## 采访 (cǎifǎng)

采访 refers to the journalistic activity of interviewing people or gathering news, and can function both as a verb (to interview/report on) and a noun (an interview or news coverage), making it essential for media and journalism vocabulary.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to interview; to cover (a news story); to report on
2. **[noun]** an interview; news coverage; a journalistic visit

## Example Sentences

**记者在赛后采访了获胜球队的主教练。** Jìzhě zài sài hòu cǎifǎngle huòshèng qiúduì de zhǔ jiàoliàn. The journalist interviewed the head coach of the winning team after the match.

**她曾多次赴战地采访，报道了大量第一手资讯。** Tā céng duō cì fù zhàndì cǎifǎng, bàodàole dàliàng dì yī shǒu zīxùn. She went to conflict zones for interviews multiple times, reporting a great deal of firsthand information.

**这位演员拒绝接受任何媒体的采访，保护个人隐私。** Zhè wèi yǎnyuán jùjué jiēshòu rènhé méitǐ de cǎifǎng, bǎohù gèrén yǐnsī. This actor refused to accept any media interviews in order to protect personal privacy.

**通过深度采访，记者还原了这次事故的真实经过。** Tōngguò shēndù cǎifǎng, jìzhě huányuánle zhè cì shìgù de zhēnshí jīngguò. Through in-depth interviews, the journalist reconstructed the true course of this accident.

**他在采访中透露了公司即将推出新产品的计划。** Tā zài cǎifǎng zhōng tòulùle gōngsī jí jiāng tuīchū xīn chǎnpǐn de jìhuà. In the interview, he revealed the company's plans to launch a new product.

**当地居民积极配合记者的采访，提供了许多重要线索。** Dāngdì jūmín jījí pèihé jìzhě de cǎifǎng, tígōngle xǔduō zhòngyào xiànsuǒ. Local residents actively cooperated with the journalist's interview, providing many important leads.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 接受采访 (jiēshòu cǎifǎng) | accept an interview |
| 拒绝采访 (jùjué cǎifǎng) | refuse an interview |
| 深度采访 (shēndù cǎifǎng) | in-depth interview |
| 现场采访 (xiànchǎng cǎifǎng) | on-site interview |
| 专访 (zhuānfǎng) | exclusive interview |
| 采访报道 (cǎifǎng bàodào) | interview report/coverage |

## Usage Notes

采访 is specifically a journalistic or media term; it should not be confused with 面试 (miànshì, job interview) or 访谈 (fǎngtán, a conversational interview/talk show format). 采访 always involves a journalist or reporter gathering information from a subject.

The action can flow both ways in the sentence: 记者采访了他 (the journalist interviewed him) and 他接受了采访 (he accepted the interview/gave an interview).

## Memory Hook

采 (to gather/pick) + 访 (to visit/call on) = to visit and gather [news/information].
