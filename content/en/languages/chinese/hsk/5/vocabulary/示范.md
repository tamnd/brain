## 示范 (shìfàn)

示范 means to demonstrate a skill or behavior as a model for others to observe and follow, widely used in education, policy, and professional training.

## Meanings
1. **[verb]** to demonstrate; to set an example; to model
2. **[noun]** demonstration; model example

## Example Sentences
**体育老师亲自示范了正确的跳远姿势。** Tǐyù lǎoshī qīnzì shìfàn le zhèngquè de tiào yuǎn zīshì. The PE teacher personally demonstrated the correct long-jump posture.

**这个试点城市将作为绿色发展的示范，供全国学习借鉴。** Zhège shìdiǎn chéngshì jiāng zuòwéi lǜsè fāzhǎn de shìfàn, gōng quánguó xuéxí jièjiàn. This pilot city will serve as a demonstration of green development for the whole country to learn from.

**领导者应该以身作则，用行动来示范企业文化。** Lǐngdǎozhě yīnggāi yǐ shēn zuò zé, yòng xíngdòng lái shìfàn qǐyè wénhuà. Leaders should lead by example and demonstrate corporate culture through their actions.

**这所学校被评为全国教育改革示范单位。** Zhè suǒ xuéxiào bèi píng wéi quánguó jiàoyù gǎigé shìfàn dānwèi. This school was recognized as a national model unit for education reform.

**师傅一边示范一边讲解，学员很快就掌握了操作步骤。** Shīfu yībiān shìfàn yībiān jiǎngjiě, xuéyuán hěn kuài jiù zhǎngwò le cāozuò bùzhòu. The master demonstrated while explaining, and trainees quickly mastered the operation steps.

**建设示范基地是推广新农业技术的重要方式。** Jiànshè shìfàn jīdì shì tuīguǎng xīn nóngyè jìshù de zhòngyào fāngshì. Establishing demonstration bases is an important way to promote new agricultural technologies.

## Usage Notes
示范 emphasizes modeling behavior or skills for others to emulate. It combines 示 (to show/display) and 范 (model/pattern). In education, it refers to a teacher demonstrating a technique. In policy, it refers to a pilot program serving as a model.

The phrase 起示范作用 (to play a modeling/demonstrative role) is particularly common in formal writing. Compare with 表演 (biǎoyǎn, to perform/act), which is for entertainment rather than instruction.

## Memory Hook
示 (to show) + 范 (model/pattern) -- to show a model -- to demonstrate; set an example.
