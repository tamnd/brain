## 诠释 (quánshì)

诠释 means to interpret or explain something in a thorough, often definitive way, and is used for interpreting texts, concepts, performances, or the meaning of experiences.

## Meanings
1. **[verb]** to interpret; to explain thoroughly; to give an interpretation of
2. **[noun]** interpretation; explanation

## Example Sentences
**这位演员用精湛的表演诠释了这个角色。** Zhè wèi yǎnyuán yòng jīngzhàn de biǎoyǎn quánshì le zhège juésè. This actor gave a superb interpretation of the character through his brilliant performance.

**不同的学者对这段历史有着不同的诠释。** Bùtóng de xuézhě duì zhè duàn lìshǐ yǒuzhe bùtóng de quánshì. Different scholars have different interpretations of this period of history.

**她用自己的亲身经历诠释了坚持的意义。** Tā yòng zìjǐ de qīnshēn jīnglì quánshì le jiānchí de yìyì. She used her own personal experience to interpret the meaning of perseverance.

**这幅画作对传统主题给出了全新的诠释。** Zhè fú huàzuò duì chuántǒng zhǔtí gěichū le quánxīn de quánshì. This painting offers a completely new interpretation of a traditional theme.

**每个人都有权利对人生的价值做出自己的诠释。** Měi gè rén dōu yǒu quánlì duì rénshēng de jiàzhí zuòchū zìjǐ de quánshì. Everyone has the right to offer their own interpretation of life's meaning.

**这首诗的诠释因文化背景不同而各有差异。** Zhè shǒu shī de quánshì yīn wénhuà bèijǐng bùtóng ér gè yǒu chāyì. The interpretation of this poem varies according to different cultural backgrounds.

## Collocations
| Collocation | Meaning |

| 独特诠释 (dútè quánshì) | unique interpretation |

## Usage Notes
诠释 is more formal and literary than 解释 (jiěshì, to explain) or 理解 (lǐjiě, to understand). It implies a fuller, more considered, and often artistic or intellectual unpacking of meaning. It is frequently used in arts criticism (how a performer interprets a role), literary analysis, and philosophical discussion.

In everyday speech 解释 is more common; 诠释 elevates the register and implies depth and completeness of the interpretation.

## Memory Hook
诠 (to explain fully) + 释 (to release/explain): fully releasing the meaning -- a thorough interpretation.
