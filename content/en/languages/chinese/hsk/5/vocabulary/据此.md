## 据此 (jùcǐ)

据此 is a formal connective expression meaning "based on this" or "accordingly," used to introduce a conclusion, decision, or action that is derived from information or conditions mentioned previously.

## Meanings

1. **[discourse marker]** based on this; accordingly; on this basis; therefore (drawing a conclusion from the preceding)

## Example Sentences

**调查结果显示该产品存在严重的安全隐患，相关部门据此决定立即召回所有问题产品。** Diàochá jiéguǒ xiǎnshì gāi chǎnpǐn cúnzài yánzhòng de ānquán yǐnhuàn, xiāngguān bùmén jùcǐ juédìng lìjí zhàohuí suǒyǒu wèntí chǎnpǐn. The investigation results showed serious safety hazards in the product; the relevant authorities accordingly decided to immediately recall all defective products.

**根据合同条款，乙方未能按时交货，甲方据此有权要求赔偿。** Gēnjù hétong tiáokuǎn, yǐfāng wèi néng ànshí jiāo huò, jiǎfāng jùcǐ yǒuquán yāoqiú péicháng. According to the contract terms, Party B failed to deliver on time; Party A accordingly has the right to demand compensation.

**分析数据显示市场需求正在快速增长，公司管理层据此制定了扩张计划。** Fēnxī shùjù xiǎnshì shìchǎng xūqiú zhèngzài kuàisù zēngzhǎng, gōngsī guǎnlǐ céng jùcǐ zhìdìngle kuòzhāng jìhuà. The analytical data showed that market demand is growing rapidly; company management accordingly formulated an expansion plan.

**气象部门发出了台风预警，各地方政府据此启动了应急预案。** Qìxiàng bùmén fāchūle táifēng yùjǐng, gè dìfāng zhèngfǔ jùcǐ qǐdòng le yìngjí yù'àn. The meteorological department issued a typhoon warning; local governments accordingly activated their emergency response plans.

**法院认定被告违反了合同约定，据此判决被告赔偿原告损失五十万元。** Fǎyuàn rèndìng bèigào wéifǎnle hétong yuēdìng, jùcǐ pànjué bèigào péicháng yuángào sǔnshī wǔshí wàn yuán. The court determined that the defendant violated the contractual agreement and accordingly ruled that the defendant compensate the plaintiff five hundred thousand yuan.

**研究人员发现了一种新型基因突变，据此开发出了针对特定癌症类型的靶向药物。** Yánjiū rényuán fāxiànle yī zhǒng xīnxíng jīyīn tūbiàn, jùcǐ kāifā chū le zhēnduì tèdìng áizhèng lèixíng de bǎ xiàng yàowù. Researchers discovered a new type of genetic mutation and accordingly developed targeted drugs for specific cancer types.

## Usage Notes

据此 is a formal, written connective that links a preceding set of facts, evidence, or conditions to a subsequent conclusion, decision, or action. It is closely related to 因此 (therefore/consequently) and 由此 (from this/hence), but 据此 more specifically means "using the preceding as the basis" for the following action or judgment.

据此 appears frequently in legal documents, official reports, academic papers, and news articles. It is more precise than the colloquial 所以 (so/therefore), making it appropriate for formal written contexts at B2 and above.

## Memory Hook

据 (according to/based on) + 此 (this) — 据此 is "based on this," using what was just stated as the foundation for what follows.
