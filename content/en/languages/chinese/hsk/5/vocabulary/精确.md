## 精确 (jīngquè)

精确 describes measurements, data, language, or actions that are exact and free of error. It conveys a higher degree of accuracy than simply 准确 and is common in scientific, technical, and analytical contexts.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** precise; exact; accurate; pinpoint

## Example Sentences

**科学实验要求数据记录必须精确，不能有丝毫误差。** Kēxué shíyàn yāoqiú shùjù jìlù bìxū jīngquè, bù néng yǒu sīháo wùchā. Scientific experiments require that data records be precise, with not the slightest margin of error.

**这台仪器能够精确测量到毫克级别的重量差异。** Zhè tái yíqì nénggòu jīngquè cèliáng dào háokè jíbié de zhòngliàng chāyì. This instrument can precisely measure weight differences down to the milligram level.

**导航系统提供精确的定位信息，帮助司机选择最佳路线。** Dǎoháng xìtǒng tígōng jīngquè de dìngwèi xìnxī, bāngzhù sījī xuǎnzé zuìjiā lùxiàn. The navigation system provides precise positioning information, helping drivers choose the optimal route.

**他对历史事件的年代记忆非常精确，几乎不会出错。** Tā duì lìshǐ shìjiàn de niándài jìyì fēicháng jīngquè, jīhū bù huì chūcuò. His memory of the dates of historical events is extremely precise and he almost never makes a mistake.

**翻译工作不仅要求语言流畅，还要求词义的精确传达。** Fānyì gōngzuò bùjǐn yāoqiú yǔyán liúchàng, hái yāoqiú cíyì de jīngquè chuándá. Translation work requires not only fluent language but also precise conveyance of word meanings.

**军事打击必须精确，以减少对平民区域的附带损伤。** Jūnshì dǎjī bìxū jīngquè, yǐ jiǎnshǎo duì píngmín qūyù de fùdài sǔnshāng. Military strikes must be precise in order to reduce collateral damage to civilian areas.

## Usage Notes

精确 emphasises exactness and the absence of error, often in quantitative or technical contexts. Compare with 准确 (accurate, correct -- more about being right rather than exact) and 精准 (precise and targeted -- often used for policies or interventions). 精确 is the strongest of the three in conveying mathematical or scientific precision.

In formal writing, 精确 frequently modifies abstract nouns: 精确的分析 (precise analysis), 精确的表述 (precise formulation). It can also be used predicatively: 数据很精确 (the data is very precise).

## Memory Hook

精 (refined/elite) + 确 (definite/certain) -- refined down to a certainty, leaving no room for doubt or error.
