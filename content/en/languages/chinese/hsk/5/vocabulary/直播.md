## 直播 (zhíbō)

直播 refers to broadcasting content in real time without pre-recording, a concept that has expanded dramatically with the rise of social media and e-commerce livestreaming in China.

## Meanings
1. **[verb]** to broadcast live; to livestream
2. **[noun]** live broadcast; livestream

## Example Sentences
**这名主播每天晚上通过直播向粉丝展示新产品。** Zhè míng zhǔbō měitiān wǎnshang tōngguò zhíbō xiàng fěnsī zhǎnshì xīn chǎnpǐn. This streamer showcases new products to fans every evening through live broadcasts.

**该体育赛事将在多个平台上进行全程直播。** Gāi tǐyù sàishì jiāng zài duō gè píngtái shàng jìnxíng quánchéng zhíbō. The sporting event will be livestreamed in full on multiple platforms.

**直播带货已经成为中国电商领域的重要销售模式。** Zhíbō dài huò yǐjīng chéngwéi Zhōngguó diànshāng lǐngyù de zhòngyào xiāoshòu móshì. Livestream shopping has become an important sales model in China's e-commerce sector.

**政府会议对外进行了网络直播，提高了透明度。** Zhèngfǔ huìyì duìwài jìnxíng le wǎngluò zhíbō, tígāo le tòumíngdù. Government meetings were livestreamed publicly, improving transparency.

**那场演唱会的直播吸引了超过三千万观众在线观看。** Nà chǎng yǎnchànghui de zhíbō xīyǐn le chāoguò sānqiān wàn guānzhòng zàixiàn guānkàn. The concert livestream attracted more than 30 million viewers online.

**他利用假期进行了一次环球旅行直播，引发广泛关注。** Tā lìyòng jiàqī jìnxíng le yī cì huánqiú lǚxíng zhíbō, yǐnfā guǎngfàn guānzhù. He conducted a round-the-world travel livestream during the holidays, attracting widespread attention.

## Usage Notes
直播 is composed of 直 (direct/straight) and 播 (to broadcast). It contrasts with 录播 (lùbō, recorded broadcast) or 回放 (huífàng, replay). The concept has been transformed by mobile internet in China, where 直播带货 (livestream commerce) became a dominant e-commerce channel.

In digital contexts, 直播 often implies an interactive format where viewers can comment and interact in real time. The term 主播 (zhǔbō, host/streamer) is derived from it.

## Memory Hook
直 (straight/directly) + 播 (to broadcast) -- broadcasting directly without delay -- live broadcast.
