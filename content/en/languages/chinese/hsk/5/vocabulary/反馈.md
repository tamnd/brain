## 反馈 (fǎnkuì)

反馈 means feedback -- information returned in response to an action, product, or performance -- and is widely used in professional, educational, and technical contexts.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** feedback; response; return of information
2. **[verb]** to feed back; to provide feedback; to report back

## Example Sentences

**老师给学生提供了详细的反馈。** Lǎoshī gěi xuésheng tígōng le xiángxì de fǎnkuì. The teacher provided detailed feedback to the students.

**公司鼓励员工对管理层的决策提出反馈。** Gōngsī gǔlì yuángōng duì guǎnlǐ céng de juécè tíchū fǎnkuì. The company encourages employees to provide feedback on management decisions.

**用户的反馈帮助我们改进了产品设计。** Yònghù de fǎnkuì bāngzhù wǒmen gǎijìn le chǎnpǐn shèjì. User feedback helped us improve the product design.

**请在活动结束后填写反馈表。** Qǐng zài huódòng jiéshù hòu tiánxiě fǎnkuì biǎo. Please fill out the feedback form after the event.

**他将调查结果反馈给了上级。** Tā jiāng diàochá jiéguǒ fǎnkuì gěi le shàngjí. He reported the survey results back to his superiors.

**及时的反馈对于学习进步非常重要。** Jíshí de fǎnkuì duìyú xuéxí jìnbù fēicháng zhòngyào. Timely feedback is very important for learning progress.

## Usage Notes

反馈 originally comes from systems theory and engineering, where it describes a signal returned to the input of a system. In modern usage it has broadened to cover any form of response or evaluation. It is a key term in education, product development, management, and customer service contexts.

As a verb, 反馈 is slightly more formal than 回复 (reply) or 回应 (respond). The pattern 将...反馈给 means "to feed back...to" someone, and is common in professional writing.

## Memory Hook

反 means "return/reverse" and 馈 means "to present/give." 反馈 is to give something back -- you return information to the sender, just like feedback loops in nature.
