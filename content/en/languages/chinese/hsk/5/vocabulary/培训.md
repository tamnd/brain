## 培训 (péixùn)

培训 refers specifically to professional, vocational, or skills-based training -- organized instruction aimed at improving specific competencies or job performance.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to train; to provide training for (professional or vocational skills)
2. **[noun]** training; professional training; a training program

## Example Sentences

**新员工入职前需要接受为期两周的培训。** Xīn yuángōng rùzhí qián xūyào jiēshòu wéiqī liǎng zhōu de péixùn. New employees need to undergo two weeks of training before starting work.

**公司每年为员工提供专业技能培训。** Gōngsī měinián wèi yuángōng tígōng zhuānyè jìnéng péixùn. The company provides professional skills training for employees every year.

**这个培训项目帮助了许多失业人员重新就业。** Zhège péixùn xiàngmù bāngzhù le xǔduō shīyè rényuán chóngxīn jiùyè. This training program helped many unemployed people find new jobs.

**她参加了一个为期三个月的管理培训课程。** Tā cānjiā le yī gè wéiqī sān gè yuè de guǎnlǐ péixùn kèchéng. She participated in a three-month management training course.

**在线培训平台降低了学习成本并提高了灵活性。** Zàixiàn péixùn píngtái jiàngdī le xuéxí chéngběn bìng tígāo le línghuó xìng. Online training platforms have reduced learning costs and increased flexibility.

**政府为农村地区提供免费的职业技能培训。** Zhèngfǔ wèi nóngcūn dìqū tígōng miǎnfèi de zhíyè jìnéng péixùn. The government provides free vocational skills training for rural areas.

## Usage Notes

培训 is specifically about practical skills and professional development, distinguishing it from the broader 培养 (to cultivate/develop) and the biological 培育 (to breed/nurture). 培训 is the standard word in HR, business, and government contexts when describing organized instruction to improve job-related skills.

Common collocates include 职业培训 (vocational training), 技能培训 (skills training), and 管理培训 (management training). The word frequently appears in compound nouns: 培训机构 (training institution), 培训课程 (training course), 培训中心 (training center).

## Memory Hook

培 (soil/cultivate) plus 训 (to instruct/train) -- 培训 is to prepare and instruct, like preparing soil and then giving instructions for planting. It is cultivation through structured teaching.
