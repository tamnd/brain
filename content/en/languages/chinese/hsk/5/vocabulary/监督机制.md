## 监督机制 (jiāndū jīzhì)

监督机制 refers to a formal institutional structure designed to ensure accountability, transparency, and proper conduct in organizations or governance systems.

## Meanings
1. **[noun]** oversight mechanism; supervisory mechanism; accountability system

## Example Sentences
**有效的监督机制是防止腐败的重要保障。** Yǒuxiào de jiāndū jīzhì shì fángzhǐ fǔbài de zhòngyào bǎozhàng. An effective oversight mechanism is an important safeguard against corruption.

**公司建立了完善的内部监督机制来规范员工行为。** Gōngsī jiànlì le wánshàn de nèibù jiāndū jīzhì lái guīfàn yuángōng xíngwéi. The company has established a sound internal oversight mechanism to regulate employee conduct.

**缺乏监督机制会使公共资金面临被滥用的风险。** Quēfá jiāndū jīzhì huì shǐ gōnggòng zījīn miànlín bèi lànyòng de fēngxiǎn. The lack of an oversight mechanism exposes public funds to the risk of misuse.

**国际社会呼吁建立更强有力的全球金融监督机制。** Guójì shèhuì hūyù jiànlì gèng qiáng yǒulì de quánqiú jīnróng jiāndū jīzhì. The international community is calling for the establishment of a stronger global financial oversight mechanism.

**这项改革的核心是完善权力运行的监督机制。** Zhè xiàng gǎigé de héxīn shì wánshàn quánlì yùnxíng de jiāndū jīzhì. The core of this reform is to improve the oversight mechanism for the exercise of power.

**独立的监督机制能够提高政府的透明度和公信力。** Dúlì de jiāndū jīzhì néng gòu tígāo zhèngfǔ de tòumíngdù hé gōngxìnlì. An independent oversight mechanism can improve government transparency and credibility.

## Usage Notes
监督机制 is a compound of 监督 (jiāndū, to supervise/oversee) and 机制 (jīzhì, mechanism/system). It appears frequently in governance, corporate compliance, and policy documents. The term emphasizes the structural and systematic nature of oversight rather than one-off supervision.

Compare with 监管 (jiānguǎn), which focuses more on regulatory control by an authority, while 监督机制 highlights the broader institutional framework that enables supervision to function.

## Memory Hook
监督 (supervision/oversight) + 机制 (mechanism/system) -- a systematic mechanism for supervision -- an oversight mechanism.
