## 协调 (xiétiáo)

协调 means to bring different elements, parties, or functions into alignment so they work together smoothly, or to describe a state where things are well-balanced and harmonious.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to coordinate, to harmonize, to bring into alignment
2. **[adjective]** coordinated, harmonious, well-proportioned

## Example Sentences

**项目经理的主要职责之一是协调各部门之间的工作。** Xiàngmù jīnglǐ de zhǔyào zhízé zhī yī shì xiétiáo gè bùmén zhī jiān de gōngzuò. One of the main responsibilities of a project manager is to coordinate work between various departments.

**良好的国际合作需要各国政府协调政策立场。** Liánghǎo de guójì hézuò xūyào gè guó zhèngfǔ xiétiáo zhèngcè lìchǎng. Good international cooperation requires governments to coordinate their policy positions.

**这套家具的颜色和风格非常协调，整体感很强。** Zhè tào jiājù de yánsè hé fēnggé fēicháng xiétiáo, zhěngtǐ gǎn hěn qiáng. The colors and style of this furniture set are very harmonious, creating a strong sense of coherence.

**他在项目中负责协调资源分配和进度管理。** Tā zài xiàngmù zhōng fùzé xiétiáo zīyuán fēnpèi hé jìndù guǎnlǐ. He is responsible for coordinating resource allocation and progress management in the project.

**经济发展与环境保护之间的协调是可持续发展的关键。** Jīngjì fāzhǎn yǔ huánjìng bǎohù zhī jiān de xiétiáo shì kě chíxù fāzhǎn de guānjiàn. Coordination between economic development and environmental protection is the key to sustainable development.

**政府成立了专门的协调机构来统筹管理这一跨部门项目。** Zhèngfǔ chénglì le zhuānmén de xiétiáo jīgòu lái tǒngchóu guǎnlǐ zhè yī kuà bùmén xiàngmù. The government established a dedicated coordination body to oversee this cross-departmental project.

## Usage Notes

协调 functions as both a verb (to coordinate) and an adjective (harmonious/well-balanced). As a verb, it appears in management, diplomacy, and organizational contexts. As an adjective, it describes visual or aesthetic harmony (颜色很协调, the colors are harmonious) or balanced development (协调发展, coordinated/balanced development).

协调 is closely related to 协商 (consultation) and 协作 (cooperation): 协商 emphasizes discussion to reach agreement; 协调 emphasizes making things work together smoothly; 协作 emphasizes working together on a common task.

## Memory Hook

协 means "to cooperate" and 调 means "to adjust/tune" -- 协调 is tuning all the parts to cooperate together, like adjusting each instrument in an orchestra until they play in perfect harmony.
