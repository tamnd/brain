## 威权主义 (wēiquán zhǔyì)

威权主义 refers to a political ideology or system in which power is concentrated in a single authority with limited political freedoms and civil liberties. It is commonly contrasted with democracy and liberalism in political discourse.

## Meanings
1. **[noun]** authoritarianism; a system of governance that prioritizes centralized control over individual freedoms

## Example Sentences
**威权主义政府通常限制公民的言论自由。** Wēiquán zhǔyì zhèngfǔ tōngcháng xiànzhì gōngmín de yánlùn zìyóu. Authoritarian governments typically restrict citizens' freedom of speech.

**许多学者研究威权主义与民主制度的区别。** Xǔduō xuézhě yánjiū wēiquán zhǔyì yǔ mínzhǔ zhìdù de qūbié. Many scholars study the differences between authoritarianism and democratic systems.

**历史上，威权主义往往伴随着对反对声音的压制。** Lìshǐ shàng, wēiquán zhǔyì wǎngwǎng bànsuízhe duì fǎnduì shēngyīn de yāzhì. Historically, authoritarianism has often been accompanied by suppression of dissent.

**经济发展并不能为威权主义提供长久的合法性。** Jīngjì fāzhǎn bìng bù néng wèi wēiquán zhǔyì tígōng chángjiǔ de héfǎxìng. Economic development cannot provide lasting legitimacy for authoritarianism.

**该国的威权主义倾向令国际观察人士感到担忧。** Gāi guó de wēiquán zhǔyì qīngxiàng lìng guójì guānchá rénshì gǎndào dānyōu. The country's authoritarian tendencies have caused concern among international observers.

**公民社会的壮大被认为是抵制威权主义的重要力量。** Gōngmín shèhuì de zhuàngdà bèi rènwéi shì dǐzhì wēiquán zhǔyì de zhòngyào lìliàng. A strong civil society is considered an important force in resisting authoritarianism.

## Usage Notes
威权主义 is the noun form (the ideology or system). The adjective "authoritarian" is often expressed as 威权的 or 威权主义的 before a noun. In academic and journalistic writing, 威权主义 is the standard term; in everyday speech, 独裁 (dictatorship) is sometimes used more loosely as a synonym, though the two concepts differ in degree.

The term carries a strongly negative connotation in contemporary usage. It is frequently paired with 民主 (democracy) as an opposing concept in political science discussions.

## Memory Hook
威 means "power/authority" and 权 means "rights/power" -- 威权 is unchecked power, and 主义 means "-ism", so 威权主义 is the ideology of unchecked power.
