## 驳斥 (bóchì)

驳斥 means to refute or rebuke an argument, claim, or accusation, carrying a strong, forceful tone that implies not only disagreeing but actively countering and dismissing the opposing view.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to refute; to rebuke; to repudiate forcefully
2. **[verb]** to counterargue against; to reject with argument

## Example Sentences

**政府发言人驳斥了媒体的相关报道，称其失实。** Zhèngfǔ fāyán rén bóchìle méitǐ de xiāngguān bàodào, chēng qí shīshí. The government spokesperson refuted the media reports, calling them inaccurate.

**他在文章中驳斥了这种长期流行的错误观点。** Tā zài wénzhāng zhōng bóchìle zhè zhǒng chángqī liúxíng de cuòwù guāndiǎn. In his article, he refuted this long-popular erroneous viewpoint.

**科学界集体驳斥了该研究的核心结论。** Kēxuéjiè jítǐ bóchìle gāi yánjiū de héxīn jiélùn. The scientific community collectively refuted the core conclusions of that research.

**她用详实的数据驳斥了对方的无理指控。** Tā yòng xiángshí de shùjù bóchìle duìfāng de wúlǐ zhǐkòng. She refuted the other party's baseless accusations with detailed data.

**这一理论已被后续研究彻底驳斥。** Zhè yī lǐlùn yǐ bèi hòuxù yánjiū chèdǐ bóchì. This theory has been thoroughly refuted by subsequent research.

**驳斥不实言论是维护公众知情权的重要手段。** Bóchì bùshí yánlùn shì wéihù gōngzhòng zhīqíng quán de zhòngyào shǒuduàn. Refuting false statements is an important means of protecting the public's right to accurate information.

## Usage Notes

驳斥 is more confrontational and decisive than 反驳 (fǎnbó), which is the more neutral term for "to rebut." 驳斥 implies a stronger, more authoritative rejection, often by an institution or recognized authority. The word carries a tone of firmly putting down an erroneous view and is common in political, scientific, and journalistic contexts.

Compare: 反驳 (to rebut; more neutral, used in arguments between equals), 驳斥 (to refute/rebuke; more forceful, often top-down), and 否认 (fǒurèn, to deny; without necessarily providing a counter-argument). At B2, learners should use 驳斥 when the refutation is forceful and backed by evidence.

## Memory Hook

BO (驳, to argue back/rebuke) + CHI (斥, to rebuke/reprimand) = to argue back with a strong rebuke.
