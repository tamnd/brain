## 模型 (móxíng)

A representation -- whether physical, mathematical, or conceptual -- that captures the essential features of a system, object, or process for purposes of understanding, prediction, or communication.

## Meanings
1. **[noun]** model (physical scale model, mathematical model, conceptual framework, AI model)

## Example Sentences
**科学家建立了一个气候变化的数学模型来预测未来趋势。** Kēxuéjiā jiànlìle yī gè qìhòu biànhuà de shùxué móxíng lái yùcè wèilái qūshì. Scientists have built a mathematical model of climate change to predict future trends.

**这款大型语言模型能够生成高质量的自然语言文本。** Zhè kuǎn dàxíng yǔyán móxíng nénggòu shēngchéng gāo zhìliàng de zìrán yǔyán wénběn. This large language model can generate high-quality natural language text.

**建筑师制作了一个精细的建筑模型向客户展示设计方案。** Jiànzhùshī zhìzuòle yī gè jīngxì de jiànzhù móxíng xiàng kèhù zhǎnshì shèjì fāng'àn. The architect made a detailed architectural model to show the design plan to the client.

**商业模型决定了一家公司如何创造并传递价值。** Shāngyè móxíng juédìngle yī jiā gōngsī rúhé chuàngzào bìng chuándì jiàzhí. A business model determines how a company creates and delivers value.

**训练一个高精度的机器学习模型需要大量的高质量数据。** Xùnliàn yī gè gāo jīngdù de jīqì xuéxí móxíng xūyào dàliàng de gāo zhìliàng shùjù. Training a high-accuracy machine learning model requires large amounts of high-quality data.

**经济学家用不同的理论模型来解释市场行为。** Jīngjìxuéjiā yòng bùtóng de lǐlùn móxíng lái jiěshì shìchǎng xíngwéi. Economists use different theoretical models to explain market behavior.

## Usage Notes
模型 is used across a wide range of domains. In AI and machine learning (the most current and prominent use), it refers to trained computational models. In science it refers to mathematical or physical representations. In architecture and product design it refers to scale models.

At B2 level, 模型 is particularly important in the context of AI discourse: 大型语言模型 (large language model, LLM), 模型训练 (model training), and 模型评估 (model evaluation) are standard phrases in Chinese tech journalism and academic writing.

## Memory Hook
模 = mold/template (模仿 = to imitate, following a mold). 型 = shape/form/type. A 模型 is a shape based on a template -- a constructed form that captures the essential pattern of something real.
