## 报道 (bàodào)

报道 refers to the act of reporting news or information by media outlets, as well as the resulting news report, coverage, or story itself.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to report; to cover (news events); to make a report
2. **[noun]** news report; coverage; reporting

## Example Sentences

**这家报社派遣记者前往灾区进行实地报道，让全国人民了解灾情的真实情况。** Zhè jiā bàoshè pàiqiǎn jìzhě qiánwǎng zāiqū jìnxíng shídì bàodào, ràng quánguó rénmín liǎojiě zāiqíng de zhēnshí qíngkuàng. This newspaper sent reporters to the disaster area for on-site reporting, allowing people across the country to understand the true situation.

**根据最新报道，该地区的洪水已经导致数千人被迫转移。** Gēnjù zuìxīn bàodào, gāi dìqū de hóngshuǐ yǐjīng dǎozhì shùqiān rén bèipò zhuǎnyí. According to the latest reports, floods in that area have already forced thousands of people to relocate.

**媒体对这起丑闻进行了大量报道，迫使相关官员公开道歉并辞职。** Méitǐ duì zhè qǐ chǒuwén jìnxíngle dàliàng bàodào, pòshǐ xiāngguān guānyuán gōngkāi dàoqiàn bìng cízhí. The media conducted extensive coverage of this scandal, forcing the relevant officials to publicly apologize and resign.

**新闻报道必须坚持客观公正的原则，不得为了吸引眼球而歪曲事实。** Xīnwén bàodào bìxū jiānchí kèguān gōngzhèng de yuánzé, bùdé wèile xīyǐn yǎnqiú ér wāiqū shìshí. News reporting must uphold the principle of objectivity and fairness and must not distort facts in order to attract attention.

**这篇深度报道揭露了食品供应链中存在的严重安全隐患。** Zhè piān shēndù bàodào jiēlùle shípǐn gōngyìng liàn zhōng cúnzài de yánzhòng ānquán yǐnhuàn. This in-depth report exposed serious safety hazards in the food supply chain.

**在重大事件发生后，记者们争分夺秒，力求第一时间向公众报道最新进展。** Zài zhòngdà shìjiàn fāshēng hòu, jìzhěmen zhēng fēn duō miǎo, lìqiú dì yī shíjiān xiàng gōngzhòng bàodào zuìxīn jìnzhǎn. After a major event occurs, journalists race against the clock, striving to report the latest developments to the public at the earliest possible moment.

## Usage Notes

报道 can function as both a verb (to report) and a noun (a report/coverage). The very similar term 报告 (bàogào) also means report, but 报告 is more formal and refers to official written reports, speeches, or findings (annual report, work report), while 报道 is specifically for journalism and news coverage.

The phrase 据报道 (according to reports; reportedly) is extremely common in written Chinese, used to cite news sources or unverified information. This is important for B2 learners reading Chinese news.

## Memory Hook

报 (to report/announce) + 道 (the way/method) — 报道 is the way of reporting, the method of bringing news to the public.
