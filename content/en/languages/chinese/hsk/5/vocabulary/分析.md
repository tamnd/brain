## 分析 (fēnxī)

分析 means to break something down into its component parts in order to understand it better, and is one of the most essential terms in academic, scientific, and professional Chinese.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to analyze; to examine in detail
2. **[noun]** analysis; a systematic examination

## Example Sentences

**我们需要仔细分析这份数据。** Wǒmen xūyào zǐxì fēnxī zhè fèn shùjù. We need to carefully analyze this data.

**报告对市场趋势进行了深入分析。** Bàogào duì shìchǎng qūshì jìnxíngle shēnrù fēnxī. The report conducted an in-depth analysis of market trends.

**他从多个角度分析了这一问题。** Tā cóng duō gè jiǎodù fēnxīle zhè yī wèntí. He analyzed the issue from multiple perspectives.

**科学家们正在分析实验结果。** Kēxuéjiāmen zhèngzài fēnxī shíyàn jiéguǒ. The scientists are analyzing the experimental results.

**这篇文章对历史事件作出了客观的分析。** Zhè piān wénzhāng duì lìshǐ shìjiàn zuòchūle kèguān de fēnxī. This article offers an objective analysis of historical events.

**通过分析，我们发现了问题的根本原因。** Tōngguò fēnxī, wǒmen fāxiànle wèntí de gēnběn yuányīn. Through analysis, we identified the root cause of the problem.

## Usage Notes

分析 is a formal, versatile word used extensively in academic writing, business reports, journalism, and scientific contexts. As a verb, it takes an object directly (分析问题, 分析数据). As a noun, it frequently appears in constructions like 对……进行分析 (to conduct an analysis of...) or 作出分析 (to make an analysis). It implies a methodical, rational process and carries a more intellectual register than everyday words like 看 or 想.

Compared with 研究 (yánjiū, to research/study), 分析 focuses specifically on the act of breaking something down and examining its parts, while 研究 implies a broader, longer-term investigation. At B2 level, learners should practice using 分析 in both verbal and nominal roles in formal sentences.

## Memory Hook

Imagine a scientist who FEN (分, divides) a sample into pieces and then XIs (析, splits) each piece apart under a microscope — that is analysis.
