## 身份认同 (shēnfèn rèntóng)

身份认同 refers to how a person understands and defines themselves in relation to others, their culture, nationality, or social roles. It is a key concept in psychology, sociology, and cultural studies. A stable 身份认同 is considered important for mental well-being and social belonging.

## Meanings
1. **noun** identity, sense of self, identification with a social group or role

## Example Sentences
**青少年时期是身份认同形成的关键阶段。** Qīngshàonián shíqī shì shēnfèn rèntóng xíngchéng de guānjiàn jiēduàn. Adolescence is a key stage in the formation of identity.

**移民常常面临身份认同的困惑。** Yímín chángcháng miànlín shēnfèn rèntóng de kùnhuò. Immigrants often face confusion about their identity.

**文化对个人的身份认同有深远影响。** Wénhuà duì gèrén de shēnfèn rèntóng yǒu shēnyuǎn yǐngxiǎng. Culture has a profound impact on personal identity.

**她通过写作来探索自己的身份认同。** Tā tōngguò xiězuò lái tànsuǒ zìjǐ de shēnfèn rèntóng. She explores her identity through writing.

**身份认同危机会导致心理压力。** Shēnfèn rèntóng wēijī huì dǎozhì xīnlǐ yālì. An identity crisis can lead to psychological stress.

**语言是维持民族身份认同的重要工具。** Yǔyán shì wéichí mínzú shēnfèn rèntóng de zhòngyào gōngjù. Language is an important tool for maintaining ethnic identity.

## Usage Notes
身份认同 is a formal academic term borrowed from psychology and sociology. 身份 means "identity/status" and 认同 means "to identify with/to acknowledge." Together they translate the English concept of "identity" as used in social science.

In everyday Chinese, people may simply say 认同感 (sense of belonging/identification) in casual contexts. 身份认同 is more often found in academic papers, journalism, and formal discussions.

## Memory Hook
身份 means "identity/status" and 认同 means "to identify with" — 身份认同 is identifying with your own identity.
