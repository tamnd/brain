## 治理 (zhìlǐ)

治理 means to govern, administer, or manage in a systematic and controlled way. It is used both for political governance and for the management of complex issues such as environmental problems.

## Meanings
1. **[verb]** to govern; to administer; to manage; to bring under control
2. **[noun]** governance; administration; management

## Example Sentences
**良好的国家治理是社会稳定的基础。** Liánghǎo de guójiā zhìlǐ shì shèhuì wěndìng de jīchǔ. Good national governance is the foundation of social stability.

**政府正在采取措施治理交通拥堵问题。** Zhèngfǔ zhèngzài cǎiqǔ cuòshī zhìlǐ jiāotōng yōngdǔ wèntí. The government is taking measures to manage the traffic congestion problem.

**公司治理结构的完善有助于提高企业竞争力。** Gōngsī zhìlǐ jiégòu de wánshàn yǒuzhù yú tígāo qǐyè jìngzhēnglì. Improving corporate governance structures helps enhance corporate competitiveness.

**水土流失的治理需要长期坚持不懈的努力。** Shuǐtǔ liúshī de zhìlǐ xūyào chángqī jiānchí bùxiè de nǔlì. Managing soil erosion requires sustained long-term efforts.

**全球治理体系面临着前所未有的挑战。** Quánqiú zhìlǐ tǐxì miànlínzhe qiánsuǒwèiyǒu de tiǎozhàn. The global governance system is facing unprecedented challenges.

**环境污染的治理需要政府、企业和公众的共同参与。** Huánjìng wūrǎn de zhìlǐ xūyào zhèngfǔ, qǐyè hé gōngzhòng de gòngtóng cānyù. Managing environmental pollution requires the joint participation of government, businesses, and the public.

## Usage Notes
治理 is a high-register term used in political science, environmental policy, and corporate management. It implies structured, systematic control and improvement over time, rather than simple day-to-day management. The word is often used to describe long-term efforts to address complex, systemic problems.

Distinguish 治理 from 管理 (guǎnlǐ, management): 管理 is the broader, more everyday word for managing people, tasks, or resources, while 治理 carries a stronger sense of bringing order to something that was previously disordered or problematic, and has a more governmental or institutional tone.

## Memory Hook
治 means "to control/cure/govern" (used in 治病 -- cure disease) and 理 means "to put in order/reason" -- 治理 is governing something back to an orderly, reasonable state.
