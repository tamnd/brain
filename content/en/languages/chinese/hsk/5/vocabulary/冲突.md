## 冲突 (chōngtū)

冲突 refers to a clash, disagreement, or collision between opposing forces, interests, values, or people, ranging from personal disputes to international conflicts.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** conflict, clash, confrontation
2. **[verb]** to conflict with, to clash

## Example Sentences

**两国之间的边境冲突已持续了数月。** Liǎng guó zhī jiān de biānjìng chōngtū yǐ chíxù le shù yuè. The border conflict between the two countries has lasted for several months.

**他的个人价值观与公司文化产生了严重冲突。** Tā de gèrén jiàzhíguān yǔ gōngsī wénhuà chǎnshēng le yánzhòng chōngtū. His personal values came into serious conflict with the company culture.

**通过对话解决冲突比诉诸武力更为明智。** Tōngguò duìhuà jiějué chōngtū bǐ sùzhū wǔlì gèngwéi míngzhì. Resolving conflicts through dialogue is wiser than resorting to force.

**工作与家庭之间的时间冲突让他感到十分疲惫。** Gōngzuò yǔ jiātíng zhī jiān de shíjiān chōngtū ràng tā gǎndào shífēn píbèi. The time conflict between work and family left him exhausted.

**文化冲突在全球化背景下是一个普遍存在的挑战。** Wénhuà chōngtū zài quánqiúhuà bèijǐng xià shì yī gè pǔbiàn cúnzài de tiǎozhàn. Cultural conflict is a universally existing challenge in the context of globalization.

**双方的利益冲突使得谈判陷入了僵局。** Shuāngfāng de lìyì chōngtū shǐ de tánpàn xiàn rù le jiāngjú. The conflict of interests between the two sides pushed the negotiations into a deadlock.

## Usage Notes

冲突 can describe anything from a minor disagreement to a full-scale war. The key is the opposition or collision of forces. As a verb, it appears in structures like 与...冲突 (to conflict with) or 产生冲突 (to create a conflict). In academic Chinese, 冲突 appears frequently in discussions of sociology, international relations, and psychology.

The word 矛盾 (máodùn, contradiction/conflict) is related but broader: 矛盾 refers to contradictions and tensions in general (including philosophical contradictions), while 冲突 implies a more active, direct clash. You would say 内心矛盾 (inner contradiction/ambivalence) but 武装冲突 (armed conflict) -- not the other way around.

## Memory Hook

冲 means "to rush/surge" and 突 means "to break through/burst" -- 冲突 is two forces rushing and bursting into each other, a head-on collision of opposing energies.
