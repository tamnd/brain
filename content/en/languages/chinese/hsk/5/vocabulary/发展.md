## 发展 (fāzhǎn)

发展 refers to development, growth, or advancement, implying movement toward a more complete, refined, or advanced state, and is one of the most widely used terms in Chinese discourse on progress.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to develop, to grow, to advance, to expand
2. **[noun]** development, growth, progress

## Example Sentences

**中国经济在过去四十年里取得了举世瞩目的发展。** Zhōngguó jīngjì zài guòqù sìshí nián lǐ qǔdé le jǔshì zhǔmù de fāzhǎn. China's economy has achieved remarkable development in the past forty years.

**教育的发展对一个国家的未来至关重要。** Jiàoyù de fāzhǎn duì yīgè guójiā de wèilái zhì guān zhòng yào. The development of education is of vital importance to a country's future.

**这家公司正在积极发展海外市场。** Zhè jiā gōngsī zhèngzài jījí fāzhǎn hǎiwài shìchǎng. This company is actively developing overseas markets.

**可持续发展是当今全球面临的共同课题。** Kě chíxù fāzhǎn shì dāngjīn quánqiú miànlín de gòngtóng kètí. Sustainable development is a common topic facing the entire world today.

**他的职业发展受到了这次机会的重大影响。** Tā de zhíyè fāzhǎn shòudào le zhè cì jīhuì de zhòngdà yǐngxiǎng. His career development was significantly influenced by this opportunity.

**科技的迅猛发展深刻改变了人类社会。** Kējì de xùnměng fāzhǎn shēnkè gǎibiàn le rénlèi shèhuì. The rapid development of technology has profoundly changed human society.

## Usage Notes

发展 is an extremely high-frequency word in Chinese, appearing in virtually every domain from politics to education to technology. At B2 level, learners should be aware that it can be used both as a verb (发展经济 -- to develop the economy) and as a noun (经济发展 -- economic development). The modifier 可持续 (sustainable) has become inseparable from 发展 in contemporary discourse.

发展 generally has a positive connotation, implying purposeful progress toward a better state, unlike the more neutral 变化 (change).

## Memory Hook

发 means to send out or to start, and 展 means to spread out -- 发展 is like seeds sent out that spread and grow into something larger and more complete.
