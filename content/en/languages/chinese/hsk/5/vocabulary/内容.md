## 内容 (nèiróng)

内容 refers to the substance, information, or material contained within something -- a book, speech, website, course, or any form of communication.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** content, substance, subject matter

## Example Sentences

**这本书的内容非常丰富，涵盖了很多领域的知识。** Zhè běn shū de nèiróng fēicháng fēngfù, hángài le hěn duō lǐngyù de zhīshi. The content of this book is very rich and covers knowledge from many fields.

**演讲的内容应该与听众的需求紧密相关。** Yǎnjiǎng de nèiróng yīnggāi yǔ tīngzhòng de xūqiú jǐnmì xiāngguān. The content of a speech should be closely related to the needs of the audience.

**社交媒体平台需要严格审查用户发布的内容。** Shèjiāo méitǐ píngtái xūyào yángé shěnchá yònghù fābù de nèiróng. Social media platforms need to strictly review the content posted by users.

**这门课的教学内容已经更新，加入了很多新案例。** Zhè mén kè de jiàoxué nèiróng yǐjīng gēngxīn, jiārù le hěn duō xīn ànlì. The course content has been updated and many new cases have been added.

**形式再好，也要有扎实的内容作为支撑。** Xíngshì zài hǎo, yě yào yǒu zhāshi de nèiróng zuòwéi zhīchēng. No matter how good the format, it needs solid content to back it up.

**这份报告的内容详实，数据充分，很有说服力。** Zhè fèn bàogào de nèiróng xiángshí, shùjù chōngfèn, hěn yǒu shuōfú lì. The report's content is thorough, data is sufficient, and it is very persuasive.

## Usage Notes

内容 is one of the most frequently used nouns in Chinese, functioning like the English word "content" in almost all contexts -- books, courses, media, discussions, and digital platforms. The growing field of 内容创作 (content creation) and 内容营销 (content marketing) has made 内容 even more prominent in digital Chinese.

A useful distinction is between 形式 (form, format) and 内容 (content, substance). The phrase 形式与内容 (form and content) is a classic rhetorical pairing, and the saying that 内容决定形式 (content determines form) is a principle in aesthetics and communication.

## Memory Hook

内 is "inside" and 容 means "to hold/contain" -- 内容 is what is held inside, the substance filling the container, the meaning filling the words.
