## 论坛 (lùntán)

论坛 refers to any organized platform for discussion and debate, from high-level international conferences to internet message boards, making it one of the most versatile nouns in modern Chinese.

## Meanings
1. **[noun]** forum; symposium (physical gathering for discussion)
2. **[noun]** online forum; discussion board (internet context)

## Example Sentences
**世界经济论坛每年在达沃斯举行。** Shìjiè jīngjì lùntán měi nián zài Dáwòsī jǔxíng. The World Economic Forum is held annually in Davos.

**他在国际学术论坛上发表了重要演讲。** Tā zài guójì xuéshù lùntán shàng fābiǎo le zhòngyào yǎnjiǎng. He delivered an important speech at an international academic forum.

**这个在线论坛吸引了数百万用户参与讨论。** Zhège zàixiàn lùntán xīyǐn le shù bǎi wàn yònghù cānyù tǎolùn. This online forum has attracted millions of users to participate in discussions.

**我在论坛上看到了一篇关于这个问题的详细分析。** Wǒ zài lùntán shàng kàndào le yī piān guānyú zhège wèntí de xiángxì fēnxī. I found a detailed analysis of this issue on the forum.

**政府举办了一场关于环保的公众论坛。** Zhèngfǔ jǔbàn le yī chǎng guānyú huánbǎo de gōngzhòng lùntán. The government organized a public forum on environmental protection.

**网络论坛是年轻人表达意见的重要平台。** Wǎngluò lùntán shì niánqīng rén biǎodá yìjiàn de zhòngyào píngtái. Online forums are an important platform for young people to express their opinions.

## Usage Notes
论坛 is used in two distinct but related senses. In formal contexts it refers to high-level symposia, conferences, or panel discussions. In internet contexts it is equivalent to the English "forum" or "message board." The context usually makes the meaning clear.

Note the association with 发言 (fāyán, to speak/make a statement) and 参与 (cānyù, to participate) in both formal and online contexts.

## Memory Hook
论 (to discuss/argue) + 坛 (a raised platform/altar): a raised platform for argument and discussion -- a forum.
