## 赞同 (zàntóng)

赞同 expresses active agreement with or approval of someone's opinion, proposal, or plan, and is widely used in formal discussion, debate, and written argument at B2 level.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to agree with; to approve of; to endorse
2. **[noun]** agreement; approval; endorsement

## Example Sentences

**我完全赞同你的观点，这个方案非常合理。** Wǒ wánquán zàntóng nǐ de guāndiǎn, zhège fāng'àn fēicháng hélǐ. I fully agree with your view; this plan is very reasonable.

**大多数委员投票赞同这项新规定。** Dàduōshù wěiyuán tóupiào zàntóng zhè xiàng xīn guīdìng. Most committee members voted to approve this new regulation.

**他不赞同用牺牲环境来换取经济增长。** Tā bù zàntóng yòng xīshēng huánjìng lái huànqǔ jīngjì zēngzhǎng. He does not agree with sacrificing the environment in exchange for economic growth.

**这一观点得到了与会专家的普遍赞同。** Zhè yī guāndiǎn dédàole yǔhuì zhuānjiā de pǔbiàn zàntóng. This viewpoint received broad agreement from the experts present.

**她点头表示赞同，没有提出任何异议。** Tā diǎntóu biǎoshì zàntóng, méiyǒu tíchū rènhé yìyì. She nodded in agreement without raising any objections.

**领导层赞同了这份改革方案，并决定尽快实施。** Lǐngdǎo céng zàntóngle zhè fèn gǎigé fāng'àn, bìng juédìng jǐnkuài shíshī. The leadership approved the reform plan and decided to implement it as soon as possible.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|

| 普遍赞同 (pǔbiàn zàntóng) | general agreement |
| 投票赞同 (tóupiào zàntóng) | vote to approve |
| 赞同意见 (zàntóng yìjiàn) | agree with an opinion |
| 不赞同 (bù zàntóng) | disagree; disapprove |

## Usage Notes

赞同 is more formal than 同意 (tóngyì) and is preferred in written and official contexts. While 同意 can mean simply "okay, agreed", 赞同 implies a more considered, positive endorsement. It is distinct from 批准 (pīzhǔn, to officially approve/authorise), which is used only by an authority granting permission.

At B2, practise using 赞同 in argumentative writing to acknowledge others' points: 我赞同...这一观点 (I agree with...this viewpoint).

## Memory Hook

赞 (praise) + 同 (same/together) = to praise and join together with someone's idea.
