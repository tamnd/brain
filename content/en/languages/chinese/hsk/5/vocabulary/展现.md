## 展现 (zhǎnxiàn)

展现 means to present or display something before others, especially in a way that allows its full scope or beauty to be seen, and is widely used in descriptive, artistic, and formal contexts.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to display; to present; to bring into full view
2. **[verb]** to unfold; to reveal gradually (a scene, quality, or situation)

## Example Sentences

**这部纪录片展现了西藏的壮丽自然风光。** Zhè bù jìlùpiàn zhǎnxiànle Xīzàng de zhuànglì zìrán fēngguāng. This documentary displayed the magnificent natural scenery of Tibet.

**她的表演充分展现了人物内心的复杂情感。** Tā de biǎoyǎn chōngfèn zhǎnxiànle rénwù nèixīn de fùzá qínggǎn. Her performance fully displayed the complex inner emotions of the character.

**这次展览展现了近年来中国当代艺术的最新成就。** Zhè cì zhǎnlǎn zhǎnxiànle jìn nián lái Zhōngguó dāngdài yìshù de zuìxīn chéngjiù. This exhibition displayed the latest achievements of contemporary Chinese art in recent years.

**随着改革的深入，新的发展机遇逐渐展现在人们面前。** Suízhe gǎigé de shēnrù, xīn de fāzhǎn jīyù zhújiàn zhǎnxiàn zài rénmen miànqián. As the reform deepened, new development opportunities gradually unfolded before people.

**他的演讲生动地展现了团队合作的重要性。** Tā de yǎnjiǎng shēngdòng de zhǎnxiànle tuánduì hézuò de zhòngyàoxìng. His speech vividly presented the importance of teamwork.

**这幅画完美地展现了江南水乡的诗意之美。** Zhè fú huà wánměi de zhǎnxiànle Jiāngnán shuǐxiāng de shīyì zhī měi. This painting perfectly displayed the poetic beauty of Jiangnan's water towns.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 充分展现 (chōngfèn zhǎnxiàn) | fully display |
| 展现风采 (zhǎnxiàn fēngcǎi) | show off one's style/charm |
| 展现才华 (zhǎnxiàn cáihuá) | display one's talent |

| 真实展现 (zhēnshí zhǎnxiàn) | authentically present |

## Usage Notes

展现 suggests a visual or perceptual quality to the display, as if unrolling a scroll or opening a curtain to reveal something to an audience. It is more dynamic and evocative than 显示 (xiǎnshì, to show/indicate) and more formal than 表现 (biǎoxiàn, to perform/show). It often appears in literary descriptions, exhibition contexts, and formal presentations.

The phrase 展现在……面前 (to unfold before someone) is a common literary structure. 展现风采 (to display one's best qualities) is a frequently used phrase in formal contexts like competitions and performances.

## Memory Hook

ZHAN (展, to open/spread out) + XIAN (现, to appear/emerge) = to spread something out so it fully appears before you.
