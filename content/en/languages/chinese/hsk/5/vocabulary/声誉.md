## 声誉 (shēngyù)

声誉 refers to the reputation or prestige that a person, organization, or brand has built over time -- the way they are regarded and spoken of by others.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** reputation; prestige; good name; standing

## Example Sentences

**这家公司凭借优质的服务赢得了良好的声誉。** Zhè jiā gōngsī píngjiè yōuzhì de fúwù yíngdé le liánghǎo de shēngyù. This company has earned a good reputation through its quality service.

**一旦声誉受损，重建信任需要很长时间。** Yīdàn shēngyù shòusǔn, chóngjiàn xìnrèn xūyào hěn cháng shíjiān. Once a reputation is damaged, rebuilding trust takes a long time.

**这所大学在国际学术界享有很高的声誉。** Zhè suǒ dàxué zài guójì xuéshù jiè xiǎngyǒu hěn gāo de shēngyù. This university enjoys a high reputation in international academic circles.

**他为保护自己的声誉不惜一切代价。** Tā wèi bǎohù zìjǐ de shēngyù bùxī yīqiè dàijià. He spared no effort to protect his reputation.

**公司的声誉是其最宝贵的无形资产。** Gōngsī de shēngyù shì qí zuì bǎoguì de wúxíng zīchǎn. A company's reputation is its most precious intangible asset.

**这次丑闻严重损害了该官员的社会声誉。** Zhè cì chǒuwén yánzhòng sǔnhài le gāi guānyuán de shèhuì shēngyù. This scandal seriously damaged the official's social reputation.

## Usage Notes

声誉 is a formal word for reputation, implying something that has been built and earned over time. 声 means "sound/voice/fame" and 誉 means "praise/honor," so 声誉 is literally "the sounds of praise" -- what people say and think about you.

Compare 声誉 with 名誉 (míngyù, honor/reputation) and 名声 (míngshēng, reputation/fame) -- they are largely synonymous, with 声誉 being slightly more formal and positive in connotation. 名声 can be positive or negative (坏名声, bad reputation), while 声誉 typically refers to a good or established reputation.

## Memory Hook

声 means "sound/voice" and 誉 means "praise." 声誉 is the voice of praise that follows you -- your reputation is literally what people say about you.
