## 潜在 (qiánzài)

潜在 describes something that exists beneath the surface or has not yet fully manifested -- potential risk, hidden talent, or latent capacity that may emerge under the right conditions.

## Meanings
1. **[adj]** potential; latent; hidden; underlying

## Example Sentences
**这个市场有着巨大的潜在商机，值得深入研究。** Zhège shìchǎng yǒuzhe jùdà de qiánzài shāngjī, zhídé shēnrù yánjiū. This market has enormous potential business opportunities worth in-depth research.

**我们必须识别并降低项目中的潜在风险。** Wǒmen bìxū shíbié bìng jiàngdī xiàngmù zhōng de qiánzài fēngxiǎn. We must identify and reduce the potential risks in the project.

**他身上有很多潜在的能力，还没有被充分发掘。** Tā shēnshàng yǒu hěn duō qiánzài de nénglì, hái méiyǒu bèi chōngfèn fājué. He has a lot of latent abilities that have not yet been fully discovered.

**这种化学物质对人体有潜在的危害。** Zhè zhǒng huàxué wùzhì duì réntǐ yǒu qiánzài de wēihài. This chemical substance poses a potential hazard to the human body.

**科技的发展揭示了人工智能在医疗领域的潜在价值。** Kējì de fāzhǎn jiēshìle rénggōng zhìnéng zài yīliáo lǐngyù de qiánzài jiàzhí. Technological development has revealed the potential value of artificial intelligence in the medical field.

**这项研究旨在找出影响学生学习成绩的潜在因素。** Zhè xiàng yánjiū zhǐ zài zhǎochū yǐngxiǎng xuéshēng xuéxí chéngjì de qiánzài yīnsù. This study aims to identify the underlying factors that affect students' academic performance.

## Usage Notes
潜在 is an attributive adjective -- it always precedes a noun (e.g., 潜在风险 -- potential risk). It cannot stand alone as a predicate adjective. The character 潜 means "hidden/submerged" (related to 潜水 -- diving, submerging) and 在 means "existing/at," so 潜在 literally means "hidden/existing beneath the surface."

This word is used across business, science, medicine, and social analysis. It carries a neutral or slightly cautionary tone when used with risk-related nouns (潜在威胁, 潜在危险) and a positive tone when used with opportunity-related nouns (潜在价值, 潜在商机).

## Memory Hook
潜 = submerged/hidden (like a diver 潜水 submerged under water), 在 = existing -- existing hidden below the surface -- latent/potential.
