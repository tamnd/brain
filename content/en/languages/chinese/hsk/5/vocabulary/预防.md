## 预防 (yùfáng)

预防 means to take preventive measures against something harmful before it occurs, and is widely used in medicine, public health, safety, and policy contexts at B2 level.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to prevent; to take precautions against; to guard against
2. **[noun]** prevention; precaution

## Example Sentences

**定期体检有助于预防疾病的发生，做到早发现早治疗。** Dìngqī tǐjiǎn yǒuzhù yú yùfáng jíbìng de fāshēng, zuòdào zǎo fāxiàn zǎo zhìliáo. Regular physical examinations help prevent the occurrence of disease, enabling early detection and early treatment.

**政府推出了一系列预防措施以遏制传染病的扩散。** Zhèngfǔ tuīchūle yī xìliè yùfáng cuòshī yǐ èzhì chuánrǎnbìng de kuòsàn. The government introduced a series of prevention measures to contain the spread of infectious diseases.

**学校定期开展安全教育，帮助学生预防各类事故。** Xuéxiào dìngqī kāizhǎn ānquán jiàoyù, bāngzhù xuéshēng yùfáng gè lèi shìgù. Schools regularly carry out safety education to help students prevent all kinds of accidents.

**预防腐败比惩治腐败更为根本和有效。** Yùfáng fǔbài bǐ chéngzhì fǔbài gèng wéi gēnběn hé yǒuxiào. Preventing corruption is more fundamental and effective than punishing it after the fact.

**接种疫苗是预防传染病最经济、最有效的手段之一。** Jiēzhòng yìmiáo shì yùfáng chuánrǎnbìng zuì jīngjì, zuì yǒuxiào de shǒuduàn zhī yī. Vaccination is one of the most economical and effective means of preventing infectious diseases.

**加强网络安全意识是预防网络诈骗的最佳途径。** Jiāqiáng wǎngluò ānquán yìshí shì yùfáng wǎngluò zhàpiàn de zuìjiā tújìng. Strengthening cybersecurity awareness is the best way to prevent online fraud.

## Usage Notes

预防 is forward-looking -- it always refers to actions taken before a harmful event to stop it from happening. The Chinese public health principle 预防为主 (prevention first) is a guiding slogan of healthcare policy. Compare with 防止 (fángzhǐ, to prevent/guard against), which can be used in a wider range of contexts but is slightly less specific about the advance-planning aspect.

At B2, the pattern 预防...的发生 (to prevent the occurrence of...) and the compound 预防措施 (prevention measures) are the most important structures to master.

## Memory Hook

预 (in advance/before) + 防 (to guard/defend) = to defend and guard in advance.
