## 严谨 (yánjǐn)

严谨 describes a careful, thorough, and precise approach to work, scholarship, or thought, often used to praise the quality of academic work, research methodology, or a person's working style.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** rigorous; meticulous; precise (describing thoroughness and care in work or thinking)
2. **[adjective]** well-organized; tight; logically consistent (describing writing or argumentation)

## Example Sentences

**这位教授的学术态度非常严谨，每一个数据都经过反复核实。** Zhè wèi jiàoshòu de xuéshù tàidù fēicháng yánjǐn, měi yī gè shùjù dōu jīngguò fǎnfù héshí. This professor has a very rigorous academic attitude; every piece of data is repeatedly verified.

**这篇论文逻辑严谨，论据充分，获得了评委的高度评价。** Zhè piān lùnwén luójí yánjǐn, lùnjù chōngfèn, huòdéle píngwěi de gāodù píngjià. This paper is logically rigorous and well-supported, earning high praise from the judges.

**做科学研究需要严谨的态度，不能允许任何数据造假。** Zuò kēxué yánjiū xūyào yánjǐn de tàidù, bù néng yǔnxǔ rènhé shùjù zàojiǎ. Scientific research requires a rigorous attitude; no falsification of data can be allowed.

**他的写作风格严谨而清晰，深受读者喜爱。** Tā de xiězuò fēnggé yánjǐn ér qīngxī, shēn shòu dúzhě xǐài. His writing style is rigorous and clear, greatly loved by readers.

**这家工程公司以严谨的设计标准和精湛的施工质量著称。** Zhè jiā gōngchéng gōngsī yǐ yánjǐn de shèjì biāozhǔn hé jīngzhàn de shīgōng zhìliàng zhùchēng. This engineering company is known for its rigorous design standards and superb construction quality.

**导师要求学生以严谨的科学态度对待每一个实验环节。** Dǎoshī yāoqiú xuéshēng yǐ yánjǐn de kēxué tàidù duìdài měi yī gè shíyàn huánjié. The supervisor requires students to approach every stage of the experiment with a rigorous scientific attitude.

## Usage Notes

严谨 is a highly positive adjective used to describe people, work, writing, or methodology that is careful, thorough, and well-organized. It is particularly common in academic and professional contexts. The word carries an implication of not cutting corners and taking quality seriously.

严谨 differs from 严格 (strict/rigorous in enforcing rules): 严谨 describes the quality of thought or output, while 严格 describes the strictness of a standard or person. 治学严谨 (rigorous in scholarship) is a classic compliment for academics in Chinese culture.

## Memory Hook

严 means "strict" and 谨 means "careful/cautious" — 严谨 is being strictly careful, the hallmark of a meticulous scholar.
