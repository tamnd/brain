## 机遇 (jīyù)

A favorable window of opportunity that arises from external circumstances -- more formal and weighty than 机会, often implying a historically significant or rare chance.

## Meanings
1. **[noun]** opportunity, chance, favorable juncture

## Example Sentences
**全球化为发展中国家提供了前所未有的发展机遇。** Quánqiú huà wèi fāzhǎn zhōng guójiā tígōngle qián suǒ wèi yǒu de fāzhǎn jīyù. Globalization has provided developing countries with unprecedented development opportunities.

**他抓住了这次千载难逢的机遇，一举成名。** Tā zhuāzhùle zhè cì qiān zài nán féng de jīyù, yī jǔ chéngmíng. He seized this once-in-a-lifetime opportunity and became famous overnight.

**数字经济的崛起为传统企业带来了挑战，也带来了机遇。** Shùzì jīngjì de juéqǐ wèi chuántǒng qǐyè dài lāile tiǎozhàn, yě dài lāile jīyù. The rise of the digital economy has brought both challenges and opportunities to traditional enterprises.

**政策的调整为外资企业进入国内市场创造了新的机遇。** Zhèngcè de tiáozhěng wèi wàizī qǐyè jìnrù guónèi shìchǎng chuàngzàole xīn de jīyù. Policy adjustments have created new opportunities for foreign enterprises to enter the domestic market.

**面对挑战与机遇并存的局面，我们需要保持冷静。** Miànduì tiǎozhàn yǔ jīyù bìngcún de júmiàn, wǒmen xūyào bǎochí lěngjìng. Facing a situation where challenges and opportunities coexist, we need to remain calm.

**这个时代是年轻人施展才华的最好机遇。** Zhège shídài shì niánqīngrén shīzhǎn cáihuá de zuì hǎo jīyù. This era is the best opportunity for young people to display their talents.

## Usage Notes
机遇 is more formal and elevated than 机会 (jīhuì, opportunity). While 机会 can refer to any everyday chance (机会难得 = rare opportunity, 有机会 = to have a chance), 机遇 implies a significant, often once-in-a-lifetime juncture arising from external historical or economic forces.

In political and business discourse, 机遇 is paired almost reflexively with 挑战 (challenge): 机遇与挑战 (opportunities and challenges) is a ubiquitous phrase in Chinese official writing. B2 learners should internalize this pairing.

## Memory Hook
机 = machine/mechanism/crucial moment (机不可失 = seize the moment). 遇 = to encounter/meet by chance. A 机遇 is a crucial moment that you happen to encounter -- a rare chance that meets you on your path.
