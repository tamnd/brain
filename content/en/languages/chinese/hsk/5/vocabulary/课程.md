## 课程 (kèchéng)

课程 refers to a structured course of study, a curriculum, or a program of classes, and is essential vocabulary for discussing education, training, and academic planning in Chinese.

## Meanings
1. **[noun]** course; class; curriculum; program of study

## Example Sentences
**这门课程每周上三次课。** Zhè mén kèchéng měi zhōu shàng sān cì kè. This course meets three times a week.

**学校为学生提供了丰富多样的选修课程。** Xuéxiào wèi xuéshēng tígōng le fēngfù duōyàng de xuǎnxiū kèchéng. The school offers students a rich variety of elective courses.

**这所大学的计算机科学课程享有很高声誉。** Zhè suǒ dàxué de jìsuànjī kēxué kèchéng xiǎngyǒu hěn gāo shēngyù. This university's computer science program enjoys a high reputation.

**在线课程让学习变得更加灵活方便。** Zàixiàn kèchéng ràng xuéxí biàndé gèngjiā línghuó fāngbiàn. Online courses have made learning more flexible and convenient.

**教师需要精心设计课程内容，以满足学生需求。** Jiàoshī xūyào jīngxīn shèjì kèchéng nèiróng, yǐ mǎnzú xuéshēng xūqiú. Teachers need to carefully design course content to meet students' needs.

**她正在修读一门关于国际法的短期课程。** Tā zhèngzài xiū dú yī mén guānyú guójì fǎ de duǎnqī kèchéng. She is taking a short-term course on international law.

## Usage Notes
课程 can refer to a single course (一门课程) or an entire curriculum (课程体系). When counting courses, the measure word is 门: 一门课程 (one course). It is used across all levels of education, from primary school curricula to university degree programs and professional training.

Distinguish from 课 (kè, a single class session or lesson) and 学科 (xuékē, a field of study/academic discipline). 课程 implies a structured sequence of learning rather than a single class.

## Memory Hook
课 (class/lesson) + 程 (journey/process): the full journey of lessons -- a course or curriculum.
