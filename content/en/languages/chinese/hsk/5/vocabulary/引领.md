## 引领 (yǐnlǐng)

To lead or pioneer means to go ahead and show the way, inspiring or guiding others to follow, often used for innovation, trends, or social progress.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to lead; to pioneer; to guide forward; to show the way; to set the trend for

## Example Sentences

**中国正在努力引领全球新能源技术的发展。** Zhōngguó zhèngzài nǔlì yǐnlǐng quánqiú xīn néngyyuán jìshù de fāzhǎn. China is working hard to lead the development of global new energy technology.

**这位设计师引领了国际时装界的新潮流。** Zhè wèi shèjì shī yǐnlǐngle guójì shízhuāng jiè de xīn cháoliú. This designer led a new trend in the international fashion world.

**改革先行者引领着整个社会走向更加公平的未来。** Gǎigé xiānxíngyǔ yǐnlǐngzhe zhěnggè shèhuì zǒuxiàng gèngjiā gōngpíng de wèilái. Reform pioneers are leading the entire society toward a more equitable future.

**优秀教师的责任不只是传授知识，更是引领学生成长。** Yōuxiù jiàoshī de zérèn bù zhǐshì chuánshòu zhīshi, gèng shì yǐnlǐng xuéshēng chéngzhǎng. An excellent teacher's responsibility is not just to impart knowledge, but more importantly to lead students' growth.

**这家科技公司引领了人工智能行业的发展方向。** Zhè jiā kējì gōngsī yǐnlǐngle réngōng zhìnéng hángyè de fāzhǎn fāngxiàng. This technology company has led the direction of development in the artificial intelligence industry.

**文化引领是推动社会文明进步的重要力量。** Wénhuà yǐnlǐng shì tuīdòng shèhuì wénmíng jìnbù de zhòngyào lìliàng. Cultural leadership is an important force for promoting the progress of social civilization.

## Usage Notes

引领 is more elevated and visionary than 引导. While 引导 implies guiding someone step by step along a process, 引领 suggests showing the way at the forefront -- the leader is ahead of others, inspiring them to follow. It carries a sense of pioneering and prestige.

引领 is commonly used in discussions of industry leadership, national development, cultural influence, and innovation. It is a favorite term in official Chinese government and Party language when describing leading roles in global development.

## Memory Hook

引 (to draw/pull) + 领 (to lead/collar) -- pulling from the front by the collar, charging ahead and bringing others in your wake. A true frontrunner.
