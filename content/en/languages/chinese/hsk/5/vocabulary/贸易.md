## 贸易 (màoyì)

贸易 refers to organized commercial exchange, particularly trade between countries or regions, and is one of the most important economic terms in Chinese.

## Meanings
1. **[noun]** trade; commerce (especially international trade)

## Example Sentences
**中美贸易关系近年来经历了很大变化。** Zhōng Měi màoyì guānxi jìnnián lái jīnglì le hěn dà biànhuà. Sino-American trade relations have undergone significant changes in recent years.

**自由贸易协定有助于降低关税壁垒。** Zìyóu màoyì xiédìng yǒuzhù yú jiàngdī guānshuì bìlěi. Free trade agreements help reduce tariff barriers.

**这个国家的经济高度依赖对外贸易。** Zhège guójiā de jīngjì gāodù yīlài duìwài màoyì. This country's economy is highly dependent on foreign trade.

**贸易逆差是许多发展中国家面临的经济挑战。** Màoyì nìchā shì xǔduō fāzhǎn zhōng guójiā miànlín de jīngjì tiǎozhàn. A trade deficit is an economic challenge facing many developing countries.

**电子商务的发展正在改变传统的贸易模式。** Diànzǐ shāngyè de fāzhǎn zhèngzài gǎibiàn chuántǒng de màoyì móshì. The development of e-commerce is changing traditional trade models.

**两国签署了一项促进双边贸易的协议。** Liǎng guó qiānshǔ le yī xiàng cùjìn shuāngbiān màoyì de xiéyì. The two countries signed an agreement to promote bilateral trade.

| 贸易战 (màoyì zhàn) | trade war |

## Usage Notes
贸易 is formal and almost always refers to trade at a commercial or international scale. For everyday buying and selling, 买卖 (mǎimài) or 交易 (jiāoyì) are more appropriate. 贸易 appears frequently in news, economic policy, and diplomatic contexts.

Key related terms: 贸易伙伴 (màoyì huǒbàn, trade partner), 贸易协定 (màoyì xiédìng, trade agreement), 世界贸易组织 (shìjiè màoyì zǔzhī, WTO).

## Memory Hook
贸 (trade/exchange) + 易 (to exchange): exchanging goods commercially -- trade.
