## 构建 (gòujiàn)

构建 means to construct or build something deliberately and systematically, and is most often used for abstract constructions such as systems, frameworks, theories, and social relationships.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to construct; to build; to create in a systematic way
2. **[verb]** to establish (a framework, system, model, or relationship)

## Example Sentences

**政府致力于构建更完善的社会保障体系。** Zhèngfǔ zhìlì yú gòujiàn gèng wánshàn de shèhuì bǎozhàng tǐxì. The government is committed to constructing a more comprehensive social security system.

**这位哲学家用毕生精力构建了一套独特的伦理理论。** Zhè wèi zhéxuéjiā yòng bìshēng jīnglì gòujiànle yī tào dútè de lúnlǐ lǐlùn. This philosopher spent his entire life constructing a unique ethical theory.

**两国之间正在努力构建互信合作的伙伴关系。** Liǎng guó zhījiān zhèngzài nǔlì gòujiàn hùxìn hézuò de huǒbàn guānxi. The two countries are working to construct a mutually trusting and cooperative partnership.

**学生需要构建自己的知识框架，才能真正理解问题。** Xuésheng xūyào gòujiàn zìjǐ de zhīshi kuàngjià, cái néng zhēnzhèng lǐjiě wèntí. Students need to build their own knowledge framework in order to truly understand issues.

**构建和谐社会是当前社会治理的核心目标之一。** Gòujiàn héxié shèhuì shì dāngqián shèhuì zhìlǐ de héxīn mùbiāo zhī yī. Constructing a harmonious society is one of the core goals of current social governance.

**工程师们正在构建一套全新的数据处理架构。** Gōngchéngshīmen zhèngzài gòujiàn yī tào quánxīn de shùjù chǔlǐ jiàgòu. Engineers are building a completely new data processing architecture.

## Usage Notes

构建 is a formal word used almost exclusively for abstract or large-scale constructions such as systems, theories, frameworks, institutions, and relationships. It is rarely used for physical construction, which is the domain of 建造 (jiànzào) or 修建 (xiūjiàn). The word implies deliberate, planned design and a process of assembling components into a coherent whole.

Compared with 建立 (jiànlì), which also means to establish, 构建 emphasizes the structural or architectural dimension of the building process. 构建体系 is a very common phrase in official language. At B2, learners should use 构建 for systematic, abstract building.

## Memory Hook

GOU (构, to frame/structure) + JIAN (建, to build/establish) = to frame and build a structured whole.
