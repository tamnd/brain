## 区块链 (qūkuài liàn)

区块链 is the Chinese term for blockchain technology -- a distributed, decentralized digital ledger that records transactions across a network of computers in a way that is secure, transparent, and difficult to alter.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** blockchain (a distributed ledger technology)

## Example Sentences

**区块链技术的核心优势在于其去中心化和不可篡改的特性。** Qūkuài liàn jìshù de héxīn yōushì zàiyú qí qù zhōngxīnhuà hé bùkě cuāngǎi de tèxìng. The core advantage of blockchain technology lies in its decentralized and tamper-proof characteristics.

**比特币是基于区块链技术的第一种加密货币。** Bǐtèbì shì jīyú qūkuài liàn jìshù de dì yī zhǒng jiāmì huòbì. Bitcoin is the first cryptocurrency based on blockchain technology.

**区块链在供应链管理、金融服务和医疗记录等领域有广泛的应用潜力。** Qūkuài liàn zài gōngyìng liàn guǎnlǐ, jīnróng fúwù hé yīliáo jìlù děng lǐngyù yǒu guǎngfàn de yìngyòng qiánlì. Blockchain has broad application potential in areas such as supply chain management, financial services, and medical records.

**许多国家的政府正在探索将区块链用于电子政务和数字身份验证。** Xǔduō guójiā de zhèngfǔ zhèngzài tànsuo jiāng qūkuài liàn yòng yú diànzǐ zhèngwù hé shùzì shēnfèn yànzhèng. Governments in many countries are exploring the use of blockchain for e-government services and digital identity verification.

**区块链的透明性使得每一笔交易记录都可以被公开追溯。** Qūkuài liàn de tòumíng xìng shǐ de měi yī bǐ jiāoyì jìlù dōu kěyǐ bèi gōngkāi zhuīsù. The transparency of blockchain makes every transaction record publicly traceable.

**智能合约是区块链技术的重要应用，可以自动执行预设条件下的合同条款。** Zhìnéng héyuē shì qūkuài liàn jìshù de zhòngyào yìngyòng, kěyǐ zìdòng zhíxíng yùshè tiáojiàn xià de hétong tiáokuǎn. Smart contracts are an important application of blockchain technology that can automatically execute contract terms under preset conditions.

## Usage Notes

区块链 is a loanword concept translated descriptively: 区块 (block) + 链 (chain). The word is used exclusively in technology and financial contexts. Key related terms include: 去中心化 (decentralization), 加密货币 (cryptocurrency), 智能合约 (smart contract), and 数字货币 (digital currency).

In Chinese technology and financial news, 区块链 is often discussed alongside 人工智能 (AI), 大数据 (big data), and 云计算 (cloud computing) as one of the transformative technologies of the digital era. China has been actively developing state-level blockchain infrastructure.

## Memory Hook

区块 are "blocks" -- sealed chunks of data -- and 链 is a "chain" that links them together in sequence. Picture a long chain where each link is a locked box of transaction records, and removing or changing one link breaks the whole chain.
