## 广泛 (guǎngfàn)

Extensive or widespread describes something that covers a large range, affects many people or areas, or involves many different kinds of things or subjects.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** extensive; widespread; broad; wide-ranging
2. **[adverb]** extensively; widely; broadly

## Example Sentences

**这种技术已经在各个行业得到了广泛应用。** Zhè zhǒng jìshù yǐjīng zài gège hángyè dédàole guǎngfàn yìngyòng. This technology has already been widely applied across various industries.

**该事件引起了社会各界的广泛关注。** Gāi shìjiàn yǐnqǐle shèhuì gèjiè de guǎngfàn guānzhù. The incident attracted widespread attention from all sectors of society.

**他兴趣广泛，喜欢音乐、绘画和体育运动。** Tā xìngqù guǎngfàn, xǐhuān yīnyuè, huìhuà hé tǐyù yùndòng. He has broad interests and enjoys music, painting, and sports.

**这项研究对社会产生了广泛而深远的影响。** Zhè xiàng yánjiū duì shèhuì chǎnshēngle guǎngfàn ér shēnyuǎn de yǐngxiǎng. This research had a wide-ranging and far-reaching impact on society.

**广泛阅读有助于扩大词汇量和提高写作水平。** Guǎngfàn yuèdú yǒuzhù yú kuòdà cíhuìliàng hé tígāo xiězuò shuǐpíng. Extensive reading helps expand vocabulary and improve writing skills.

**新政策在全国范围内得到了广泛支持。** Xīn zhèngcè zài quánguó fànwéi nèi dédàole guǎngfàn zhīchí. The new policy received widespread support nationwide.

## Usage Notes

广泛 is a versatile adjective/adverb that commonly modifies nouns describing coverage or scope (广泛影响 -- widespread influence) and verbs describing actions done broadly (广泛应用 -- widely applied). It emphasizes breadth rather than depth.

In academic and official writing, 广泛 is extremely common and can appear both before nouns (广泛的影响 -- widespread influence) and after verbs (得到广泛应用 -- to be widely applied). Note that 广泛 suggests diversity and spread, while 大量 (a large amount) focuses on quantity.

## Memory Hook

广 (broad/wide) + 泛 (to float/overflow) -- imagine water overflowing widely in all directions, spreading broadly everywhere.
