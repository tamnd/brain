## 利润 (lìrùn)

利润 refers to the financial gain a business makes after all costs, expenses, and taxes have been subtracted from total revenue -- the bottom-line measure of commercial success.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** profit (the net financial gain of a business)

## Example Sentences

**该公司今年的净利润同比增长了百分之二十。** Gāi gōngsī jīnnián de jìng lìrùn tóng bǐ zēngzhǎng le bǎifēnzhī èrshí. The company's net profit grew by twenty percent year-on-year this year.

**追求利润最大化是许多企业的核心经营目标。** Zhuīqiú lìrùn zuìdàhuà shì xǔduō qǐyè de héxīn jīngyíng mùbiāo. Pursuing maximum profit is the core business objective of many enterprises.

**利润率的下降表明市场竞争正在加剧。** Lìrùn lǜ de xiàjiàng biǎomíng shìchǎng jìngzhēng zhèngzài jiājù. The decline in profit margins indicates that market competition is intensifying.

**这家餐厅虽然客流量大，但食材成本高，利润十分有限。** Zhè jiā cāntīng suīrán kè liúliàng dà, dàn shícái chéngběn gāo, lìrùn shífēn yǒuxiàn. Although this restaurant has a large customer flow, the food ingredient costs are high and profit is very limited.

**投资者最关心的是企业的长期盈利能力和利润增长潜力。** Tóuzī zhě zuì guānxīn de shì qǐyè de chángqī yínglì nénglì hé lìrùn zēngzhǎng qiánlì. What investors care most about is the company's long-term profitability and profit growth potential.

**政府征收企业所得税会直接影响公司的税后利润。** Zhèngfǔ zhēngshōu qǐyè suǒdéshuì huì zhíjiē yǐngxiǎng gōngsī de shuì hòu lìrùn. Government corporate income tax collection directly affects a company's after-tax profit.

## Usage Notes

利润 is a purely financial/business term. Compare with related terms: 收入 (income/revenue) is what comes in before deductions; 利润 is what remains after costs; 利息 is interest income; 利益 is a broader term for benefit or interest in a general sense. 盈利 (yínglì) is a verb form meaning "to make a profit/be profitable," while 利润 is the noun form.

Common business Chinese phrases include 利润空间 (profit margin/room for profit), 薄利多销 (thin profit but high volume), and 暴利 (windfall profit/excessive profit, with negative connotation).

## Memory Hook

利 means "profit/benefit" and 润 means "to moisten/enrich" -- 利润 is the enriching benefit that flows back to the business after all expenses are paid, the moisture that nourishes the enterprise's growth.
