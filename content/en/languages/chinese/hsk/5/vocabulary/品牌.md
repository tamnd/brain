## 品牌 (pǐnpái)

品牌 refers to a brand -- the name, image, and identity associated with a product or company -- and is a key term in business, marketing, and consumer culture.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** brand; brand name; trademark identity

## Example Sentences

**苹果是全球最具价值的品牌之一。** Píngguǒ shì quánqiú zuì jù jiàzhí de pǐnpái zhī yī. Apple is one of the most valuable brands in the world.

**建立一个强大的品牌需要多年的努力。** Jiànlì yī gè qiángdà de pǐnpái xūyào duō nián de nǔlì. Building a strong brand requires many years of effort.

**消费者越来越倾向于选择本土品牌。** Xiāofèi zhě yuè lái yuè qīngxiàng yú xuǎnzé běntǔ pǐnpái. Consumers are increasingly inclined to choose domestic brands.

**这个品牌以高质量和可靠性著称。** Zhège pǐnpái yǐ gāo zhìliàng hé kěkào xìng zhùchēng. This brand is known for its high quality and reliability.

**品牌形象对于公司的长期发展至关重要。** Pǐnpái xíngxiàng duìyú gōngsī de chángqī fāzhǎn zhìguān zhòngyào. Brand image is crucial to the long-term development of a company.

**他们花费大量资金进行品牌推广。** Tāmen huāfèi dàliàng zījīn jìnxíng pǐnpái tuīguǎng. They spent large amounts of money on brand promotion.

## Usage Notes

品牌 is the standard modern term for "brand" in Chinese business and marketing. 品 means "quality/product" and 牌 means "sign/nameplate/license," so 品牌 literally refers to the mark of quality on a product.

In everyday speech, 牌子 (páizi) is the more colloquial synonym for brand, while 品牌 is used in formal and business contexts. Brand-related vocabulary forms an important cluster: 品牌战略 (brand strategy), 品牌忠诚度 (brand loyalty), 品牌效应 (brand effect).

## Memory Hook

品 means "quality" and 牌 means "signboard/label." A 品牌 is the quality label that identifies a product -- the brand's promise of quality stamped on everything it makes.
