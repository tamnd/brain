## 全面 (quánmiàn)

全面 describes something that covers all aspects or dimensions of a subject without omission, indicating thoroughness and comprehensiveness.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** comprehensive, all-round, thorough
2. **[adverb]** comprehensively, in all respects

## Example Sentences

**我们需要对这个问题进行全面深入的分析。** Wǒmen xūyào duì zhège wèntí jìnxíng quánmiàn shēnrù de fēnxī. We need to conduct a comprehensive and in-depth analysis of this issue.

**这份报告全面反映了当前经济形势。** Zhè fèn bàogào quánmiàn fǎnyìng le dāngqián jīngjì xíngshì. This report comprehensively reflects the current economic situation.

**学校致力于培养学生的全面发展。** Xuéxiào zhìlì yú péiyǎng xuéshēng de quánmiàn fāzhǎn. The school is committed to fostering the all-round development of students.

**政府宣布了一项全面的医疗改革方案。** Zhèngfǔ xuānbù le yī xiàng quánmiàn de yīliáo gǎigé fāng'àn. The government announced a comprehensive healthcare reform plan.

**要全面了解一个人，需要时间和耐心。** Yào quánmiàn liǎojiě yī gè rén, xūyào shíjiān hé nàixīn. Understanding a person comprehensively requires time and patience.

**这场改革全面涵盖了教育、医疗和住房三个领域。** Zhè chǎng gǎigé quánmiàn hángài le jiàoyù, yīliáo hé zhùfáng sān gè lǐngyù. This reform comprehensively covers three areas: education, healthcare, and housing.

## Usage Notes

全面 is one of the most frequently used adjectives in formal Chinese, especially in policy documents, academic writing, and news reports. It often collocates with verbs like 推进 (advance), 深化 (deepen), 了解 (understand), and 分析 (analyze). The phrase 全面发展 (all-round development) is a cornerstone expression in Chinese educational and political discourse.

全面 differs from 整体 (overall) in that 全面 emphasizes covering all aspects/dimensions, while 整体 refers to the whole as a single unit. Similarly, 全面 differs from 全部 (all, entirety) in that 全部 refers to total quantity, while 全面 emphasizes breadth and comprehensiveness.

## Memory Hook

全 means "complete/whole" and 面 means "face/side/aspect" -- 全面 covers every face and every side of something, leaving no angle unexamined.
