## 优化 (yōuhuà)

优化 means to optimize or systematically improve something to achieve better performance, efficiency, or quality, and is widely used in technical, business, and policy contexts.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to optimize, to improve systematically, to streamline
2. **[noun]** optimization, improvement (of processes, structures, or performance)

## Example Sentences

**工程师正在优化算法，以提高程序运行速度。** Gōngchéng shī zhèngzài yōuhuà suànfǎ, yǐ tígāo chéngxù yùnxíng sùdù. Engineers are optimizing algorithms to improve program execution speed.

**政府计划优化营商环境，吸引更多外资。** Zhèngfǔ jìhuà yōuhuà yíngshāng huánjìng, xīyǐn gèng duō wài zī. The government plans to optimize the business environment to attract more foreign investment.

**通过优化供应链管理，公司大幅降低了运营成本。** Tōngguò yōuhuà gōngyìng liàn guǎnlǐ, gōngsī dàfú jiàngdī le yùnyíng chéngběn. By optimizing supply chain management, the company significantly reduced operating costs.

**这次机构改革的目的是优化资源配置。** Zhè cì jīgòu gǎigé de mùdì shì yōuhuà zīyuán pèizhì. The purpose of this institutional reform is to optimize the allocation of resources.

**搜索引擎优化是数字营销的核心技术之一。** Sōusuǒ yǐnqíng yōuhuà shì shùzì yíngxiāo de héxīn jìshù zhī yī. Search engine optimization is one of the core technologies of digital marketing.

**经过持续优化，用户体验得到了显著改善。** Jīngguò chíxù yōuhuà, yònghù tǐyàn dédào le xiǎnzhù gǎishàn. After continuous optimization, the user experience has been significantly improved.

## Usage Notes

优化 is a high-frequency term in modern Chinese, especially in technology, business administration, and government policy contexts. It implies a systematic, analytical approach to making something work better -- not just changing it, but improving it toward an optimal state. It is used for both processes (优化流程) and structures (优化结构), and has become a key term in the internet and tech industry (算法优化, 代码优化).

At B2 level, 优化 is essential vocabulary for anyone working in or studying technology, business, or public policy.

## Memory Hook

优 means excellent/superior and 化 means to transform -- 优化 is to transform something toward excellence, like a chef refining a recipe until every ingredient is in its perfect place.
