## 测试 (cèshì)

测试 refers to the process of examining, evaluating, or checking the performance, quality, or correctness of something -- commonly used in technical, academic, and product development contexts.

## Meanings
1. **[verb]** to test; to examine; to evaluate; to trial
2. **[noun]** test; trial; examination; assessment

## Example Sentences
**软件发布前必须经过严格的测试。** Ruǎnjiàn fābù qián bìxū jīngguò yángé de cèshì. Software must undergo rigorous testing before release.

**这款新产品正处于测试阶段，尚未正式上市。** Zhè kuǎn xīn chǎnpǐn zhèng chǔyú cèshì jiēduàn, shàngwèi zhèngshì shàngshì. This new product is in the testing phase and has not yet officially launched.

**老师安排了一次小测试来检验学生的学习效果。** Lǎoshī ānpáile yī cì xiǎo cèshì lái jiǎnyàn xuéshēng de xuéxí xiàoguǒ. The teacher arranged a small test to check the students' learning progress.

**工程师每天对系统进行多次测试，确保稳定运行。** Gōngchéngshī měitiān duì xìtǒng jìnxíng duō cì cèshì, quèbǎo wěndìng yùnxíng. Engineers test the system multiple times daily to ensure stable operation.

**这项测试的结果将决定项目是否继续推进。** Zhè xiàng cèshì de jiéguǒ jiāng juédìng xiàngmù shìfǒu jìxù tuījìn. The results of this test will determine whether the project continues to move forward.

**他通过了驾驶技能测试，终于拿到了驾照。** Tā tōngguòle jiàshǐ jìnéng cèshì, zhōngyú ná dàole jiàzhào. He passed the driving skills test and finally got his driver's license.

## Usage Notes
测试 is a versatile term used across many domains: software testing, product quality checks, academic assessments, and medical screenings. It emphasizes systematic evaluation to verify performance or quality.

Compare 测试 with 考试 (kǎoshì, examination): 考试 refers specifically to formal academic or qualifying exams, while 测试 has a broader technical and evaluative meaning. For a software engineer, 测试代码 (testing code) uses 测试; for a student, 参加考试 (taking an exam) uses 考试.

## Memory Hook
测 = measure/survey (water + then = measure water depth) and 试 = try/attempt -- measuring by attempting -- to test.
