## 包容的 (bāoróng de)

包容的 describes an attitude, environment, or system that welcomes and accepts diversity -- whether of people, ideas, or backgrounds. It conveys both tolerance and active openness, and is central to discussions of social cohesion and multiculturalism.

## Meanings
1. **[adj]** inclusive; tolerant; accepting of diversity; open-minded

## Example Sentences
**包容的社会能够接纳不同背景的人共同生活。** Bāoróng de shèhuì nénggòu jiēnà bùtóng bèijǐng de rén gòngtóng shēnghuó. An inclusive society can accommodate people from different backgrounds living together.

**学校应该营造包容的学习环境，尊重每位学生的个性。** Xuéxiào yīnggāi yíngzào bāoróng de xuéxí huánjìng, zūnzhòng měi wèi xuéshēng de gèxìng. Schools should create an inclusive learning environment that respects each student's individuality.

**他以包容的胸怀对待不同意见，赢得了同事的尊敬。** Tā yǐ bāoróng de xiōnghuái duìdài bùtóng yìjiàn, yíngdéle tóngshì de zūnjìng. He approached differing opinions with an inclusive mindset and earned his colleagues' respect.

**建立包容的工作文化有助于提高员工的归属感。** Jiànlì bāoróng de gōngzuò wénhuà yǒuzhù yú tígāo yuángōng de guīshǔgǎn. Building an inclusive work culture helps increase employees' sense of belonging.

**一个包容的文化政策有助于促进民族和谐。** Yī gè bāoróng de wénhuà zhèngcè yǒuzhù yú cùjìn mínzú héxié. An inclusive cultural policy helps promote ethnic harmony.

**包容的领导风格往往能激发团队更强的创造力。** Bāoróng de lǐngdǎo fēnggé wǎngwǎng néng jīfā tuánduì gèng qiáng de chuàngzàolì. An inclusive leadership style often stimulates greater creativity in the team.

## Usage Notes
包容的 combines the verb 包容 (to tolerate/to include) with the adjective marker 的. The verb 包容 itself means "to be tolerant of" or "to accommodate," and appears in constructions like 包容差异 (to be inclusive of differences).

Do not confuse 包容 with 宽容 (kuānróng -- lenient/forgiving). 包容 focuses on accepting diversity, while 宽容 emphasizes forgiving flaws or mistakes.

## Memory Hook
包 means "to wrap/to contain" and 容 means "to hold/to accommodate" -- 包容 means to hold everything inside without pushing anything out.
