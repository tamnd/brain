## 标准 (biāozhǔn)

An established benchmark, norm, or criterion that serves as a reference for quality, behavior, or measurement -- also used as an adjective meaning "standard" or "normal."

## Meanings
1. **[noun]** standard, criterion, benchmark, norm
2. **[adjective]** standard, orthodox, correct (as in standard Mandarin pronunciation)

## Example Sentences
**她的普通话发音非常标准，让外国学生十分羡慕。** Tā de pǔtōnghuà fāyīn fēicháng biāozhǔn, ràng wàiguó xuéshēng shífēn xiànmù. Her Mandarin pronunciation is very standard, making foreign students very envious.

**这款产品必须符合国家质量标准才能上市销售。** Zhè kuǎn chǎnpǐn bìxū fúhé guójiā zhìliàng biāozhǔn cái néng shàngshì xiāoshòu. This product must meet national quality standards before it can be sold on the market.

**制定统一的行业标准有助于规范市场秩序。** Zhìdìng tǒngyī de hángyè biāozhǔn yǒuzhù yú guīfàn shìchǎng zhìxù. Formulating unified industry standards helps regulate market order.

**不同国家对食品安全的标准存在一定差异。** Bùtóng guójiā duì shípǐn ānquán de biāozhǔn cúnzài yīdìng chāyì. Different countries have certain differences in their food safety standards.

**他始终坚持以高标准要求自己的工作质量。** Tā shǐzhōng jiānchí yǐ gāo biāozhǔn yāoqiú zìjǐ de gōngzuò zhìliàng. He has always insisted on holding himself to high standards in his work quality.

**国际标准化组织负责制定全球通用的技术标准。** Guójì biāozhǔnhuà zǔzhī fùzé zhìdìng quánqiú tōngyòng de jìshù biāozhǔn. The International Organization for Standardization is responsible for formulating globally applicable technical standards.

## Usage Notes
标准 is used in virtually every domain of life -- language, manufacturing, food safety, education, and ethics. As an adjective it commonly modifies 普通话: 标准普通话 means Mandarin spoken according to the official phonological norm, which in China is 普通话 (Putonghua).

In business and policy, 标准 is closely linked to regulation: 符合标准 (to meet standards), 低于标准 (below standard), and 超出标准 (above standard) are all common constructions.

## Memory Hook
标 = a marker/target (something pointed to as a reference point). 准 = accurate/precise. A 标准 is the marker you aim at -- the precise reference point that defines what is correct or acceptable.
