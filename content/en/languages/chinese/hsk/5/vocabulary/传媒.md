## 传媒 (chuánméi)

传媒 refers to media organizations and platforms that transmit information to the public, encompassing print, broadcast, and digital media, and is used as both a general term for "the media" and in industry-specific contexts.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** media; mass media (organizations and platforms for mass communication)
2. **[noun]** the communications or media industry

## Example Sentences

**传媒行业正经历深刻变革，传统报纸和电视台面临来自新媒体的巨大冲击。** Chuánméi hángyè zhèng jīnglì shēnkè biàngé, chuántǒng bàozhǐ hé diànshìtái miànlín láizì xīn méitǐ de jùdà chōngjī. The media industry is undergoing profound changes, with traditional newspapers and TV stations facing enormous pressure from new media.

**这家大型传媒集团旗下拥有多个电视频道、报纸和网络平台。** Zhè jiā dàxíng chuánméi jítuán qíxià yōngyǒu duō gè diànshì píndào, bàozhǐ hé wǎngluò píngtái. This large media conglomerate owns multiple television channels, newspapers, and online platforms.

**社交媒体的兴起彻底改变了传媒生态，每个人都可以成为信息的传播者。** Shèjiāo méitǐ de xīngqǐ chèdǐ gǎibiànle chuánméi shēngtài, měi gè rén dōu kěyǐ chéngwéi xìnxī de chuánbòzhě. The rise of social media has completely changed the media landscape; everyone can become a disseminator of information.

**传媒专业的学生需要掌握新闻采写、视频制作和数字营销等多方面技能。** Chuánméi zhuānyè de xuéshēng xūyào zhǎngwò xīnwén cǎi xiě, shìpín zhìzuò hé shùzì yíngxiāo děng duō fāngmiàn jìnéng. Students majoring in media need to master skills in journalism, video production, digital marketing, and other areas.

**政府要求传媒机构严格遵守新闻报道的相关法规，确保信息的真实准确。** Zhèngfǔ yāoqiú chuánméi jīgòu yángé zūnshǒu xīnwén bàodào de xiāngguān fǎguī, quèbǎo xìnxī de zhēnshí zhǔnquè. The government requires media organizations to strictly comply with relevant regulations on news reporting to ensure the accuracy of information.

**在信息爆炸的时代，传媒的责任不仅是传递信息，更是帮助公众辨别真伪。** Zài xìnxī bàozhà de shídài, chuánméi de zérèn bù jǐn shì chuándì xìnxī, gèng shì bāngzhù gōngzhòng biànbié zhēn wěi. In an era of information explosion, the responsibility of the media is not only to convey information but also to help the public distinguish truth from falsehood.

## Usage Notes

传媒 is a formal term for the media industry or media organizations as a whole. It is widely used in professional, academic, and policy contexts. In everyday speech, 媒体 (méitǐ) is more commonly used to refer to "the media," while 传媒 tends to appear in industry and academic contexts, such as 传媒专业 (media studies major) or 传媒集团 (media group).

The distinction between 传统传媒 (traditional media) and 新媒体 (new media) or 新兴传媒 (emerging media) is a common theme in current Chinese discourse on media transformation.

## Memory Hook

传 (to transmit/spread) + 媒 (medium/intermediary) — 传媒 is the "medium for transmitting" information, i.e., the media.
