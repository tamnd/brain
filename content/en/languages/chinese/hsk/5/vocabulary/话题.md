## 话题 (huàtí)

话题 is a versatile noun for any topic of conversation or discussion, from casual everyday subjects to trending social issues, and is especially common in informal and media contexts.

## Meanings
1. **[noun]** topic; subject of conversation; talking point

## Example Sentences
**天气是人们最常见的话题之一。** Tiānqì shì rénmen zuì chángjiàn de huàtí zhī yī. The weather is one of the most common topics of conversation.

**这部电影上映后成为了社交媒体上的热门话题。** Zhè bù diànyǐng shàngyìng hòu chéngle shèjiāo méitǐ shàng de rèmén huàtí. After its release, this film became a trending topic on social media.

**他们聊了很久，换了好几个话题。** Tāmen liáo le hěn jiǔ, huàn le hǎo jǐ gè huàtí. They chatted for a long time and switched through several topics.

**这是一个敏感话题，需要谨慎讨论。** Zhè shì yīgè mǐngǎn huàtí, xūyào jǐnshèn tǎolùn. This is a sensitive topic that requires careful discussion.

**他善于找到合适的话题打开话匣子。** Tā shànyú zhǎodào héshì de huàtí dǎkāi huàxiázi. He is skilled at finding the right topic to start a conversation.

**环境保护已经成为全球关注的共同话题。** Huánjìng bǎohù yǐjīng chéngle quánqiú guānzhù de gòngtóng huàtí. Environmental protection has become a shared topic of global attention.

## Collocations
| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 热门话题 (rèmén huàtí) | hot/trending topic |
| 敏感话题 (mǐngǎn huàtí) | sensitive topic |
| 转换话题 (zhuǎnhuàn huàtí) | to change the subject |
| 引发话题 (yǐnfā huàtí) | to spark a topic |
| 社会话题 (shèhuì huàtí) | social topic |

## Usage Notes
话题 is less formal than 议题 (yìtí, agenda item) and more conversational in feel. It is the go-to word for topics that arise naturally in conversation or social media, not necessarily in a formal meeting context.

In the digital age, 话题 is used extensively in social media (微博话题, Weibo topic; 热搜话题, trending search topic). The phrase #话题# (hashtag format) is used to mark trending topics on Chinese platforms.

## Memory Hook
话 (words/speech) + 题 (topic/question): the topic around which words flow -- a conversation topic.
