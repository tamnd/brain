## 归结 (guījié)

归结 means to trace something back to an underlying cause or principle, or to reduce a complex matter to its essential point, similar to "to boil down to" or "to come down to" in English.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to attribute to; to trace back to; to reduce to
2. **[verb]** to come down to; to boil down to (a core issue)

## Example Sentences

**问题的根源可以归结为管理上的失误。** Wèntí de gēnyuán kěyǐ guījié wéi guǎnlǐ shàng de shīwù. The root of the problem can be attributed to management errors.

**他把自己的成功归结于勤奋和坚持。** Tā bǎ zìjǐ de chénggōng guījié yú qínfèn hé jiānchí. He attributed his success to hard work and perseverance.

**无论怎样分析，这件事最终归结为利益分配问题。** Wúlùn zěnyàng fēnxī, zhè jiàn shì zuìzhōng guījié wéi lìyì fēnpèi wèntí. No matter how it is analyzed, this matter ultimately boils down to the distribution of interests.

**这些复杂的现象都可以归结为同一种机制。** Zhèxiē fùzá de xiànxiàng dōu kěyǐ guījié wéi tóngyī zhǒng jīzhì. These complex phenomena can all be attributed to the same mechanism.

**讨论到最后，所有的争议都归结为一个核心问题。** Tǎolùn dào zuìhòu, suǒyǒu de zhēngyì dōu guījié wéi yī gè héxīn wèntí. In the end, all the disputes boiled down to one core issue.

**历史学家将帝国的衰落归结于内部腐败和外部压力。** Lìshǐxuéjiā jiāng dìguó de shuāiluò guījié yú nèibù fǔbài hé wàibù yālì. Historians attributed the decline of the empire to internal corruption and external pressure.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 归结为 (guījié wéi) | to be attributed to; to boil down to |
| 归结于 (guījié yú) | to attribute to (a cause) |
| 最终归结 (zuìzhōng guījié) | ultimately comes down to |
| 归结原因 (guījié yuányīn) | to identify the reason |
| 可以归结 (kěyǐ guījié) | can be attributed/reduced to |
| 归结一点 (guījié yī diǎn) | to put it in one point |

## Usage Notes

归结 is always followed by 为 or 于 when indicating what something is attributed to. The pattern 归结为…… is the most common structure. It is more analytical than simply saying 因为 (because) and implies a careful tracing of cause and effect.

Compared with 归因于 (to attribute to, a more direct causal attribution), 归结 has the additional sense of condensing a complex matter into its essential explanation. It is used in analytical writing, academic papers, and formal argumentation.

## Memory Hook

GUI (归, to return) + JIE (结, conclusion/tie) = to return to the core conclusion that ties everything together.
