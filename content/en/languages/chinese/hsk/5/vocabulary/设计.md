## 设计 (shèjì)

设计 encompasses the full range of design activities -- from graphic and product design to architectural planning and the design of systems or experiments -- making it indispensable in creative and technical contexts.

## Meanings
1. **[verb]** to design; to plan; to devise
2. **[noun]** design; a plan; a blueprint

## Example Sentences
**这座建筑由著名建筑师设计，风格独特。** Zhè zuò jiànzhú yóu zhùmíng jiànzhùshī shèjì, fēnggé dútè. This building was designed by a famous architect and has a distinctive style.

**产品的外观设计直接影响消费者的购买决策。** Chǎnpǐn de wàiguān shèjì zhíjiē yǐngxiǎng xiāofèizhě de gòumǎi juécè. A product's appearance design directly affects consumers' purchasing decisions.

**她花了三个月时间设计这套课程体系。** Tā huā le sān gè yuè shíjiān shèjì zhè tào kèchéng tǐxì. She spent three months designing this curriculum system.

**这个网站的界面设计简洁直观。** Zhège wǎngzhàn de jièmiàn shèjì jiǎnjié zhíguān. The interface design of this website is clean and intuitive.

**工程师正在设计一种更高效的电池。** Gōngchéngshī zhèngzài shèjì yī zhǒng gèng gāoxiào de diànchí. Engineers are designing a more efficient battery.

**这项研究的实验方案是由团队共同设计的。** Zhè xiàng yánjiū de shíyàn fāng'àn shì yóu tuánduì gòngtóng shèjì de. The experimental protocol for this study was designed jointly by the team.

## Usage Notes
设计 is both a verb and a noun, and covers design in a very broad sense: aesthetic, functional, structural, and procedural. It appears in a wide range of fields from art and architecture to engineering and education.

The verb 设计 often collocates with 方案 (fāng'àn, plan/scheme) and 系统 (xìtǒng, system). When used with a negative connotation, 精心设计 can imply a "carefully orchestrated" scheme, occasionally with a manipulative undertone depending on context.

## Memory Hook
设 (to set up/establish) + 计 (to plan/calculate): setting up a calculated plan -- to design.
