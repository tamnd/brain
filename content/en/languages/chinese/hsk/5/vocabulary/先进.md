## 先进 (xiānjìn)

先进 describes something that is at the forefront of development, ahead of current standards, and worthy of being learned from or emulated.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** advanced, progressive, cutting-edge
2. **[noun]** an advanced individual or model worker (used in socialist contexts to praise exemplary people)

## Example Sentences

**我们应该积极学习和引进先进的技术。** Wǒmen yīnggāi jījí xuéxí hé yǐnjìn xiānjìn de jìshù. We should actively learn from and introduce advanced technology.

**这家工厂采用了最先进的生产设备。** Zhè jiā gōngchǎng cǎiyòng le zuì xiānjìn de shēngchǎn shèbèi. This factory has adopted the most advanced production equipment.

**先进的教育理念强调学生的主动参与。** Xiānjìn de jiàoyù lǐniàn qiángdiào xuéshēng de zhǔdòng cānyù. Advanced educational concepts emphasize active student participation.

**我们要向先进学习，找出自身的不足。** Wǒmen yào xiàng xiānjìn xuéxí, zhǎochū zìshēn de bùzú. We should learn from those ahead of us and identify our own shortcomings.

**引进先进的管理经验对企业发展至关重要。** Yǐnjìn xiānjìn de guǎnlǐ jīngyàn duì qǐyè fāzhǎn zhìguān zhòngyào. Introducing advanced management experience is crucial for enterprise development.

**这项先进的医疗技术已在多家医院推广应用。** Zhè xiàng xiānjìn de yīliáo jìshù yǐ zài duō jiā yīyuàn tuīguǎng yìngyòng. This advanced medical technology has been promoted and applied in many hospitals.

## Usage Notes

先进 carries a strong positive connotation and is widely used in formal writing, news reports, and official discourse. It often implies not just technical advancement but also being a model worthy of imitation. In Chinese political and social contexts, 先进 also serves as a commendation title -- 先进工作者 (advanced worker) or 先进集体 (advanced collective) -- given to exemplary individuals or organizations.

At B2 level, 先进 is essential for discussing technology, education, and social progress. The contrast between 先进 (advanced) and 落后 (backward/lagging) is a common rhetorical pairing in analytical Chinese writing.

## Memory Hook

先 means "first/ahead" and 进 means "to advance" -- 先进 is literally "advancing ahead," the one who leads the way forward.
