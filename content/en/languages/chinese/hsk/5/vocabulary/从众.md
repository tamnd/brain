## 从众 (cóngzhòng)

从众 describes the social phenomenon where individuals change their behavior, opinions, or actions to match the majority or the group. It is a core concept in social psychology, closely related to peer pressure and groupthink. While 从众 can promote social harmony, excessive conformity may suppress individuality and independent thinking.

## Meanings
1. **noun/verb** conformity; to conform, to follow the crowd, to go along with the majority

## Example Sentences
**从众心理是人类社会中普遍存在的现象。** Cóngzhòng xīnlǐ shì rénlèi shèhuì zhōng pǔbiàn cúnzài de xiànxiàng. The conformity mentality is a universally present phenomenon in human society.

**她拒绝从众，坚持了自己的看法。** Tā jùjué cóngzhòng, jiānchí le zìjǐ de kànfǎ. She refused to conform and stuck to her own view.

**从众行为在青少年群体中尤其常见。** Cóngzhòng xíngwéi zài qīngshàonián qúntǐ zhōng yóuqí chángjiàn. Conformist behavior is especially common among teenagers.

**盲目从众可能导致错误的决策。** Mángmù cóngzhòng kěnéng dǎozhì cuòwù de juécè. Blindly following the crowd can lead to wrong decisions.

**研究发现压力越大从众倾向越明显。** Yánjiū fāxiàn yālì yuè dà cóngzhòng qīngxiàng yuè míngxiǎn. Research finds that the greater the pressure, the stronger the tendency to conform.

**他不愿从众，总是寻找独特的解决方案。** Tā bù yuàn cóngzhòng, zǒngshì xúnzhǎo dútè de jiějué fāng'àn. He was unwilling to conform and always looked for unique solutions.

## Usage Notes
从众 is used both as a verb (to conform) and as a noun modifier (conformity). The compound 从众心理 (conformity psychology/mentality) is extremely common in Chinese social psychology discussions. 从众 carries a slightly negative connotation of losing one's individuality, but it can be neutral in descriptive contexts.

Do not confuse 从众 with 服从 (to obey/submit), which implies obeying authority rather than following a peer group.

## Memory Hook
从 means "to follow" and 众 means "the masses/crowd" — 从众 is literally following the crowd.
