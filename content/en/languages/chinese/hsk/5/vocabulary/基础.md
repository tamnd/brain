## 基础 (jīchǔ)

基础 refers to the foundation or basis upon which something is built, whether physical, intellectual, or institutional, implying the indispensable groundwork that supports everything above it.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** foundation, basis, groundwork (physical or abstract)
2. **[adjective]** basic, foundational (as in 基础教育, basic education)

## Example Sentences

**扎实的理论基础是进行高水平研究的前提。** Zhāshí de lǐlùn jīchǔ shì jìnxíng gāo shuǐpíng yánjiū de qiántí. A solid theoretical foundation is a prerequisite for conducting high-level research.

**诚信是社会文明的重要基础。** Chéngxìn shì shèhuì wénmíng de zhòng yào jīchǔ. Integrity is an important foundation of social civilization.

**基础教育的质量直接影响国家未来的发展潜力。** Jīchǔ jiàoyù de zhìliàng zhíjiē yǐngxiǎng guójiā wèilái de fāzhǎn qiánlì. The quality of basic education directly affects a country's future development potential.

**这栋大楼的地基基础出了问题，整体结构受到威胁。** Zhè dòng dàlóu de dìjī jīchǔ chū le wèntí, zhěngtǐ jiégòu shòudào wēixié. The foundation of this building has a problem, and the overall structure is threatened.

**他的成功得益于他在青年时期打下的坚实基础。** Tā de chénggōng déyì yú tā zài qīngnián shíqī dǎ xià de jiānshí jīchǔ. His success is due to the solid foundation he laid in his youth.

**在现有基础上进一步创新，是这个团队的目标。** Zài xiànyǒu jīchǔ shàng jìnyībù chuàngxīn, shì zhège tuánduì de mùbiāo. Further innovation on the existing foundation is the goal of this team.

## Usage Notes

基础 is one of the most fundamental and widely used nouns in Chinese. It works both as a standalone noun (他的中文基础很好 -- his Chinese foundation is solid) and as an adjective modifier (基础教育, 基础研究). The preposition phrase 在...基础上 (on the basis of, building on) is a very common construction in academic and official writing.

At B2 level, 基础 is essential for virtually every domain of academic, professional, and everyday discourse involving preparation, prerequisites, and groundwork.

## Memory Hook

基 means a base or foundation (like the base of a building), and 础 also means a plinth or base stone -- 基础 is doubly foundational, the base stones upon which everything rests.
