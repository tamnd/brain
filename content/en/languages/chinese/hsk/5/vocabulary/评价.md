## 评价 (píngjià)

评价 means to assess or judge the value, quality, or significance of something, and is widely used in academic, critical, and professional discourse.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to evaluate; to assess; to appraise
2. **[noun]** evaluation; assessment; judgment

## Example Sentences

**这部电影获得了观众的高度评价。** Zhè bù diànyǐng huòdéle guānzhòng de gāodù píngjià. This film received high praise from audiences.

**我们应该客观地评价历史人物。** Wǒmen yīnggāi kèguān de píngjià lìshǐ rénwù. We should evaluate historical figures objectively.

**老师对他的作文给出了详细的评价。** Lǎoshī duì tā de zuòwén gěichūle xiángxì de píngjià. The teacher gave a detailed evaluation of his essay.

**该项目的综合评价非常积极。** Gāi xiàngmù de zōnghé píngjià fēicháng jījí. The overall assessment of the project was very positive.

**社会对这项政策的评价存在分歧。** Shèhuì duì zhè xiàng zhèngcè de píngjià cúnzài fēnqí. Public opinion on this policy is divided.

**他的学术成就受到了国际学界的广泛评价。** Tā de xuéshù chéngjiù shòudàole guójì xuéjiè de guǎngfàn píngjià. His academic achievements have been widely assessed by the international scholarly community.

## Usage Notes

评价 carries a sense of making a considered, reasoned judgment and is more formal than 评论 (pínglùn, comment/review), which can be more casual or one-sided. While 评论 often refers to commentary or critique in media contexts, 评价 implies a balanced or official assessment. The construction 对……作出评价 or 对……进行评价 is common in formal writing.

Note that 高度评价 (to speak highly of) is a fixed phrase used frequently in official and academic language. At B2 level, learners should distinguish between using 评价 as a noun (一个客观的评价) and as a verb (评价一部作品).

## Memory Hook

PING (评, to comment) on the JIA (价, value/price) tag of something means to evaluate its worth.
