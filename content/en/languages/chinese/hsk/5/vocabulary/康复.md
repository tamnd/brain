## 康复 (kāngfù)

Recovery or rehabilitation refers to the process of returning to normal health and functioning after illness, injury, or a difficult period.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to recover; to recuperate; to return to health
2. **[noun]** recovery; rehabilitation; convalescence

## Example Sentences

**经过三个月的治疗，他终于康复出院了。** Jīngguò sān gè yuè de zhìliáo, tā zhōngyú kāngfù chūyuànle. After three months of treatment, he finally recovered and was discharged from the hospital.

**康复训练帮助他重新获得了行走能力。** Kāngfù xùnliàn bāngzhù tā chóngxīn huòdéle xíngzǒu nénglì. Rehabilitation training helped him regain his ability to walk.

**医生建议他手术后进行系统的康复治疗。** Yīshēng jiànyì tā shǒushù hòu jìnxíng xìtǒng de kāngfù zhìliáo. The doctor advised him to undergo systematic rehabilitation treatment after surgery.

**她患病后积极配合治疗，康复情况良好。** Tā huànbìng hòu jījí pèihé zhìliáo, kāngfù qíngkuàng liánghǎo. After falling ill, she actively cooperated with treatment and her recovery is going well.

**心理康复和身体康复同样重要。** Xīnlǐ kāngfù hé shēntǐ kāngfù tóngyàng zhòngyào. Psychological recovery is just as important as physical recovery.

**康复中心为患者提供专业的物理治疗和职业培训。** Kāngfù zhōngxīn wèi huànzhě tígōng zhuānyè de wùlǐ zhìliáo hé zhíyè péixùn. The rehabilitation center provides patients with professional physiotherapy and vocational training.

## Usage Notes

康复 is primarily used in medical and health contexts. As a verb it means "to recover" (他康复了 -- he has recovered). As a noun or attributive, it modifies healthcare-related terms (康复治疗 -- rehabilitation therapy; 康复中心 -- rehabilitation center).

康复 implies a full return to health rather than merely stopping being sick. It is more formal than the common word 好 (to get better) and is appropriate in medical, journalistic, and formal spoken contexts. The field of 康复医学 (rehabilitation medicine) uses this term extensively.

## Memory Hook

康 (health/well-being) + 复 (to return/restore) -- to return to health, restoring your body to its natural healthy state.
