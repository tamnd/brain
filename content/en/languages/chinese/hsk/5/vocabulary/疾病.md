## 疾病 (jíbìng)

疾病 is the standard formal term for disease or illness, used in medical, public health, and academic contexts to refer to any disorder affecting the body or mind.

## Meanings
1. **[noun]** disease; illness; sickness

## Example Sentences
**慢性疾病的预防比治疗更为重要。** Mànxìng jíbìng de yùfáng bǐ zhìliáo gèng wéi zhòngyào. Prevention of chronic diseases is more important than treatment.

**传染性疾病可以通过多种途径在人群中传播。** Chuánrǎnxìng jíbìng kěyǐ tōngguò duō zhǒng tújìng zài rénqún zhōng chuánbō. Infectious diseases can spread through populations via multiple routes.

**良好的生活习惯有助于降低患疾病的风险。** Liánghǎo de shēnghuó xíguàn yǒuzhù yú jiàngdī huàn jíbìng de fēngxiǎn. Good lifestyle habits help reduce the risk of contracting disease.

**医学研究的进步使许多曾经无法治愈的疾病得到了控制。** Yīxué yánjiū de jìnbù shǐ xǔduō céngjīng wúfǎ zhìyù de jíbìng dédào le kòngzhì. Medical advances have brought under control many diseases that were once incurable.

**心理疾病和身体疾病一样需要得到重视和治疗。** Xīnlǐ jíbìng hé shēntǐ jíbìng yīyàng xūyào dédào zhòngshì hé zhìliáo. Mental illness deserves the same attention and treatment as physical disease.

**该地区缺乏医疗资源，导致许多疾病得不到及时救治。** Gāi dìqū quēfá yīliáo zīyuán, dǎozhì xǔduō jíbìng dé bu dào jíshí jiùzhì. The area lacks medical resources, causing many diseases to go without timely treatment.

## Usage Notes
疾病 is the formal and standard term for disease or illness in Chinese. In casual speech, people often use 病 (bìng) alone or 生病 (shēngbìng, to get sick). 疾病 is preferred in medical writing, policy documents, and news media.

The two characters are near-synonyms: 疾 originally meant a less serious or quickly-onset illness, while 病 meant a more severe or chronic condition. Combined, 疾病 covers the full range of diseases and illnesses. Compare with 症状 (zhèngzhuàng, symptoms), which refers to the observable signs of a disease.

## Memory Hook
疾 (swift illness) + 病 (sickness) -- two words for being sick combined into one comprehensive term for disease.
