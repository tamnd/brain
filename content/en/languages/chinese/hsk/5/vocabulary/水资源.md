## 水资源 (shuǐ zīyuán)

水资源 refers to the available supplies of fresh water on Earth, including rivers, lakes, groundwater, and rainfall, that can be used for human consumption, agriculture, and industry.

## Meanings
1. **[noun]** water resources; freshwater supply

## Example Sentences
**全球水资源分布极不均匀，许多地区面临严重缺水问题。** Quánqiú shuǐ zīyuán fēnbù jí bù jūnyún, xǔduō dìqū miànlín yánzhòng quēshuǐ wèntí. Global water resources are very unevenly distributed, and many regions face serious water shortages.

**保护水资源是每个公民的责任。** Bǎohù shuǐ zīyuán shì měi gè gōngmín de zérèn. Protecting water resources is every citizen's responsibility.

**农业是消耗水资源最多的行业之一。** Nóngyè shì xiāohào shuǐ zīyuán zuì duō de hángyè zhī yī. Agriculture is one of the industries that consumes the most water resources.

**气候变化正在加剧全球水资源短缺问题。** Qìhòu biànhuà zhèngzài jiājù quánqiú shuǐ zīyuán duǎnquē wèntí. Climate change is exacerbating the problem of global water resource scarcity.

**这个国家正在投资新技术来更有效地管理水资源。** Zhège guójiā zhèngzài tóuzī xīn jìshù lái gèng yǒuxiào de guǎnlǐ shuǐ zīyuán. This country is investing in new technology to manage water resources more effectively.

**水资源的合理利用对可持续发展至关重要。** Shuǐ zīyuán de hélǐ lìyòng duì kěchíxù fāzhǎn zhìguān zhòngyào. The rational use of water resources is vital to sustainable development.

## Usage Notes
水资源 is a formal compound noun combining 水 (water) and 资源 (resources/natural resources). It is used in environmental, geographical, and policy contexts. The word emphasizes water as a finite, manageable resource rather than simply the substance of water.

When discussing water issues in Chinese, 水资源 is the standard formal term. In everyday conversation, people might just say 水 (water) or refer to specific forms like 地下水 (groundwater) or 河流 (rivers). The compound 资源 implies that water is being treated as a natural asset to be managed and conserved.

## Memory Hook
水 = water, 资 = assets/resources, 源 = source/origin -- the sourced assets of water, i.e., water as a natural resource to manage.
