## 网络安全 (wǎngluò ānquán)

网络安全 refers to the protection of computer networks, systems, and data from unauthorised access, cyberattacks, and digital threats. It is a key term in technology, policy, and national security discourse.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** cybersecurity; network security; information security in digital environments

## Example Sentences

**网络安全是国家安全的重要组成部分，直接关系到社会稳定。** Wǎngluò ānquán shì guójiā ānquán de zhòngyào zǔchéng bùfèn, zhíjiē guānxì dào shèhuì wěndìng. Cybersecurity is an important component of national security and directly affects social stability.

**企业应建立完善的网络安全防护体系，定期进行安全漏洞检测。** Qǐyè yīng jiànlì wánshàn de wǎngluò ānquán fánghù tǐxì, dìngqī jìnxíng ānquán lòudòng jiǎncè. Enterprises should establish a comprehensive cybersecurity defence system and conduct regular security vulnerability assessments.

**随着物联网的普及，家用设备也面临越来越多的网络安全威胁。** Suízhe wùliánwǎng de pǔjí, jiāyòng shèbèi yě miànlín yuè lái yuè duō de wǎngluò ānquán wēixié. With the spread of the Internet of Things, household devices also face an increasing number of cybersecurity threats.

**各国政府纷纷出台网络安全法规，以应对跨境网络攻击事件。** Gè guó zhèngfǔ fēnfēn chūtái wǎngluò ānquán fǎguī, yǐ yìngduì kuà jìng wǎngluò gōngjī shìjiàn. Governments around the world are introducing cybersecurity laws and regulations to address cross-border cyberattack incidents.

**个人用户也应提高网络安全意识，不随意点击不明链接。** Gèrén yònghù yě yīng tígāo wǎngluò ānquán yìshí, bù suíyì diǎnjī bùmíng liànjiē. Individual users should also improve their cybersecurity awareness and avoid clicking on unknown links carelessly.

**这所大学新设立了网络安全专业，以满足社会对专业人才的迫切需求。** Zhè suǒ dàxué xīn shèlìle wǎngluò ānquán zhuānyè, yǐ mǎnzú shèhuì duì zhuānyè réncái de pòqiè xūqiú. This university has newly established a cybersecurity major to meet society's urgent demand for specialised professionals.

## Usage Notes

网络安全 is the standard Chinese term covering what English calls "cybersecurity," "network security," and "information security" in digital contexts. China's 《网络安全法》(Cybersecurity Law, 2017) gave the term legal and regulatory weight, making it central to current affairs reading.

At B2 level, learners should know that 维护网络安全 (safeguard cybersecurity) and 网络安全威胁 (cybersecurity threats) are the most productive collocations in reading and writing tasks about technology and national security.

## Memory Hook

网络 (network) + 安全 (safety/security) -- keeping the network safe: cybersecurity.
