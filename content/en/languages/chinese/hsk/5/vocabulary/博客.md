## 博客 (bókè)

博客 is the Chinese term for a blog -- an online platform where an individual regularly publishes written content, opinions, and experiences, typically in reverse chronological order.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** blog (an online journal or website of personal content)
2. **[noun]** blogger (a person who writes a blog, by extension)

## Example Sentences

**他通过博客分享自己在旅行中的所见所闻，积累了大量粉丝。** Tā tōngguò bókè fēnxiǎng zìjǐ zài lǚxíng zhōng de suǒ jiàn suǒ wén, jīlěi le dàliàng fěnsī. He shared his travel observations and experiences through a blog and accumulated a large number of followers.

**写博客是一种有效的方式，可以锻炼写作能力和整理思维。** Xiě bókè shì yī zhǒng yǒuxiào de fāngshì, kěyǐ duànliàn xiězuò nénglì hé zhěnglǐ sīwéi. Writing a blog is an effective way to practice writing skills and organize one's thinking.

**这位科技博客作者每周发布关于最新科技趋势的深度分析文章。** Zhè wèi kējì bókè zuòzhě měi zhōu fābù guānyú zuìxīn kējì qūshì de shēndù fēnxī wénzhāng. This tech blogger publishes in-depth analysis articles on the latest technology trends every week.

**随着社交媒体的兴起，传统博客的影响力有所下降。** Suízhe shèjiāo méitǐ de xīngqǐ, chuántǒng bókè de yǐngxiǎng lì yǒusuǒ xiàjiàng. With the rise of social media, the influence of traditional blogs has somewhat declined.

**许多企业建立了官方博客，用于发布公司新闻和行业见解。** Xǔduō qǐyè jiànlì le guānfāng bókè, yòng yú fābù gōngsī xīnwén hé hángyè jiànjiě. Many companies have established official blogs for publishing company news and industry insights.

**他的个人博客已经运营了十年，积累了超过一千篇文章。** Tā de gèrén bókè yǐjīng yùnyíng le shí nián, jīlěi le chāoguò yīqiān piān wénzhāng. His personal blog has been running for ten years and has accumulated more than a thousand articles.

## Usage Notes

博客 is a phonetic loanword translation of "blog" (bó-kè sounds like "blog"). It was coined during the early 2000s blogging era and became extremely popular. The character 博 also means "broad/extensive," giving a secondary meaning of "extensive/rich writing," which is a clever choice.

In contemporary Chinese digital culture, traditional blogs (博客) have been largely superseded by social media platforms like 微博 (Weibo, microblog), 微信公众号 (WeChat public account), and short-video platforms. However, 博客 remains the standard term in Chinese, and long-form personal blogs still exist. A blogger is called 博主 (bózhǔ) or 博客作者 (bókè zuòzhě).

## Memory Hook

博 means "broad/extensive" and 客 means "guest/visitor" -- a 博客 invites visitors to a broad, extensive collection of personal thoughts, a home where anyone can come and read.
