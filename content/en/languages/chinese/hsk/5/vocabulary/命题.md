## 命题 (mìngtí)

命题 has two related meanings: as a noun it means a proposition, thesis, or stated problem; as a verb it means to set the topic for an essay, exam, or debate.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** proposition; thesis; problem statement; stated topic
2. **[verb]** to set a topic; to formulate a question; to propose a problem (for an exam or essay)

## Example Sentences

**这道数学题的命题非常清晰。** Zhè dào shùxué tí de mìngtí fēicháng qīngxī. The proposition of this math problem is very clear.

**高考作文的命题每年都会有所不同。** Gāokǎo zuòwén de mìngtí měinián dōu huì yǒusuǒ bùtóng. The topic set for college entrance exam essays varies every year.

**这是一个关于自由与责任的哲学命题。** Zhè shì yī gè guānyú zìyóu yǔ zérèn de zhéxué mìngtí. This is a philosophical proposition about freedom and responsibility.

**老师负责为本次考试命题。** Lǎoshī fùzé wèi běn cì kǎoshì mìngtí. The teacher is responsible for setting the questions for this exam.

**这个命题引发了学术界的广泛讨论。** Zhège mìngtí yǐnfā le xuéshujiè de guǎngfàn tǎolùn. This proposition sparked widespread discussion in academic circles.

**辩论赛的命题是关于人工智能的伦理问题。** Biànlùnsài de mìngtí shì guānyú réngōng zhìnéng de lúnlǐ wèntí. The proposition for the debate was about the ethical issues of artificial intelligence.

## Usage Notes

命题 is used in both academic/philosophical contexts and in educational settings. In philosophy and logic, a 命题 is a declarative statement that can be evaluated as true or false. In education, the verb sense (to set a topic) is common in discussions of exam or essay design.

The compound 命题作文 (essay with a given topic) is a specific genre of writing in Chinese education where the topic is provided by the teacher or exam board. This contrasts with 话题作文 (topic essay) where only a theme is suggested.

## Memory Hook

命 means "to name/decree" and 题 means "topic/question." 命题 is to decree a topic -- the official naming of what must be discussed or proven.
