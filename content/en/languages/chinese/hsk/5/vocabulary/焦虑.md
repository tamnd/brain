## 焦虑 (jiāolǜ)

焦虑 describes the emotional state of anxiety, worry, and unease -- a persistent feeling of apprehension about uncertain outcomes or future events.

## Meanings
1. **[noun]** anxiety; worry; apprehension
2. **[adj]** anxious; worried; uneasy

## Example Sentences
**考试前夕，很多学生都会感到焦虑。** Kǎoshì qiányè, hěn duō xuéshēng dōu huì gǎndào jiāolǜ. On the eve of an exam, many students feel anxious.

**长期的工作压力让她产生了严重的焦虑情绪。** Chángqī de gōngzuò yālì ràng tā chǎnshēngle yánzhòng de jiāolǜ qíngxù. Long-term work pressure caused her to develop serious anxiety.

**他因为对未来感到迷茫而整天处于焦虑之中。** Tā yīnwèi duì wèilái gǎndào mímáng ér zhěngtiān chǔyú jiāolǜ zhī zhōng. He was anxious all day because he felt lost about the future.

**冥想和运动是缓解焦虑的有效方法。** Míngxiǎng hé yùndòng shì huǎnjiě jiāolǜ de yǒuxiào fāngfǎ. Meditation and exercise are effective ways to relieve anxiety.

**父母对孩子的教育质量产生了很大的焦虑感。** Fùmǔ duì háizi de jiàoyù zhìliàng chǎnshēngle hěn dà de jiāolǜ gǎn. Parents have developed a strong sense of anxiety about the quality of their children's education.

**心理咨询师帮助他识别和应对焦虑的根源。** Xīnlǐ zīxúnshī bāngzhù tā shíbié hé yìngduì jiāolǜ de gēnyuán. The counselor helped him identify and address the root causes of his anxiety.

## Usage Notes
焦虑 is used as both a noun and an adjective. As a noun, it names the condition (感到焦虑 -- feel anxiety, 产生焦虑 -- develop anxiety). As an adjective, it describes the emotional state (焦虑的心情 -- anxious mood).

比较 焦虑 with 担心 (dānxīn, to worry): 担心 is a milder, everyday word for worrying about specific things, while 焦虑 implies a deeper, more persistent psychological state of anxiety. In mental health contexts, 焦虑症 (anxiety disorder) is the clinical term.

## Memory Hook
焦 = scorched/anxious (the character shows something burning -- like nerves on fire), 虑 = to consider/worry -- burning with worry -- anxiety.
