## 网络 (wǎngluò)

网络 literally means a net-like structure. In modern Chinese it refers to computer networks, the internet, and the online world in general. It is a broader and more versatile term than 互联网 (the global Internet) and can refer to any type of network.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** network (computer, social, transport, etc.)
2. **[noun]** the internet; the online world (colloquially)
3. **[adjective]** online; internet-based; cyber

## Example Sentences

**网络谣言传播速度极快，往往在核实之前已造成严重社会影响。** Wǎngluò yáoyán chuánbō sùdù jí kuài, wǎngwǎng zài héshí zhīqián yǐ zàochéng yánzhòng shèhuì yǐngxiǎng. Online rumours spread extremely fast, often causing serious social impact before they can be verified.

**这家公司建立了覆盖全国的物流配送网络，保证了次日达服务。** Zhè jiā gōngsī jiànlìle fùgài quánguó de wùliú pèisòng wǎngluò, bǎozhèngle cì rì dá fúwù. This company has established a logistics and distribution network covering the whole country, guaranteeing next-day delivery.

**网络购物的便利性使得越来越多的消费者选择在线消费。** Wǎngluò gòuwù de biànlìxìng shǐdé yuè lái yuè duō de xiāofèizhě xuǎnzé zàixiàn xiāofèi. The convenience of online shopping has led more and more consumers to choose to shop online.

**政府加强了对网络内容的监管，打击有害信息的传播。** Zhèngfǔ jiāqiángle duì wǎngluò nèiróng de jiāngguǎn, dǎjí yǒuhài xìnxī de chuánbō. The government has strengthened oversight of online content to combat the spread of harmful information.

**社交网络改变了人们的交流方式，也带来了新的隐私挑战。** Shèjiāo wǎngluò gǎibiànle rénmen de jiāoliú fāngshì, yě dàiláile xīn de yǐnsī tiǎozhàn. Social networks have changed the way people communicate and also brought new privacy challenges.

**在偏远地区扩大网络覆盖范围是缩小数字鸿沟的关键一步。** Zài piānyuǎn dìqū kuòdà wǎngluò fùgài fànwéi shì suōxiǎo shùzì hónggōu de guānjiàn yī bù. Expanding network coverage in remote areas is a key step in narrowing the digital divide.

## Usage Notes

网络 is broader than 互联网 (the Internet). 互联网 specifically means the global public internet, while 网络 can be any network: a railway network (铁路网络), a supply chain network (供应链网络), a social network (社交网络), or the internet in general.

As an adjective/modifier, 网络 commonly appears before nouns: 网络时代 (internet age), 网络文化 (internet culture), 网络游戏 (online game). This productive use makes it one of the most important modifier words in contemporary Chinese.

## Memory Hook

网 (net) + 络 (entwine/link) -- a net whose threads are all linked together, forming a network that catches and connects everything.
