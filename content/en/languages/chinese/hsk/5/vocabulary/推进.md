## 推进 (tuījìn)

To push something forward in a deliberate, organized way -- used for projects, reforms, and policies that require sustained effort over time.

## Meanings
1. **[verb]** to advance, to push forward, to drive (a plan or process)

## Example Sentences
**政府正在积极推进绿色能源改革。** Zhèngfǔ zhèngzài jījí tuījìn lǜsè néngyuán gǎigé. The government is actively pushing forward green energy reforms.

**这个项目的推进速度超出了预期。** Zhège xiàngmù de tuījìn sùdù chāochūle yùqī. The pace at which this project advanced exceeded expectations.

**公司决定推进数字化转型战略。** Gōngsī juédìng tuījìn shùzìhuà zhuǎnxíng zhànlüè. The company decided to push forward its digital transformation strategy.

**推进教育公平是本届政府的核心目标。** Tuījìn jiàoyù gōngpíng shì běn jiè zhèngfǔ de héxīn mùbiāo. Advancing educational equity is the core goal of this administration.

**双方同意共同推进贸易谈判。** Shuāngfāng tóngyì gòngtóng tuījìn màoyì tánpàn. Both sides agreed to jointly push forward trade negotiations.

**这项技术的推进需要大量资金支持。** Zhè xiàng jìshù de tuījìn xūyào dàliàng zījīn zhīchí. Advancing this technology requires substantial financial support.

## Usage Notes
推进 implies deliberate, organized effort over time and is almost always used in formal or semi-formal contexts -- news articles, policy documents, and business reports. It differs from 推动 (tuīdòng), which emphasizes initiating or giving an impetus, while 推进 stresses the ongoing process of moving something along a defined path.

At B2 level, learners should note that 推进 frequently pairs with abstract nouns like 改革, 战略, and 进程, making it a key word for reading Chinese news or discussing current affairs.

## Memory Hook
Picture a bulldozer (推 = push) slowly advancing (进 = enter/move forward) along a construction site -- it does not stop, it just keeps pushing ahead steadily.
