## 外汇 (wàihuì)

外汇 refers to foreign exchange or foreign currency -- money of foreign countries used in international trade and financial transactions, as well as the system for exchanging currencies.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** foreign exchange; foreign currency; forex

## Example Sentences

**中国拥有全球最大的外汇储备之一。** Zhōngguó yōngyǒu quánqiú zuìdà de wàihuì chǔbèi zhī yī. China has one of the largest foreign exchange reserves in the world.

**出口贸易是该国获取外汇的主要来源。** Chūkǒu màoyì shì gāi guó huòqǔ wàihuì de zhǔyào láiyuán. Export trade is the main source of foreign exchange earnings for the country.

**外汇市场每天的交易量高达数万亿美元。** Wàihuì shìchǎng měitiān de jiāoyì liàng gāodá shù wàn yì měiyuán. The foreign exchange market has a daily trading volume of trillions of dollars.

**政府对外汇流出实施了严格的管制措施。** Zhèngfǔ duì wàihuì liúchū shíshī le yángé de guǎnzhì cuòshī. The government implemented strict controls on foreign exchange outflows.

**去国外旅行前，最好先换好外汇。** Qù guówài lǚxíng qián, zuì hǎo xiān huàn hǎo wàihuì. Before traveling abroad, it is best to exchange foreign currency in advance.

**外汇汇率的波动对进出口企业影响巨大。** Wàihuì huìlǜ de bōdòng duì jìnchūkǒu qǐyè yǐngxiǎng jùdà. Fluctuations in foreign exchange rates have a huge impact on import and export companies.

## Usage Notes

外汇 is a key term in economics, finance, and international trade. 外 means "foreign" and 汇 means "remittance/exchange," so 外汇 is foreign remittance -- money flowing from abroad or currency used internationally.

外汇储备 (foreign exchange reserves) is an important economic indicator widely discussed in Chinese financial news. The forex market is a standard topic in economics education at the B2 level, and understanding 外汇管制 (foreign exchange controls) is important for understanding Chinese economic policy.

## Memory Hook

外 means "foreign/outside" and 汇 means "to converge/remit money." 外汇 is foreign money that flows in -- the currency coming from outside the country's borders.
