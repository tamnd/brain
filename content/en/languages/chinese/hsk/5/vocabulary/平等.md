## 平等 (píngděng)

Equality means the state of being equal, particularly in terms of rights, status, and opportunities, without discrimination based on gender, race, class, or other factors.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** equality; equal standing; parity
2. **[adjective]** equal; on an equal footing; equitable

## Example Sentences

**宪法保障所有公民在法律面前一律平等。** Xiànfǎ bǎozhàng suǒyǒu gōngmín zài fǎlǜ miànqián yīlǜ píngděng. The constitution guarantees that all citizens are equal before the law.

**性别平等是现代社会追求的重要目标之一。** Xìngbié píngděng shì xiàndài shèhuì zhuīqiú de zhòngyào mùbiāo zhī yī. Gender equality is one of the important goals pursued by modern society.

**谈判双方在平等互利的基础上达成了协议。** Tánpàn shuāngfāng zài píngděng hùlì de jīchǔ shàng dáchéngle xiéyì. The two negotiating parties reached an agreement on the basis of equality and mutual benefit.

**教育平等意味着每个孩子都应该获得优质教育的机会。** Jiàoyù píngděng yìwèizhe měi gè háizi dōu yīnggāi huòdé yōuzhì jiàoyù de jīhuì. Educational equality means every child should have access to quality education.

**他坚持在职场中争取平等的晋升机会。** Tā jiānchí zài zhíchǎng zhōng zhēngqǔ píngděng de jìnshēng jīhuì. He persisted in fighting for equal promotion opportunities in the workplace.

**真正的平等不仅是权利上的平等，也是机会上的平等。** Zhēnzhèng de píngděng bùjǐn shì quánlì shàng de píngděng, yě shì jīhuì shàng de píngděng. True equality is not only equality of rights but also equality of opportunity.

## Usage Notes

平等 is a fundamental value term used in legal, social, and political discourse. As an adjective, it describes equal treatment or status (平等的机会 -- equal opportunities). As a noun, it refers to the principle or state of equality (追求平等 -- to pursue equality).

Note the related term 公平 (fairness/equity), which emphasizes just treatment, while 平等 specifically emphasizes equal standing. In international relations, 平等互利 (equality and mutual benefit) is a standard diplomatic phrase in Chinese foreign policy language.

## Memory Hook

平 (level/flat) + 等 (rank/grade) -- when everyone is on a flat, level playing field with no one ranked higher or lower, that is 平等.
