## 相对 (xiāngduì)

相对 indicates that something is relative rather than absolute, or that two things face or relate to each other, and is commonly used in academic and analytical discourse.

## Meanings
1. **[adverb]** relatively; comparatively
2. **[adjective]** relative; mutual; face-to-face

## Example Sentences
**这个城市的生活成本相对较低，适合年轻人居住。** Zhège chéngshì de shēnghuó chéngběn xiāngduì jiào dī, shìhé niánqīngrén jūzhù. The cost of living in this city is relatively low, making it suitable for young people.

**在这个问题上，双方的立场相对接近，分歧不大。** Zài zhège wèntí shàng, shuāngfāng de lìchǎng xiāngduì jiējìn, fēnqí bù dà. On this issue, the positions of both sides are relatively close, with little disagreement.

**知识是相对的，随着时代进步我们的认知也在不断更新。** Zhīshì shì xiāngduì de, suízhe shídài jìnbù wǒmen de rènzhī yě zài bùduàn gēngxīn. Knowledge is relative; our understanding is constantly updated as the times progress.

**两座山峰相对而立，形成了壮观的景象。** Liǎng zuò shānfēng xiāngduì ér lì, xíngchéng le zhuàngguān de jǐngxiàng. The two mountain peaks stand facing each other, forming a magnificent view.

**这项工作相对简单，不需要太多专业知识。** Zhè xiàng gōngzuò xiāngduì jiǎndān, bù xūyào tài duō zhuānyè zhīshì. This work is relatively simple and does not require much specialized knowledge.

**相对于传统方法，这种新技术更加高效节能。** Xiāngduì yú chuántǒng fāngfǎ, zhè zhǒng xīn jìshù gèngjiā gāoxiào jiénéng. Relative to traditional methods, this new technology is more efficient and energy-saving.

## Collocations
| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 相对较高/低 (xiāngduì jiào gāo/dī) | relatively high/low |
| 相对稳定 (xiāngduì wěndìng) | relatively stable |
| 相对于 (xiāngduì yú) | relative to; compared to |
| 相对独立 (xiāngduì dúlì) | relatively independent |
| 绝对与相对 (juéduì yǔ xiāngduì) | absolute and relative |
| 相对优势 (xiāngduì yōushì) | comparative advantage |

## Usage Notes
相对 is the antonym of 绝对 (juéduì, absolute). As an adverb, it modifies adjectives to indicate a comparative rather than absolute degree: 相对容易 (relatively easy), 相对重要 (relatively important).

The construction 相对于 + noun is useful for making explicit comparisons: 相对于去年 (relative to last year), 相对于竞争对手 (compared to competitors). This structure is common in analysis and academic writing.

## Memory Hook
相 (mutually) + 对 (facing/corresponding to) -- facing each other in relation -- relative; comparatively.
