## 信息茧房 (xìnxī jiǎnfáng)

信息茧房 refers to the phenomenon where individuals become enclosed in a self-reinforcing information environment due to algorithmic filtering and selective exposure, limiting their access to diverse viewpoints and creating an "echo chamber" effect.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** information cocoon; filter bubble; echo chamber (the phenomenon of individuals being enclosed in a self-reinforcing, algorithmically curated information environment)

## Example Sentences

**算法推荐系统在提高用户体验的同时，也加剧了信息茧房现象，让人们只看到与自己观点相符的内容。** Suànfǎ tuījiàn xìtǒng zài tígāo yònghù tǐyàn de tóngshí, yě jiājùle xìnxī jiǎnfáng xiànxiàng, ràng rénmen zhǐ kàndào yǔ zìjǐ guāndiǎn xiāng fú de nèiróng. While algorithmic recommendation systems improve user experience, they also exacerbate the information cocoon phenomenon, causing people to only see content that aligns with their own views.

**打破信息茧房需要用户主动拓展信息来源，接触不同立场和观点的内容。** Dǎpò xìnxī jiǎnfáng xūyào yònghù zhǔdòng tuòzhǎn xìnxī láiyuán, jiēchù bùtóng lìchǎng hé guāndiǎn de nèiróng. Breaking out of the information cocoon requires users to actively expand their information sources and engage with content from different positions and perspectives.

**学者警告说，社交媒体上的信息茧房效应可能加深社会分裂，削弱公民社会的凝聚力。** Xuézhě jǐnggào shuō, shèjiāo méitǐ shàng de xìnxī jiǎnfáng xiàoyìng kěnéng jiāshēn shèhuì fēnliè, xuēruò gōngmín shèhuì de níngjùlì. Scholars warn that the information cocoon effect on social media may deepen social divisions and weaken the cohesion of civil society.

**年轻人长期生活在信息茧房中，容易形成偏激的世界观，缺乏对复杂现实的全面认识。** Niánqīngrén chángqī shēnghuó zài xìnxī jiǎnfáng zhōng, róngyì xíngchéng piānjī de shìjièguān, quēfá duì fùzá xiànshí de quánmiàn rènshí. Young people who live in an information cocoon for a long time are prone to developing extreme worldviews and lack a comprehensive understanding of complex realities.

**媒体素养教育的一个重要目标是帮助学生识别和突破信息茧房的局限。** Méitǐ sùyǎng jiàoyù de yī gè zhòngyào mùbiāo shì bāngzhù xuéshēng shíbié hé tūpò xìnxī jiǎnfáng de júxiàn. One important goal of media literacy education is to help students identify and break through the limitations of the information cocoon.

**平台公司应当承担社会责任，通过技术手段减少信息茧房效应，促进多元声音的流通。** Píngtái gōngsī yīngdāng chéngdān shèhuì zérèn, tōngguò jìshù shǒuduàn jiǎnshǎo xìnxī jiǎnfáng xiàoyìng, cùjìn duōyuán shēngyīn de liútōng. Platform companies should assume social responsibility and reduce the information cocoon effect through technological means to promote the circulation of diverse voices.

## Usage Notes

信息茧房 is a Chinese term coined by legal scholar Cass Sunstein (translated into Chinese from his concept of the "information cocoon" in his book Republic.com). The metaphor of a silkworm's cocoon (茧) is apt: the cocoon is self-spun and encloses the creature inside. In Chinese digital culture and academic discourse, it is used widely to describe algorithmically reinforced echo chambers.

This term is distinctly more literary than "filter bubble" (过滤泡沫) and is preferred in Chinese academic and media writing. It frequently appears in discussions of social media platforms, recommendation algorithms, political polarization, and media literacy.

## Memory Hook

信息 (information) + 茧 (silkworm cocoon) + 房 (room/house) — you are a silkworm living inside a cocoon-room made of information, sealed off from the outside world.
