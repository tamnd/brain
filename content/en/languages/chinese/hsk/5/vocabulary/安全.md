## 安全 (ānquán)

Safety or security refers to the condition of being protected from or unlikely to cause danger, risk, or injury, and is a fundamental concern in both personal and public life.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** safe; secure; free from danger
2. **[noun]** safety; security

## Example Sentences

**这个地区的治安状况良好，居民感到很安全。** Zhège dìqū de zhì'ān zhuàngkuàng liánghǎo, jūmín gǎndào hěn ānquán. The public order in this area is good, and residents feel very safe.

**网络安全已经成为国家战略的重要组成部分。** Wǎngluò ānquán yǐjīng chéngwéi guójiā zhànlüè de zhòngyào zǔchéng bùfèn. Cybersecurity has become an important component of national strategy.

**驾驶时系好安全带是最基本的安全措施。** Jiàshǐ shí jì hǎo ānquándài shì zuì jīběn de ānquán cuòshī. Wearing a seatbelt while driving is the most basic safety measure.

**食品安全问题引起了社会各界的广泛关注。** Shípǐn ānquán wèntí yǐnqǐle shèhuì gèjiè de guǎngfàn guānzhù. Food safety issues have attracted widespread concern across society.

**在紧急情况下，确保人员安全是首要任务。** Zài jǐnjí qíngkuàng xià, quèbǎo rényuán ānquán shì shǒuyào rènwù. In an emergency, ensuring personnel safety is the top priority.

**孩子独自回家，妈妈非常担心他的安全。** Háizi dúzì huí jiā, māma fēicháng dānxīn tā de ānquán. The child went home alone, and the mother was very worried about his safety.

## Usage Notes

安全 functions as both an adjective and a noun in Chinese. As an adjective it typically follows a verb (感到安全 -- to feel safe; 保持安全 -- to stay safe). As a noun it appears before other nouns to form compounds (安全问题 -- safety issue; 安全感 -- sense of security).

At HSK 5 level, learners encounter 安全 in more specialized compound forms: 国家安全 (national security), 信息安全 (information security), 生产安全 (work/production safety). The related noun 安全感 (sense of security) is common in psychological and social discussions.

## Memory Hook

安 (peaceful/calm) + 全 (complete/whole) -- when you are completely peaceful and whole, that is true safety.
