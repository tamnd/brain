## 研发 (yánfā)

研发 is the abbreviation of 研究与开发 (research and development), and refers to the systematic activity of researching and developing new technologies, products, or processes.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to research and develop (a technology or product)
2. **[noun]** research and development; R&D

## Example Sentences

**该公司每年将营业额的15%投入研发。** Gāi gōngsī měi nián jiāng yíngyè é de 15% tóurù yánfā. The company invests 15% of its annual revenue into R&D.

**这款新药历经十年研发，终于获批上市。** Zhè kuǎn xīn yào lì jīng shí nián yánfā, zhōngyú huòpī shàngshì. This new drug, after ten years of research and development, finally received approval to go to market.

**提升自主研发能力是企业保持竞争力的关键。** Tíshēng zìzhǔ yánfā nénglì shì qǐyè bǎochí jìngzhēnglì de guānjiàn. Improving independent R&D capability is key for companies to maintain competitiveness.

**研发团队正在攻克新型电池技术的核心难题。** Yánfā tuánduì zhèngzài gōngkè xīnxíng diànchí jìshù de héxīn nántí. The R&D team is working to overcome the core technical challenges of new battery technology.

**国家对高新技术领域的研发投入逐年增加。** Guójiā duì gāoxīn jìshù lǐngyù de yánfā tóurù zhú nián zēngjiā. State investment in R&D in high-tech fields has been increasing year by year.

**这家初创公司的核心竞争力在于其强大的研发实力。** Zhè jiā chūchuàng gōngsī de héxīn jìngzhēnglì zài yú qí qiángdà de yánfā shílì. This startup's core competitiveness lies in its strong R&D capability.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|

| 自主研发 (zìzhǔ yánfā) | independent R&D |
| 研发成果 (yánfā chéngguǒ) | R&D results/output |
| 研发费用 (yánfā fèiyòng) | R&D expenditure |

## Usage Notes

研发 is primarily a business and technology term and is especially common in discussions of corporate strategy, industrial policy, and innovation. It is more specific than 研究 (yánjiū, research) alone, as it always implies a practical development dimension aimed at producing usable technology or products.

自主研发 (independent R&D) is a politically and economically significant phrase in China, contrasting with reliance on foreign technology. The phrase is frequently used in discussions of technological self-sufficiency. At B2, learners in business or technology fields will encounter 研发 constantly.

## Memory Hook

YAN (研, to research/grind into fine detail) + FA (发, to develop/release) = to research in detail and then develop into a product.
