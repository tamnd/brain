## 知识 (zhīshì)

知识 refers to the body of facts, principles, and understanding gained through experience or education, a fundamental concept in learning, science, and philosophy.

## Meanings
1. **[noun]** knowledge; information; learning

## Example Sentences
**掌握扎实的基础知识是进一步深造的前提。** Zhǎngwò zhāshi de jīchǔ zhīshì shì jìnyībù shēnzào de qiántí. Mastering solid foundational knowledge is a prerequisite for further advanced study.

**互联网使人们获取知识的方式发生了根本性变化。** Hùliánwǎng shǐ rénmen huòqǔ zhīshì de fāngshì fāshēng le gēnběnxìng biànhuà. The internet has fundamentally changed the way people acquire knowledge.

**知识就是力量，这句话在信息时代尤为重要。** Zhīshì jiùshì lìliàng, zhè jù huà zài xìnxī shídài yóuwéi zhòngyào. Knowledge is power -- this saying is especially true in the information age.

**她的法律知识帮助她在谈判中占据了有利地位。** Tā de fǎlǜ zhīshì bāngzhù tā zài tánpàn zhōng zhànjù le yǒulì dìwèi. Her knowledge of law helped her gain an advantageous position in the negotiations.

**跨学科知识的整合是解决复杂问题的关键。** Kuà xuékē zhīshì de zhěnghé shì jiějué fùzá wèntí de guānjiàn. The integration of interdisciplinary knowledge is key to solving complex problems.

**这本书系统梳理了人工智能领域的核心知识。** Zhè běn shū xìtǒng shūlǐ le réngōng zhìnéng lǐngyù de héxīn zhīshì. This book systematically organizes the core knowledge in the field of artificial intelligence.

## Usage Notes
知识 is an uncountable noun in Chinese and cannot be pluralized. It covers both abstract knowledge and practical know-how. A common distinction in Chinese is between 知识 (theoretical knowledge) and 技能 (jìnéng, skill or practical ability).

知识 appears in many important compound words: 知识分子 (zhīshì fènzǐ, intellectual), 知识产权 (intellectual property), 知识经济 (knowledge economy). These compounds are important for reading current-events and policy texts.

## Memory Hook
知 (to know) + 识 (to recognize/understand) -- knowing and recognizing things -- knowledge.
