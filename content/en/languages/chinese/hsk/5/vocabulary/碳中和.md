## 碳中和 (tàn zhōnghé)

碳中和 refers to achieving a net-zero balance between carbon dioxide emitted and carbon dioxide removed from the atmosphere, a central goal of global climate policy.

## Meanings
1. **[noun]** carbon neutrality; net-zero carbon emissions

## Example Sentences
**中国承诺在2060年前实现碳中和目标。** Zhōngguó chéngnuò zài 2060 nián qián shíxiàn tàn zhōnghé mùbiāo. China has pledged to achieve carbon neutrality by 2060.

**实现碳中和需要能源结构的根本性转变。** Shíxiàn tàn zhōnghé xūyào néngyuán jiégòu de gēnběnxìng zhuǎnbiàn. Achieving carbon neutrality requires a fundamental transformation of the energy structure.

**许多跨国企业已将碳中和纳入其可持续发展战略。** Xǔduō kuàguó qǐyè yǐ jiāng tàn zhōnghé nàrù qí kě chíxù fāzhǎn zhànlüè. Many multinational companies have incorporated carbon neutrality into their sustainable development strategies.

**种植树木是实现碳中和的重要途径之一。** Zhòngzhí shùmù shì shíxiàn tàn zhōnghé de zhòngyào tújìng zhī yī. Planting trees is one of the important paths to achieving carbon neutrality.

**碳中和目标推动了全球可再生能源的快速发展。** Tàn zhōnghé mùbiāo tuīdòng le quánqiú kě zàishēng néngyuán de kuàisù fāzhǎn. The carbon neutrality goal has driven the rapid development of global renewable energy.

**政府推出了一系列激励政策来帮助企业走向碳中和。** Zhèngfǔ tuīchū le yī xìliè jīlì zhèngcè lái bāngzhù qǐyè zǒuxiàng tàn zhōnghé. The government introduced a series of incentive policies to help enterprises move toward carbon neutrality.

## Usage Notes
碳中和 is composed of 碳 (carbon), 中 (middle/neutral), and 和 (harmony/balance). The term entered mainstream Chinese vocabulary with the rise of climate policy discussions and China's "双碳" (double carbon) goals: 碳达峰 (carbon peak) by 2030 and 碳中和 by 2060.

Related key terms include 碳排放 (tàn páifàng, carbon emissions), 碳足迹 (tàn zújì, carbon footprint), and 低碳 (dītàn, low-carbon). Understanding this vocabulary cluster is important for reading climate and sustainability texts in Chinese.

## Memory Hook
碳 (carbon) + 中和 (neutralize/balance) -- making carbon balanced at zero -- carbon neutrality.
