## 人工智能 (réngōng zhìnéng)

人工智能 refers to the simulation of human intelligence processes by computer systems, encompassing machine learning, natural language processing, and other technologies that enable machines to perform tasks that typically require human intelligence.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** artificial intelligence (AI); the field of computer science focused on creating intelligent machines

## Example Sentences

**人工智能技术正在深刻改变医疗、教育、金融等各个行业。** Réngōng zhìnéng jìshù zhèngzài shēnkè gǎibiàn yīliáo, jiàoyù, jīnróng děng gègè hángyè. Artificial intelligence technology is profoundly transforming industries including healthcare, education, and finance.

**这款人工智能助手能够理解自然语言，并提供个性化的回答和建议。** Zhè kuǎn réngōng zhìnéng zhùshǒu nénggòu lǐjiě zìrán yǔyán, bìng tígōng gèxìnghuà de huídá hé jiànyì. This AI assistant can understand natural language and provide personalized answers and suggestions.

**许多专家认为，人工智能的发展将同时带来新的就业机会和失业风险。** Xǔduō zhuānjiā rènwéi, réngōng zhìnéng de fāzhǎn jiāng tóngshí dàilái xīn de jiùyè jīhuì hé shīyè fēngxiǎn. Many experts believe that the development of AI will simultaneously bring new employment opportunities and risks of unemployment.

**在围棋领域，人工智能程序已经超越了人类最顶尖的棋手。** Zài wéiqí lǐngyù, réngōng zhìnéng chéngxù yǐjīng chāoyuèle rénlèi zuì dǐngjiān de qíshǒu. In the field of Go, AI programs have already surpassed the world's top human players.

**人工智能的伦理问题，如算法偏见和隐私保护，受到越来越多的关注。** Réngōng zhìnéng de lúnlǐ wèntí, rú suànfǎ piānjiàn hé yǐnsī bǎohù, shòudào yuèlái yuèduō de guānzhù. Ethical issues of AI, such as algorithmic bias and privacy protection, are attracting increasing attention.

**中国将人工智能列为国家战略发展重点，大力推动相关技术的研发与应用。** Zhōngguó jiāng réngōng zhìnéng lièwéi guójiā zhànlüè fāzhǎn zhòngdiǎn, dàlì tuīdòng xiāngguān jìshù de yánfā yǔ yìngyòng. China has identified AI as a national strategic development priority and is vigorously promoting the research, development, and application of related technologies.

## Usage Notes

人工智能 is often abbreviated as AI or 人工智能 in Chinese texts. It has become one of the most prominent terms in Chinese technology and policy discourse. The abbreviation AI is widely understood and used even in Chinese media alongside the full Chinese term.

The term often appears in conjunction with 大数据 (big data), 云计算 (cloud computing), 机器学习 (machine learning), and 深度学习 (deep learning). China's national AI strategy (新一代人工智能发展规划) makes this a particularly politically and economically important term.

## Memory Hook

人工 (artificial/man-made) + 智能 (intelligence/smart ability) — intelligence that is man-made, rather than natural, i.e., artificial intelligence.
