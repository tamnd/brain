## 影响 (yǐngxiǎng)

Influence or impact refers to the effect that one person, thing, or event has on another, shaping its development, behavior, or outcome.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to influence; to affect; to have an impact on
2. **[noun]** influence; impact; effect

## Example Sentences

**气候变化正在深刻影响全球的生态系统。** Qìhòu biànhuà zhèngzài shēnkè yǐngxiǎng quánqiú de shēngtài xìtǒng. Climate change is profoundly affecting global ecosystems.

**父母的言行举止对孩子有着深远的影响。** Fùmǔ de yán xíng jǔzhǐ duì háizi yǒuzhe shēnyuǎn de yǐngxiǎng. The words and actions of parents have a far-reaching influence on their children.

**这场经济危机对全球各国都产生了负面影响。** Zhè chǎng jīngjì wēijī duì quánqiú gèguó dōu chǎnshēngle fùmiàn yǐngxiǎng. This economic crisis had a negative impact on countries around the world.

**社交媒体对年轻人的价值观影响越来越大。** Shèjiāo méitǐ duì niánqīng rén de jiàzhíguān yǐngxiǎng yuèláiyuè dà. Social media's influence on the values of young people is growing.

**他的老师对他一生的发展产生了积极的影响。** Tā de lǎoshī duì tā yīshēng de fāzhǎn chǎnshēngle jījí de yǐngxiǎng. His teacher had a positive influence on his development throughout his life.

**噪音影响了居民的睡眠质量和生活品质。** Zàoyīn yǐngxiǎngle jūmín de shuìmián zhìliàng hé shēnghuó pǐnzhì. Noise has affected residents' sleep quality and quality of life.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|

| 积极影响 (jījí yǐngxiǎng) | positive influence |

| 影响力 (yǐngxiǎng lì) | influence; clout |

## Usage Notes

影响 is one of the most fundamental verbs in academic and formal Chinese. As a verb, the structure is X 影响 Y (X influences Y). As a noun, it takes measure words like 重大影响 (significant impact) or is counted as 一定的影响 (a certain influence).

Key compounds include 影响力 (influence/clout as a quality someone possesses) and 有影响力的人 (influential person). Note the difference between 影响 (neutral -- to affect) and 影响力 (the power or strength of influence as an attribute).

## Memory Hook

影 (shadow) + 响 (sound/echo) -- just as a shadow follows wherever you go and a sound echoes after it's made, influence follows and reverberates from its source.
