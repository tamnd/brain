## 专业 (zhuānyè)

专业 refers to a field of specialized study or professional expertise, and as an adjective it describes something done with high skill and expertise at a professional level.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** major; academic discipline; field of study or work
2. **[adjective]** professional; specialized; expert-level

## Example Sentences

**她大学选择了计算机科学专业，毕业后进入了一家知名科技公司。** Tā dàxué xuǎnzéle jìsuànjī kēxué zhuānyè, bìyè hòu jìnrùle yī jiā zhīmíng kējì gōngsī. She chose computer science as her major in college and joined a well-known tech company after graduation.

**这位医生的手术技术非常专业，深受患者信赖。** Zhè wèi yīshēng de shǒushù jìshù fēicháng zhuānyè, shēn shòu huànzhě xìnlài. This doctor's surgical skills are highly professional and greatly trusted by patients.

**转换专业是一个重大决定，需要仔细考虑未来的职业规划。** Zhuǎnhuàn zhuānyè shì yī gè zhòngdà juédìng, xūyào zǐxì kǎolǜ wèilái de zhíyè guīhuà. Changing majors is a major decision that requires careful consideration of future career plans.

**这家公司提供专业的法律咨询服务，客户来自各行各业。** Zhè jiā gōngsī tígōng zhuānyè de fǎlǜ zīxún fúwù, kèhù láizì gè háng gè yè. This company provides professional legal consulting services with clients from all industries.

**他在这个专业领域深耕了二十年，积累了丰富的实践经验。** Tā zài zhège zhuānyè lǐngyù shēngēngle èrshí nián, jīlěile fēngfù de shíjiàn jīngyàn. He has worked deeply in this professional field for twenty years, accumulating rich practical experience.

**大学生在选择专业时，应该综合考虑个人兴趣和就业前景。** Dàxuéshēng zài xuǎnzé zhuānyè shí, yīnggāi zōnghé kǎolǜ gèrén xìngqù hé jiùyè qiánjǐng. When choosing a major, college students should consider both personal interests and employment prospects.

## Usage Notes

专业 functions as both a noun and an adjective in Chinese. As a noun it commonly appears in educational contexts referring to a university major or a professional field. As an adjective it means "professional" or "expert-level," often contrasting with 业余 (amateur).

Note that 专业 in the sense of "major" is specific to the Chinese university system where students choose a 专业 upon entering. In professional contexts, 专业 often collocates with nouns like 团队 (team), 服务 (service), and 意见 (opinion) to emphasize quality and reliability.

## Memory Hook

专 means "dedicated/focused" and 业 means "craft/work" — 专业 is someone fully dedicated to their craft, hence "professional" or "specialized field."
