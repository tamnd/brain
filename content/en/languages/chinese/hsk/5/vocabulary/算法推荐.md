## 算法推荐 (suànfǎ tuījiàn)

算法推荐 refers to the technology by which platforms use data-driven algorithms to automatically push content, products, or information tailored to individual users based on their behaviour and preferences.

## Meanings

1. **[noun phrase]** algorithmic recommendation; algorithm-based content or product suggestion

## Example Sentences

**短视频平台依靠算法推荐吸引用户不断刷新内容。** Duǎn shìpín píngtái yīkào suànfǎ tuījiàn xīyǐn yònghù bùduàn shuāxīn nèiróng. Short-video platforms rely on algorithmic recommendation to keep users endlessly scrolling through content.

**算法推荐在提高用户体验的同时也带来了信息茧房的隐患。** Suànfǎ tuījiàn zài tígāo yònghù tǐyàn de tóngshí yě dàiláile xìnxī jiǎnfáng de yǐnhuàn. While algorithmic recommendation improves user experience, it also creates the risk of information cocoons.

**监管部门要求平台公开算法推荐的基本原则，保障用户的知情权。** Jiānguǎn bùmén yāoqiú píngtái gōngkāi suànfǎ tuījiàn de jīběn yuánzé, bǎozhàng yònghù de zhīqínquán. Regulators require platforms to disclose the basic principles of their algorithmic recommendation systems to protect users' right to information.

**研究人员发现，算法推荐容易强化用户已有的观点，形成回音壁效应。** Yánjiū rényuán fāxiàn, suànfǎ tuījiàn róngyì qiánghuà yònghù yǐyǒu de guāndiǎn, xíngchéng huíyīn bì xiàoyìng. Researchers found that algorithmic recommendation tends to reinforce users' existing views, creating an echo-chamber effect.

**电商平台通过算法推荐为每位顾客提供个性化的购物体验。** Diànshāng píngtái tōngguò suànfǎ tuījiàn wèi měi wèi gùkè tígōng gèxìnghuà de gòuwù tǐyàn. E-commerce platforms use algorithmic recommendation to provide each customer with a personalised shopping experience.

**未成年人保护法规定，针对未成年人的算法推荐不得含有不良内容。** Wèi chéngnián rén bǎohù fǎguī guīdìng, zhēnduì wèi chéngnián rén de suànfǎ tuījiàn bùdé hányǒu bùliáng nèiróng. Child protection regulations stipulate that algorithmic recommendations targeted at minors must not contain harmful content.

## Usage Notes

算法推荐 is a compound of 算法 (algorithm) and 推荐 (recommend). It is a formal, technical term widely used in discussions of social media, e-commerce, and digital regulation. At B2 level it appears frequently in opinion essays about technology and society.

The term often appears alongside 信息茧房 (information cocoon/filter bubble) and 大数据 (big data), and writers frequently debate its benefits (personalisation, efficiency) against its risks (manipulation, polarisation).

## Memory Hook

算 (calculate) + 法 (method) + 推 (push) + 荐 (recommend) -- a calculating method that pushes things it recommends straight to you.
