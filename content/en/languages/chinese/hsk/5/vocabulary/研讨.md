## 研讨 (yántǎo)

研讨 means to conduct research-based discussion or deliberation on a topic, and is most associated with formal academic, professional, or policy-making contexts like seminars and workshops.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to research and discuss; to deliberate; to examine through seminar-style discourse
2. **[verb]** to workshop; to collectively analyze and debate (a problem or topic)

## Example Sentences

**各国代表聚集一堂，研讨气候变化的应对策略。** Gèguó dàibiǎo jùjí yītáng, yántǎo qìhòu biànhuà de yìngduì cèlüè. Representatives from various countries gathered to discuss and deliberate on strategies for responding to climate change.

**这次研讨会吸引了来自全国各地的专家学者。** Zhè cì yántǎohuì xīyǐnle láizì quánguó gèdì de zhuānjiā xuézhě. This seminar attracted experts and scholars from across the country.

**课题组成员每周定期研讨最新的研究进展。** Kètí zǔ chéngyuán měi zhōu dìngqī yántǎo zuìxīn de yánjiū jìnzhǎn. The research group members regularly discuss the latest research progress each week.

**他参加了一个专门研讨人工智能伦理问题的国际论坛。** Tā cānjiāle yī gè zhuānmén yántǎo réngōng zhìnéng lúnlǐ wèntí de guójì lùntán. He participated in an international forum specially dedicated to deliberating on AI ethics issues.

**政策制定者正在研讨如何完善现有的社会保障制度。** Zhèngcè zhìdìng zhě zhèngzài yántǎo rúhé wánshàn xiànyǒu de shèhuì bǎozhàng zhìdù. Policymakers are deliberating on how to improve the existing social security system.

**该研讨班为青年学者提供了发表研究成果的机会。** Gāi yántǎo bān wèi qīngnián xuézhě tígōngle fābiǎo yánjiū chéngguǒ de jīhuì. The workshop provided young scholars with an opportunity to present their research findings.

## Usage Notes

研讨 combines 研究 (yánjiū, to research) with 讨论 (tǎolùn, to discuss), and its most common nominal form is 研讨会 (seminar/symposium), which is one of the most frequently encountered terms in academic and professional life in China. The verb form 研讨 is slightly less common than the noun 研讨会 but appears regularly in formal writing.

Compared with 探讨 (tàntǎo), which emphasizes open-ended intellectual exploration, 研讨 has a more institutional, organized quality, implying a formal group process. At B2, learners should know both 研讨 and 研讨会 as key academic vocabulary.

## Memory Hook

YAN (研, to grind/research finely) + TAO (讨, to discuss) = to finely research and then discuss together.
