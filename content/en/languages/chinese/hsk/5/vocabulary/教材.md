## 教材 (jiàocái)

The printed, digital, or multimedia resources used in formal teaching -- broader than just textbooks and including all instructional content.

## Meanings
1. **[noun]** teaching material, textbook, instructional resource

## Example Sentences
**这套教材专为中级汉语学习者设计。** Zhè tào jiàocái zhuān wèi zhōngjí Hànyǔ xuéxízhě shèjì. This set of teaching materials is designed specifically for intermediate Chinese learners.

**学校决定更新老化的数学教材以适应新课程标准。** Xuéxiào juédìng gēngxīn lǎohuà de shùxué jiàocái yǐ shìyìng xīn kèchéng biāozhǔn. The school decided to update its outdated math textbooks to meet the new curriculum standards.

**数字化教材让学生可以随时随地学习。** Shùzìhuà jiàocái ràng xuéshēng kěyǐ suíshí suídì xuéxí. Digital teaching materials allow students to learn anytime, anywhere.

**编写一套高质量的教材需要多年的积累。** Biānxiě yī tào gāo zhìliàng de jiàocái xūyào duō nián de jīlěi. Writing a high-quality set of teaching materials requires many years of accumulated experience.

**老师建议学生除课本外多参考其他教材。** Lǎoshī jiànyì xuéshēng chú kèběn wài duō cānkǎo qítā jiàocái. The teacher suggested that students consult other teaching materials in addition to the textbook.

**开放教育资源运动致力于向所有人提供免费教材。** Kāifàng jiàoyù zīyuán yùndòng zhìlì yú xiàng suǒyǒu rén tígōng miǎnfèi jiàocái. The Open Educational Resources movement is dedicated to providing free teaching materials to everyone.

## Usage Notes
教材 is broader than 课本 (kèběn, textbook). While 课本 refers specifically to the bound book used in class, 教材 encompasses all instructional resources: workbooks, audio files, videos, and online platforms. In official educational discourse, 教材建设 (development of teaching materials) is a policy goal.

At B2 level, 教材 is essential vocabulary for discussing education systems, curriculum design, and language learning. It frequently appears in news about school reforms and publishing.

## Memory Hook
教 means "to teach" and 材 means "material/timber" -- the raw material that teaching is built from. 教材 is literally the timber of education.
