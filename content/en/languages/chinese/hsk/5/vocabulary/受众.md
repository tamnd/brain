## 受众 (shòuzhòng)

受众 refers to the intended audience or target group for media, advertising, publications, or any form of communication -- the people who receive and consume a message.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** audience; target audience; readership; viewership

## Example Sentences

**这部电影的受众主要是年轻人。** Zhè bù diànyǐng de shòuzhòng zhǔyào shì niánqīngrén. The primary audience for this film is young people.

**广告公司需要了解产品的目标受众。** Guǎnggào gōngsī xūyào liǎojiě chǎnpǐn de mùbiāo shòuzhòng. Advertising companies need to understand the target audience of a product.

**这本杂志的受众是受过高等教育的读者。** Zhè běn zázhì de shòuzhòng shì shòuguò gāoděng jiàoyù de dúzhě. The readership of this magazine is readers with higher education.

**社交媒体帮助品牌精准触达受众。** Shèjiāo méitǐ bāngzhù pǐnpái jīngzhǔn chùdá shòuzhòng. Social media helps brands reach their audience with precision.

**内容创作者应该根据受众的需求调整风格。** Nèiróng chuàngzuò zhě yīnggāi gēnjù shòuzhòng de xūqiú tiáozhěng fēnggé. Content creators should adjust their style based on the needs of their audience.

**扩大受众范围是这次宣传活动的主要目标。** Kuòdà shòuzhòng fànwéi shì zhè cì xuānchuán huódòng de zhǔyào mùbiāo. Expanding the audience reach is the main goal of this promotional campaign.

## Usage Notes

受众 is a media and communications term borrowed from academic and journalistic Chinese. 受 means "to receive" and 众 means "the masses/many people," so 受众 literally means "those who receive." It is more formal and specific than 观众 (spectators/viewers) or 听众 (listeners), as it emphasizes the communicative relationship between sender and receiver.

In marketing contexts, 目标受众 (target audience) is the standard term, equivalent to the English "target demographic." 受众 is primarily a written/formal word; in casual speech, people might say 观众 or 用户 instead.

## Memory Hook

受 means "receive" and 众 means "the masses" -- 受众 are the masses who receive your message, i.e., your audience.
