## 臭氧层 (chòuyǎng céng)

臭氧层 refers to the ozone layer -- the region of Earth's stratosphere containing a high concentration of ozone (O3) that absorbs most of the Sun's ultraviolet radiation. It is a key term in environmental science and ecology.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** the ozone layer; the stratospheric ozone layer

## Example Sentences

**臭氧层的破坏会导致到达地表的紫外线增加，从而引发皮肤癌等疾病。** Chòuyǎng céng de pòhuài huì dǎozhì dào dá dìbiǎo de zǐwàixiàn zēngjiā, cóng ér yǐnfā pífūái děng jíbìng. Destruction of the ozone layer causes an increase in ultraviolet radiation reaching the Earth's surface, which can trigger diseases such as skin cancer.

**1987年签署的《蒙特利尔议定书》是保护臭氧层的重要国际协议。** 1987 nián qiānshǔ de "Méngtèlìěr Yìdìng Shū" shì bǎohù chòuyǎng céng de zhòngyào guójì xiéyì. The Montreal Protocol signed in 1987 is an important international agreement for protecting the ozone layer.

**氯氟烃等化学物质会分解臭氧分子，加速臭氧层的损耗。** Lǜ fú tīng děng huàxué wùzhì huì fēnjiě chòuyǎng fēnzǐ, jiāsù chòuyǎng céng de sǔnhào. Chemicals such as chlorofluorocarbons break down ozone molecules, accelerating the depletion of the ozone layer.

**科学家监测显示，在国际社会的共同努力下，南极上空的臭氧层正在缓慢恢复。** Kēxuéjiā jiāncè xiǎnshì, zài guójì shèhuì de gòngtóng nǔlì xià, Nánjí shàngkōng de chòuyǎng céng zhèngzài huǎnmàn huīfù. Scientific monitoring shows that, thanks to the joint efforts of the international community, the ozone layer above Antarctica is slowly recovering.

**保护臭氧层需要各国协调配合，限制有害化学品的生产和使用。** Bǎohù chòuyǎng céng xūyào gè guó xiétiáo pèihé, xiànzhì yǒuhài huàxuépǐn de shēngchǎn hé shǐyòng. Protecting the ozone layer requires coordinated cooperation among countries to restrict the production and use of harmful chemicals.

**了解臭氧层的形成原理和受损机制，有助于我们更好地理解环境保护的重要性。** Liǎojiě chòuyǎng céng de xíngchéng yuánlǐ hé shòu sǔn jīzhì, yǒuzhù yú wǒmen gèng hǎo de lǐjiě huánjìng bǎohù de zhòngyyào xìng. Understanding the formation principles and damage mechanisms of the ozone layer helps us better understand the importance of environmental protection.

## Usage Notes

臭氧层 = 臭氧 (ozone) + 层 (layer). 臭氧 literally means "smelly oxygen" (臭 = smelly; 氧 = oxygen), reflecting the distinctive pungent smell of ozone. 层 (layer) is commonly used in Chinese for stratified structures: 大气层 (atmosphere), 臭氧层 (ozone layer), 冰层 (ice layer).

The topic of 臭氧层 often appears in HSK reading passages about environmental issues alongside 温室效应 (greenhouse effect), 全球变暖 (global warming), and 气候变化 (climate change). Knowing the key vocabulary of environmental harm (破坏, 损耗) and protection (保护, 恢复) is essential.

## Memory Hook

臭 (smelly) + 氧 (oxygen) + 层 (layer) -- a layer of "smelly oxygen" high above us that shields us from the sun's harmful rays.
