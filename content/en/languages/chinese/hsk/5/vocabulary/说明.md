## 说明 (shuōmíng)

说明 is one of the most common verbs for explaining or clarifying in Chinese, and as a noun it refers to explanatory text, instructions, or descriptions.

## Meanings
1. **[verb]** to explain; to illustrate; to indicate; to show
2. **[noun]** explanation; instructions; description; manual

## Example Sentences
**请说明你为什么选择这个专业。** Qǐng shuōmíng nǐ wèishénme xuǎnzé zhège zhuānyè. Please explain why you chose this major.

**这份产品说明书写得非常清楚。** Zhè fèn chǎnpǐn shuōmíngshū xiě de fēicháng qīngchǔ. This product instruction manual is written very clearly.

**这些数据说明市场需求正在增长。** Zhèxiē shùjù shuōmíng shìchǎng xūqiú zhèngzài zēngzhǎng. These figures show that market demand is growing.

**老师详细说明了考试的规则和要求。** Lǎoshī xiángxì shuōmíng le kǎoshì de guīzé hé yāoqiú. The teacher explained the exam rules and requirements in detail.

**能做到这一点，说明你已经掌握了核心技能。** Néng zuòdào zhè yī diǎn, shuōmíng nǐ yǐjīng zhǎngwò le héxīn jìnéng. Being able to do this shows that you have already mastered the core skills.

**请附上简短的说明，介绍你的研究背景。** Qǐng fùshàng jiǎnduǎn de shuōmíng, jièshào nǐ de yánjiū bèijǐng. Please attach a brief description introducing your research background.

## Collocations
| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 说明书 (shuōmíngshū) | instruction manual |
| 加以说明 (jiāyǐ shuōmíng) | to give an explanation |
| 说明问题 (shuōmíng wèntí) | to illustrate the problem |
| 需要说明 (xūyào shuōmíng) | it needs to be noted that |
| 详细说明 (xiángxì shuōmíng) | to explain in detail |

## Usage Notes
说明 is extremely versatile as both a verb and a noun. As a verb it can mean "to explain" (to a person), "to indicate/show" (with data or evidence as subject), or "to illustrate." As a noun it often appears in the compound 说明书 (instruction manual, product guide).

Distinguish 说明 from 解释 (jiěshì): 说明 focuses on describing or illustrating facts; 解释 often implies defending or justifying something, or clarifying a misunderstanding.

## Memory Hook
说 (to speak/say) + 明 (to make clear/bright): speaking until something is clear -- to explain.
