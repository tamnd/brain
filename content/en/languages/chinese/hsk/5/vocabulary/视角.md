## 视角 (shìjiǎo)

视角 literally means "viewing angle" and is used both literally (the angle of a camera lens) and figuratively (a perspective or standpoint). In literary and critical contexts it translates as "point of view" or "narrative perspective." It is more visual and spatial in connotation than 观点 (guāndiǎn), which focuses on opinion, or 立场 (lìchǎng), which emphasizes position or stance.

## Meanings
1. **[n]** perspective; point of view; angle of vision (literal or figurative)

## Example Sentences
**从儿童的视角来看，这个世界充满了神奇和未知。** Cóng értóng de shìjiǎo lái kàn, zhège shìjiè chōngmǎn le shénjì hé wèizhī. Seen from a child's perspective, this world is full of wonder and the unknown.

**这部纪录片采用了普通工人的视角，展现了工厂的日常生活。** Zhè bù jìlùpiān cǎiyòng le pǔtōng gōngrén de shìjiǎo, zhǎnxiàn le gōngchǎng de rìcháng shēnghuó. This documentary adopted the perspective of ordinary workers to present daily life in the factory.

**转换视角有时能帮助我们更全面地理解一个复杂的问题。** Zhuǎnhuàn shìjiǎo yǒushí néng bāngzhù wǒmen gèng quánmiàn de lǐjiě yī gè fùzá de wèntí. Shifting perspective can sometimes help us understand a complex problem more comprehensively.

**小说家选择了一个旁观者的视角来叙述这段历史。** Xiǎoshuōjiā xuǎnzé le yī gè pángguānzhě de shìjiǎo lái xùshù zhè duàn lìshǐ. The novelist chose the perspective of an onlooker to narrate this piece of history.

**不同文化背景的读者对同一文本可能持有完全不同的视角。** Bùtóng wénhuà bèijǐng de dúzhě duì tóngyī wénběn kěnéng chíyǒu wánquán bùtóng de shìjiǎo. Readers from different cultural backgrounds may hold entirely different perspectives on the same text.

**广角镜头能提供更宽广的视角，适合拍摄风景。** Guǎngjiǎo jìngtóu néng tígōng gèng kuānguǎng de shìjiǎo, shìhé pāishè fēngjǐng. A wide-angle lens can provide a broader field of view, making it suitable for landscape photography.

## Usage Notes
视角 is versatile, appearing in literary criticism (叙事视角), journalism (报道视角), philosophy, and even photography (广角视角). It differs from 观点 in that 观点 means "viewpoint" or "opinion" (what you think), while 视角 emphasizes the angle or standpoint from which you look (how you see). The phrase 换个视角 (huàn gè shìjiǎo) — "change your perspective" — is common in motivational and analytical contexts.

In academic writing, 视角 often appears in the pattern 从X视角来看/分析/研究, meaning "from the perspective of X." This is a very productive structure for thesis statements and analytical paragraphs.

## Memory Hook
视 means to see or look; 角 means angle or corner — 视角 is the "angle from which you see," i.e., perspective.
