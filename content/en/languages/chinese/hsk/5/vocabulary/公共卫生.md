## 公共卫生 (gōnggòng wèishēng)

公共卫生 refers to organized efforts by society and governments to protect, promote, and improve the health of entire populations, as opposed to individual medical care.

## Meanings

1. **[noun phrase]** public health (the science and practice of protecting community health)

## Example Sentences

**新冠疫情暴露了全球公共卫生体系的脆弱性。** Xīnguān yìqíng bàolù le quánqiú gōnggòng wèishēng tǐxì de cuìruò xìng. The COVID-19 pandemic exposed the fragility of global public health systems.

**政府加大了对公共卫生基础设施的投资。** Zhèngfǔ jiādà le duì gōnggòng wèishēng jīchǔ shèshī de tóuzī. The government increased investment in public health infrastructure.

**公共卫生问题不仅影响个人健康，也影响社会稳定。** Gōnggòng wèishēng wèntí bùjǐn yǐngxiǎng gèrén jiànkāng, yě yǐngxiǎng shèhuì wěndìng. Public health issues affect not only individual health but also social stability.

**加强公共卫生教育是预防疾病传播的关键措施。** Jiāqiáng gōnggòng wèishēng jiàoyù shì yùfáng jíbìng chuánbō de guānjiàn cuòshī. Strengthening public health education is a key measure for preventing the spread of diseases.

**这所大学设有专门的公共卫生学院，培养相关专业人才。** Zhè suǒ dàxué shè yǒu zhuānmén de gōnggòng wèishēng xuéyuàn, péiyǎng xiāngguān zhuānyè réncái. This university has a dedicated school of public health that trains professionals in the field.

**公共卫生紧急状态需要政府、医疗机构和公众共同应对。** Gōnggòng wèishēng jǐnjí zhuàngtài xūyào zhèngfǔ, yīliáo jīgòu hé gōngzhòng gòngtóng yìngduì. A public health emergency requires governments, medical institutions, and the public to respond together.

## Usage Notes

公共卫生 is a formal compound used extensively in official, academic, and medical contexts. 公共 (public/communal) + 卫生 (hygiene/health) together specifically denote organized, population-level health management. In everyday speech, people may say 卫生 or 健康 when talking about personal health, but 公共卫生 is reserved for systemic or community-level health discussions.

The phrase 公共卫生事件 (public health event/incident) is the official Chinese term for what international organizations call a "public health emergency." This distinction in register is important for B2-level academic and news comprehension.

## Memory Hook

公共 is "public/shared by all" and 卫生 is "guarding life/hygiene" -- 公共卫生 is the collective guarding of everyone's health, a shield for the whole community.
