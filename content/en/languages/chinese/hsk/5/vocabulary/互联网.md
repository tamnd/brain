## 互联网 (hùliánwǎng)

互联网 is the standard Chinese term for the Internet, referring to the global network of interconnected computers that enables the exchange of information and communication worldwide.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** the Internet; the global network connecting computers and devices worldwide

## Example Sentences

**互联网的出现彻底改变了人们获取信息和交流沟通的方式。** Hùliánwǎng de chūxiàn chèdǐ gǎibiànle rénmen huòqǔ xìnxī hé jiāoliú gōutōng de fāngshì. The emergence of the Internet has fundamentally changed the way people access information and communicate.

**中国的互联网用户数量已超过十亿，位居全球首位。** Zhōngguó de hùliánwǎng yònghù shùliàng yǐ chāoguò shí yì, wèijū quánqiú shǒuwèi. China's number of Internet users has exceeded one billion, ranking first in the world.

**互联网经济的快速发展催生了电子商务、网络支付等新型商业模式。** Hùliánwǎng jīngjì de kuàisù fāzhǎn cuīshēngle diànzǐ shāngyè, wǎngluò zhīfù děng xīnxíng shāngyè móshì. The rapid development of the Internet economy has given rise to new business models such as e-commerce and online payment.

**在偏远山区，许多学生无法接入互联网，这加剧了教育不平等问题。** Zài piānyuǎn shānqū, xǔduō xuéshēng wúfǎ jiērù hùliánwǎng, zhè jiājùle jiàoyù bù píngděng wèntí. In remote mountainous areas, many students cannot access the Internet, which exacerbates educational inequality.

**互联网安全问题日益突出，个人信息泄露和网络诈骗现象屡见不鲜。** Hùliánwǎng ānquán wèntí rìyì tūchū, gèrén xìnxī xìelòu hé wǎngluò zhàpiàn xiànxiàng lǚ jiàn bù xiān. Internet security issues are becoming increasingly prominent, with personal information leaks and online fraud occurring frequently.

**互联网+战略旨在将互联网技术与传统产业深度融合，推动经济转型升级。** Hùliánwǎng jiā zhànlüè zhǐ zài jiāng hùliánwǎng jìshù yǔ chuántǒng chǎnyè shēndù rónghé, tuīdòng jīngjì zhuǎnxíng shēngjí. The "Internet Plus" strategy aims to deeply integrate Internet technology with traditional industries to promote economic transformation and upgrading.

## Usage Notes

互联网 is the formal standard term for the Internet. In informal contexts, 网络 (network) or 上网 (going online) are more commonly used in speech. 互联网+ (Internet Plus) is a Chinese government strategy launched in 2015 to integrate the Internet with traditional industries.

Note the difference between 互联网 (the Internet, referring to the global network) and 网络 (network, which can refer to any network including local networks or the Internet generally). 互联网 specifically implies the global, public internet.

## Memory Hook

互 (mutual/inter-) + 联 (connect/link) + 网 (net/network) — the net where everything is mutually connected, i.e., the Inter-net.
