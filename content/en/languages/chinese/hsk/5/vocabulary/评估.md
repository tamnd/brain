## 评估 (pínggū)

评估 refers to a systematic process of assessing, appraising, or evaluating something -- such as risk, performance, value, or impact -- and is a staple term in business, policy, and academic contexts.

## Meanings
1. **[verb]** to evaluate; to assess; to appraise
2. **[noun]** evaluation; assessment; appraisal

## Example Sentences
**公司每年对员工进行绩效评估。** Gōngsī měi nián duì yuángōng jìnxíng jīxiào pínggū. The company conducts annual performance evaluations of its employees.

**在投资之前，必须对风险进行充分评估。** Zài tóuzī zhīqián, bìxū duì fēngxiǎn jìnxíng chōngfèn pínggū. Before investing, you must conduct a thorough risk assessment.

**专家对这个项目的可行性进行了全面评估。** Zhuānjiā duì zhège xiàngmù de kěxíngxìng jìnxíng le quánmiàn pínggū. Experts conducted a comprehensive feasibility assessment of this project.

**评估结果显示，该政策效果良好。** Pínggū jiéguǒ xiǎnshì, gāi zhèngcè xiàoguǒ liánghǎo. The evaluation results show that the policy is working well.

**教师需要定期评估学生的学习进度。** Jiàoshī xūyào dìngqī pínggū xuéshēng de xuéxí jìndù. Teachers need to regularly assess students' learning progress.

**这份报告对环境影响做了详细评估。** Zhè fèn bàogào duì huánjìng yǐngxiǎng zuò le xiángxì pínggū. This report conducted a detailed evaluation of the environmental impact.

## Usage Notes
评估 implies a methodical, often formal process of measurement and judgment, more systematic than simple 评价 (píngjià, to comment on/assess informally). 评估 is the go-to term in professional and institutional contexts: performance reviews, risk assessments, feasibility studies, and impact analyses.

The verb 进行 (jìnxíng, to carry out) is the standard collocate: 进行评估. The object of evaluation typically follows 对...: 对项目进行评估.

## Memory Hook
评 (to comment/judge) + 估 (to estimate/assess): judging and estimating together -- a systematic evaluation.
