## 消极 (xiāojí)

消极 describes a negative, passive, or pessimistic attitude or approach -- the opposite of 积极 (jījí, positive/active). It implies a lack of initiative, motivation, or optimism.

## Meanings
1. **[adj]** negative (attitude); passive; pessimistic; lacking initiative

## Example Sentences
**面对困难时，消极的态度只会让情况更糟。** Miànduì kùnnan shí, xiāojí de tàidù zhǐ huì ràng qíngkuàng gèng zāo. When facing difficulties, a negative attitude will only make things worse.

**他在工作中表现得非常消极，经常抱怨却从不提出建议。** Tā zài gōngzuò zhōng biǎoxiàn de fēicháng xiāojí, jīngcháng bàoyuàn què cóng bù tíchū jiànyì. He is very passive at work, always complaining but never offering suggestions.

**消极情绪如果长期积累，会对心理健康造成影响。** Xiāojí qíngjù rúguǒ chángqī jīlěi, huì duì xīnlǐ jiànkāng zàochéng yǐngxiǎng. If negative emotions accumulate over a long period, they can affect mental health.

**我们不应该对这件事采取消极的应对方式。** Wǒmen bù yīnggāi duì zhè jiàn shì cǎiqǔ xiāojí de yìngduì fāngshì. We should not take a passive approach in dealing with this matter.

**老师鼓励学生用积极的态度看待挑战，而不是消极地逃避。** Lǎoshī gǔlì xuéshēng yòng jījí de tàidù kàndài tiǎozhàn, ér bù shì xiāojí de táobì. The teacher encourages students to face challenges with a positive attitude rather than passively avoiding them.

**长时间失业容易让人产生消极心理。** Cháng shíjiān shīyè róngyì ràng rén chǎnshēng xiāojí xīnlǐ. Long-term unemployment can easily cause a person to develop a negative mindset.

## Usage Notes
消极 is the direct antonym of 积极 (jījí, positive/active/proactive). Both are commonly used in educational, psychological, and professional settings to describe mindsets and behaviors. While 消极 can describe both an attitude (pessimistic) and a behavior (passive/inactive), the context usually makes the meaning clear.

Note that 消极 does not mean "negative" in the mathematical sense (负 fù is used for that) nor in the test result sense (阴性 yīnxìng). Its usage is restricted to attitudes, behaviors, emotions, and their effects.

## Memory Hook
消 = disappear/diminish, 极 = extreme/pole (also 积极 -- the opposite) -- at the extreme of diminishing energy -- negative, passive, having no drive.
