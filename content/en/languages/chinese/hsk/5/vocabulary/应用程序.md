## 应用程序 (yìngyòng chéngxù)

An application or app is a software program designed to perform a specific function directly for the user, whether on a computer, smartphone, or other device.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** application; app; software application; program

## Example Sentences

**这款应用程序可以帮助用户管理日常任务和日程安排。** Zhè kuǎn yìngyòng chéngxù kěyǐ bāngzhù yònghù guǎnlǐ rìcháng rènwù hé rìchéng ānpái. This application can help users manage daily tasks and schedules.

**下载这个应用程序需要注册账户并同意服务条款。** Xiàzài zhège yìngyòng chéngxù xūyào zhùcè zhànghù bìng tóngyì fúwù tiáokuǎn. Downloading this application requires registering an account and agreeing to the terms of service.

**开发一款成功的应用程序需要了解用户的真实需求。** Kāifā yī kuǎn chénggōng de yìngyòng chéngxù xūyào liǎojiě yònghù de zhēnshí xūqiú. Developing a successful application requires understanding the real needs of users.

**他在工作中每天都会用到多种应用程序。** Tā zài gōngzuò zhōng měi tiān dōu huì yòngdào duō zhǒng yìngyòng chéngxù. He uses multiple applications in his work every day.

**该应用程序已更新至最新版本，修复了多个漏洞。** Gāi yìngyòng chéngxù yǐ gēngxīn zhì zuìxīn bǎnběn, xiūfùle duōgè lòudòng. The application has been updated to the latest version, fixing multiple vulnerabilities.

**手机应用程序已经成为人们生活中不可缺少的工具。** Shǒujī yìngyòng chéngxù yǐjīng chéngwéi rénmen shēnghuó zhōng bùkě quēshǎo de gōngjù. Mobile apps have become indispensable tools in people's lives.

## Usage Notes

应用程序 is the full formal term for "application/app." In everyday speech, it is very commonly shortened to 应用 (yìngyòng) or simply APP (pronounced in Chinese as "ai-pi-pi"). On formal product pages, in technical documentation, and in official contexts, the full 应用程序 is preferred.

The abbreviated form 应用 is increasingly used as a noun on its own (下载这个应用 -- download this app). Learners at B2 level should be comfortable with both the full form and the shortened forms.

## Memory Hook

应用 (application/use) + 程序 (program/procedure) -- a program you apply for a specific use. The word itself describes exactly what an app is: a "use-program."
