## 范例 (fànlì)

范例 refers to a model or exemplary case that can serve as a standard or reference for others. It implies not just any example but a particularly good or instructive one worthy of being followed or studied.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** model example; exemplary case; paradigm; template

## Example Sentences

**这家企业的可持续发展实践成为业界公认的范例，吸引了众多企业前来参观学习。** Zhè jiā qǐyè de kě chíxù fāzhǎn shíjiàn chéngwéi yèjiè gōngrèn de fànlì, xīyǐnle zhòngduō qǐyè qiánlái cānguān xuéxí. This company's sustainable development practices have become a widely recognised model example in the industry, attracting many enterprises to visit and learn from them.

**这座城市的旧城改造项目被誉为历史文化保护与现代城市发展完美结合的范例。** Zhè zuò chéngshì de jiù chéng gǎizào xiàngmù bèi yù wéi lìshǐ wénhuà bǎohù yǔ xiàndài chéngshì fāzhǎn wánměi jiéhé de fànlì. The old town renovation project in this city has been praised as a model example of the perfect integration of historical and cultural preservation with modern urban development.

**他的创业故事堪称从零到百亿的商业范例，激励了无数年轻人追梦。** Tā de chuàngyè gùshì kān chēng cóng líng dào bǎi yì de shāngyè fànlì, jīlìle wúshù niánqīng rén zhuī mèng. His entrepreneurial story is a model example of going from zero to ten billion, inspiring countless young people to pursue their dreams.

**教师应当善用典型范例来阐明抽象概念，帮助学生加深理解。** Jiàoshī yīngdāng shàn yòng diǎnxíng fànlì lái chǎnmíng chōuxiàng gàiniàn, bāngzhù xuéshēng jiāshēn lǐjiě. Teachers should make good use of typical model examples to clarify abstract concepts and help students deepen their understanding.

**这个国际合作项目被认为是不同文化背景下成功协作的范例。** Zhège guójì hézuò xiàngmù bèi rènwéi shì bùtóng wénhuà bèijǐng xià chénggōng xiézuò de fànlì. This international cooperation project is considered a model example of successful collaboration across different cultural backgrounds.

**公司将此次危机处理的成功经验整理成范例文档，供日后参考。** Gōngsī jiāng cǐ cì wēijī chǔlǐ de chénggōng jīngyàn zhěnglǐ chéng fànlì wéndàng, gòng rìhòu cānkǎo. The company compiled the successful experience from this crisis management into a model document for future reference.

## Usage Notes

范例 is more formal and elevated than 例子 (example -- neutral, everyday). 范例 implies the example is exemplary and instructive -- worth imitating or studying. Compare with 典范 (dǐanfàn -- model/paragon, often applied to persons of exemplary character) and 榜样 (bǎngyàng -- role model/example, often for behaviour and moral conduct).

In academic writing, 范例 is often used when discussing case studies (案例 is also common) or when an example is held up as a standard to be emulated.

## Memory Hook

范 (model/pattern/boundary) + 例 (example/case) -- an example that sets the pattern: a model case.
