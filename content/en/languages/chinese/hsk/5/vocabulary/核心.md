## 核心 (héxīn)

核心 refers to the central, most essential part or element of something, the nucleus around which everything else is organized or depends.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** core, nucleus, central element
2. **[adjective]** core, central, key (as in 核心概念, core concept)

## Example Sentences

**创新是推动经济发展的核心动力。** Chuàngxīn shì tuīdòng jīngjì fāzhǎn de héxīn dònglì. Innovation is the core driving force behind economic development.

**以人为本是这项政策的核心理念。** Yǐ rén wéi běn shì zhè xiàng zhèngcè de héxīn lǐniàn. People-centered is the core concept of this policy.

**这家公司的核心竞争力在于其独特的技术专利。** Zhè jiā gōngsī de héxīn jìngjēng lì zàiyú qí dútè de jìshù zhuānlì. This company's core competitiveness lies in its unique technology patents.

**解决这个问题的核心在于改变思维方式。** Jiějué zhège wèntí de héxīn zàiyú gǎibiàn sīwéi fāngshì. The core of solving this problem lies in changing the way of thinking.

**团队的核心成员是整个项目成功的关键。** Tuánduì de héxīn chéngyuán shì zhěnggè xiàngmù chénggōng de guānjiàn. The core members of the team are the key to the success of the entire project.

**这门学科的核心是理解人类行为的内在动机。** Zhè mén xuékē de héxīn shì lǐjiě rénlèi xíngwéi de nèizài dòngji. The core of this discipline is understanding the intrinsic motivations behind human behavior.

## Usage Notes

核心 is both a noun and an adjective modifier, making it extremely versatile. As a noun, 核心在于 (the core/key lies in) is a common construction for identifying the most important aspect of something. As an adjective, it precedes important nouns: 核心技术 (core technology), 核心价值观 (core values). The phrase 以X为核心 (centered on X, with X as the core) is a standard construction in formal Chinese.

At B2 level, 核心 is indispensable for analytical writing and professional communication where identifying the central element is essential.

## Memory Hook

核 is a kernel or pit (as in a fruit's core), and 心 is heart -- 核心 is the innermost heart-kernel, the seed at the very center from which everything else grows.
