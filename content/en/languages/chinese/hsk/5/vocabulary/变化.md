## 变化 (biànhuà)

变化 refers to change or variation as a process or observable phenomenon, often emphasizing the state of flux itself rather than the agent causing it.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** change, variation, transformation (as a phenomenon)
2. **[verb]** to change, to vary, to fluctuate

## Example Sentences

**近年来，中国城市面貌发生了巨大的变化。** Jìn nián lái, Zhōngguó chéngshì miànmào fāshēng le jùdà de biànhuà. In recent years, the appearance of Chinese cities has undergone enormous changes.

**气候变化是当今世界面临的最大挑战之一。** Qìhòu biànhuà shì dāngjīn shìjiè miànlín de zuì dà tiǎozhàn zhī yī. Climate change is one of the greatest challenges facing the world today.

**市场情况瞬息万变，我们必须密切关注变化。** Shìchǎng qíngkuàng shùnxī wàn biàn, wǒmen bìxū mìqiē guānzhù biànhuà. Market conditions change rapidly, and we must closely monitor the changes.

**随着年龄的增长，人的思想也会发生变化。** Suízhe niánlíng de zēngzhǎng, rén de sīxiǎng yě huì fāshēng biànhuà. As people grow older, their thinking also undergoes changes.

**这首曲子的旋律变化非常丰富，令人回味。** Zhè shǒu qǔzi de xuánlǜ biànhuà fēicháng fēngfù, lìng rén huíwèi. The melodic variations in this piece are very rich, leaving a lingering impression.

**他的面色变化让我意识到这个话题让他不安。** Tā de miànsè biànhuà ràng wǒ yìshí dào zhège huàtí ràng tā bù'ān. The changes in his expression made me realize that this topic was making him uneasy.

## Usage Notes

变化 describes change as a natural, observable process and does not require a human agent. This distinguishes it from 改变, which implies someone actively making a change. 变化 often appears in the structure 发生变化 (to undergo change) or as a modifier in compound nouns like 气候变化 (climate change) and 变化规律 (patterns of change).

At B2 level, understanding the distinction between 变化 (neutral, descriptive of change as phenomenon) and 改变 (agent-driven change) is essential for precise academic and professional writing.

## Memory Hook

变 is change, 化 is transformation -- 变化 is change transforming naturally around you, like seasons shifting without anyone's command.
