## 保守的 (bǎoshǒu de)

保守的 describes attitudes, policies, or people that prefer established traditions, resist rapid change, or advocate for cautious approaches. In politics it refers to right-leaning or status-quo-oriented positions; more broadly it means "cautious" or "restrained."

## Meanings
1. **[adj]** conservative; resistant to change; cautious; traditional; restrained

## Example Sentences
**保守的政治立场通常强调传统价值观和社会稳定。** Bǎoshǒu de zhèngzhì lìchǎng tōngcháng qiángdiào chuántǒng jiàzhíguān hé shèhuì wěndìng. Conservative political positions typically emphasize traditional values and social stability.

**他对新技术持保守的态度，不愿轻易改变工作方式。** Tā duì xīn jìshù chí bǎoshǒu de tàidù, bù yuàn qīngyì gǎibiàn gōngzuò fāngshì. He holds a conservative attitude toward new technology and is reluctant to change his working methods.

**这家公司的投资策略一贯比较保守，偏向低风险资产。** Zhè jiā gōngsī de tóuzī cèlüè yīguàn bǐjiào bǎoshǒu, piānxiàng dī fēngxiǎn zīchǎn. The company's investment strategy has consistently been conservative, favoring low-risk assets.

**她的穿着风格比较保守，喜欢简洁大方的服装。** Tā de chuānzhuó fēnggé bǐjiào bǎoshǒu, xǐhuān jiǎnjié dàfāng de fúzhuāng. Her clothing style is fairly conservative; she prefers simple and elegant outfits.

**保守的经济政策有时能在不确定时期维持稳定。** Bǎoshǒu de jīngjì zhèngcè yǒushí néng zài bùquèdìng shíqī wéichí wěndìng. Conservative economic policies can sometimes maintain stability during uncertain periods.

**面对改革压力，部分官员仍坚持保守的立场。** Miànduì gǎigé yālì, bùfèn guānyuán réng jiānchí bǎoshǒu de lìchǎng. Faced with reform pressure, some officials still maintain a conservative stance.

## Usage Notes
保守的 is versatile across contexts. In finance, 保守的投资 means a cautious or low-risk investment. In politics, it corresponds to "conservative" in the Western political sense. In everyday speech, it often simply means "restrained" or "not bold."

The phrase 保守估计 (conservative estimate) is a common fixed expression meaning "at the very least" or "at minimum."

## Memory Hook
保 means "to keep/protect" and 守 means "to guard/defend" -- 保守 means guarding what already exists, so 保守的 means conservative.
