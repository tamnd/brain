## 关键 (guānjiàn)

关键 refers to the key, decisive, or most critical element in a situation or problem, the factor whose presence or absence determines the outcome.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** key, crucial element, decisive factor
2. **[adjective]** key, critical, decisive (as in 关键时刻, critical moment)

## Example Sentences

**解决这一问题的关键在于找到各方利益的平衡点。** Jiějué zhè yī wèntí de guānjiàn zàiyú zhǎodào gè fāng lìyì de pínghéng diǎn. The key to solving this problem lies in finding the balance point of all parties' interests.

**关键时刻，他保持了冷静，做出了正确的决定。** Guānjiàn shíkè, tā bǎochí le lěngjìng, zuòchū le zhèngquè de juédìng. At the critical moment, he remained calm and made the right decision.

**人才是企业最关键的资产，没有之一。** Réncái shì qǐyè zuì guānjiàn de zīchǎn, méiyǒu zhī yī. Talent is the most crucial asset of an enterprise, without exception.

**掌握关键数据是制定有效战略的前提。** Zhǎngwò guānjiàn shùjù shì zhìdìng yǒuxiào zhànlüè de qiántí. Mastering key data is a prerequisite for formulating effective strategies.

**在这场谈判中，细节往往是决定成败的关键。** Zài zhè chǎng tánpàn zhōng, xìjié wǎngwǎng shì juédìng chéngbài de guānjiàn. In this negotiation, details are often the key that determines success or failure.

**环境教育是培养下一代可持续发展意识的关键途径。** Huánjìng jiàoyù shì péiyǎng xià yī dài kě chíxù fāzhǎn yìshí de guānjiàn tújìng. Environmental education is the key pathway for cultivating the next generation's awareness of sustainable development.

## Usage Notes

关键 functions as both a noun (the key/crux) and an adjective (key, critical). The construction 关键在于 (the key lies in) is parallel to 核心在于, and both are used to identify the most important element of a situation. At a more colloquial level, 关键是 (the key thing is / what matters is) introduces the most important point in a statement.

Note that 至关重要 (of vital/crucial importance) is a fixed four-character expression derived from 关键 that is very common in formal writing.

## Memory Hook

关 means a gate or pass (a strategic point), and 键 means a key -- 关键 is the key to the gate, the single item that opens or closes everything else.
