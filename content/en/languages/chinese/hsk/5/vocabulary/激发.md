## 激发 (jīfā)

激发 means to stimulate or trigger something into action -- typically used for feelings, potential, interest, creativity, or reactions that are aroused by an external stimulus.

## Meanings
1. **[verb]** to stimulate; to arouse; to trigger; to bring out; to activate

## Example Sentences
**这本书激发了他对科学的浓厚兴趣。** Zhè běn shū jīfāle tā duì kēxué de nóng hòu xìngqù. This book stimulated his strong interest in science.

**老师的提问激发了学生们的思考和讨论热情。** Lǎoshī de tíwèn jīfāle xuéshēngmen de sīkǎo hé tǎolùn rèqíng. The teacher's questions aroused the students' enthusiasm for thinking and discussion.

**艺术往往能激发人们对美的感知和情感共鸣。** Yìshù wǎngwǎng néng jīfā rénmen duì měi de gǎnzhī hé qínggǎn gòngmíng. Art often stimulates people's perception of beauty and emotional resonance.

**这次竞赛激发了参赛者的创造力和团队合作精神。** Zhè cì jìngsài jīfāle cānsài zhě de chuàngzàolì hé tuánduì hézuò jīngshén. This competition stimulated participants' creativity and team spirit.

**面对挑战，逆境往往能激发人内心深处的潜力。** Miànduì tiǎozhàn, nìjìng wǎngwǎng néng jīfā rén nèixīn shēnchù de qiánlì. When facing challenges, adversity often stimulates a person's deepest potential.

**新的工作机会激发了她重新学习技能的动力。** Xīn de gōngzuò jīhuì jīfāle tā chóngxīn xuéxí jìnéng de dònglì. The new job opportunity triggered her motivation to relearn skills.

## Usage Notes
激发 and 激励 are related but distinct. 激励 focuses on motivating a person toward a goal (motivating someone to try harder), while 激发 focuses on triggering or arousing something latent -- a feeling, interest, or potential. The object of 激发 is typically an abstract quality or feeling (兴趣, 创造力, 热情), not directly a person.

激发 is widely used in education, psychology, and creative contexts to describe how stimuli bring out hidden capacities or feelings. The word 激 (stir up/excite) is shared with 激励, 激动, and 激烈, all carrying a sense of intense activation.

## Memory Hook
激 = to stir up/excite, 发 = to send out/release -- stirring something up and releasing it -- stimulating and triggering something to come out.
