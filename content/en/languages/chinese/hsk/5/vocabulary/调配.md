## 调配 (diàopèi)

调配 means to distribute, allocate, or coordinate resources, personnel, or materials according to need, and is a key term in logistics, management, and supply chain contexts.

## Meanings
1. **[verb]** to allocate; to deploy; to coordinate and distribute; to blend (in cooking/pharmacy)

## Example Sentences
**公司根据各部门的需求合理调配人力资源。** Gōngsī gēnjù gè bùmén de xūqiú hélǐ diàopèi rénlì zīyuán. The company rationally allocates human resources according to the needs of each department.

**在紧急情况下，政府迅速调配物资支援灾区。** Zài jǐnjí qíngkuàng xià, zhèngfǔ xùnsù diàopèi wùzī zhīyuán zāiqū. In emergencies, the government quickly allocates supplies to support disaster areas.

**这项工程需要协调多个部门，统一调配资源。** Zhè xiàng gōngchéng xūyào xiétiáo duō gè bùmén, tǒngyī diàopèi zīyuán. This project requires coordinating multiple departments and uniformly allocating resources.

**厨师精心调配了各种调料，做出了一道美食。** Chúshī jīngxīn diàopèi le gèzhǒng tiáoliào, zuòchū le yī dào měishí. The chef carefully blended various seasonings to create a delicious dish.

**后勤部门负责统筹调配运输车辆。** Hòuqín bùmén fùzé tǒngchóu diàopèi yùnshū chēliàng. The logistics department is responsible for coordinating and dispatching transport vehicles.

**合理调配时间是高效工作的重要前提。** Hélǐ diàopèi shíjiān shì gāoxiào gōngzuò de zhòngyào qiántí. Rationally allocating time is an important prerequisite for efficient work.

## Usage Notes
调配 (diàopèi) is distinct from 调整 (tiáozhěng, to adjust). Note that 调 here is pronounced diào (4th tone), not tiáo (2nd tone). The diào pronunciation is common in words involving transferring or mobilizing: 调查 (diàochá, to investigate), 调动 (diàodòng, to transfer/mobilize), 调配 (diàopèi, to allocate).

调配 can also mean to blend or mix ingredients, especially in cooking, pharmacy, or chemistry. In organizational contexts it specifically means to allocate and coordinate resources across a system.

## Memory Hook
调 (to transfer/mobilize) + 配 (to match/assign): transferring and matching things where they are needed -- to allocate.
