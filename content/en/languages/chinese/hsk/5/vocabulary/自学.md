## 自学 (zìxué)

自学 means to study or learn independently without a teacher or formal instruction. It emphasises self-discipline, initiative, and the ability to acquire knowledge through one's own effort.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to teach oneself; to study independently; to self-study
2. **[noun]** self-study; independent learning

## Example Sentences

**他没有接受过正规的音乐教育，完全靠自学成为了一名专业的吉他手。** Tā méiyǒu jiēshòuguò zhèngguī de yīnyuè jiàoyù, wánquán kào zìxué chéngwéile yī míng zhuānyè de jítā shǒu. He received no formal music education and became a professional guitarist entirely through self-study.

**互联网时代使自学变得更加便捷，各种免费的优质学习资源唾手可得。** Hùliánwǎng shídài shǐ zìxué biàn dé gèng jiā biànjié, gè zhǒng miǎnfèi de yōuzhì xuéxí zīyuán tuò shǒu kě dé. The internet era has made self-study much more convenient, with high-quality free learning resources readily available.

**自学需要极强的自律性，许多人在没有外部督促的情况下难以坚持。** Zìxué xūyào jí qiáng de zìlǜ xìng, xǔduō rén zài méiyǒu wàibù dūcù de qíngkuàng xià nán yǐ jiānchí. Self-study requires very strong self-discipline; many people find it hard to persist without external supervision.

**她用了两年时间自学日语，通过了日语能力考试最高级别。** Tā yòngle liǎng nián shíjiān zìxué Rìyǔ, tōngguòle Rìyǔ nénglì kǎoshì zuìgāo jíbié. She spent two years teaching herself Japanese and passed the highest level of the Japanese Language Proficiency Test.

**开放教育理念鼓励学生培养自学能力，为终身学习奠定基础。** Kāifàng jiàoyù lǐniàn gǔlì xuéshēng péiyǎng zìxué nénglì, wèi zhōngshēn xuéxí diàndìng jīchǔ. The concept of open education encourages students to develop self-study ability, laying the foundation for lifelong learning.

**这门课程采用混合式教学模式，课堂学习与课后自学相结合。** Zhè mén kèchéng cǎiyòng hùnhé shì jiāoxué móshì, kètáng xuéxí yǔ kè hòu zìxué xiāng jiéhé. This course adopts a blended learning model that combines classroom instruction with post-class self-study.

## Usage Notes

自学 is a compound of 自 (self) + 学 (study/learn). It emphasises the learner's agency and independence. In Chinese educational culture, 自学成才 (to become talented through self-study) is a phrase that carries strong positive connotations of perseverance and self-reliance.

自学考试 (Zìkǎo) is also a specific Chinese national higher education examination system allowing people to obtain degree-equivalent qualifications through self-study, which is different from the generic use of 自学.

## Memory Hook

自 (self) + 学 (study/learn) -- studying on your own, with yourself as the teacher and the student.
