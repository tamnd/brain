## 数字经济 (shùzì jīngjì)

An economic system built on digital technologies, the internet, and data -- including e-commerce, fintech, cloud computing, and AI-driven industries.

## Meanings
1. **[noun]** digital economy

## Example Sentences
**中国的数字经济规模已跃居全球前列。** Zhōngguó de shùzì jīngjì guīmó yǐ yuèjū quánqiú qiánliè. China's digital economy has leaped to a leading position globally.

**数字经济的快速发展创造了大量新型就业岗位。** Shùzì jīngjì de kuàisù fāzhǎn chuàngzàole dàliàng xīnxíng jiùyè gǎngwèi. The rapid development of the digital economy has created a large number of new types of jobs.

**政府将发展数字经济列为国家战略优先事项。** Zhèngfǔ jiāng fāzhǎn shùzì jīngjì lièwéi guójiā zhànlüè yōuxiān shìxiàng. The government has listed developing the digital economy as a national strategic priority.

**数字经济与实体经济的深度融合是未来的发展方向。** Shùzì jīngjì yǔ shítǐ jīngjì de shēndù rónghé shì wèilái de fāzhǎn fāngxiàng. The deep integration of the digital economy and the real economy is the direction of future development.

**发展中国家正努力缩小数字经济领域的差距。** Fāzhǎn zhōng guójiā zhèng nǔlì suōxiǎo shùzì jīngjì lǐngyù de chājù. Developing countries are working to narrow the gap in the digital economy.

**平台经济是数字经济中增长最快的组成部分之一。** Píngtái jīngjì shì shùzì jīngjì zhōng zēngzhǎng zuì kuài de zǔchéng bùfèn zhī yī. Platform economy is one of the fastest-growing components of the digital economy.

## Usage Notes
数字经济 is a key term in contemporary Chinese economic policy and frequently appears in government work reports, academic papers, and business news. It is often discussed alongside 实体经济 (real/physical economy), and the relationship between the two is a major policy topic.

B2 learners preparing to read Chinese economic or technology news should treat 数字经济 as a fixed compound and learn its standard collocations. It is also closely linked to other HSK 5 terms like 人工智能, 云计算, and 大数据.

## Memory Hook
数字 = digital (literally "numbers"), 经济 = economy. A 数字经济 is an economy that runs on numbers -- on data, code, and digital transactions rather than physical goods alone.
