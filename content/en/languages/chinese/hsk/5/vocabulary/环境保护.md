## 环境保护 (huánjìng bǎohù)

环境保护 refers to the actions, policies, and practices aimed at protecting the natural environment from degradation and harm caused by human activities.

## Meanings
1. **[noun phrase]** environmental protection; conservation of the natural environment

## Example Sentences
**环境保护是全人类共同面临的重大责任。** Huánjìng bǎohù shì quán rénlèi gòngtóng miànlín de zhòngdà zérèn. Environmental protection is a major responsibility shared by all of humanity.

**政府出台了一系列法规，以加强环境保护工作。** Zhèngfǔ chūtáile yī xìliè fǎguī, yǐ jiāqiáng huánjìng bǎohù gōngzuò. The government introduced a series of regulations to strengthen environmental protection work.

**环境保护与经济发展并不是相互对立的关系。** Huánjìng bǎohù yǔ jīngjì fāzhǎn bìng bù shì xiānghù duìlì de guānxi. Environmental protection and economic development are not in opposition to each other.

**从小培养孩子的环境保护意识非常重要。** Cóng xiǎo péiyǎng háizi de huánjìng bǎohù yìshí fēicháng zhòngyào. It is very important to cultivate children's environmental awareness from an early age.

**这家企业积极投入环境保护项目，赢得了社会的广泛赞誉。** Zhè jiā qǐyè jījí tóurù huánjìng bǎohù xiàngmù, yíngdéle shèhuì de guǎngfàn zànyù. This enterprise actively invested in environmental protection projects and earned wide social acclaim.

**个人在日常生活中也可以为环境保护做出贡献。** Gèrén zài rìcháng shēnghuó zhōng yě kěyǐ wèi huánjìng bǎohù zuòchū gòngxiàn. Individuals can also contribute to environmental protection in daily life.

## Usage Notes
环境保护 is often abbreviated as 环保 (huánbǎo) in everyday speech and informal writing. 环保 functions both as a noun and an adjective: 环保产品 (eco-friendly products), 搞环保 (engage in environmental protection).

This term is a cornerstone of Chinese environmental discourse and appears in government policy documents, school curricula, and public campaigns. The phrase 绿色发展 (green development) -- sustainable development that prioritizes both economic growth and environmental protection -- is closely related and frequently appears in Chinese policy language.

## Memory Hook
环境 = environment (surrounding conditions), 保护 = protect -- protecting the surrounding environment -- environmental protection.
