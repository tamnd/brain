## 相应 (xiāngyìng)

相应 describes something that corresponds to, matches, or responds proportionally to something else, commonly used in formal writing and policy language.

## Meanings
1. **[adjective]** corresponding; appropriate; matching
2. **[adverb]** accordingly; correspondingly

## Example Sentences
**政府出台了相应的政策来应对经济下行压力。** Zhèngfǔ chūtái le xiāngyìng de zhèngcè lái yìngduì jīngjì xiàxíng yālì. The government introduced corresponding policies to address the downward pressure on the economy.

**员工工资应与其工作量和技能水平相应。** Yuángōng gōngzī yīng yǔ qí gōngzuòliàng hé jìnéng shuǐpíng xiāngyìng. Employee salaries should correspond to their workload and skill level.

**随着业务规模的扩大，公司也进行了相应的人员调整。** Suízhe yèwù guīmó de kuòdà, gōngsī yě jìnxíng le xiāngyìng de rényuán tiáozhěng. As the business scaled up, the company made corresponding personnel adjustments.

**发生变化后，相关部门应及时做出相应反应。** Fāshēng biànhuà hòu, xiāngguān bùmén yīng jíshí zuò chū xiāngyìng fǎnyìng. After changes occur, relevant departments should respond accordingly in a timely manner.

**购买该产品需要支付相应的费用和手续费。** Gòumǎi gāi chǎnpǐn xūyào zhīfù xiāngyìng de fèiyòng hé shǒuxùfèi. Purchasing the product requires paying the corresponding fees and handling charges.

**课程难度的提升带来了相应的学习压力。** Kèchéng nándù de tíshēng dài lái le xiāngyìng de xuéxí yālì. The increase in course difficulty brought corresponding study pressure.

## Usage Notes
相应 expresses a proportional or matching relationship between two things. It is formal and appears frequently in policy documents, regulations, and academic writing. It often modifies nouns: 相应的政策 (corresponding policies), 相应的措施 (corresponding measures).

As an adverb, 相应地 can be used at the start of a clause to mean "accordingly" or "correspondingly." Compare with 相关 (xiāngguān, related/relevant), which indicates association without the proportional matching sense.

## Memory Hook
相 (mutual/corresponding) + 应 (to respond/suit) -- mutually responding -- corresponding; accordingly.
