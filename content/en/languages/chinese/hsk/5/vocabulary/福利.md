## 福利 (fúlì)

福利 refers to benefits, services, or programs designed to improve people's well-being, including both government social welfare and employee benefits provided by employers.

## Meanings
1. **[noun]** welfare; benefits; well-being provisions

## Example Sentences
**该公司提供了完善的员工福利，包括医疗保险和带薪假期。** Gāi gōngsī tígōng le wánshàn de yuángōng fúlì, bāokuò yīliáo bǎoxiǎn hé dài xīn jiàqī. The company offers comprehensive employee benefits including health insurance and paid leave.

**政府加大了对低收入家庭的社会福利支持力度。** Zhèngfǔ jiādà le duì dīshōurù jiātíng de shèhuì fúlì zhīchí lìdù. The government has increased social welfare support for low-income families.

**儿童福利机构为孤儿和困境儿童提供庇护和教育。** Értóng fúlì jīgòu wèi gūér hé kùnjìng értóng tígōng bìhù hé jiàoyù. Child welfare institutions provide shelter and education for orphans and children in difficult circumstances.

**提高全民福利水平是政府施政的核心目标之一。** Tígāo quánmín fúlì shuǐpíng shì zhèngfǔ shīzhèng de héxīn mùbiāo zhī yī. Improving the overall welfare level of all citizens is one of the core goals of government administration.

**优厚的福利待遇吸引了大量优秀人才加入这家公司。** Yōuhòu de fúlì dàiyù xīyǐn le dàliàng yōuxiù réncái jiārù zhè jiā gōngsī. The generous benefits package attracted a large number of talented people to join the company.

**老龄化趋势加大了养老福利体系的财政压力。** Lǎolínghuà qūshì jiādà le yǎnglǎo fúlì tǐxì de cáizhèng yālì. The aging trend has increased the fiscal pressure on the elderly welfare system.

## Usage Notes
福利 covers both public social welfare (government-provided services and support) and private employment benefits (company-provided perks). In everyday Chinese, 福利 often has a positive connotation, associated with good treatment and care.

In the context of employment, 福利待遇 (benefit package) is a common compound. The phrase 这是福利 (this is a perk/benefit) is used informally to describe something unexpectedly enjoyable or advantageous.

## Memory Hook
福 (good fortune/happiness) + 利 (benefit/profit) -- happiness and benefit provided -- welfare and benefits.
