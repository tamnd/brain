## 感悟 (gǎnwù)

感悟 refers to a deep personal insight or realization gained through experience, observation, or reflection, often with a philosophical or emotionally significant dimension. It can also be used as a verb meaning to come to understand or realize through lived experience.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** insight; realization; personal understanding gained through experience
2. **[verb]** to come to understand; to realize through reflection or experience

## Example Sentences

**经历了这次挫折，他对人生有了新的感悟，明白了坚持的重要性。** Jīnglìle zhè cì cuòzhé, tā duì rénshēng yǒule xīn de gǎnwù, míngbáile jiānchí de zhòngyào xìng. After experiencing this setback, he gained new insights into life and understood the importance of perseverance.

**读完这本书后，请写一篇文章，分享你的感悟和思考。** Dú wán zhè běn shū hòu, qǐng xiě yī piān wénzhāng, fēnxiǎng nǐ de gǎnwù hé sīkǎo. After reading this book, please write an article sharing your insights and reflections.

**旅途中的种种经历让她深刻感悟到，真正的财富是人与人之间真诚的情感。** Lǚtú zhōng de zhǒngzhǒng jīnglì ràng tā shēnkè gǎnwù dào, zhēnzhèng de cáifù shì rén yǔ rén zhī jiān zhēnchéng de qínggǎn. The many experiences along her journey led her to deeply realize that true wealth is the sincere feelings between people.

**每逢重要节日，他都会写下自己对生命和时光的感悟，留作纪念。** Měi féng zhòngyào jiérì, tā dōu huì xiě xià zìjǐ duì shēngmìng hé shíguāng de gǎnwù, liú zuò jìniàn. On every important holiday, he writes down his insights about life and time as a keepsake.

**这位老教授的每一堂课都充满人生感悟，令学生受益终身。** Zhè wèi lǎo jiàoshòu de měi yī táng kè dōu chōngmǎn rénshēng gǎnwù, lìng xuéshēng shòuyì zhōngshēn. Every class taught by this senior professor was filled with life insights, benefiting students throughout their lives.

**站在夕阳下的海边，他静静感悟着岁月的流逝和人生的短暂。** Zhàn zài xīyáng xià de hǎibiān, tā jìngjìng gǎnwù zhe suìyuè de liúshì hé rénshēng de duǎnzàn. Standing by the sea under the setting sun, he quietly reflected on the passing of time and the brevity of life.

## Usage Notes

感悟 is more profound and introspective than 感受 (feeling/to feel). While 感受 captures immediate feelings and impressions, 感悟 implies a deeper, often philosophical realization that comes after reflection or experience. It frequently appears in personal essays (散文), speeches, and diary-like writing.

感悟 is a distinctly Chinese concept that combines emotion (感) with enlightenment or awakening (悟), and it carries a meditative, philosophical quality. It is often associated with turning experiences into wisdom.

## Memory Hook

感 (to feel/be moved) + 悟 (to awaken/realize) — 感悟 is being moved by an experience until you awaken to a deeper truth.
