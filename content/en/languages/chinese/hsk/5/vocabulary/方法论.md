## 方法论 (fāngfǎlùn)

方法论 refers to methodology -- the systematic study, framework, or set of principles governing the methods used in a particular discipline or field of inquiry.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** methodology (the study of or framework for methods)
2. **[noun]** methodological approach (guiding principles behind how one works)

## Example Sentences

**社会科学的研究方法论与自然科学存在显著差异。** Shèhuì kēxué de yánjiū fāngfǎlùn yǔ zìrán kēxué cúnzài xiǎnzhù chāyì. The research methodology of social sciences differs significantly from that of natural sciences.

**马克思主义哲学为我们提供了认识和改造世界的科学方法论。** Mǎkèsī zhǔyì zhéxué wèi wǒmen tígōng le rènshi hé gǎizào shìjiè de kēxué fāngfǎlùn. Marxist philosophy provides us with a scientific methodology for understanding and transforming the world.

**论文答辩时，导师特别强调了研究方法论的重要性。** Lùnwén dábìan shí, dǎoshī tèbié qiángdiào le yánjiū fāngfǎlùn de zhòng yào xìng. During the dissertation defense, the supervisor especially emphasized the importance of research methodology.

**这门课的核心是训练学生的批判性思维和方法论意识。** Zhè mén kè de héxīn shì xùnliàn xuéshēng de pīpàn xìng sīwéi hé fāngfǎlùn yìshí. The core of this course is to train students' critical thinking and methodological awareness.

**历史研究的方法论包括文献考证、口述历史和比较分析等。** Lìshǐ yánjiū de fāngfǎlùn bāokuò wénxiàn kǎozhèng, kǒushù lìshǐ hé bǐjiào fēnxī děng. Historical research methodology includes textual verification, oral history, and comparative analysis, among others.

**他的研究在方法论上有所创新，因此获得了学界好评。** Tā de yánjiū zài fāngfǎlùn shàng yǒusuǒ chuàngxīn, yīncǐ huòdé le xuéjiè hǎopíng. His research achieved methodological innovation and thus received praise from the academic community.

## Usage Notes

方法论 is a technical academic term that goes beyond 方法 (method). While 方法 refers to a specific technique or approach, 方法论 refers to the study of, or the principled framework underlying, methods as a whole. It is primarily used in academic, philosophical, and professional contexts. In Chinese academic writing, 研究方法论 (research methodology) is a standard section of any scholarly work.

At B2 level, 方法论 is important for academic writing courses, graduate study preparation, and discussions of research design and philosophy.

## Memory Hook

方法 is method, and 论 is theory/discourse -- 方法论 is the theory of methods, like stepping back from the tools to ask: why use these tools and not others?
