## 确认 (quèrèn)

确认 means to confirm or verify something with certainty, often involving a deliberate check or acknowledgment, and is used across formal, professional, and everyday contexts.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to confirm; to verify; to acknowledge
2. **[verb]** to make certain of; to establish definitively

## Example Sentences

**请您确认一下预订信息。** Qǐng nín quèrèn yīxià yùdìng xìnxi. Please confirm your reservation details.

**会议前需要确认所有参会人员名单。** Huìyì qián xūyào quèrèn suǒyǒu cānhuì rényuán míngdān. The list of all attendees needs to be confirmed before the meeting.

**经过检查，工程师确认了设备故障的原因。** Jīngguò jiǎnchá, gōngchéngshī quèrènle shèbèi gùzhàng de yuányīn. After inspection, the engineer confirmed the cause of the equipment failure.

**双方已确认合同条款，即将签署。** Shuāngfāng yǐ quèrèn hétóng tiáokuǎn, jíjiāng qiānshǔ. Both parties have confirmed the contract terms and are about to sign.

**系统发送了一封邮件以确认注册成功。** Xìtǒng fāsòngle yī fēng yóujiàn yǐ quèrèn zhùcè chénggōng. The system sent an email to confirm successful registration.

**目击者的陈述确认了监控视频中的情况。** Mùjī zhě de chénshù quèrènle jiāngkòng shìpín zhōng de qíngkuàng. The witness's account confirmed the situation shown in the surveillance footage.

## Usage Notes

确认 is more action-oriented than 证实 (zhèngshí, to verify/confirm a fact). While 证实 is about verifying factual truth through evidence, 确认 often implies an active step of checking and acknowledging, such as confirming a booking or an identity. It is widely used in professional correspondence, contracts, and official procedures.

The phrase 确认无误 (confirmed with no errors) is a standard expression in formal and business writing. 确认收到 is the standard way to acknowledge receipt of documents or goods. At B2, learners should recognize 确认 as a transactional, procedural verb.

## Memory Hook

QUE (确, definite/certain) + REN (认, to recognize) = to recognize something as definitely certain.
