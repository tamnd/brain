## 完善 (wánshàn)

To perfect or improve something means to make it more complete, thorough, and free of flaws, often referring to systems, policies, or mechanisms.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to improve; to perfect; to refine; to make more complete
2. **[adjective]** perfect; well-rounded; comprehensive; fully developed

## Example Sentences

**政府致力于完善社会保障体系。** Zhèngfǔ zhìlì yú wánshàn shèhuì bǎozhàng tǐxì. The government is committed to improving the social security system.

**公司需要不断完善内部管理制度。** Gōngsī xūyào bùduàn wánshàn nèibù guǎnlǐ zhìdù. The company needs to continuously improve its internal management systems.

**这部法律还不完善，需要进一步修订。** Zhè bù fǎlǜ hái bù wánshàn, xūyào jìnyībù xiūdìng. This law is not yet perfect and needs further revision.

**完善的基础设施是吸引外资的重要条件。** Wánshàn de jīchǔ shèshī shì xīyǐn wàizī de zhòngyào tiáojiàn. Well-developed infrastructure is an important condition for attracting foreign investment.

**他通过不断学习来完善自己的专业技能。** Tā tōngguò bùduàn xuéxí lái wánshàn zìjǐ de zhuānyè jìnéng. He improves his professional skills through continuous learning.

**完善法律法规是维护社会公平正义的基础。** Wánshàn fǎlǜ fǎguī shì wéihù shèhuì gōngpíng zhèngyì de jīchǔ. Improving laws and regulations is the foundation for maintaining social fairness and justice.

## Usage Notes

完善 is both a verb and an adjective. As a verb, it means "to improve/perfect" something that is already partially good but needs refinement. It implies incremental improvement rather than complete overhaul. It is especially common in government, policy, and institutional contexts.

As an adjective, 完善 describes something as comprehensive and fully developed (完善的体系 -- a well-developed system). Note that 完善 implies a high standard but with room for ongoing improvement, unlike 完美 (perfect, flawless) which implies no room for improvement.

## Memory Hook

完 (complete) + 善 (good/virtuous) -- making something completely good is what it means to 完善 it.
