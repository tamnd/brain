## 触动 (chùdòng)

触动 means to emotionally move or stir someone, or to touch on something sensitive, and sits between the physical act of touching and the deeper sense of being emotionally affected.

## Meanings
1. **[verb]** to move (emotionally); to stir; to touch the heart
2. **[verb]** to touch; to disturb; to impinge on (something sensitive)

## Example Sentences
**这部电影深深触动了我的内心。** Zhè bù diànyǐng shēnshēn chùdòng le wǒ de nèixīn. This film deeply moved my heart.

**他的故事触动了在场所有人。** Tā de gùshi chùdòng le zàichǎng suǒyǒu rén. His story moved everyone present.

**不要触动他的痛处，他还没有走出来。** Bùyào chùdòng tā de tòngchù, tā hái méiyǒu zǒu chūlái. Do not touch on his sore spot; he has not yet moved past it.

**一首老歌触动了她对童年的回忆。** Yī shǒu lǎo gē chùdòng le tā duì tóngnián de huíyì. An old song stirred her memories of childhood.

**这篇文章触动了很多读者的心弦。** Zhè piān wénzhāng chùdòng le hěn duō dúzhě de xīnxián. This article struck a chord with many readers.

**他的一席话触动了我，让我重新思考自己的选择。** Tā de yī xí huà chùdòng le wǒ, ràng wǒ chóngxīn sīkǎo zìjǐ de xuǎnzé. His words moved me and made me rethink my choices.

## Collocations

## Usage Notes
触动 can be used for both emotional resonance (moving, stirring) and for touching on sensitive topics. In literary and journalistic writing it often describes a profound emotional impact. The object is typically an emotional state or memory (内心, 心弦, 情感) rather than a physical object.

Distinguish from 感动 (gǎndòng), which specifically means to be emotionally moved to a strong degree, often to tears. 触动 can be subtler and does not necessarily imply deep emotion -- it can also mean merely stirring or prompting reflection.

## Memory Hook
触 (to touch/contact) + 动 (to move): to touch something so that it moves -- to touch someone emotionally.
