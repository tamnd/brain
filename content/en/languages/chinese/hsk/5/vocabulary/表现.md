## 表现 (biǎoxiàn)

表现 is a versatile and high-frequency word meaning to show or express something (especially a quality or emotion), and as a noun, it refers to one's performance, behavior, or the way something is manifested.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to show; to express; to manifest; to perform
2. **[noun]** performance; behavior; the way something is expressed or shown

## Example Sentences

**他在课堂上的表现一直很出色。** Tā zài kètáng shàng de biǎoxiàn yīzhí hěn chūsè. His performance in class has always been excellent.

**这部作品很好地表现了人与自然的和谐关系。** Zhè bù zuòpǐn hěn hǎo de biǎoxiànle rén yǔ zìrán de héxié guānxi. This work effectively expresses the harmonious relationship between humans and nature.

**她在面试中的表现给招聘方留下了深刻印象。** Tā zài miànshì zhōng de biǎoxiàn gěi zhāopìn fāng liúxiàle shēnkè yìnxiàng. Her performance in the interview left a deep impression on the recruitment team.

**市场的疲软表现令投资者感到担忧。** Shìchǎng de písǎo biǎoxiàn lìng tóuzī zhě gǎndào dānyōu. The sluggish performance of the market made investors worried.

**优秀的演员能将人物内心的矛盾淋漓尽致地表现出来。** Yōuxiù de yǎnyuán néng jiāng rénwù nèixīn de máodùn línlíjìnzhì de biǎoxiàn chūlái. Excellent actors can express the inner contradictions of a character to the fullest.

**这首音乐表现了作曲家对战争的深刻反思。** Zhè shǒu yīnyuè biǎoxiànle zuòqǔjiā duì zhànzhēng de shēnkè fǎnsī. This piece of music expresses the composer's deep reflection on war.

## Usage Notes

表现 is one of the most flexible words in formal Chinese. As a verb, it is used to describe how a quality, emotion, or idea is expressed through behavior, art, or performance. As a noun, it describes the quality of someone's behavior or performance in a given context (his academic performance, her interview performance).

Compared with 体现 (tǐxiàn, to embody), 表现 is broader and can describe both abstract expression and concrete behavior. 表现手法 (expressive technique) is a key term in literary and artistic criticism. At B2, learners should be confident using 表现 in both its verb and noun roles.

## Memory Hook

BIAO (表, surface/outside) + XIAN (现, to appear) = something appearing on the surface for others to see.
