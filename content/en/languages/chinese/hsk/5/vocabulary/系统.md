## 系统 (xìtǒng)

系统 refers to an organised set of interconnected components that function as a whole. As an adjective it means systematic or comprehensive. It is one of the most productive words in formal Chinese, appearing across science, technology, medicine, education, and management.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** system (biological, technological, social, administrative, etc.)
2. **[adjective]** systematic; comprehensive; well-organised

## Example Sentences

**人体的免疫系统是抵御疾病入侵的第一道防线。** Réntǐ de miǎnyì xìtǒng shì dǐyù jíbìng rùqīn de dì yī dào fánggxiàn. The human immune system is the first line of defence against disease invasion.

**操作系统崩溃后，所有未保存的文件都丢失了。** Cāozuò xìtǒng bēngkuì hòu, suǒyǒu wèi bǎocún de wénjiàn dōu diūshīle. After the operating system crashed, all unsaved files were lost.

**学好一门语言需要系统地学习语法、词汇和发音。** Xué hǎo yī mén yǔyán xūyào xìtǒng de xuéxí yǔfǎ, cíhuì hé fāyīn. Learning a language well requires systematic study of grammar, vocabulary, and pronunciation.

**医院建立了完善的患者信息管理系统，大幅提升了诊疗效率。** Yīyuàn jiànlìle wánshàn de huànzhě xìnxī guǎnlǐ xìtǒng, dàfú tíshēngle zhěnliáo xiàolǜ. The hospital has established a comprehensive patient information management system, significantly improving diagnostic and treatment efficiency.

**气候系统的变化是当前全球环境科学研究的核心议题。** Qìhòu xìtǒng de biànhuà shì dāngqián quánqiú huánjìng kēxué yánjiū de héxīn yìtí. Changes in the climate system are a central topic in current global environmental science research.

**他对这一领域进行了系统的研究，撰写了一部颇具影响力的著作。** Tā duì zhè yī lǐngyù jìnxíngle xìtǒng de yánjiū, zhuǎnxiěle yī bù pō jù yǐngxiǎnglì de zhùzuò. He conducted systematic research in this field and wrote a highly influential work.

## Usage Notes

系统 as a noun is extremely versatile and combines with almost any domain word: 教育系统 (education system), 金融系统 (financial system), 生态系统 (ecosystem). As an adverb, 系统地 (systematically) modifies verbs: 系统地学习 (study systematically).

At B2 level learners should note that 系统性 (systemic/systematic, as an adjective before nouns) differs subtly from 系统 used directly as an adjective: 系统性问题 (a systemic problem) vs. 系统学习 (systematic study).

## Memory Hook

系 (connect/tie) + 统 (unify/overall) -- tying everything together into a unified whole; that is a system.
