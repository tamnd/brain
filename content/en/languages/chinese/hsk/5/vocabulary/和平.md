## 和平 (hépíng)

和平 means peace -- the absence of war and conflict, a state of harmony and tranquility -- and is one of the most important political and social vocabulary words in Chinese.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** peace; a state of peace; peaceful coexistence
2. **[adjective]** peaceful; non-violent; pacifist

## Example Sentences

**世界和平是全人类共同的愿望。** Shìjiè hépíng shì quán rénlèi gòngtóng de yuànwàng. World peace is the common desire of all humanity.

**两国签署了和平条约，结束了长达数年的战争。** Liǎng guó qiānshǔ le hépíng tiáoyuē, jiéshù le chángdá shùnián de zhànzhēng. The two countries signed a peace treaty, ending years of war.

**我们应该通过和平方式解决国际争端。** Wǒmen yīnggāi tōngguò hépíng fāngshì jiějué guójì zhēngduān. We should resolve international disputes through peaceful means.

**维护和平是每一个国家的责任。** Wéihù hépíng shì měi yī gè guójiā de zérèn. Maintaining peace is the responsibility of every country.

**那个地区已经经历了数十年的和平与繁荣。** Nàgè dìqū yǐjīng jīnglì le shùshí nián de hépíng yǔ fánróng. That region has experienced decades of peace and prosperity.

**他一生致力于推动世界和平。** Tā yīshēng zhìlì yú tuīdòng shìjiè hépíng. He devoted his life to promoting world peace.

## Usage Notes

和平 is a foundational political term in Chinese, often appearing in diplomatic language, government documents, and international relations discourse. The phrase 和平共处五项原则 (Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence) is a cornerstone of Chinese foreign policy.

As an adjective, 和平 modifies nouns like 方式 (means), 解决 (resolution), and 环境 (environment). As a noun, it often takes verbs like 维护 (uphold), 破坏 (undermine), 追求 (pursue), and 实现 (achieve).

## Memory Hook

和 means "harmony" and 平 means "flat/calm/equal." 和平 is the harmony of a calm, flat surface -- still waters with no waves of conflict.
