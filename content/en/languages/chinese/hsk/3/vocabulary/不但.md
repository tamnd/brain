## 不但 (búdàn)

不但 means "not only" and always introduces the first of two additive clauses. It is nearly always paired with 而且 (érqiě — but also) or 还 (hái — also) in the second clause, forming the "not only X, but also Y" pattern.

## Meanings

1. **[conjunction]** Not only (used with 而且 or 还 in the second clause).

## Example Sentences

**他不但聪明，而且很努力。**
Tā búdàn cōngming, érqiě hěn nǔlì.
He is not only smart but also very hardworking.

**这家餐厅不但好吃，而且价格便宜。**
Zhè jiā cāntīng búdàn hǎochī, érqiě jiàgé piányí.
This restaurant is not only delicious but also affordable.

**她不但会说中文，还会说日文。**
Tā búdàn huì shuō Zhōngwén, hái huì shuō Rìwén.
She can not only speak Chinese but also Japanese.

**这个方法不但有效，而且简单易学。**
Zhège fāngfǎ búdàn yǒuxiào, érqiě jiǎndān yì xué.
This method is not only effective but also simple and easy to learn.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 不但…而且… | not only…but also… |
| 不但…还… | not only…but also… |
| 不但如此 | not only that |
| 不但没有 | not only did not |
| 不但…甚至… | not only…even… |

## Memory Hook

不 (not) plus 但 (only / merely). Canceling the "merely" — you are going beyond the mere minimum to add something even more.
