## 问候 (wènhòu)

问候 means to greet someone warmly or to send one's regards, expressing care and goodwill toward another person.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to greet; to send regards — to express friendly concern or good wishes to someone
2. **[noun]** greetings; regards — a warm expression of goodwill directed at someone

## Example Sentences

**请代我向你的父母问候。** Qǐng dài wǒ xiàng nǐ de fùmǔ wènhòu. Please send my regards to your parents.

**他发来了节日问候。** Tā fā lái le jiérì wènhòu. He sent holiday greetings.

**她每次见面都热情地问候大家。** Tā měi cì jiànmiàn dōu rèqíng de wènhòu dàjiā. She warmly greets everyone whenever she meets them.

**收到朋友的问候让我感到很温暖。** Shōudào péngyǒu de wènhòu ràng wǒ gǎndào hěn wēnnuǎn. Receiving greetings from friends makes me feel very warm.

## Memory Hook

问 (to ask; to inquire) plus 候 (to wait on; to inquire after someone's health) — asking after someone's wellbeing: that is a genuine 问候.
