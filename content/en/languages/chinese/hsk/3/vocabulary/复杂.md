## 复杂 (fùzá)

复杂 describes something that is complex or complicated, involving many interwoven parts, factors, or relationships that make it hard to understand or deal with.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** Complex; complicated; intricate.

## Example Sentences

**这个问题很复杂，需要时间解决。**
Zhège wèntí hěn fùzá, xūyào shíjiān jiějué.
This problem is very complicated and requires time to solve.

**情况比我想象的更复杂。**
Qíngkuàng bǐ wǒ xiǎngxiàng de gèng fùzá.
The situation is more complicated than I imagined.

**汉字的书写规则比较复杂。**
Hànzì de shūxiě guīzé bǐjiào fùzá.
The writing rules for Chinese characters are relatively complex.

**她的心情很复杂，说不清楚。**
Tā de xīnqíng hěn fùzá, shuō bu qīngchǔ.
Her feelings are very complicated — hard to put into words.

## Memory Hook

复 (to repeat / layers of folds) plus 杂 (mixed/miscellaneous). Layers of mixed, folded things piled on top of each other — that is complexity.
