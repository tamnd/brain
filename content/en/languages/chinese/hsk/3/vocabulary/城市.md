## 城市 (chéngshì)

城市 means city or urban area. It refers to any large, populated settlement with developed infrastructure, as opposed to countryside (农村 nóngcūn).

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** City; urban area; town (large).

## Example Sentences

**北京是中国最大的城市之一。**
Běijīng shì Zhōngguó zuì dà de chéngshì zhī yī.
Beijing is one of the largest cities in China.

**他从农村来到城市工作。**
Tā cóng nóngcūn láidào chéngshì gōngzuò.
He came from the countryside to work in the city.

**这个城市的交通非常方便。**
Zhège chéngshì de jiāotōng fēicháng fāngbiàn.
Transportation in this city is very convenient.

**城市的生活节奏比农村快。**
Chéngshì de shēnghuó jiézòu bǐ nóngcūn kuài.
The pace of life in the city is faster than in the countryside.

## Memory Hook

城 (city wall — earth 土 protecting the enclosed settlement) plus 市 (market). A walled settlement with a market — the ancient core of every city.
