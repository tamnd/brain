## 出现 (chūxiàn)

To come into existence or view; used when something appears, emerges, or occurs where it was not before.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To appear, to come into view, to show up.
2. **[verb]** To occur, to arise, to emerge (of a problem or situation).

## Example Sentences

**天空中出现了一道彩虹。**
Tiānkōng zhōng chūxiàn le yī dào cǎihóng.
A rainbow appeared in the sky.

**最近出现了一种新病毒。**
Zuìjìn chūxiàn le yī zhǒng xīn bìngdú.
A new virus has appeared recently.

**他突然出现在我面前。**
Tā tūrán chūxiàn zài wǒ miànqián.
He suddenly appeared in front of me.

**工作中出现了一些问题，我们需要解决。**
Gōngzuò zhōng chūxiàn le yīxiē wèntí, wǒmen xūyào jiějué.
Some problems have arisen at work; we need to solve them.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 出现问题 | a problem arises |
| 突然出现 | appear suddenly |
| 出现变化 | a change appears |
| 首次出现 | appear for the first time |
| 出现在… | appear in/at... |

## Memory Hook

出 means "to exit/come out" and 现 means "to be visible/present" — coming out and becoming visible is to appear.
