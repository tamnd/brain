## 邻居 (línjū)

邻居 refers to a neighbor — someone who lives next door or in close proximity. It is a neutral, everyday word used in both city apartment contexts and traditional neighborhood settings. 邻 relates to proximity and adjacency, while 居 means "to reside / dwell."

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Neighbor; next-door resident; someone living nearby.

## Example Sentences

**我的邻居是一对年轻夫妻。**
Wǒ de línjū shì yī duì niánqīng fūqī.
My neighbor is a young couple.

**邻居帮我照看了猫。**
Línjū bāng wǒ zhàokàn le māo.
My neighbor helped look after my cat.

**我们和邻居的关系很好。**
Wǒmen hé línjū de guānxi hěn hǎo.
We have a great relationship with our neighbors.

**昨晚邻居家很吵，我没睡好。**
Zuówǎn línjū jiā hěn chǎo, wǒ méi shuì hǎo.
The neighbor's place was very noisy last night and I didn't sleep well.

## Memory Hook

邻 contains the right-ear radical 阝 (used for places and areas) suggesting locality. 居 means "to dwell." A 邻居 is simply someone who dwells in your locality — within earshot of your daily life.
