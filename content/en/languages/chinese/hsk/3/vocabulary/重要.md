## 重要 (zhòngyào)

重要 is one of the most common adjectives in Chinese, meaning "important" or "significant." It describes anything that carries weight or consequence — a task, a person, a date, a decision. It can modify a noun (重要的事 — an important thing) or follow a verb (很重要 — very important).

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** Important; significant; essential; of great consequence.

## Example Sentences

**健康是最重要的事情。**
Jiànkāng shì zuì zhòngyào de shìqíng.
Health is the most important thing.

**这次考试非常重要，你一定要认真准备。**
Zhè cì kǎoshì fēicháng zhòngyào, nǐ yīdìng yào rènzhēn zhǔnbèi.
This exam is very important; you must prepare carefully.

**他在公司里扮演着重要的角色。**
Tā zài gōngsī lǐ bànyǎn zhe zhòngyào de juésè.
He plays an important role in the company.

**有一件重要的事情要告诉你。**
Yǒu yī jiàn zhòngyào de shìqíng yào gàosù nǐ.
There is an important matter I need to tell you.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 非常重要 | very important |

| 重要的事 | an important thing |
| 重要会议 | important meeting |
| 至关重要 | of crucial importance |

## Memory Hook

重 means "heavy / weighty" and 要 means "to want / need / be necessary." Something 重要 is heavy with necessity — it is weighty and needed, so you cannot ignore it.
