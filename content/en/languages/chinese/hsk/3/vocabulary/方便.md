## 方便 (fāngbiàn)

方便 means convenient or to be convenient. It describes something that is easy, practical, and saves time or effort. It can also function as a verb meaning to make things convenient or to accommodate. In colloquial Chinese it can also be a polite way to say "to use the toilet."

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** Convenient; easy to use or access; practical.
2. **[verb]** To be convenient (for someone); to be suitable.

## Example Sentences

**住在地铁附近很方便。**
Zhù zài dìtiě fùjìn hěn fāngbiàn.
Living near the subway is very convenient.

**你方便的话，我们下午见面吧。**
Nǐ fāngbiàn dehuà, wǒmen xiàwǔ jiànmiàn ba.
If it is convenient for you, let us meet in the afternoon.

**网上购物非常方便。**
Wǎngshàng gòuwù fēicháng fāngbiàn.
Online shopping is very convenient.

**这个地方交通方便，什么都有。**
Zhège dìfang jiāotōng fāngbiàn, shénme dōu yǒu.
This place has convenient transportation and has everything.

## Memory Hook

方 (square/orderly) plus 便 (easy/comfortable). When everything is orderly, life becomes comfortable and easy. That is 方便. Note that 方便面 (instant noodles) literally means "convenient noodles" — the most famous 方便 product in China, ready in minutes with no fuss.
