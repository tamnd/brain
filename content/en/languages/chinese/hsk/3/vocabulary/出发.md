## 出发 (chūfā)

出发 means to set off or depart from a location, usually the beginning of a trip or journey. It focuses on the moment of departure.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To set off; to depart; to start (a journey).

## Example Sentences

**我们明天早上八点出发。**
Wǒmen míngtiān zǎoshang bā diǎn chūfā.
We set off at eight o'clock tomorrow morning.

**大家都准备好了，可以出发了。**
Dàjiā dōu zhǔnbèi hǎo le, kěyǐ chūfā le.
Everyone is ready; we can set off now.

**他们从北京出发，去上海旅游。**
Tāmen cóng Běijīng chūfā, qù Shànghǎi lǚyóu.
They departed from Beijing and went to Shanghai to travel.

**出发前，请检查一下行李。**
Chūfā qián, qǐng jiǎnchá yīxià xínglǐ.
Before setting off, please check your luggage.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |

## Memory Hook

出 (exit / go out) plus 发 (send off / launch). Going out and being launched — the moment you step out and your journey begins.
