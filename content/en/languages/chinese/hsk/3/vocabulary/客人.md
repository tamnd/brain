## 客人 (kèrén)

客人 means guest or visitor. It refers to anyone who comes to your home as an invited visitor or a customer arriving at a business. Chinese culture places great importance on hospitality toward 客人.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Guest; visitor; customer.

## Example Sentences

**我们家今天有客人来。**
Wǒmen jiā jīntiān yǒu kèrén lái.
We have guests coming to our home today.

**要好好招待客人。**
Yào hǎohǎo zhāodài kèrén.
We should take good care of our guests.

**客人已经到了，请开门。**
Kèrén yǐjīng dào le, qǐng kāimén.
The guests have already arrived; please open the door.

**酒店里的客人很多。**
Jiǔdiàn lǐ de kèrén hěn duō.
There are many guests in the hotel.

## Memory Hook

客 has a roof (宀) over a person who has come from afar (夂 + 各). A 客人 is literally "a person who arrived from outside and entered under your roof." Roll out the welcome mat.
