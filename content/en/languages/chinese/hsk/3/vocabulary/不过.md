## 不过 (búguò)

不过 means however or but, used to introduce a mild contrast or qualification. It is softer and more conversational than 但是 (dànshì), making it ideal for gentle corrections or adding a caveat.

## Meanings

1. **[conjunction]** However; but; only (introducing a mild contrast or qualification).
2. **[adverb]** Only; merely; nothing more than.

## Example Sentences

**这道菜很好吃，不过有点辣。**
Zhè dào cài hěn hǎochī, búguò yǒu diǎn là.
This dish is very tasty; however, it's a bit spicy.

**我很想去，不过没有时间。**
Wǒ hěn xiǎng qù, búguò méiyǒu shíjiān.
I really want to go; however, I don't have time.

**他说得不错，不过还有一些小错误。**
Tā shuō de bùcuò, búguò hái yǒu yīxiē xiǎo cuòwù.
He spoke well; however, there are still some small errors.

**这不过是个小问题，不用担心。**
Zhè búguò shì gè xiǎo wèntí, bú yòng dānxīn.
This is merely a small problem; don't worry.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 不过是 | merely; nothing but |
| 说不过 | can't argue against |
| 不过如此 | nothing more than this; that's all |
| 如此不过 | so, nothing more |
| 只不过 | merely; just |

## Memory Hook

不 (not) plus 过 (pass/exceed): it does not exceed a certain point — it holds back with a gentle "but." A soft brake on the sentence.
