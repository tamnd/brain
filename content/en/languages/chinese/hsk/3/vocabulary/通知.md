## 通知 (tōngzhī)

通知 means to inform or notify someone about something, especially an official or important matter. As a noun it refers to a notice, announcement, or circular.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To notify; to inform; to let know.
2. **[noun]** A notice; an announcement; a notification.

## Example Sentences

**老师通知我们明天考试。**
Lǎoshī tōngzhī wǒmen míngtiān kǎoshì.
The teacher notified us that there is an exam tomorrow.

**请把这个通知发给所有人。**
Qǐng bǎ zhège tōngzhī fāgěi suǒyǒu rén.
Please send this notice to everyone.

**公司发出了一份新通知。**
Gōngsī fāchū le yīfèn xīn tōngzhī.
The company issued a new notice.

**我已经通知了所有同学。**
Wǒ yǐjīng tōngzhī le suǒyǒu tóngxué.
I have already notified all classmates.

## Memory Hook

通 means "through / communicate" and 知 means "to know." Pass knowledge through to someone — that is the act of notification.
