## 原因 (yuányīn)

原因 means the reason or cause behind an event or situation. It asks or states "why" something happened.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Reason; cause; explanation.

## Example Sentences

**你知道他迟到的原因吗？**
Nǐ zhīdào tā chídào de yuányīn ma?
Do you know the reason he was late?

**找出问题的原因是解决问题的第一步。**
Zhǎochū wèntí de yuányīn shì jiějué wèntí de dìyī bù.
Finding the reason for a problem is the first step to solving it.

**她失败的原因是没有认真准备。**
Tā shībài de yuányīn shì méiyǒu rènzhēn zhǔnbèi.
The reason she failed was that she didn't prepare seriously.

**不明原因的发烧需要看医生。**
Bù míng yuányīn de fāshāo xūyào kàn yīshēng.
A fever of unknown cause requires a doctor's visit.
