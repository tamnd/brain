## 机场 (jīchǎng)

机场 is the standard word for airport, the facility where passengers board and depart from aircraft.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** airport — a place with runways and terminals for the departure and arrival of aircraft and passengers

## Example Sentences

**我们明天早上六点去机场。** Wǒmen míngtiān zǎoshang liù diǎn qù jīchǎng. We are going to the airport tomorrow at six in the morning.

**机场离市中心很远。** Jīchǎng lí shì zhōngxīn hěn yuǎn. The airport is far from the city center.

**他在机场等了两个小时。** Tā zài jīchǎng děng le liǎng gè xiǎoshí. He waited at the airport for two hours.

**请提前两小时到达机场。** Qǐng tíqián liǎng xiǎoshí dàodá jīchǎng. Please arrive at the airport two hours in advance.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |

## Memory Hook

机 (airplane) plus 场 (field; venue) — the field where airplanes gather: that is the 机场.
