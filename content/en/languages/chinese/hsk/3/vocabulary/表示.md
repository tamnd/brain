## 表示 (biǎoshì)

表示 means to express or indicate. It is used when conveying emotions, opinions, or meanings, whether through words, gestures, or symbols. It is more formal and deliberate than simply 说 (to say).

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To express; to show; to convey (feelings, attitudes, opinions).
2. **[verb]** To indicate; to mean; to represent; to signify.

## Example Sentences

**他向我表示了感谢。**
Tā xiàng wǒ biǎoshì le gǎnxiè.
He expressed his thanks to me.

**她点头表示同意。**
Tā diǎntóu biǎoshì tóngyì.
She nodded to indicate agreement.

**红灯表示停止，绿灯表示通行。**
Hóngdēng biǎoshì tíngzhǐ, lǜdēng biǎoshì tōngxíng.
A red light indicates stop; a green light indicates go.

**他表示愿意帮助我们。**
Tā biǎoshì yuànyì bāngzhù wǒmen.
He expressed willingness to help us.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 表示感谢 | to express gratitude |
| 表示同意 | to indicate agreement |
| 表示反对 | to express opposition |
| 表示欢迎 | to express welcome |
| 表示理解 | to show understanding |

## Memory Hook

表 means outward surface or to express externally, and 示 means to show or indicate. 表示 is to show something outward from within, like using a gesture or word to let others see what is inside your mind or heart.
