## 辛苦 (xīnkǔ)

辛苦 describes work or effort that is tiring, demanding, or difficult. It is also commonly used as a polite expression to acknowledge and appreciate someone's hard work.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** hard; toilsome; physically or mentally exhausting
2. **[verb]** to work hard; to go through hardship and toil

## Example Sentences

**你辛苦了，快来休息一下。** Nǐ xīnkǔ le, kuài lái xiūxi yīxià. You have worked hard; come and rest for a bit.

**农民的工作很辛苦，要每天在田里劳动。** Nóngmín de gōngzuò hěn xīnkǔ, yào měitiān zài tián lǐ láodòng. Farmers' work is very hard; they have to labor in the fields every day.

**妈妈为了家庭辛苦了一辈子。** Māma wèile jiātíng xīnkǔ le yī bèizi. Mom worked hard for the family her whole life.

**谢谢你为这个项目这么辛苦。** Xièxiè nǐ wèi zhège xiàngmù zhème xīnkǔ. Thank you for working so hard on this project.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 辛苦了 (xīnkǔ le) | you've worked hard (polite acknowledgment) |
| 非常辛苦 (fēicháng xīnkǔ) | very hard; exhausting |
| 吃辛苦 (chī xīnkǔ) | to endure hardship |
| 辛苦工作 (xīnkǔ gōngzuò) | to work hard |

## Memory Hook

辛 means "bitter/painful" and 苦 also means "bitter/suffering" — when both bitterness and suffering come together in one word, that is truly 辛苦 hard labor.
