## 照 (zhào)

照 is a versatile verb with several core meanings centered on light and reflection. It means to take a photograph, to shine light on something, to reflect, and in compounds to look after. 照片 (photograph) comes from this character.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To take a photo; to photograph.
2. **[verb]** To shine on; to illuminate.
3. **[verb]** To reflect (in a mirror).
4. **[verb]** To follow or accord with (in compounds like 按照, 照常).

## Example Sentences

**我们在长城照了很多相。**
Wǒmen zài Chángchéng zhào le hěn duō xiàng.
We took many photos at the Great Wall.

**阳光照在水面上，非常美丽。**
Yángguāng zhào zài shuǐmiàn shàng, fēicháng měilì.
The sunlight shines on the water surface, which is very beautiful.

**她对着镜子照了照。**
Tā duìzhe jìngzi zhào le zhào.
She looked at herself in the mirror.

**这张照片拍得真好看。**
Zhè zhāng zhàopiàn pāi de zhēn hǎokàn.
This photograph turned out really beautiful.

## Memory Hook

照 has 火 (fire) at the bottom and a sun-like top. Light shines downward, illuminating everything below, like the sun burning and casting its rays on the world. Shining light captures images, which is how photos work.
