## 爱好 (àihào)

爱好 refers to a personal hobby or interest, something you enjoy doing in your spare time.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** a hobby or interest that someone enjoys
2. **[verb]** to love; to be fond of something

## Example Sentences

**我的爱好是画画和读书。** Wǒ de àihào shì huà huà hé dú shū. My hobbies are painting and reading.

**你有什么爱好？** Nǐ yǒu shénme àihào? What hobbies do you have?

**他非常爱好音乐，每天都练习钢琴。** Tā fēicháng àihào yīnyuè, měitiān dōu liànxí gāngqín. He is very fond of music and practices piano every day.

**培养一个爱好对身心健康很有好处。** Péiyǎng yī gè àihào duì shēnxīn jiànkāng hěn yǒu hǎochù. Developing a hobby is very beneficial for your physical and mental health.

Think of 爱 (love) plus 好 (good): a hobby is something you love and find good.
