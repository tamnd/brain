## 耳朵 (ěrduo)

耳朵 means ears. It refers to the organs of hearing on the sides of the head. Note that 朵 is pronounced in the neutral tone (duo) in this compound.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Ear; ears (the organs of hearing).

## Example Sentences

**他的耳朵很灵，什么声音都能听到。**
Tā de ěrduo hěn líng, shénme shēngyīn dōu néng tīngdào.
His ears are very sharp; he can hear any sound.

**我的耳朵有点儿疼。**
Wǒ de ěrduo yǒudiǎnr téng.
My ear hurts a little.

**音乐太吵了，我的耳朵受不了。**
Yīnyuè tài chǎo le, wǒ de ěrduo shòu bu liǎo.
The music is too loud; my ears cannot take it.

**他用手捂住了耳朵。**
Tā yòng shǒu wǔzhù le ěrduo.
He covered his ears with his hands.

## Memory Hook

耳 is the ancient pictograph of an ear. Adding 朵 (which means a small rounded piece, like a flower) creates 耳朵, the full word for ear. Picture your ear as a small flower attached to the side of your head.
