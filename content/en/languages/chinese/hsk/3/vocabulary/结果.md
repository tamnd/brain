## 结果 (jiéguǒ)

结果 refers to the final result or outcome of an action or process. It can also function as a conjunction meaning "as a result" to link cause and outcome in narrative.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Result; outcome; conclusion.
2. **[conjunction]** As a result; consequently; in the end.

## Example Sentences

**考试结果出来了吗？**
Kǎoshì jiéguǒ chūlái le ma?
Have the exam results come out?

**他努力学习，结果考了满分。**
Tā nǔlì xuéxí, jiéguǒ kǎo le mǎnfēn.
He studied hard; as a result, he got a perfect score.

**不管结果怎样，我都不后悔。**
Bùguǎn jiéguǒ zěnyàng, wǒ dōu bú hòuhuǐ.
No matter the result, I have no regrets.

**等待结果的时间最难熬。**
Děngdài jiéguǒ de shíjiān zuì nán'áo.
Waiting for the result is the hardest part.

## Memory Hook

结 (to tie up / conclude — silk radical 纟) plus 果 (fruit). The fruit that ties off and appears at the end of the tree's effort — the result that ripens from all your work.
