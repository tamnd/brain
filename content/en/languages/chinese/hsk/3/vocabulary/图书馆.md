## 图书馆 (túshūguǎn)

图书馆 is the word for library. The three characters break down as: 图 (picture/image), 书 (book), 馆 (a public building or hall). A library is literally a hall of picture-books and writings.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Library (a public or institutional building for reading and borrowing books).

## Example Sentences

**我每天去图书馆学习。**
Wǒ měitiān qù túshūguǎn xuéxí.
I go to the library to study every day.

**图书馆里不能大声说话。**
Túshūguǎn lǐ bù néng dàshēng shuōhuà.
You cannot speak loudly in the library.

**学校图书馆有很多书。**
Xuéxiào túshūguǎn yǒu hěn duō shū.
The school library has a lot of books.

**我在图书馆借了两本书。**
Wǒ zài túshūguǎn jiè le liǎng běn shū.
I borrowed two books from the library.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 去图书馆 | to go to the library |
| 图书馆借书 | to borrow books from the library |
| 学校图书馆 | school library |
| 公共图书馆 | public library |
| 图书馆还书 | to return books to the library |

## Memory Hook

图 (picture) plus 书 (book) plus 馆 (public hall) = a public hall full of books and pictures. The three characters build the concept step by step.
