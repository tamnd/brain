## 认识 (rènshi)

认识 expresses familiarity with a person or the ability to recognize something. It differs from 知道 (zhīdào), which means "to know a fact." 认识 implies direct personal acquaintance or perceptual recognition.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To know (a person); to be acquainted with.
2. **[verb]** To recognize; to identify (a character, face, or object).

## Example Sentences

**你认识那个人吗？**
Nǐ rènshi nàge rén ma?
Do you know that person?

**我们是在大学认识的。**
Wǒmen shì zài dàxué rènshi de.
We met (got to know each other) in university.

**他认识很多汉字。**
Tā rènshi hěn duō Hànzì.
He can recognize many Chinese characters.

**很高兴认识你。**
Hěn gāoxìng rènshi nǐ.
Nice to meet you. (Literally: Very happy to know you.)

## Memory Hook

认 contains 言 (speech/words) and 忍 (endure): you have "heard enough words" about someone to form a picture of them. 识 (knowledge) confirms you now hold that picture in mind.
