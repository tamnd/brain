## 周末 (zhōumò)

周末 means weekend, referring to Saturday and Sunday. 周 means week and 末 means end, so 周末 is literally the end of the week. It is used just like "weekend" in English and appears in common expressions about free time and plans.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Weekend (Saturday and Sunday).

## Example Sentences

**这个周末你有什么计划？**
Zhège zhōumò nǐ yǒu shénme jìhuà?
What plans do you have this weekend?

**我喜欢周末去爬山。**
Wǒ xǐhuān zhōumò qù páshān.
I like to go hiking on weekends.

**周末我们一起吃饭吧。**
Zhōumò wǒmen yīqǐ chīfàn ba.
Let us have dinner together this weekend.

**上个周末我去了博物馆。**
Shàng gè zhōumò wǒ qù le bówùguǎn.
Last weekend I went to the museum.

## Memory Hook

周 is week and 末 is end. The weekend is the tail end of the week, the part where you finally stop working and relax.
