## 形象 (xíngxiàng)

形象 refers to the appearance, image, or impression that a person, organization, or character projects to others.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** image; figure; appearance; impression
2. **[adjective]** vivid; graphic; lifelike

## Example Sentences

**他注意保持自己的公众形象。** Tā zhùyì bǎochí zìjǐ de gōngzhòng xíngxiàng. He pays attention to maintaining his public image.

**这个广告损害了公司的形象。** Zhège guǎnggào sǔnhài le gōngsī de xíngxiàng. This advertisement damaged the company's image.

**老师用形象的例子解释了这个难懂的概念。** Lǎoshī yòng xíngxiàng de lìzi jiěshì le zhège nán dǒng de gàiniàn. The teacher used vivid examples to explain this difficult concept.

**小说中的这个人物形象非常生动。** Xiǎoshuō zhōng de zhège rénwù xíngxiàng fēicháng shēngdòng. The character's image in the novel is very vivid.

## Memory Hook

形 means "shape/form" and 象 means "appearance/likeness" — 形象 is the shape and appearance something shows to the world.
