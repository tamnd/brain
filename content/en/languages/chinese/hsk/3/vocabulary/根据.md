## 根据 (gēnjù)

根据 introduces the source, evidence, or basis for a statement or action. It means "based on" or "according to," and is used to justify conclusions, rules, or decisions.

## Meanings

1. **[preposition]** Based on; according to; in light of.
2. **[noun]** Basis; grounds; evidence.

## Example Sentences

**根据天气预报，明天会下雨。**
Gēnjù tiānqì yùbào, míngtiān huì xià yǔ.
According to the weather forecast, it will rain tomorrow.

**根据规定，不能在这里停车。**
Gēnjù guīdìng, bù néng zài zhèlǐ tíngchē.
According to regulations, parking is not allowed here.

**你说的话有什么根据？**
Nǐ shuō de huà yǒu shénme gēnjù?
What is the basis for what you said?

**根据我的经验，这种方法最有效。**
Gēnjù wǒ de jīngyàn, zhè zhǒng fāngfǎ zuì yǒuxiào.
Based on my experience, this method is most effective.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 根据…来判断 | judge based on… |
| 有根据 | well-founded; evidence-based |
| 没有根据 | groundless; baseless |
| 根据情况 | depending on the situation |
| 根据研究 | based on research |

## Memory Hook

根 means "root" (tree radical 木 with a bottom mark for the root). 据 means "to hold / base." Your argument is only as strong as its root — its basis and foundation.
