## 了解 (liǎojiě)

了解 means to understand something in depth, gained through investigation, experience, or close contact. It implies a richer knowledge than simply 知道 (zhīdào — to know a fact).

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To understand well; to know in detail; to be acquainted with.
2. **[verb]** To find out; to learn about through inquiry.

## Example Sentences

**我不太了解这个情况。**
Wǒ bú tài liǎojiě zhège qíngkuàng.
I don't know this situation very well.

**你了解中国文化吗？**
Nǐ liǎojiě Zhōngguó wénhuà ma?
Do you understand Chinese culture?

**我们需要多了解彼此。**
Wǒmen xūyào duō liǎojiě bǐcǐ.
We need to understand each other better.

**通过这次旅行，我更了解了这个城市。**
Tōngguò zhècì lǚxíng, wǒ gèng liǎojiě le zhège chéngshì.
Through this trip, I understood this city much better.

## Memory Hook

了 means "finished" and 解 means "untie." You untie all the knots of confusion until everything is finished — fully understood.
