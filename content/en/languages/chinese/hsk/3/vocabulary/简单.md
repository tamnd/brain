## 简单 (jiǎndān)

简单 means simple, easy, or uncomplicated. It describes things that are not complex or difficult to understand, do, or explain.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** Simple; easy; uncomplicated; plain.
2. **[adjective]** Ordinary; plain (of a person's manner or lifestyle).

## Example Sentences

**这道题很简单，你一定能做。**
Zhè dào tí hěn jiǎndān, nǐ yīdìng néng zuò.
This problem is very simple; you can definitely do it.

**他过着很简单的生活。**
Tā guòzhe hěn jiǎndān de shēnghuó.
He lives a very simple life.

**请用简单的话解释一下。**
Qǐng yòng jiǎndān de huà jiěshì yīxià.
Please explain using simple words.

**这个方法简单易学。**
Zhège fāngfǎ jiǎndān yì xué.
This method is simple and easy to learn.

## Memory Hook

简 originally referred to bamboo writing strips (short and concise), and 单 means single or alone. 简单 is the simplest single strip: no complexity, no extras, just the bare essentials.
