## 活动 (huódòng)

活动 refers to an organized activity or event, and as a verb it means to move around or exercise.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** activity; event; exercise
2. **[verb]** to move about; to exercise; to do physical activity

## Example Sentences

**学校今天下午有一个活动。** Xuéxiào jīntiān xiàwǔ yǒu yī gè huódòng. The school has an event this afternoon.

**多做户外活动对身体有好处。** Duō zuò hùwài huódòng duì shēntǐ yǒu hǎochù. Doing more outdoor activities is good for your health.

**坐了一天，出去活动活动吧。** Zuò le yī tiān, chūqù huódòng huódòng ba. After sitting all day, let's go outside and move around a bit.

**这次文化活动吸引了很多人参加。** Zhè cì wénhuà huódòng xīyǐnle hěn duō rén cānjiā. This cultural event attracted many people to participate.

## Memory Hook

活 means "to live/lively" and 动 means "to move" — living things move, and an 活动 is anything that gets you lively and moving.
