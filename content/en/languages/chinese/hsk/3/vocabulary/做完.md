## 做完 (zuòwán)

A verb-result compound where 做 (to do) + 完 (finished/complete) indicates that an action has been completed from start to finish.

## Meanings

1. **[verb + complement]** To finish doing something, to complete a task.

## Example Sentences

**我做完作业才能出去玩。**
Wǒ zuòwán zuòyè cái néng chūqù wán.
Only after I finish my homework can I go out to play.

**他已经做完了所有的工作。**
Tā yǐjīng zuòwán le suǒyǒu de gōngzuò.
He has already finished all the work.

**你做完了吗？**
Nǐ zuòwán le ma?
Have you finished?

**这道题太难了，我没做完。**
Zhè dào tí tài nán le, wǒ méi zuòwán.
This problem is too difficult; I didn't finish it.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 做完作业 | finish homework |
| 做完工作 | finish the work |
| 没做完 | didn't finish |
| 做不完 | cannot finish |
| 做完了 | have finished |

## Memory Hook

完 is the result complement — it tells you the action reached its endpoint of completion. 做完 = do until done.
