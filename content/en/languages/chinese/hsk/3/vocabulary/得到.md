## 得到 (dédào)

得到 is a resultative verb meaning "to get" or "to obtain." The 到 suffix indicates successful completion — the getting actually happened. It is used for obtaining tangible objects, intangible rewards, information, and abstract things like support or recognition.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To get; to obtain; to receive; to achieve — successfully acquire something concrete or abstract.

## Example Sentences

**他终于得到了他梦寐以求的工作。**
Tā zhōngyú dédào le tā mèngmèiyǐqiú de gōngzuò.
He finally got the job he had always dreamed of.

**这本书很难得到，图书馆也没有。**
Zhè běn shū hěn nán dédào, túshūguǎn yě méiyǒu.
This book is hard to get; even the library doesn't have it.

**她的努力得到了老师的表扬。**
Tā de nǔlì dédào le lǎoshī de biǎoyáng.
Her hard work earned praise from the teacher.

**我们的建议没有得到重视。**
Wǒmen de jiànyì méiyǒu dédào zhòngshì.
Our suggestions were not taken seriously.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 得到支持 | to gain support |
| 得到认可 | to receive recognition/approval |
| 得到机会 | to get an opportunity |
| 得到帮助 | to receive help |
| 得到消息 | to receive news/information |

## Memory Hook

得 means "to get/obtain" and 到 means "to arrive/reach." Together they reinforce the idea that you reached out and the thing actually arrived in your hands — the obtaining was completed.
