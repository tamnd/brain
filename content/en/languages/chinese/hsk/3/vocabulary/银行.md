## 银行 (yínháng)

银行 is the standard word for a bank — the financial institution where you deposit money, make transfers, take out loans, and exchange currency. 银 means "silver" (a historical reference to silver as currency) and 行 means "business establishment / trade." Together they evoke a silver-dealing establishment.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Bank; financial institution.

## Example Sentences

**我要去银行取一些钱。**
Wǒ yào qù yínháng qǔ yīxiē qián.
I need to go to the bank to withdraw some money.

**请问附近有银行吗？**
Qǐngwèn fùjìn yǒu yínháng ma?
Excuse me, is there a bank nearby?

**他在银行工作，负责贷款业务。**
Tā zài yínháng gōngzuò, fùzé dàikuǎn yèwù.
He works at a bank and is responsible for loan services.

**我需要在银行开一个账户。**
Wǒ xūyào zài yínháng kāi yīgè zhànghù.
I need to open an account at the bank.

## Memory Hook

银 (silver) plus 行 (trade establishment). In imperial China, trading houses dealt in silver bars. The modern bank is just a very large, very official silver-trading house — so the name stuck.
