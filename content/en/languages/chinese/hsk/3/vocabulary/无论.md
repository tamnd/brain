## 无论 (wúlùn)

无论 means no matter or regardless of. It introduces a condition or possibility that does not change the outcome. It must always be followed later in the sentence by 都 or 也 to complete the meaning. The pattern is: 无论 + condition + 都/也 + result.

## Meanings

1. **[conjunction]** No matter; regardless of. Always used in the pattern 无论...都/也.

## Example Sentences

**无论多忙，他都会打电话给父母。**
Wúlùn duō máng, tā dōu huì dǎ diànhuà gěi fùmǔ.
No matter how busy he is, he always calls his parents.

**无论你去哪里，我都支持你。**
Wúlùn nǐ qù nǎlǐ, wǒ dōu zhīchí nǐ.
Wherever you go, I will support you.

**无论天气好坏，他也坚持跑步。**
Wúlùn tiānqì hǎo huài, tā yě jiānchí pǎobù.
Regardless of the weather, he keeps running.

**无论发生什么，要保持冷静。**
Wúlùn fāshēng shénme, yào bǎochí lěngjìng.
No matter what happens, stay calm.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 无论...都 | no matter...still |
| 无论...也 | no matter...also/still |
| 无论如何 | no matter what; in any case |
| 无论何时 | no matter when |
| 无论谁 | no matter who |

## Memory Hook

无 (none) plus 论 (to discuss/consider). No matter what is discussed or considered, the result stays the same. Always lock it with 都 or 也 at the end.
