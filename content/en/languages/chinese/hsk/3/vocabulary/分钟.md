## 分钟 (fēnzhōng)

A measure word for expressing the duration of time in minutes. Contrast with 分 (fēn), which refers to the clock position (e.g., 3:05).

## Meanings

1. **[measure word / noun]** Minute(s) of duration (how long something takes).

## Example Sentences

**从这里走到学校只需要十分钟。**
Cóng zhèlǐ zǒu dào xuéxiào zhǐ xūyào shí fēnzhōng.
It only takes ten minutes to walk from here to school.

**请等我五分钟。**
Qǐng děng wǒ wǔ fēnzhōng.
Please wait five minutes for me.

**我每天运动三十分钟。**
Wǒ měitiān yùndòng sānshí fēnzhōng.
I exercise for thirty minutes every day.

**会议还有十分钟就结束了。**
Huìyì hái yǒu shí fēnzhōng jiù jiéshù le.
The meeting will end in ten more minutes.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 几分钟 | a few minutes |
| 十分钟 | ten minutes |
| 花了…分钟 | took ... minutes |
| 等了…分钟 | waited ... minutes |
| 半小时/三十分钟 | thirty minutes |

## Memory Hook

分 divides and 钟 means "clock" — splitting the clock face into sixty equal divisions gives you the minutes of duration.
