## 信用卡 (xìnyòngkǎ)

A plastic payment card issued by a bank that allows the holder to borrow funds to make purchases, repaid later.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Credit card, a bank-issued payment card on credit.

## Example Sentences

**我可以用信用卡付款吗？**
Wǒ kěyǐ yòng xìnyòngkǎ fùkuǎn ma?
Can I pay with a credit card?

**他的信用卡被盗用了。**
Tā de xìnyòngkǎ bèi dào yòng le.
His credit card was fraudulently used.

**使用信用卡要注意不要过度消费。**
Shǐyòng xìnyòngkǎ yào zhùyì bùyào guòdù xiāofèi.
When using a credit card, be careful not to overspend.

**我忘带信用卡了，只能用现金。**
Wǒ wàng dài xìnyòngkǎ le, zhǐ néng yòng xiànjīn.
I forgot to bring my credit card, so I can only use cash.

## Memory Hook

信用 means "credit/trust" and 卡 means "card" — a card backed by your trustworthiness is a credit card.
