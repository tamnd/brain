## 至少 (zhìshǎo)

至少 means "at least." It sets a minimum — the actual amount, frequency, or degree may be more, but it is guaranteed to be no less than what follows. It can modify numbers, time expressions, and frequency words.

## Meanings

1. **[adverb]** At least; no less than; at a minimum — sets a lower bound on quantity, frequency, or degree.

## Example Sentences

**你至少要喝八杯水。**
Nǐ zhìshǎo yào hē bā bēi shuǐ.
You should drink at least eight glasses of water.

**他至少需要三天才能完成这个任务。**
Tā zhìshǎo xūyào sān tiān cái néng wánchéng zhège rènwù.
He needs at least three days to complete this task.

**这件衣服至少值五百块钱。**
Zhè jiàn yīfu zhìshǎo zhí wǔbǎi kuài qián.
This piece of clothing is worth at least five hundred yuan.

**我每周至少锻炼三次。**
Wǒ měi zhōu zhìshǎo duànliàn sān cì.
I exercise at least three times a week.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 至少…以上 | at least…or more |
| 至少需要 | at least need / require |
| 至少有 | there are at least |
| 至少一次 | at least once |
| 至少可以 | at the very least, (one) can |

## Memory Hook

至 means "to reach / to the extreme of." 少 means "few / little." 至少 = "reaching the extreme of few" — the absolute minimum you can accept.
