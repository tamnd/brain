## 描写 (miáoxiě)

描写 means to describe or depict something in detail, especially through language or writing. It is commonly used when talking about literary descriptions or vivid portrayals of people, scenery, and feelings.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To describe; to depict; to portray something in detail through words.
2. **[noun]** Description; depiction (as a noun, referring to the act or style of depicting).

## Example Sentences

**这篇文章描写了美丽的春天。**
Zhè piān wénzhāng miáoxiě le měilì de chūntiān.
This article depicts a beautiful spring.

**他的描写非常生动。**
Tā de miáoxiě fēicháng shēngdòng.
His description is very vivid.

**请用几句话描写一下你的家乡。**
Qǐng yòng jǐ jù huà miáoxiě yīxià nǐ de jiāxiāng.
Please describe your hometown in a few sentences.

**这本小说对人物的描写很细致。**
Zhè běn xiǎoshuō duì rénwù de miáoxiě hěn xìzhì.
The novel's portrayal of characters is very detailed.

## Memory Hook

描 contains the hand radical and 苗 (seedling). 写 means to write. Think of carefully sketching every detail of a seedling in words, stroke by stroke.
