## 准时 (zhǔnshí)

准时 means doing something exactly at the scheduled or agreed time, neither early nor late.

## Meanings

1. **[adj/adv]** on time; punctual; at the scheduled time

## Example Sentences

**请大家明天八点准时到达。** Qǐng dàjiā míngtiān bā diǎn zhǔnshí dàodá. Please everyone arrive on time at eight o'clock tomorrow.

**他每天都准时上班，从不迟到。** Tā měitiān dōu zhǔnshí shàngbān, cóng bù chídào. He arrives at work on time every day and is never late.

**火车准时出发，一分钟都没有晚。** Huǒchē zhǔnshí chūfā, yī fēnzhōng dōu méiyǒu wǎn. The train departed on time, not even a minute late.

**准时是一种对他人的尊重。** Zhǔnshí shì yī zhǒng duì tārén de zūnzhòng. Being punctual is a form of respect for others.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 准时到达 | to arrive on time |
| 准时上班 | to go to work on time |
| 准时出发 | to depart on time |
| 不能准时 | unable to be on time |

## Memory Hook

准 means "accurate/precise" and 时 means "time." Being punctual is being precise about time.
