## 传统 (chuántǒng)

传统 refers to customs, values, and practices handed down from earlier generations, and describes things that are rooted in such heritage.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** tradition; heritage; custom passed down through generations
2. **[adj]** traditional; conventional

## Example Sentences

**春节是中国最重要的传统节日。** Chūnjié shì Zhōngguó zuì zhòngyào de chuántǒng jiérì. Spring Festival is the most important traditional holiday in China.

**他很尊重家族的传统。** Tā hěn zūnzhòng jiāzú de chuántǒng. He has great respect for his family's traditions.

**这家餐厅用传统方法做菜。** Zhè jiā cāntīng yòng chuántǒng fāngfǎ zuò cài. This restaurant cooks using traditional methods.

**传统和现代可以和谐地共存。** Chuántǒng hé xiàndài kěyǐ héxié de gòngcún. Tradition and modernity can coexist harmoniously.

## Memory Hook

传 means "to pass on" and 统 means "system/lineage." Tradition is a system of values passed down through generations.
