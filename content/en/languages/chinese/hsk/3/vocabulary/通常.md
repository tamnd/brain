## 通常 (tōngcháng)

通常 means "usually" or "normally." It describes what is typical, standard, or happens in general under ordinary circumstances. It is interchangeable with 一般 in many contexts but has a slightly more formal or written feel.

## Meanings

1. **[adverb]** Usually; normally; generally; as a rule — describes what typically or habitually happens.

### Compare 通常 vs 往往

| Word | Use |
|------|-----|
| 通常 | Neutral statement of what is normal or typical |
| 往往 | Implies a pattern based on observation, often with a mildly subjective or explanatory nuance |

## Example Sentences

**她通常七点起床，然后去跑步。**
Tā tōngcháng qī diǎn qǐchuáng, rán hòu qù pǎobù.
She usually gets up at seven and then goes running.

**这家商店通常九点开门。**
Zhè jiā shāngdiàn tōngcháng jiǔ diǎn kāimén.
This store normally opens at nine o'clock.

**通常情况下，我们先开会再做决定。**
Tōngcháng qíngkuàng xià, wǒmen xiān kāihuì zài zuò juédìng.
Under normal circumstances, we hold a meeting before making a decision.

**感冒通常一周左右就会好。**
Gǎnmào tōngcháng yī zhōu zuǒyòu jiù huì hǎo.
A cold usually gets better in about a week.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|

## Memory Hook

通 means "to pass through / general." 常 means "ordinary / usual." 通常 = "generally passing through the ordinary" — what flows through everyday life without exception.
