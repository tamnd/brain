## 成绩 (chéngjì)

成绩 refers to grades, scores, or the concrete results of effort. It is used for academic performance, work outcomes, and any tangible achievement that can be measured.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Grades; scores; academic performance.
2. **[noun]** Achievement; results (of work or effort).

## Example Sentences

**他的考试成绩很好。**
Tā de kǎoshì chéngjì hěn hǎo.
His exam scores are very good.

**努力学习，成绩自然会提高。**
Nǔlì xuéxí, chéngjì zìrán huì tígāo.
Study hard and your grades will naturally improve.

**这学期的成绩让父母非常高兴。**
Zhè xuéqī de chéngjì ràng fùmǔ fēicháng gāoxìng.
This semester's grades made his parents very happy.

**她的工作成绩受到了领导的表扬。**
Tā de gōngzuò chéngjì shòudào le lǐngdǎo de biǎoyáng.
Her work achievements were praised by the leadership.

## Memory Hook

成 (to accomplish / succeed) plus 绩 (results / merit — silk radical). The silk thread of accomplished merit — the woven record of everything you have achieved.
