## 标准 (biāozhǔn)

标准 means standard, criterion, or norm. As a noun it refers to a benchmark or rule used for evaluation. As an adjective it means standardized or proper. 标准答案 is a model answer, and 标准普通话 refers to standard Mandarin pronunciation.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Standard; criterion; norm; benchmark.
2. **[adjective]** Standard; standardized; conforming to a norm.

## Example Sentences

**她的普通话发音非常标准。**
Tā de Pǔtōnghuà fāyīn fēicháng biāozhǔn.
Her Mandarin pronunciation is very standard.

**这道题没有标准答案。**
Zhè dào tí méiyǒu biāozhǔn dá'àn.
This question has no standard answer.

**我们要按照统一标准来评分。**
Wǒmen yào ànzhào tǒngyī biāozhǔn lái píngfēn.
We need to score according to a unified standard.

**生活水平的标准因人而异。**
Shēnghuó shuǐpíng de biāozhǔn yīn rén ér yì.
The standard for living conditions varies from person to person.

## Memory Hook

标 (mark/sign) plus 准 (accurate/precise). A standard is the precise mark that everyone aims to hit. It is the target on the wall.
