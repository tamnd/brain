## 跑回去 (pǎo huíqu)

跑回去 is a verb with a compound directional complement. 跑 (to run) is the base verb; 回去 (go back, away from the speaker) is the directional complement indicating a return movement directed away from the current location. Compare 跑回来 (run back here, toward the speaker).

## Structure

跑 (run) + 回去 (directional complement: go back away from speaker) = run back (away).

Other base verbs with 回去: 走回去 (walk back), 骑回去 (ride back), 飞回去 (fly back).

## Example Sentences

**他忘记带书包，又跑回去拿了。**
Tā wàngjì dài shūbāo, yòu pǎo huíqu ná le.
He forgot his backpack and ran back to get it.

**快跑回去！外面要下雨了。**
Kuài pǎo huíqu! Wàimiàn yào xià yǔ le.
Run back quickly! It is about to rain.

**她跑回去找她的手机。**
Tā pǎo huíqu zhǎo tā de shǒujī.
She ran back to look for her phone.

**比赛结束后，运动员们都跑回去休息了。**
Bǐsài jiéshù hòu, yùndòngyuánmen dōu pǎo huíqu xiūxi le.
After the competition ended, the athletes all ran back to rest.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 跑回去拿 | run back to get (something) |
| 赶紧跑回去 | hurry and run back |
| 跑回来 | run back here (toward speaker) |
| 走回去 | walk back |
| 骑回去 | ride back |

## Memory Hook

跑 (run — foot radical 足 showing movement on foot) + 回 (return / circle back) + 去 (go away). Picture someone sprinting back down the road they came from — returning at speed, moving away from you.
