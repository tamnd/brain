## 精彩 (jīngcǎi)

精彩 describes something that is wonderful, brilliant, or spectacular — typically a performance, speech, film, or activity that stands out for its excellence and engages the audience fully.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** Wonderful; brilliant; splendid; exciting; superb.

## Example Sentences

**这部电影非常精彩，值得一看。**
Zhè bù diànyǐng fēicháng jīngcǎi, zhídé yī kàn.
This film is wonderful and worth watching.

**他的演讲很精彩，大家都鼓掌了。**
Tā de yǎnjiǎng hěn jīngcǎi, dàjiā dōu gǔzhǎng le.
His speech was brilliant and everyone applauded.

**今天的表演真精彩！**
Jīntiān de biǎoyǎn zhēn jīngcǎi!
Today's performance was truly wonderful!

**比赛的最后一刻非常精彩，大家都很兴奋。**
Bǐsài de zuìhòu yīkè fēicháng jīngcǎi, dàjiā dōu hěn xīngfèn.
The final moment of the competition was spectacular and everyone was very excited.

## Memory Hook

精 (essence / refined — the finest rice grain 米 refined down) plus 彩 (colorful / splendid). The colorful essence of something — the most refined, dazzling part on display.
