## 大约 (dàyuē)

大约 is an adverb meaning "approximately" or "about," used before numbers or time expressions to indicate that the value is an estimate, not a precise measurement.

## Meanings

1. **[adverb]** approximately; about; roughly; around (placed before numbers, quantities, or time expressions to indicate an approximation)

## Usage Note

大约 is placed before the number or quantity it modifies. It can also appear before a verb to express a general estimate of likelihood or manner, similar to "roughly speaking."

Compare with 大概 (dàgài), which is very similar and often interchangeable. The difference is subtle:
- 大约 is more commonly used for numerical estimates (quantities, time, distance)
- 大概 can also express probability or uncertainty about a situation ("probably," "I suppose")

## Example Sentences

**从这里到车站大约要走二十分钟。** Cóng zhèlǐ dào chēzhàn dàyuē yào zǒu èrshí fēnzhōng. It takes approximately twenty minutes to walk from here to the station.

**这本书大约有三百页。** Zhè běn shū dàyuē yǒu sānbǎi yè. This book has about three hundred pages.

**他们大约下午三点到达。** Tāmen dàyuē xiàwǔ sān diǎn dàodá. They will arrive at around three in the afternoon.

**这个项目大约需要三个月完成。** Zhège xiàngmù dàyuē xūyào sān gè yuè wánchéng. This project will take approximately three months to complete.

## Memory Hook

大 means "big / broad" and 约 means "agreement / approximately." A "broad agreement" with a number means you are not pinning it down exactly; you are giving a rough, big-picture figure, so 大约 means "approximately."
