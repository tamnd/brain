## 危险 (wēixiǎn)

危险 means dangerous or risky. As an adjective it describes a person, place, or action that poses a threat. As a noun it refers to danger or risk itself.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** Dangerous; risky; hazardous.
2. **[noun]** Danger; risk; peril.

## Example Sentences

**这条路很危险，晚上不要一个人走。**
Zhè tiáo lù hěn wēixiǎn, wǎnshang bú yào yīgè rén zǒu.
This road is very dangerous; don't walk it alone at night.

**游泳时不注意安全很危险。**
Yóuyǒng shí bù zhùyì ānquán hěn wēixiǎn.
Not paying attention to safety while swimming is very dangerous.

**他的病情处于危险之中。**
Tā de bìngqíng chǔyú wēixiǎn zhī zhōng.
His condition is in danger.

**不要把孩子留在车里，这很危险。**
Bú yào bǎ háizi liú zài chē lǐ, zhè hěn wēixiǎn.
Don't leave a child in the car; it is very dangerous.

## Memory Hook

危 shows a person on a cliff edge, about to fall. 险 has a hill radical 阝 — steep terrain. A person teetering on steep terrain: danger.
