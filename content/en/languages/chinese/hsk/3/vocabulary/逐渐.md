## 逐渐 (zhújiàn)

逐渐 is an adverb describing a process that happens slowly and incrementally over time, without sudden jumps or breaks.

## Meanings

1. **[adverb]** gradually; little by little; progressively (placed before a verb or adjective to indicate slow, steady change)

## Usage Note

逐渐 is placed directly before the verb or adjective it modifies. It emphasizes the gradual nature of a change and often appears with verbs of change such as 变 (biàn, to change), 增加 (zēngjiā, to increase), 减少 (jiǎnshǎo, to decrease), 了解 (liǎojiě, to understand), and 适应 (shìyìng, to adapt).

Compare with 慢慢 (mànmàn), which means "slowly" and focuses on pace, while 逐渐 focuses on the incremental, step-by-step nature of change.

## Example Sentences

**他的中文水平逐渐提高了。** Tā de Zhōngwén shuǐpíng zhújiàn tígāo le. His Chinese level has gradually improved.

**天气逐渐变暖，春天来了。** Tiānqì zhújiàn biàn nuǎn, chūntiān lái le. The weather is gradually getting warmer; spring has arrived.

**我逐渐了解了他的性格。** Wǒ zhújiàn liǎojiě le tā de xìnggé. I gradually came to understand his personality.

**城市里的人口逐渐增加。** Chéngshì lǐ de rénkǒu zhújiàn zēngjiā. The population in the city is gradually increasing.

## Memory Hook

逐 means "to pursue / to follow one by one" and 渐 means "gradually." Picture someone chasing a goal step by step, following each small increment, moving forward gradually.
