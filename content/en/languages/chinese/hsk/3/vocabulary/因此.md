## 因此 (yīncǐ)

因此 means therefore or as a result. It introduces the logical consequence of a stated cause, and is slightly more formal than 所以 (suǒyǐ). It can begin a sentence on its own.

## Meanings

1. **[conjunction]** Therefore; as a result; for this reason; consequently.

## Example Sentences

**他非常努力，因此成绩很好。**
Tā fēicháng nǔlì, yīncǐ chéngjì hěn hǎo.
He worked very hard; therefore his grades are very good.

**外面下雨了，因此我们取消了活动。**
Wàimiàn xià yǔ le, yīncǐ wǒmen qǔxiāo le huódòng.
It was raining outside; as a result, we cancelled the activity.

**她生病了，因此没能参加比赛。**
Tā shēngbìng le, yīncǐ méi néng cānjiā bǐsài.
She was sick; therefore she couldn't participate in the competition.

**因此，我们需要改变计划。**
Yīncǐ, wǒmen xūyào gǎibiàn jìhuà.
Therefore, we need to change the plan.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 因此…所以… | for this reason…so… |
| 正因此 | precisely for this reason |
| 因此认为 | therefore believe |
| 因此决定 | therefore decide |
| 由此可见 | from this it can be seen |

## Memory Hook

因 (cause) plus 此 (this). "This cause" leads to the result — pointing your finger at the reason and presenting the outcome.
