## 热闹 (rènao)

热闹 means lively, bustling, or full of activity and noise in a cheerful way. It describes a place or event that is busy, noisy, and full of people in a positive, festive sense. 热 means hot or enthusiastic, and 闹 means noisy or to make a commotion.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** Lively; bustling; full of cheerful noise and activity.
2. **[noun]** Excitement; lively scene; a bustling spectacle.

## Example Sentences

**春节期间，街上非常热闹。**
Chūnjié qījiān, jiē shàng fēicháng rènao.
During Spring Festival, the streets are extremely lively.

**这家夜市又热闹又好玩。**
Zhè jiā yèshì yòu rènao yòu hǎowán.
This night market is both lively and fun.

**他喜欢热闹，不喜欢一个人待着。**
Tā xǐhuān rènao, bù xǐhuān yī gè rén dāizhe.
He likes lively environments and does not like being alone.

**大家都去看热闹了。**
Dàjiā dōu qù kàn rènao le.
Everyone went to watch the exciting scene.

## Memory Hook

热 (hot) plus 闹 (noisy). A lively place is hot with energy and loud with voices. Picture a crowded night market where the heat of bodies and the noise of vendors make everything exciting.
