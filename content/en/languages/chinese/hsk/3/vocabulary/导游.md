## 导游 (dǎoyóu)

导游 means tour guide. It refers to a person whose job is to lead tourists and explain sites of interest. It can also function as a verb meaning to guide tourists, though the noun use is far more common at this level.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Tour guide; a person who leads and informs tourists.

## Example Sentences

**我们的导游说话很有趣。**
Wǒmen de dǎoyóu shuōhuà hěn yǒuqù.
Our tour guide is very interesting to listen to.

**她的工作是导游。**
Tā de gōngzuò shì dǎoyóu.
Her job is to be a tour guide.

**导游带我们参观了故宫。**
Dǎoyóu dài wǒmen cānguān le Gùgōng.
The tour guide took us to visit the Forbidden City.

**这位导游会说英语和中文。**
Zhè wèi dǎoyóu huì shuō Yīngyǔ hé Zhōngwén.
This tour guide can speak English and Chinese.

## Memory Hook

导 means to lead or guide and 游 means to travel or tour. A 导游 is literally a "lead-travel" person, someone whose job is to guide you through your journey.
