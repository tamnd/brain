## 参考 (cānkǎo)

参考 means to consult or refer to something for information or guidance, and can also be used as a noun meaning reference material.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to refer to; to consult; to take as reference
2. **[noun]** reference; consultation

## Example Sentences

**写论文时要参考很多资料。** Xiě lùnwén shí yào cānkǎo hěn duō zīliào. When writing a paper, you need to refer to many sources.

**这只是供参考，不一定完全正确。** Zhè zhǐshì gōng cānkǎo, bù yīdìng wánquán zhèngquè. This is just for reference; it may not be entirely accurate.

**你可以参考这本书来完成作业。** Nǐ kěyǐ cānkǎo zhè běn shū lái wánchéng zuòyè. You can refer to this book to complete your homework.

**老师给我们提供了一些参考资料。** Lǎoshī gěi wǒmen tígōng le yīxiē cānkǎo zīliào. The teacher provided us with some reference materials.

## Memory Hook

参 (to participate/join) + 考 (to examine): you join in the examination of existing knowledge before forming your own view.
