## 互联网 (hùliánwǎng)

互联网 is the standard Chinese term for the internet, emphasizing its nature as an interconnected global network.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** internet; the web — the worldwide network connecting computers and people across the globe

## Example Sentences

**我每天都用互联网工作。** Wǒ měi tiān dōu yòng hùliánwǎng gōngzuò. I use the internet every day for work.

**互联网改变了我们的生活。** Hùliánwǎng gǎibiàn le wǒmen de shēnghuó. The internet has changed our lives.

**没有互联网，我什么都做不了。** Méiyǒu hùliánwǎng, wǒ shénme dōu zuò bu liǎo. Without the internet, I cannot do anything.

**互联网上有很多有用的信息。** Hùliánwǎng shàng yǒu hěn duō yǒuyòng de xìnxī. There is a lot of useful information on the internet.

## Memory Hook

互 (mutual) plus 联 (connect) plus 网 (net) — a net that mutually connects everything: the 互联网.
