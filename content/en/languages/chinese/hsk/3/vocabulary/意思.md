## 意思 (yìsi)

意思 is an extremely common word used to ask about or explain what something means, or what someone intends.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** meaning; sense (of a word or expression)
2. **[noun]** idea; intention; what someone means
3. **[adjective]** interesting (有意思 yǒu yìsi)

## Example Sentences

**这个词是什么意思？** Zhège cí shì shénme yìsi? What is the meaning of this word?

**你的意思是说我错了吗？** Nǐ de yìsi shì shuō wǒ cuò le ma? Do you mean to say that I was wrong?

**这部电影很有意思。** Zhè bù diànyǐng hěn yǒu yìsi. This movie is very interesting.

**他那句话什么意思，我没听懂。** Tā nà jù huà shénme yìsi, wǒ méi tīng dǒng. I did not understand what he meant by that sentence.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 什么意思 | what does it mean |

| 没意思 | boring; pointless |

## Memory Hook

意 means "mind/intent" and 思 means "thought" — the 意思 is the thought in someone's mind, the meaning they intend.
