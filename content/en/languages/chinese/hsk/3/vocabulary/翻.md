## 翻 (fān)

翻 means to turn something over, flip through pages, or cross over a barrier such as a wall or mountain.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to turn over; to flip; to browse through; to cross over

## Example Sentences

**他翻了翻书，找到了那一页。** Tā fān le fān shū, zhǎodào le nà yī yè. He flipped through the book and found that page.

**请翻到第三十页。** Qǐng fān dào dì sānshí yè. Please turn to page thirty.

**孩子把桌上的东西都翻了一遍。** Háizi bǎ zhuō shàng de dōngxi dōu fān le yī biàn. The child rummaged through everything on the table.

**翻过这座山，前面就是我们的目的地。** Fān guò zhè zuò shān, qiánmiàn jiùshì wǒmen de mùdìdì. After crossing this mountain, our destination is just ahead.

## Memory Hook

Picture the wings (羽) of a bird flipping over as it turns in flight: that flipping motion is 翻.
