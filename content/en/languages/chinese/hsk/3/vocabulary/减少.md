## 减少 (jiǎnshǎo)

To cause something to become smaller in number, amount, or degree; or for something to become less on its own.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To reduce, to decrease, to lessen (transitive).
2. **[verb]** To decline, to drop, to diminish (intransitive).

## Example Sentences

**我们要减少浪费，保护环境。**
Wǒmen yào jiǎnshǎo làngfèi, bǎohù huánjìng.
We should reduce waste and protect the environment.

**今年公司的成本减少了很多。**
Jīnnián gōngsī de chéngběn jiǎnshǎo le hěn duō.
This year the company's costs have decreased a lot.

**他每天减少糖的摄入量。**
Tā měitiān jiǎnshǎo táng de shèrù liàng.
He reduces his daily sugar intake every day.

**空气污染的减少需要大家共同努力。**
Kōngqì wūrǎn de jiǎnshǎo xūyào dàjiā gòngtóng nǔlì.
Reducing air pollution requires everyone to work together.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 减少浪费 | reduce waste |
| 减少污染 | reduce pollution |
| 大幅减少 | drastically reduce |
| 逐渐减少 | gradually decrease |
| 减少数量 | reduce quantity |

## Memory Hook

减 means "to subtract/reduce" and 少 means "few/less" — subtracting until there is less and less.
