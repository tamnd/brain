## 农村 (nóngcūn)

The rural area or countryside, as opposed to the city (城市 chéngshì), where farming and agriculture are the main activities.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Countryside, rural area, village area.
2. **[noun]** The rural regions of a country (as a societal concept).

## Example Sentences

**他出生在农村，后来搬到了城市。**
Tā chūshēng zài nóngcūn, hòulái bāndào le chéngshì.
He was born in the countryside and later moved to the city.

**农村的空气比城市新鲜。**
Nóngcūn de kōngqì bǐ chéngshì xīnxiān.
The air in the countryside is fresher than in the city.

**很多年轻人从农村来到城市工作。**
Hěn duō niánqīngrén cóng nóngcūn lái dào chéngshì gōngzuò.
Many young people come from the countryside to work in cities.

**政府正在帮助改善农村的生活条件。**
Zhèngfǔ zhèngzài bāngzhù gǎishàn nóngcūn de shēnghuó tiáojiàn.
The government is helping to improve living conditions in rural areas.

## Memory Hook

农 means "farming/agriculture" and 村 means "village" — a farming village is the countryside.
