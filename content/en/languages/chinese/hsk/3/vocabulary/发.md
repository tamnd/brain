## 发 (fā)

发 is a high-frequency multi-meaning verb. Its core idea is sending out or giving off something, whether that is a message, heat, light, or growth. Note: as fà it means hair, but at HSK 3 the fā reading is primary.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To send; to dispatch (a message, email, parcel).
2. **[verb]** To emit; to give off (heat, light, sound).
3. **[verb]** To issue; to hand out (documents, certificates).

## Example Sentences

**我给你发一条消息。**
Wǒ gěi nǐ fā yī tiáo xiāoxi.
I will send you a message.

**太阳发出强烈的光。**
Tàiyáng fāchū qiángliè de guāng.
The sun emits intense light.

**学校给每个学生发了一本书。**
Xuéxiào gěi měi gè xuéshēng fā le yī běn shū.
The school issued each student a book.

**他发烧了，要休息。**
Tā fā shāo le, yào xiūxi.
He has a fever and needs to rest.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 发消息 | to send a message |
| 发烧 | to have a fever (emit + burn) |
| 发展 | to develop, development |
| 发现 | to discover |
| 发出 | to emit, to send out |

## Memory Hook

Picture a post office worker (发) dispatching letters in every direction: sending messages, issuing stamps, and watching heat pour from the radiator. Everything goes outward from one source.
