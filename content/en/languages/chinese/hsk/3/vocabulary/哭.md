## 哭 (kū)

哭 means to cry or weep, expressing sadness, pain, or strong emotion through tears and often sound.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to cry; to weep; to shed tears from emotion or pain

## Example Sentences

**她看完那部电影就哭了。** Tā kàn wán nà bù diànyǐng jiù kū le. She cried after finishing that movie.

**小孩子摔倒了，开始大声哭。** Xiǎo háizi shuāi dǎo le, kāishǐ dàshēng kū. The little child fell down and started crying loudly.

**别哭了，一切都会好的。** Bié kū le, yīqiē dōu huì hǎo de. Stop crying; everything will be fine.

**他听到那个消息，忍不住哭了起来。** Tā tīng dào nà gè xiāoxi, rěn bu zhù kū le qǐlái. When he heard that news, he could not help but burst into tears.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |

| 哭鼻子 (kū bízi) | to cry (colloquial, child's speech) |
| 忍不住哭 (rěn bu zhù kū) | cannot help but cry |

## Memory Hook

The character 哭 shows two 口 (mouths) on top, which look like two wide-open crying eyes, with a 犬 (dog) underneath — picture a dog howling with its mouth wide open: 哭.
