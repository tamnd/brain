## 变 (biàn)

变 means to change or become something different. It describes a transformation in state, appearance, or quality. It is often followed by an adjective describing the new state.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To change; to become; to transform.

## Example Sentences

**天气突然变冷了。**
Tiānqì tūrán biàn lěng le.
The weather suddenly became cold.

**他变了很多，我都认不出来了。**
Tā biàn le hěn duō, wǒ dōu rèn bù chūlái le.
He has changed so much I almost didn't recognize him.

**努力学习，才能变优秀。**
Nǔlì xuéxí, cái néng biàn yōuxiù.
Only by studying hard can you become excellent.

**这里的环境变好了。**
Zhèlǐ de huánjìng biàn hǎo le.
The environment here has improved (become better).

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 变化 | change (noun/verb) |
| 变好 | become better |
| 变坏 | become worse |
| 发生变化 | undergo a change |
| 变得 | become (followed by adj) |

## Memory Hook

变 has 言 (speech) over 攴 (to tap/beat): a word taps you and you transform. Something said can change everything.
