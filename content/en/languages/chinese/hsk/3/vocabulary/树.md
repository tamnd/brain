## 树 (shù)

树 means tree. It is one of the most basic nature words in Chinese. It also appears in compounds related to planting or establishing something, reflecting the idea that a tree is planted and grows over time.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Tree; a tall woody plant.
2. **[verb]** To plant; to establish; to set up (in formal or written use).

## Example Sentences

**公园里有很多大树。**
Gōngyuán lǐ yǒu hěn duō dà shù.
There are many large trees in the park.

**这棵树已经长了一百年了。**
Zhè kē shù yǐjīng zhǎng le yī bǎi nián le.
This tree has been growing for a hundred years.

**春天来了，树上开满了花。**
Chūntiān lái le, shù shàng kāi mǎn le huā.
Spring has arrived; the trees are covered with blossoms.

**我们在学校门口种了一棵树。**
Wǒmen zài xuéxiào ménkǒu zhòng le yī kē shù.
We planted a tree at the school entrance.

## Memory Hook

The character 树 has 木 (wood) inside it. A tree is the original source of wood. Think of 木 growing bigger and taller, becoming the full 树 character.
