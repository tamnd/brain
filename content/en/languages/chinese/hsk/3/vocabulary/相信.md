## 相信 (xiāngxìn)

相信 means to believe or to trust. It expresses confidence in the truth of a statement or faith in a person or outcome.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To believe; to trust; to have faith in.

## Example Sentences

**我相信你说的话。**
Wǒ xiāngxìn nǐ shuō de huà.
I believe what you said.

**你相信他能做到吗？**
Nǐ xiāngxìn tā néng zuò dào ma?
Do you believe he can do it?

**我相信努力一定会有回报。**
Wǒ xiāngxìn nǔlì yīdìng huì yǒu huíbào.
I believe hard work will definitely pay off.

**她不相信这是真的。**
Tā bù xiāngxìn zhè shì zhēn de.
She doesn't believe this is true.

## Memory Hook

相 means mutual or together, and 信 means trust or faith. 相信 is a mutual meeting of minds where your beliefs align with another's words. When you 相信, you and the truth are on the same side.
