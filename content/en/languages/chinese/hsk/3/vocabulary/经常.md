## 经常 (jīngcháng)

经常 means often or frequently. It describes actions that happen on a regular, habitual basis. It is placed before the verb it modifies.

## Meanings

1. **[adverb]** Often; frequently; regularly; habitually.

## Example Sentences

**我经常去图书馆看书。**
Wǒ jīngcháng qù túshūguǎn kàn shū.
I often go to the library to read.

**他经常帮助同学，大家都很喜欢他。**
Tā jīngcháng bāngzhù tóngxué, dàjiā dōu hěn xǐhuān tā.
He often helps classmates; everyone likes him very much.

**你经常锻炼吗？**
Nǐ jīngcháng duànliàn ma?
Do you exercise often?

**她经常说汉语，所以进步很快。**
Tā jīngcháng shuō Hànyǔ, suǒyǐ jìnbù hěn kuài.
She speaks Chinese often, so she progresses quickly.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |

## Memory Hook

经 (silk threads running through — a continuing process) plus 常 (ordinary / usual). A continuing, ordinary thing — a process so regular it has become part of the fabric of life.
