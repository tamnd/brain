## 才 (cái)

才 is a restrictive adverb with several closely related uses: it can mark that something happens later than expected ("not until"), stress a small or barely sufficient amount ("only/just"), or imply that a condition must first be met before a result follows. It always precedes the verb.

## Meanings

1. **[adverb]** Only then; not until — the action happens later or with more difficulty than expected.
2. **[adverb]** Just; only; merely — stressing a small quantity or extent.
3. **[adverb]** Only if/when (in conditional chains): [condition] + 才 + [result].

### Pattern

| Use | Structure | Example |
|-----|-----------|---------|
| Late/reluctant action | time phrase + 才 + V | 他十点才来。 He didn't come until ten. |
| Small quantity | 才 + number | 我才吃了一口。 I only ate one bite. |
| Conditional | [only if…] + 才 + V | 努力了才能成功。 Only by working hard can you succeed. |

## Example Sentences

**他昨天晚上十二点才睡觉。**
Tā zuótiān wǎnshàng shí'èr diǎn cái shuìjiào.
He didn't go to sleep until midnight last night.

**我才认识她三天，还不太了解她。**
Wǒ cái rènshi tā sān tiān, hái bú tài liǎojiě tā.
I have only known her for three days and don't understand her well yet.

**练习了很久，他才学会这首曲子。**
Liànxí le hěn jiǔ, tā cái xuéhuì zhè shǒu qǔzi.
Only after practicing for a long time did he finally learn this piece.

**有了健康，才能享受生活。**
Yǒu le jiànkāng, cái néng xiǎngshòu shēnghuó.
Only when you have health can you enjoy life.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 这才 | only now; just now finally |
| 才刚 | only just (did something) |
| 才能 | can only then (result clause) |
| 才知道 | only then did (I) know |
| 才明白 | only then did (I) understand |

## Memory Hook

才 also means "talent." Imagine someone with a small sliver of talent — just barely enough — who arrives late to the competition. Late arrival and bare sufficiency are the core feelings of 才.
