## 满足 (mǎnzú)

满足 means to satisfy or to be satisfied. As a verb it means to meet or fulfill a need, demand, or requirement. As an adjective it describes the feeling of contentment. 满 means full, and 足 means foot/sufficient. Together they convey being fully content.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To satisfy; to fulfill; to meet a need or requirement.
2. **[adjective]** Satisfied; content; feeling that enough has been provided.

## Example Sentences

**这份工作满足了我对薪水的要求。**
Zhè fèn gōngzuò mǎnzú le wǒ duì xīnshuǐ de yāoqiú.
This job meets my salary requirements.

**他感到很满足，因为家人都很健康。**
Tā gǎndào hěn mǎnzú, yīnwèi jiārén dōu hěn jiànkāng.
He feels very satisfied because his family is all healthy.

**我们要努力满足顾客的需求。**
Wǒmen yào nǔlì mǎnzú gùkè de xūqiú.
We must work hard to satisfy customers' needs.

**孩子的愿望终于满足了。**
Háizi de yuànwàng zhōngyú mǎnzú le.
The child's wish was finally fulfilled.

## Memory Hook

满 (full) plus 足 (sufficient/foot). When you are 满足, you are so full and sufficient that you could stand steady on both feet without wanting anything more.
