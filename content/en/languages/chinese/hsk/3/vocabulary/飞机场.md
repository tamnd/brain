## 飞机场 (fēijīchǎng)

飞机场 is the full form for "airport." It is also commonly shortened to 机场 (jīchǎng) in everyday speech. 飞机 means "airplane" and 场 means "field / ground." Together they describe a field where airplanes operate. Both forms are correct and widely used.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Airport; airfield; the facility for aircraft departure and arrival.

## Example Sentences

**我明天早上要去飞机场接朋友。**
Wǒ míngtiān zǎoshang yào qù fēijīchǎng jiē péngyǒu.
I need to go to the airport to pick up a friend tomorrow morning.

**飞机场离市中心很远，要坐一个小时的地铁。**
Fēijīchǎng lí shì zhōngxīn hěn yuǎn, yào zuò yī gè xiǎoshí de dìtiě.
The airport is far from the city center; it takes an hour by subway.

**他在飞机场等了两个小时，飞机才到。**
Tā zài fēijīchǎng děng le liǎng gè xiǎoshí, fēijī cái dào.
He waited at the airport for two hours before the plane arrived.

**请问去飞机场怎么走？**
Qǐngwèn qù fēijīchǎng zěnme zǒu?
Excuse me, how do I get to the airport?

## Memory Hook

飞 (to fly) plus 机 (machine) plus 场 (field / ground). An airport is literally the ground for flying machines — picture a vast field where planes taxi, take off, and land.
