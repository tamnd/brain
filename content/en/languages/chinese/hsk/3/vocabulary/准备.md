## 准备 (zhǔnbèi)

准备 means to prepare or get ready. It is used before a noun (prepare something) or before a verb phrase (prepare to do something / plan to do something).

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To prepare; to get ready; to make preparations.
2. **[verb]** To plan to; to intend to (followed by a verb).

## Example Sentences

**我在准备考试。**
Wǒ zài zhǔnbèi kǎoshì.
I am preparing for the exam.

**你准备好了吗？**
Nǐ zhǔnbèi hǎo le ma?
Are you ready?

**妈妈准备做晚饭了。**
Māma zhǔnbèi zuò wǎnfàn le.
Mom is about to make dinner.

**我们准备明天出发。**
Wǒmen zhǔnbèi míngtiān chūfā.
We plan to set off tomorrow.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|

## Memory Hook

准 (accurate/standard) plus 备 (equipped). To prepare is to get your equipment standardized and ready — everything in its place, nothing left to chance.
