## 加油 (jiāyóu)

Literally "add oil/fuel," used as an energetic cheer meaning "come on," "go for it," or "keep it up." Also used literally when refueling a vehicle.

## Meanings

1. **[interjection]** Come on! Go for it! Keep it up! (encouragement cheer).
2. **[verb]** To add fuel, to refuel (a vehicle).

## Example Sentences

**比赛的时候，观众大声喊：加油！**
Bǐsài de shíhou, guānzhòng dàshēng hǎn: jiāyóu!
During the competition, the audience shouted loudly: Come on!

**你要参加考试了，加油！**
Nǐ yào cānjiā kǎoshì le, jiāyóu!
You're about to take the exam — go for it!

**我去加油站加油。**
Wǒ qù jiāyóu zhàn jiāyóu.
I'm going to the gas station to refuel.

**朋友们为她加油，让她更有信心了。**
Péngyǒumen wèi tā jiāyóu, ràng tā gèng yǒu xìnxīn le.
Friends cheered her on, giving her more confidence.

## Memory Hook

A car runs faster when you add fuel — add oil (加油) to a person and they run faster too, powered by your encouragement.
