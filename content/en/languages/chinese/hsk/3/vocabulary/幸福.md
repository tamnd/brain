## 幸福 (xìngfú)

幸福 expresses a deep, lasting sense of well-being and contentment, going beyond momentary happiness to describe a fulfilled life.

## Meanings

1. **[noun/adj]** happiness; a happy and blessed state; deep contentment; feeling fortunate and fulfilled

## Example Sentences

**和家人在一起，我感到很幸福。** Hé jiārén zài yīqǐ, wǒ gǎndào hěn xìngfú. Being together with family, I feel very happy and blessed.

**幸福不是靠钱买来的。** Xìngfú bù shì kào qián mǎi lái de. Happiness cannot be bought with money.

**他们结婚多年，依然很幸福。** Tāmen jiéhūn duō nián, yīrán hěn xìngfú. They have been married for many years and are still very happy.

**祝你们婚姻幸福，白头到老。** Zhù nǐmen hūnyīn xìngfú, báitóu dào lǎo. Wishing you a happy marriage and a life together until old age.

## Memory Hook

幸 means "lucky/fortunate" and 福 means "blessing/good fortune." True happiness is when luck and blessings fill your life at once.
