## 爬 (pá)

爬 means to climb or to crawl. It describes the action of moving upward using hands and feet, or moving on all fours along a surface.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To climb; to scale (a mountain, wall, tree).
2. **[verb]** To crawl; to move on hands and knees.

## Example Sentences

**他们爬到了山顶。**
Tāmen pá dào le shāndǐng.
They climbed to the top of the mountain.

**小孩子在地上爬来爬去。**
Xiǎo háizi zài dìshang pá lái pá qù.
The little child is crawling back and forth on the ground.

**我想爬那棵大树。**
Wǒ xiǎng pá nà kē dà shù.
I want to climb that big tree.

**我们爬山爬了三个小时。**
Wǒmen páshān pá le sān gè xiǎoshí.
We hiked for three hours.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 爬山 | to hike / climb a mountain |
| 爬上去 | to climb up |
| 爬下来 | to climb down |
| 爬楼梯 | to climb stairs |
| 爬来爬去 | to crawl around |

## Memory Hook

The character 爬 contains 爪 (claw) on top, suggesting hands gripping and pulling upward. Think of a cat using its claws to climb a tree.
