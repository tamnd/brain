## 担心 (dānxīn)

担心 means to worry or feel anxious about someone or something.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to worry; to be concerned about
2. **[adjective]** worried; anxious

## Example Sentences

**妈妈总是担心我的健康。** Māma zǒngshì dānxīn wǒ de jiànkāng. My mom always worries about my health.

**不用担心，一切都会好起来的。** Búyòng dānxīn, yīqiē dōu huì hǎo qǐlái de. Don't worry; everything will be fine.

**他担心考试会不及格。** Tā dānxīn kǎoshì huì bù jígé. He is worried that he will fail the exam.

**你看起来很担心，发生什么事了？** Nǐ kàn qǐlái hěn dānxīn, fāshēng shénme shì le? You look worried; what happened?

## Memory Hook

担 (to carry/bear) + 心 (heart): you are carrying your heart with worry, burdened by concern.
