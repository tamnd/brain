## 要求 (yāoqiú)

要求 means to require, demand, or request. As a noun it refers to a requirement or standard that must be met. It carries more force than 请求 (qǐngqiú — to request politely).

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To require; to demand; to ask for; to expect.
2. **[noun]** A requirement; a demand; a standard.

## Example Sentences

**老师要求我们每天练习写字。**
Lǎoshī yāoqiú wǒmen měitiān liànxí xiězì.
The teacher requires us to practice writing characters every day.

**你有什么要求？**
Nǐ yǒu shénme yāoqiú?
What requirements do you have?

**他的要求太高了。**
Tā de yāoqiú tài gāo le.
His requirements are too high.

**这份工作的要求是会说英文。**
Zhè fèn gōngzuò de yāoqiú shì huì shuō Yīngwén.
The requirement for this job is being able to speak English.

## Memory Hook

要 means "want / need" and 求 means "seek / beg." When you both want and seek something from someone, you are demanding it — that is a requirement.
