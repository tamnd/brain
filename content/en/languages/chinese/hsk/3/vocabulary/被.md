## 被 (bèi)

被 is the standard passive marker in Mandarin. It introduces the agent (the doer) and signals that the subject receives the action, often one with an unpleasant or unwanted result.

## Meanings

1. **[preposition]** By (introduces the agent in a passive construction).
2. **[verb]** To suffer; to be subjected to.

## Example Sentences

**我的自行车被偷了。**
Wǒ de zìxíngchē bèi tōu le.
My bicycle was stolen.

**这个问题被解决了。**
Zhège wèntí bèi jiějué le.
This problem has been solved.

**他被老师批评了。**
Tā bèi lǎoshī pīpíng le.
He was criticized by the teacher.

**信被寄出去了。**
Xìn bèi jìchūqù le.
The letter was sent out.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 被骗 | to be deceived |
| 被批评 | to be criticized |
| 被选上 | to be selected |
| 被允许 | to be permitted |
| 被发现 | to be discovered |

## Memory Hook

被 contains 衣 (clothing) on the right: imagine your coat being taken off your back without your consent, a classic passive experience.
