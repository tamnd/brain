## 教育 (jiàoyù)

教育 refers to the process of education, including teaching, learning, and the cultivation of knowledge and character.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** education; schooling; upbringing
2. **[verb]** to educate; to teach; to instruct

## Example Sentences

**教育是国家发展的基础。** Jiàoyù shì guójiā fāzhǎn de jīchǔ. Education is the foundation of a country's development.

**他的父母非常重视孩子的教育。** Tā de fùmǔ fēicháng zhòngshì háizi de jiàoyù. His parents attach great importance to their child's education.

**这所学校的教育质量很高。** Zhè suǒ xuéxiào de jiàoyù zhìliàng hěn gāo. The quality of education at this school is very high.

**老师教育学生要诚实守信。** Lǎoshī jiàoyù xuéshēng yào chéngshí shǒuxìn. The teacher educates students to be honest and trustworthy.

## Memory Hook

教 means "to teach" and 育 means "to raise/nurture" — education is the combination of teaching and nurturing a person to grow.
