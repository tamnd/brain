## 影响 (yǐngxiǎng)

影响 means to influence or affect. It describes one thing having an effect on another, positively or negatively. As a noun it refers to the influence or impact itself.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To influence; to affect; to impact.
2. **[noun]** An influence; an effect; an impact.

## Example Sentences

**天气影响了我们的计划。**
Tiānqì yǐngxiǎng le wǒmen de jìhuà.
The weather affected our plans.

**他对我有很大的影响。**
Tā duì wǒ yǒu hěn dà de yǐngxiǎng.
He has had a great influence on me.

**这件事不会影响我们的关系。**
Zhè jiàn shì bù huì yǐngxiǎng wǒmen de guānxi.
This matter will not affect our relationship.

**父母的行为会影响孩子的成长。**
Fùmǔ de xíngwéi huì yǐngxiǎng háizi de chéngzhǎng.
Parents' behavior will influence children's development.

## Memory Hook

影 means "shadow" (something cast by light). 响 means "sound / echo." Your shadow and your echo spread beyond you — that is your influence reaching others.
