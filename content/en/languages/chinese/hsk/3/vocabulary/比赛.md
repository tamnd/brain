## 比赛 (bǐsài)

比赛 is the standard word for a competition, match, or contest. It is used for sports, academic contests, and any formal competitive event. It can also function as a verb meaning "to compete."

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Competition; match; contest; race.
2. **[verb]** To compete; to have a match.

## Example Sentences

**他参加了学校的演讲比赛。**
Tā cānjiā le xuéxiào de yǎnjiǎng bǐsài.
He participated in the school speech competition.

**今天下午有一场足球比赛。**
Jīntiān xiàwǔ yǒu yī chǎng zúqiú bǐsài.
There is a football match this afternoon.

**她在比赛中得了第一名。**
Tā zài bǐsài zhōng dé le dìyī míng.
She came first in the competition.

**来，我们比赛跑步！**
Lái, wǒmen bǐsài pǎobù!
Come on, let's have a running race!

## Memory Hook

比 (compare — two people side by side) plus 赛 (to race / contest). Two people placed side by side to see who is better — that is a competition.
