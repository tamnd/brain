## 为了 (wèile)

A preposition that introduces a purpose or goal, equivalent to "in order to" or "for the sake of."

## Meanings

1. **[preposition]** In order to, for the purpose of.
2. **[preposition]** For the sake of (someone or something).

## Example Sentences

**为了健康，他每天跑步。**
Wèile jiànkāng, tā měitiān pǎobù.
In order to stay healthy, he runs every day.

**她为了孩子放弃了工作。**
Tā wèile háizi fàngqì le gōngzuò.
She gave up her job for the sake of her children.

**为了通过考试，我努力学习。**
Wèile tōngguò kǎoshì, wǒ nǔlì xuéxí.
In order to pass the exam, I study hard.

**他存钱是为了买一辆新车。**
Tā cún qián shì wèile mǎi yī liàng xīn chē.
He saves money in order to buy a new car.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 为了健康 | for the sake of health |
| 为了家人 | for the sake of family |
| 为了成功 | in order to succeed |
| 为了将来 | for the future |
| 为了这个目标 | for this goal |

## Memory Hook

为 means "for" and 了 marks completion of purpose — you have set a target "for" and you aim toward it.
