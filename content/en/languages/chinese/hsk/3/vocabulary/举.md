## 举 (jǔ)

A verb meaning to lift, raise, or hold up something, and also to give or cite an example.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To lift, to raise, to hold up.
2. **[verb]** To give (an example), to cite, to name.

## Example Sentences

**请举手回答问题。**
Qǐng jǔ shǒu huídá wèntí.
Please raise your hand to answer the question.

**老师举了一个很好的例子。**
Lǎoshī jǔ le yīgè hěn hǎo de lìzi.
The teacher gave a very good example.

**他举起了沉重的箱子。**
Tā jǔ qǐ le chénzhòng de xiāngzi.
He lifted up the heavy box.

**你能举一个例子吗？**
Nǐ néng jǔ yīgè lìzi ma?
Can you give an example?

Picture someone raising both hands high above their head, calling attention — that is 举 in action.
