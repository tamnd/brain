## 往往 (wǎngwǎng)

往往 means "often" or "as a rule," describing a pattern that has been observed to repeat. Unlike 通常 (which states a neutral norm), 往往 draws on experience to note a tendency — it often carries a subtle explanatory or analytical tone.

## Meanings

1. **[adverb]** Often; frequently; as a rule; tend to — describes a recurring tendency or pattern observed from experience.

### Compare 往往 vs 常常

| Word | Use |
|------|-----|
| 往往 | Based on observed patterns; often used with conditions or explanations (e.g., 在…情况下，往往…) |
| 常常 | Simple high frequency; interchangeable with 经常 in most cases |

## Example Sentences

**遇到困难时，他往往先冷静下来再思考。**
Yùdào kùnnán shí, tā wǎngwǎng xiān lěngjìng xià lái zài sīkǎo.
When he encounters difficulties, he tends to calm down first before thinking it through.

**好的机会往往来得不是时候。**
Hǎo de jīhuì wǎngwǎng lái de bú shì shíhòu.
Good opportunities often come at inconvenient times.

**天气越冷，往往越容易感冒。**
Tiānqì yuè lěng, wǎngwǎng yuè róngyì gǎnmào.
The colder the weather, the more likely one tends to catch a cold.

**成功的人往往比别人更努力。**
Chénggōng de rén wǎngwǎng bǐ biérén gèng nǔlì.
Successful people often work harder than others.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 往往会 | tends to; often will |
| 往往如此 | often the case; tends to be so |
| 往往容易 | often tends to be easy/likely |
| 在…情况下往往 | in… situations, one often… |
| 往往意味着 | often means/implies |

## Memory Hook

往 means "to go toward / the past." Repeated twice, 往往 paints a picture of the same thing going in the same direction over and over again — a recurring pattern from the past that keeps repeating.
