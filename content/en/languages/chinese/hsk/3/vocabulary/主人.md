## 主人 (zhǔrén)

Refers to the host of a gathering, the owner of a property, or a master in a relationship of authority.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Host (of a party, household, or event).
2. **[noun]** Owner, master (of a property or animal).

## Example Sentences

**主人热情地欢迎了客人。**
Zhǔrén rèqíng de huānyíng le kèrén.
The host warmly welcomed the guests.

**这只狗的主人是我的邻居。**
Zhè zhī gǒu de zhǔrén shì wǒ de línjū.
The owner of this dog is my neighbor.

**主人为客人准备了丰盛的晚餐。**
Zhǔrén wèi kèrén zhǔnbèi le fēngshèng de wǎncān.
The host prepared a sumptuous dinner for the guests.

**他是这栋房子的主人。**
Tā shì zhè dòng fángzi de zhǔrén.
He is the owner of this house.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 主人公 | protagonist, main character |
| 房子的主人 | owner of the house |
| 热情的主人 | warm/enthusiastic host |
| 主人翁 | master, owner (sense of ownership) |
| 主人和客人 | host and guest |

## Memory Hook

主 means "main/primary" and 人 means "person" — the main person in a home is the host or owner.
