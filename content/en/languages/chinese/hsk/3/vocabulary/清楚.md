## 清楚 (qīngchu)

清楚 means clear, distinct, or clearly understood. It describes something that is easy to perceive or understand, whether a sound, image, explanation, or situation. It can also mean to be clear about something as a verb. Its opposite is 模糊 (blurry, vague).

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** Clear; distinct; easy to perceive or understand.
2. **[verb]** To be clear about; to understand clearly.

## Example Sentences

**你说得不清楚，再说一遍。**
Nǐ shuō de bù qīngchu, zài shuō yī biàn.
You did not explain clearly; say it again.

**照片拍得很清楚。**
Zhàopiàn pāi de hěn qīngchu.
The photo was taken very clearly.

**我不清楚他为什么这么做。**
Wǒ bù qīngchu tā wèishénme zhème zuò.
I am not clear about why he did this.

**请说清楚你的要求。**
Qǐng shuō qīngchu nǐ de yāoqiú.
Please state your requirements clearly.

## Collocations

## Memory Hook

清 (clear water) plus 楚 (distinct). Imagine clear, still water where you can see all the way to the bottom without any murkiness. That transparency is 清楚.
