## 只要 (zhǐyào)

只要 states the minimum or sole condition needed for a result to follow. It is paired with 就 (jiù) in the second clause, forming a pattern that says: "as long as X, then Y will follow."

## Meanings

1. **[conjunction]** As long as; provided that; if only.

## Example Sentences

**只要努力，就会成功。**
Zhǐyào nǔlì, jiù huì chénggōng.
As long as you work hard, you will succeed.

**只要你来，我就高兴。**
Zhǐyào nǐ lái, wǒ jiù gāoxìng.
As long as you come, I'll be happy.

**只要有时间，我就去看你。**
Zhǐyào yǒu shíjiān, wǒ jiù qù kàn nǐ.
As long as I have time, I'll come see you.

**只要坚持每天练习，你的口语就会变好。**
Zhǐyào jiānchí měitiān liànxí, nǐ de kǒuyǔ jiù huì biàn hǎo.
As long as you keep practicing every day, your spoken Chinese will improve.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 只要…就… | as long as…then… |
| 只要可以 | as long as possible |
| 只要一个条件 | only one condition needed |
| 只要你愿意 | as long as you are willing |
| 只要努力 | as long as you work hard |

## Memory Hook

只 (only) plus 要 (want/require): "only require this one thing" — the minimum condition that opens the door to the result.
