## 过敏 (guòmǐn)

过敏 describes an allergic reaction or the state of being allergic to something. The key pattern is 对...过敏 (to be allergic to...). It can function as a verb (to have an allergy) or a noun (an allergy). It is essential vocabulary for discussing health and medical situations.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To be allergic; to have an allergic reaction.
2. **[noun]** Allergy; hypersensitivity.

## Example Sentences

**我对花粉过敏。**
Wǒ duì huāfěn guòmǐn.
I am allergic to pollen.

**他吃了虾以后过敏了，脸上起了红点。**
Tā chī le xiā yǐhòu guòmǐn le, liǎn shàng qǐ le hóngdiǎn.
After eating shrimp he had an allergic reaction and red spots appeared on his face.

**你有没有过敏史？**
Nǐ yǒu méiyǒu guòmǐn shǐ?
Do you have a history of allergies?

**这种药可能会引起过敏反应。**
Zhè zhǒng yào kěnéng huì yǐnqǐ guòmǐn fǎnyìng.
This medicine may cause an allergic reaction.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 对...过敏 | be allergic to... |
| 过敏反应 | allergic reaction |
| 过敏症状 | allergy symptoms |
| 过敏史 | allergy history |
| 食物过敏 | food allergy |

## Memory Hook

过 means "to go too far / exceed" and 敏 means "sensitive / quick." Allergy is when your body's sensitivity goes too far — it overreacts to something harmless as if it were an enemy.
