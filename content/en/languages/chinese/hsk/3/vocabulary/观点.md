## 观点 (guāndiǎn)

观点 refers to a person's particular position or perspective on an issue.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** viewpoint; standpoint; perspective; point of view

## Example Sentences

**我同意你的观点，这个计划很好。** Wǒ tóngyì nǐ de guāndiǎn, zhège jìhuà hěn hǎo. I agree with your viewpoint; this plan is very good.

**每个人对这件事都有不同的观点。** Měi gè rén duì zhè jiàn shì dōu yǒu bùtóng de guāndiǎn. Everyone has a different perspective on this matter.

**你能说明一下你的观点吗？** Nǐ néng shuōmíng yīxià nǐ de guāndiǎn ma? Could you explain your viewpoint?

**从历史的观点来看，这是一个重要事件。** Cóng lìshǐ de guāndiǎn lái kàn, zhè shì yīgè zhòngyào shìjiàn. From a historical perspective, this is an important event.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 个人观点 | personal viewpoint |
| 不同观点 | different perspectives |

| 从…观点来看 | from the perspective of... |

## Memory Hook

观 means "to view/observe" and 点 means "point." A 观点 is the point from which you observe the world.
