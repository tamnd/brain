## 不管 (bùguǎn)

不管 means "no matter" or "regardless of." It introduces a condition that covers all possibilities, and the second clause — always marked by 都 or 也 — states what holds true under every one of those possibilities.

## Meanings

1. **[conjunction]** No matter (what / how / who / whether); regardless of — always paired with 都 or 也 in the result clause.

### Pattern

不管 + [question word / A 还是 B] + 都/也 + [result]

| Slot | Example |
|------|---------|
| Question word | 不管怎样，都要坚持。 No matter what, you must persist. |
| A 还是 B | 不管晴天还是雨天，他都跑步。 Whether sunny or rainy, he runs. |
| 多 + adj | 不管多难，也要完成。 No matter how hard, it must be finished. |

## Example Sentences

**不管天气怎么样，我们都要去。**
Bùguǎn tiānqì zěnme yàng, wǒmen dōu yào qù.
No matter what the weather is like, we are going.

**不管你信不信，这是真的。**
Bùguǎn nǐ xìn bù xìn, zhè shì zhēn de.
Whether you believe it or not, it is true.

**不管多忙，他也会给家人打电话。**
Bùguǎn duō máng, tā yě huì gěi jiārén dǎ diànhuà.
No matter how busy he is, he still calls his family.

**不管结果怎样，我们都尽力了。**
Bùguǎn jiéguǒ zěnyàng, wǒmen dōu jìnlì le.
Whatever the result, we gave it our best.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 不管怎样 | no matter what; anyway |
| 不管如何 | no matter how; regardless |
| 不管是…还是… | whether it is…or… |
| 不管多… | no matter how (adj)… |
| 不管什么情况 | no matter what the situation |

## Memory Hook

管 means "to manage / to care about." 不管 = "not caring about" any condition — so the result always stays the same regardless.
