## 决定 (juédìng)

决定 means to decide or make a decision. It can function as both a verb and a noun, covering personal choices and formal resolutions.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To decide; to determine; to resolve.
2. **[noun]** A decision; a resolution.

## Example Sentences

**我决定去北京工作。**
Wǒ juédìng qù Běijīng gōngzuò.
I decided to go to Beijing to work.

**你有没有做决定？**
Nǐ yǒu méiyǒu zuò juédìng?
Have you made a decision?

**老师决定推迟考试。**
Lǎoshī juédìng tuīchí kǎoshì.
The teacher decided to postpone the exam.

**这个决定非常重要。**
Zhège juédìng fēicháng zhòngyào.
This decision is very important.

## Memory Hook

决 contains water 冫 — a river that has already decided which way it will flow. Once water finds its course, the direction is set.
