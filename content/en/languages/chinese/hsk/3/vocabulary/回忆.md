## 回忆 (huíyì)

回忆 means both the act of recalling past events and the memories themselves, often carrying a nostalgic or reflective tone.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to recall; to recollect; to think back on
2. **[noun]** memory; recollection; reminiscence

## Example Sentences

**我经常回忆起小时候的事情。** Wǒ jīngcháng huíyì qǐ xiǎoshíhou de shìqing. I often recall things from my childhood.

**那段时光是我最美好的回忆。** Nà duàn shíguāng shì wǒ zuì měihǎo de huíyì. That period of time is my most beautiful memory.

**他在日记里写下了所有的回忆。** Tā zài rìjì lǐ xiě xià le suǒyǒu de huíyì. He wrote down all his memories in his diary.

**看到这张照片，我回忆起了那次旅行。** Kàndào zhè zhāng zhàopiàn, wǒ huíyì qǐ le nà cì lǚxíng. Seeing this photo, I recalled that trip.

## Memory Hook

回 means "to return" and 忆 means "to remember" — 回忆 is your mind returning to something it has remembered.
