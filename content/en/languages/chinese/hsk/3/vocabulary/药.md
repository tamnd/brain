## 药 (yào)

药 means medicine or medication. It refers to any substance taken to treat illness or pain, including both Western pharmaceuticals and traditional Chinese remedies.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Medicine; medication; drug; remedy.

## Example Sentences

**医生给我开了一些药。**
Yīshēng gěi wǒ kāi le yīxiē yào.
The doctor prescribed some medicine for me.

**你吃药了吗？**
Nǐ chī yào le ma?
Did you take your medicine?

**这种药要饭后服用。**
Zhè zhǒng yào yào fàn hòu fúyòng.
This medicine should be taken after meals.

**药店在超市旁边。**
Yàodiàn zài chāoshì pángbiān.
The pharmacy is next to the supermarket.

## Memory Hook

药 contains 草 (grass/herb) at the top, because ancient medicines came from plants and herbs. Below is 约 (about/approximately), giving the pronunciation. Medicine is literally the essence of healing herbs, approximated into a cure.
