## 自己 (zìjǐ)

自己 is a reflexive pronoun meaning "oneself," used to refer back to the subject of the sentence.

## Meanings

1. **[pron]** oneself; self; by oneself; one's own

## Example Sentences

**你要相信自己，你一定能做到。** Nǐ yào xiāngxìn zìjǐ, nǐ yīdìng néng zuò dào. You must believe in yourself; you can definitely do it.

**这件事你要自己决定，不要依赖别人。** Zhè jiàn shì nǐ yào zìjǐ juédìng, bùyào yīlài biérén. You should decide this matter yourself and not rely on others.

**她自己做了一顿饭，很有成就感。** Tā zìjǐ zuò le yī dùn fàn, hěn yǒu chéngjiùgǎn. She cooked a meal by herself and felt a great sense of accomplishment.

**我们要对自己的行为负责。** Wǒmen yào duì zìjǐ de xíngwéi fùzé. We must take responsibility for our own actions.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 相信自己 | to believe in oneself |
| 自己做 | to do it oneself |
| 靠自己 | to rely on oneself |
| 对自己 | toward oneself |

## Memory Hook

自 means "self/from" and 己 means "oneself." Together they emphasize the personal, independent self doing something on their own terms.
