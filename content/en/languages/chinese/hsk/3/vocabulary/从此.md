## 从此 (cóngcǐ)

从此 is an adverb marking a turning point in time, meaning "from this point on" or "since then," used to introduce what has been true or what will be true from a specific moment forward.

## Meanings

1. **[adverb]** from this time on; henceforth; since then (marks a moment as a new starting point, after which things continue in a new direction)

## Usage Note

从此 refers to a specific moment that serves as a dividing line. Everything described after 从此 is a lasting consequence or ongoing state that began at that moment. It often appears in narratives, fairy tales, and personal turning-point stories.

Compare with 从那以后 (cóng nà yǐhòu, "from then on"), which is more colloquial and interchangeable. 从此 sounds slightly more literary and final, like a narrator closing a chapter.

## Example Sentences

**他们结婚了，从此过上了幸福的生活。** Tāmen jiéhūn le, cóngcǐ guò shàng le xìngfú de shēnghuó. They got married and from then on lived a happy life.

**那次事故改变了他的人生，从此他更加珍惜每一天。** Nà cì shìgù gǎibiàn le tā de rénshēng, cóngcǐ tā gèngjiā zhēnxī měi yī tiān. That accident changed his life; since then, he has cherished every day even more.

**她离开家乡以后，从此再也没有回来。** Tā líkāi jiāxiāng yǐhòu, cóngcǐ zài yě méiyǒu huí lái. After she left her hometown, she never came back again.

**我们握手和解，从此成为了好朋友。** Wǒmen wòshǒu héjiě, cóngcǐ chéngwéile hǎo péngyou. We shook hands and made peace; from that point on we became good friends.

## Memory Hook

从 means "from" and 此 means "this / here." 从此 literally means "from this (point)." Picture drawing a line on a timeline with your finger: everything to the right of that line is 从此.
