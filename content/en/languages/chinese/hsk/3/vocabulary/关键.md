## 关键 (guānjiàn)

关键 refers to the most critical factor or turning point in a situation, and can also serve as an adjective meaning crucial or decisive.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** the key; the crux — the most important or decisive element
2. **[adjective]** key; crucial; pivotal — critically important

## Example Sentences

**关键是要坚持下去。** Guānjiàn shì yào jiānchí xiàqù. The key is to keep persisting.

**这是一个关键时刻。** Zhè shì yī gè guānjiàn shíkè. This is a crucial moment.

**找到问题的关键才能解决它。** Zhǎodào wèntí de guānjiàn cái néng jiějué tā. You must find the crux of the problem before you can solve it.

**他在关键时候帮助了我。** Tā zài guānjiàn shíhou bāngzhù le wǒ. He helped me at a critical moment.

## Memory Hook

关 (to close; to lock) plus 键 (key for a lock) — the key that opens or locks the door: that is the 关键 of any situation.
