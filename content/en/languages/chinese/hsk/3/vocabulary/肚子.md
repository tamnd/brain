## 肚子 (dùzi)

肚子 means belly or stomach in the colloquial sense. It is the everyday spoken word for the abdomen and is used when talking about hunger, fullness, or stomach pain.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Belly; stomach; abdomen (colloquial, everyday usage).

## Example Sentences

**我的肚子很饿。**
Wǒ de dùzi hěn è.
My stomach is very hungry.

**我肚子疼，可能吃坏东西了。**
Wǒ dùzi téng, kěnéng chī huài dōngxi le.
My stomach hurts; I may have eaten something bad.

**吃太多了，肚子撑得很。**
Chī tài duō le, dùzi chēng de hěn.
I ate too much; my stomach is very full.

**他摸了摸自己的肚子说饿了。**
Tā mō le mō zìjǐ de dùzi shuō è le.
He patted his belly and said he was hungry.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 肚子饿 | to be hungry |
| 肚子疼 | stomachache |
| 肚子饱 | to be full |
| 闹肚子 | to have stomach trouble / diarrhea |
| 大肚子 | big belly |

## Memory Hook

肚子 contains 月 (the body radical, originally 肉 meaning flesh) plus 土 (earth). Your belly is the earthy center of your body, the round mound where food goes. When your 肚子 calls, it is time to eat.
