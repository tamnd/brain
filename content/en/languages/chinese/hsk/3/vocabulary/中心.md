## 中心 (zhōngxīn)

Refers to the center point of a place or the core focus of a topic or activity.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Center, the middle point of a space or area.
2. **[noun]** Core, the main focus or hub of something.

## Example Sentences

**购物中心在城市的中心。**
Gòuwù zhōngxīn zài chéngshì de zhōngxīn.
The shopping center is in the center of the city.

**北京是中国的政治中心。**
Běijīng shì Zhōngguó de zhèngzhì zhōngxīn.
Beijing is the political center of China.

**这个社区有一个运动中心。**
Zhège shèqū yǒu yīgè yùndòng zhōngxīn.
This community has a sports center.

**他是整个团队的中心人物。**
Tā shì zhěnggè tuánduì de zhōngxīn rénwù.
He is the central figure of the whole team.

## Memory Hook

中 means "middle" and 心 means "heart" — the heart of the middle is the center of everything.
