## 帮忙 (bāng máng)

帮忙 means to help or to lend a hand. It is a verb-object compound where 帮 means to assist and 忙 means busyness or affairs. Because it is a verb-object compound, the object of the help can be inserted between the two characters: 帮他忙 (help him), not 帮忙他.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To help; to give a hand; to assist with tasks.

## Example Sentences

**你能帮我忙吗？**
Nǐ néng bāng wǒ máng ma?
Can you help me?

**谢谢你帮忙。**
Xièxie nǐ bāng máng.
Thank you for your help.

**我去帮忙搬家具。**
Wǒ qù bāng máng bān jiājù.
I will go help move the furniture.

**他帮了我一个大忙。**
Tā bāng le wǒ yī gè dà máng.
He did me a great favor.

## Collocations

## Memory Hook

帮 is to assist and 忙 is busy work or affairs. To 帮忙 is to take on part of someone's busy affairs and carry it for them, giving them relief from their burden.
