## 丢 (diū)

丢 means to lose something, to misplace it, or to leave something behind unintentionally.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to lose; to misplace; to leave behind; to drop

## Example Sentences

**我把钱包丢了。** Wǒ bǎ qiánbāo diū le. I lost my wallet.

**他在地铁上丢了手机。** Tā zài dìtiě shàng diū le shǒujī. He lost his phone on the subway.

**不小心把钥匙丢在家里了。** Bù xiǎoxīn bǎ yàoshi diū zài jiā lǐ le. He accidentally left his keys at home.

**丢东西真的很麻烦，要小心保管好。** Diū dōngxi zhēnde hěn máfan, yào xiǎoxīn bǎoguǎn hǎo. Losing things is really troublesome; keep them safe carefully.

Picture something slipping out of your hand and falling away; that sinking feeling of something gone is 丢.
