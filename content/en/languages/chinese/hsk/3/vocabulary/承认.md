## 承认 (chéngrèn)

承认 means to admit or acknowledge something, whether a fact, a mistake, or another person's status.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to admit; to acknowledge; to recognize; to confess

## Example Sentences

**他终于承认了自己的错误。** Tā zhōngyú chéngrèn le zìjǐ de cuòwù. He finally admitted his mistake.

**你要勇敢地承认问题才能解决它。** Nǐ yào yǒnggǎn de chéngrèn wèntí cái néng jiějué tā. You must bravely acknowledge the problem before you can solve it.

**这个国家正式承认了他们的政府。** Zhège guójiā zhèngshì chéngrèn le tāmen de zhèngfǔ. This country formally recognized their government.

**她不愿意承认自己害怕。** Tā bù yuànyì chéngrèn zìjǐ hàipà. She was unwilling to admit that she was afraid.

## Memory Hook

承 (to receive/bear) + 认 (to recognize): you bear the weight of recognition, accepting something as real.
