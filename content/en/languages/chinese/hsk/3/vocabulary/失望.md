## 失望 (shīwàng)

失望 describes the feeling when reality falls short of expectations, literally "to lose one's hope."

## Meanings

1. **[adj/verb]** disappointed; to lose hope; to feel let down

## Example Sentences

**他没有通过考试，父母很失望。** Tā méiyǒu tōngguò kǎoshì, fùmǔ hěn shīwàng. He did not pass the exam and his parents were very disappointed.

**别让我失望，我相信你能做到。** Bié ràng wǒ shīwàng, wǒ xiāngxìn nǐ néng zuò dào. Don't disappoint me, I believe you can do it.

**我对那部电影感到有点失望。** Wǒ duì nà bù diànyǐng gǎndào yǒudiǎn shīwàng. I felt a little disappointed with that film.

**虽然结果让人失望，但我们不会放弃。** Suīrán jiéguǒ ràng rén shīwàng, dàn wǒmen bù huì fàngqì. Although the result was disappointing, we will not give up.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 感到失望 | to feel disappointed |

| 失望而归 | to return disappointed |

## Memory Hook

失 means "to lose" and 望 means "hope/expectation." Disappointment is literally losing what you hoped for.
