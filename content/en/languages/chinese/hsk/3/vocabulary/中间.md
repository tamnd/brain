## 中间 (zhōngjiān)

中间 describes the position in the center of a space or the interval between two points in time, people, or things.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** the middle; the center point of a space or object
2. **[noun]** between; in the interval separating two things or people
3. **[noun]** among; within a group

## Example Sentences

**书在桌子中间。** Shū zài zhuōzi zhōngjiān. The book is in the middle of the table.

**他坐在两个朋友中间。** Tā zuò zài liǎng gè péngyǒu zhōngjiān. He is sitting between two friends.

**在这些学生中间，她的成绩最好。** Zài zhèxiē xuésheng zhōngjiān, tā de chéngjì zuì hǎo. Among these students, her grades are the best.

**我们中间休息了十分钟。** Wǒmen zhōngjiān xiūxi le shí fēnzhōng. We took a ten-minute break in the middle.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 在…中间 (zài…zhōngjiān) | in the middle of; between |
| 中间位置 (zhōngjiān wèizhi) | middle position |
| 中间人 (zhōngjiān rén) | middleman; intermediary |
| 站在中间 (zhàn zài zhōngjiān) | to stand in the middle |

## Memory Hook

中 means "center" and 间 means "space between" — put them together and you get the space right in the center between two things.
