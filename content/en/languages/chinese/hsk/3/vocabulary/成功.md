## 成功 (chénggōng)

成功 means success or to succeed. As a verb it describes the successful completion of a goal or action. As a noun it refers to the state or result of succeeding. It is one of the most motivating and frequently used words in Chinese culture.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To succeed; to be successful.
2. **[noun]** Success; achievement.

## Example Sentences

**他终于成功了！**
Tā zhōngyú chénggōng le!
He finally succeeded!

**成功需要努力和坚持。**
Chénggōng xūyào nǔlì hé jiānchí.
Success requires hard work and persistence.

**实验成功了。**
Shíyàn chénggōng le.
The experiment succeeded.

**祝你成功！**
Zhù nǐ chénggōng!
Wishing you success!

## Memory Hook

成 means to accomplish or complete and 功 means merit or achievement. 成功 is when your effort (功) reaches completion (成), when all the work you did finally pays off and the goal is achieved.
