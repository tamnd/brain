## 紧张 (jǐnzhāng)

紧张 means nervous or tense in an emotional sense, but also describes a tight, busy, or strained situation — a shortage of time, resources, or a pressured environment.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** Nervous; anxious; tense (emotional state).
2. **[adjective]** Tight; strained; pressed (for time, resources, etc.).

## Example Sentences

**考试前我非常紧张。**
Kǎoshì qián wǒ fēicháng jǐnzhāng.
I am very nervous before exams.

**别紧张，你一定能做好。**
Bié jǐnzhāng, nǐ yīdìng néng zuò hǎo.
Don't be nervous; you can definitely do well.

**最近工作很紧张，没有时间休息。**
Zuìjìn gōngzuò hěn jǐnzhāng, méiyǒu shíjiān xiūxi.
Work has been very hectic lately; there's no time to rest.

**他说话时很紧张，声音都在抖。**
Tā shuōhuà shí hěn jǐnzhāng, shēngyīn dōu zài dǒu.
He was very nervous when speaking; his voice was trembling.

## Memory Hook

紧 (tight — silk threads wound tight) plus 张 (stretch / open wide). Silk stretched tight to the point of snapping — the physical feeling of nerves.
