## 到处 (dàochù)

到处 means everywhere or all over — no place is excluded. It emphasizes the exhaustive range of a search or the wide distribution of something.

## Meanings

1. **[adverb]** Everywhere; all over; in every place.

## Example Sentences

**我到处找，都找不到钥匙。**
Wǒ dàochù zhǎo, dōu zhǎo bu dào yàoshi.
I looked everywhere but couldn't find the key.

**节日期间，到处都是人。**
Jiérì qījiān, dàochù dōu shì rén.
During the holiday, there are people everywhere.

**他去过很多国家，到处旅行。**
Tā qùguò hěn duō guójiā, dàochù lǚxíng.
He has been to many countries, traveling everywhere.

**春天来了，到处都是花。**
Chūntiān lái le, dàochù dōu shì huā.
Spring has come; there are flowers everywhere.

## Collocations

## Memory Hook

到 (arrive) plus 处 (place). Arriving at every place — your presence or your search has reached every location without exception.
