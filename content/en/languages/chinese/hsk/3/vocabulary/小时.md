## 小时 (xiǎoshí)

小时 means hour, the unit of time equal to 60 minutes. It is the standard word for duration. Do not confuse it with 时候 (shíhou, time/moment) or 时间 (shíjiān, time as a concept). 小时 always refers to a count of hours.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Hour (a unit of time, 60 minutes).

## Example Sentences

**我每天学习两个小时。**
Wǒ měitiān xuéxí liǎng gè xiǎoshí.
I study for two hours every day.

**从这里到北京要三个小时。**
Cóng zhèlǐ dào Běijīng yào sān gè xiǎoshí.
It takes three hours to get from here to Beijing.

**他等了一个多小时。**
Tā děng le yī gè duō xiǎoshí.
He waited for over an hour.

**这部电影有两个小时。**
Zhè bù diànyǐng yǒu liǎng gè xiǎoshí.
This movie is two hours long.

## Memory Hook

小 means small and 时 means time. An hour is a small unit of time carved out from the larger day. Line up 24 of these small-time blocks and you have a full day.
