## 反而 (fǎn'ér)

反而 is an adverb that introduces a result contradicting what was expected, intended, or previously described, meaning "on the contrary," "instead," or "unexpectedly."

## Meanings

1. **[adverb]** on the contrary; instead; unexpectedly (used when the actual result is the opposite of, or differs significantly from, the expected or desired outcome)

## Usage Note

反而 is placed before the verb or adjective in the clause describing the unexpected result. It typically follows a clause establishing the expectation or condition. The key feature of 反而 is reversal: the result goes against what logic, effort, or intention would predict.

Structure: [expected cause/action] + 反而 + [opposite/unexpected result]

Contrast with 却 (què), which simply marks a contrast ("but / yet"), and 倒 (dào), which can also mark unexpected reversal. 反而 specifically stresses that the outcome is the reverse of what was expected.

- 我以为安慰他会有用，反而让他更难过了。 I thought comforting him would help; on the contrary, it made him more upset.

## Example Sentences

**他越解释，我反而越糊涂了。** Tā yuè jiěshì, wǒ fǎn'ér yuè hútu le. The more he explained, the more confused I became.

**吃了药以后，他的病反而更严重了。** Chī le yào yǐhòu, tā de bìng fǎn'ér gèng yánzhòng le. After taking the medicine, his condition unexpectedly got worse.

**我以为他会生气，他反而笑了起来。** Wǒ yǐwéi tā huì shēngqì, tā fǎn'ér xiào le qǐlái. I thought he would be angry, but on the contrary he burst out laughing.

**减少睡眠时间反而让他感觉更累了。** Jiǎnshǎo shuìmián shíjiān fǎn'ér ràng tā gǎnjué gèng lèi le. Reducing sleep time unexpectedly made him feel even more tired.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 越...反而越... | the more... the more (contrary) |
| 反而更 | on the contrary, even more |
| 反而好 | on the contrary, it is better |
| 反而变得 | instead became |
| 反而让人 | instead makes one (feel...) |

## Memory Hook

反 means "to reverse / contrary" and 而 is a conjunction meaning "but / and." Together: "reversing and" — the action turns around and goes the opposite direction from what you expected.
