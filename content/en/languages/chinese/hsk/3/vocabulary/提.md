## 提 (tí)

提 is a versatile verb meaning to raise, lift, or bring up something, whether physically or in conversation.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to mention; to bring up (a topic)
2. **[verb]** to carry (something) by hand; to lift

## Example Sentences

**他提到了一个很有趣的问题。** Tā tídào le yīgè hěn yǒuqù de wèntí. He brought up a very interesting question.

**会议上，经理提出了新的计划。** Huìyì shàng, jīnglǐ tíchū le xīn de jìhuà. At the meeting, the manager raised a new plan.

**请不要再提这件事了。** Qǐng bùyào zài tí zhè jiàn shì le. Please do not bring up this matter again.

**她用手提着一个大包。** Tā yòng shǒu tízhe yīgè dà bāo. She was carrying a large bag by hand.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 提出 | to put forward; to raise (a proposal) |
| 提到 | to mention |
| 提问 | to ask a question |
| 提高 | to raise; to improve |

## Memory Hook

提 has the hand radical (扌) — you use a hand to lift things up, whether a bag or a topic in conversation.
