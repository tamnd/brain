## 适合 (shìhé)

适合 describes the quality of fitting or being suited to a particular person, purpose, or situation, implying a good match between two things.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to suit; to be appropriate for; to fit well
2. **[adjective]** suitable; appropriate; fitting

## Example Sentences

**这件衣服很适合你。** Zhè jiàn yīfu hěn shìhé nǐ. This piece of clothing suits you very well.

**这个工作适合喜欢和人交流的人。** Zhège gōngzuò shìhé xǐhuan hé rén jiāoliú de rén. This job is suitable for people who enjoy communicating with others.

**这本书适合初学者阅读。** Zhè běn shū shìhé chūxuézhě yuèdú. This book is appropriate for beginners to read.

**我觉得这里的气候不太适合老年人居住。** Wǒ juéde zhèlǐ de qìhòu bù tài shìhé lǎoniánrén jūzhù. I think the climate here is not very suitable for elderly people to live in.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 非常适合 (fēicháng shìhé) | very suitable |
| 适合…的人 (shìhé…de rén) | suitable for people who... |
| 不适合 (bù shìhé) | unsuitable; not appropriate |
| 最适合 (zuì shìhé) | most suitable; best fit |
| 适合年龄 (shìhé niánlíng) | appropriate age |

## Memory Hook

适 contains the movement radical 辶 with 舌 (tongue) — finding the right fit is like a tongue finding the right flavor. 合 means "to combine/fit together" — when both elements agree, you have 适合, a perfect fit.
