## 讨论 (tǎolùn)

讨论 means to discuss or talk something over with one or more people, usually to reach an understanding, make a decision, or explore different views.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To discuss; to talk over; to debate.
2. **[noun]** A discussion; a debate.

## Example Sentences

**我们来讨论一下这个问题。**
Wǒmen lái tǎolùn yīxià zhège wèntí.
Let's discuss this problem.

**经过讨论，大家达成了一致意见。**
Jīngguò tǎolùn, dàjiā dáchéng le yīzhì yìjiàn.
After the discussion, everyone reached a consensus.

**老师请同学们分组讨论。**
Lǎoshī qǐng tóngxuémen fēn zǔ tǎolùn.
The teacher asked the students to discuss in groups.

**这个话题值得深入讨论。**
Zhège huàtí zhídé shēnrù tǎolùn.
This topic deserves an in-depth discussion.

## Memory Hook

讨 (to pursue / request — speech radical 讠 making a claim) plus 论 (to reason / discuss). Making a spoken claim and reasoning it out — the back-and-forth of discussion.
