## 增长 (zēngzhǎng)

增长 describes a rise or increase in something, particularly in measurable quantities such as population, income, or knowledge.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to grow; to increase — to become greater in number, amount, or degree
2. **[noun]** growth; increase — the act or result of growing in size, amount, or extent

## Example Sentences

**今年的销售额增长了百分之二十。** Jīnnián de xiāoshòu'é zēngzhǎng le bǎifēnzhī èrshí. Sales revenue grew by twenty percent this year.

**人口增长给环境带来了压力。** Rénkǒu zēngzhǎng gěi huánjìng dài lái le yālì. Population growth has put pressure on the environment.

**阅读能增长知识。** Yuèdú néng zēngzhǎng zhīshì. Reading can increase knowledge.

**经济保持了稳定增长。** Jīngjì bǎochí le wěndìng zēngzhǎng. The economy has maintained steady growth.

## Memory Hook

增 (to add; to increase) plus 长 (to grow; to lengthen) — adding and growing upward together: 增长.
