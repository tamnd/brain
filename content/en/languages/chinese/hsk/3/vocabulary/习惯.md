## 习惯 (xíguàn)

习惯 can function as a noun meaning "habit" or as a verb meaning "to be used to / accustomed to" something.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** habit; custom; routine
2. **[verb]** to be used to; to be accustomed to

## Example Sentences

**他有每天早起的习惯。** Tā yǒu měitiān zǎo qǐ de xíguàn. He has the habit of getting up early every day.

**我已经习惯了在这里生活。** Wǒ yǐjīng xíguàn le zài zhèlǐ shēnghuó. I am already used to living here.

**改变一个坏习惯需要很长时间。** Gǎibiàn yīgè huài xíguàn xūyào hěn cháng shíjiān. It takes a long time to change a bad habit.

**你习惯吃辣的食物吗？** Nǐ xíguàn chī là de shíwù ma? Are you used to eating spicy food?

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 好习惯 | good habit |
| 坏习惯 | bad habit |
| 生活习惯 | lifestyle habits |
| 习惯了 | to have gotten used to |

## Memory Hook

习 means "to practice/repeat" and 惯 means "to be accustomed" — repeating something until it becomes second nature is a 习惯.
