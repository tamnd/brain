## 任何 (rènhé)

任何 is a universal quantifier used before nouns to mean "any," "whatever," or "whichever," indicating that no member of a set is excluded.

## Meanings

1. **[pronoun / adjective]** any; whatever; whichever (used before a noun to indicate "no matter which one" or "every possible one," often in negative or conditional sentences)

## Usage Note

任何 must be followed by a noun (or noun phrase). It is most commonly used in:
- Negative sentences: 没有任何问题 (no problem whatsoever)
- Conditional sentences: 如果有任何问题 (if there is any problem)
- Universal statements: 任何人都可以参加 (anyone can participate)

Compare with 任意 (rènyì), which means "arbitrary / any at one's discretion," used more in math or technical contexts.

## Example Sentences

**我没有任何问题。** Wǒ méiyǒu rènhé wèntí. I have no questions whatsoever.

**任何人都可以参加这个活动。** Rènhé rén dōu kěyǐ cānjiā zhège huódòng. Anyone can participate in this activity.

**他不接受任何批评。** Tā bù jiēshòu rènhé pīpíng. He does not accept any criticism.

**如果有任何困难，请告诉我。** Rúguǒ yǒu rènhé kùnnán, qǐng gàosù wǒ. If there is any difficulty, please let me know.

## Memory Hook

任 means "to entrust / to allow freely" and 何 means "what / which." Together: "freely whatever" or "whichever you allow." If something is open to 任何, it is open to every possible option without restriction.
