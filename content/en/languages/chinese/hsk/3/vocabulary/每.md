## 每 (měi)

每 means every or each. It is placed before a measure word or time expression to indicate that something applies to all members of a group without exception. Common patterns include 每天 (every day), 每次 (every time), and 每个人 (every person).

## Meanings

1. **[determiner]** Every; each — applies to all members of a set without exception.

## Example Sentences

**我每天早上跑步。**
Wǒ měitiān zǎoshang pǎobù.
I go running every morning.

**每个人都有自己的优点。**
Měi gè rén dōu yǒu zìjǐ de yōudiǎn.
Every person has their own strengths.

**他每次来都带礼物。**
Tā měi cì lái dōu dài lǐwù.
Every time he comes, he brings a gift.

**每年春节，全家人都会聚在一起。**
Měi nián Chūnjié, quán jiā rén dōu huì jù zài yīqǐ.
Every year at Spring Festival, the whole family gathers together.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|

## Memory Hook

每 looks like a person with a crown on top. Think of it as "every single crowned ruler," every one without exception. No one is left out.
