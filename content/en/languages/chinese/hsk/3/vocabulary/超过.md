## 超过 (chāoguò)

超过 means to exceed or go beyond a certain number, limit, or standard. It is used for quantities, speeds, scores, and any measurable value.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To exceed; to surpass; to go beyond; to be more than.

## Example Sentences

**今天气温超过了三十五度。**
Jīntiān qìwēn chāoguò le sānshíwǔ dù.
Today's temperature exceeded 35 degrees.

**这次考试她的成绩超过了所有人。**
Zhècì kǎoshì tā de chéngjì chāoguò le suǒyǒu rén.
Her score this exam surpassed everyone else's.

**参加比赛的人数超过了五百。**
Cānjiā bǐsài de rénshù chāoguò le wǔbǎi.
The number of people participating in the competition exceeded five hundred.

**开车不能超过限速。**
Kāichē bù néng chāoguò xiàn sù.
You cannot exceed the speed limit when driving.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 超过限制 | exceed the limit |
| 超过预期 | exceed expectations |
| 远超过 | far exceed |
| 数量超过 | the quantity exceeds |
| 超过一半 | more than half |

## Memory Hook

超 (to leap over / surpass — a footstep 走 leaping higher 召) plus 过 (to pass). Your foot leaps and passes the mark — you have gone beyond the boundary.
