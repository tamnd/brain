## 问题 (wèntí)

问题 is one of the most versatile and frequent nouns in Chinese. It covers both a question (something you ask) and a problem (something that needs fixing). Context makes the meaning clear. 没问题 (no problem) is an extremely common expression of agreement or reassurance.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Question; something asked in order to find an answer.
2. **[noun]** Problem; issue; something that has gone wrong or needs solving.

## Example Sentences

**你有什么问题吗？**
Nǐ yǒu shénme wèntí ma?
Do you have any questions?

**这台电脑有问题，开不了机。**
Zhè tái diànnǎo yǒu wèntí, kāi bù liǎo jī.
This computer has a problem and won't turn on.

**没问题，我来帮你。**
Méi wèntí, wǒ lái bāng nǐ.
No problem, I'll help you.

**这道数学题很难，我不知道怎么解决这个问题。**
Zhè dào shùxué tí hěn nán, wǒ bù zhīdào zěnme jiějué zhège wèntí.
This math problem is very hard; I don't know how to solve it.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 没问题 | no problem |
| 解决问题 | solve a problem |
| 回答问题 | answer a question |
| 出了问题 | something went wrong |
| 提问题 | ask a question / raise a question |

## Memory Hook

问 means "to ask" (mouth radical 口 inside a gate 门 — your mouth at the door, asking to enter). 题 means "topic / subject." A 问题 is a topic you are asking about — whether curious (question) or concerned (problem).
