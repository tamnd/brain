## 表达 (biǎodá)

表达 means to express or communicate thoughts, feelings, or ideas through words or actions.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to express; to convey; to communicate (ideas or feelings)

## Example Sentences

**他不善于表达自己的感情。** Tā bù shànyú biǎodá zìjǐ de gǎnqíng. He is not good at expressing his feelings.

**请用汉语表达你的想法。** Qǐng yòng Hànyǔ biǎodá nǐ de xiǎngfǎ. Please express your thoughts in Chinese.

**她用鲜花表达了对妈妈的感谢。** Tā yòng xiānhuā biǎodá le duì māma de gǎnxiè. She expressed her gratitude to her mother with fresh flowers.

**学习一门语言就是学习如何表达自己。** Xuéxí yī mén yǔyán jiùshì xuéxí rúhé biǎodá zìjǐ. Learning a language is learning how to express yourself.

## Memory Hook

表 (surface/show) + 达 (to reach/convey): you bring what is inside to the surface and let it reach others.
