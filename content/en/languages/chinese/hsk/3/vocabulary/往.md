## 往 (wǎng)

往 is a preposition meaning toward or in the direction of. It indicates the direction in which movement or action proceeds. It often precedes directional words like 前 (forward), 后 (backward), 左 (left), 右 (right), and 上 (up). Compare with 向 (xiàng), which is very similar but slightly more formal.

## Meanings

1. **[preposition]** Toward; in the direction of; going to.

## Example Sentences

**往前走一百米就到了。**
Wǎng qián zǒu yībǎi mǐ jiù dào le.
Walk forward one hundred meters and you will arrive.

**请往右看。**
Qǐng wǎng yòu kàn.
Please look to the right.

**公共汽车往北开。**
Gōnggòng qìchē wǎng běi kāi.
The bus goes toward the north.

**往这边走，别走错了。**
Wǎng zhè biān zǒu, bié zǒu cuò le.
Go this way; do not take the wrong path.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 往前 | forward, ahead |
| 往后 | backward, in the future |
| 往左/右 | to the left/right |
| 往上/下 | upward/downward |
| 往来 | to come and go; contacts |

## Memory Hook

往 shows a person (彳, a walking radical) with a king (王) ahead. You walk toward where the king is. 往 always points your feet in a specific direction.
