## 照片 (zhàopiàn)

照片 means photograph or photo. It refers to any picture taken by a camera, whether printed or digital.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Photograph; photo; picture.

## Example Sentences

**这张照片拍得很好看。**
Zhè zhāng zhàopiàn pāi de hěn hǎokàn.
This photo turned out really nice.

**我想看你小时候的照片。**
Wǒ xiǎng kàn nǐ xiǎo shíhou de zhàopiàn.
I want to see your childhood photos.

**她把照片发给了我。**
Tā bǎ zhàopiàn fā gěi le wǒ.
She sent the photo to me.

**我们一起拍张照片吧！**
Wǒmen yīqǐ pāi zhāng zhàopiàn ba!
Let's take a photo together!

## Memory Hook

照 means to shine or illuminate (like a camera flash), and 片 means a flat piece or slice. A photo is a "light slice" capturing a frozen moment.
