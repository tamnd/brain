## 检查 (jiǎnchá)

检查 means to check, inspect, or examine. It is widely used for medical checkups, reviewing work, security checks, and quality inspection. 检 means to inspect, and 查 means to look into or investigate.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To check; to examine; to inspect (homework, health, luggage, quality).
2. **[noun]** An inspection; a checkup; a review.

## Example Sentences

**老师让我们检查作业有没有错误。**
Lǎoshī ràng wǒmen jiǎnchá zuòyè yǒu méiyǒu cuòwù.
The teacher asked us to check our homework for mistakes.

**医生给他做了全面检查。**
Yīshēng gěi tā zuò le quánmiàn jiǎnchá.
The doctor gave him a thorough medical examination.

**上飞机前要检查证件。**
Shàng fēijī qián yào jiǎnchá zhèngjiàn.
You need to have your documents checked before boarding the plane.

**定期检查身体对健康很重要。**
Dìngqī jiǎnchá shēntǐ duì jiànkāng hěn zhòngyào.
Regular physical checkups are very important for your health.

## Memory Hook

检 (inspect) plus 查 (investigate). Checking something is a two-step process: inspect it up close, then investigate what you find. Both steps together make a thorough 检查.
