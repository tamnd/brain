## 说清楚 (shuō qīngchǔ)

说清楚 is a verb-complement structure. 说 (to speak / say) is the base verb and 清楚 (clear / clearly) is the result complement, showing that the act of speaking achieves clarity. The negative form 说不清楚 means "can't explain clearly." 没说清楚 means "didn't explain clearly."

## Structure

说 (speak) + 清楚 (result complement: clear) = speak with the result of being clear.

Other complements that can follow 说: 说完 (finish saying), 说对 (say correctly), 说错 (say incorrectly).

## Example Sentences

**请你把这件事说清楚。**
Qǐng nǐ bǎ zhè jiàn shì shuō qīngchǔ.
Please explain this matter clearly.

**他说不清楚自己的想法。**
Tā shuō bù qīngchǔ zìjǐ de xiǎngfǎ.
He cannot clearly express his own thoughts.

**我没说清楚，让你误会了。**
Wǒ méi shuō qīngchǔ, ràng nǐ wùhuì le.
I did not explain it clearly and caused you to misunderstand.

**你能不能说清楚，我听不太懂。**
Nǐ néng bù néng shuō qīngchǔ, wǒ tīng bù tài dǒng.
Can you say it clearly? I don't quite understand.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 说清楚了 | explained clearly (completed) |
| 说不清楚 | cannot explain clearly |
| 把...说清楚 | explain... clearly (with 把) |
| 没说清楚 | did not explain clearly |
| 请说清楚 | please say it clearly |

## Memory Hook

说 is your mouth working. 清楚 is a bright, unmuddied view (清 means clear water; 楚 means distinct). When you 说清楚, your words are like clear water — the listener can see straight to the bottom.
