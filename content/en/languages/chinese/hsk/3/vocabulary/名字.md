## 名字 (míngzi)

名字 refers to the name of a person or thing, typically the given name or full name used to identify someone.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** name; given name; first name

## Example Sentences

**你叫什么名字？** Nǐ jiào shénme míngzi? What is your name?

**我的名字叫李明，很高兴认识你。** Wǒ de míngzi jiào Lǐ Míng, hěn gāoxìng rènshi nǐ. My name is Li Ming; nice to meet you.

**请在这里写上你的名字。** Qǐng zài zhèlǐ xiě shàng nǐ de míngzi. Please write your name here.

**她忘了那位老师的名字。** Tā wàngle nà wèi lǎoshī de míngzi. She forgot that teacher's name.

## Memory Hook

名 means "fame/name" (mouth + evening — calling your name aloud at dusk) and 字 means "written character" — your 名字 is both the sound of your name and the characters that write it.
