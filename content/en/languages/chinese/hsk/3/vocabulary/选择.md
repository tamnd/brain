## 选择 (xuǎnzé)

选择 means to choose or select from available options. It functions as both a verb and a noun, covering everyday decisions and formal selections alike.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To choose; to select; to pick.
2. **[noun]** A choice; a selection; an option.

## Example Sentences

**你选择哪个答案？**
Nǐ xuǎnzé nǎge dá'àn?
Which answer do you choose?

**这是你自己的选择。**
Zhè shì nǐ zìjǐ de xuǎnzé.
This is your own choice.

**我选择留在这里工作。**
Wǒ xuǎnzé liú zài zhèlǐ gōngzuò.
I chose to stay here and work.

**面对很多选择，他不知道怎么办。**
Miànduì hěn duō xuǎnzé, tā bù zhīdào zěnme bàn.
Faced with many choices, he didn't know what to do.

## Memory Hook

选 (to select by separating — 走 going + 巽 choosing) plus 择 (to pick out). Both characters relate to singling out the right one from a group.
