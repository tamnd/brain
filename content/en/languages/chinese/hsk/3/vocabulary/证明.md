## 证明 (zhèngmíng)

证明 functions as both a verb and a noun. As a verb it means to prove or demonstrate that something is true. As a noun it refers to a certificate, letter of proof, or other official document that verifies a fact.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To prove; to demonstrate; to show evidence that something is true.
2. **[noun]** Proof; certificate; verification document.

## Example Sentences

**你能证明你当时在家吗？**
Nǐ néng zhèngmíng nǐ dāngshí zài jiā ma?
Can you prove that you were home at the time?

**他的成绩证明了他的努力。**
Tā de chéngjì zhèngmíng le tā de nǔlì.
His results proved his hard work.

**请出示你的工作证明。**
Qǐng chūshì nǐ de gōngzuò zhèngmíng.
Please show your employment certificate.

**事实证明，她是对的。**
Shìshí zhèngmíng, tā shì duì de.
The facts proved she was right.

## Memory Hook

证 contains the speech radical 讠 and means "testimony." 明 means "clear / bright." Together: making something bright and clear through testimony — that is proof.
