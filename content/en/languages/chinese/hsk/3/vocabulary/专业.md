## 专业 (zhuānyè)

专业 refers to a field of specialization, such as a university major, a professional trade, or the quality of being highly skilled and expert.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** a major or field of study at a university or college
2. **[noun]** a specialty; a specific professional skill or area of expertise
3. **[adjective]** professional; specialized; expert-level

## Example Sentences

**你的专业是什么？** Nǐ de zhuānyè shì shénme? What is your major?

**他是专业的厨师，做的菜非常好吃。** Tā shì zhuānyè de chúshī, zuò de cài fēicháng hǎochī. He is a professional chef; the food he makes is delicious.

**我学的专业是计算机科学。** Wǒ xué de zhuānyè shì jìsuànjī kēxué. The major I am studying is computer science.

**这家公司提供专业的法律服务。** Zhè jiā gōngsī tígōng zhuānyè de fǎlǜ fúwù. This company provides professional legal services.

## Memory Hook

Think of 专 (focused) plus 业 (work or industry): someone who focuses all their energy on one line of work is a true 专业 professional.
