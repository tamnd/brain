## 偶尔 (ǒu'ěr)

An adverb meaning something happens from time to time, but not regularly — infrequently or on rare occasions.

## Meanings

1. **[adverb]** Occasionally, once in a while, now and then.
2. **[adverb]** Seldom, rarely (but not never).

## Example Sentences

**我偶尔去看电影放松一下。**
Wǒ ǒu'ěr qù kàn diànyǐng fàngsōng yīxià.
I occasionally go to watch movies to relax.

**他平时不喝酒，偶尔喝一点儿。**
Tā píngshí bù hējiǔ, ǒu'ěr hē yīdiǎnr.
He doesn't usually drink, but occasionally has a little.

**她偶尔会忘记带钥匙。**
Tā ǒu'ěr huì wàngjì dài yàoshi.
She occasionally forgets to bring her keys.

**偶尔换一种饮食方式对身体有好处。**
Ǒu'ěr huàn yī zhǒng yǐnshí fāngshì duì shēntǐ yǒu hǎochù.
Occasionally changing your eating habits is good for your health.

## Memory Hook

偶 suggests "by chance/accidental" and 尔 is a classical suffix — it is the rare accidental encounter, not a routine.
