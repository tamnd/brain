## 低 (dī)

Describes something that is physically low in height, or metaphorically below average in value, quality, or degree.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** Low, short in height (opposite of 高 gāo).
2. **[adjective]** Low (in degree, price, temperature, or level).

## Example Sentences

**今天气温很低，要穿厚衣服。**
Jīntiān qìwēn hěn dī, yào chuān hòu yīfu.
The temperature is very low today; you need to wear thick clothes.

**这里的物价比城市低得多。**
Zhèlǐ de wùjià bǐ chéngshì dī de duō.
Prices here are much lower than in the city.

**他说话的声音很低，我听不清楚。**
Tā shuōhuà de shēngyīn hěn dī, wǒ tīng bù qīngchǔ.
His voice is very low; I can't hear clearly.

**这个椅子太低了，不舒服。**
Zhège yǐzi tài dī le, bù shūfu.
This chair is too low; it's not comfortable.
