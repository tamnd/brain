## 请假 (qǐngjià)

请假 is a verb meaning to formally request time off from work, school, or another obligation. 请 means "to request" and 假 here means "leave / time off" (not "fake"). It is used any time you need to notify a supervisor or teacher that you will be absent.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To ask for leave; to request time off; to take a day off (with permission).

## Example Sentences

**我明天要请假去看医生。**
Wǒ míngtiān yào qǐngjià qù kàn yīshēng.
I need to take tomorrow off to see the doctor.

**他向老师请了两天假。**
Tā xiàng lǎoshī qǐng le liǎng tiān jià.
He asked the teacher for two days off.

**请假需要提前告诉老板。**
Qǐngjià xūyào tíqián gàosù lǎobǎn.
You need to inform your boss in advance when taking leave.

**她感冒了，所以请假在家休息。**
Tā gǎnmào le, suǒyǐ qǐngjià zài jiā xiūxi.
She had a cold, so she took a day off to rest at home.

## Memory Hook

请 means "to invite / request" (speech radical 讠 plus 青 "blue-green / youth"). 假 means "holiday / leave." You are requesting that a holiday be granted to you — politely inviting your boss to let you go.
