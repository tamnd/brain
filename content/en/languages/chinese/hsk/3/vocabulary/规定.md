## 规定 (guīdìng)

规定 refers to a rule, regulation, or stipulation set by an authority. As a verb it means to stipulate or prescribe. It describes something that has been formally decided and must be followed.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Rule; regulation; stipulation; provision.
2. **[verb]** To stipulate; to prescribe; to specify.

## Example Sentences

**你必须遵守学校的规定。**
Nǐ bìxū zūnshǒu xuéxiào de guīdìng.
You must follow the school rules.

**规定不允许在这里停车。**
Guīdìng bù yǔnxǔ zài zhèlǐ tíngchē.
The regulations do not allow parking here.

**公司规定每天九点开始上班。**
Gōngsī guīdìng měitiān jiǔ diǎn kāishǐ shàngbān.
The company stipulates that work begins at nine every day.

**按照规定，你需要提前预约。**
ànzhào guīdìng, nǐ xūyào tíqián yùyuē.
According to the rules, you need to make an appointment in advance.

## Memory Hook

规 (compass — the tool that draws circles within limits) plus 定 (to fix / determine). A compass that fixes the boundary — rules draw the circle you must stay within.
