## 经过 (jīngguò)

经过 means to pass by a place or go through a process. As a preposition it introduces the means or process through which a result was achieved.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To pass by; to pass through (a place or process).
2. **[preposition]** After; through; as a result of (introducing a process).

## Example Sentences

**我每天上学都要经过那个公园。**
Wǒ měitiān shàngxué dōu yào jīngguò nàgè gōngyuán.
I pass by that park every day on my way to school.

**经过努力，他终于成功了。**
Jīngguò nǔlì, tā zhōngyú chénggōng le.
After working hard, he finally succeeded.

**这条路经过市中心。**
Zhè tiáo lù jīngguò shì zhōngxīn.
This road passes through the city center.

**经过多次讨论，大家达成了一致意见。**
Jīngguò duō cì tǎolùn, dàjiā dáchéng le yīzhì yìjiàn.
After many discussions, everyone reached a consensus.

## Memory Hook

经 (silk threads running through fabric) plus 过 (to pass). Like thread passing through cloth — you go through something and come out the other side.
