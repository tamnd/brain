## 耐心 (nàixīn)

耐心 describes the ability to remain calm and composed while waiting or dealing with something slow or difficult.

## Meanings

1. **[adj/noun]** patient; patience; the ability to endure delay or difficulty without losing composure

## Example Sentences

**老师很耐心地给我们解释每一道题。** Lǎoshī hěn nàixīn de gěi wǒmen jiěshì měi yī dào tí. The teacher patiently explained every question to us.

**做这份工作需要很大的耐心。** Zuò zhè fèn gōngzuò xūyào hěn dà de nàixīn. This job requires a great deal of patience.

**请耐心等待，我们马上就好。** Qǐng nàixīn děngdài, wǒmen mǎshàng jiù hǎo. Please wait patiently; we will be ready very soon.

**跟小孩子说话要有耐心。** Gēn xiǎo háizi shuōhuà yào yǒu nàixīn. You need to have patience when talking to young children.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 有耐心 | to have patience |
| 缺乏耐心 | to lack patience |
| 耐心等待 | to wait patiently |
| 耐心解释 | to explain patiently |

## Memory Hook

耐 means "to endure/tolerate" and 心 means "heart." Patience is a heart that can endure and keep steady through difficulty.
