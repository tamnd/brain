## 继续 (jìxù)

继续 means to continue doing something or to carry on without stopping, picking up where one left off.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to continue; to go on; to carry on; to keep doing

## Example Sentences

**请继续说，我在听。** Qǐng jìxù shuō, wǒ zài tīng. Please continue speaking; I am listening.

**休息一下，然后继续工作。** Xiūxi yīxià, ránhòu jìxù gōngzuò. Take a rest, then continue working.

**尽管很难，他还是继续努力了。** Jǐnguǎn hěn nán, tā háishi jìxù nǔlì le. Even though it was difficult, he continued to work hard.

**她决定继续她的学业。** Tā juédìng jìxù tā de xuéyè. She decided to continue her studies.

## Memory Hook

继 means "to succeed/follow" and 续 means "to continue/connect" — 继续 is like adding a new thread that follows and connects to the one before it.
