## 感激 (gǎnjī)

感激 expresses a deep sense of gratitude, stronger and more formal than simply saying thank you.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to be grateful; to feel gratitude; to appreciate deeply

## Example Sentences

**我非常感激您对我的帮助。** Wǒ fēicháng gǎnjī nín duì wǒ de bāngzhù. I am deeply grateful for your help.

**她感激地看着老师，说不出话来。** Tā gǎnjī de kànzhe lǎoshī, shuō bù chū huà lái. She looked at the teacher with gratitude, unable to say a word.

**他对所有帮助过他的人都感激不尽。** Tā duì suǒyǒu bāngzhù guò tā de rén dōu gǎnjī bù jìn. He is endlessly grateful to everyone who has helped him.

**我们应该感激父母为我们所做的一切。** Wǒmen yīnggāi gǎnjī fùmǔ wèi wǒmen suǒ zuò de yīqiē. We should be grateful for everything our parents do for us.

## Memory Hook

感 (to feel) + 激 (to stir/arouse): gratitude is a feeling so strong it stirs and moves your heart deeply.
