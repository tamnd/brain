## 单位 (dānwèi)

单位 commonly refers to one's work unit or employer organization in China, and can also mean a unit of measurement.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** work unit; organization; employer
2. **[noun]** unit (of measurement)

## Example Sentences

**他的单位在市中心。** Tā de dānwèi zài shì zhōngxīn. His workplace is in the city center.

**你在哪个单位工作？** Nǐ zài nǎge dānwèi gōngzuò? Which organization do you work for?

**这道数学题要注意单位的换算。** Zhè dào shùxué tí yào zhùyì dānwèi de huànsuàn. In this math problem, pay attention to the unit conversion.

**单位给我们安排了住房。** Dānwèi gěi wǒmen ānpái le zhùfáng. The work unit arranged housing for us.

## Memory Hook

单 (single/individual) + 位 (position/place): your single designated position in an organization is your 单位.
