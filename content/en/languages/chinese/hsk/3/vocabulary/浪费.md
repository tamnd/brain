## 浪费 (làngfèi)

浪费 means to use resources, time, or money carelessly, using more than is necessary.

## Meanings

1. **[verb/adj]** to waste; to squander; wasteful

## Example Sentences

**不要浪费食物，吃多少做多少。** Bùyào làngfèi shíwù, chī duōshao zuò duōshao. Don't waste food; only make as much as you will eat.

**浪费时间就是浪费生命。** Làngfèi shíjiān jiùshì làngfèi shēngmìng. Wasting time is wasting your life.

**买了不用的东西是一种浪费。** Mǎi le bùyòng de dōngxi shì yī zhǒng làngfèi. Buying things you won't use is a form of waste.

**他觉得这个会议很浪费时间。** Tā juéde zhège huìyì hěn làngfèi shíjiān. He felt that this meeting was a waste of time.

## Memory Hook

浪 means "wave/wild" and 费 means "expense." Wasting is letting your expenses run as wild as ocean waves.
