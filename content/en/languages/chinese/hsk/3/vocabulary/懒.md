## 懒 (lǎn)

懒 describes a person who is unwilling to exert effort and avoids work or activity.

## Meanings

1. **[adj]** lazy; idle; disinclined to work or make effort

## Example Sentences

**他太懒了，作业总是不做。** Tā tài lǎn le, zuòyè zǒng shì bù zuò. He is too lazy and never does his homework.

**今天天气很好，我却懒得出门。** Jīntiān tiānqì hěn hǎo, wǒ què lǎn de chūmén. The weather is nice today but I am too lazy to go out.

**你不能这么懒，要努力才会成功。** Nǐ bùnéng zhème lǎn, yào nǔlì cái huì chénggōng. You cannot be this lazy; you need to work hard to succeed.

**周末的早上，她喜欢懒懒地睡到很晚。** Zhōumò de zǎoshang, tā xǐhuān lǎn lǎn de shuì dào hěn wǎn. On weekend mornings, she likes to lazily sleep in very late.

## Memory Hook

The heart radical (忄) on the left of 懒 shows this is an emotion: a heart that just does not want to move.
