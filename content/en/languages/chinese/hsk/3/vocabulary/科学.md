## 科学 (kēxué)

科学 means science as a noun and scientific as an adjective. It encompasses both the natural sciences and the general idea of rational, evidence-based thinking.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Science; the natural sciences.
2. **[adjective]** Scientific; rational; evidence-based.

## Example Sentences

**科学对人类的发展很重要。**
Kēxué duì rénlèi de fāzhǎn hěn zhòngyào.
Science is very important for human development.

**这种方法不太科学。**
Zhè zhǒng fāngfǎ bú tài kēxué.
This method is not very scientific.

**她从小就对科学很感兴趣。**
Tā cóng xiǎo jiù duì kēxué hěn gǎn xìngqù.
She has been interested in science since childhood.

**我们要用科学的方法解决问题。**
Wǒmen yào yòng kēxué de fāngfǎ jiějué wèntí.
We should use scientific methods to solve problems.

## Memory Hook

科 means a branch of study or department, and 学 means to study or learning. 科学 is literally the study of branches of knowledge — the systematic pursuit of understanding across all fields of inquiry.
