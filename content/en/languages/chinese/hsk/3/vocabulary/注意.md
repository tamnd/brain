## 注意 (zhùyì)

注意 means to pay attention or be careful. It is widely used in instructions, warnings, and advice, both as a command and as a statement about directing one's focus.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To pay attention to; to watch out for; to be careful about.
2. **[noun]** Attention; notice.

## Example Sentences

**注意安全！**
Zhùyì ānquán!
Be careful! / Watch out for safety!

**请注意听老师说话。**
Qǐng zhùyì tīng lǎoshī shuōhuà.
Please pay attention and listen to the teacher.

**他没有注意到那个错误。**
Tā méiyǒu zhùyì dào nàgè cuòwù.
He didn't notice that mistake.

**学习汉字时，要注意笔顺。**
Xuéxí Hànzì shí, yào zhùyì bǐshùn.
When learning Chinese characters, pay attention to stroke order.

## Memory Hook

注 means "to pour / focus" (water radical). 意 means "meaning / intention." Pour your entire intention into something — that is what it means to pay attention.
