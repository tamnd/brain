## 糖 (táng)

糖 means both sugar as an ingredient and candy or sweets as a treat. Context determines which is meant, though both meanings share the same character.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Sugar (the ingredient; granulated, white, brown).
2. **[noun]** Candy; sweets; a piece of sweet confectionery.

## Example Sentences

**这个蛋糕放了太多糖。**
Zhège dàngāo fàng le tài duō táng.
This cake has too much sugar in it.

**小孩子都喜欢吃糖。**
Xiǎo háizi dōu xǐhuan chī táng.
Children all love eating candy.

**医生说我不能吃太多糖。**
Yīshēng shuō wǒ bù néng chī tài duō táng.
The doctor said I cannot eat too much sugar.

**你要不要来一颗糖？**
Nǐ yào bu yào lái yī kē táng?
Would you like a piece of candy?

## Memory Hook

糖 contains 米 (rice/grain) on the left, because traditional sugar was extracted from grain or cane. The right side 唐 (táng) gives the pronunciation. Grain plus that sweet flavor equals 糖.
