## 意见 (yìjiàn)

意见 means opinion, view, or feedback. It is what someone thinks about a topic, plan, or situation. It can be a personal viewpoint or a formal objection or suggestion.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Opinion; view; feedback; objection.

## Example Sentences

**你有什么意见？**
Nǐ yǒu shénme yìjiàn?
What are your opinions?

**大家的意见不一致，还需要讨论。**
Dàjiā de yìjiàn bù yīzhì, hái xūyào tǎolùn.
Everyone's opinions are not aligned; more discussion is needed.

**请提出你的意见和建议。**
Qǐng tíchū nǐ de yìjiàn hé jiànyì.
Please share your opinions and suggestions.

**他对这个决定有不同的意见。**
Tā duì zhège juédìng yǒu bùtóng de yìjiàn.
He has a different opinion about this decision.

意 (meaning / intention — sound 音 from the heart 心) plus 见 (to see). The view that comes from seeing with your heart — your inner opinion made visible.
