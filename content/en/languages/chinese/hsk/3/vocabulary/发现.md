## 发现 (fāxiàn)

发现 means to discover or find out something that was previously unknown or unnoticed. It can refer to physical findings, realizations, or new knowledge.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To discover; to find; to notice; to realize.
2. **[noun]** A discovery; a finding.

## Example Sentences

**我发现了一个好方法。**
Wǒ fāxiàn le yīgè hǎo fāngfǎ.
I discovered a good method.

**他发现钱包不见了。**
Tā fāxiàn qiánbāo bú jiàn le.
He discovered that his wallet was missing.

**科学家发现了一种新物质。**
Kēxuéjiā fāxiàn le yī zhǒng xīn wùzhì.
Scientists discovered a new substance.

**我最近发现她越来越认真了。**
Wǒ zuìjìn fāxiàn tā yuè lái yuè rènzhēn le.
I recently noticed she has been getting more and more serious.

## Memory Hook

发 means "to send out / emit." 现 means "appear." Something sends itself out and appears before your eyes — that is the moment of discovery.
