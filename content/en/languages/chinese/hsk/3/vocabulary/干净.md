## 干净 (gānjìng)

干净 describes something that is free from dirt, stains, or clutter, and can apply to both physical objects and abstract things like speech or actions.

## Meanings

1. **[adj]** clean; free from dirt or impurities
2. **[adj]** neat; tidy; uncluttered

## Example Sentences

**这个房间非常干净。** Zhège fángjiān fēicháng gānjìng. This room is very clean.

**她每天都把桌子擦干净。** Tā měitiān dōu bǎ zhuōzi cā gānjìng. She wipes the table clean every day.

**请把手洗干净再吃饭。** Qǐng bǎ shǒu xǐ gānjìng zài chīfàn. Please wash your hands clean before eating.

**空气很干净，天空也很蓝。** Kōngqì hěn gānjìng, tiānkōng yě hěn lán. The air is clean and the sky is also very blue.

## Memory Hook

Think of "干" (dry) plus "净" (pure): something that is both dry and pure is perfectly clean.
