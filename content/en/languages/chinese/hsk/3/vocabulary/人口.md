## 人口 (rénkǒu)

Refers to the total number of people living in a country, city, or region.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Population, the total number of inhabitants.
2. **[noun]** Number of people (in a household or area).

## Example Sentences

**中国的人口超过十四亿。**
Zhōngguó de rénkǒu chāoguò shísì yì.
China's population exceeds 1.4 billion.

**这个城市的人口增长很快。**
Zhège chéngshì de rénkǒu zēngzhǎng hěn kuài.
The population of this city is growing very fast.

**农村人口越来越少了。**
Nóngcūn rénkǒu yuèláiyuè shǎo le.
The rural population is getting smaller and smaller.

**世界人口已经超过八十亿。**
Shìjiè rénkǒu yǐjīng chāoguò bāshí yì.
The world's population has already exceeded 8 billion.

## Memory Hook

人 is "person" and 口 is "mouth" — count the mouths to feed and you get the population.
