## 项目 (xiàngmù)

项目 refers to a project or task that is organized and planned, as well as to an individual item or entry in a list. In work and school contexts it most often means a project (research project, work project, sports event). In administrative contexts it refers to a line item or category.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Project; program; planned undertaking.
2. **[noun]** Item; entry (in a list, form, or schedule).

## Example Sentences

**我们正在做一个新的项目。**
Wǒmen zhèngzài zuò yīgè xīn de xiàngmù.
We are working on a new project.

**这个项目需要三个月才能完成。**
Zhège xiàngmù xūyào sān gè yuè cái néng wánchéng.
This project will take three months to complete.

**运动会有很多比赛项目。**
Yùndònghuì yǒu hěn duō bǐsài xiàngmù.
The sports meet has many competition events.

**请认真填写表格上的每一个项目。**
Qǐng rènzhēn tiánxiě biǎogé shàng de měi yīgè xiàngmù.
Please carefully fill in every item on the form.

## Memory Hook

项 has the phonetic component 工 (work) and 页 (page/head). 目 means "eye / item." A 项目 is an item that deserves its own line — an eye-catching entry with work attached.
