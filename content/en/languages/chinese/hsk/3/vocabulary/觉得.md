## 觉得 (juéde)

觉得 expresses a subjective feeling, opinion, or impression about something, equivalent to "feel that" or "think that" in English.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to feel (that); to think (that); to find something to be a certain way

## Example Sentences

**我觉得这道题太难了。** Wǒ juéde zhè dào tí tài nán le. I think this question is too difficult.

**你觉得这家餐厅怎么样？** Nǐ juéde zhè jiā cāntīng zěnmeyàng? What do you think of this restaurant?

**她觉得有点累，想休息一下。** Tā juéde yǒudiǎn lèi, xiǎng xiūxi yīxià. She feels a little tired and wants to rest.

**我觉得学中文其实很有意思。** Wǒ juéde xué Zhōngwén qíshí hěn yǒuyìsi. I think learning Chinese is actually very interesting.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 觉得好 | to feel good; to think it is good |
| 觉得奇怪 | to find it strange |
| 觉得累 | to feel tired |
| 觉得有意思 | to find it interesting |
| 让人觉得 | to make someone feel |

## Memory Hook

觉 contains the "see/perceive" radical and 得 means "to get/obtain" — 觉得 is what you get when your senses perceive something: a feeling or impression.
