## 否则 (fǒuzé)

否则 means otherwise or or else — it states the negative consequence that will follow if the condition or instruction in the first clause is not fulfilled.

## Meanings

1. **[conjunction]** Otherwise; or else; if not.

## Example Sentences

**快点，否则就迟到了。**
Kuài diǎn, fǒuzé jiù chídào le.
Hurry up, otherwise you'll be late.

**你必须认真复习，否则考试会不及格。**
Nǐ bìxū rènzhēn fùxí, fǒuzé kǎoshì huì bù jígé.
You must review carefully, otherwise you'll fail the exam.

**记得带雨伞，否则会被淋湿。**
Jìde dài yǔsǎn, fǒuzé huì bèi lín shī.
Remember to bring an umbrella, or else you'll get wet.

**请按时完成，否则会影响整个计划。**
Qǐng ànshí wánchéng, fǒuzé huì yǐngxiǎng zhěnggè jìhuà.
Please finish on time, otherwise it will affect the whole plan.

## Memory Hook

否 (negate / no — a mouth 口 under something being denied) plus 则 (rule / then). The rule of negation: what happens when you break the condition — the "or else" rule.
