## 建议 (jiànyì)

建议 means to suggest or recommend, and as a noun it refers to the suggestion itself. It is polite and constructive, used for offering ideas or advice to someone who can choose to accept them.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To suggest; to recommend; to propose.
2. **[noun]** A suggestion; a recommendation; advice.

## Example Sentences

**我建议你多休息。**
Wǒ jiànyì nǐ duō xiūxi.
I suggest you rest more.

**你有什么建议？**
Nǐ yǒu shénme jiànyì?
Do you have any suggestions?

**老师给了我很多有用的建议。**
Lǎoshī gěi le wǒ hěn duō yǒuyòng de jiànyì.
The teacher gave me many useful suggestions.

**我建议大家先讨论一下，然后再做决定。**
Wǒ jiànyì dàjiā xiān tǎolùn yīxià, rán hòu zài zuò juédìng.
I suggest that everyone discuss it first, and then make a decision.

## Memory Hook

建 (to build / establish) plus 议 (to discuss / deliberate). Build up a discussion — a structured proposal you have thought through and erected for others to consider.
