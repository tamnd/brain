## 汤 (tāng)

汤 means soup or broth. It refers to any hot liquid dish made by simmering ingredients. Soup is a fundamental part of Chinese meals. The character has the water radical (氵) on the left, reflecting its liquid nature.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Soup; broth; a hot liquid dish.

## Example Sentences

**妈妈今天做了一锅鸡汤。**
Māma jīntiān zuò le yī guō jītāng.
Mom made a pot of chicken soup today.

**天冷的时候喝汤很暖和。**
Tiān lěng de shíhou hē tāng hěn nuǎnhuo.
Drinking soup when it is cold is very warming.

**这碗汤太烫了，等一下再喝。**
Zhè wǎn tāng tài tàng le, děng yīxià zài hē.
This bowl of soup is too hot; wait a moment before drinking it.

**我点了一份番茄蛋花汤。**
Wǒ diǎn le yī fèn fānqié dànhuā tāng.
I ordered a tomato and egg drop soup.

## Memory Hook

汤 has the water radical (氵). Soup is essentially flavored water. When you see those three drops of water on the left, think of a steaming, fragrant bowl of 汤. In Chinese cuisine, soup is not just a first course — it is often drunk throughout the meal or at the end. Famous soups include 鸡汤 (chicken broth), 骨头汤 (pork bone soup), and 番茄蛋花汤 (tomato egg drop soup).
