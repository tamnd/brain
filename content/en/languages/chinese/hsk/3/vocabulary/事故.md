## 事故 (shìgù)

Refers to an unintended, harmful incident, most commonly a traffic accident or workplace accident.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Accident, incident (usually involving injury or damage).
2. **[noun]** Mishap, unforeseen harmful event.

## Example Sentences

**路上发生了一起交通事故。**
Lù shàng fāshēng le yī qǐ jiāotōng shìgù.
A traffic accident occurred on the road.

**工厂里发生了安全事故。**
Gōngchǎng lǐ fāshēng le ānquán shìgù.
A safety accident occurred in the factory.

**为了防止事故，我们要小心驾驶。**
Wèile fángzhǐ shìgù, wǒmen yào xiǎoxīn jiàshǐ.
In order to prevent accidents, we should drive carefully.

**那次事故造成了很大的损失。**
Nà cì shìgù zàochéng le hěn dà de sǔnshī.
That accident caused a great deal of loss.

## Memory Hook

事 means "matter/event" and 故 means "old/cause/incident" — an unexpected old-style disruption to normal matters.
