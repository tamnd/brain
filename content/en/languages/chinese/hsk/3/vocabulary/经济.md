## 经济 (jīngjì)

经济 refers to the economy or the study of economics, and can also function as an adjective meaning economical or affordable.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** economy; economics — the system of production, trade, and consumption of goods and services
2. **[adjective]** financial; economical — relating to finance, or cost-effective

## Example Sentences

**中国经济发展很快。** Zhōngguó jīngjì fāzhǎn hěn kuài. China's economy is developing very quickly.

**他学习经济学。** Tā xuéxí jīngjìxué. He studies economics.

**这个方案更经济实惠。** Zhège fāng'àn gèng jīngjì shíhuì. This option is more economical and cost-effective.

**全球经济受到了很大影响。** Quánqiú jīngjì shòudào le hěn dà yǐngxiǎng. The global economy was greatly affected.

## Collocations

## Memory Hook

经 (to manage; to pass through) plus 济 (to help; to benefit) — managing resources to benefit people: that is 经济.
