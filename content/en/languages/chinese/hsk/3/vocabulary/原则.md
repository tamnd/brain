## 原则 (yuánzé)

原则 refers to a fundamental principle, rule, or standard that guides one's behavior or decisions.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** principle; fundamental rule; standard

## Example Sentences

**诚实是他做人的基本原则。** Chéngshí shì tā zuòrén de jīběn yuánzé. Honesty is his basic principle in life.

**我们做事必须遵守一定的原则。** Wǒmen zuòshì bìxū zūnshǒu yīdìng de yuánzé. We must follow certain principles in what we do.

**这个问题在原则上没有问题。** Zhège wèntí zài yuánzé shàng méiyǒu wèntí. In principle, there is no problem with this matter.

**他坚持自己的原则，从不妥协。** Tā jiānchí zìjǐ de yuánzé, cóng bù tuǒxié. He sticks to his principles and never compromises.

## Memory Hook

原 means "original/foundational" and 则 means "rule" — a 原则 is a foundational rule you live by.
