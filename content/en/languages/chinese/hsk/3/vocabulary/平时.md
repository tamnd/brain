## 平时 (píngshí)

平时 refers to ordinary or usual times, as opposed to special occasions or holidays. It means "normally" or "on a typical day," describing habitual behavior during regular periods.

## Meanings

1. **[adverb/noun]** Usually; normally; ordinarily; in ordinary times; on a regular day.

## Example Sentences

**平时你几点起床？**
Píngshí nǐ jǐ diǎn qǐchuáng?
What time do you usually get up?

**他平时不喝酒，但是今天喝了一点。**
Tā píngshí bù hē jiǔ, dànshì jīntiān hē le yīdiǎn.
He doesn't usually drink, but today he had a little.

**平时很安静的她，今天突然说了很多话。**
Píngshí hěn ānjìng de tā, jīntiān tūrán shuō le hěn duō huà.
She, who is usually very quiet, suddenly talked a lot today.

**平时多积累词汇，考试时就不会慌张。**
Píngshí duō jīlěi cíhuì, kǎoshì shí jiù bú huì huāngzhāng.
If you accumulate vocabulary regularly, you won't panic during the exam.

## Memory Hook

平 (flat / normal — a flat surface with nothing sticking up) plus 时 (time). Flat, ordinary time — the calm, unremarkable days that make up most of life.
