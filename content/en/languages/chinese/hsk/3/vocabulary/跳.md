## 跳 (tiào)

跳 describes the action of jumping or leaping, and extends to dancing and skipping.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to jump; to leap; to spring
2. **[verb]** to dance (as in 跳舞)

## Example Sentences

**那只猫跳上了桌子。** Nà zhī māo tiào shàng le zhuōzi. That cat jumped onto the table.

**孩子们在草地上跳来跳去。** Háizimen zài cǎodì shàng tiào lái tiào qù. The children were jumping around on the grass.

**她每天早上都跳绳锻炼。** Tā měitiān zǎoshang dōu tiào shéng duànliàn. She jumps rope every morning for exercise.

**他们在舞台上跳了一支很美的舞。** Tāmen zài wǔtái shàng tiào le yī zhī hěn měi de wǔ. They performed a beautiful dance on the stage.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 跳舞 | to dance |
| 跳绳 | to jump rope |
| 跳高 | high jump |
| 跳远 | long jump |

## Memory Hook

跳 has the foot radical (足) on the left — your feet leave the ground when you 跳.
