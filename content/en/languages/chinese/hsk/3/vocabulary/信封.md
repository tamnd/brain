## 信封 (xìnfēng)

A paper container used for mailing letters, folded and sealed to enclose written correspondence.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Envelope, a paper casing for sending letters.

## Example Sentences

**请把信放进信封里，再寄出去。**
Qǐng bǎ xìn fàng jìn xìnfēng lǐ, zài jì chūqù.
Please put the letter into the envelope, then mail it.

**桌上有几个空的信封。**
Zhuō shàng yǒu jǐ gè kōng de xìnfēng.
There are a few empty envelopes on the table.

**他在信封上写了收件人的地址。**
Tā zài xìnfēng shàng xiě le shōujiànrén de dìzhǐ.
He wrote the recipient's address on the envelope.

**妈妈把钱装进红信封里给孩子们。**
Māma bǎ qián zhuāng jìn hóng xìnfēng lǐ gěi háizimen.
Mom put money into red envelopes for the children.

## Memory Hook

信 means "letter" and 封 means "to seal/envelope" — the sealed container for a letter is the envelope.
