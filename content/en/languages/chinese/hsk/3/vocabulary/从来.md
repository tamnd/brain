## 从来 (cónglái)

An adverb always used with a negative word (不 or 没有) to express that something has never happened or has consistently not been the case.

## Meanings

1. **[adverb]** Never (used with 不 for habits: 从来不).
2. **[adverb]** Never/not once (used with 没有 for past events: 从来没有).

## Example Sentences

**他从来不吃辣的食物。**
Tā cónglái bù chī là de shíwù.
He never eats spicy food.

**我从来没有去过非洲。**
Wǒ cónglái méiyǒu qùguò Fēizhōu.
I have never been to Africa.

**她从来不迟到。**
Tā cónglái bù chídào.
She never arrives late.

**他从来没有说过谎。**
Tā cónglái méiyǒu shuō guò huǎng.
He has never told a lie.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 从来不 | never (habitual negation) |
| 从来没有 | never have (past negation) |
| 从来不…过 | have never done (experience) |
| 从来如此 | always been this way (rare positive use) |
| 从来没想到 | never imagined |

## Memory Hook

从来 literally means "from the very beginning coming here" — if you follow that whole long path and find nothing, the answer has always been "never."
