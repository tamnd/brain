## 放心 (fàngxīn)

放心 means to let go of worry and feel at ease about something or someone.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to rest assured; to stop worrying; to feel relieved

## Example Sentences

**你放心，我会照顾好她的。** Nǐ fàngxīn, wǒ huì zhàogù hǎo tā de. You can rest assured, I will take good care of her.

**妈妈说放心吧，一切都会好的。** Māma shuō fàngxīn ba, yīqiē dōu huì hǎo de. Mom said don't worry, everything will be fine.

**听到他平安到达，我才放心了。** Tīng dào tā píng'ān dàodá, wǒ cái fàngxīn le. Only after hearing he arrived safely did I feel relieved.

**你放心去吧，家里的事情我来处理。** Nǐ fàngxīn qù ba, jiālǐ de shìqing wǒ lái chǔlǐ. Go without worry, I will handle things at home.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 放心去做 | go ahead and do it without worry |
| 请放心 | please rest assured |
| 不放心 | to be worried; uneasy |
| 放心不下 | unable to stop worrying |

## Memory Hook

Imagine placing (放) your heart (心) down on a soft cushion: you have set your worries aside and can finally relax.
