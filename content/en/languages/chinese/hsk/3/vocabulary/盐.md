## 盐 (yán)

盐 means salt. It refers to the common mineral used in cooking and food seasoning, as well as in broader chemical contexts meaning salts.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Salt; table salt; sodium chloride.

## Example Sentences

**这道菜放的盐太多了。**
Zhè dào cài fàng de yán tài duō le.
This dish has too much salt in it.

**做汤的时候要加一点儿盐。**
Zuò tāng de shíhou yào jiā yīdiǎnr yán.
When making soup you need to add a little salt.

**医生说他吃的盐太多，要少吃。**
Yīshēng shuō tā chī de yán tài duō, yào shǎo chī.
The doctor said he eats too much salt and should eat less.

**请把盐递给我。**
Qǐng bǎ yán dì gěi wǒ.
Please pass the salt to me.

## Memory Hook

The character 盐 has 皿 (a vessel) at the bottom, like a dish of salt on the table. Salt was historically so valuable it was kept in dedicated containers. Think of a salt dish waiting to be passed around the dinner table.
