## 难过 (nánguò)

难过 expresses the feeling of sadness, distress, or emotional discomfort, and can also describe physical discomfort.

## Meanings

1. **[adj]** sad; unhappy; to feel bad; distressed

## Example Sentences

**他离开的时候，我感到很难过。** Tā líkāi de shíhou, wǒ gǎndào hěn nánguò. When he left, I felt very sad.

**看到别人受苦，她心里难过极了。** Kàn dào biérén shòukǔ, tā xīnlǐ nánguò jí le. Seeing others suffer, she felt extremely sad inside.

**不要难过，每件事都会过去的。** Bùyào nánguò, měi jiàn shì dōu huì guòqù de. Don't feel bad; everything will pass.

**失去好朋友让我难过了好几天。** Shīqù hǎo péngyou ràng wǒ nánguò le hǎo jǐ tiān. Losing a good friend left me feeling sad for several days.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 感到难过 | to feel sad |
| 很难过 | very sad |
| 心里难过 | sad in one's heart |
| 让人难过 | to make someone feel bad |

## Memory Hook

难 means "difficult" and 过 means "to pass/get through." When you are sad, it is hard to get through the day.
