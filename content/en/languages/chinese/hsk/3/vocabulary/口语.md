## 口语 (kǒuyǔ)

口语 refers specifically to spoken language or colloquial speech, as distinguished from written language (书面语).

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** spoken language; oral language; colloquial speech
2. **[noun]** speaking ability; oral proficiency

## Example Sentences

**她的中文口语非常流利。** Tā de Zhōngwén kǒuyǔ fēicháng liúlì. Her spoken Chinese is very fluent.

**我需要多练习口语，不只是写字。** Wǒ xūyào duō liànxí kǒuyǔ, bù zhǐshì xiě zì. I need to practice speaking more, not just writing.

**这个词在口语中很常用。** Zhège cí zài kǒuyǔ zhōng hěn cháng yòng. This word is very commonly used in spoken language.

**口语考试比写作考试更让我紧张。** Kǒuyǔ kǎoshì bǐ xiězuò kǎoshì gèng ràng wǒ jǐnzhāng. The oral exam makes me more nervous than the writing exam.

## Memory Hook

口 means "mouth" and 语 means "language" — 口语 is quite literally the language that comes from your mouth.
