## 自然 (zìrán)

自然 can refer to the natural world, describe something as unforced and natural in manner, or act as an adverb meaning "of course."

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** nature; the natural world
2. **[adj]** natural; unaffected; spontaneous
3. **[adv]** naturally; of course; as a matter of course

## Example Sentences

**我们要保护大自然。** Wǒmen yào bǎohù dà zìrán. We must protect nature.

**她说话的方式很自然，让人感觉很舒服。** Tā shuōhuà de fāngshì hěn zìrán, ràng rén gǎnjué hěn shūfu. The way she speaks is very natural and makes people feel comfortable.

**只要你努力，结果自然会好的。** Zhǐyào nǐ nǔlì, jiéguǒ zìrán huì hǎo de. As long as you work hard, the result will naturally be good.

**自然界里有很多我们不了解的东西。** Zìránjiè lǐ yǒu hěn duō wǒmen bù liǎojiě de dōngxi. There are many things in the natural world that we do not understand.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 大自然 | nature; the great natural world |
| 自然界 | the natural world |
| 自然而然 | naturally; as a natural result |
| 自然科学 | natural science |

## Memory Hook

自 means "self" and 然 means "so/thus." What is natural is simply how things are by themselves, without being forced.
