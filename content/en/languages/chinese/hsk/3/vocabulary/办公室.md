## 办公室 (bàngōngshì)

办公室 is an office, a place where administrative or professional work is carried out.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** office; a place where people work at desks

## Example Sentences

**他的办公室在三楼。** Tā de bàngōngshì zài sān lóu. His office is on the third floor.

**我每天八点到办公室上班。** Wǒ měitiān bā diǎn dào bàngōngshì shàngbān. I arrive at the office every day at eight o'clock.

**办公室里有很多同事。** Bàngōngshì lǐ yǒu hěn duō tóngshì. There are many colleagues in the office.

**这个办公室的环境非常舒适。** Zhège bàngōngshì de huánjìng fēicháng shūshì. The environment of this office is very comfortable.

## Memory Hook

办 (to handle business) + 公 (public/official) + 室 (room) = the room where you handle official business.
