## 害怕 (hàipà)

害怕 means to be afraid or to fear. It expresses a feeling of fear or fright in response to something dangerous, threatening, or unknown. It is used with 对 to say what one is afraid of, or directly before the feared thing.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To be afraid; to fear; to be scared.

## Example Sentences

**我害怕黑暗。**
Wǒ hàipà hēi'àn.
I am afraid of the dark.

**她不害怕困难。**
Tā bù hàipà kùnnan.
She is not afraid of difficulties.

**孩子们害怕打针。**
Háizimen hàipà dǎzhēn.
Children are afraid of injections.

**别害怕，我在这里。**
Bié hàipà, wǒ zài zhèlǐ.
Do not be afraid; I am here.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 不害怕 | not afraid |
| 很害怕 | very afraid |
| 害怕...吗 | are you afraid of...? |
| 对...害怕 | afraid of... |
| 害怕失败 | afraid of failure |

## Memory Hook

害 means harm or evil, and 怕 means to fear. When you sense harm nearby, 害怕 kicks in: your brain fires a warning that something harmful is close. Fear is the body's response to potential harm.
