## 比较 (bǐjiào)

比较 works both as an adverb meaning "relatively" or "comparatively" and as a verb meaning "to compare." As an adverb it softens or qualifies a description without an explicit comparison partner.

## Meanings

1. **[adverb]** Relatively; comparatively; rather; fairly.
2. **[verb]** To compare; to contrast.

## Example Sentences

**今天天气比较冷。**
Jīntiān tiānqì bǐjiào lěng.
Today's weather is relatively cold.

**我比较喜欢吃中餐。**
Wǒ bǐjiào xǐhuān chī Zhōngcān.
I rather prefer eating Chinese food.

**请比较这两种方法。**
Qǐng bǐjiào zhè liǎng zhǒng fāngfǎ.
Please compare these two methods.

**这道题比较难，需要多思考。**
Zhè dào tí bǐjiào nán, xūyào duō sīkǎo.
This question is relatively difficult and requires more thought.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 比较好 | relatively good |
| 比较一下 | compare them |
| 相比较 | in comparison |
| 经比较 | after comparison |
| 比较研究 | comparative study |

## Memory Hook

比 (side by side — two people next to each other) plus 较 (to weigh / compare). Two people standing side by side, being weighed against each other — that is comparison.
