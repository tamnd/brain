## 道理 (dàolǐ)

道理 refers to the sense or logic behind an argument, a moral truth, or a general principle that guides how things should be understood.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** reason; logic; truth; principle — the underlying rationale or moral logic of a statement, belief, or action

## Example Sentences

**你说的很有道理。** Nǐ shuō de hěn yǒu dàolǐ. What you said makes a lot of sense.

**这个道理很简单。** Zhège dàolǐ hěn jiǎndān. This principle is very simple.

**他不明白这个道理。** Tā bù míngbái zhège dàolǐ. He does not understand this reasoning.

**生活中有很多道理需要慢慢学。** Shēnghuó zhōng yǒu hěn duō dàolǐ xūyào mànmàn xué. There are many life lessons that must be learned gradually.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|

## Memory Hook

道 means "way" and 理 means "logic" — 道理 is the logical way of thinking that leads to truth.
