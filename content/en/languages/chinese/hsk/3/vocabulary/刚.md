## 刚 (gāng)

An adverb meaning something happened very recently (just now) or something barely meets a condition. Unlike 刚才 (gānɡcái, a time noun for the recent past), 刚 can modify ongoing and future-oriented statements.

## Meanings

1. **[adverb]** Just, just now (action very recently completed or still fresh).
2. **[adverb]** Barely, just enough (meeting a threshold exactly).

## Example Sentences

**我刚到家，还没来得及休息。**
Wǒ gāng dào jiā, hái méi láidejí xiūxi.
I just got home and haven't had time to rest yet.

**他刚学中文，进步很快。**
Tā gāng xué zhōngwén, jìnbù hěn kuài.
He just started learning Chinese and is progressing quickly.

**水刚好够用。**
Shuǐ gāng hǎo gòu yòng.
The water is just barely enough.

**她刚走，你来晚了一步。**
Tā gāng zǒu, nǐ lái wǎn le yī bù.
She just left; you came one step too late.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 刚到 | just arrived |
| 刚开始 | just started |
| 刚好 | just right, exactly |
| 刚刚 | just, just a moment ago |
| 刚才 | just now (past reference only) |

## Memory Hook

Think of something fresh out of the oven — barely cooled, still steaming — that is the "just now" freshness of 刚.
