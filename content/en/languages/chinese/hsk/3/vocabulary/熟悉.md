## 熟悉 (shúxī)

熟悉 means to be familiar with something through repeated contact, practice, or experience. It is the feeling of knowing something well enough that it no longer feels foreign.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To know well; to be familiar with; to be acquainted with.
2. **[adjective]** Familiar; well-known (to oneself).

## Example Sentences

**我对这个城市很熟悉。**
Wǒ duì zhège chéngshì hěn shúxī.
I am very familiar with this city.

**他熟悉这里的环境，可以带我们参观。**
Tā shúxī zhèlǐ de huánjìng, kěyǐ dài wǒmen cānguān.
He knows the environment here well and can show us around.

**多读多写，才能熟悉汉字的写法。**
Duō dú duō xiě, cái néng shúxī Hànzì de xiěfǎ.
Only by reading and writing more can you become familiar with how to write Chinese characters.

**这首歌的旋律很熟悉，我在哪里听过。**
Zhè shǒu gē de xuánlǜ hěn shúxī, wǒ zài nǎlǐ tīng guò.
This song's melody is very familiar; I've heard it somewhere before.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 对…熟悉 | be familiar with… |
| 不太熟悉 | not very familiar with |
| 越来越熟悉 | becoming more and more familiar |
| 熟悉情况 | be familiar with the situation |
| 互相熟悉 | know each other well |

## Memory Hook

熟 (cooked / ripe — fire 灬 at the bottom making something fully done) plus 悉 (to know in detail). Fully cooked knowledge — something so well-known it has been through the fire of repeated exposure.
