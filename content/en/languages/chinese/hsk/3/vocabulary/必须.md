## 必须 (bìxū)

必须 expresses a strong obligation or necessity. It means "must" or "have to," and is stronger than 应该 (yīnggāi — should). The negative form is 不必 (bùbì — don't need to) rather than 不必须.

## Meanings

1. **[modal verb]** Must; have to; it is necessary to.

## Example Sentences

**你必须按时交作业。**
Nǐ bìxū ànshí jiāo zuòyè.
You must hand in your homework on time.

**进入这里必须出示证件。**
Jìnrù zhèlǐ bìxū chūshì zhèngjiàn.
You must show identification to enter here.

**学习语言必须多练习。**
Xuéxí yǔyán bìxū duō liànxí.
Learning a language requires a lot of practice.

**他必须今天做完这件事。**
Tā bìxū jīntiān zuòwán zhè jiàn shì.
He must finish this today.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 必须要 | absolutely must |
| 不必 | don't need to / unnecessary |
| 必须注意 | must pay attention |
| 必须完成 | must complete |
| 必须遵守 | must comply with |

## Memory Hook

必 (certainly / definitely — a dagger through the center, no way around it) plus 须 (must / beard — something that grows whether you want it to or not). Unavoidable necessity on both sides.
