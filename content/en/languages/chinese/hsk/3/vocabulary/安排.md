## 安排 (ānpái)

安排 means to arrange or schedule something, and can also refer to an arrangement or plan itself.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to arrange; to organize; to schedule
2. **[noun]** an arrangement; a plan; a schedule

## Example Sentences

**老师安排我们明天去参观博物馆。** Lǎoshī ānpái wǒmen míngtiān qù cānguān bówùguǎn. The teacher arranged for us to visit the museum tomorrow.

**你今天有什么安排？** Nǐ jīntiān yǒu shénme ānpái? What are your plans for today?

**他们把婚礼安排得非常周到。** Tāmen bǎ hūnlǐ ānpái de fēicháng zhōudào. They arranged the wedding very thoughtfully.

**公司安排了一次团队活动。** Gōngsī ānpái le yī cì tuánduì huódòng. The company arranged a team-building activity.

## Memory Hook

安 means "peaceful" and 排 means "to line up": a good arrangement puts everything peacefully in a row.
