## 脖子 (bózi)

脖子 means neck. It refers to the part of the body that connects the head to the shoulders. The second syllable 子 is a neutral-tone suffix commonly added to body part words.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Neck; the connecting part between head and shoulders.

## Example Sentences

**他的脖子有点儿疼。**
Tā de bózi yǒudiǎnr téng.
His neck hurts a little.

**她围着一条漂亮的围巾在脖子上。**
Tā wéizhe yī tiáo piàoliang de wéijīn zài bózi shang.
She has a beautiful scarf wrapped around her neck.

**低头看手机时间太长，脖子会疼。**
Dītóu kàn shǒujī shíjiān tài cháng, bózi huì téng.
Looking down at your phone for too long will cause neck pain.

**猫伸长了脖子往上看。**
Māo shēncháng le bózi wǎng shàng kàn.
The cat stretched its neck to look upward.

## Memory Hook

脖 contains the body radical 月 (flesh) plus 孛 which suggests something sticking out. Your neck is the fleshy part that juts out between your head and your body. Think of a giraffe's elongated 脖子 reaching for leaves.
