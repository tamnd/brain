## 顺序 (shùnxù)

顺序 refers to the order or sequence of things — the arrangement in which steps, people, or events follow each other. It implies a proper or intended flow, often used when giving instructions, describing procedures, or talking about queues and rankings.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Order; sequence; arrangement; the correct or logical order of steps or items.

## Example Sentences

**请按顺序回答问题。**
Qǐng àn shùnxù huídá wèntí.
Please answer the questions in order.

**这些步骤的顺序不能乱。**
Zhèxiē bùzhòu de shùnxù bù néng luàn.
The sequence of these steps cannot be mixed up.

**孩子们按顺序排队进教室。**
Háizimen àn shùnxù páiduì jìn jiàoshì.
The children lined up in order to enter the classroom.

**我把文件的顺序弄错了，需要重新整理。**
Wǒ bǎ wénjiàn de shùnxù nòng cuò le, xūyào chóngxīn zhěnglǐ.
I got the order of the documents wrong and need to reorganize them.

## Memory Hook

顺 means "to follow along smoothly" and 序 means "order / preface" (the beginning that sets the flow). 顺序 is smooth, flowing order — everything following naturally from one to the next.
