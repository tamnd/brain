## 经历 (jīnglì)

经历 refers to lived experience or the act of going through something personally, emphasizing the process rather than just the knowledge gained.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** experience; personal history; what one has been through
2. **[verb]** to experience; to go through; to live through

## Example Sentences

**他有丰富的工作经历。** Tā yǒu fēngfù de gōngzuò jīnglì. He has rich work experience.

**这次旅行是我一生中最难忘的经历。** Zhè cì lǚxíng shì wǒ yīshēng zhōng zuì nánwàng de jīnglì. This trip is the most unforgettable experience of my life.

**她经历了很多困难，但从未放弃。** Tā jīnglìle hěn duō kùnnan, dàn cóng wèi fàngqì. She went through many difficulties but never gave up.

**不同的经历让我们成为不同的人。** Bùtóng de jīnglì ràng wǒmen chéngwéi bùtóng de rén. Different experiences make us into different people.

## Memory Hook

经 means "to pass through" and 历 means "to go through/calendar" — your 经历 is the record of everything you have passed through in life.
