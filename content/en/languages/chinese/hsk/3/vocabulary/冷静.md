## 冷静 (lěngjìng)

冷静 describes the ability to stay calm, rational, and composed, especially under pressure or in an emotional situation.

## Meanings

1. **[adj]** calm; cool-headed; composed; collected; not easily flustered

## Example Sentences

**遇到紧急情况，要保持冷静。** Yù dào jǐnjí qíngkuàng, yào bǎochí lěngjìng. In an emergency, you must stay calm.

**她很冷静地分析了整个问题。** Tā hěn lěngjìng de fēnxī le zhěnggè wèntí. She calmly analyzed the entire problem.

**生气的时候最好先冷静一下，再说话。** Shēngqì de shíhou zuìhǎo xiān lěngjìng yīxià, zài shuōhuà. When you are angry, it is best to calm down first before speaking.

**他在压力下依然保持冷静，令人敬佩。** Tā zài yālì xià yīrán bǎochí lěngjìng, lìng rén jìngpèi. He remained calm under pressure, which is admirable.

## Memory Hook

冷 means "cold" and 静 means "quiet/still." To be cool-headed is to keep your emotions cold and quiet like still water.
