## 有意思 (yǒu yìsi)

有意思 describes something that is interesting, fun, amusing, or thought-provoking enough to hold one's attention.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** interesting; engaging; worth paying attention to
2. **[adjective]** fun; amusing; entertaining

## Example Sentences

**这本书非常有意思，我一口气读完了。** Zhè běn shū fēicháng yǒu yìsi, wǒ yī kǒu qì dú wán le. This book is extremely interesting; I read it all in one go.

**今天的课很有意思，老师讲了很多故事。** Jīntiān de kè hěn yǒu yìsi, lǎoshī jiǎng le hěn duō gùshi. Today's class was very interesting; the teacher told many stories.

**他是一个很有意思的人，总有新想法。** Tā shì yī gè hěn yǒu yìsi de rén, zǒng yǒu xīn xiǎngfǎ. He is a very interesting person who always has new ideas.

**这个游戏有意思，我们再玩一次吧。** Zhège yóuxì yǒu yìsi, wǒmen zài wán yī cì ba. This game is fun; let's play it one more time.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|

| 挺有意思 (tǐng yǒu yìsi) | quite interesting |
| 真有意思 (zhēn yǒu yìsi) | really interesting |
| 没有意思 (méiyǒu yìsi) | boring; uninteresting |

## Memory Hook

有 means "to have," 意思 means "meaning/interest" — something 有意思 literally "has meaning/interest," so it grabs your attention and keeps it.
