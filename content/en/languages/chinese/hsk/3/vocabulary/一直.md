## 一直 (yīzhí)

一直 means continuously, all along, or straight ahead. For time it means an action has been happening without interruption. For direction it means go straight without turning.

## Meanings

1. **[adverb]** All along; continuously; the whole time.
2. **[adverb]** Straight; in a straight line (direction).

## Example Sentences

**他一直在努力学习。**
Tā yīzhí zài nǔlì xuéxí.
He has been studying hard all along.

**一直走，不要转弯。**
Yīzhí zǒu, bú yào zhuǎnwān.
Go straight; don't turn.

**我一直等你，你怎么才来？**
Wǒ yīzhí děng nǐ, nǐ zěnme cái lái?
I've been waiting for you the whole time; what took you so long?

**她一直对我很好。**
Tā yīzhí duì wǒ hěn hǎo.
She has always been very good to me.

## Memory Hook

一 (one) plus 直 (straight). One straight line that never bends — unbroken, continuous, always in the same direction.
