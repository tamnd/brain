## 考虑 (kǎolǜ)

考虑 means to consider or think something over carefully. It implies deliberate reflection before making a decision, rather than a quick reaction.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To consider; to think over; to take into account; to deliberate.

## Example Sentences

**我需要考虑一下这个问题。**
Wǒ xūyào kǎolǜ yīxià zhège wèntí.
I need to think this problem over for a moment.

**他考虑了很久才做出决定。**
Tā kǎolǜ le hěn jiǔ cái zuòchū juédìng.
He deliberated for a long time before making his decision.

**你有没有考虑过去国外工作？**
Nǐ yǒu méiyǒu kǎolǜ guò qù guówài gōngzuò?
Have you ever considered working abroad?

**这个建议值得认真考虑。**
Zhège jiànyì zhídé rènzhēn kǎolǜ.
This suggestion is worth considering seriously.

## Memory Hook

考 means to examine or test, and 虑 means to think or worry. When you 考虑 something, you are examining it with careful thought, testing every angle before deciding. Give your brain a proper exam before committing.
