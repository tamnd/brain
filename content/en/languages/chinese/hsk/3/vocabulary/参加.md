## 参加 (cānjiā)

参加 means to take part in an organized activity, event, or group. It is used for competitions, meetings, classes, clubs, and social events.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To participate in; to take part in; to attend.
2. **[verb]** To join (an organization or group).

## Example Sentences

**他参加了这次比赛。**
Tā cānjiā le zhècì bǐsài.
He participated in this competition.

**你会参加明天的会议吗？**
Nǐ huì cānjiā míngtiān de huìyì ma?
Will you attend tomorrow's meeting?

**她参加了学校的合唱团。**
Tā cānjiā le xuéxiào de héchàngtuán.
She joined the school choir.

**我打算参加汉语考试。**
Wǒ dǎsuàn cānjiā Hànyǔ kǎoshì.
I plan to take the Chinese exam.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 参加比赛 | participate in a competition |
| 参加会议 | attend a meeting |
| 参加考试 | take an exam |
| 参加活动 | join an activity |
| 参加工作 | start working / join the workforce |

## Memory Hook

参 (three people stepping forward) plus 加 (add): three people add themselves to an event — they all participate.
