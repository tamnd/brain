## 明白 (míngbai)

明白 means to understand or to be clear about something. As a verb it means to grasp the meaning or truth of something. As an adjective it describes something clear and easy to understand. It is more colloquial than 理解 (to comprehend).

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To understand; to get it; to be clear about something.
2. **[adjective]** Clear; obvious; easy to understand.

## Example Sentences

**你明白我的意思吗？**
Nǐ míngbai wǒ de yìsi ma?
Do you understand what I mean?

**现在我明白了，谢谢你的解释。**
Xiànzài wǒ míngbai le, xièxiè nǐ de jiěshì.
Now I understand, thank you for the explanation.

**这道题的答案很明白。**
Zhè dào tí de dá'àn hěn míngbai.
The answer to this question is very clear.

**她明白地告诉我她不想去。**
Tā míngbái de gàosù wǒ tā bù xiǎng qù.
She clearly told me she did not want to go.

## Collocations

## Memory Hook

明 (bright) plus 白 (white). When something is bright white and well-lit, you can see it clearly. To 明白 is to have the lights on inside your head.
