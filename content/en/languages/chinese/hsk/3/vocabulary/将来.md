## 将来 (jiānglái)

将来 means "the future" or "in the future." It refers to an unspecified future time that is more distant or open-ended than 以后 (after a specific event). 将来 is often used when talking about life goals, dreams, or general prospects.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** The future — the time ahead, used as a subject or object.
2. **[adverb]** In the future; someday — placed before the verb to indicate a future time frame.

### Compare 将来 vs 以后

| Word | Usage |
|------|-------|
| 将来 | Open-ended future; life plans, hopes, dreams |
| 以后 | After a specific point in time (e.g., 毕业以后 after graduation) |

## Example Sentences

**你将来想做什么工作？**
Nǐ jiānglái xiǎng zuò shénme gōngzuò?
What kind of work do you want to do in the future?

**将来的事谁也说不准。**
Jiānglái de shì shuí yě shuō bu zhǔn.
No one can say for certain what the future holds.

**她希望将来能环游世界。**
Tā xīwàng jiānglái néng huán yóu shìjiè.
She hopes to travel around the world someday.

**努力学习是为了将来有更好的机会。**
Nǔlì xuéxí shì wèile jiānglái yǒu gèng hǎo de jīhuì.
Studying hard is so that you will have better opportunities in the future.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 将来的目标 | future goals |
| 将来的发展 | future development |
| 对将来充满希望 | full of hope for the future |
| 为将来做准备 | prepare for the future |
| 将来有一天 | one day in the future; someday |

## Memory Hook

将 suggests "about to / going forward" (like a general advancing). 来 means "to come." The future is what is coming forward toward you — 将来.
