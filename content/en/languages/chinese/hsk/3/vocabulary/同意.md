## 同意 (tóngyì)

同意 means to agree, consent, or approve. It expresses alignment with an opinion, plan, or request. It is the direct opposite of 反对 (to oppose). It can follow a subject directly and takes an object that is the thing agreed to.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To agree; to consent; to approve.

## Example Sentences

**我同意你的看法。**
Wǒ tóngyì nǐ de kànfǎ.
I agree with your view.

**父母同意了他的计划。**
Fùmǔ tóngyì le tā de jìhuà.
His parents approved his plan.

**大家都同意这个决定。**
Dàjiā dōu tóngyì zhège juédìng.
Everyone agrees with this decision.

**你同意吗？**
Nǐ tóngyì ma?
Do you agree?

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 表示同意 | to express agreement |
| 不同意 | to disagree |
| 同意意见 | to agree with an opinion |
| 征得同意 | to obtain consent |
| 双方同意 | both sides agree |

## Memory Hook

同 means same and 意 means meaning or intention. To 同意 is to share the same meaning or intention as another person, that is, to be on the same page and agree.
