## 感动 (gǎndòng)

感动 describes being emotionally moved or touched — a feeling of being stirred deeply by something kind, beautiful, or heroic. It is a positive emotional response.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To move emotionally; to touch deeply; to be moved by.
2. **[adjective]** Moved; touched (emotionally).

## Example Sentences

**他的故事让我非常感动。**
Tā de gùshi ràng wǒ fēicháng gǎndòng.
His story moved me deeply.

**看到这一幕，她感动地哭了。**
Kàndào zhè yī mù, tā gǎndòng de kū le.
Seeing this scene, she was so moved she cried.

**他为了家人的付出真的很感动人。**
Tā wèile jiārén de fùchū zhēn de hěn gǎndòng rén.
His sacrifices for his family are truly moving.

**这首歌很感动，每次听都想哭。**
Zhè shǒu gē hěn gǎndòng, měi cì tīng dōu xiǎng kū.
This song is very touching; I feel like crying every time I hear it.

## Memory Hook

感 (to feel — heart radical 心 at the bottom) plus 动 (to move). Your heart is moved — feeling that starts in the chest and causes movement, like tears or a gasp.
