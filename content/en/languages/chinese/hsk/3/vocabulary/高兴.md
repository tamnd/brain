## 高兴 (gāoxìng)

高兴 describes a bright, uplifted mood of gladness or pleasure, often triggered by a positive event.

## Meanings

1. **[adj]** happy; glad; pleased; in good spirits

## Example Sentences

**听到你要来，我真的很高兴！** Tīng dào nǐ yào lái, wǒ zhēn de hěn gāoxìng! Hearing that you are coming, I am really happy!

**他通过考试了，全家都很高兴。** Tā tōngguò kǎoshì le, quánjiā dōu hěn gāoxìng. He passed the exam and the whole family was pleased.

**高兴的时候，她喜欢唱歌。** Gāoxìng de shíhou, tā xǐhuān chànggē. When she is happy, she likes to sing.

**这个消息让每个人都高兴起来。** Zhège xiāoxi ràng měi gèrén dōu gāoxìng qǐlái. This news made everyone feel glad.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 非常高兴 | very happy |
| 让人高兴 | to make someone happy |
| 高兴地笑 | to smile happily |
| 不高兴 | unhappy; displeased |

## Memory Hook

高 means "high/tall" and 兴 means "excitement/spirit." When you are happy, your spirits are running high.
