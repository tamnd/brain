## 送 (sòng)

送 covers three related actions: giving something as a gift, delivering or sending something, and accompanying someone to the door or to their destination.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To give as a gift; to present.
2. **[verb]** To deliver; to send.
3. **[verb]** To see someone off; to accompany to the door or station.

## Example Sentences

**我送你一本书作为礼物。**
Wǒ sòng nǐ yī běn shū zuòwéi lǐwù.
I'll give you a book as a gift.

**我去送他到机场。**
Wǒ qù sòng tā dào jīchǎng.
I went to see him off at the airport.

**快递员来送包裹了。**
Kuàidìyuán lái sòng bāoguǒ le.
The courier came to deliver the parcel.

**她的生日，大家都送了她礼物。**
Tā de shēngrì, dàjiā dōu sòng le tā lǐwù.
For her birthday, everyone gave her gifts.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 送礼物 | give a gift |
| 送别 | see someone off / farewell |
| 快递送 | courier delivery |
| 送孩子上学 | take a child to school |
| 白送 | give away for free |

## Memory Hook

送 has 辶 (movement radical at the bottom): your feet carry you forward to deliver or escort — movement is the core of 送.
