## 打印 (dǎyìn)

打印 means to print, referring to printing documents or images on a printer. 打 is a versatile verb used in many compounds and here means to strike or operate. 印 means to print or stamp. Together they describe the action of a printer pressing ink onto paper.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To print (using a printer); to make a printed copy.

## Example Sentences

**请帮我打印这份文件。**
Qǐng bāng wǒ dǎyìn zhè fèn wénjiàn.
Please help me print this document.

**我需要打印十份报告。**
Wǒ xūyào dǎyìn shí fèn bàogào.
I need to print ten copies of the report.

**打印机没墨水了。**
Dǎyìnjī méi mòshuǐ le.
The printer is out of ink.

**你可以在图书馆打印资料。**
Nǐ kěyǐ zài túshūguǎn dǎyìn zīliào.
You can print materials at the library.

## Memory Hook

打 is to strike and 印 is to stamp or print. A printer strikes ink onto paper the way a stamp hits an inkpad and then paper. 打印 captures that striking-and-stamping action perfectly.
