## 错误 (cuòwù)

错误 refers to a mistake, error, or fault — something done incorrectly. As a noun it names the error itself; as an adjective it describes something as wrong or mistaken. It is slightly more formal than 错 alone and is common in written and spoken Chinese.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Mistake; error; fault; incorrect action.
2. **[adjective]** Wrong; incorrect; mistaken.

## Example Sentences

**我犯了一个很严重的错误。**
Wǒ fàn le yīgè hěn yánzhòng de cuòwù.
I made a very serious mistake.

**这篇文章里有几个语法错误。**
Zhè piān wénzhāng lǐ yǒu jǐ gè yǔfǎ cuòwù.
There are several grammar errors in this essay.

**承认错误是勇敢的表现。**
Chéngrèn cuòwù shì yǒnggǎn de biǎoxiàn.
Admitting mistakes is a sign of courage.

**这个想法是错误的，我们需要重新考虑。**
Zhège xiǎngfǎ shì cuòwù de, wǒmen xūyào chóngxīn kǎolǜ.
This idea is wrong; we need to reconsider.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |

## Memory Hook

错 has the metal radical 钅 plus 昔 — literally "old metal gone wrong." 误 has the speech radical 讠 plus a phonetic — a word gone astray. Stack them: doubly off track, a real mistake.
