## 平均 (píngjūn)

平均 refers to an equal distribution or the mathematical average of a set of values.

## Meanings

1. **[adj/noun/verb]** average; equal; per capita; to distribute evenly; the mean

## Example Sentences

**这个班同学的平均年龄是十七岁。** Zhège bān tóngxué de píngjūn niánlíng shì shíqī suì. The average age of students in this class is seventeen.

**我每天平均睡七个小时。** Wǒ měitiān píngjūn shuì qī gè xiǎoshí. I sleep an average of seven hours every day.

**把这些苹果平均分给大家。** Bǎ zhèxiē píngguǒ píngjūn fēn gěi dàjiā. Divide these apples equally among everyone.

**这个城市的平均收入比以前高了很多。** Zhège chéngshì de píngjūn shōurù bǐ yǐqián gāo le hěn duō. The average income of this city is much higher than before.

## Memory Hook

平 means "flat/level" and 均 means "even/equal." 平均 is making everything as flat and even as a calm lake surface.
