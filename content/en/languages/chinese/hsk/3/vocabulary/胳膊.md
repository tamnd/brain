## 胳膊 (gēbo)

胳膊 means arm. It refers to the full arm from shoulder to wrist. Note the pronunciation: in everyday speech 胳膊 is often pronounced gēbo with a neutral tone on the second syllable.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Arm (from shoulder to wrist).

## Example Sentences

**他的胳膊受伤了。**
Tā de gēbo shòushāng le.
His arm is injured.

**请举起你的胳膊。**
Qǐng jǔqǐ nǐ de gēbo.
Please raise your arm.

**她挽着他的胳膊走进了餐厅。**
Tā wǎnzhe tā de gēbo zǒu jìn le cāntīng.
She walked into the restaurant holding his arm.

**跑步后我的胳膊有点酸。**
Pǎobù hòu wǒ de gēbo yǒudiǎn suān.
After running, my arms ache a little.

## Memory Hook

Both characters contain the body radical 月 (flesh/body part). 胳膊 is made entirely of body-part components, making it visually unmistakable as a body word. Think of two fleshy sections: upper arm and lower arm together.
