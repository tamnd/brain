## 记住 (jì zhù)

记住 is a verb meaning to remember or memorize something firmly. The complement 住 indicates that the result of the action 记 (to record/remember) is secured — the information is fixed in memory and will not slip away.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To remember; to keep in mind; to memorize successfully.
2. **[verb]** To make sure to remember; to bear in mind (often used as an instruction).

## Example Sentences

**你一定要记住这个单词。**
Nǐ yīdìng yào jì zhù zhège dāncí.
You must remember this word.

**我记住了她的电话号码。**
Wǒ jì zhù le tā de diànhuà hàomǎ.
I memorized her phone number.

**请记住，明天早上八点开会。**
Qǐng jì zhù, míngtiān zǎoshang bā diǎn kāihuì.
Please remember: the meeting is at 8 a.m. tomorrow.

**这首诗我已经记住了。**
Zhè shǒu shī wǒ yǐjīng jì zhù le.
I have already memorized this poem.

## Memory Hook

记 means "to record" (the speech radical 讠 tells you it involves mental noting). 住 means "to stay." When you 记住 something, the memory stays — it does not walk out the door.
