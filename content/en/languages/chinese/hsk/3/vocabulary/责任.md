## 责任 (zérèn)

责任 refers to a responsibility or duty that a person is expected to fulfill and is accountable for. It carries a moral weight — having 责任 means you own the outcome. The key phrases 负责任 (be responsible) and 有责任 (have a responsibility) are very common.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Responsibility; duty; obligation.
2. **[noun]** Accountability; blame (when something goes wrong).

## Example Sentences

**父母有责任照顾好自己的孩子。**
Fùmǔ yǒu zérèn zhàogù hǎo zìjǐ de háizi.
Parents have a responsibility to take good care of their children.

**他是一个很有责任感的人。**
Tā shì yīgè hěn yǒu zérèngǎn de rén.
He is a person with a strong sense of responsibility.

**这件事的责任在我，不在你。**
Zhè jiàn shì de zérèn zài wǒ, bù zài nǐ.
The responsibility for this matter lies with me, not you.

**作为经理，你要负责任。**
Zuòwéi jīnglǐ, nǐ yào fù zérèn.
As a manager, you must be responsible.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 负责任 | to be responsible / take responsibility |
| 有责任 | to have a responsibility |
| 责任感 | sense of responsibility |
| 承担责任 | to bear / take on responsibility |
| 推卸责任 | to shirk responsibility |

## Memory Hook

责 means "to demand / reproach" and 任 means "to take on / bear." Responsibility is what you take on and can be reproached for if you fail — it weighs on you from both directions.
