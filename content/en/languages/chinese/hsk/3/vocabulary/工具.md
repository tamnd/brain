## 工具 (gōngjù)

工具 refers to any physical or abstract tool used to get work done, from a hammer to a language.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** tool; instrument; implement
2. **[noun]** means; vehicle (for achieving something)

## Example Sentences

**这个工具箱里有很多工具。** Zhège gōngjùxiāng lǐ yǒu hěn duō gōngjù. There are many tools in this toolbox.

**语言是人类交流的重要工具。** Yǔyán shì rénlèi jiāoliú de zhòngyào gōngjù. Language is an important tool for human communication.

**他用工具把椅子修好了。** Tā yòng gōngjù bǎ yǐzi xiū hǎo le. He used a tool to fix the chair.

**学习也需要好的工具，比如词典。** Xuéxí yě xūyào hǎo de gōngjù, bǐrú cídiǎn. Studying also requires good tools, such as a dictionary.

## Memory Hook

工 means "work" and 具 means "equipment" — a 工具 is literally the equipment you need for work.
