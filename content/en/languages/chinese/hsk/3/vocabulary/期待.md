## 期待 (qīdài)

期待 expresses the feeling of eagerly waiting and hoping for something to happen.

## Meanings

1. **[verb/noun]** to look forward to; to expect with hope; eager anticipation

## Example Sentences

**我非常期待这次旅行。** Wǒ fēicháng qīdài zhè cì lǚxíng. I am really looking forward to this trip.

**大家都在期待他的新专辑。** Dàjiā dōu zài qīdài tā de xīn zhuānjí. Everyone is looking forward to his new album.

**我期待着和你再次见面。** Wǒ qīdài zhe hé nǐ zàicì jiànmiàn. I look forward to seeing you again.

**父母对孩子有很多期待。** Fùmǔ duì háizi yǒu hěn duō qīdài. Parents have many expectations for their children.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 非常期待 | really looking forward to |
| 充满期待 | full of anticipation |
| 满足期待 | to meet expectations |
| 期待已久 | long-awaited |

## Memory Hook

期 means "a period of time" and 待 means "to wait." 期待 is waiting through a period of time with hope in your heart.
