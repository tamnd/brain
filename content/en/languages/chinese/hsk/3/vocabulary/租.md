## 租 (zū)

租 means to rent as both a verb and noun. It can describe both the act of renting something from someone and the act of leasing something out to someone else, depending on context.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To rent; to lease; to hire (something from someone).
2. **[verb]** To rent out; to let (something to someone).
3. **[noun]** Rent; rental fee.

## Example Sentences

**我想在这里租一间房间。**
Wǒ xiǎng zài zhèlǐ zū yī jiān fángjiān.
I would like to rent a room here.

**这套公寓每个月租多少钱？**
Zhè tào gōngyù měi gè yuè zū duōshao qián?
How much is the rent for this apartment per month?

**他把房子租给了一个学生。**
Tā bǎ fángzi zū gěi le yīgè xuésheng.
He rented the house out to a student.

**我们可以租一辆自行车游览城市。**
Wǒmen kěyǐ zū yī liàng zìxíngchē yóulǎn chéngshì.
We can rent a bicycle to tour the city.

## Memory Hook

租 contains 禾 (grain) and 且 (moreover). Historically, rent was often paid in grain. Think of a farmer handing over a share of their harvest as payment for using the land.
