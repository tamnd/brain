## 积极 (jījí)

积极 means positive or proactive. It describes an attitude of enthusiasm, initiative, and optimism rather than passivity or negativity. It is commonly used to praise attitude and behavior.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** Positive; proactive; active; enthusiastic.
2. **[adjective]** Optimistic; constructive (in attitude).

## Example Sentences

**他对工作非常积极。**
Tā duì gōngzuò fēicháng jījí.
He is very proactive about his work.

**我们要以积极的态度面对困难。**
Wǒmen yào yǐ jījí de tàidu miànduì kùnnán.
We should face difficulties with a positive attitude.

**她在课堂上总是积极回答问题。**
Tā zài kètáng shang zǒng shì jījí huídá wèntí.
She always actively answers questions in class.

**这个消息对我们来说是积极的。**
Zhège xiāoxi duì wǒmen lái shuō shì jījí de.
This news is positive for us.

## Memory Hook

积 means to accumulate, and 极 means extreme or pole. 积极 is like accumulating energy at the positive pole. Think of a battery: the positive (积极) terminal is where energy flows out actively.
