## 文章 (wénzhāng)

文章 refers to a piece of written work, such as a newspaper article, essay, or composition.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** article; essay; composition; written piece

## Example Sentences

**他写了一篇关于环境保护的文章。** Tā xiě le yī piān guānyú huánjìng bǎohù de wénzhāng. He wrote an article about environmental protection.

**这篇文章写得很好，逻辑清晰。** Zhè piān wénzhāng xiě de hěn hǎo, luójí qīngxī. This article is very well written and logically clear.

**老师要求我们每周写一篇文章。** Lǎoshī yāoqiú wǒmen měi zhōu xiě yī piān wénzhāng. The teacher requires us to write one article every week.

**我在网上读到了一篇有趣的文章。** Wǒ zài wǎngshàng dúdào le yī piān yǒuqù de wénzhāng. I read an interesting article online.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 写文章 | to write an article |
| 一篇文章 | one article (measure word 篇) |
| 发表文章 | to publish an article |
| 文章内容 | content of an article |

## Memory Hook

文 means "writing/culture" and 章 means "chapter/section" — a 文章 is a section of writing.
