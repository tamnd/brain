## 既然 (jìrán)

既然 means "since" or "now that" — it accepts an established fact or situation as a premise, then draws a logical or practical conclusion. It is paired with 就 (jiù) or 那就 (nà jiù) in the second clause.

## Meanings

1. **[conjunction]** Since; now that; given that; seeing that (accepting a fact as a premise).

## Example Sentences

**既然来了，就多待几天吧。**
Jìrán lái le, jiù duō dāi jǐ tiān ba.
Since you're already here, why not stay a few more days?

**既然决定了，就不要后悔。**
Jìrán juédìng le, jiù bú yào hòuhuǐ.
Now that you've decided, don't regret it.

**既然你不喜欢，那就换一个吧。**
Jìrán nǐ bù xǐhuān, nà jiù huàn yīgè ba.
Since you don't like it, then let's change it.

**既然大家都同意，我们就开始吧。**
Jìrán dàjiā dōu tóngyì, wǒmen jiù kāishǐ ba.
Since everyone agrees, let's start.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 既然…就… | since…then… |
| 既然如此 | since that is the case |
| 既然决定 | since (you've) decided |
| 既然来了 | since (you've) come |
| 既然知道 | since (you) know |

## Memory Hook

既 (already done — a person turning away 旡 from a finished meal 皀) plus 然 (so / that being the case). Already done, that being the case — now you must act on what has already happened.
