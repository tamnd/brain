## 水果 (shuǐguǒ)

水果 means fruit. It refers to the sweet, edible products of plants. 水 means water, and 果 means fruit or result. The combination evokes juicy, water-filled fruit.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Fruit; the sweet edible product of a plant, such as apples, oranges, or bananas.

## Example Sentences

**多吃水果对身体好。**
Duō chī shuǐguǒ duì shēntǐ hǎo.
Eating more fruit is good for your health.

**这家超市的水果又新鲜又便宜。**
Zhè jiā chāoshì de shuǐguǒ yòu xīnxiān yòu piányí.
The fruit at this supermarket is both fresh and cheap.

**她每天早餐都吃一些水果。**
Tā měitiān zǎocān dōu chī yīxiē shuǐguǒ.
She eats some fruit for breakfast every day.

**热带水果的味道特别甜。**
Rèdài shuǐguǒ de wèidào tèbié tián.
Tropical fruits taste especially sweet.

## Memory Hook

水 (water) plus 果 (fruit/result). A juicy fruit is a water-filled result of the plant's growth. When you bite into a peach, the water inside pours out.
