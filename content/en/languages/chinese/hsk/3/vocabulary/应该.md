## 应该 (yīnggāi)

应该 is a modal verb expressing what one ought to do, what is expected, or what is proper under the circumstances.

## Meanings

1. **[modal verb]** should; ought to; be supposed to

## Example Sentences

**你应该多喝水，这对身体好。** Nǐ yīnggāi duō hē shuǐ, zhè duì shēntǐ hǎo. You should drink more water; it is good for your health.

**学生应该按时完成作业。** Xuésheng yīnggāi ànshí wánchéng zuòyè. Students should complete their homework on time.

**这件事你应该告诉老师。** Zhè jiàn shì nǐ yīnggāi gàosù lǎoshī. You should tell the teacher about this matter.

**他已经到了，应该在楼上。** Tā yǐjīng dào le, yīnggāi zài lóu shàng. He has already arrived; he should be upstairs.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 应该做 | should do |
| 不应该 | should not; ought not |
| 本来应该 | was supposed to |
| 应该没问题 | should be no problem |

## Memory Hook

应 means "to respond/comply" and 该 means "should/due" — 应该 is what you are due to comply with, what is expected of you.
