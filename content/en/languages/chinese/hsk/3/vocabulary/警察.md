## 警察 (jǐngchá)

警察 means police or police officer. It refers both to individual law enforcement officers and to the police as an institution. The measure word for one officer is 名 (míng) or 个 (gè).

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Police officer; policeman; policewoman.
2. **[noun]** The police (as an institution or force).

## Example Sentences

**快叫警察！**
Kuài jiào jǐngchá!
Call the police quickly!

**那位警察帮助了迷路的孩子。**
Nà wèi jǐngchá bāngzhù le mílù de háizi.
That police officer helped the lost child.

**警察正在调查这件事。**
Jǐngchá zhèngzài diàochá zhè jiàn shì.
The police are currently investigating this matter.

**她长大以后想当警察。**
Tā zhǎngdà yǐhòu xiǎng dāng jǐngchá.
She wants to become a police officer when she grows up.

## Memory Hook

警 means to warn or be alert (it contains 言, words, and 敬, reverence — speaking with watchful authority), and 察 means to observe or investigate. A 警察 is someone who warns and investigates: the vigilant observer of the law.
