## 感谢 (gǎnxiè)

感谢 means to thank someone or feel gratitude. It is more formal and heartfelt than the everyday 谢谢 (xièxiè), and is commonly used in speeches, letters, and sincere expressions of appreciation.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To thank; to express gratitude toward.
2. **[noun]** Gratitude; thanks.

## Example Sentences

**我非常感谢你的帮助。**
Wǒ fēicháng gǎnxiè nǐ de bāngzhù.
I'm very grateful for your help.

**感谢大家的支持！**
Gǎnxiè dàjiā de zhīchí!
Thank you all for your support!

**他感谢老师多年的教导。**
Tā gǎnxiè lǎoshī duō nián de jiàodǎo.
He is grateful for the teacher's years of guidance.

**请接受我的感谢。**
Qǐng jiēshòu wǒ de gǎnxiè.
Please accept my thanks.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 表示感谢 | express gratitude |

| 万分感谢 | extremely grateful |

## Memory Hook

感 means "to feel" (heart radical 心 at the bottom). 谢 means "to thank." Feeling moves from the heart into words of thanks — a complete circuit of gratitude.
