## 借 (jiè)

A verb that means both "to borrow" (from someone) and "to lend" (to someone), depending on context. Direction is indicated by the surrounding words.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To borrow (take temporarily from someone).
2. **[verb]** To lend (give temporarily to someone).

## Example Sentences

**我可以借一下你的笔吗？**
Wǒ kěyǐ jiè yīxià nǐ de bǐ ma?
May I borrow your pen for a moment?

**他向图书馆借了几本书。**
Tā xiàng túshūguǎn jiè le jǐ běn shū.
He borrowed a few books from the library.

**你能借我一点儿钱吗？**
Nǐ néng jiè wǒ yīdiǎnr qián ma?
Can you lend me some money?

**这本书我已经借给他了。**
Zhè běn shū wǒ yǐjīng jiè gěi tā le.
I have already lent this book to him.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 借书 | borrow a book |
| 借钱 | borrow / lend money |
| 借给 | lend to (someone) |
| 向…借 | borrow from... |
| 借口 | excuse, pretext |

## Memory Hook

One character, two directions: 借 to me is borrowing, 借 to you is lending — the object flows between both people.
