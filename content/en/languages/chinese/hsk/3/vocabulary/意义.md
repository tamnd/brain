## 意义 (yìyì)

意义 refers to the deeper significance, value, or purpose behind something, going beyond surface-level meaning (unlike 意思 yìsi, which is more about literal meaning).

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** meaning; significance; importance; value

## Example Sentences

**这件事对我有很重要的意义。** Zhè jiàn shì duì wǒ yǒu hěn zhòngyào de yìyì. This matter holds great significance for me.

**帮助别人是一件很有意义的事情。** Bāngzhù biérén shì yī jiàn hěn yǒu yìyì de shìqing. Helping others is a very meaningful thing.

**你认为生活的意义是什么？** Nǐ rènwéi shēnghuó de yìyì shì shénme? What do you think the meaning of life is?

**这个纪念碑具有深刻的历史意义。** Zhège jìniànbēi jùyǒu shēnkè de lìshǐ yìyì. This monument has profound historical significance.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 有意义 | meaningful; significant |
| 没有意义 | meaningless |
| 重要意义 | important significance |
| 历史意义 | historical significance |

## Memory Hook

While 意思 (yìsi) is "what does this word mean?", 意义 (yìyì) is "why does this matter?" — 义 relates to righteousness and value, so 意义 carries weight and importance.
