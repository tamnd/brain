## 按照 (ànzhào)

按照 means in accordance with or following a specific rule, plan, order, or standard. It describes compliance or conformity with an established guideline.

## Meanings

1. **[preposition]** According to; in accordance with; following; as per.

## Example Sentences

**请按照老师的要求完成作业。**
Qǐng ànzhào lǎoshī de yāoqiú wánchéng zuòyè.
Please complete the homework according to the teacher's requirements.

**按照规定，上课不能用手机。**
Ànzhào guīdìng, shàngkè bù néng yòng shǒujī.
According to the rules, phones cannot be used in class.

**他按照计划完成了所有的工作。**
Tā ànzhào jìhuà wánchéng le suǒyǒu de gōngzuò.
He completed all the work according to the plan.

**请按照顺序回答问题。**
Qǐng ànzhào shùnxù huídá wèntí.
Please answer the questions in order.

## Memory Hook

按 means "to press down" (hand 扌 pressing). 照 means "to shine on / follow." Press down firmly and follow the light — strict compliance with what is illuminated.
