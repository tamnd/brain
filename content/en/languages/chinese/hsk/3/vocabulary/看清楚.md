## 看清楚 (kàn qīngchǔ)

看清楚 is a verb-complement structure. 看 (to look / see) is the base verb and 清楚 (clear) is the result complement, showing that looking achieves a clear understanding or perception. It is used for visual clarity — reading something carefully, seeing something distinctly, or making sure you noticed correctly.

## Structure

看 (look / see) + 清楚 (result complement: clear) = look with the result of seeing clearly.

Other complements that can follow 看: 看完 (finish reading), 看懂 (understand by reading), 看错 (misread).

## Example Sentences

**你看清楚了吗？这里写的是六，不是八。**
Nǐ kàn qīngchǔ le ma? Zhèlǐ xiě de shì liù, bú shì bā.
Did you look clearly? What is written here is six, not eight.

**请把合同看清楚再签字。**
Qǐng bǎ hétong kàn qīngchǔ zài qiānzì.
Please read the contract carefully before signing.

**光线太暗，我看不清楚。**
Guāngxiàn tài àn, wǒ kàn bù qīngchǔ.
The light is too dim; I cannot see clearly.

**他没戴眼镜，没看清楚那个人的脸。**
Tā méi dài yǎnjìng, méi kàn qīngchǔ nàgè rén de liǎn.
He was not wearing his glasses and did not see that person's face clearly.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 看清楚了 | saw / read clearly (completed) |
| 看不清楚 | cannot see clearly |
| 把...看清楚 | look at... carefully (with 把) |
| 没看清楚 | did not see clearly |
| 看清楚再说 | make sure you see it clearly before speaking |

## Memory Hook

看 has a hand (手) over an eye (目) — shielding your eyes to see into the distance. Add 清楚 (crystal-clear water). 看清楚 is shielding your eyes and peering hard until the image is as clear as a calm pond.
