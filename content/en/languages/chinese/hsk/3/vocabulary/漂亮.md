## 漂亮 (piàoliang)

漂亮 describes something or someone that is visually beautiful or attractive. It can also describe an action done impressively well.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** pretty; beautiful; attractive in appearance
2. **[adjective]** well done; impressive; excellent (describing an action or result)

## Example Sentences

**她穿了一条漂亮的裙子来参加聚会。** Tā chuān le yī tiáo piàoliang de qúnzi lái cānjiā jùhuì. She wore a pretty dress to the party.

**这幅画画得真漂亮。** Zhè fú huà huà de zhēn piàoliang. This painting is really beautiful.

**他打了一个漂亮的球，赢得了比赛。** Tā dǎ le yī gè piàoliang de qiú, yíng dé le bǐsài. He made a brilliant shot and won the match.

**春天的花园非常漂亮，到处都是花。** Chūntiān de huāyuán fēicháng piàoliang, dàochù dōu shì huā. The garden is very beautiful in spring; flowers are everywhere.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 非常漂亮 (fēicháng piàoliang) | very beautiful |
| 长得漂亮 (zhǎng de piàoliang) | to look pretty |
| 漂亮话 (piàoliang huà) | flattering words; pretty talk |
| 打得漂亮 (dǎ de piàoliang) | played brilliantly |

## Memory Hook

漂 contains the water radical 氵, evoking someone floating gracefully on water, and 亮 means "bright" — something 漂亮 is so bright and graceful it floats into your eyes.
