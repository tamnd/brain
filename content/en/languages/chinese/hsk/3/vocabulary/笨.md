## 笨 (bèn)

笨 describes someone who is slow to understand or physically clumsy, and is the natural opposite of 聪明.

## Meanings

1. **[adj]** clumsy; stupid; dull-witted; awkward; not nimble

## Example Sentences

**我觉得我很笨，总是学不好数学。** Wǒ juéde wǒ hěn bèn, zǒng shì xué bù hǎo shùxué. I feel like I am quite dumb; I can never learn math well.

**他手很笨，做手工总是做不好。** Tā shǒu hěn bèn, zuò shǒugōng zǒng shì zuò bù hǎo. He has clumsy hands and can never do crafts well.

**别说自己笨，只是需要多练习。** Bié shuō zìjǐ bèn, zhǐ shì xūyào duō liànxí. Don't say you're dumb; you just need more practice.

**这个方法太笨了，有更好的办法。** Zhège fāngfǎ tài bèn le, yǒu gèng hǎo de bànfǎ. This method is too clumsy; there is a better way.

## Memory Hook

The character 笨 shows bamboo (竹) over the root (本). Bamboo is stiff and unbending: a fitting image for someone who cannot adapt quickly.
