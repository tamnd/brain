## 热爱 (rè'ài)

热爱 expresses a deep, passionate love for something, stronger than 喜欢 (xǐhuān) or 爱 (ài) alone.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to love deeply; to be passionate about something

## Example Sentences

**我热爱音乐，每天都要练习。** Wǒ rè'ài yīnyuè, měitiān dōu yào liànxí. I am passionate about music and practice every day.

**他热爱自己的工作，从不感到累。** Tā rè'ài zìjǐ de gōngzuò, cóng bù gǎndào lèi. He deeply loves his work and never feels tired.

**她从小热爱绘画，长大后成了画家。** Tā cóng xiǎo rè'ài huìhuà, zhǎngdà hòu chéngle huàjiā. She loved painting from a young age and became a painter when she grew up.

**我们都热爱自己的祖国。** Wǒmen dōu rè'ài zìjǐ de zǔguó. We all deeply love our homeland.

## Memory Hook

热 means "hot" and 爱 means "love" — 热爱 is love so strong it burns hot.
