## 地图 (dìtú)

地图 is the word for a map — any visual representation of geographic or spatial information used to find locations or navigate.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Map.

## Example Sentences

**我找不到路，需要看地图。**
Wǒ zhǎo bu dào lù, xūyào kàn dìtú.
I can't find the way; I need to look at the map.

**手机上有地图应用，很方便。**
Shǒujī shàng yǒu dìtú yìngyòng, hěn fāngbiàn.
There are map apps on the phone; very convenient.

**请在地图上指出这个地方。**
Qǐng zài dìtú shàng zhǐchū zhège dìfang.
Please point out this place on the map.

**他研究了很久的地图，才找到了那条路。**
Tā yánjiū le hěn jiǔ de dìtú, cái zhǎodào le nà tiáo lù.
He studied the map for a long time before finally finding that road.

## Memory Hook

地 (ground / earth) plus 图 (picture / diagram). A picture of the earth — what a map literally is.
