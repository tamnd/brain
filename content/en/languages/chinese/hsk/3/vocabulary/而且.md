## 而且 (érqiě)

而且 adds a further point to what was just said, often strengthening or extending the previous statement. It is commonly paired with 不但 (búdàn — not only) to form the "not only... but also..." pattern.

## Meanings

1. **[conjunction]** Moreover; furthermore; in addition; and also; what's more.

## Example Sentences

**他聪明，而且很努力。**
Tā cōngming, érqiě hěn nǔlì.
He is smart and also very hardworking.

**这本书不但好看，而且有用。**
Zhè běn shū búdàn hǎokàn, érqiě yǒuyòng.
This book is not only interesting but also useful.

**她会说中文，而且说得非常流利。**
Tā huì shuō Zhōngwén, érqiě shuō de fēicháng liúlì.
She can speak Chinese, and moreover speaks it very fluently.

**这里的环境好，而且价格也合理。**
Zhèlǐ de huánjìng hǎo, érqiě jiàgé yě hélǐ.
The environment here is good, and moreover the prices are reasonable.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 不但…而且… | not only…but also… |
| 而且还 | and what's more |
| 不仅…而且… | not just…but also… |
| 而且更 | and even more so |
| 甚至而且 | and even |

## Memory Hook

而 (connecting two things) plus 且 (furthermore / moreover). Two connectors stacked together — double the addition, double the emphasis.
