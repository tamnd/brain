## 产品 (chǎnpǐn)

Refers to items that have been manufactured or produced, either physical goods or the output of a creative process.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Product, manufactured goods.
2. **[noun]** Output, result of a production process.

## Example Sentences

**这家公司的产品质量很好。**
Zhè jiā gōngsī de chǎnpǐn zhìliàng hěn hǎo.
The quality of this company's products is very good.

**他们正在开发一款新产品。**
Tāmen zhèngzài kāifā yī kuǎn xīn chǎnpǐn.
They are developing a new product.

**这个产品很受消费者欢迎。**
Zhège chǎnpǐn hěn shòu xiāofèizhě huānyíng.
This product is very popular with consumers.

**我们需要提高产品的竞争力。**
Wǒmen xūyào tígāo chǎnpǐn de jìngzhēnglì.
We need to improve the competitiveness of our products.

## Memory Hook

产 means "to produce" and 品 means "goods/item" — things that have been produced become products.
