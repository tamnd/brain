## 原来 (yuánlái)

原来 has two main uses: describing the original or former state of something, and expressing a realization or discovery ("so it turns out that...").

## Meanings

1. **[adverb]** originally; formerly (describing a past state)
2. **[adverb]** as it turns out; so... (expressing a realization)

## Example Sentences

**原来他就是那个有名的作家！** Yuánlái tā jiù shì nàgè yǒumíng de zuòjiā! So he is that famous writer after all!

**这里原来是一片森林，现在变成了城市。** Zhèlǐ yuánlái shì yī piàn sēnlín, xiànzài biànchéng le chéngshì. This place was originally a forest; now it has become a city.

**我以为你不喜欢音乐，原来你会弹吉他！** Wǒ yǐwéi nǐ bù xǐhuān yīnyuè, yuánlái nǐ huì tán jítā! I thought you did not like music; it turns out you can play guitar!

**原来的计划被取消了，我们换了新方案。** Yuánlái de jìhuà bèi qǔxiāo le, wǒmen huàn le xīn fāng'àn. The original plan was cancelled and we switched to a new one.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 原来如此 | so that is how it is |
| 原来的 | the original / former |
| 原来是 | it turns out to be |
| 原来以为 | originally thought |

## Memory Hook

原 means "original/source" and 来 means "come" — 原来 brings you back to the original point, either what was there before or the true story that has now come to light.
