## 大方 (dàfang)

大方 has two related senses: being generous with money or resources, and behaving in a natural, composed, and graceful manner without shyness.

## Meanings

1. **[adj]** generous; open-handed with money or gifts
2. **[adj]** natural and poised; graceful; composed; not awkward or shy

## Example Sentences

**他非常大方，总是请朋友吃饭。** Tā fēicháng dàfang, zǒng shì qǐng péngyou chīfàn. He is very generous and always treats his friends to meals.

**她站在台上举止大方，一点都不紧张。** Tā zhàn zài tái shàng jǔzhǐ dàfang, yīdiǎn dōu bù jǐnzhāng. She stood on stage with natural poise and was not nervous at all.

**穿上这件衣服，你看起来很大方。** Chuān shàng zhè jiàn yīfu, nǐ kàn qǐlái hěn dàfang. Wearing this outfit, you look very elegant and composed.

**这位老师对学生很大方，经常送书给他们。** Zhè wèi lǎoshī duì xuéshēng hěn dàfang, jīngcháng sòng shū gěi tāmen. This teacher is very generous to students and often gives them books.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 举止大方 | to behave with natural grace |

## Memory Hook

大 means "big/great" and 方 means "square/upright." A generous, poised person has a big, upright spirit with nothing to hide.
