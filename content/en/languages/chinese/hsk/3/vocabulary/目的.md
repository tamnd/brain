## 目的 (mùdì)

目的 means purpose, goal, or aim. It refers to the intended result or objective behind an action or plan.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Purpose; goal; aim; objective; destination.

## Example Sentences

**你来这里的目的是什么？**
Nǐ lái zhèlǐ de mùdì shì shénme?
What is your purpose in coming here?

**我学汉语的目的是为了工作。**
Wǒ xué hànyǔ de mùdì shì wèile gōngzuò.
My purpose in learning Chinese is for work.

**他的目的只有一个，就是赢。**
Tā de mùdì zhǐyǒu yīgè, jiùshì yíng.
He has only one goal, and that is to win.

**达到目的以后，他就离开了。**
Dádào mùdì yǐhòu, tā jiù líkāi le.
After achieving his goal, he left.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 达到目的 | to achieve a goal |
| 目的是 | the purpose is |
| 以...为目的 | with the aim of |
| 目的地 | destination |
| 明确目的 | to clarify the goal |

## Memory Hook

目 means eye and 的 here functions as the target marker. Your 目的 is literally the target your eye is fixed on. Keep your eyes on the goal.
