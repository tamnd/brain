## 部分 (bùfen)

部分 refers to a part, portion, or section of a larger whole. It is used to indicate that something applies to only some, not all, of a group or thing. The tone on 分 is neutral (bùfen, not bùfèn) in most standard speech.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Part; portion; section; segment.
2. **[noun]** Some (a subset of a group), as in "part of the students."

## Example Sentences

**这只是整个计划的一部分。**
Zhè zhǐshì zhěnggè jìhuà de yī bùfen.
This is only one part of the entire plan.

**一部分学生同意，另一部分不同意。**
Yī bùfen xuésheng tóngyì, lìng yī bùfen bù tóngyì.
Some students agreed; another portion did not.

**请把这部分内容再读一遍。**
Qǐng bǎ zhè bùfen nèiróng zài dú yī biàn.
Please read this section once more.

**大部分工作已经完成了。**
Dà bùfen gōngzuò yǐjīng wánchéng le.
Most of the work has already been completed.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 大部分 | the majority / most part |
| 一部分 | a part / some |
| 小部分 | a small part |
| 这部分 | this section / this part |
| 主要部分 | the main part |

## Memory Hook

部 means "section / department" and 分 means "to divide / portion." Put them together: a section that has been divided off — a distinct portion of something larger.
