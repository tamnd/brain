## 理解 (lǐjiě)

理解 means to understand or comprehend something at a deeper level, beyond surface recognition, and can also function as a noun meaning understanding or comprehension.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to understand; to comprehend — to grasp the meaning, logic, or feeling behind something
2. **[noun]** understanding; comprehension — the state of having grasped something deeply

## Example Sentences

**我不太理解他的意思。** Wǒ bú tài lǐjiě tā de yìsi. I do not quite understand what he means.

**请你理解我的难处。** Qǐng nǐ lǐjiě wǒ de nánchù. Please understand my difficulty.

**对这篇文章的理解因人而异。** Duì zhè piān wénzhāng de lǐjiě yīn rén ér yì. Understanding of this article differs from person to person.

**他终于理解了父母的良苦用心。** Tā zhōngyú lǐjiě le fùmǔ de liángkǔ yòngxīn. He finally understood his parents' good intentions.

## Memory Hook

理 (logic; reason) plus 解 (to untangle; to solve) — untangling the logic of something: that is 理解.
