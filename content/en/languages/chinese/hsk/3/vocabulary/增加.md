## 增加 (zēngjiā)

增加 means to increase or add to something. It describes a quantity, level, or amount becoming larger, whether gradually or deliberately.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To increase; to add; to grow; to raise (a quantity).

## Example Sentences

**这个城市的人口不断增加。**
Zhège chéngshì de rénkǒu búduàn zēngjiā.
The population of this city keeps increasing.

**他增加了每天的练习时间。**
Tā zēngjiā le měitiān de liànxí shíjiān.
He increased his daily practice time.

**公司决定增加工资。**
Gōngsī juédìng zēngjiā gōngzī.
The company decided to increase salaries.

**我们需要增加预算。**
Wǒmen xūyào zēngjiā yùsuàn.
We need to increase the budget.

## Memory Hook

增 (grow taller — the earth radical 土 underneath something rising) plus 加 (add). The earth grows upward as you add more on top — steady increase.
