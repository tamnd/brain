## 用途 (yòngtú)

用途 refers to the practical use or purpose of an object, material, or technology, often implying a range of possible applications.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** use; purpose; the function or intended application of something
2. **[noun]** application; a specific way in which something can be used

## Example Sentences

**这种材料的用途非常广泛。** Zhè zhǒng cáiliào de yòngtú fēicháng guǎngfàn. This material has a very wide range of uses.

**请说明这台设备的用途。** Qǐng shuōmíng zhè tái shèbèi de yòngtú. Please explain the purpose of this device.

**竹子的用途很多，可以做家具，也可以做食物。** Zhúzi de yòngtú hěn duō, kěyǐ zuò jiājù, yě kěyǐ zuò shíwù. Bamboo has many uses; it can be made into furniture and also eaten as food.

**你知道这个软件有什么用途吗？** Nǐ zhīdào zhège ruǎnjiàn yǒu shénme yòngtú ma? Do you know what this software is used for?

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 广泛用途 (guǎngfàn yòngtú) | wide range of uses |
| 多种用途 (duō zhǒng yòngtú) | multiple uses |
| 实际用途 (shíjì yòngtú) | practical use |
| 用途广 (yòngtú guǎng) | versatile; many uses |

## Memory Hook

用 means "use" and 途 means "road/path" — the 用途 of a tool is the road down which you can use it, showing all the directions it can take you.
