## 等 (děng)

等 has two distinct but equally common uses. As a verb it means to wait for someone or something. As a sentence-final particle after a list of items it means and so on or et cetera.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To wait; to wait for.
2. **[particle]** Et cetera; and so on; and the like (after a list of examples).

## Example Sentences

**请在这里等我。**
Qǐng zài zhèlǐ děng wǒ.
Please wait for me here.

**我们等他来了再出发。**
Wǒmen děng tā lái le zài chūfā.
We will wait until he arrives before setting off.

**桌上有苹果、香蕉、橙子等水果。**
Zhuō shang yǒu píngguǒ, xiāngjiāo, chéngzi děng shuǐguǒ.
On the table there are apples, bananas, oranges, and other fruits.

**他学了英语、法语、西班牙语等语言。**
Tā xué le yīngyǔ, fǎyǔ, xībānyáyǔ děng yǔyán.
He has studied English, French, Spanish, and other languages.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 等一下 | wait a moment |
| 等到 | to wait until |
| 等着 | to be waiting |
| 等等 | et cetera; wait a second |
| 等他来 | wait for him to come |

## Memory Hook

等 contains 竹 (bamboo) at the top and suggests patience. Bamboo grows slowly but steadily. When you 等, you are as patient as bamboo growing, whether waiting for a person or listing items that continue on like bamboo shoots.
