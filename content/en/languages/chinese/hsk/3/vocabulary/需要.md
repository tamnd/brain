## 需要 (xūyào)

需要 expresses necessity or requirement. It can precede a noun (need something) or a verb phrase (need to do something), making it extremely versatile in everyday speech.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To need; to require.
2. **[noun]** A need; a requirement; a demand.

## Example Sentences

**我需要你的帮助。**
Wǒ xūyào nǐ de bāngzhù.
I need your help.

**学习中文需要很多时间。**
Xuéxí Zhōngwén xūyào hěn duō shíjiān.
Learning Chinese requires a lot of time.

**你还需要什么吗？**
Nǐ hái xūyào shénme ma?
Do you still need anything?

**这项任务需要大家合作完成。**
Zhè xiàng rènwù xūyào dàjiā hézuò wánchéng.
This task requires everyone to work together to complete it.

## Collocations

## Memory Hook

需 has 雨 (rain) on top: even rain has needs, falling where it must. 要 means "want/require." Together: something the sky itself requires.
