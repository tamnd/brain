## 尤其 (yóuqí)

尤其 is an adverb meaning "especially" or "in particular," used to highlight one specific element within a broader set as being more notable, extreme, or deserving of attention than the others.

## Meanings

1. **[adverb]** especially; particularly; in particular (used to single out one specific item or aspect from a broader group, emphasizing it as the most notable member)

## Usage Note

尤其 typically introduces the item being highlighted, which comes after it. The structure is often:

[general statement], 尤其 (是) + [highlighted item/clause]

The optional 是 after 尤其 makes the singling-out even more explicit.

Compare with 特别 (tèbié) and 格外 (géwài):
- 尤其 singles out one item within a set: "All X are good, but this one especially"
- 特别 is a general intensifier: "This is especially / very"
- 格外 means "beyond the usual baseline in this moment"

## Example Sentences

**他喜欢各种体育运动，尤其喜欢游泳。** Tā xǐhuān gè zhǒng tǐyù yùndòng, yóuqí xǐhuān yóuyǒng. He likes all kinds of sports, especially swimming.

**北京的冬天很冷，尤其是在一月份。** Běijīng de dōngtiān hěn lěng, yóuqí shì zài yī yuèfèn. Beijing's winter is very cold, especially in January.

**她对语言很感兴趣，尤其对中文和日文。** Tā duì yǔyán hěn gǎn xìngqù, yóuqí duì Zhōngwén hé Rìwén. She is very interested in languages, particularly Chinese and Japanese.

**这道菜很好吃，尤其是那个汤，味道极好。** Zhè dào cài hěn hào chī, yóuqí shì nàge tāng, wèidào jí hǎo. This dish is very tasty, especially the soup, which has an outstanding flavor.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 尤其是 | especially; in particular (followed by noun/clause) |
| 尤其喜欢 | especially like |
| 尤其重要 | especially important |
| 尤其注意 | pay particular attention to |
| 尤其在 | especially in (a situation/time) |

## Memory Hook

尤 on its own means "outstanding / especially," and 其 means "that / those." Together, 尤其 points at the one that stands out: "that outstanding one" in the group. It is the star that gets singled out from the rest.
