## 题目 (títu)

题目 covers the title of an essay, book, or speech, the topic or subject of a discussion, and an exam or exercise question. It is closely related to 题 (tí) alone, but 题目 is the full form used in most formal and everyday contexts.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Title; heading (of an essay, article, or work).
2. **[noun]** Topic; subject (of a speech or discussion).
3. **[noun]** Question; exercise problem (in a test or homework assignment).

## Example Sentences

**这篇文章的题目是什么？**
Zhè piān wénzhāng de títu shì shénme?
What is the title of this essay?

**老师给我们出了十道题目。**
Lǎoshī gěi wǒmen chū le shí dào títu.
The teacher gave us ten questions.

**今天演讲的题目是"环境保护"。**
Jīntiān yǎnjiǎng de títu shì "huánjìng bǎohù".
The topic of today's speech is "environmental protection."

**这道题目很难，我做了半个小时才做出来。**
Zhè dào títu hěn nán, wǒ zuò le bàn gè xiǎoshí cái zuò chūlái.
This question was very hard; it took me half an hour to figure out.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 作文题目 | essay title / composition topic |
| 出题目 | set questions |
| 答题目 | answer questions |
| 演讲题目 | speech topic |
| 考试题目 | exam question |

## Memory Hook

题 has the "right" phonetic and 页 (page / head), suggesting a heading. 目 means "eye / item." A 题目 is the headline item your eye lands on first — the title that announces what follows.
