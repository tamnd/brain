## 突然 (tūrán)

突然 describes something that happens unexpectedly and without prior warning.

## Meanings

1. **[adv/adj]** suddenly; all of a sudden; abrupt; unexpected

## Example Sentences

**他突然站起来，大声说了一句话。** Tā tūrán zhàn qǐlái, dàshēng shuō le yī jù huà. He suddenly stood up and said something loudly.

**天气突然变冷了，要多穿衣服。** Tiānqì tūrán biàn lěng le, yào duō chuān yīfu. The weather suddenly got cold, you should wear more clothes.

**会议突然被取消了，大家都很意外。** Huìyì tūrán bèi qǔxiāo le, dàjiā dōu hěn yìwài. The meeting was suddenly cancelled and everyone was surprised.

**她突然笑了起来，让我不知道怎么反应。** Tā tūrán xiào le qǐlái, ràng wǒ bù zhīdào zěnme fǎnyìng. She suddenly burst out laughing and I didn't know how to react.

突 shows something bursting upward through a hole: a sudden eruption that no one saw coming.
