## 来自 (láizì)

来自 is used to express where a person or thing originates from, whether a place, a source, or a background.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to come from; to be from — indicating the origin, source, or place of provenance of a person or thing

## Example Sentences

**他来自北京。** Tā láizì Běijīng. He comes from Beijing.

**这个想法来自一本书。** Zhège xiǎngfǎ láizì yī běn shū. This idea comes from a book.

**班里有很多来自不同国家的学生。** Bān lǐ yǒu hěn duō láizì bùtóng guójiā de xuéshēng. There are many students from different countries in the class.

**这种水果来自南方。** Zhè zhǒng shuǐguǒ láizì nánfāng. This kind of fruit comes from the south.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 来自哪里 | to be from where |
| 来自不同地方 | to come from different places |
| 来自家庭 | to come from family |
| 压力来自… | the pressure comes from… |

## Memory Hook

来 (to come) plus 自 (from; self) — coming from a place: 来自 points to the source.
