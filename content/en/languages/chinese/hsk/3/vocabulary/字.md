## 字 (zì)

字 refers to a written character or script unit in Chinese, and by extension can mean a word, a letter, or someone's handwriting style.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** a written character (a single Chinese character or letter)
2. **[noun]** a word or written form of a word
3. **[noun]** handwriting; one's style of writing

## Example Sentences

**这个字怎么写？** Zhège zì zěnme xiě? How do you write this character?

**他的字写得很漂亮。** Tā de zì xiě de hěn piàoliang. His handwriting is very beautiful.

**这篇文章一共有五百个字。** Zhè piān wénzhāng yīgòng yǒu wǔbǎi gè zì. This article has five hundred characters in total.

**学中文要先学认字。** Xué Zhōngwén yào xiān xué rèn zì. To learn Chinese, you must first learn to recognize characters.

## Memory Hook

The character 字 shows a child (子) under a roof (宀), an image of a child learning to write at home, which is exactly what 字 represents: written characters learned from childhood.
