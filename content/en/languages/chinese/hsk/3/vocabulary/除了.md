## 除了 (chúle)

除了 is a preposition with two contrasting functions. With 以外/之外 and a negative, it means "except" (excluding something from a group). With 以外/之外 and 还/也, it means "besides / in addition to" (adding something extra). Recognizing which pattern is used is key to understanding the meaning.

## Meanings

1. **[preposition]** Except; apart from; excluding (paired with negatives like 不/没).
2. **[preposition]** Besides; in addition to; other than (paired with 还 or 也).

## Example Sentences

**除了他，大家都来了。**
Chúle tā, dàjiā dōu lái le.
Except for him, everyone came.

**除了汉语，她还会说英语和法语。**
Chúle Hànyǔ, tā hái huì shuō Yīngyǔ hé Fǎyǔ.
Besides Chinese, she can also speak English and French.

**除了周末，他每天都要上班。**
Chúle zhōumò, tā měitiān dōu yào shàngbān.
Except on weekends, he goes to work every day.

**除了努力学习，你还需要注意休息。**
Chúle nǔlì xuéxí, nǐ hái xūyào zhùyì xiūxi.
In addition to studying hard, you also need to make sure to rest.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 除了...以外 | except for... / besides... |
| 除了...还... | besides..., also... |
| 除了...都... | except for..., all... |
| 除了这个 | except for this / besides this |
| 除了以上 | except for the above |

## Memory Hook

除 means "to remove / exclude." 了 is a completion marker. 除了 = "having removed it" — you set something aside from the rest. Then decide: are you leaving it out (except) or noting the extras (besides)?
