## 运动 (yùndòng)

运动 covers physical activity broadly: it can mean doing exercise, playing a sport, or engaging in a social or political movement. In everyday speech it most often refers to working out or playing sports. It functions as both a noun and a verb.

## Meanings

1. **[noun / verb]** Exercise; physical activity; to work out.
2. **[noun]** Sport; athletic event.
3. **[noun]** Movement; campaign (social or political).

## Example Sentences

**我每天早上都去运动。**
Wǒ měitiān zǎoshang dōu qù yùndòng.
I exercise every morning.

**你喜欢什么运动？**
Nǐ xǐhuān shénme yùndòng?
What sport do you like?

**多运动对身体有好处。**
Duō yùndòng duì shēntǐ yǒu hǎochù.
Exercising more is good for your health.

**他是学校运动队的成员。**
Tā shì xuéxiào yùndòngduì de chéngyuán.
He is a member of the school sports team.

## Memory Hook

运 means "to transport / move" and 动 means "to move / act." 运动 is movement in motion — your body being transported through space with purpose and energy.
