## 锻炼 (duànliàn)

锻炼 means to exercise or train, whether for physical fitness or to develop a skill or quality.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to exercise; to work out; to train; to toughen

## Example Sentences

**他每天早上去公园锻炼身体。** Tā měitiān zǎoshang qù gōngyuán duànliàn shēntǐ. He goes to the park to exercise every morning.

**锻炼对身体健康非常重要。** Duànliàn duì shēntǐ jiànkāng fēicháng zhòngyào. Exercise is very important for physical health.

**这次实习让我锻炼了很多能力。** Zhè cì shíxí ràng wǒ duànliàn le hěn duō nénglì. This internship helped me develop many abilities.

**坚持锻炼能让你感觉精力充沛。** Jiānchí duànliàn néng ràng nǐ gǎnjué jīnglì chōngpèi. Sticking to exercise makes you feel full of energy.

## Memory Hook

锻 (to forge metal) + 炼 (to refine/smelt): just as a smith forges and refines iron to make it strong, you forge and refine your body through exercise.
