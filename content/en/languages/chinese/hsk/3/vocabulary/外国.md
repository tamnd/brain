## 外国 (wàiguó)

外国 refers to any country that is not one's own, and is commonly used as a modifier in compounds related to foreign people, culture, or language.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** foreign country; abroad — a country other than one's own homeland

## Example Sentences

**他在外国工作了五年。** Tā zài wàiguó gōngzuò le wǔ nián. He worked abroad for five years.

**她对外国文化很感兴趣。** Tā duì wàiguó wénhuà hěn gǎn xìngqù. She is very interested in foreign cultures.

**很多外国人来中国旅游。** Hěn duō wàiguórén lái Zhōngguó lǚyóu. Many foreigners come to China to travel.

**他一直想去外国留学。** Tā yīzhí xiǎng qù wàiguó liúxué. He has always wanted to study abroad.

## Memory Hook

外 (outside) plus 国 (country) — the country outside your own: that is 外国.
