## 数字 (shùzì)

数字 refers to a written numeral or digit, and can also refer to any numerical figure or statistic.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** number; numeral; digit; figure

## Example Sentences

**这个数字太大了，我数不清。** Zhège shùzì tài dà le, wǒ shǔ bù qīng. This number is too large; I cannot count it.

**请在这里填写你的手机号码数字。** Qǐng zài zhèlǐ tiánxiě nǐ de shǒujī hàomǎ shùzì. Please write your mobile phone number digits here.

**那个公司的销售数字每年都在增长。** Nàgè gōngsī de xiāoshòu shùzì měinián dōu zài zēngzhǎng. That company's sales figures grow every year.

**阿拉伯数字比汉字数字更容易写。** Ālābó shùzì bǐ Hànzì shùzì gèng róngyì xiě. Arabic numerals are easier to write than Chinese character numerals.

## Memory Hook

数 means "to count" and 字 means "character/word" — a 数字 is literally a "counting character," a numeral.
