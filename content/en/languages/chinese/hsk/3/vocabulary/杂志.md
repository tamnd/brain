## 杂志 (zázhì)

杂志 means magazine or periodical. 杂 means miscellaneous or varied, and 志 means record or publication. A magazine is a varied collection of articles and records published regularly.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Magazine; periodical; a regularly published collection of articles on various topics.

## Example Sentences

**她喜欢看时尚杂志。**
Tā xǐhuān kàn shíshàng zázhì.
She likes reading fashion magazines.

**这本杂志每月出版一次。**
Zhè běn zázhì měi yuè chūbǎn yī cì.
This magazine is published once a month.

**图书馆里有很多不同类型的杂志。**
Túshūguǎn lǐ yǒu hěn duō bùtóng lèixíng de zázhì.
There are many different types of magazines in the library.

**我在机场买了一本杂志来打发时间。**
Wǒ zài jīchǎng mǎi le yī běn zázhì lái dǎfa shíjiān.
I bought a magazine at the airport to pass the time.

## Memory Hook

杂 (varied/mixed) plus 志 (record). A magazine is a mixed record of many different topics bound together, a little bit of everything in one publication.
