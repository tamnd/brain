## 放弃 (fàngqì)

放弃 means to give up or abandon a goal, plan, idea, or right.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to give up; to abandon; to relinquish; to quit

## Example Sentences

**他放弃了这次去国外学习的机会。** Tā fàngqì le zhè cì qù guówài xuéxí de jīhuì. He gave up the opportunity to study abroad this time.

**不管有多难，都不能放弃。** Bùguǎn yǒu duō nán, dōu bù néng fàngqì. No matter how difficult it is, you must not give up.

**她放弃了原来的工作，开始了自己的事业。** Tā fàngqì le yuánlái de gōngzuò, kāishǐ le zìjǐ de shìyè. She gave up her original job and started her own business.

**我不想放弃这个梦想。** Wǒ bù xiǎng fàngqì zhège mèngxiǎng. I do not want to give up this dream.

## Memory Hook

放 (to release/let go) + 弃 (to abandon): you release your grip and abandon what you were holding onto.
