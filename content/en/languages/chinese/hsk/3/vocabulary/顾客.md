## 顾客 (gùkè)

顾客 refers to a customer or client — a person who buys goods or services from a business. 顾 means "to look after / attend to" and 客 means "guest / visitor." Together they capture the service-oriented mindset: a customer is a guest you attend to.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Customer; client; patron; shopper.

## Example Sentences

**这家餐厅的顾客很多，总是要排队。**
Zhè jiā cāntīng de gùkè hěn duō, zǒngshì yào páiduì.
This restaurant has many customers and there is always a queue.

**顾客就是上帝。**
Gùkè jiùshì shàngdì.
The customer is always right (lit. "customers are God").

**店员热情地招待每一位顾客。**
Diànyuán rèqíng de zhāodài měi yī wèi gùkè.
The shop assistant warmly received every customer.

**这个产品很受顾客欢迎。**
Zhège chǎnpǐn hěn shòu gùkè huānyíng.
This product is very popular with customers.

## Memory Hook

顾 (to look after / attend) plus 客 (guest). A customer is an honored guest you look after — the commercial version of hosting a visitor in your home.
