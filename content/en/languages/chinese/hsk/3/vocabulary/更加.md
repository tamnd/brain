## 更加 (gèngjiā)

更加 is an adverb meaning "even more" or "all the more," used when a quality increases beyond a level that has already been established or mentioned.

## Meanings

1. **[adverb]** even more; still more; all the more (used to indicate that a degree becomes higher or stronger than a previously mentioned or implied level)

## Usage Note

更加 is placed before an adjective or verb. It requires a context or contrast to push against: a baseline that is then exceeded. It is stronger than 更 (gèng) alone, and both are stronger than 很 or 非常 for expressing comparative increase.

Compare:
- 他比我更高。 He is taller than me. (simple comparison using 更)
- 他比我更加高了。 He is even taller than me now. (更加 adds greater emphasis on the increasing degree)

更加 often appears after a condition or cause that explains why the degree has increased further.

## Example Sentences

**经过训练，她的技术更加熟练了。** Jīngguò xùnliàn, tā de jìshù gèngjiā shúliàn le. After training, her skills became even more proficient.

**听了他的故事，我更加佩服他了。** Tīng le tā de gùshi, wǒ gèngjiā pèifú tā le. After hearing his story, I admire him all the more.

**这次失败让他更加努力地学习。** Zhè cì shībài ràng tā gèngjiā nǔlì de xuéxí. This failure made him study even harder.

**天黑了，外面更加安静了。** Tiān hēi le, wàimiàn gèngjiā ānjìng le. It got dark, and outside became even quieter.

## Memory Hook

更 means "even more / further" and 加 means "to add / increase." Together, 更加 means "add even more to what is already there" — degree is piling on top of degree.
