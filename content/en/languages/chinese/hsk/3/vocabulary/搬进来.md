## 搬进来 (bān jìnlái)

搬进来 is a verb with a compound directional complement. 搬 (to move / carry heavy objects) is the base verb; 进来 (come in / enter, toward the speaker) shows that something moves into an enclosed space in the direction of the speaker. Contrast with 搬进去 (move in, away from the speaker).

## Structure

搬 (move / carry) + 进来 (directional complement: enter toward speaker) = move something in here.

Other base verbs with 进来: 走进来 (walk in), 拿进来 (carry in), 带进来 (bring in).

## Example Sentences

**外面下雨了，快把椅子搬进来。**
Wàimiàn xià yǔ le, kuài bǎ yǐzi bān jìnlái.
It is raining outside; quickly bring the chairs inside.

**你帮我把这些书搬进来，放在书架上。**
Nǐ bāng wǒ bǎ zhèxiē shū bān jìnlái, fàng zài shūjià shàng.
Help me move these books inside and put them on the shelf.

**新邻居刚搬进来，我们去打个招呼吧。**
Xīn línjū gāng bān jìnlái, wǒmen qù dǎ gè zhāohu ba.
The new neighbors just moved in; let's go say hello.

**那张桌子太重了，两个人才搬进来。**
Nà zhāng zhuōzi tài zhòng le, liǎng gè rén cái bān jìnlái.
That table was too heavy; it took two people to carry it in.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 搬进去 | move in (away from speaker) |
| 搬出来 | move out here |
| 搬出去 | move out (away) |
| 把...搬进来 | move... in (with 把) |
| 帮忙搬进来 | help move in |

## Memory Hook

搬 (move heavy items — hands working hard) + 进 (enter) + 来 (come here). Something bulky is being carried through the entrance and arriving where you are standing — it comes in to meet you.
