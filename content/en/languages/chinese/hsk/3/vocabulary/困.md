## 困 (kùn)

困 describes the feeling of drowsiness and the need to sleep, especially during the day.

## Meanings

1. **[adj]** sleepy; drowsy; tired and needing sleep

## Example Sentences

**我昨晚没睡好，今天很困。** Wǒ zuówǎn méi shuì hǎo, jīntiān hěn kùn. I didn't sleep well last night and I'm very sleepy today.

**下午两点是最容易困的时候。** Xiàwǔ liǎng diǎn shì zuì róngyì kùn de shíhou. Two in the afternoon is when it's easiest to feel sleepy.

**他困得眼睛都睁不开了。** Tā kùn de yǎnjing dōu zhēng bù kāi le. He was so drowsy he could barely open his eyes.

**喝杯咖啡吧，这样你就不困了。** Hē bēi kāfēi ba, zhèyàng nǐ jiù bù kùn le. Have a cup of coffee; then you won't feel sleepy.

The character 困 shows a tree (木) trapped inside a box: like you, boxed in by sleepiness and unable to move.
