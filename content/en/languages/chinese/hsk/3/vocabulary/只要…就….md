## 只要…就… (zhǐyào...jiù...)

只要…就… expresses a conditional relationship where 只要 introduces the one condition that is sufficient for the result in the 就 clause to follow. It emphasizes that the condition is minimal: this alone is enough.

## Structure

**只要 + [Condition] + 就 + [Result]**

The subject may appear before 只要 or between 只要 and the condition.

## Example Sentences

**只要每天练习，就能进步。**
Zhǐyào měitiān liànxí, jiù néng jìnbù.
As long as you practice every day, you will improve.

**只要你同意，我们就可以开始。**
Zhǐyào nǐ tóngyì, wǒmen jiù kěyǐ kāishǐ.
As long as you agree, we can get started.

**只要天气好，他就去跑步。**
Zhǐyào tiānqì hǎo, tā jiù qù pǎobù.
As long as the weather is good, he goes for a run.

**只要有网络，我就能工作。**
Zhǐyào yǒu wǎngluò, wǒ jiù néng gōngzuò.
As long as there is internet, I can work.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 只要…就… | as long as…then… |
| 只要努力就行 | as long as you work hard, that's enough |
| 只要可以 | as long as it is possible |
| 只要你愿意 | as long as you are willing |
| 只要一个条件 | only one condition needed |

## Memory Hook

只 means "only"; 要 means "require." The pattern says: only require this one thing, and the result is guaranteed. 就 then delivers on that promise.
