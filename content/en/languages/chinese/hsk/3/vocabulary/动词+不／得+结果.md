## 动词+不/得+结果 (Verb + Potential Complement)

The potential complement inserts 得 (de) or 不 (bù) between a verb and its result complement to express whether the action can or cannot achieve the intended result. 得 signals possibility; 不 signals impossibility.

## Structure

- **Affirmative:** Verb + 得 + Result Complement
- **Negative:** Verb + 不 + Result Complement

Common result complements: 完 (wán, finish), 到 (dào, reach/attain), 了 (liǎo, manage to), 懂 (dǒng, understand), 好 (hǎo, do well), 见 (jiàn, perceive).

## Example Sentences

**这本书太难了，我看不懂。**
Zhè běn shū tài nán le, wǒ kàn bù dǒng.
This book is too difficult; I can't understand it.

**放心，这点工作我做得完。**
Fàngxīn, zhè diǎn gōngzuò wǒ zuò de wán.
Don't worry, I can finish this amount of work.

**音乐太小声了，我听不见。**
Yīnyuè tài xiǎoshēng le, wǒ tīng bù jiàn.
The music is too quiet; I can't hear it.

**今天的作业不多，你一定写得完。**
Jīntiān de zuòyè bù duō, nǐ yīdìng xiě de wán.
Today's homework isn't much; you can definitely finish writing it.

## Common Potential Complements

| Pattern | Meaning |
|---------|---------|
| 听得懂 / 听不懂 | can / can't understand (by listening) |
| 看得见 / 看不见 | can / can't see |
| 做得完 / 做不完 | can / can't finish doing |
| 吃得了 / 吃不了 | can / can't manage to eat (all) |
| 写得好 / 写不好 | can / can't write well |

## Memory Hook

得 (de) opens the gate between the action and the result; 不 closes it. Picture a door between the verb and its outcome: 得 means the door swings open, 不 means it stays shut.
