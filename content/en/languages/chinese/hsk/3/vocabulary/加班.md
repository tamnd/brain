## 加班 (jiābān)

加班 refers to working beyond regular working hours, either as a verb (to work overtime) or as a noun (overtime work).

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to work overtime — to continue working past the normal end of the workday
2. **[noun]** overtime — extra work done beyond the regular schedule

## Example Sentences

**我昨天加班到很晚。** Wǒ zuótiān jiābān dào hěn wǎn. I worked overtime until very late yesterday.

**他经常加班，很辛苦。** Tā jīngcháng jiābān, hěn xīnkǔ. He works overtime often; it is very tiring.

**这个月加班太多了。** Zhège yuè jiābān tài duō le. There has been too much overtime this month.

**加班有额外的工资吗？** Jiābān yǒu éwài de gōngzī ma? Is there extra pay for working overtime?

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 经常加班 | to frequently work overtime |
| 加班费 | overtime pay |
| 不想加班 | to not want to work overtime |
| 周末加班 | to work overtime on weekends |

## Memory Hook

加 (to add) plus 班 (shift; class) — adding an extra shift on top of your regular one: 加班.
