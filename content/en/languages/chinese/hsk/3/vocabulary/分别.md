## 分别 (fēnbié)

As an adverb: separately or respectively (each in their own way). As a verb: to part from someone, to say goodbye.

## Meanings

1. **[adverb]** Separately, respectively, individually.
2. **[verb]** To part (from someone), to separate, to say goodbye.

## Example Sentences

**这两道题，请分别回答。**
Zhè liǎng dào tí, qǐng fēnbié huídá.
Please answer these two questions separately.

**他们在机场分别了，都流了眼泪。**
Tāmen zài jīchǎng fēnbié le, dōu liú le yǎnlèi.
They parted at the airport and both shed tears.

**这三件衣服分别是红色、蓝色和绿色的。**
Zhè sān jiàn yīfu fēnbié shì hóngsè, lánsè hé lǜsè de.
These three pieces of clothing are red, blue, and green respectively.

**我们分别去了不同的地方旅游。**
Wǒmen fēnbié qù le bùtóng de dìfāng lǚyóu.
We each went to different places to travel.

## Memory Hook

分 means "to divide/separate" and 别 means "to part/different" — dividing apart is the act of separating or listing each one distinctly.
