## 经验 (jīngyàn)

经验 refers to practical experience gained by doing or living through something. It differs from 经历 (jīnglì — life experience / personal history) in that 经验 emphasizes the lessons and knowledge gained.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Experience; practical knowledge; know-how.

## Example Sentences

**他有丰富的工作经验。**
Tā yǒu fēngfù de gōngzuò jīngyàn.
He has rich work experience.

**这次失败让我积累了很多经验。**
Zhècì shībài ràng wǒ jīlěi le hěn duō jīngyàn.
This failure helped me accumulate a lot of experience.

**根据我的经验，这种方法最有效。**
Gēnjù wǒ de jīngyàn, zhè zhǒng fāngfǎ zuì yǒuxiào.
Based on my experience, this method is most effective.

**没有经验的人很难做好这份工作。**
Méiyǒu jīngyàn de rén hěn nán zuò hǎo zhè fèn gōngzuò.
Someone without experience will find it very hard to do this job well.

## Memory Hook

经 (threads running through cloth — a continuous process) plus 验 (to verify / test). Running through the test of time verifies what you truly know — that is experience.
