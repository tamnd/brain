## 气氛 (qìfēn)

气氛 refers to the overall mood, atmosphere, or feeling of a place, event, or social situation.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** atmosphere; ambiance; mood; vibe

## Example Sentences

**今晚的聚会气氛很热闹。** Jīn wǎn de jùhuì qìfēn hěn rènào. The atmosphere at tonight's gathering is very lively.

**教室里的学习气氛非常好。** Jiàoshì lǐ de xuéxí qìfēn fēicháng hǎo. The study atmosphere in the classroom is very good.

**这家咖啡厅的气氛很轻松舒适。** Zhè jiā kāfēitīng de qìfēn hěn qīngsōng shūshì. The atmosphere of this cafe is very relaxed and comfortable.

**节日的气氛让每个人都感到开心。** Jiérì de qìfēn ràng měi gè rén dōu gǎndào kāixīn. The festive atmosphere makes everyone feel happy.

## Memory Hook

气 means "air/breath/energy" and 氛 also relates to mist or vapors in the air — together they describe the invisible energy that fills a space and sets the mood.
