## 回答 (huídá)

回答 means to answer or reply to a question or inquiry. It is used as both a verb and a noun. As a verb it takes the question or questioner as its object. As a noun it refers to the response itself.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To answer; to reply; to respond (to a question or inquiry).
2. **[noun]** An answer; a response; a reply.

## Example Sentences

**请回答我的问题。**
Qǐng huídá wǒ de wèntí.
Please answer my question.

**他没有回答。**
Tā méiyǒu huídá.
He did not answer.

**她的回答让我很满意。**
Tā de huídá ràng wǒ hěn mǎnyì.
Her answer satisfied me very much.

**你怎么回答他？**
Nǐ zěnme huídá tā?
How did you reply to him?

## Memory Hook

回 means to return and 答 means to answer. A response is a verbal return: someone throws a question at you and you throw an answer back. That exchange is 回答.
