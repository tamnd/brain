## 眼睛 (yǎnjing)

眼睛 means eyes. It refers to the organs of vision on the face. Note that 睛 is pronounced in the neutral tone (jing, not jīng) in this compound.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Eyes; the organs of sight.

## Example Sentences

**她的眼睛很大很漂亮。**
Tā de yǎnjing hěn dà hěn piàoliang.
Her eyes are very big and beautiful.

**我的眼睛有点儿不舒服。**
Wǒ de yǎnjing yǒudiǎnr bù shūfu.
My eyes feel a little uncomfortable.

**请闭上眼睛，放松一下。**
Qǐng bìshang yǎnjing, fàngsōng yīxià.
Please close your eyes and relax.

**看书时间太长，眼睛很累。**
Kàn shū shíjiān tài cháng, yǎnjing hěn lèi.
After reading for too long, my eyes are very tired.

## Memory Hook

眼 is the eye radical and 睛 also contains 目 (eye). Double eye characters make 眼睛 unmistakable. The word looks like two eyes staring back at you from the page.
