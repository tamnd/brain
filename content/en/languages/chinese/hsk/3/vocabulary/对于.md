## 对于 (duìyú)

对于 is a formal preposition meaning regarding, as for, or with respect to. It introduces a topic that the rest of the sentence comments on. It is more formal than 对 and is common in written Chinese and speeches. The topic introduced by 对于 is the frame for the whole statement.

## Meanings

1. **[preposition]** Regarding; as for; with respect to; concerning.

## Example Sentences

**对于这个问题，我有不同的看法。**
Duìyú zhège wèntí, wǒ yǒu bùtóng de kànfǎ.
Regarding this question, I have a different view.

**对于学习，他很认真。**
Duìyú xuéxí, tā hěn rènzhēn.
As for studying, he is very serious.

**对于这件事，我不了解。**
Duìyú zhè jiàn shì, wǒ bù liǎojiě.
I do not know much about this matter.

**对于未来，她充满信心。**
Duìyú wèilái, tā chōngmǎn xìnxīn.
She is full of confidence regarding the future.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 对于...来说 | as far as... is concerned |
| 对于这个问题 | regarding this problem |
| 对于...有看法 | to have a view on... |
| 对于...感兴趣 | interested in... |
| 对于...负责 | to be responsible for... |

## Memory Hook

对 means facing toward and 于 is a classical preposition meaning at/in/to. 对于 frames a topic by pointing directly at it: "facing toward this topic, here is what I think."
