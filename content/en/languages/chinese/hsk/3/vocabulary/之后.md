## 之后 (zhīhòu)

之后 means "after" or "afterwards." It marks a point in time that comes after a specific event, action, or moment. It follows a noun, verb phrase, or clause that identifies the reference point.

## Meanings

1. **[adverb]** After; afterwards; following — indicates that the main action or event takes place after the stated reference point.

### Compare 之后 vs 以后

| Word | Usage |
|------|-------|
| 之后 | Slightly more formal; always requires a preceding reference point (event or time) |
| 以后 | Can stand alone meaning "in the future" or follow a reference point |

## Example Sentences

**吃饭之后，我们去散步吧。**
Chīfàn zhīhòu, wǒmen qù sànbù ba.
After eating, let's go for a walk.

**毕业之后，他去了一家大公司工作。**
Bìyè zhīhòu, tā qù le yī jiā dà gōngsī gōngzuò.
After graduating, he went to work at a large company.

**会议结束之后，大家都回家了。**
Huìyì jiéshù zhīhòu, dàjiā dōu huí jiā le.
After the meeting ended, everyone went home.

**三个月之后，他的中文有了很大的进步。**
Sān gè yuè zhīhòu, tā de Zhōngwén yǒu le hěn dà de jìnbù.
After three months, his Chinese improved greatly.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 此后 | after this; hereafter (formal) |
| …之后不久 | not long after… |
| 完成之后 | after completing |
| 到达之后 | after arriving |
| 放学之后 | after school |

## Memory Hook

之 is a classical possessive/linking particle. 后 means "behind / after." 之后 = "after [what was] mentioned" — standing behind the reference event, looking forward.
