## 面包 (miànbāo)

面包 is the word for bread — the baked product made from flour and eaten widely in Western-style breakfasts, cafes, and bakeries across China. 面 means "flour / noodles" and 包 means "bundle / wrapped thing." The measure word for a loaf is 条 (tiáo) and for a slice or individual roll it is 片 (piàn) or 个 (gè).

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Bread; any baked flour-based bread product.

## Example Sentences

**我早饭吃了两片面包和一个鸡蛋。**
Wǒ zǎofàn chī le liǎng piàn miànbāo hé yīgè jīdàn.
I had two slices of bread and one egg for breakfast.

**这家面包店的面包很好吃。**
Zhè jiā miànbāodiàn de miànbāo hěn hǎo chī.
The bread at this bakery is very delicious.

**你喜欢吃白面包还是全麦面包？**
Nǐ xǐhuān chī bái miànbāo háishi quánmài miànbāo?
Do you prefer white bread or whole-wheat bread?

**冰箱里还有一条面包，你可以吃。**
Bīngxiāng lǐ hái yǒu yī tiáo miànbāo, nǐ kěyǐ chī.
There is still a loaf of bread in the fridge; you can have it.

## Memory Hook

面 (flour) plus 包 (to wrap / bundle). Bread is essentially flour bundled and baked into a rounded shape — a flour bundle. The image is simple and accurate.
