## 解决 (jiějué)

解决 means to resolve a problem or difficulty completely. The action implies bringing a situation to a satisfactory conclusion, not just addressing it temporarily.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To solve; to resolve; to settle; to deal with (a problem).

## Example Sentences

**我们一起来解决这个问题。**
Wǒmen yīqǐ lái jiějué zhège wèntí.
Let's solve this problem together.

**这个问题很快就解决了。**
Zhège wèntí hěn kuài jiù jiějué le.
This problem was resolved very quickly.

**他想了很多办法来解决困难。**
Tā xiǎng le hěn duō bànfǎ lái jiějué kùnnán.
He thought of many methods to resolve the difficulty.

**这件事还没有解决。**
Zhè jiàn shì hái méiyǒu jiějué.
This matter has not yet been resolved.

## Memory Hook

解 means "to untie" (a hand 角 loosening a knot with a knife 刀). 决 means "decided." Untie the knot and make the decisive cut — the problem is solved.
