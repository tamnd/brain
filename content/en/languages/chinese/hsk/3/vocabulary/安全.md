## 安全 (ānquán)

安全 means safe or safety. As an adjective it describes a person, place, or situation as free from danger. As a noun it refers to the concept of safety or security. It is a high-frequency word in everyday life and public announcements.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** Safe; secure; free from danger.
2. **[noun]** Safety; security.

## Example Sentences

**过马路要注意安全。**
Guò mǎlù yào zhùyì ānquán.
Pay attention to safety when crossing the road.

**这里很安全，不用担心。**
Zhèlǐ hěn ānquán, bú yòng dānxīn.
It is very safe here; do not worry.

**安全第一。**
Ānquán dì yī.
Safety first.

**你到了吗？安全吗？**
Nǐ dào le ma? Ānquán ma?
Have you arrived? Are you safe?

## Memory Hook

安 shows a woman (女) under a roof (宀), the image of domestic safety and calm. 全 means complete or whole. When you are 安全, you are completely at rest under your roof, whole and unhurt.
