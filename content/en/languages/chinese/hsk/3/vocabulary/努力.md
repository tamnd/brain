## 努力 (nǔlì)

To exert great effort toward a goal; also used as an adjective to describe a hard-working person and as a noun meaning "effort."

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To work hard, to make an effort, to strive.
2. **[adjective]** Hard-working, diligent, industrious.
3. **[noun]** Effort, hard work.

## Example Sentences

**他很努力，每天都学习到很晚。**
Tā hěn nǔlì, měitiān dōu xuéxí dào hěn wǎn.
He is very hard-working and studies until very late every day.

**只要努力，就一定能成功。**
Zhǐyào nǔlì, jiù yīdìng néng chénggōng.
As long as you work hard, you will certainly succeed.

**她的成功来自多年的努力。**
Tā de chénggōng láizì duō nián de nǔlì.
Her success comes from years of effort.

**我们要更加努力地工作。**
Wǒmen yào gèngjiā nǔlì de gōngzuò.
We need to work even harder.

## Memory Hook

努 contains the element for "woman/strength" and 力 means "strength/force" — channeling all your strength to push forward.
