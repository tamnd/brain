## 差不多 (chàbuduō)

差不多 is used to say that two things are about the same, or that something is nearly complete or approximately true.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** about the same; similar; nearly equal
2. **[adverb]** almost; nearly; approximately

## Example Sentences

**这两件衣服的价格差不多。** Zhè liǎng jiàn yīfu de jiàgé chàbuduō. The prices of these two pieces of clothing are about the same.

**他差不多每天都来这里运动。** Tā chàbuduō měitiān dōu lái zhèlǐ yùndòng. He comes here to exercise almost every day.

**我的汉语和他的差不多。** Wǒ de Hànyǔ hé tā de chàbuduō. My Chinese is about the same level as his.

**作业差不多做完了，再有十分钟就好。** Zuòyè chàbuduō zuò wán le, zài yǒu shí fēnzhōng jiù hǎo. The homework is almost done; another ten minutes and it will be finished.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 差不多了 | almost done; almost there |
| 差不多一样 | almost the same |
| 差不多每天 | almost every day |
| 差不多吧 | probably; I suppose so |
| 差不多的价格 | about the same price |

## Memory Hook

差 (difference) + 不多 (not much): the difference is not much, so it is almost the same.
