## 兴趣 (xìngqù)

兴趣 describes the interest or enthusiasm one feels toward an activity, subject, or person.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** interest; hobby; enthusiasm for something

## Example Sentences

**他对历史很有兴趣。** Tā duì lìshǐ hěn yǒu xìngqù. He has great interest in history.

**你有什么兴趣爱好？** Nǐ yǒu shénme xìngqù àihào? What interests and hobbies do you have?

**孩子们对这个游戏产生了浓厚的兴趣。** Háizimen duì zhège yóuxì chǎnshēng le nónghòu de xìngqù. The children developed a strong interest in this game.

**我对学汉语的兴趣越来越大。** Wǒ duì xué Hànyǔ de xìngqù yuèlái yuè dà. My interest in learning Chinese is growing more and more.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 有兴趣 | to have interest; to be interested |
| 对…感兴趣 | to be interested in... |
| 兴趣爱好 | interests and hobbies |
| 浓厚的兴趣 | strong/deep interest |

## Memory Hook

兴 relates to "excitement/elation" and 趣 means "fun/interesting" — 兴趣 is the excited fun you feel when drawn to something.
