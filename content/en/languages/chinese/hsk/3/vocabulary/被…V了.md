## 被…V了 (bèi...V le)

被…V了 is the standard way to form a passive sentence in Mandarin. 被 introduces the agent (who performed the action), the verb follows, and 了 marks the action as completed. The construction often implies an unwanted or unexpected outcome for the subject.

## Structure

**Subject + 被 + (Agent) + Verb + 了**

The agent after 被 is optional. When the agent is unknown or unimportant, it may be omitted: **Subject + 被 + Verb + 了**.

## Example Sentences

**我的钱包被人偷了。**
Wǒ de qiánbāo bèi rén tōu le.
My wallet was stolen.

**那扇窗户被风吹开了。**
Nà shàn chuānghù bèi fēng chuīkāi le.
That window was blown open by the wind.

**他的提议被大家接受了。**
Tā de tíyì bèi dàjiā jiēshòu le.
His proposal was accepted by everyone.

**蛋糕被小孩子吃完了。**
Dàngāo bèi xiǎo háizi chīwán le.
The cake was all eaten up by the child.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 被偷了 | was stolen |
| 被批评了 | was criticized |
| 被选上了 | was chosen, selected |
| 被发现了 | was discovered |
| 被吃完了 | was completely eaten up |

## Memory Hook

Think of 被 as a "hit by" marker. The subject is on the receiving end of an action, and 了 stamps the result as done. Subject → was hit by → agent → verb → done.
