## 把…V完 (bǎ...V wán)

把…V完 combines the disposal marker 把 with the result complement 完 (wán, to finish) to express that someone completely used up, finished, or disposed of the object. The focus is on the object being fully dealt with.

## Structure

**Subject + 把 + Object + Verb + 完 (+ 了)**

了 is often added at the end to mark completion as a past event.

## Example Sentences

**他把作业做完了。**
Tā bǎ zuòyè zuòwán le.
He finished all his homework.

**请把这碗饭吃完。**
Qǐng bǎ zhè wǎn fàn chīwán.
Please finish this bowl of rice.

**我把那本书看完了，非常精彩。**
Wǒ bǎ nà běn shū kànwán le, fēicháng jīngcǎi.
I finished reading that book; it was wonderful.

**她把所有的钱都花完了。**
Tā bǎ suǒyǒu de qián dōu huāwán le.
She spent all her money.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 把饭吃完 | finish the meal |
| 把作业做完 | finish the homework |
| 把书看完 | finish reading the book |
| 把水喝完 | finish the water |
| 把钱花完 | spend all the money |

## Memory Hook

把 grabs the object; 完 seals the deal. Together they say: "I took hold of this thing and saw it all the way through to the end."
