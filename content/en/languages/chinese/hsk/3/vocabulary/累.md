## 累 (lèi)

累 describes the state of being tired or exhausted, whether from physical activity, mental effort, or a long day.

## Meanings

1. **[adj]** tired; exhausted; fatigued; worn out

## Example Sentences

**工作了一整天，我真的很累。** Gōngzuò le yī zhěng tiān, wǒ zhēn de hěn lèi. After working a whole day, I am really tired.

**你看起来很累，早点休息吧。** Nǐ kàn qǐlái hěn lèi, zǎodiǎn xiūxi ba. You look very tired; get some rest early.

**爬山之后，大家都累坏了。** Páshān zhīhòu, dàjiā dōu lèi huài le. After climbing the mountain, everyone was exhausted.

**照顾小孩子真的很累，但也很快乐。** Zhàogù xiǎo háizi zhēn de hěn lèi, dàn yě hěn kuàilè. Taking care of small children is truly tiring, but also very joyful.

## Memory Hook

The character 累 shows threads (silk/rope) piled on top of a field (田) — like endless tasks stacking up until you are weighed down and tired.
