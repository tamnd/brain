## 发展 (fāzhǎn)

发展 functions as both a noun and a verb. As a noun it means development or growth. As a verb it means to develop, expand, or grow. It is widely used for economic, personal, technological, and social contexts.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Development; growth; progress.
2. **[verb]** To develop; to expand; to grow.

## Example Sentences

**中国的经济发展很快。**
Zhōngguó de jīngjì fāzhǎn hěn kuài.
China's economic development is very fast.

**我们需要发展新技术。**
Wǒmen xūyào fāzhǎn xīn jìshù.
We need to develop new technology.

**这个城市的发展让我印象深刻。**
Zhège chéngshì de fāzhǎn ràng wǒ yìnxiàng shēnkè.
The development of this city deeply impressed me.

**公司正在发展海外市场。**
Gōngsī zhèngzài fāzhǎn hǎiwài shìchǎng.
The company is expanding into overseas markets.

## Memory Hook

发 means to emit or send out and 展 means to spread out or unfold. Development is like a seed sending out roots that spread in every direction — emitting growth and unfolding potential.
