## 包括 (bāokuò)

包括 means to include or to comprise. It is used to list what is contained within a set, group, or scope. It often appears at the start of a list introduced by a general statement.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To include; to comprise; to consist of.

## Example Sentences

**这个价格包括早餐。**
Zhège jiàgé bāokuò zǎocān.
This price includes breakfast.

**我们班包括三十个学生。**
Wǒmen bān bāokuò sānshí gè xuésheng.
Our class includes thirty students.

**他的工作包括很多内容。**
Tā de gōngzuò bāokuò hěn duō nèiróng.
His work includes many different things.

**这本书包括五个章节。**
Zhè běn shū bāokuò wǔ gè zhāngjié.
This book comprises five chapters.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 包括在内 | included within |
| 不包括 | not including, excluding |
| 包括...在内 | including... |
| 主要包括 | mainly includes |
| 包括以下 | includes the following |

## Memory Hook

包 means to wrap and 括 means to enclose with brackets. Together they form "wrap and enclose" — 包括 gathers everything inside its scope, just as brackets enclose a list.
