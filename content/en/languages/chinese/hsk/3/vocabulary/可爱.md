## 可爱 (kě'ài)

可爱 describes something or someone that is cute, adorable, or charming in an endearing way.

## Meanings

1. **[adj]** cute; adorable; lovely; charming

## Example Sentences

**这只小狗真可爱！** Zhè zhī xiǎogǒu zhēn kě'ài! This little dog is so cute!

**她的女儿长得很可爱，人人都喜欢她。** Tā de nǚ'ér zhǎng de hěn kě'ài, rén rén dōu xǐhuān tā. Her daughter is very cute and everyone likes her.

**你画的小熊猫看起来很可爱。** Nǐ huà de xiǎo xióngmāo kàn qǐlái hěn kě'ài. The little panda you drew looks very cute.

**他虽然严肃，但有时候说的话很可爱。** Tā suīrán yánsù, dàn yǒushíhou shuō de huà hěn kě'ài. He seems serious, but sometimes the things he says are quite adorable.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 长得可爱 | to look cute |
| 非常可爱 | very cute |
| 可爱的孩子 | cute child |
| 可爱的动物 | cute animal |
| 打扮得可爱 | dressed in a cute way |

## Memory Hook

可 means "worthy of" and 爱 means "love" — something 可爱 is literally worthy of being loved, which is exactly what cute things are.
