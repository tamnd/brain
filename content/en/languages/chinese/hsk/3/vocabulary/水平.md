## 水平 (shuǐpíng)

水平 refers to the level or standard of skill, quality, or ability that someone or something has reached.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** level; standard; proficiency — the degree of skill, ability, or quality achieved in a particular area

## Example Sentences

**他的中文水平很高。** Tā de Zhōngwén shuǐpíng hěn gāo. His Chinese proficiency is very high.

**我需要提高自己的英语水平。** Wǒ xūyào tígāo zìjǐ de Yīngyǔ shuǐpíng. I need to improve my English level.

**这家餐厅的烹饪水平非常专业。** Zhè jiā cāntīng de pēngrèn shuǐpíng fēicháng zhuānyè. The cooking standard at this restaurant is very professional.

**两队的水平相差不大。** Liǎng duì de shuǐpíng xiāngchā bù dà. The two teams are not far apart in terms of level.

水 (water) plus 平 (flat; level) — water always finds its own level: your skill finds its 水平 too.
