## 气候 (qìhòu)

气候 means climate. It refers to the long-term weather patterns typical of a region, as opposed to 天气 (tiānqì), which is the weather on any given day. 气 means air or atmosphere, and 候 means season or time period.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Climate; the typical long-term weather conditions of a place or region.

## Example Sentences

**这里的气候四季分明。**
Zhèlǐ de qìhòu sìjì fēnmíng.
The climate here has four distinct seasons.

**全球气候变化是一个严重的问题。**
Quánqiú qìhòu biànhuà shì yī gè yánzhòng de wèntí.
Global climate change is a serious problem.

**我喜欢这里温暖的气候。**
Wǒ xǐhuān zhèlǐ wēnnuǎn de qìhòu.
I like the warm climate here.

**热带气候的地方雨水很多。**
Rèdài qìhòu de dìfang yǔshuǐ hěn duō.
Places with a tropical climate have a lot of rainfall.

## Memory Hook

气 (air/atmosphere) plus 候 (season/period). Climate is the atmosphere measured over a long period. Think of checking the air over many seasons to describe a place's climate.
