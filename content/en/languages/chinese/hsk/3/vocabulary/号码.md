## 号码 (hàomǎ)

号码 refers to an identifying number such as a phone number, room number, or ticket number.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** number; numeral used for identification (phone, room, seat, etc.)

## Example Sentences

**你能把你的电话号码告诉我吗？** Nǐ néng bǎ nǐ de diànhuà hàomǎ gàosù wǒ ma? Can you tell me your phone number?

**我的房间号码是三零六。** Wǒ de fángjiān hàomǎ shì sān líng liù. My room number is 306.

**他忘了自己的银行卡号码。** Tā wàng le zìjǐ de yínhángkǎ hàomǎ. He forgot his bank card number.

**请写下你的座位号码。** Qǐng xiě xià nǐ de zuòwèi hàomǎ. Please write down your seat number.

## Memory Hook

号 means "number/sign" and 码 means "code" — a 号码 is the coded number that identifies something specifically.
