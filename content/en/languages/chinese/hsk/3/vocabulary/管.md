## 管 (guǎn)

管 means to manage, oversee, or be responsible for. It also means to bother with or concern oneself with, and appears in the important phrase 不管 meaning no matter or regardless.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To manage; to be in charge of; to oversee.
2. **[verb]** To concern oneself with; to bother about; to interfere in.
3. **[verb]** To discipline; to control.

## Example Sentences

**这件事谁来管？**
Zhè jiàn shì shuí lái guǎn?
Who is in charge of this matter?

**他管不了那么多事。**
Tā guǎn bùliǎo nàme duō shì.
He cannot handle that many things.

**不管天气怎么样，我都要去。**
Bùguǎn tiānqì zěnmeyàng, wǒ dōu yào qù.
No matter what the weather is like, I will go.

**你别管我的事！**
Nǐ bié guǎn wǒ de shì!
Mind your own business! (Don't interfere in my affairs!)

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |

| 管得着 | to have the right to interfere |

## Memory Hook

管 looks like a pipe (竹 bamboo tube). A pipe controls what flows through it, just as 管 controls people or situations. 不管 means you are not controlling anything, regardless of what flows through.
