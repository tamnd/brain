## 词语 (cíyǔ)

词语 refers to individual words or set phrases in the Chinese language, often used in the context of vocabulary study.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** word; phrase; expression — a unit of vocabulary, including single words and fixed multi-character expressions

## Example Sentences

**这个词语很难记。** Zhège cíyǔ hěn nán jì. This word is hard to remember.

**请用这个词语造一个句子。** Qǐng yòng zhège cíyǔ zào yī gè jùzi. Please make a sentence using this word.

**学习新词语需要多练习。** Xuéxí xīn cíyǔ xūyào duō liànxí. Learning new vocabulary requires a lot of practice.

**这本书里有很多有用的词语。** Zhè běn shū lǐ yǒu hěn duō yǒuyòng de cíyǔ. This book contains many useful words and expressions.

## Memory Hook

词 means "word" and 语 means "language" — a 词语 is the building block of language, a single word or phrase.
