## 搬 (bān)

搬 means to move something from one place to another, usually by carrying it. It is used both for moving objects and for moving house or residence. 搬家 (to move house) is one of the most common compounds.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To move an object by carrying it from one place to another.
2. **[verb]** To move house; to relocate one's residence.

## Example Sentences

**帮我搬一下这张桌子。**
Bāng wǒ bān yīxià zhè zhāng zhuōzi.
Help me move this table.

**我们下个月要搬家。**
Wǒmen xià gè yuè yào bānjiā.
We are moving house next month.

**他搬到了一个新城市。**
Tā bān dào le yī gè xīn chéngshì.
He moved to a new city.

**这些箱子太重了，一个人搬不动。**
Zhèxiē xiāngzi tài zhòng le, yī gè rén bān bu dòng.
These boxes are too heavy; one person cannot move them.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 搬家 | to move house |
| 搬进 | to move into |
| 搬出 | to move out of |
| 搬走 | to move away |
| 搬运 | to transport; to haul |

## Memory Hook

搬 has the hand radical (扌) and a component suggesting effort. Picture gripping something heavy with both hands and heaving it across the room. That physical effort of relocation is 搬.
