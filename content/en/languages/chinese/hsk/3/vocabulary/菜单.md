## 菜单 (càidān)

菜单 means menu. It is the list of dishes and drinks available at a restaurant. 菜 means dish and 单 means a list or slip of paper.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Menu; bill of fare.

## Example Sentences

**请给我看看菜单。**
Qǐng gěi wǒ kànkan càidān.
Please let me see the menu.

**服务员，这里的菜单有英文版吗？**
Fúwùyuán, zhèlǐ de càidān yǒu yīngwén bǎn ma?
Waiter, does the menu here have an English version?

**他看了半天菜单，还不知道点什么。**
Tā kàn le bàntiān càidān, hái bù zhīdào diǎn shénme.
He looked at the menu for a long time but still did not know what to order.

**这家餐厅的菜单上有很多素菜。**
Zhè jiā cāntīng de càidān shang yǒu hěn duō sùcài.
The menu at this restaurant has many vegetarian dishes.

## Memory Hook

菜 (dish) plus 单 (single sheet, list). A 菜单 is literally a list sheet of dishes. Picture a single piece of paper covered in the names of every dish the kitchen can make.
