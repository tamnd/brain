## 奇怪 (qíguài)

奇怪 describes something that is strange, odd, or unexpected — outside of normal behavior, appearance, or events. It can also express puzzlement at something unusual.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** Strange; odd; weird; unusual.

## Example Sentences

**这件事很奇怪，我想不明白。**
Zhè jiàn shì hěn qíguài, wǒ xiǎng bu míngbái.
This thing is very strange; I can't figure it out.

**他今天的行为很奇怪。**
Tā jīntiān de xíngwéi hěn qíguài.
His behavior today is very strange.

**奇怪，钥匙怎么不见了？**
Qíguài, yàoshi zěnme bú jiàn le?
How strange — where did the key go?

**她有一些奇怪的习惯，比如总是站着吃饭。**
Tā yǒu yīxiē qíguài de xíguàn, bǐrú zǒngshì zhàn zhe chīfàn.
She has some odd habits, like always eating standing up.

## Memory Hook

奇 (odd / singular — a big 大 person on a horse 可, standing out from the crowd) plus 怪 (weird). Something stands out so much it becomes weird — that is 奇怪.
