## 尽管 (jǐnguǎn)

尽管 is a conjunction that introduces a concessive clause, acknowledging a fact or obstacle while stating that it does not prevent the outcome in the main clause.

## Meanings

1. **[conjunction]** although; despite; even though (used to introduce a concessive condition that does not change the result)

## Usage Note

尽管 introduces the subordinate (concessive) clause, and the main clause often uses 还是 (háishi, still), 仍然 (réngrán, still), 却 (què, but/yet), or 但是 (dànshì, but) to show contrast. The structure is:

尽管 + [concessive clause], [main clause with 还是/仍然/但是]

Compare with 虽然...但是, which is essentially interchangeable. 尽管 can feel slightly more emphatic, stressing that the obstacle is real but ultimately irrelevant to the outcome.

- 尽管很难，他还是坚持下去了。 Although it was very hard, he still persisted.
- 虽然很难，但是他坚持下去了。 Although it was very hard, he persisted.

## Example Sentences

**尽管天气很冷，他每天仍然去跑步。** Jǐnguǎn tiānqì hěn lěng, tā měitiān réngrán qù pǎobù. Although the weather is very cold, he still goes running every day.

**尽管失败了很多次，她还是没有放弃。** Jǐnguǎn shībài le hěn duō cì, tā háishi méiyǒu fàngqì. Despite failing many times, she still did not give up.

**尽管他不太同意，但最终接受了这个决定。** Jǐnguǎn tā bù tài tóngyì, dàn zuìzhōng jiēshòu le zhège juédìng. Although he did not quite agree, he eventually accepted the decision.

**尽管我们来自不同的国家，我们却成了好朋友。** Jǐnguǎn wǒmen láizì bùtóng de guójiā, wǒmen què chéngle hǎo péngyou. Even though we come from different countries, we became good friends.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 尽管...还是... | although... still... |
| 尽管...但是... | although... but... |
| 尽管...仍然... | despite... still... |
| 尽管...却... | even though... yet... |
| 尽管如此 | even so; despite this |

## Memory Hook

尽管 contains 尽 (jìn/jǐn, "to the fullest extent") and 管 ("to manage / to concern"). Think of it as "no matter how much this concerns you," the result still holds. The obstacle is acknowledged to the fullest, yet it changes nothing.
