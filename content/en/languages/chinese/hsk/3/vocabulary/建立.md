## 建立 (jiànlì)

建立 means to create or found something that did not previously exist, such as a system, relationship, or organization.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to establish; to build; to set up; to found; to create

## Example Sentences

**两国之间建立了良好的外交关系。** Liǎng guó zhījiān jiànlì le liánghǎo de wàijiāo guānxi. The two countries established good diplomatic relations.

**他努力建立自己的事业。** Tā nǔlì jiànlì zìjǐ de shìyè. He worked hard to build his own career.

**我们应该建立一个诚实的沟通方式。** Wǒmen yīnggāi jiànlì yī gè chéngshí de gōutōng fāngshì. We should establish an honest way of communicating.

**学校建立了新的奖学金制度。** Xuéxiào jiànlì le xīn de jiǎngxuéjīn zhìdù. The school set up a new scholarship system.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|

## Memory Hook

建 means "to construct" and 立 means "to stand/erect." 建立 is constructing something until it stands on its own.
