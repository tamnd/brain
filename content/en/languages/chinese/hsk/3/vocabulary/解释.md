## 解释 (jiěshì)

解释 means to explain or interpret. It is used when clarifying something complex, giving reasons for behavior, or providing a translation of meaning. It also functions as a noun meaning an explanation.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To explain; to clarify; to interpret; to give reasons.
2. **[noun]** Explanation; interpretation; clarification.

## Example Sentences

**请你解释一下这句话的意思。**
Qǐng nǐ jiěshì yīxià zhè jù huà de yìsi.
Please explain the meaning of this sentence briefly.

**他解释了为什么迟到了。**
Tā jiěshì le wèishénme chídào le.
He explained why he was late.

**这个现象很难解释。**
Zhège xiànxiàng hěn nán jiěshì.
This phenomenon is very hard to explain.

**她的解释让我明白了一切。**
Tā de jiěshì ràng wǒ míngbái le yīqiè.
Her explanation made me understand everything.

## Memory Hook

解 means to undo or untie, and 释 means to release or explain. When you 解释, you untie the knot of confusion and release understanding. A good explanation unravels the tangle so everything becomes clear.
