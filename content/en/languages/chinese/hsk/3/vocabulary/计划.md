## 计划 (jìhuà)

计划 refers to a more formal or structured plan, often written out or thought through in advance. As a verb it means to plan. It is more systematic than 打算 (dǎsuàn), which describes personal intentions.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Plan; program; schedule.
2. **[verb]** To plan; to schedule.

## Example Sentences

**我们制定了一个旅游计划。**
Wǒmen zhìdìng le yīgè lǚyóu jìhuà.
We drew up a travel plan.

**这个项目的计划已经完成了。**
Zhège xiàngmù de jìhuà yǐjīng wánchéng le.
The plan for this project has been completed.

**她按计划完成了所有的工作。**
Tā àn jìhuà wánchéng le suǒyǒu de gōngzuò.
She completed all the work according to plan.

**我们需要改变原来的计划。**
Wǒmen xūyào gǎibiàn yuánlái de jìhuà.
We need to change the original plan.

## Memory Hook

计 (calculate / plan — speech radical 讠 plus ten 十, counting precisely) plus 划 (to mark out / delineate). Carefully counting out and marking boundaries — the precision of a real plan.
