## 特点 (tèdiǎn)

特点 refers to the distinctive qualities or features that set something or someone apart from others.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** characteristic; feature; distinctive quality; trait

## Example Sentences

**这种水果的特点是又甜又多汁。** Zhè zhǒng shuǐguǒ de tèdiǎn shì yòu tián yòu duō zhī. The characteristic of this fruit is that it is both sweet and juicy.

**每个地方都有自己的文化特点。** Měi gè dìfāng dōu yǒu zìjǐ de wénhuà tèdiǎn. Every place has its own cultural characteristics.

**你能介绍一下这个产品的特点吗？** Nǐ néng jièshào yīxià zhège chǎnpǐn de tèdiǎn ma? Can you introduce the features of this product?

**她的特点是做事认真，从不马虎。** Tā de tèdiǎn shì zuòshì rènzhēn, cóng bù mǎhu. Her characteristic is being thorough and never careless.

## Memory Hook

特 means "special/unique" and 点 means "point/dot" — a 特点 is a special point that makes something stand out.
