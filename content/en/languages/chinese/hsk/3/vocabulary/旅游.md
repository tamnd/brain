## 旅游 (lǚyóu)

旅游 means to travel for pleasure or sightseeing, and also refers to tourism as an activity or industry.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to travel; to go on a trip; to tour
2. **[noun]** tourism; travel; a trip

## Example Sentences

**我很喜欢旅游，每年都会去一个新地方。** Wǒ hěn xǐhuān lǚyóu, měi nián dōu huì qù yī gè xīn dìfāng. I love traveling and go to a new place every year.

**他们去云南旅游了两个星期。** Tāmen qù Yúnnán lǚyóu le liǎng gè xīngqī. They traveled to Yunnan for two weeks.

**旅游可以让人开阔眼界。** Lǚyóu kěyǐ ràng rén kāikuò yǎnjiè. Travel can broaden one's horizons.

**中国的旅游业发展越来越快。** Zhōngguó de lǚyóuyè fāzhǎn yuè lái yuè kuài. China's tourism industry is developing faster and faster.

## Memory Hook

旅 means "to travel/journey" and 游 means "to roam/swim" — 旅游 is the free-flowing journey of a traveler roaming from place to place.
