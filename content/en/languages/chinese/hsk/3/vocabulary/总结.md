## 总结 (zǒngjié)

总结 means both a summary and the act of summarizing. As a verb it means to sum up or draw conclusions. As a noun it refers to a summary, wrap-up, or conclusion. It is used in academic, professional, and everyday contexts.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To summarize; to sum up; to draw a conclusion.
2. **[noun]** A summary; a conclusion; a wrap-up.

## Example Sentences

**请总结一下今天学的内容。**
Qǐng zǒngjié yīxià jīntiān xué de nèiróng.
Please summarize what we learned today.

**他做了一个很好的总结。**
Tā zuò le yī gè hěn hǎo de zǒngjié.
He gave a very good summary.

**总结经验，改进工作。**
Zǒngjié jīngyàn, gǎijìn gōngzuò.
Summarize the experience and improve the work.

**会议最后，领导做了总结。**
Huìyì zuìhòu, lǐngdǎo zuò le zǒngjié.
At the end of the meeting, the leader gave a summary.

## Memory Hook

总 means to gather everything together, and 结 means to tie or conclude. 总结 is the act of tying all your thoughts together into one neat bundle at the end. It is the bow on the package.
