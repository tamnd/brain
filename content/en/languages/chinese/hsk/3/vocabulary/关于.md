## 关于 (guānyú)

关于 means about or concerning — it introduces the topic that a statement, book, discussion, or question is focused on. It often appears at the start of a sentence.

## Meanings

1. **[preposition]** About; concerning; regarding; on the subject of.

## Example Sentences

**我有一个关于这件事的问题。**
Wǒ yǒu yīgè guānyú zhè jiàn shì de wèntí.
I have a question about this matter.

**这本书是关于中国历史的。**
Zhè běn shū shì guānyú Zhōngguó lìshǐ de.
This book is about Chinese history.

**关于这个话题，大家的意见不一样。**
Guānyú zhège huàtí, dàjiā de yìjiàn bù yīyàng.
Regarding this topic, everyone has different opinions.

**我们今天讨论的是关于环境保护的问题。**
Wǒmen jīntiān tǎolùn de shì guānyú huánjìng bǎohù de wèntí.
Today we are discussing the issue of environmental protection.

## Memory Hook

关 (to close / concern — 关 gates of concern) plus 于 (at / in / regarding). The gates of your concern opening onto a topic — that is what关于 frames.
