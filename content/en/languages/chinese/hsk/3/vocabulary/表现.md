## 表现 (biǎoxiàn)

表现 refers to how someone behaves or performs, and can also mean to display or show something.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to show; to display; to demonstrate
2. **[noun]** performance; behavior; showing

## Example Sentences

**这个学生在课堂上表现得很好。** Zhège xuéshēng zài kètáng shàng biǎoxiàn de hěn hǎo. This student performs very well in class.

**她的表现让老师非常满意。** Tā de biǎoxiàn ràng lǎoshī fēicháng mǎnyì. Her performance made the teacher very satisfied.

**他想通过努力工作来表现自己的能力。** Tā xiǎng tōngguò nǔlì gōngzuò lái biǎoxiàn zìjǐ de nénglì. He wants to demonstrate his abilities through hard work.

**孩子们在舞台上的表现让家长们都鼓掌。** Háizimen zài wǔtái shàng de biǎoxiàn ràng jiāzhǎngmen dōu gǔzhǎng. The children's performance on stage made all the parents applaud.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 表现好 | to perform well |
| 表现出色 | to perform outstandingly |
| 工作表现 | work performance |
| 表现自己 | to show oneself; to stand out |
| 表现得 | to behave/perform (in a manner) |

## Memory Hook

表 (surface/outside) + 现 (to appear): what appears on the outside is your 表现.
