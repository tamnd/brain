## 赶 (gǎn)

赶 means to rush to a place or hurry to catch something such as a bus or train before it departs.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to rush; to hurry; to catch (a bus, train, etc.); to drive away

## Example Sentences

**我要赶火车，得快点儿走。** Wǒ yào gǎn huǒchē, děi kuài diǎnr zǒu. I need to catch the train, so I have to move faster.

**她早上起晚了，赶着去上班。** Tā zǎoshang qǐ wǎn le, gǎnzhe qù shàngbān. She woke up late in the morning and rushed to work.

**我赶不上那班飞机了。** Wǒ gǎn bù shàng nà bān fēijī le. I cannot make that flight anymore.

**他们赶到医院的时候，手术已经结束了。** Tāmen gǎndào yīyuàn de shíhòu, shǒushù yǐjīng jiéshù le. By the time they rushed to the hospital, the surgery was already over.

## Collocations

## Memory Hook

Picture yourself sprinting after a bus that is pulling away; that urgent chase is exactly what 赶 feels like.
