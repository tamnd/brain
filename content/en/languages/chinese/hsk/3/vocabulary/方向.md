## 方向 (fāngxiàng)

方向 means direction, referring to the way something points, moves, or the course one takes in life or work.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** direction; orientation; course

## Example Sentences

**你知道车站在哪个方向吗？** Nǐ zhīdào chēzhàn zài nǎge fāngxiàng ma? Do you know which direction the station is in?

**我在这个城市总是搞不清楚方向。** Wǒ zài zhège chéngshì zǒngshì gǎo bù qīngchǔ fāngxiàng. I can never get my sense of direction straight in this city.

**他找到了自己未来的发展方向。** Tā zhǎodào le zìjǐ wèilái de fāzhǎn fāngxiàng. He found the direction for his future development.

**向北走，然后往左转，方向就对了。** Xiàng běi zǒu, ránhòu wǎng zuǒ zhuǎn, fāngxiàng jiù duì le. Go north, then turn left, and the direction will be correct.

## Memory Hook

方 (square/direction) + 向 (facing toward): wherever you are squared up and facing is your 方向.
