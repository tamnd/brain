## 成熟 (chéngshú)

成熟 describes something that has reached full development, whether fruit ripening on a tree or a person's emotional and intellectual growth.

## Meanings

1. **[adj/verb]** mature; ripe; fully developed; to ripen; to come of age emotionally

## Example Sentences

**这些苹果已经成熟，可以摘了。** Zhèxiē píngguǒ yǐjīng chéngshú, kěyǐ zhāi le. These apples are ripe and can be picked.

**经历了很多事，他变得更成熟了。** Jīnglì le hěn duō shì, tā biàn de gèng chéngshú le. Having gone through a lot, he has become more mature.

**这个计划还不成熟，需要再讨论。** Zhège jìhuà hái bù chéngshú, xūyào zài tǎolùn. This plan is not yet mature; it needs further discussion.

**她思考问题的方式非常成熟。** Tā sīkǎo wèntí de fāngshì fēicháng chéngshú. The way she thinks about problems is very mature.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 成熟的想法 | mature thinking |

## Memory Hook

成 means "to become/accomplish" and 熟 means "cooked/ripe." Something mature has been fully cooked by time and experience.
