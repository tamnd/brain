## 方法 (fāngfǎ)

方法 means a method or approach — a systematic or deliberate way of doing something. It is more structured than 办法 (bànfǎ — way to deal with something), which is more casual and practical.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Method; way; approach; technique.

## Example Sentences

**你有什么好的学习方法？**
Nǐ yǒu shénme hǎo de xuéxí fāngfǎ?
Do you have any good study methods?

**这种方法效果很好。**
Zhè zhǒng fāngfǎ xiàoguǒ hěn hǎo.
This method is very effective.

**我们需要找到更好的解决方法。**
Wǒmen xūyào zhǎodào gèng hǎo de jiějué fāngfǎ.
We need to find a better method of solving this.

**用不同的方法可以得到相同的结果。**
Yòng bùtóng de fāngfǎ kěyǐ dédào xiāngtóng de jiéguǒ.
Using different methods can produce the same result.

## Memory Hook

方 (square / direction) plus 法 (law / rule). A law set in a specific direction — a rule for how to proceed. That directed rule is a method.
