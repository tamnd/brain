## 可能 (kěnéng)

可能 works both as an adjective meaning possible and as an adverb meaning maybe or possibly. When used as an adjective it follows 是 or describes a noun. When used as an adverb it precedes a verb or verb phrase to express uncertainty.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** Possible; that can happen.
2. **[adverb]** Maybe; possibly; perhaps.
3. **[noun]** Possibility; chance.

## Example Sentences

**他可能今天不来了。**
Tā kěnéng jīntiān bù lái le.
He possibly will not come today.

**这是不可能的事情。**
Zhè shì bù kěnéng de shìqing.
This is an impossible thing.

**你有没有可能帮我？**
Nǐ yǒu méiyǒu kěnéng bāng wǒ?
Is there any possibility you could help me?

**他可能已经到了。**
Tā kěnéng yǐjīng dào le.
He may have already arrived.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 不可能 | impossible |
| 有可能 | there is a possibility |
| 很可能 | very likely |
| 可能性 | possibility (as a noun) |
| 尽可能 | as much as possible |

## Memory Hook

可 means "can/may" and 能 means "able." Something that is 可能 is both permissible and able to happen, making it possible. Drop it before a verb and it expresses that the action may or may not occur.
