## 拿出去 (ná chūqu)

拿出去 is a verb with a compound directional complement. 拿 (to take / hold) is the base verb; 出去 (go out / exit, away from the speaker) shows that the object moves from inside to outside and away from the current location. Compare 拿出来 (take out here, toward the speaker).

## Structure

拿 (take / hold) + 出去 (directional complement: out and away) = take something out (away from speaker).

Other base verbs with 出去: 走出去 (walk out), 搬出去 (move out), 带出去 (bring out).

## Example Sentences

**请把这些垃圾拿出去扔掉。**
Qǐng bǎ zhèxiē lājī ná chūqu rēng diào.
Please take these trash bags out and throw them away.

**他把那本书拿出去还给图书馆了。**
Tā bǎ nà běn shū ná chūqu huán gěi túshūguǎn le.
He took the book out and returned it to the library.

**不要把公司的文件拿出去。**
Bùyào bǎ gōngsī de wénjiàn ná chūqu.
Do not take company documents outside.

**你能帮我把这个箱子拿出去吗？**
Nǐ néng bāng wǒ bǎ zhège xiāngzi ná chūqu ma?
Could you help me take this box outside?

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 拿出去扔 | take out and throw away |
| 拿出来 | take out here (toward speaker) |
| 把...拿出去 | take... out (with 把) |
| 带出去 | bring / take out (along) |
| 搬出去 | move out |

## Memory Hook

拿 (hold in your hand) + 出 (exit, out) + 去 (go away). You pick something up, carry it through the exit, and go away — the object leaves the space and travels away from you.
