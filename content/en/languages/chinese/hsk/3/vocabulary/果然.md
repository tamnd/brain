## 果然 (guǒrán)

果然 expresses that something happened exactly as predicted or expected. It carries a tone of confirmation or mild vindication — "just as I thought." It always refers to something that has already been verified, not something still uncertain.

## Meanings

1. **[adverb]** As expected; sure enough; indeed; just as predicted — confirms that a prior expectation was correct.

## Example Sentences

**他说今天会下雨，果然下起来了。**
Tā shuō jīntiān huì xià yǔ, guǒrán xià qǐlái le.
He said it would rain today, and sure enough it did.

**我猜他会迟到，果然他又迟到了。**
Wǒ cāi tā huì chídào, guǒrán tā yòu chídào le.
I guessed he would be late, and as expected, he was late again.

**这道菜果然很好吃，大家都说不错。**
Zhè dào cài guǒrán hěn hǎochī, dàjiā dōu shuō bùcuò.
This dish is indeed delicious; everyone says it's great.

**他努力学习，果然考了第一名。**
Tā nǔlì xuéxí, guǒrán kǎo le dì yī míng.
He studied hard and, as expected, came first in the exam.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 果然如此 | it is indeed so; just as expected |
| 果然不出所料 | exactly as anticipated |
| 果然成功 | succeeded as expected |
| 果然有效 | proved to be effective |
| 果然是 | it really is; sure enough it is |

## Memory Hook

果 means "fruit/result." 果然 = the result is "naturally so" (然 = naturally/so). The fruit of prediction ripened exactly as foreseen.
