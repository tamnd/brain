## 换乘 (huànchéng)

换乘 means to transfer or change from one mode of transport to another during a journey, such as switching subway lines, buses, or trains. 换 means to change or switch, and 乘 means to ride or board a vehicle.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To transfer; to change vehicles, lines, or modes of transportation mid-journey.

## Example Sentences

**在这里换乘二号线。**
Zài zhèlǐ huànchéng èr hào xiàn.
Transfer to Line 2 here.

**我需要在北京换乘火车。**
Wǒ xūyào zài Běijīng huànchéng huǒchē.
I need to transfer to a train in Beijing.

**这张地图显示了所有换乘站。**
Zhè zhāng dìtú xiǎnshì le suǒyǒu huànchéng zhàn.
This map shows all the transfer stations.

**换乘的时候要注意方向。**
Huànchéng de shíhou yào zhùyì fāngxiàng.
Pay attention to the direction when transferring.

## Memory Hook

换 (change) plus 乘 (ride). You change what you ride. Every time you step off one train and onto another, that is 换乘.
