## 作用 (zuòyòng)

作用 refers to the function or role that something plays, or the effect and impact it has on something else.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** function; role; the purpose something serves
2. **[noun]** effect; impact; the result produced by an action or substance

## Example Sentences

**这种药对发烧有很好的作用。** Zhè zhǒng yào duì fāshāo yǒu hěn hǎo de zuòyòng. This medicine has a very good effect on fever.

**老师在学生的成长中起了很大的作用。** Lǎoshī zài xuésheng de chéngzhǎng zhōng qǐ le hěn dà de zuòyòng. Teachers play a very important role in students' growth.

**这个工具的作用是拧螺丝。** Zhège gōngjù de zuòyòng shì nǐng luósī. The function of this tool is to tighten screws.

**运动对健康有积极的作用。** Yùndòng duì jiànkāng yǒu jījí de zuòyòng. Exercise has a positive effect on health.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 起作用 (qǐ zuòyòng) | to take effect; to work |
| 有作用 (yǒu zuòyòng) | to be effective; to have an effect |
| 重要作用 (zhòngyào zuòyòng) | important role |
| 副作用 (fù zuòyòng) | side effect |
| 发挥作用 (fāhuī zuòyòng) | to bring into play; to fulfill one's role |

## Memory Hook

作 means "to do/make" and 用 means "use" — the 作用 of something is what it does and what it is used for.
