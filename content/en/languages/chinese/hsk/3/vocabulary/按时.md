## 按时 (ànshí)

按时 means on time or punctually, in accordance with the scheduled or agreed time. 按 means according to or in line with, and 时 means time. It emphasizes doing something exactly when it is supposed to be done.

## Meanings

1. **[adverb]** On time; punctually; according to schedule.

## Example Sentences

**请按时完成作业。**
Qǐng ànshí wánchéng zuòyè.
Please finish the homework on time.

**他每天都按时上班。**
Tā měitiān dōu ànshí shàngbān.
He goes to work on time every day.

**吃药要按时，不能忘。**
Chī yào yào ànshí, bù néng wàng.
You must take your medicine on time and not forget.

**飞机按时起飞了。**
Fēijī ànshí qǐfēi le.
The plane took off on time.

## Memory Hook

按 means to press or follow, 时 means time. Press yourself to follow time exactly, like pressing a button at the exact right moment.
