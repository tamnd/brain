## 越来越 (yuè lái yuè)

越来越 is a fixed pattern meaning "more and more" or "increasingly." It is placed before an adjective or verb and describes a quality that keeps intensifying over time.

## Meanings

1. **[adverb pattern]** More and more; increasingly; getting (adjective)-er and (adjective)-er.

## Example Sentences

**他的汉语越来越好了。**
Tā de Hànyǔ yuè lái yuè hǎo le.
His Chinese is getting better and better.

**天气越来越冷了。**
Tiānqì yuè lái yuè lěng le.
The weather is getting colder and colder.

**她越来越喜欢学中文了。**
Tā yuè lái yuè xǐhuān xué Zhōngwén le.
She likes learning Chinese more and more.

**这里越来越热闹了。**
Zhèlǐ yuè lái yuè rènao le.
This place is getting livelier and livelier.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 越来越好 | getting better and better |
| 越来越难 | getting harder and harder |
| 越来越多 | more and more (quantity) |
| 越来越快 | getting faster and faster |
| 越来越喜欢 | liking more and more |

## Memory Hook

越 (to cross / exceed), 来 (come), 越 (exceed again): crossing one threshold, then coming, then crossing the next — always moving past the previous limit.
