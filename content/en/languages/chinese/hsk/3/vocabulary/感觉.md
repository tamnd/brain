## 感觉 (gǎnjué)

感觉 refers to a sensation, feeling, or perception, and can be used as a verb meaning to feel or sense something.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to feel; to sense; to perceive
2. **[noun]** feeling; sensation; sense; impression

## Example Sentences

**我感觉有点儿累。** Wǒ gǎnjué yǒudiǎnr lèi. I feel a little tired.

**这首歌给我一种很温暖的感觉。** Zhè shǒu gē gěi wǒ yī zhǒng hěn wēnnuǎn de gǎnjué. This song gives me a very warm feeling.

**你感觉这道菜味道怎么样？** Nǐ gǎnjué zhè dào cài wèidào zěnmeyàng? How does this dish taste to you?

**她有一种感觉，今天会发生什么好事。** Tā yǒu yī zhǒng gǎnjué, jīntiān huì fāshēng shénme hǎoshì. She had a feeling that something good would happen today.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 有感觉 | to have a feeling |
| 感觉好 | to feel good |
| 给人感觉 | to give someone a feeling |
| 第一感觉 | first impression |
| 感觉不对 | something feels wrong |

## Memory Hook

感 (to feel/sense) + 觉 (to perceive/awaken): awareness that awakens inside you is your 感觉.
