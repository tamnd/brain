## 由于 (yóuyú)

由于 means "due to," "because of," or "owing to." It introduces a cause or reason and is more formal and written than 因为. The result clause is often introduced by 所以, 因此, or 才.

## Meanings

1. **[preposition/conjunction]** Due to; because of; owing to; as a result of — introduces the cause of a situation, often in formal or written contexts.

### Compare 由于 vs 因为

| Word | Style | Notes |
|------|-------|-------|
| 由于 | Formal / written | Typically fronts the sentence; result clause uses 所以/因此 |
| 因为 | Neutral / spoken | Can appear in either clause; result clause uses 所以 |

## Example Sentences

**由于天气恶劣，比赛被取消了。**
Yóuyú tiānqì èliè, bǐsài bèi qǔxiāo le.
Due to bad weather, the competition was cancelled.

**由于他的努力，项目终于完成了。**
Yóuyú tā de nǔlì, xiàngmù zhōngyú wánchéng le.
Owing to his efforts, the project was finally completed.

**由于交通堵塞，我们迟到了半个小时。**
Yóuyú jiāotōng dǔsāi, wǒmen chídào le bàn gè xiǎoshí.
Due to traffic congestion, we were half an hour late.

**由于经验不足，他在工作中犯了一些错误。**
Yóuyú jīngyàn bùzú, tā zài gōngzuò zhōng fàn le yīxiē cuòwù.
Owing to a lack of experience, he made some mistakes at work.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 由于原因 | due to (some) reason |
| 由于…，所以… | because of…, therefore… |
| 由于…，因此… | owing to…, thus… |
| 由于不足 | due to insufficient… |
| 由于影响 | due to the influence of |

## Memory Hook

由 means "from / arising from." 于 marks the source or origin. 由于 = arising from — the cause is the starting point from which everything else flows.
