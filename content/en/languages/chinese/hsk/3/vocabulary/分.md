## 分 (fēn)

A versatile word: a unit of time equal to one minute, a scoring unit (point), or a fraction of one-tenth of a basic unit.

## Meanings

1. **[noun / measure word]** Minute (unit of time, 1/60 of an hour).
2. **[noun]** Point, score (in an exam or competition).
3. **[noun]** Fraction, one-tenth unit (of yuan, etc.).

## Example Sentences

**现在是三点四十五分。**
Xiànzài shì sān diǎn sìshíwǔ fēn.
It is now 3:45 (three forty-five).

**他考试得了九十五分。**
Tā kǎoshì dé le jiǔshíwǔ fēn.
He scored 95 points on the exam.

**我只需要五分钟就能到。**
Wǒ zhǐ xūyào wǔ fēnzhōng jiù néng dào.
I only need five minutes to get there.

**一元等于一百分。**
Yī yuán děngyú yībǎi fēn.
One yuan equals one hundred fen.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 几分钟 | a few minutes |
| 得分 | to score points |
| 满分 | full score |
| 三点半/三点三十分 | 3:30 |
| 分数 | score, grade |

## Memory Hook

分 literally means "to divide" — a minute divides an hour into sixty parts; a point divides the total score into units.
