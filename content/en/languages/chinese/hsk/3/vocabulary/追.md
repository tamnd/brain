## 追 (zhuī)

追 means to run after or chase something, and by extension to actively pursue a goal, dream, or romantic interest.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to chase; to run after someone or something to catch them
2. **[verb]** to pursue; to seek after a goal, dream, or romantic partner

## Example Sentences

**那只狗在追一只猫。** Nà zhī gǒu zài zhuī yī zhī māo. That dog is chasing a cat.

**警察追上了那个小偷。** Jǐngchá zhuī shàng le nà gè xiǎotōu. The police caught up with the thief.

**他一直在追自己的音乐梦想。** Tā yīzhí zài zhuī zìjǐ de yīnyuè mèngxiǎng. He has always been pursuing his music dream.

**快追！他跑了！** Kuài zhuī! Tā pǎo le! Quick, chase him! He ran away!
