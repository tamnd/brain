## 在乎 (zàihu)

在乎 means "to care about" or "to mind." It expresses emotional investment in something — being affected by it, taking it to heart, or being bothered by it. It is most common in negative or interrogative forms: 不在乎 ("not care") and 在乎吗 ("do you care?").

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To care about; to mind; to take to heart; to be affected by — expresses that something matters emotionally to the subject.

## Example Sentences

**他根本不在乎别人的看法。**
Tā gēnběn bú zàihu biérén de kànfǎ.
He does not care at all about other people's opinions.

**你在乎他吗？**
Nǐ zàihu tā ma?
Do you care about him?

**我不在乎结果，只要我尽力了就好。**
Wǒ bú zàihu jiéguǒ, zhǐyào wǒ jìnlì le jiù hǎo.
I don't care about the result; as long as I gave it my best, that's enough.

**她很在乎家人的感受。**
Tā hěn zàihu jiārén de gǎnshòu.
She cares a great deal about how her family feels.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 不在乎 | not care; indifferent |
| 很在乎 | care a lot |
| 在乎别人 | care about others |
| 在乎面子 | care about face/reputation |
| 根本不在乎 | not care at all |

## Memory Hook

在 = "to be / to exist at." 乎 is a classical particle expressing a state. 在乎 = "your heart exists there" at that thing — you are emotionally present at it, meaning it matters to you.
