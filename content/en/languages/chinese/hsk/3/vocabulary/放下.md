## 放下 (fàngxia)

放下 means to put down or set something down. It is also used figuratively to mean letting go of a burden, worry, or grudge. 放 means to release or place, and 下 indicates a downward direction.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To put down; to set down (physically place something on a surface).
2. **[verb]** To let go of; to stop holding onto (a feeling, worry, or attachment).

## Example Sentences

**请把手机放下，认真听课。**
Qǐng bǎ shǒujī fàngxia, rènzhēn tīng kè.
Please put down your phone and listen to the class carefully.

**把包放下，休息一下。**
Bǎ bāo fàngxia, xiūxi yīxià.
Put down your bag and rest for a bit.

**他无法放下对过去的遗憾。**
Tā wúfǎ fàngxia duì guòqù de yíhàn.
He cannot let go of regrets about the past.

**放下工作，陪陪家人。**
Fàngxia gōngzuò, péi pei jiārén.
Put work aside and spend time with your family.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 放下手机 | to put down the phone |
| 放下包袱 | to let go of burdens |
| 放下工作 | to set work aside |
| 放下心来 | to feel relieved; to stop worrying |
| 放不下 | cannot let go of; cannot put down |

## Memory Hook

放 (release) plus 下 (down). Releasing something downward, whether a heavy bag onto the floor or a heavy feeling from your heart.
