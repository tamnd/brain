## 主动 (zhǔdòng)

主动 describes the quality of acting proactively, on one's own accord, rather than waiting to be told or prompted by others.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** active; proactive; taking the lead
2. **[adverb]** on one's own initiative; voluntarily; without being asked

## Example Sentences

**她主动帮我搬家，我很感动。** Tā zhǔdòng bāng wǒ bānjiā, wǒ hěn gǎndòng. She proactively helped me move house, and I was very touched.

**学生应该主动问问题。** Xuésheng yīnggāi zhǔdòng wèn wèntí. Students should actively ask questions.

**他主动承认了自己的错误。** Tā zhǔdòng chéngrèn le zìjǐ de cuòwù. He took the initiative to admit his own mistake.

**你要主动一点，别总是等着别人来找你。** Nǐ yào zhǔdòng yīdiǎn, bié zǒng shì děngzhe biérén lái zhǎo nǐ. You should be more proactive; don't always wait for others to come to you.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |

| 主动帮忙 (zhǔdòng bāngmáng) | to proactively offer help |
| 主动联系 (zhǔdòng liánxi) | to reach out on one's own initiative |
| 主动学习 (zhǔdòng xuéxí) | to study proactively |

## Memory Hook

主 means "master/owner" and 动 means "move/act" — the person who is the master of their own actions moves first without being told.
