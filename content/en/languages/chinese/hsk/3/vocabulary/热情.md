## 热情 (rèqíng)

热情 describes both the quality of warmth and enthusiasm shown toward people, and a passionate feeling toward an activity or goal.

## Meanings

1. **[adj]** warm; enthusiastic; welcoming
2. **[noun]** passion; enthusiasm; warmth

## Example Sentences

**这里的人们对游客非常热情。** Zhèlǐ de rénmen duì yóukè fēicháng rèqíng. The people here are very warm and welcoming to visitors.

**她对音乐充满热情，每天都练习。** Tā duì yīnyuè chōngmǎn rèqíng, měitiān dōu liànxí. She is filled with passion for music and practices every day.

**老师热情地帮助每一位同学。** Lǎoshī rèqíng de bāngzhù měi yī wèi tóngxué. The teacher warmly helped every student.

**做事要有热情，才能做好。** Zuò shì yào yǒu rèqíng, cái néng zuò hǎo. You need passion to do things well.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 充满热情 | full of enthusiasm |

| 热情帮助 | to help enthusiastically |
| 失去热情 | to lose passion |

## Memory Hook

热 means "hot" and 情 means "feeling/emotion." 热情 is a hot, burning feeling of warmth toward others or a purpose.
