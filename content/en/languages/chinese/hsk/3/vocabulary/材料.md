## 材料 (cáiliào)

材料 can refer to physical materials used to make something, or written materials such as data, documents, or reference information.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** material; ingredient; substance used to make something
2. **[noun]** data; written materials; documents; information

## Example Sentences

**这道菜需要很多材料。** Zhè dào cài xūyào hěn duō cáiliào. This dish requires many ingredients.

**请把相关材料发给我。** Qǐng bǎ xiāngguān cáiliào fā gěi wǒ. Please send me the relevant materials.

**建造这栋楼用了很多高质量的材料。** Jiànzào zhè dòng lóu yòng le hěn duō gāo zhìliàng de cáiliào. Many high-quality materials were used to build this building.

**老师给我们准备了大量的学习材料。** Lǎoshī gěi wǒmen zhǔnbèi le dàliàng de xuéxí cáiliào. The teacher prepared a large amount of study materials for us.

## Memory Hook

材 (timber/talent) + 料 (ingredient/material): the raw timber and ingredients that make up everything you need.
