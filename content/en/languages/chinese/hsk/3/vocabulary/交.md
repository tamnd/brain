## 交 (jiāo)

A verb meaning to hand over something to someone, to submit work, or to form a relationship such as friendship.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To hand over, to submit, to deliver.
2. **[verb]** To make (friends), to establish (a relationship).

## Example Sentences

**请把作业交给老师。**
Qǐng bǎ zuòyè jiāo gěi lǎoshī.
Please hand the homework to the teacher.

**他很容易交到朋友。**
Tā hěn róngyì jiāo dào péngyǒu.
He makes friends very easily.

**你的报告明天要交。**
Nǐ de bàogào míngtiān yào jiāo.
Your report needs to be submitted tomorrow.

**我在旅行中交了很多新朋友。**
Wǒ zài lǚxíng zhōng jiāo le hěn duō xīn péngyǒu.
I made many new friends during my trip.

## Memory Hook

交 looks like two people crossing paths and passing something between them — a handover at the intersection.
