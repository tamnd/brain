## 同事 (tóngshì)

同事 means colleague or coworker, referring to someone who works at the same organization or shares the same workplace. It is the standard, polite word for a professional peer.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Colleague; coworker.

## Example Sentences

**他是我的同事，我们一起工作。**
Tā shì wǒ de tóngshì, wǒmen yīqǐ gōngzuò.
He is my colleague; we work together.

**同事们帮我完成了任务。**
Tóngshìmen bāng wǒ wánchéng le rènwu.
My colleagues helped me complete the task.

**我和同事的关系很好。**
Wǒ hé tóngshì de guānxi hěn hǎo.
I have a good relationship with my colleagues.

**新来的同事很热情。**
Xīn lái de tóngshì hěn rèqíng.
The new colleague is very warm and enthusiastic.

## Memory Hook

同 means same and 事 means matter or affairs. A 同事 shares the same work matters with you, doing the same kind of thing in the same place every day.
