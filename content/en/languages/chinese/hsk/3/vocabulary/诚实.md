## 诚实 (chéngshí)

诚实 describes a person or behavior that is honest and truthful, free from deception. It is mostly used as an adjective to describe character, and can also function as a noun referring to the quality of honesty itself.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** Honest; truthful; sincere; not deceptive.
2. **[noun]** Honesty; truthfulness (as a moral quality).

## Example Sentences

**他是一个非常诚实的人。**
Tā shì yīgè fēicháng chéngshí de rén.
He is a very honest person.

**做人要诚实，不能说谎。**
Zuòrén yào chéngshí, bù néng shuōhuǎng.
One must be honest in life and must not lie.

**她诚实地告诉了我真实情况。**
Tā chéngshí de gàosù le wǒ zhēnshí qíngkuàng.
She honestly told me the real situation.

**诚实是最重要的品质之一。**
Chéngshí shì zuì zhòngyào de pǐnzhì zhī yī.
Honesty is one of the most important qualities.

## Memory Hook

诚 has the speech radical 讠 plus 成 (to accomplish) — your words accomplish what you say. 实 means "real / solid." Honest words are solid and real, not hollow promises.
