## HSK 3 Lesson Plan

HSK 3 corresponds to A2 level on the CEFR scale. These 18 lessons cover the grammatical structures and vocabulary required to pass the HSK 3 exam and communicate in everyday Chinese situations.

| # | Lesson | Grammar Focus | Key Vocabulary |
|---|--------|---------------|----------------|
| 1 | [The 把 Construction](01-ba-construction/) | 把 + O + V + complement | 把, 放, 打开, 关上, 送, 带 |
| 2 | [Passive Voice with 被](02-passive-bei/) | 被 + agent + V, 遭到, 受到 | 被, 让, 叫, 打, 骗, 偷 |
| 3 | [Result Complements](03-result-complements/) | V + 好/完/懂/到/住/错 | 做好, 吃完, 听懂, 找到, 记住, 答错 |
| 4 | [Direction Complements](04-direction-complements/) | V + 进/出/上/下/回/过/起 + 来/去 | 跑进来, 走出去, 拿起来, 放下去 |
| 5 | [Potential Complements](05-potential-complements/) | V + 得/不 + result | 听得懂, 看不见, 进得去, 搬不动 |
| 6 | [Concessive and Causal](06-concessive-causal/) | 虽然...但是, 因为...所以, 尽管...还是 | 虽然, 但是, 因为, 所以, 尽管, 还是 |
| 7 | [Conditional Sentences](07-conditional-sentences/) | 如果...就, 只要...就, 只有...才 | 如果, 就, 只要, 只有, 才, 要不然 |
| 8 | [Simultaneous Actions](08-simultaneous-actions/) | 一边...一边, 边...边, 同时 | 一边, 同时, 边走边说 |
| 9 | [Sequence Expressions](09-sequence-expressions/) | 先...再/然后...最后, 接下来 | 首先, 然后, 接着, 最后, 之后 |
| 10 | [Duration and Frequency](10-duration-frequency/) | V + 了 + duration, 每 + time + V | 经常, 有时候, 偶尔, 从来不, 一直, 总是 |
| 11 | [Chinese Culture: Festivals](11-festivals/) | 在 + festival + 的时候..., 习惯 collocations | 春节, 中秋节, 端午节, 饺子, 月饼, 红包 |
| 12 | [Travel and Tourism](12-travel-tourism/) | 从...到..., 经过, 路过 | 旅游, 景点, 导游, 预订, 行李, 护照 |
| 13 | [Health and Wellness](13-health-wellness/) | 应该 + V, 建议 + V/clause | 健康, 锻炼, 睡眠, 营养, 检查, 手术 |
| 14 | [Study and Career](14-study-career/) | 为了...而, 专业 collocations | 大学, 专业, 毕业, 成绩, 奖学金, 实习 |
| 15 | [Describing People](15-describing-people/) | Adj + 极了, V + 得 + degree | 性格, 活泼, 认真, 懒, 聪明, 勇敢 |
| 16 | [Media and Technology](16-media-technology/) | 用 + tool + V, 关于 + topic + ... | 手机, 电脑, 应用, 下载, 软件, 更新 |
| 17 | [Environment and Nature](17-environment-nature/) | 越来越 + adj, 变得 + adj | 环境, 污染, 保护, 自然, 树, 动物 |
