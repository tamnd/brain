## 概述 (gàishù)

概述 means to summarize or give an overview — to describe something briefly but comprehensively, covering the essential points without going into detail. It is commonly used at the beginning of academic papers, reports, and presentations to provide a bird's-eye view before diving into specifics. As a noun, it refers to a summary or overview document.

## Meanings
1. **[v]** to summarize; to give a general overview of
2. **[n]** an overview; a summary; a general account

## Example Sentences
**论文摘要应简洁概述研究目的、方法、主要发现及结论。** Lùnwén zhāiyào yīng jiǎnjié gàishù yánjiū mùdì, fāngfǎ, zhǔyào fāxiàn jí jiélùn. The thesis abstract should concisely summarize the research objectives, methods, key findings, and conclusions.

**在正式讨论之前，主席对过去一年的工作进展作了简要概述。** Zài zhèngshì tǎolùn zhīqián, zhǔxí duì guòqù yī nián de gōngzuò jìnzhǎn zuòle jiǎnyào gàishù. Before the formal discussion, the chair gave a brief overview of the work progress over the past year.

**这本导论性著作对西方哲学史作了系统的概述，适合入门读者。** Zhè běn dǎolùnxìng zhùzuò duì xīfāng zhéxué shǐ zuòle xìtǒng de gàishù, shìhé rùmén dúzhě. This introductory work provides a systematic overview of the history of Western philosophy, suitable for beginning readers.

**报告第一节概述了项目背景与政策环境，为后续分析奠定了基础。** Bàogào dì yī jié gàishùle xiàngmù bèijǐng yǔ zhèngcè huánjìng, wèi hòuxù fēnxī diàndìngle jīchǔ. The first section of the report summarized the project background and policy environment, laying the foundation for subsequent analysis.

**教授在第一堂课上概述了整门课程的知识框架与学习目标。** Jiàoshòu zài dì yī táng kè shàng gàishùle zhěng mén kèchéng de zhīshi kuàngjià yǔ xuéxí mùbiāo. The professor summarized the knowledge framework and learning objectives of the entire course in the first class.

**国情概述报告为外国投资者提供了理解中国市场的初步参考。** Guóqíng gàishù bàogào wèi wàiguó tóuzīzhě tígōngle lǐjiě zhōngguó shìchǎng de chūbù cānkǎo. The national conditions overview report provided foreign investors with an initial reference for understanding the Chinese market.

## Usage Notes

概述 is less comprehensive than 综述 (zōngshù), which implies a full literature review or synthesis of existing research. 概述 is a brief, accessible summary, while 综述 is thorough and scholarly. Compare also with 概括 (gàikuò), which means to generalize or summarize and is often used for summarizing arguments, while 概述 is more often used for summarizing a situation, background, or body of work.

As a noun, 概述 appears in titles and section headings: 研究概述 (research overview), 项目概述 (project summary). As a verb, it typically follows a subject + 对… + 作了概述 pattern (gave an overview of…). It is formal and academic; avoid using it in casual speech.

## Memory Hook

概 (general, approximate) + 述 (to narrate): "narrating in general terms" — the big picture before the details.
