## 梳理 (shūlǐ)

梳理 literally means to comb (hair), and metaphorically means to sort through, organize, or untangle complex information, ideas, or issues systematically. It implies bringing order to something that is complex or messy. It is widely used in academic and professional contexts when reviewing literature, organizing thoughts, or analyzing a tangled situation.

## Meanings
1. **[v]** to comb through; to sort and organize systematically
2. **[v]** to untangle; to clarify (a complex issue or body of information)

## Example Sentences
**研究者系统梳理了改革开放以来农村土地政策的演变脉络。** Yánjiūzhě xìtǒng shūlǐle gǎigé kāifàng yǐlái nóngcūn tǔdì zhèngcè de yǎnbiàn màiluò. The researcher systematically combed through the evolution of rural land policy since reform and opening up.

**在动笔之前，作家花了数周时间梳理人物关系与情节逻辑。** Zài dòngbǐ zhīqián, zuòjiā huāle shù zhōu shíjiān shūlǐ rénwù guānxi yǔ qíngjié luójí. Before writing, the author spent several weeks combing through the character relationships and plot logic.

**这份政策简报梳理了三十年来城镇化进程中暴露的主要制度问题。** Zhè fèn zhèngcè jiǎnbào shūlǐle sānshí nián lái chéngzhènhuà jìnchéng zhōng bàolù de zhǔyào zhìdù wèntí. This policy brief combed through the major institutional problems exposed in the urbanization process over thirty years.

**法律顾问帮助客户梳理了复杂的合同条款，指出了潜在的法律风险。** Fǎlǜ gùwèn bāngzhù kèhù shūlǐle fùzá de hétong tiáokuǎn, zhǐchūle qiánzài de fǎlǜ fēngxiǎn. The legal counsel helped the client comb through the complex contract terms, pointing out potential legal risks.

**导师建议她先梳理现有文献，再确定研究的切入点与创新方向。** Dǎoshī jiànyì tā xiān shūlǐ xiànyǒu wénxiàn, zài quèdìng yánjiū de qiērùdiǎn yǔ chuàngxīn fāngxiàng. The supervisor advised her to first sort through the existing literature before identifying the research entry point and direction of innovation.

**他在采访前仔细梳理了自己想要传达的核心观点与论证思路。** Tā zài cǎifǎng qián zǐxì shūlǐle zìjǐ xiǎng yào chuándá de héxīn guāndiǎn yǔ lùnzhèng sīlù. Before the interview, he carefully organized the core points he wanted to convey and his line of argument.

## Usage Notes

梳理 is a vivid metaphor: just as combing brings order to tangled hair, 梳理 brings clarity to complex information. It is softer and more patient in connotation than 整理 (zhěnglǐ, to organize/tidy up), implying a more careful, analytical process rather than simply sorting or filing. It is also more analytical than 整合 (zhěnghé, to integrate).

A key collocation is 梳理脉络 (to trace the development thread), very common in historical and literary analysis. In academic writing, 梳理文献 (to review the literature by combing through it) is slightly less formal than 文献综述 but implies the same process. 梳理思路 (to organize one's thinking) is useful in both academic and everyday contexts.

## Memory Hook

梳 (a comb — the 木 wood radical with teeth) + 理 (to organize, to put in order): "using a comb to bring order" — patiently untangling complexity into clarity.
