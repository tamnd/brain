## 演绎 (yǎnyì)

演绎 has two major senses. In logic and academia, it means to deduce — to derive specific conclusions from general principles (deductive reasoning). In arts and performance, it means to perform, enact, or adapt — to bring a story, role, or theme to life. This duality makes it a versatile word used in both intellectual and creative contexts.

## Meanings
1. **[v]** to deduce; to reason deductively from general to specific
2. **[v]** to perform; to enact; to present or adapt (a story, role, or theme)

## Example Sentences
**数学定理的证明过程本质上是一种演绎推理。** Shùxué dìnglǐ de zhèngmíng guòchéng běnzhì shàng shì yī zhǒng yǎnyì tuīlǐ. The process of proving mathematical theorems is essentially a form of deductive reasoning.

**这部电影将经典神话演绎成了一个当代都市故事。** Zhè bù diànyǐng jiāng jīngdiǎn shénhuà yǎnyì chéngle yīgè dāngdài dūshì gùshi. This film adapted the classic myth into a contemporary urban story.

**演绎法从普遍原则出发，推导出适用于具体情境的结论。** Yǎnyìfǎ cóng pǔbiàn yuánzé chūfā, tuīdǎo chū shìyòng yú jùtǐ qíngjìng de jiélùn. Deductive reasoning starts from universal principles and derives conclusions applicable to specific situations.

**她将这首古典乐曲演绎得既忠于原作又富有个人风格。** Tā jiāng zhè shǒu gǔdiǎn yuèqǔ yǎnyì de jì zhōngyú yuánzuò yòu fùyǒu gèrén fēnggé. She performed this classical piece in a way that was both faithful to the original and full of personal style.

**侦探根据现有线索演绎出了案件的完整经过。** Zhēntàn gēnjù xiànyǒu xiànsuǒ yǎnyì chūle ànjiàn de wánzhěng jīngguò. The detective deduced the complete sequence of events in the case from existing clues.

**他的一生演绎了一代创业者不屈不挠的奋斗精神。** Tā de yīshēng yǎnyìle yīdài chuàngyèzhě bùqū bùnáo de fèndòu jīngshén. His entire life was a living embodiment of the indomitable entrepreneurial spirit of a generation.

## Usage Notes

演绎 is the logical counterpart of 归纳 (guīnà, inductive reasoning). In philosophical and scientific contexts, 演绎 always refers to deduction — drawing specific conclusions from general premises. In cultural and artistic contexts, it means to perform or adapt, often implying a creative reinterpretation.

A common error is to use 演绎 when only 表演 (biǎoyǎn) or 演出 (yǎnchū) is intended. 演绎 in the performance sense typically implies a creative or interpretive dimension — bringing something to life in a new or meaningful way — not just a routine performance. The expression 精彩演绎 (wonderful performance/adaptation) is widely used in arts journalism.

## Memory Hook

演 (to perform, to develop) + 绎 (to unravel, to interpret): "unraveling the general thread and developing it into specific form" — both deduction and performance.
