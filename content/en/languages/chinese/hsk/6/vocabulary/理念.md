## 理念 (lǐniàn)

理念 refers to a guiding idea, philosophical concept, or foundational belief that underpins a system, approach, or institution. It is more principled and abstract than 想法 (idea/thought) and more concrete than 哲学 (philosophy). It is a high-frequency word in policy documents, institutional discourse, and intellectual commentary.

## Meanings
1. **[n]** concept, guiding idea (a foundational principle behind practice)
2. **[n]** philosophy, belief (a deep-seated conviction shaping one's approach)

## Example Sentences
**"以人为本"的发展理念要求政策制定者将公民福祉置于经济增长之上。** "Yǐ rén wéi běn" de fāzhǎn lǐniàn yāoqiú zhèngcè zhìdìng zhě jiāng gōngmín fúzhǐ zhì yú jīngjì zēngzhǎng zhī shàng. The development concept of "people first" requires policymakers to place citizens' well-being above economic growth.

**这所学校的教育理念强调培养学生的独立思考能力，而非灌输标准答案。** Zhè suǒ xuéxiào de jiàoyù lǐniàn qiángdiào péiyǎng xuéshēng de dúlì sīkǎo nénglì, ér fēi guànshū biāozhǔn dá'àn. This school's educational philosophy emphasizes cultivating students' ability to think independently, rather than instilling standard answers.

**可持续发展的理念已从环境领域扩展到经济、社会与文化的各个层面。** Kě chíxù fāzhǎn de lǐniàn yǐ cóng huánjìng lǐngyù kuòzhǎn dào jīngjì, shèhuì yǔ wénhuà de gègè céngmiàn. The concept of sustainable development has expanded from the environmental sphere to all dimensions of economy, society, and culture.

**这家企业的核心理念是：在创造商业价值的同时承担起相应的社会责任。** Zhè jiā qǐyè de héxīn lǐniàn shì: zài chuàngzào shāngyè jiàzhí de tóngshí chéngdān qǐ xiāngyìng de shèhuì zérèn. This company's core philosophy is to assume corresponding social responsibility while creating commercial value.

**不同的法律理念导致各国在隐私权保护问题上采取了截然不同的立法路径。** Bùtóng de fǎlǜ lǐniàn dǎozhì gèguó zài yǐnsīquán bǎohù wèntí shàng cǎiqǔ le jiérán bùtóng de lìfǎ lùjìng. Different legal philosophies have led countries to adopt entirely different legislative approaches on the issue of privacy protection.

**这位建筑师的设计理念是将传统美学与现代功能需求无缝融合。** Zhè wèi jiànzhú shī de shèjì lǐniàn shì jiāng chuántǒng měixué yǔ xiàndài gōngnéng xūqiú wúfèng rónghé. This architect's design philosophy is to seamlessly integrate traditional aesthetics with modern functional requirements.

## Usage Notes

理念 sits between the abstract (哲学, philosophy) and the practical (方法, method). It is heavily used in official Chinese discourse, corporate branding, and educational contexts. Unlike 观念 (guānniàn, notion/idea), which can be any view or belief, 理念 implies a systematic, principled, and often prescriptive concept. In policy texts, phrases like 绿色发展理念 or 创新理念 are standard.

The verb 践行 (to put into practice) frequently collocates with 理念: 践行绿色理念 (to put the green concept into practice).

## Memory Hook

理 (principle/reason) + 念 (thought/idea) — a 理念 is a principled thought, a foundational idea grounded in reasoning.
