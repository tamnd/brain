## 概括 (gàikuò)

概括 means to generalize or summarize, capturing the essential features or key points of something in broad, concise terms. It implies distilling complexity into a clear, representative statement. The word is used both for summarizing content and for characterizing something in a few defining terms. It is slightly more interpretive than 概述, as it often involves identifying the most salient features.

## Meanings
1. **[v]** to generalize; to summarize; to capture the key points
2. **[adj]** broad; general; approximate (used in expressions like 概括地说)

## Example Sentences
**用一句话概括全文的主旨是检验阅读理解深度的有效方式。** Yòng yī jù huà gàikuò quánwén de zhǔzhì shì jiǎnyàn yuèdú lǐjiě shēndù de yǒuxiào fāngshì. Summarizing the main idea of an entire text in one sentence is an effective way to test the depth of reading comprehension.

**这个理论可以概括为：环境决定行为，而非性格决定命运。** Zhège lǐlùn kěyǐ gàikuòwéi: huánjìng juédìng xíngwéi, ér fēi xìnggé juédìng mìngyùn. This theory can be summarized as: environment determines behavior, not character determines fate.

**概括而言，本次调查反映出三个核心社会矛盾。** Gàikuò ér yán, běn cì diàochá fǎnyìng chū sān gè héxīn shèhuì máodùn. Broadly speaking, this survey reflects three core social contradictions.

**历史学家很难用简单的标签概括一个复杂历史人物的历史贡献。** Lìshǐxuéjiā hěn nán yòng jiǎndān de biāoqiān gàikuò yīgè fùzá lìshǐ rénwù de lìshǐ gòngxiàn. Historians find it difficult to summarize the historical contributions of a complex historical figure with simple labels.

**这段文字高度概括了中华文明数千年演进的基本脉络。** Zhè duàn wénzì gāodù gàikuòle zhōnghuá wénmíng shùqiān nián yǎnjìn de jīběn màiluò. This passage highly summarizes the basic trajectory of Chinese civilization's evolution over thousands of years.

**演讲结束前，她以四个关键词概括了整场报告的核心主张。** Yǎnjiǎng jiéshù qián, tā yǐ sì gè guānjiàncí gàikuòle zhěng chǎng bàogào de héxīn zhǔzhāng. Before ending the speech, she summarized the core arguments of the entire report with four keywords.

## Usage Notes

概括 and 概述 are close in meaning but distinct in emphasis. 概述 typically provides a structured overview (often longer), while 概括 captures the essence in fewer words and with more interpretive precision. 概括 is frequently used in teaching and test contexts, where students must 概括文章大意 (summarize the main idea of an article).

The expression 概括地说 or 概括而言 (broadly/generally speaking) is very useful in academic and professional presentations when signaling a high-level statement. 概括 can also be used as an adjective: 这个描述过于概括 (this description is too broad/vague). The pattern 概括为 (to be reduced/summarized to) is common: 可以概括为三点 (can be summarized in three points).

## Memory Hook

概 (general, overall) + 括 (to include, to encompass): "encompassing everything in a general statement" — capturing the whole in a few words.
