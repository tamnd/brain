## 智慧 (zhìhuì)

智慧 refers to wisdom — the highest synthesis of knowledge, experience, and moral insight that enables sound judgment and deep understanding. It transcends mere intelligence (聪明) or knowledge (知识) and carries philosophical and spiritual resonance across Confucian, Buddhist, and Daoist traditions.

## Meanings
1. **[n]** wisdom (profound insight combined with sound moral judgment)
2. **[n]** intelligence, ingenuity (in more practical contexts, creative problem-solving)

## Example Sentences
**真正的智慧不在于博闻强记，而在于能够在复杂情境中做出正确的判断。** Zhēnzhèng de zhìhuì bù zàiyú bó wén qiángjì, ér zàiyú néng gòu zài fùzá qíngjìng zhōng zuòchū zhèngquè de pànduàn. True wisdom lies not in extensive learning and memory but in the ability to make correct judgments in complex situations.

**中国传统哲学蕴含着关于人与自然关系的深邃智慧，值得当代社会重新审视。** Zhōngguó chuántǒng zhéxué yùnhán zhe guānyú rén yǔ zìrán guānxi de shēnsuì zhìhuì, zhídé dāngdài shèhuì chóngxīn shěnshì. Chinese traditional philosophy contains profound wisdom about the relationship between humans and nature, worth reconsidering in contemporary society.

**历史的智慧告诉我们，穷兵黩武的帝国无一不是走向衰亡。** Lìshǐ de zhìhuì gàosu wǒmen, qióng bīng dú wǔ de dìguó wú yī bùshì zǒuxiàng shuāiwáng. The wisdom of history tells us that empires that exhaust their resources in endless warfare inevitably decline.

**在这场复杂的外交谈判中，代表团展现出了高超的政治智慧与谈判技巧。** Zài zhè chǎng fùzá de wàijiāo tánpàn zhōng, dàibiǎotuán zhǎnxiàn chū le gāochāo de zhèngzhì zhìhuì yǔ tánpàn jìqiǎo. In this complex diplomatic negotiation, the delegation displayed superb political wisdom and negotiation skills.

**面对前所未有的挑战，人类需要集体智慧与全球合作，才能找到可行的解决之道。** Miàn duì qián suǒ wèi yǒu de tiǎozhàn, rénlèi xūyào jítǐ zhìhuì yǔ quánqiú hézuò, cái néng zhǎodào kěxíng de jiějué zhīdào. Faced with unprecedented challenges, humanity needs collective wisdom and global cooperation to find viable solutions.

**她在八十年的人生中积累了丰富的生活智慧，令每一位与她交谈过的人深受启迪。** Tā zài bāshí nián de rénshēng zhōng jīlěi le fēngfù de shēnghuó zhìhuì, lìng měi yī wèi yǔ tā jiāotán guò de rén shēn shòu qǐdí. In her eighty years of life she accumulated rich life wisdom, profoundly enlightening everyone who ever talked with her.

## Usage Notes

智慧 is the highest term in a hierarchy of Chinese cognitive vocabulary: 知识 (knowledge/information) < 聪明 (cleverness/intelligence) < 智慧 (wisdom). 智慧 implies both depth of understanding and ethical soundness. In modern usage, 智慧 has been productively combined with technology vocabulary: 智慧城市 (smart city), 智慧医疗 (smart healthcare) — though here it is closer to "smart/intelligent" than to traditional wisdom.

At C1 level, learners should distinguish 智慧 from 聪明 (smart/clever, more intellectual), 机智 (witty/quick-witted, more situational), and 睿智 (sagacious, even more elevated and literary).

## Memory Hook

智 (wisdom/knowledge) + 慧 (wisdom/insight) — both characters mean wisdom: 智慧 is wisdom doubled, the deepest form of human understanding.
