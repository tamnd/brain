## 整合 (zhěnghé)

整合 means to integrate or consolidate — to bring together separate, possibly diverse elements and combine them into a unified, coherent whole. It implies more than just gathering; it suggests reorganizing and optimizing the combined result. It is widely used in business (mergers), education (curriculum integration), and policy (resource consolidation).

## Meanings
1. **[v]** to integrate; to consolidate; to merge into a unified whole
2. **[v]** to reorganize and optimize by combining resources or elements

## Example Sentences
**该集团通过整合旗下三家子公司的研发资源，大幅提升了创新能力。** Gāi jítuán tōngguò zhěnghé qíxià sān jiā zǐgōngsī de yánfā zīyuán, dàfú tíshēngle chuàngxīn nénglì. The group significantly enhanced its innovation capacity by integrating the R&D resources of its three subsidiaries.

**跨学科研究需要整合来自不同领域的理论框架与研究方法。** Kuà xuékē yánjiū xūyào zhěnghé láizì bùtóng lǐngyù de lǐlùn kuàngjià yǔ yánjiū fāngfǎ. Interdisciplinary research requires integrating theoretical frameworks and research methods from different fields.

**政府正努力整合分散的社会保障资源，建立更高效的福利体系。** Zhèngfǔ zhèng nǔlì zhěnghé fēnsàn de shèhuì bǎozhàng zīyuán, jiànlì gèng gāoxiào de fúlì tǐxì. The government is working to consolidate scattered social security resources and establish a more efficient welfare system.

**这所大学将传统文化课程整合进现代通识教育体系，取得了良好效果。** Zhè suǒ dàxué jiāng chuántǒng wénhuà kèchéng zhěnghéjìn xiàndài tōngshí jiàoyù tǐxì, qǔdéle liánghǎo xiàoguǒ. This university achieved good results by integrating traditional culture courses into the modern general education system.

**数字化转型的核心是整合企业内部的数据孤岛，实现信息的互联互通。** Shùzìhuà zhuǎnxíng de héxīn shì zhěnghé qǐyè nèibù de shùjù gūdǎo, shíxiàn xìnxī de hùlián hùtōng. The core of digital transformation is to integrate the internal data silos of enterprises and achieve interconnected information flow.

**优秀的领导者能够整合团队成员的不同优势，形成强大的协同效应。** Yōuxiù de lǐngdǎozhě nénggòu zhěnghé tuánduì chéngyuán de bùtóng yōushì, xíngchéng qiángdà de xiétóng xiàoyìng. Excellent leaders can integrate the different strengths of team members to create a powerful synergistic effect.

## Usage Notes

整合 is a dynamic, active word that goes beyond mere assembly. It implies reorganization and optimization, not just putting things together. This distinguishes it from 合并 (hébìng, to merge or combine), which is more neutral and simply means combining two things. 整合 carries the additional sense that the result is better organized or more efficient than the sum of its parts.

In business and management, 资源整合 (resource integration) is a key strategic concept. In education, 课程整合 (curriculum integration) refers to interdisciplinary curriculum design. In technology, 系统整合 (systems integration) is a standard IT project type. The word is productive: you can say 整合内外部资源 (integrate internal and external resources) or 整合多方力量 (integrate the forces of multiple parties).

## Memory Hook

整 (to put in order, to organize) + 合 (to combine, to fit together): "organizing and combining into a coherent whole" — integration that creates a unified, optimized result.
