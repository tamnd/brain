## 进程 (jìnchéng)

进程 refers to the course or unfolding of a process, particularly large-scale historical, political, or social processes that advance over time. It is more formal and grand-scale than 过程 (guòchéng, process/course) and implies a forward-moving trajectory. It is common in historical narrative, political analysis, and academic writing.

## Meanings
1. **[n]** process, course (the unfolding of a historical or large-scale development)
2. **[n]** progress, advancement (the degree of progress made in a process)

## Example Sentences
**工业革命极大地加速了人类文明的历史进程。** Gōngyè gémìng jídà de jiāsù le rénlèi wénmíng de lìshǐ jìnchéng. The Industrial Revolution greatly accelerated the historical course of human civilization.

**推进法治建设的进程，需要立法、执法、司法三者的协调配合。** Tuījìn fǎzhì jiànshè de jìnchéng, xūyào lìfǎ, zhífǎ, sīfǎ sānzhě de xiétiáo pèihé. Advancing the process of rule-of-law construction requires the coordinated cooperation of legislation, law enforcement, and the judiciary.

**这一政策调整将对该地区的经济一体化进程产生深远影响。** Zhè yī zhèngcè tiáozhěng jiāng duì gāi dìqū de jīngjì yītǐhuà jìnchéng chǎnshēng shēnyuǎn yǐngxiǎng. This policy adjustment will have a far-reaching impact on the economic integration process of the region.

**民主化进程并非一帆风顺，往往伴随着社会的震荡与阵痛。** Mínzhǔhuà jìnchéng bìng fēi yīfān fēng shùn, wǎngwǎng bànsuí zhe shèhuì de zhèndàng yǔ zhèntòng. The process of democratization is not always smooth sailing; it is often accompanied by social turbulence and growing pains.

**新冠疫情在一定程度上打乱了许多国家原定的经济复苏进程。** Xīnguān yìqíng zài yīdìng chéngdù shàng dǎluàn le xǔduō guójiā yuándìng de jīngjì fùsū jìnchéng. The COVID-19 pandemic to a certain extent disrupted the planned economic recovery process in many countries.

**谈判进程因一方的突然退出而陷入停滞，国际社会对此深感忧虑。** Tánpàn jìnchéng yīn yīfāng de tūrán tuìchū ér xiànrù tíngzhì, guójì shèhuì duì cǐ shēn gǎn yōulǜ. The negotiation process fell into a standstill due to the sudden withdrawal of one party, causing deep concern in the international community.

## Collocations
| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 历史进程 | historical process / course of history |
| 推进进程 | to advance the process |
| 民主化进程 | process of democratization |
| 加速进程 | to accelerate the process |

## Usage Notes

进程 and 过程 are close but 进程 has a stronger forward-moving, large-scale connotation: you would say 历史进程 (course of history) but 解题过程 (problem-solving process). 进程 also implies that the process is unfolding and ongoing in a meaningful direction, making it more appropriate for grand narratives of civilization, politics, or social development.

In computer science contexts, 进程 also refers to a "process" in the technical sense (a running program), but this usage is domain-specific and separate from the literary/political meaning.

## Memory Hook

进 (to advance/progress) + 程 (course/journey) — 进程 is the advancing course, the forward movement of a great journey.
