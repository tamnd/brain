## 文脉 (wénmài)

文脉 uses the metaphor of a "vein" (脉) to describe the continuous thread of cultural tradition, the internal coherence of a text, or the living transmission of literary and intellectual heritage. It can refer to the cultural continuity of a civilization, the thematic thread running through a body of work, or the contextual logic of a passage.

## Meanings
1. **[n]** cultural lineage, cultural vein (the continuous thread of a cultural tradition)
2. **[n]** textual thread, contextual coherence (the internal logic or through-line of a text)

## Example Sentences
**保护历史街区，本质上是保护城市的文脉与集体记忆，而非仅仅保存建筑外观。** Bǎohù lìshǐ jiēqū, běnzhì shàng shì bǎohù chéngshì de wénmài yǔ jítǐ jìyì, ér fēi jǐnjǐn bǎocún jiànzhú wàiguān. Protecting historic districts is essentially about protecting the cultural lineage and collective memory of a city, not merely preserving architectural appearances.

**这位学者致力于梳理中国文学思想的文脉传承，探寻其在当代的延续与变迁。** Zhè wèi xuézhě zhìlì yú shūlǐ Zhōngguó wénxué sīxiǎng de wénmài chuánchéng, tànxún qí zài dāngdài de yánxù yǔ biànqiān. This scholar is dedicated to tracing the cultural lineage of Chinese literary thought and exploring its continuation and transformation in the contemporary era.

**城市化进程中，许多传统村落的文脉遭到了不可逆转的割断。** Chéngshìhuà jìnchéng zhōng, xǔduō chuántǒng cūnluò de wénmài zāodào le bùkě nì zhuǎn de gēduàn. In the process of urbanization, the cultural lineage of many traditional villages has suffered irreversible severance.

**阅读这部诗集，需要把握其内在的文脉，才能理解每首诗在整体结构中的位置。** Yuèdú zhè bù shījí, xūyào bǎwò qí nèizài de wénmài, cái néng lǐjiě měi shǒu shī zài zhěngtǐ jiégòu zhōng de wèizhì. Reading this poetry collection requires grasping its internal textual thread in order to understand the position of each poem in the overall structure.

**传统节日是民族文脉的重要载体，其仪式蕴含着深厚的历史积淀。** Chuántǒng jiérì shì mínzú wénmài de zhòngyào zàitǐ, qí yíshì yùnhán zhe shēnhòu de lìshǐ jīdiàn. Traditional festivals are important carriers of a people's cultural lineage; their rituals contain profound historical accumulation.

**这位作曲家试图在现代音乐语言中延续中国古典音乐的文脉。** Zhè wèi zuòqǔ jiā shìtú zài xiàndài yīnyuè yǔyán zhōng yánxù Zhōngguó gǔdiǎn yīnyuè de wénmài. This composer attempts to continue the cultural lineage of Chinese classical music within a modern musical language.

## Usage Notes

文脉 is a relatively literary and formal term, more common in cultural criticism, urban planning discourse, and literary analysis than in everyday speech. It borrows the biological metaphor of 脉 (pulse/vein) to suggest that culture has a living, circulatory continuity that can be sustained or severed. The related term 文化脉络 is a near-synonym but more explicitly analytical.

Distinguish from 语境 (context of utterance) and 背景 (background): 文脉 emphasizes continuity and transmission across time, while 语境 focuses on immediate communicative context.

## Memory Hook

文 (culture/writing) + 脉 (vein/pulse) — 文脉 is the cultural pulse, the living vein of tradition running through a civilization or text.
