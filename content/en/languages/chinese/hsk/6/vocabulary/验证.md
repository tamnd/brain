## 验证 (yànzhèng)

验证 means to verify or validate — to test something empirically or logically to confirm that it is true, correct, or effective. It implies a systematic process of checking, testing, or confirming, and is central to scientific method, technology, and academic research. It is also used in everyday contexts such as identity verification.

## Meanings
1. **[v]** to verify; to validate; to confirm through testing or evidence
2. **[v]** to prove empirically; to demonstrate the truth of (a hypothesis or theory)

## Example Sentences
**实验数据充分验证了研究团队最初提出的理论假设。** Shíyàn shùjù chōngfèn yànzhèngle yánjiū tuánduì zuìchū tíchū de lǐlùn jiǎshè. The experimental data fully validated the theoretical hypotheses initially proposed by the research team.

**这一历史事件再次验证了"得民心者得天下"的古老智慧。** Zhè yī lìshǐ shìjiàn zàicì yànzhèngle "dé mínxīn zhě dé tiānxià" de gǔlǎo zhìhuì. This historical event once again verified the ancient wisdom that "he who wins the hearts of the people wins all under heaven."

**系统上线前，工程师对所有功能模块进行了严格的验证测试。** Xìtǒng shàngxiàn qián, gōngchéngshī duì suǒyǒu gōngnéng mókuài jìnxíngle yángé de yànzhèng cèshì. Before the system went live, engineers conducted rigorous validation testing on all functional modules.

**现代考古发现不断验证着古代文献记载的历史真实性。** Xiàndài kǎogǔ fāxiàn bùduàn yànzhèngzhe gǔdài wénxiàn jìzǎi de lìshǐ zhēnshíxìng. Modern archaeological discoveries continuously verify the historical authenticity of ancient documentary records.

**在提交正式报告之前，所有统计数据必须经过独立机构的交叉验证。** Zài tíjiāo zhèngshì bàogào zhīqián, suǒyǒu tǒngjì shùjù bìxū jīngguò dúlì jīgòu de jiāochā yànzhèng. Before submitting the formal report, all statistical data must undergo cross-validation by an independent institution.

**这项临床试验的目的是验证新药对特定癌症类型的治疗有效性。** Zhè xiàng línchuáng shìyàn de mùdì shì yànzhèng xīnyào duì tèdìng áizhèng lèixíng de zhìliáo yǒuxiàoxìng. The purpose of this clinical trial is to verify the therapeutic efficacy of the new drug for specific cancer types.

## Usage Notes

验证 is closely tied to the empirical method and is fundamental in scientific, technological, and academic discourse. It differs from 证明 (zhèngmíng), which means to prove (often through logic or argument), in that 验证 specifically implies testing or empirical confirmation. 验证 is often the step that follows a hypothesis (假设): you propose a hypothesis and then verify it.

In technology contexts, 验证 is used for validation and verification in software engineering (功能验证, 验证码). In everyday life, 身份验证 (identity verification) and 验证码 (verification code/CAPTCHA) are common uses. The past form 经过验证 (proven, validated) functions as a modifier: 经过验证的方法 (a validated method).

## Memory Hook

验 (to test, to inspect) + 证 (to prove, evidence): "testing something and obtaining proof" — empirical confirmation.
