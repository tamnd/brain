HSK 2 builds on the foundation of HSK 1, adding the grammar patterns and vocabulary needed to handle real situations: navigating a city, visiting a doctor, shopping, expressing opinions, and talking about the past. These 15 lessons cover all core HSK 2 grammar structures with natural dialogues and targeted practice.

## Lessons

| # | Title | Grammar Focus |
|---|-------|---------------|
| 1 | [Comparisons](01-comparisons) | A比B + adj, 没有 comparison, 一样 |
| 2 | [Directions & Navigation](02-directions-navigation) | 左/右/直走, 从...到... |
| 3 | [Expressing Opinions](03-expressing-opinions) | 觉得 + clause, 认为 |
| 4 | [Past Events](04-past-events) | V + 了 + O, 没有 + V (past), 刚才 |
| 5 | [Making Plans](05-making-plans) | 打算 + V, 准备 + V, 明天我要... |
| 6 | [At the Restaurant](06-at-the-restaurant) | 给我 + N (ordering), 可以...吗？ |
| 7 | [Shopping & Bargaining](07-shopping-bargaining) | 能不能便宜一点？, 太贵了, 打折 |
| 8 | [Health & At the Doctor](08-health-doctor) | 哪里不舒服？, 痛了多长时间？ |
| 9 | [Phone & Communication](09-phone-communication) | 打电话, 发短信, 在线 |
| 10 | [Weather Deep Dive](10-weather-deep-dive) | 越来越 + adj, 天气预报说... |

| 15 | [HSK 2 Review](15-hsk2-review) | Review of all A1+ patterns |
