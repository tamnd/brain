## 时候 (shíhou)

Refers to a specific point in time, a moment, or an occasion. It answers the question "when?" and is used in constructions like 的时候 (when..., at the time of...). Key contrast: 时候 is a point or occasion, while 时间 (shíjiān) refers to duration or time as an abstract concept.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** A point in time, a moment, an occasion.

## Example Sentences

**我小的时候喜欢唱歌。**
Wǒ xiǎo de shíhou xǐhuan chànggē.
When I was young, I liked to sing.

**你什么时候回来？**
Nǐ shénme shíhou huílái?
When are you coming back?

**吃饭的时候不要看手机。**
Chīfàn de shíhou bùyào kàn shǒujī.
Don't look at your phone while eating.

**那个时候他还是学生。**
Nàge shíhou tā háishi xuésheng.
At that time he was still a student.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 的时候 | when (doing something) |
| 什么时候 | when (question) |
| 那个时候 | at that time |
| 小时候 | when one was young |
| 有时候 | sometimes |

## Memory Hook

时 is time and 候 is to wait/season: 时候 captures a moment you are waiting for or living through, not a stretch of time.
