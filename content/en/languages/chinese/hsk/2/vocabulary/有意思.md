## 有意思 (yǒu yìsi)

Means interesting or fun. Literally "has meaning/significance." Used to express that something is engaging or amusing. The opposite is 没意思 (méi yìsi), boring or pointless.

## Meanings

1. **[adj phrase]** Interesting, fun, amusing, worth your attention.

## Example Sentences

**这本书很有意思。**
Zhè běn shū hěn yǒu yìsi.
This book is very interesting.

**中国文化很有意思。**
Zhōngguó wénhuà hěn yǒu yìsi.
Chinese culture is very interesting.

**这个游戏一点也不有意思。**
Zhège yóuxì yīdiǎn yě bù yǒu yìsi.
This game is not interesting at all.

**他说的话很有意思，大家都笑了。**
Tā shuō de huà hěn yǒu yìsi, dàjiā dōu xiào le.
What he said was very amusing and everyone laughed.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 很有意思 | very interesting |
| 没有意思 | boring, not interesting |
| 真有意思 | truly interesting/amusing |
| 有意思的事 | an interesting thing |
| 越来越有意思 | getting more and more interesting |

## Memory Hook

有 is "to have" and 意思 is "meaning/significance": if something 有意思, it has meaning — it is worth paying attention to.
