## 真 (zhēn)

Used as an adverb before adjectives or verbs to intensify meaning, equivalent to "really" or "truly." Also used as an adjective meaning genuine or real. A very common intensifier in spoken Chinese.

## Meanings

1. **[adv]** Really, truly (intensifier before adjectives and verbs).
2. **[adj]** Real, genuine, authentic.

## Example Sentences

**这个苹果真甜！**
Zhège píngguǒ zhēn tián!
This apple is really sweet!

**他说的是真的吗？**
Tā shuō de shì zhēn de ma?
Is what he said true?

**你真厉害！**
Nǐ zhēn lìhai!
You are really impressive!

**这是真的皮包，不是假的。**
Zhè shì zhēn de pí bāo, bú shì jiǎ de.
This is a genuine leather bag, not a fake.

## Memory Hook

真 has 目 (eye) at the bottom: your eye sees what is 真 — what is real and true, not an illusion.
