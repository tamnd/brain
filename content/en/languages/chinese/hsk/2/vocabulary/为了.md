## 为了 (wèile)

A preposition meaning "in order to" or "for the sake of," used to state the purpose or motivation behind an action. It introduces a goal and precedes the verb phrase that achieves it.

## Meanings

1. **[prep]** In order to, for the purpose of, for the sake of.

## Example Sentences

**为了学好汉语，他每天练习。**
Wèile xuéhǎo Hànyǔ, tā měi tiān liànxí.
In order to learn Chinese well, he practices every day.

**她为了家人努力工作。**
Tā wèile jiārén nǔlì gōngzuò.
She works hard for the sake of her family.

**为了健康，我开始跑步了。**
Wèile jiànkāng, wǒ kāishǐ pǎobù le.
For the sake of my health, I started running.

**他为了省钱，不去餐厅吃饭。**
Tā wèile shěng qián, bù qù cāntīng chīfàn.
In order to save money, he does not eat at restaurants.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 为了…而… | in order to ... do ... |
| 为了健康 | for the sake of health |
| 为了省钱 | in order to save money |
| 为了家人 | for the family |
| 为了将来 | for the future |

## Memory Hook

为 means "for/to act as" and 了 marks completion: you do something completely "for" a goal — that is 为了.
