## 请 (qǐng)

Placed before a verb to make a polite request meaning "please." Also used as a verb meaning to invite someone or to treat someone to a meal or drink.

## Meanings

1. **[adv]** Please (polite marker before a verb).
2. **[verb]** To invite someone to come or attend.
3. **[verb]** To treat someone to food or a drink.

## Example Sentences

**请进，欢迎你。**
Qǐng jìn, huānyíng nǐ.
Please come in. Welcome.

**我请你吃饭吧。**
Wǒ qǐng nǐ chīfàn ba.
Let me treat you to a meal.

**请问，洗手间在哪里？**
Qǐngwèn, xǐshǒujiān zài nǎlǐ?
Excuse me, where is the restroom?

**他请了很多朋友来参加生日会。**
Tā qǐng le hěn duō péngyou lái cānjiā shēngrì huì.
He invited many friends to attend his birthday party.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 请进 | please come in |
| 请问 | excuse me / may I ask |
| 请坐 | please sit down |
| 请客 | to treat guests, to pick up the bill |
| 请你… | please (do something) |

## Memory Hook

A waiter bowing and gesturing inward says 请 — it is the sound of gracious hospitality.
