## 拿 (ná)

拿 means to take, hold, or carry something with the hand. It implies using your hand to pick up or hold an object. It is a physical, hands-on action.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To take, to pick up, to hold (with the hand).
2. **[verb]** To carry, to bring.

## Example Sentences

**你能帮我拿一下这个吗？**
Nǐ néng bāng wǒ ná yīxià zhège ma?
Can you help me hold this for a moment?

**她手里拿着一本书。**
Tā shǒu lǐ ná zhe yī běn shū.
She is holding a book in her hand.

**请拿一下你的护照。**
Qǐng ná yīxià nǐ de hùzhào.
Please take out your passport.

**我去拿一杯水给你。**
Wǒ qù ná yī bēi shuǐ gěi nǐ.
I'll go get a glass of water for you.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 拿走 | to take away |
| 拿出来 | to take out |
| 拿到 | to obtain, to get |
| 拿手 | good at (lit. in your hand's skill) |

## Memory Hook

拿 = 合 (combine) + 手 (hand): bring things together with your hand. Your hand reaches out and grabs.
