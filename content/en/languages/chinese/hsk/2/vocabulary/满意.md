## 满意 (mǎnyì)

满意 means satisfied or content, used when something meets your expectations or desires. 满 means "full/filled" and 意 means "intention/meaning," together conveying "filled with the intended result."

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** Satisfied, content, pleased.

## Example Sentences

**我对这个结果很满意。**
Wǒ duì zhège jiéguǒ hěn mǎnyì.
I am very satisfied with this result.

**老师对学生的表现很满意。**
Lǎoshī duì xuésheng de biǎoxiàn hěn mǎnyì.
The teacher is very satisfied with the students' performance.

**你满意吗？**
Nǐ mǎnyì ma?
Are you satisfied?

**客人对服务不太满意。**
Kèrén duì fúwù bù tài mǎnyì.
The guest is not very satisfied with the service.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |

## Memory Hook

满 (full) + 意 (intention): when reality fills up all your expectations, you are 满意.
