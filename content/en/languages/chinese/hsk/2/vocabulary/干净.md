## 干净 (gānjìng)

干净 describes something free of dirt, mess, or impurity. It works as a predicate adjective (桌子很干净 — the table is clean) or a pre-noun modifier (干净的衣服 — clean clothes). Its opposite is 脏 (zāng — dirty).

## Meanings

1. **[adj]** Clean; spotless — free of dirt or stains.
2. **[adj]** Tidy; neat — well-organized and orderly.

## Example Sentences

**这家餐厅非常干净。**
Zhè jiā cāntīng fēicháng gānjìng.
This restaurant is extremely clean.

**请把手洗干净。**
Qǐng bǎ shǒu xǐ gānjìng.
Please wash your hands clean.

**他的房间总是很干净。**
Tā de fángjiān zǒngshì hěn gānjìng.
His room is always very clean.

**穿干净的衣服去面试。**
Chuān gānjìng de yīfu qù miànshì.
Wear clean clothes to the interview.

## Memory Hook

干 (gān) means "dry" and 净 means "pure" — something 干净 is both dry and pure, with no dirt in sight.
