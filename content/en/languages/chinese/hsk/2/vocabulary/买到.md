## 买到 (mǎi dào)

买到 is a verb + result complement. 买 means "to buy" and 到 is a result complement indicating that the action reached its goal — you obtained what you were trying to buy. It highlights the successful outcome of the purchasing attempt. The negative 买不到 means "can't get / impossible to find/buy," while 没买到 means "tried but failed to buy."

## Meanings

1. **[v+comp]** Manage to buy; successfully obtain — the purchase attempt succeeded.
2. **[v+comp]** Get hold of — used when an item is hard to find and you finally secured it.

## Example Sentences

**我终于买到票了！**
Wǒ zhōngyú mǎi dào piào le!
I finally managed to get a ticket!

**这个颜色很难买到。**
Zhège yánsè hěn nán mǎi dào.
This color is very hard to find/buy.

**你买到你想要的东西了吗？**
Nǐ mǎi dào nǐ xiǎng yào de dōngxi le ma?
Did you manage to buy the things you wanted?

**那本书现在买不到了。**
Nà běn shū xiànzài mǎi bu dào le.
That book can't be bought anywhere now.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 买不到 | can't buy; unavailable |
| 买得到 | can be bought; available |
| 没买到 | failed to buy; couldn't get |
| 买到票 | managed to get a ticket |
| 买到便宜货 | got a bargain |

## Memory Hook

买 (buy) + 到 (reach/arrive) = your shopping action reached the goal. 到 is the moment the item lands in your hands.
