## 刚才 (gāngcái)

刚才 means "just now" or "a moment ago" and always refers to a completed action in the past. Compare with 刚 (gāng), which means "just" and focuses on actions that are very recent but can still be ongoing or present: 他刚来 (He just arrived, he's here now) vs. 他刚才来了 (He came a moment ago, he may have left). 刚才 is only past tense; 刚 can be used for the immediate present.

## Meanings

1. **[time word]** Just now; a moment ago. Refers to something that happened a short time before the current moment.

## Example Sentences

**你刚才说什么？我没听清楚。**
Nǐ gāngcái shuō shénme? Wǒ méi tīng qīngchǔ.
What did you just say? I didn't hear clearly.

**刚才有人打电话找你。**
Gāngcái yǒu rén dǎ diànhuà zhǎo nǐ.
Someone called for you just now.

**他刚才还在这里，现在不见了。**
Tā gāngcái hái zài zhèlǐ, xiànzài bù jiàn le.
He was just here a moment ago; now he's gone.

**我刚才忘了关灯。**
Wǒ gāngcái wàng le guāndēng.
I forgot to turn off the lights just now.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 刚才说的 (gāngcái shuō de) | what was just said |
| 刚才发生的 (gāngcái fāshēng de) | what just happened |
| 就在刚才 (jiù zài gāngcái) | just this moment ago |
| 刚才还... (gāngcái hái...) | was still...just now |
| 刚 vs 刚才 | 刚 = very recently (can be present); 刚才 = a moment ago (past only) |

## Memory Hook

刚 means "just/just now" and 才 adds the sense of "only then." Together they sharpen the focus: the action happened, and it happened only a short moment ago — just now, no earlier.
