## 右边 (yòubiān)

右边 is a direction noun meaning "the right side." Use the structure 在 + [reference point] + 右边 to express location. It pairs naturally with 左边 (left side) when giving directions or describing spatial layouts. For turning, use 向右 or 往右走.

## Meanings

1. **[n]** Right side; the right — location relative to a reference point or speaker.
2. **[n]** On the right — used with 在 to place objects in space.

## Example Sentences

**超市在学校的右边。**
Chāoshì zài xuéxiào de yòubiān.
The supermarket is to the right of the school.

**请在右边等我。**
Qǐng zài yòubiān děng wǒ.
Please wait for me on the right.

**你看右边那栋楼，那就是图书馆。**
Nǐ kàn yòubiān nà dòng lóu, nà jiù shì túshūguǎn.
Look at that building on the right — that's the library.

**向右转，然后直走。**
Xiàng yòu zhuǎn, ránhòu zhí zǒu.
Turn right, then go straight.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 在…右边 | to the right of … |
| 右边的门 | the door on the right |
| 向右转 | turn right |
| 左边和右边 | left and right |
| 右边第一个 | the first one on the right |

## Memory Hook

右 contains 口 (mouth) — people tend to use their right hand to bring food to their mouth, linking 右 to "right."
