## 清楚 (qīngchu)

Used as an adjective meaning clear or distinct, and as a verb meaning to understand clearly. It describes both physical clarity (e.g., sound, image) and mental clarity (e.g., knowing something well).

## Meanings

1. **[adj]** Clear, distinct, easy to perceive.
2. **[verb]** To know clearly, to understand well.

## Example Sentences

**你说得很清楚，我听懂了。**
Nǐ shuō de hěn qīngchu, wǒ tīngdǒng le.
You spoke very clearly; I understood.

**这张照片不清楚。**
Zhè zhāng zhàopiàn bù qīngchu.
This photo is not clear.

**我不清楚他的名字。**
Wǒ bù qīngchu tā de míngzi.
I am not sure about his name.

**请说清楚，我没听明白。**
Qǐng shuō qīngchu, wǒ méi tīng míngbai.
Please speak clearly. I did not understand.
