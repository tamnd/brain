## 左边 (zuǒbiān)

左边 is a direction noun meaning "the left side." To say something is located to the left, use the structure 在 + [reference point] + 左边. When giving turn directions, use 向左 (turn left) or 往左走 (walk to the left) rather than 左边.

## Meanings

1. **[n]** Left side; the left — location relative to a reference point or speaker.
2. **[n]** On the left — used with 在 to place objects in space.

## Example Sentences

**银行在邮局的左边。**
Yínháng zài yóujú de zuǒbiān.
The bank is to the left of the post office.

**请坐在我的左边。**
Qǐng zuò zài wǒ de zuǒbiān.
Please sit on my left side.

**学校在这条街的左边。**
Xuéxiào zài zhè tiáo jiē de zuǒbiān.
The school is on the left side of this street.

**你的左边有一家咖啡店。**
Nǐ de zuǒbiān yǒu yī jiā kāfēidiàn.
There is a café on your left.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 在…左边 | to the left of … |
| 左边的门 | the door on the left |
| 向左转 | turn left |
| 左边走 | walk/go to the left |
| 左边第一个 | the first one on the left |

## Memory Hook

左 contains 工 (work) on top of a hand radical — remember "use your left hand at work" to lock in the meaning.
