## 特别 (tèbié)

特别 serves as both an intensifying adverb (especially, particularly — stronger than 很) and a descriptive adjective (special, unusual). As an adverb it sits before adjectives or verbs: 特别好吃 (especially delicious). As an adjective it describes things that stand out from the norm: 这很特别 (this is special/unusual).

## Meanings

1. **[adv]** Especially; particularly — an intensifier stronger than 非常, implying something exceeds expectations.
2. **[adj]** Special; unique; extraordinary — different from what is ordinary or expected.

## Example Sentences

**这道菜特别好吃。**
Zhè dào cài tèbié hǎochī.
This dish is especially delicious.

**我特别喜欢北京的冬天。**
Wǒ tèbié xǐhuan Běijīng de dōngtiān.
I especially like Beijing's winter.

**今天有特别的事情要告诉你。**
Jīntiān yǒu tèbié de shìqing yào gàosu nǐ.
Today I have something special to tell you.

**她的发型很特别，大家都注意到了。**
Tā de fàxíng hěn tèbié, dàjiā dōu zhùyì dào le.
Her hairstyle is very unique; everyone noticed it.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 特别好 | especially good |
| 特别喜欢 | especially like; really fond of |
| 很特别 | very special/unique |
| 特别的礼物 | a special gift |
| 特别是 | especially; in particular |

## Memory Hook

特 relates to "exceptional" and 别 means "different" — something 特别 is exceptionally different from everything else around it.
