## 找到 (zhǎo dào)

找到 is a verb + result complement. 找 means "to look for / search" and 到 is the result complement indicating that the searching action reached its goal — you actually found it. Without 到, 找 is just the act of searching (outcome unknown). 找不到 means "can't find" and 没找到 means "searched but didn't find."

## Meanings

1. **[v+comp]** Find; locate — the search was successful.
2. **[v+comp]** Track down; come across — discover something after looking for it.

## Example Sentences

**我找到我的钥匙了。**
Wǒ zhǎo dào wǒ de yàoshi le.
I found my keys.

**你找到工作了吗？**
Nǐ zhǎo dào gōngzuò le ma?
Have you found a job?

**他找不到回家的路。**
Tā zhǎo bu dào huí jiā de lù.
He can't find the way home.

**我们终于找到了一家好餐厅。**
Wǒmen zhōngyú zhǎo dào le yī jiā hǎo cāntīng.
We finally found a good restaurant.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 找不到 | can't find |
| 找得到 | can be found; able to find |
| 没找到 | didn't find; search failed |
| 找到了 | found it! |
| 找到工作 | find a job |

## Memory Hook

找 (search) + 到 (arrive/reach) = your search arrived at the target. 到 is the moment of "got it!" when the search ends successfully.
