## 事情 (shìqing)

Refers to an affair, matter, or situation that involves doing or handling something. It is broader than 东西 (physical things) and covers events, tasks, and issues in daily life.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Matter, affair, business, thing (non-physical).

## Example Sentences

**我有一件事情想告诉你。**
Wǒ yǒu yī jiàn shìqing xiǎng gàosu nǐ.
I have something I want to tell you.

**这件事情很重要。**
Zhè jiàn shìqing hěn zhòngyào.
This matter is very important.

**他今天有很多事情要做。**
Tā jīntiān yǒu hěn duō shìqing yào zuò.
He has many things to do today.

**你在担心什么事情？**
Nǐ zài dānxīn shénme shìqing?
What matter are you worrying about?

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 一件事情 | one matter/thing |
| 很多事情 | many things |
| 处理事情 | to handle a matter |
| 重要的事情 | an important matter |
| 没什么事情 | nothing going on, no big deal |

## Memory Hook

事 means affair/work and 情 means situation/feeling: 事情 covers everything that "matters" in daily life.
