## 面条 (miàntiáo)

面条 means noodles. 面 means flour or dough and 条 is the measure word for long, thin, strip-shaped items. Together they describe strips of dough: noodles. A beloved and essential Chinese food.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Noodles.

## Example Sentences

**我中午想吃面条。**
Wǒ zhōngwǔ xiǎng chī miàntiáo.
I want to eat noodles at noon.

**妈妈做的面条很好吃。**
Māma zuò de miàntiáo hěn hǎochī.
The noodles mom makes are delicious.

**他每天早上都吃面条。**
Tā měitiān zǎoshang dōu chī miàntiáo.
He eats noodles every morning.

**这碗面条里有很多蔬菜。**
Zhè wǎn miàntiáo lǐ yǒu hěn duō shūcài.
This bowl of noodles has a lot of vegetables in it.

## Memory Hook

面 (flour dough) + 条 (long strips): long strips of flour dough. Picture pulling noodles into long strips.
