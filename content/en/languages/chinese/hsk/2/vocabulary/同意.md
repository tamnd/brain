## 同意 (tóngyì)

Means to agree with an opinion, or to consent to a request or plan. It can be used with a person (agree with someone) or with a proposal (agree to something).

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To agree, to consent, to approve.

## Example Sentences

**我同意你的看法。**
Wǒ tóngyì nǐ de kànfǎ.
I agree with your view.

**他不同意这个计划。**
Tā bù tóngyì zhège jìhuà.
He does not agree with this plan.

**你同意还是不同意？**
Nǐ tóngyì háishi bù tóngyì?
Do you agree or disagree?

**大家都同意了，可以出发了。**
Dàjiā dōu tóngyì le, kěyǐ chūfā le.
Everyone agreed, so we can set off.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 同意意见 | to agree with an opinion |
| 不同意 | to disagree, to object |
| 表示同意 | to express agreement |
| 同意计划 | to approve a plan |
| 都同意 | everyone agrees |

## Memory Hook

同 means same and 意 means meaning/intent: when you share the same intent as someone, you 同意.
