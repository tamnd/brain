## 爬山 (páshān)

爬山 means to climb a mountain or go hiking. 爬 means to crawl or climb and 山 means mountain. It is a verb-object phrase and a popular leisure activity in China.

## Meanings

1. **[verb phrase]** To climb a mountain, to go hiking.

## Example Sentences

**周末我们去爬山吧。**
Zhōumò wǒmen qù páshān ba.
Let's go hiking this weekend.

**他喜欢爬山，每个月都去一次。**
Tā xǐhuān páshān, měi gè yuè dōu qù yī cì.
He likes hiking and goes once a month.

**今天爬山爬得很累。**
Jīntiān páshān pá de hěn lèi.
Hiking today was very tiring.

**爬山对身体很好。**
Páshān duì shēntǐ hěn hǎo.
Climbing mountains is good for your health.

## Memory Hook

爬 shows a reptile creeping (爪 claws + 巴 tail): climbing a mountain feels like crawling on all fours up the slope.
