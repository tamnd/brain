## 方便 (fāngbiàn)

方便 describes things that are convenient, handy, or easy to do. It can describe locations (交通方便 — convenient transport), actions (这样做比较方便 — this way is more convenient), or be used in polite requests (方便的话… — if it's convenient for you…).

## Meanings

1. **[adj]** Convenient; handy — easy to use, access, or do.
2. **[adj]** Suitable; appropriate — used in polite questions about whether the time is right.

## Example Sentences

**这里交通很方便。**
Zhèlǐ jiāotōng hěn fāngbiàn.
The transport here is very convenient.

**用手机支付非常方便。**
Yòng shǒujī zhīfù fēicháng fāngbiàn.
Paying by mobile phone is extremely convenient.

**方便的话，你能来一下吗？**
Fāngbiàn dehuà, nǐ néng lái yīxià ma?
If it's convenient for you, could you come over?

**住在学校附近很方便。**
Zhù zài xuéxiào fùjìn hěn fāngbiàn.
Living near the school is very convenient.

## Memory Hook

方 means "way/direction" and 便 relates to "ease" — a 方便 thing puts a clear, easy path in front of you.
