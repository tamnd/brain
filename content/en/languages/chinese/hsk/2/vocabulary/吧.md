## 吧 (ba)

吧 is a versatile sentence-final particle. At HSK 2 level, it appears in three main roles: making suggestions, softening assumptions, and forming tag questions. It always carries a neutral tone (ba).

## Meanings

1. **[particle]** Suggestion or invitation: "let's..." or "shall we...?" Placed at the end of a proposal to make it sound friendly rather than commanding.
2. **[particle]** Assumption or inference: "I suppose..." or "probably..." Used when the speaker thinks something is true but is not fully certain.
3. **[particle]** Tag question or soft confirmation: "right?" or "isn't it?" Invites the listener to agree or correct.

## Example Sentences

**我们走吧。**
Wǒmen zǒu ba.
Let's go.

**你是学生吧？**
Nǐ shì xuéshēng ba?
You're a student, right?

**外面很冷吧？**
Wàimiàn hěn lěng ba?
It's pretty cold outside, I imagine?

**先吃饭吧，再说其他的。**
Xiān chīfàn ba, zài shuō qítā de.
Let's eat first, then talk about the rest.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |

## Memory Hook

Picture someone nudging you toward the door saying "Ba?" — half suggestion, half question. 吧 always adds that gentle, inviting push to whatever comes before it.
