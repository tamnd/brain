## 除了 (chúle)

除了 introduces either an exception ("except for X, everything else...") or an addition ("besides X, also..."). The meaning flips based on what follows. With 都 it usually means "except"; with 还/也 it means "in addition to." The paired word 以外 (yǐwài) can optionally close the phrase.

## Meanings

1. **[preposition]** Except for; other than. 除了X，都... — "Except for X, everything/everyone..."
2. **[preposition]** Besides; in addition to. 除了X，还/也... — "Besides X, also..."

## Example Sentences

**除了他，大家都来了。**
Chúle tā, dàjiā dōu lái le.
Except for him, everyone came.

**除了中文，她还会说法语。**
Chúle Zhōngwén, tā hái huì shuō Fǎyǔ.
Besides Chinese, she can also speak French.

**除了周末以外，我每天都上班。**
Chúle zhōumò yǐwài, wǒ měitiān dōu shàngbān.
Except for weekends, I go to work every day.

**除了苹果，我还买了香蕉。**
Chúle píngguǒ, wǒ hái mǎi le xiāngjiāo.
Besides apples, I also bought bananas.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 除了...以外 (chúle...yǐwài) | except for...; apart from... |
| 除了...还... (chúle...hái...) | besides..., also... |
| 除了...都... (chúle...dōu...) | except for..., all... |
| 除了...之外 (chúle...zhīwài) | apart from... (formal variant) |
| 除此之外 (chú cǐ zhīwài) | apart from this; besides this |

## Memory Hook

除 contains the character for "excess/spare" — think of 除了 as scooping out one item from a pile. What remains "all" fits one pattern; the scooped-out piece is the exception or the extra.
