## 男 (nán)

男 means male or man. It is used as an adjective before nouns (男生, 男朋友) or as a noun referring to a male person. Its opposite is 女 (nǚ), female.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** Male.
2. **[noun]** A man, a boy.

## Example Sentences

**他是一个男生。**
Tā shì yīgè nánshēng.
He is a male student / a boy.

**男厕所在右边。**
Nán cèsuǒ zài yòubiān.
The men's restroom is on the right.

**她有一个男朋友。**
Tā yǒu yīgè nán péngyou.
She has a boyfriend.

**班里有十个男同学。**
Bān lǐ yǒu shí gè nán tóngxué.
There are ten male classmates in the class.

## Memory Hook

男 = 田 (field) + 力 (strength): the man works the field with his strength. A classic ancient image of the male role.
