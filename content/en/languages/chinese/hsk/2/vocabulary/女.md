## 女 (nǚ)

女 means female or woman. It is used as an adjective before nouns (女生, 女朋友) or as a noun referring to a female person. Its opposite is 男 (nán), male. The character is one of the most recognizable in Chinese.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** Female.
2. **[noun]** A woman, a girl.

## Example Sentences

**她是一个女生。**
Tā shì yīgè nǚshēng.
She is a female student / a girl.

**女厕所在左边。**
Nǚ cèsuǒ zài zuǒbiān.
The women's restroom is on the left.

**他有一个女朋友。**
Tā yǒu yīgè nǚ péngyou.
He has a girlfriend.

**这位女老师教数学。**
Zhè wèi nǚ lǎoshī jiāo shùxué.
This female teacher teaches math.

## Memory Hook

女 depicts a person with arms crossed and legs together, a graceful seated posture. One of the oldest pictographic characters in Chinese.
