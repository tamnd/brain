## 有名 (yǒumíng)

有名 is an adjective meaning famous or well-known. It is not a verb, so do not use it with a direct object. Say 他很有名 (he is very famous), not *他有名了某人. Scope can be specified with 在…很有名 (famous in/among…) or 全国有名 (nationally famous).

## Meanings

1. **[adj]** Famous; well-known — recognized by many people.
2. **[adj]** Renowned; celebrated — having a strong positive reputation in a field or area.

## Example Sentences

**这位歌手非常有名。**
Zhè wèi gēshǒu fēicháng yǒumíng.
This singer is extremely famous.

**长城在全世界都很有名。**
Chángchéng zài quánshìjiè dōu hěn yǒumíng.
The Great Wall is very famous throughout the whole world.

**他是一位有名的医生。**
Tā shì yī wèi yǒumíng de yīshēng.
He is a renowned doctor.

**这家店的饺子在本地很有名。**
Zhè jiā diàn de jiǎozi zài běndì hěn yǒumíng.
This shop's dumplings are very famous locally.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 很有名 | very famous |
| 全国有名 | nationally famous |
| 有名的地方 | a famous place |
| 有名的人 | a famous person |
| 在…很有名 | famous in/among … |

## Memory Hook

有 means "have" and 名 means "name" — someone who is 有名 is someone whose name everyone has heard of.
