## 认为 (rènwéi)

Used to express a personal opinion or belief, equivalent to "I think that..." or "I consider..." It introduces a clause and is more formal than 觉得, focusing on intellectual judgment rather than feelings.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To think that, to consider, to be of the opinion that.

## Example Sentences

**我认为这个方法很好。**
Wǒ rènwéi zhège fāngfǎ hěn hǎo.
I think this method is very good.

**他认为学中文不难。**
Tā rènwéi xué Zhōngwén bù nán.
He thinks learning Chinese is not difficult.

**你认为谁是对的？**
Nǐ rènwéi shéi shì duì de?
Who do you think is right?

**大家都认为她很聪明。**
Dàjiā dōu rènwéi tā hěn cōngming.
Everyone considers her to be very smart.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 我认为… | I think / I believe ... |
| 认为…很重要 | to consider something important |
| 普遍认为 | generally considered |
| 不认为 | do not think that |
| 认为…是对的 | to think something is correct |

## Memory Hook

认 means to recognize and 为 means to act as: you recognize something as true, so you 认为 it.
