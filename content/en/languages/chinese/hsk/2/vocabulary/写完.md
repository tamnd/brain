## 写完 (xiě wán)

写完 is a verb + result complement. 写 means "to write" and 完 is the result complement meaning "finished / done completely." Together they mean writing has been finished to completion — nothing is left undone. The complement 完 can attach to many verbs (做完, 吃完, 看完) to express the same idea of total completion. The negative is 没写完 (didn't finish writing) or 写不完 (can't finish writing).

## Meanings

1. **[v+comp]** Finish writing — the writing task is done from start to finish.
2. **[v+comp]** Complete written work — used for homework, essays, or any written task.

## Example Sentences

**我写完作业了。**
Wǒ xiě wán zuòyè le.
I have finished my homework.

**你写完了吗？可以交给我了。**
Nǐ xiě wán le ma? Kěyǐ jiāo gěi wǒ le.
Have you finished writing? You can hand it in to me now.

**作文还没写完，得继续写。**
Zuòwén hái méi xiě wán, děi jìxù xiě.
The essay isn't finished yet; I need to keep writing.

**他一小时就写完了那篇报告。**
Tā yī xiǎoshí jiù xiě wán le nà piān bàogào.
He finished writing that report in just one hour.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 写完作业 | finish writing homework |
| 没写完 | not finished writing yet |
| 写不完 | can't finish writing (too much) |
| 写完了 | (have) finished writing |
| 快写完了 | almost done writing |

## Memory Hook

写 (write) + 完 (complete/whole) — 完 signals "the circle is closed," meaning you wrote all the way to the very end.
