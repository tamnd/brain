## 回答 (huídá)

回答 means to answer or reply, especially in response to a direct question. 回 means "return" and 答 means "reply," together forming "returning a reply."

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To answer, to reply (a question).
2. **[noun]** An answer, a reply.

## Example Sentences

**请回答我的问题。**
Qǐng huídá wǒ de wèntí.
Please answer my question.

**他的回答很好。**
Tā de huídá hěn hǎo.
His answer was very good.

**我不知道怎么回答。**
Wǒ bù zhīdào zěnme huídá.
I don't know how to answer.

**她认真地回答了老师的问题。**
Tā rènzhēn de huídá le lǎoshī de wèntí.
She carefully answered the teacher's question.

## Memory Hook

回 (return) + 答 (reply): you return the words someone sent you. A ball thrown back.
