## 告诉 (gàosu)

告诉 means to tell or to inform someone of something. The structure is: 告诉 + person + content. The person told is placed directly after 告诉, and the information comes after that person. It is a double-object verb.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To tell; to inform; to let someone know. Communicates a piece of information to a specific person.

## Example Sentences

**请告诉我你的名字。**
Qǐng gàosu wǒ nǐ de míngzi.
Please tell me your name.

**他告诉我明天不上班。**
Tā gàosu wǒ míngtiān bù shàngbān.
He told me there is no work tomorrow.

**别告诉他这件事。**
Bié gàosu tā zhè jiàn shì.
Don't tell him about this matter.

**如果你知道答案，请告诉大家。**
Rúguǒ nǐ zhīdào dá'àn, qǐng gàosu dàjiā.
If you know the answer, please tell everyone.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 告诉某人 (gàosu mǒu rén) | to tell someone |
| 别告诉 (bié gàosu) | don't tell |
| 请告诉我 (qǐng gàosu wǒ) | please tell me |
| 我告诉你 (wǒ gàosu nǐ) | let me tell you |
| 没有人告诉我 (méiyǒu rén gàosu wǒ) | nobody told me |

## Memory Hook

告 contains an ox (牛) on top and a mouth (口) below — an ox bellowing to announce something. 诉 means to speak/express. Put them together: an announcement spoken aloud to someone. That is exactly what 告诉 does.
