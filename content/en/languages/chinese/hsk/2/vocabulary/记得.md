## 记得 (jìde)

记得 means to remember or to recall something. 记 means "to record/memorize" and 得 here is a resultative complement indicating success: you recorded it and it stuck. The opposite is 忘了 (wàng le), to forget.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To remember, to recall.

## Example Sentences

**我记得你说过这句话。**
Wǒ jìde nǐ shuō guò zhè jù huà.
I remember you said that.

**你记得他的名字吗？**
Nǐ jìde tā de míngzi ma?
Do you remember his name?

**我不记得在哪里见过她。**
Wǒ bù jìde zài nǎlǐ jiàn guò tā.
I don't remember where I met her.

**记得明天带雨伞！**
Jìde míngtiān dài yǔsǎn!
Remember to bring an umbrella tomorrow!

## Collocations

| 还记得 | still remember |

## Memory Hook

记 (record) + 得 (obtained/succeeded): you recorded it and the memory stuck. Compare with 忘了: the recording failed.
