## 快乐 (kuàilè)

快乐 means happy or joyful. It describes a genuine inner feeling of happiness. It is commonly used in greetings and well wishes, such as 生日快乐 (Happy Birthday) and 新年快乐 (Happy New Year).

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** Happy, joyful.

## Example Sentences

**生日快乐！**
Shēngrì kuàilè!
Happy birthday!

**他每天都很快乐。**
Tā měitiān dōu hěn kuàilè.
He is happy every day.

**孩子们玩得很快乐。**
Háizimen wán de hěn kuàilè.
The children are playing happily.

**我希望你永远快乐。**
Wǒ xīwàng nǐ yǒngyuǎn kuàilè.
I hope you are happy forever.

## Memory Hook

快 (fast/pleasant) + 乐 (music/joy): joy rushes in quickly like an upbeat song.
