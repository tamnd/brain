## 所以 (suǒyǐ)

A conjunction that introduces a result or conclusion. It is almost always paired with 因为 (yīnwèi, because) in the pattern 因为…所以… meaning "because... therefore..." Either part may be omitted in conversation.

## Meanings

1. **[conj]** So, therefore, that is why (introducing a result or conclusion).

## Example Sentences

**因为下雨，所以我没出去。**
Yīnwèi xià yǔ, suǒyǐ wǒ méi chūqù.
Because it was raining, I did not go out.

**他生病了，所以没来上课。**
Tā shēng bìng le, suǒyǐ méi lái shàngkè.
He was sick, so he did not come to class.

**这里很安静，所以我喜欢在这里学习。**
Zhèlǐ hěn ānjìng, suǒyǐ wǒ xǐhuan zài zhèlǐ xuéxí.
It is very quiet here, so I like to study here.

**我忘带钱包了，所以不能买。**
Wǒ wàng dài qiánbāo le, suǒyǐ bù néng mǎi.
I forgot my wallet, so I cannot buy it.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 因为…所以… | because ... therefore ... |
| 所以说 | that is why, so one can say |
| 就所以 | (informal) so that is why |
| 所以才… | that is precisely why ... |
| 不所以 | (not standard; avoid) |

## Memory Hook

所 means "place/reason" and 以 means "by means of": the reason leads you to the conclusion, 所以.
