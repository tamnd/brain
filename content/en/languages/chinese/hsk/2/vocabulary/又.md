## 又 (yòu)

Used before a verb to indicate that something happened again (past repetition). Key contrast with 再 (zài): 又 describes repetition that has already occurred, while 再 describes repetition that will or should happen in the future.

## Meanings

1. **[adv]** Again (past repetition): something happened one more time.
2. **[adv]** Also, both, and (linking two attributes simultaneously).

## Example Sentences

**他又来了。**
Tā yòu lái le.
He came again.

**她又忘记了。**
Tā yòu wàngjì le.
She forgot again.

**这道菜又好看又好吃。**
Zhè dào cài yòu hǎokàn yòu hǎochī.
This dish is both good-looking and delicious.

**我昨天去了，今天又去了。**
Wǒ zuótiān qù le, jīntiān yòu qù le.
I went yesterday and went again today.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 又…了 | did something again |
| 又…又… | both ... and ... |
| 又来了 | here we go again |
| 又一次 | once more, one more time |
| 又忘了 | forgot again |

## Memory Hook

Picture someone turning the same corner twice: 又 is the second footprint falling in the same spot — past repetition already done.
