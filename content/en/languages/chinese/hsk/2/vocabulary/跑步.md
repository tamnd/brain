## 跑步 (pǎobù)

跑步 means to run or jog. 跑 means to run and 步 means step. It is a verb-object phrase used for running as exercise or sport. It is one of the most common fitness activities mentioned in Chinese.

## Meanings

1. **[verb phrase]** To run, to jog.

## Example Sentences

**我每天早上跑步。**
Wǒ měitiān zǎoshang pǎobù.
I go jogging every morning.

**跑步对健康很有好处。**
Pǎobù duì jiànkāng hěn yǒu hǎochù.
Running is very beneficial for health.

**他跑步跑了三十分钟。**
Tā pǎobù pǎo le sānshí fēnzhōng.
He ran for thirty minutes.

**你喜欢跑步吗？**
Nǐ xǐhuān pǎobù ma?
Do you like running?

## Memory Hook

跑 has 足 (foot radical) on the left: running is what your feet do urgently. 步 (steps) reminds you it's about moving your feet one step after another, fast.
