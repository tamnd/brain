## 外 (wài)

A locative word meaning outside or beyond. Used to indicate physical location (outside a room, building) or figurative scope (beyond that, in addition to). The opposite is 内 (nèi, inside/interior).

## Meanings

1. **[noun/locative]** Outside, exterior.
2. **[noun]** Other, beyond, in addition to.

## Example Sentences

**外面很冷，多穿点衣服。**
Wàimiàn hěn lěng, duō chuān diǎn yīfu.
It is very cold outside. Wear more clothes.

**请在门外等一下。**
Qǐng zài mén wài děng yīxià.
Please wait outside the door.

**除了学汉语，他在课外还学钢琴。**
Chúle xué Hànyǔ, tā zài kè wài hái xué gāngqín.
Besides studying Chinese, he also studies piano outside of class.

**窗外有一棵大树。**
Chuāng wài yǒu yī kē dà shù.
There is a big tree outside the window.

## Memory Hook

外 is composed of 夕 (evening) and 卜 (divination): ancient fortune-telling happened outside the walls at dusk, pointing to what is 外.
