## 画 (huà)

画 means to draw or paint as a verb, and a drawing or painting as a noun. It covers any act of making visual art with lines or color.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To draw, to paint.
2. **[noun]** A picture, a painting.

## Example Sentences

**她喜欢画画。**
Tā xǐhuān huà huà.
She likes drawing. (The first 画 is the verb, the second is the noun.)

**他画了一幅山水画。**
Tā huà le yī fú shānshuǐ huà.
He painted a landscape painting.

**孩子们在画动物。**
Háizimen zài huà dòngwù.
The children are drawing animals.

**墙上挂着一幅画。**
Qiáng shàng guà zhe yī fú huà.
A painting is hanging on the wall.

## Memory Hook

The character 画 shows a brush laying down a boundary around a field: every drawing creates borders and shapes.
