## 再 (zài)

An adverb meaning "again" for future or hypothetical repetition. Key contrast with 又 (yòu): 再 is used for actions that will happen again or are being requested, while 又 describes repetition that has already occurred.

## Meanings

1. **[adv]** Again (future repetition), one more time.
2. **[adv]** Then (after something else happens first).
3. **[adv]** More, further (with adjectives or longer).

## Example Sentences

**请再说一遍。**
Qǐng zài shuō yī biàn.
Please say it again.

**你再等一下，我马上来。**
Nǐ zài děng yīxià, wǒ mǎshàng lái.
Wait a moment longer. I will be right there.

**我们先吃饭，再去看电影。**
Wǒmen xiān chīfàn, zài qù kàn diànyǐng.
Let's eat first, then go watch a movie.

**再见！**
Zàijiàn!
Goodbye! (See you again!)

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 再见 | goodbye (see again) |
| 再说一遍 | say it again |
| 再等一下 | wait a bit more |
| 先…再… | first ... then ... |
| 再也不 | never again |

## Memory Hook

再 contains two horizontal lines stacked: think of a second layer, a second time — something coming around again in the future.
