## 机会 (jīhuì)

机会 means opportunity or chance. 机 here carries the sense of "a critical moment" and 会 means "occasion." Together they describe a favorable window of time to act.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Opportunity, chance.

## Example Sentences

**这是一个很好的机会。**
Zhè shì yīgè hěn hǎo de jīhuì.
This is a great opportunity.

**我没有机会去中国。**
Wǒ méiyǒu jīhuì qù Zhōngguó.
I haven't had the chance to go to China.

**希望你抓住这个机会。**
Xīwàng nǐ zhuā zhù zhège jīhuì.
I hope you seize this opportunity.

**有机会我们再见面吧。**
Yǒu jīhuì wǒmen zài jiànmiàn ba.
Let's meet again when there's a chance.

## Memory Hook

机 (critical moment) + 会 (meeting): an opportunity is when a critical moment meets you.
