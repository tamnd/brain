## 船 (chuán)

船 is the general word for any watercraft — a rowboat, ferry, or ocean liner. The measure word is 艘 (sōu) for larger ships, but 条 (tiáo) or simply 一条船 are also common in everyday speech.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Boat; ship; vessel. Any vehicle that travels on water.

## Example Sentences

**我们坐船去那个小岛。**
Wǒmen zuò chuán qù nàgè xiǎodǎo.
We took a boat to that small island.

**河上有很多船。**
Hé shàng yǒu hěn duō chuán.
There are many boats on the river.

**这艘船从上海开往北京。**
Zhè sōu chuán cóng Shànghǎi kāi wǎng Běijīng.
This ship sails from Shanghai toward Beijing.

**坐船旅行比坐飞机慢，但更有意思。**
Zuò chuán lǚxíng bǐ zuò fēijī màn, dàn gèng yǒu yìsi.
Traveling by boat is slower than by plane, but more interesting.

## Memory Hook

The character 船 has 舟 (zhōu, a small boat) on the left — that radical is your anchor for all watercraft characters. Add the right side and you get a full-size 船 that can carry more than a simple 舟.
