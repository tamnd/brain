## 啤酒 (píjiǔ)

啤酒 means beer. 啤 is a phonetic borrowing representing "beer" and 酒 means alcoholic drink. It is the most commonly consumed alcoholic beverage in China.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Beer.

## Example Sentences

**你喜欢喝啤酒吗？**
Nǐ xǐhuān hē píjiǔ ma?
Do you like drinking beer?

**我要一瓶啤酒。**
Wǒ yào yī píng píjiǔ.
I want a bottle of beer.

**天热的时候喝冰啤酒很舒服。**
Tiān rè de shíhou hē bīng píjiǔ hěn shūfu.
Drinking cold beer when it's hot is very comfortable.

**他们边吃饭边喝啤酒。**
Tāmen biān chīfàn biān hē píjiǔ.
They drank beer while eating.

## Memory Hook

啤 is borrowed phonetically from "beer" (pí sounds like "beer"). 酒 (the wine/alcohol radical with three drops of liquid) confirms it is a drink. Beer = pí-jiǔ.
