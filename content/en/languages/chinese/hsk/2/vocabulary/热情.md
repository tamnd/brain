## 热情 (rèqíng)

Describes a person who is warm, friendly, and enthusiastic in dealing with others. As a noun it means passion or enthusiasm. It is often used to praise someone's hospitality or eagerness.

## Meanings

1. **[adj]** Warm, enthusiastic, hospitable.
2. **[noun]** Passion, enthusiasm, warmth.

## Example Sentences

**她对客人很热情。**
Tā duì kèrén hěn rèqíng.
She is very warm toward guests.

**他对工作充满热情。**
Tā duì gōngzuò chōngmǎn rèqíng.
He is full of passion for his work.

**中国人非常热情好客。**
Zhōngguórén fēicháng rèqíng hàokè.
Chinese people are very warm and hospitable.

**谢谢你热情地帮助我。**
Xièxie nǐ rèqíng de bāngzhù wǒ.
Thank you for your enthusiastic help.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 很热情 | very warm, very enthusiastic |
| 热情地 | warmly, enthusiastically (adverb form) |
| 热情好客 | warm and hospitable |
| 充满热情 | full of enthusiasm |
| 对…热情 | enthusiastic about / warm toward ... |

## Memory Hook

热 means hot and 情 means feeling: 热情 is a heart that burns hot with warmth for others.
