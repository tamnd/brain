## 看见 (kàn jiàn)

看见 is a verb + result complement structure. 看 means "to look/watch" and 见 is the complement meaning "perceive/succeed in seeing." Together they mean the act of looking was successful — you actually saw something. Compare 看 (looking, possibly without result) with 看见 (looked and did see). The negative is 没看见 (didn't see / couldn't see).

## Meanings

1. **[v+comp]** See; catch sight of — look and successfully perceive with the eyes.
2. **[v+comp]** Notice; spot — become aware of something through sight.

## Example Sentences

**我看见他了！他在那边。**
Wǒ kàn jiàn tā le! Tā zài nàbiān.
I see him! He's over there.

**你看见我的手机了吗？**
Nǐ kàn jiàn wǒ de shǒujī le ma?
Did you see my phone?

**天太黑了，我什么都看不见。**
Tiān tài hēi le, wǒ shénme dōu kàn bu jiàn.
It's too dark; I can't see anything.

**她一进门就看见了那束花。**
Tā yī jìn mén jiù kàn jiàn le nà shù huā.
As soon as she walked in the door, she saw that bouquet of flowers.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |

| 看得见 | can see; able to see |
| 没看见 | didn't see |
| 看见了 | (have) seen |
| 突然看见 | suddenly caught sight of |

## Memory Hook

看 (look) + 见 (perceive) = the action of looking reached its target. 见 is the green light that says "your eyes found it."
