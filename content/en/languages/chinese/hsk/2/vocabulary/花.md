## 花 (huā)

花 has two core uses: as a noun it means flower, and as a verb it means to spend money or time. Context makes the meaning clear. Both uses are common in everyday speech.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Flower, blossom.
2. **[verb]** To spend (money or time).

## Example Sentences

**桌子上有一束花。**
Zhuōzi shàng yǒu yī shù huā.
There is a bunch of flowers on the table.

**这件衣服花了我很多钱。**
Zhè jiàn yīfu huā le wǒ hěn duō qián.
This piece of clothing cost me a lot of money.

**她喜欢种花。**
Tā xǐhuān zhòng huā.
She likes growing flowers.

**我花了两个小时做作业。**
Wǒ huā le liǎng gè xiǎoshí zuò zuòyè.
I spent two hours doing homework.
