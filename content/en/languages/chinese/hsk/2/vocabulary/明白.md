## 明白 (míngbai)

明白 means to understand or to be clear about something, especially a situation, intention, or meaning. It often captures the moment of suddenly grasping something. Key contrast: 懂 (dǒng) is used for understanding a skill or subject (I understand Chinese grammar), while 明白 is for understanding a specific situation, message, or meaning.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To understand, to realize (a situation or meaning).
2. **[adjective]** Clear, obvious.

## Example Sentences

**你明白我的意思吗？**
Nǐ míngbai wǒ de yìsi ma?
Do you understand what I mean?

**我现在明白了。**
Wǒ xiànzài míngbai le.
I understand now.

**他终于明白了为什么失败。**
Tā zhōngyú míngbai le wèishénme shībài.
He finally understood why he failed.

**请说清楚一点，我不明白。**
Qǐng shuō qīngchǔ yīdiǎn, wǒ bù míngbai.
Please speak more clearly, I don't understand.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 明白了 | understood, got it |

| 听明白 | understood after listening |

| 明白道理 | to understand the reasoning |

## Memory Hook

明 (bright) + 白 (white): when something becomes 明白, the fog clears and everything is bright white with clarity. That "aha" moment.
