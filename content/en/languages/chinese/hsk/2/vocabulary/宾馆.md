## 宾馆 (bīnguǎn)

宾馆 is the standard Mandarin word for a hotel or guesthouse. It is used for mid-range to upscale accommodations. Related words: 酒店 (jiǔdiàn) is also common for hotel, and 旅馆 (lǚguǎn) refers to a smaller, more basic inn.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Hotel; guesthouse. A place that provides lodging for paying guests.

## Example Sentences

**我住在市中心的一家宾馆里。**
Wǒ zhù zài shì zhōngxīn de yī jiā bīnguǎn lǐ.
I am staying at a hotel in the city center.

**这家宾馆的房间很干净。**
Zhè jiā bīnguǎn de fángjiān hěn gānjìng.
The rooms at this hotel are very clean.

**请问附近有宾馆吗？**
Qǐngwèn fùjìn yǒu bīnguǎn ma?
Excuse me, is there a hotel nearby?

**我们在宾馆住了三天。**
Wǒmen zài bīnguǎn zhù le sān tiān.
We stayed at the hotel for three days.

## Memory Hook

宾 means "guest" and 馆 means "building for a specific purpose" (like 图书馆, library). A 宾馆 is literally a "guest building" — exactly what a hotel is.
