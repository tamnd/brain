## 健康 (jiànkāng)

健康 functions both as an adjective (healthy) and a noun (health). As an adjective it modifies people and lifestyles: 你身体很健康 (you are in good health). As a noun it follows 为了 (for the sake of) or 注意 (pay attention to): 注意健康 (take care of your health).

## Meanings

1. **[adj]** Healthy; in good health — physically or mentally well.
2. **[n]** Health — one's state of physical or mental well-being.

## Example Sentences

**你要注意健康。**
Nǐ yào zhùyì jiànkāng.
You should pay attention to your health.

**运动对健康很有好处。**
Yùndòng duì jiànkāng hěn yǒu hǎochù.
Exercise is very beneficial for health.

**他的身体非常健康。**
Tā de shēntǐ fēicháng jiànkāng.
He is in very good health.

**为了健康，她每天早起跑步。**
Wèile jiànkāng, tā měitiān zǎo qǐ pǎobù.
For the sake of her health, she gets up early to run every day.

## Memory Hook

健 contains the radical for "person" next to 建 (build) — a healthy person actively builds up their body every day.
