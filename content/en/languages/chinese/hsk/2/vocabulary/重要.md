## 重要 (zhòngyào)

重要 is an adjective meaning important or significant. It follows the standard adjective pattern: 很重要 (very important), 不重要 (not important), 最重要 (most important). It can also precede a noun as a modifier: 重要的事情 (an important matter).

## Meanings

1. **[adj]** Important; significant — of great value or consequence.
2. **[adj]** Essential; critical — cannot be overlooked or neglected.

## Example Sentences

**健康比金钱更重要。**
Jiànkāng bǐ jīnqián gèng zhòngyào.
Health is more important than money.

**这次考试很重要，要好好准备。**
Zhè cì kǎoshì hěn zhòngyào, yào hǎohāo zhǔnbèi.
This exam is very important; study well for it.

**最重要的事是什么？**
Zuì zhòngyào de shì shì shénme?
What is the most important thing?

**他说了一件重要的事。**
Tā shuō le yī jiàn zhòngyào de shì.
He said something important.

## Memory Hook

重 means "heavy" and 要 means "want/need" — something 重要 is so heavy with necessity that you really need to pay attention to it.
