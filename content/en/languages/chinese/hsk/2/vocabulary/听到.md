## 听到 (tīng dào)

听到 is a verb + result complement. 听 means "to listen" and 到 is a result complement indicating that the action reached its goal — the sound actually arrived and was registered. This is different from 听 alone (which just means the act of listening, without guaranteeing the sound was received). The negative is 没听到 (didn't hear) or 听不到 (can't hear).

## Meanings

1. **[v+comp]** Hear; catch a sound — listen and successfully register the sound.
2. **[v+comp]** Come to hear; learn by hearing — receive information through the ears.

## Example Sentences

**我听到有人敲门。**
Wǒ tīng dào yǒu rén qiāo mén.
I heard someone knocking on the door.

**你听到老师说的话了吗？**
Nǐ tīng dào lǎoshī shuō de huà le ma?
Did you hear what the teacher said?

**外面太吵，我什么都听不到。**
Wàimiàn tài chǎo, wǒ shénme dōu tīng bu dào.
It's too noisy outside; I can't hear anything.

**他听到这个消息，高兴极了。**
Tā tīng dào zhège xiāoxi, gāoxìng jí le.
When he heard the news, he was extremely happy.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 听不到 | cannot hear; unable to hear |
| 听得到 | can hear; able to hear |
| 没听到 | didn't hear |
| 听到消息 | hear the news |
| 刚听到 | just heard |

## Memory Hook

听 (listen) + 到 (arrive/reach) = the sound traveled all the way and arrived at your ears. 到 confirms delivery.
