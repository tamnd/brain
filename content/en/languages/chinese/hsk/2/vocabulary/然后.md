## 然后 (rán hòu)

A conjunction used to connect two actions or events in sequence, meaning "then" or "after that." It always follows the first completed action and introduces what comes next.

## Meanings

1. **[conj]** Then, after that (indicating sequence of events or steps).

## Example Sentences

**我先吃早饭，然后去上班。**
Wǒ xiān chī zǎofàn, rán hòu qù shàngbān.
I eat breakfast first, then go to work.

**先洗手，然后再吃东西。**
Xiān xǐ shǒu, rán hòu zài chī dōngxi.
Wash your hands first, then eat.

**她学了一年汉语，然后去了中国。**
Tā xué le yī nián Hànyǔ, rán hòu qù le Zhōngguó.
She studied Chinese for a year, then went to China.

**我们先看电影，然后去吃饭，好吗？**
Wǒmen xiān kàn diànyǐng, rán hòu qù chīfàn, hǎo ma?
We watch a movie first, then go eat. Does that work?

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 先…然后… | first ... then ... |
| 然后再… | and then (do something else) |
| 然后呢？ | And then? What next? |
| 然后就… | and then right away ... |
| 然后去… | then go to ... |

## Memory Hook

然 means "like that / so," and 后 means "after." Put them together: "after that" — things follow in order.
