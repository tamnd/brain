## 段 (duàn)

段 is a measure word for sections or segments cut from a longer whole. It counts stretches of road (一段路), paragraphs of text (一段话 / 一段文章), periods of time (一段时间), and segments of things like rope or pipe. The key idea is a delimited portion cut from something continuous.

## Meanings

1. **[mw]** Section; segment — a portion of something continuous (road, text, rope, timeline).
2. **[mw]** Paragraph; passage — used for a block of text or spoken content.

## Example Sentences

**请读第一段。**
Qǐng dú dì yī duàn.
Please read the first paragraph.

**我们已经走了很长一段路了。**
Wǒmen yǐjīng zǒu le hěn cháng yī duàn lù le.
We have already walked a very long stretch of road.

**他们在一起生活了一段时间。**
Tāmen zài yīqǐ shēnghuó le yī duàn shíjiān.
They lived together for a period of time.

**这段话写得很好，我很喜欢。**
Zhè duàn huà xiě de hěn hǎo, wǒ hěn xǐhuan.
This passage is written very well; I like it a lot.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 一段路 | a stretch of road |
| 一段时间 | a period of time |
| 一段话 | a passage of text; a paragraph |
| 一段视频 | a video clip / a segment of video |
| 第一段 | the first paragraph / section |

## Memory Hook

段 means a chunk cut away — imagine a segment of road or text snipped off from a longer whole, like a slice from a long baguette.
