## 旁边 (pángbiān)

旁边 means beside or next to. It is a location word (localizer) placed after a noun to indicate the space immediately adjacent to something. Structure: Noun + 旁边.

## Meanings

1. **[noun / localizer]** The side, beside, next to.

## Example Sentences

**银行旁边有一家咖啡店。**
Yínháng pángbiān yǒu yī jiā kāfēi diàn.
There is a café next to the bank.

**请坐在我旁边。**
Qǐng zuò zài wǒ pángbiān.
Please sit beside me.

**书包在椅子旁边。**
Shūbāo zài yǐzi pángbiān.
The backpack is next to the chair.

**他站在门旁边等我。**
Tā zhàn zài mén pángbiān děng wǒ.
He stood beside the door waiting for me.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 在…旁边 | next to... |
| 旁边的人 | the person next to (you/me) |
| 站在旁边 | to stand beside |
| 坐在旁边 | to sit beside |
| 学校旁边 | next to the school |

## Memory Hook

旁 contains 方 (direction/side) twice with a roof: space on all sides. 边 (side/edge) narrows it to one side right next to you.
