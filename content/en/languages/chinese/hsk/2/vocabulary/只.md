## 只 (zhǐ)

只 narrows a statement to a single item, amount, or condition. It always sits before the verb or the element it restricts. Note: 只 is also a measure word for small animals (yī zhī māo — one cat), but that is a separate word with a different tone (zhī) and usage.

## Meanings

1. **[adv]** Only; merely; just — limits quantity or scope.
2. **[adv]** Used in patterns 只有…才… (only if…then…) and 只要…就… (as long as…then…).

## Example Sentences

**我只有一个问题。**
Wǒ zhǐ yǒu yī gè wèntí.
I only have one question.

**只有努力，才能成功。**
Zhǐyǒu nǔlì, cái néng chénggōng.
Only through hard work can one succeed.

**只要你来，我就高兴。**
Zhǐyào nǐ lái, wǒ jiù gāoxìng.
As long as you come, I'll be happy.

**他只吃蔬菜，不吃肉。**
Tā zhǐ chī shūcài, bù chī ròu.
He only eats vegetables; he doesn't eat meat.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 只有…才… | only if … then … (conditional) |
| 只要…就… | as long as … then … |
| 只是 | it's just that; merely |
| 只好 | have no choice but to |
| 只剩 | only … left / remaining |

## Memory Hook

Think of 只 as a spotlight: it shines on one thing and leaves everything else in the dark.
