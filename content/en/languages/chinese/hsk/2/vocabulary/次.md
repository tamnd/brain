## 次 (cì)

次 is the measure word for occurrences or instances of an action — equivalent to "time" as in "three times." It differs from 遍 (biàn), which emphasizes completing the full course of an action, while 次 simply counts how many times something happened.

## Meanings

1. **[measure word]** Time; occurrence. Counts how many times an action takes place.
2. **[noun/adjective]** Order; sequence; second (in rank). Used in compounds like 次序 (order) and 其次 (secondly).

## Example Sentences

**我去过北京两次。**
Wǒ qùguò Běijīng liǎng cì.
I have been to Beijing twice.

**请再说一次。**
Qǐng zài shuō yī cì.
Please say it one more time.

**这部电影我看了三次。**
Zhè bù diànyǐng wǒ kàn le sān cì.
I have watched this movie three times.

**每次考试他都考得很好。**
Měi cì kǎoshì tā dōu kǎo de hěn hǎo.
Every time there is an exam, he does very well.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 一次 (yī cì) | once; one time |
| 每次 (měi cì) | every time |
| 这次 (zhè cì) | this time |
| 上次 (shàng cì) | last time |
| 下次 (xià cì) | next time |

## Memory Hook

次 contains 冫 (ice/water) on the left and 欠 (lack/yawn) on the right. Think of counting waves — each wave that washes up is one 次, one occurrence rolling in after another.
