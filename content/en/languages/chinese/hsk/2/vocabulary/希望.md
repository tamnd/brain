## 希望 (xīwàng)

Expresses a desire for something desirable but uncertain, similar to "I hope that..." Key contrast: 希望 is used for things you hope may happen (uncertain outcome), while 想 expresses wanting something or missing someone.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To hope (that something will happen).
2. **[noun]** Hope, aspiration.

## Example Sentences

**我希望你一切都好。**
Wǒ xīwàng nǐ yīqiè dōu hǎo.
I hope everything is going well for you.

**她希望明年去中国留学。**
Tā xīwàng míngnián qù Zhōngguó liúxué.
She hopes to study abroad in China next year.

**只要努力，就有希望。**
Zhǐyào nǔlì, jiù yǒu xīwàng.
As long as you work hard, there is hope.

**他希望这次考试能通过。**
Tā xīwàng zhè cì kǎoshì néng tōngguò.
He hopes he can pass this exam.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 希望…能… | hope that (someone) can ... |
| 有希望 | there is hope |
| 没有希望 | there is no hope |
| 实现希望 | to realize one's hope |
| 充满希望 | full of hope |

## Memory Hook

希 suggests rarity and 望 means to gaze toward: hoping is gazing at something rare and distant, wishing it would come.
