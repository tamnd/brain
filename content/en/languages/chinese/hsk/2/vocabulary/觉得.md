## 觉得 (juéde)

觉得 expresses a subjective feeling or personal impression. It is used when your opinion comes from how something feels to you rather than from logic. Key contrast: 认为 (rènwéi) is used for reasoned, formal opinions, while 觉得 is feeling-based and personal.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To feel (physically or emotionally).
2. **[verb]** To think, to find (expressing a personal impression).

## Example Sentences

**我觉得这道菜很好吃。**
Wǒ juéde zhè dào cài hěn hǎochī.
I feel this dish is delicious.

**你觉得这个主意怎么样？**
Nǐ juéde zhège zhǔyì zěnmeyàng?
What do you think of this idea?

**我觉得有点累。**
Wǒ juéde yǒudiǎn lèi.
I feel a little tired.

**她觉得他说的话很有道理。**
Tā juéde tā shuō de huà hěn yǒu dàolǐ.
She feels that what he said makes a lot of sense.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 觉得好 | feel it is good |
| 觉得累 | feel tired |
| 觉得奇怪 | find it strange |
| 你觉得呢 | what do you think? |
| 觉得满意 | feel satisfied |

## Memory Hook

觉 contains 见 (to see/sense): 觉得 is what your senses tell you, a gut-level impression, not a reasoned conclusion.
