## 自己 (zìjǐ)

自己 is a reflexive pronoun meaning "oneself." Unlike English, the same form works for all persons: 我自己 (myself), 你自己 (yourself), 他自己 (himself). It emphasizes that the subject performs the action independently, without help. It can follow the subject for emphasis or replace a pronoun object.

## Meanings

1. **[pron]** Oneself — reflexive, referring back to the subject of the sentence.
2. **[pron]** By oneself; on one's own — stressing independence or personal effort.

## Example Sentences

**我自己来，不需要你帮忙。**
Wǒ zìjǐ lái, bù xūyào nǐ bāngmáng.
I'll do it myself; I don't need your help.

**他自己做了一顿饭。**
Tā zìjǐ zuò le yī dùn fàn.
He cooked a meal by himself.

**你要相信自己。**
Nǐ yào xiāngxìn zìjǐ.
You need to believe in yourself.

**孩子们可以自己穿衣服了。**
Háizimen kěyǐ zìjǐ chuān yīfu le.
The children can dress themselves now.

## Collocations

## Memory Hook

自 means "self" and 己 also means "self" — the word doubles up on "self" to make the reflexive meaning unmistakable.
