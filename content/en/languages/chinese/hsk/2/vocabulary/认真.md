## 认真 (rènzhēn)

认真 describes an attitude of taking something seriously and giving it your full, careful attention. It can modify behavior (认真工作 — work conscientiously) or describe a person (她很认真 — she is very serious/diligent). It is distinct from 努力 (hard work in terms of effort) — 认真 emphasizes carefulness and focus.

## Meanings

1. **[adj]** Serious; earnest — taking something with genuine care and attention.
2. **[adj]** Conscientious; meticulous — thorough and careful in what one does.

## Example Sentences

**她学习很认真。**
Tā xuéxí hěn rènzhēn.
She is very serious about her studies.

**请认真听老师讲课。**
Qǐng rènzhēn tīng lǎoshī jiǎngkè.
Please listen carefully to the teacher's lesson.

**他对工作非常认真。**
Tā duì gōngzuò fēicháng rènzhēn.
He is extremely conscientious about his work.

**认真写作业，不要随便写。**
Rènzhēn xiě zuòyè, bùyào suíbiàn xiě.
Write your homework carefully; don't do it carelessly.

## Memory Hook

认 means "recognize/know" and 真 means "true/real" — being 认真 means you truly recognize what matters and deal with it honestly.
