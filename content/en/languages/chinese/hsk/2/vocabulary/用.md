## 用 (yòng)

As a verb it means to use or to employ something. As a preposition it introduces the instrument or method, similar to "with" or "using." It is one of the most common and versatile words in Mandarin.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To use, to employ.
2. **[prep]** With, by means of, using.

## Example Sentences

**我用筷子吃饭。**
Wǒ yòng kuàizi chīfàn.
I eat with chopsticks.

**你可以用我的手机。**
Nǐ kěyǐ yòng wǒ de shǒujī.
You can use my phone.

**他用中文写了一封信。**
Tā yòng Zhōngwén xiě le yī fēng xìn.
He wrote a letter in Chinese.

**这个方法不好用。**
Zhège fāngfǎ bù hǎo yòng.
This method is not practical/easy to use.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 用筷子 | use chopsticks |

## Memory Hook

用 looks like a bucket with a handle: you pick it up and put it to work — to 用 is to put something to use.
