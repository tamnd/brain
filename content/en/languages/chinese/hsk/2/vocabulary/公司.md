## 公司 (gōngsī)

公司 is the standard word for a company, firm, or corporation. It covers businesses of all sizes, from small startups to large multinationals. The measure word for companies is 家 (jiā): 一家公司 (a company).

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Company; firm; corporation. An organized business entity.

## Example Sentences

**他在一家大公司工作。**
Tā zài yī jiā dà gōngsī gōngzuò.
He works at a large company.

**这家公司有一千多名员工。**
Zhè jiā gōngsī yǒu yīqiān duō míng yuángōng.
This company has more than one thousand employees.

**我想创办自己的公司。**
Wǒ xiǎng chuàngbàn zìjǐ de gōngsī.
I want to start my own company.

**公司明天有一个重要的会议。**
Gōngsī míngtiān yǒu yīgè zhòngyào de huìyì.
The company has an important meeting tomorrow.

## Memory Hook

公 means "public/official" and 司 means "to manage/administer." A 公司 is a publicly organized body that manages a business. Think of it as an officially managed operation.
