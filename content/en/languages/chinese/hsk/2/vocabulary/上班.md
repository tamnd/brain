## 上班 (shàngbān)

A verb-object compound meaning to go to work or to be on duty. The opposite is 下班 (xiàbān), to get off work. It refers to the act of starting or being at one's work shift.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To go to work, to start work, to be at work.

## Example Sentences

**我每天八点上班。**
Wǒ měi tiān bā diǎn shàngbān.
I go to work at eight o'clock every day.

**他今天不舒服，没去上班。**
Tā jīntiān bù shūfu, méi qù shàngbān.
He was not feeling well today and did not go to work.

**你上班的地方远不远？**
Nǐ shàngbān de dìfang yuǎn bu yuǎn?
Is your workplace far away?

**她上班很忙，没时间打电话。**
Tā shàngbān hěn máng, méi shíjiān dǎ diànhuà.
She is busy at work and has no time to call.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 去上班 | to go to work |
| 上班时间 | working hours |
| 上班族 | office workers, working people |
| 上班路上 | on the way to work |
| 不用上班 | not need to go to work |

## Memory Hook

上 means "up/on" and 班 means "class/shift." Clocking on to your shift is going 上班.
