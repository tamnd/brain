## 多么 (duōme)

多么 is an exclamation intensifier used to express strong emotion or admiration. It appears before an adjective in exclamatory sentences, often paired with 啊 at the end. It translates as "how," "what a," or "so very." It is not used in regular questions; use 多 (duō) for those: 你多高？(How tall are you?)

## Meanings

1. **[adverb]** How; what a; so very. Used in exclamatory sentences to intensify the adjective that follows.

## Example Sentences

**多么漂亮的花啊！**
Duōme piàoliang de huā a!
What beautiful flowers!

**这里的风景多么美啊！**
Zhèlǐ de fēngjǐng duōme měi a!
How beautiful the scenery here is!

**多么有趣的故事！**
Duōme yǒuqù de gùshi!
What an interesting story!

**不管有多么困难，他都坚持下去。**
Bùguǎn yǒu duōme kùnnán, tā dōu jiānchí xiàqù.
No matter how difficult it is, he keeps going.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 多么...啊 (duōme...a) | how...! / what a...! |
| 多么好 (duōme hǎo) | how wonderful |
| 多么漂亮 (duōme piàoliang) | how beautiful |
| 不管多么 (bùguǎn duōme) | no matter how |
| 多么高兴 (duōme gāoxìng) | how happy |

## Memory Hook

多 means "many/much" and 么 softens it into a particle. Together they say "so very much" — an emotional burst amplifying whatever adjective follows. Feel the 多 (a lot) powering up the next word.
