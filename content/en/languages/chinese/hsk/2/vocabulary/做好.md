## 做好 (zuò hǎo)

做好 is a verb + result complement. 做 means "to do/make" and 好 here is not a stand-alone adjective but a result complement meaning "well / properly / to a satisfactory state." The combination means to complete something in a thorough, satisfactory way — not just finishing, but finishing well. The negative forms are 没做好 (didn't do it well) and 做不好 (can't do it well).

## Meanings

1. **[v+comp]** Do well; do properly — complete a task to a good or satisfactory standard.
2. **[v+comp]** Get ready; prepare thoroughly — as in 做好准备 (be well prepared).

## Example Sentences

**你要把这件事做好。**
Nǐ yào bǎ zhè jiàn shì zuò hǎo.
You need to do this matter well.

**他没做好工作，老板很不高兴。**
Tā méi zuò hǎo gōngzuò, lǎobǎn hěn bù gāoxìng.
He didn't do his work well; the boss was very unhappy.

**请做好准备，考试快开始了。**
Qǐng zuò hǎo zhǔnbèi, kǎoshì kuài kāishǐ le.
Please get well prepared; the exam is about to start.

**只要努力，你一定能做好。**
Zhǐyào nǔlì, nǐ yīdìng néng zuò hǎo.
As long as you work hard, you will definitely be able to do it well.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 做好准备 | be well prepared; get ready |
| 没做好 | didn't do well |
| 做不好 | can't do well |
| 做得好 | done well (complement of degree) |
| 做好了 | (have) done it well / it's done |

## Memory Hook

做 (do/make) + 好 (good) = the work came out good. 好 as a result complement is your quality stamp of approval at the end.
