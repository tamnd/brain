## 总是 (zǒngshì)

总是 describes an action that happens every time or is characteristic of someone. It refers to a repeated habit or a defining trait, not necessarily a continuous state. This sets it apart from 一直 (yīzhí), which means "continuously" or "the whole time." Place 总是 before the verb.

## Meanings

1. **[adv]** Always; invariably — happens every time, a consistent habit.
2. **[adv]** Keeps doing; perpetually — a repeated tendency (sometimes with mild complaint).

## Example Sentences

**他总是迟到。**
Tā zǒngshì chídào.
He is always late.

**我妈妈总是担心我。**
Wǒ māma zǒngshì dānxīn wǒ.
My mother is always worrying about me.

**这家餐厅总是很忙。**
Zhè jiā cāntīng zǒngshì hěn máng.
This restaurant is always busy.

**你总是这样说，但从来不做。**
Nǐ zǒngshì zhèyàng shuō, dàn cónglái bù zuò.
You always say that but never do it.

## Memory Hook

总 means "always, in all cases" — imagine a judge who 总是 (always) delivers the same verdict no matter what.
