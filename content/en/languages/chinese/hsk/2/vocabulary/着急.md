## 着急 (zháojí)

Describes a state of anxiety, worry, or urgency. Used when someone is nervous about a situation or feels pressed for time. Common in the phrase 别着急 (don't worry / take it easy).

## Meanings

1. **[adj/verb]** To be anxious, worried, or in a hurry; to feel urgent concern.

## Example Sentences

**别着急，还有时间。**
Bié zháojí, hái yǒu shíjiān.
Don't worry. There is still time.

**他很着急，一直看表。**
Tā hěn zháojí, yīzhí kàn biǎo.
He is very anxious and keeps looking at his watch.

**妈妈着急地等着儿子回家。**
Māma zháojí de děng zhe érzi huí jiā.
The mother waited anxiously for her son to come home.

**你不用着急，慢慢来。**
Nǐ bùyòng zháojí, màn man lái.
No need to rush. Take it slow.

## Memory Hook

着 indicates a state and 急 means urgent: being caught in an urgent state, that tense feeling is 着急.
