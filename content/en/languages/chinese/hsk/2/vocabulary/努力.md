## 努力 (nǔlì)

努力 is a versatile word used as a verb (to work hard), adjective (diligent), or noun (effort). As a verb it often precedes another verb to show the manner of action: 努力学习 (study hard). As a noun it appears after 付出 (put in effort) or 经过 (through effort).

## Meanings

1. **[v/adj]** Work hard; be diligent — exert great effort toward a goal.
2. **[n]** Effort; hard work — the energy or dedication put into something.

## Example Sentences

**你要努力学习汉语。**
Nǐ yào nǔlì xuéxí Hànyǔ.
You need to study Chinese hard.

**只要努力，就一定能成功。**
Zhǐyào nǔlì, jiù yīdìng néng chénggōng.
As long as you work hard, you will definitely succeed.

**她很努力，每天都工作到很晚。**
Tā hěn nǔlì, měitiān dōu gōngzuò dào hěn wǎn.
She is very hardworking; she works late every day.

**经过多年努力，他终于实现了梦想。**
Jīngguò duō nián nǔlì, tā zhōngyú shíxiàn le mèngxiǎng.
After years of hard work, he finally realized his dream.

努 contains 女 (woman) and 力 means "strength" — picture someone pushing forward with all their strength to achieve a goal.
