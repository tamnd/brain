## 帮助 (bāngzhù)

帮助 means to help or assist. It functions both as a verb and as a noun, so you can "give 帮助" or "帮助 someone do something."

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To help; to assist. Often followed by a person object and then a verb phrase.
2. **[noun]** Help; assistance. Can be the object of verbs like 给 (to give) or 需要 (to need).

## Example Sentences

**谢谢你的帮助。**
Xièxie nǐ de bāngzhù.
Thank you for your help.

**我可以帮助你学中文。**
Wǒ kěyǐ bāngzhù nǐ xué Zhōngwén.
I can help you learn Chinese.

**她帮助了很多人。**
Tā bāngzhù le hěn duō rén.
She has helped many people.

**如果你需要帮助，请告诉我。**
Rúguǒ nǐ xūyào bāngzhù, qǐng gàosu wǒ.
If you need help, please let me know.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 需要帮助 (xūyào bāngzhù) | to need help |
| 提供帮助 (tígōng bāngzhù) | to provide assistance |
| 互相帮助 (hùxiāng bāngzhù) | to help each other |
| 帮助别人 (bāngzhù biérén) | to help others |
| 请求帮助 (qǐngqiú bāngzhù) | to ask for help |

## Memory Hook

帮 sounds like "bong" — imagine someone banging a drum to rally people together to help. 助 means to assist, reinforcing the idea of lending a hand.
