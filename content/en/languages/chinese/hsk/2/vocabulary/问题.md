## 问题 (wèntí)

Covers both a question someone asks and a problem or issue that needs to be addressed. In everyday speech it is one of the most versatile nouns, used in education, work, and daily conversation.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Question (something asked).
2. **[noun]** Problem, issue, matter needing attention.

## Example Sentences

**你有什么问题吗？**
Nǐ yǒu shénme wèntí ma?
Do you have any questions?

**这个问题很难回答。**
Zhège wèntí hěn nán huídá.
This question is very hard to answer.

**这台电脑有问题。**
Zhè tái diànnǎo yǒu wèntí.
There is a problem with this computer.

**没问题，我来帮你。**
Méi wèntí, wǒ lái bāng nǐ.
No problem. I will help you.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 有问题 | to have a question/problem |
| 没问题 | no problem |
| 回答问题 | to answer a question |
| 解决问题 | to solve a problem |
| 出问题 | to go wrong, to have an issue |

## Memory Hook

问 means to ask and 题 means question/topic: combined, 问题 is "the thing you ask about" — a question or problem.
