## 谢谢 (xièxiè)

谢谢 means "thank you." It is the standard, all-purpose expression of gratitude in Mandarin and is used in virtually all social contexts.

## Meanings

1. **[phrase]** Thank you; thanks (expressing gratitude for help, a gift, or a kind action).

## Example Sentences

**谢谢你帮我。**
Xièxiè nǐ bāng wǒ.
Thank you for helping me.

**A: 给你。 B: 谢谢！**
A: Gěi nǐ. B: Xièxiè!
A: Here you go. B: Thank you!

**非常感谢您的帮助。**
Fēicháng gǎnxiè nín de bāngzhù.
Thank you very much for your help. (formal)

**谢谢老师！**
Xièxiè lǎoshī!
Thank you, teacher!

## Usage Notes

谢谢 is followed by the person's name or pronoun to specify who you are thanking: 谢谢你 (thank you, to one person), 谢谢您 (thank you, formal). You can also follow it with a verb phrase: 谢谢你来 (thank you for coming). The standard responses are 不客气 (bù kèqi, you're welcome — lit. no need to be polite) and 没关系 (méi guānxi, it's nothing — used more when someone apologizes, but also acceptable here). For stronger thanks, use 非常感谢 (fēicháng gǎnxiè). Note: 不客气 responds to thanks; 没关系 responds more naturally to apologies.

## Collocations or Common Phrases

| Phrase | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| 谢谢你 | thank you (to one person) |
| 谢谢您 | thank you (formal) |

| 多谢 | many thanks (casual) |
| 谢谢帮忙 | thank you for the help |

## Memory Hook

谢谢 repeats itself — doubling 谢 (to thank) makes the thanks twice as warm and sincere.
