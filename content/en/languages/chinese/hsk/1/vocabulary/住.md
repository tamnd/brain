## 住 (zhù)

住 means to live at a place or to stay somewhere. It is used to state where someone lives and also appears as a verb complement meaning "firmly" or "in place" (e.g., 记住 remember firmly, 停住 stop in place).

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To live, to reside (at a location).
2. **[verb complement]** Firmly, in place (in compounds like 记住, 停住).

## Example Sentences

**你住在哪里？**
Nǐ zhù zài nǎlǐ?
Where do you live?

**我住在上海已经三年了。**
Wǒ zhù zài Shànghǎi yǐjīng sān nián le.
I have been living in Shanghai for three years.

**他在酒店住了一个星期。**
Tā zài jiǔdiàn zhù le yī gè xīngqī.
He stayed at the hotel for one week.

**请记住这个地址。**
Qǐng jì zhù zhège dìzhǐ.
Please remember this address firmly.

## Memory Hook

住 has the person radical (亻) on the left and a master/candle (主) on the right. A person standing at their master's place is a person who lives there.
