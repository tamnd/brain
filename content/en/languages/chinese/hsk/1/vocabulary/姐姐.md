## 姐姐 (jiějiě)

姐姐 means older sister. Like 哥哥, it specifically refers to a sister who is older than the speaker.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Older sister, elder sister.

## Example Sentences

**我姐姐在上海读书。**
Wǒ jiějiě zài Shànghǎi dúshū.
My older sister is studying in Shanghai.

**姐姐比我大三岁。**
Jiějiě bǐ wǒ dà sān suì.
My older sister is three years older than me.

**你的姐姐漂亮吗？**
Nǐ de jiějiě piàoliang ma?
Is your older sister beautiful?

**姐姐每天都打电话给我。**
Jiějiě měitiān dōu dǎ diànhuà gěi wǒ.
My older sister calls me every day.

## Memory Hook

姐 has the woman radical (女) plus 且 (moreover). An older sister is a woman who is, moreover, looked up to.
