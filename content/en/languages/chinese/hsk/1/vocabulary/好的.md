## 好的 (hǎo de)

好的 means "okay," "all right," or "sure." It is a warm, affirmative response used to confirm agreement, accept a request, or acknowledge information.

## Meanings

1. **[phrase]** Okay; all right; sure; got it (affirmative acknowledgment or agreement).

## Example Sentences

**A: 明天八点见面，好吗？ B: 好的！**
A: Míngtiān bā diǎn jiànmiàn, hǎo ma? B: Hǎo de!
A: Let's meet at eight tomorrow, okay? B: Sure!

**A: 你能帮我一下吗？ B: 好的，没问题。**
A: Nǐ néng bāng wǒ yīxià ma? B: Hǎo de, méi wèntí.
A: Can you help me? B: Okay, no problem.

**老师，我知道了，好的。**
Lǎoshī, wǒ zhīdào le, hǎo de.
Teacher, I understand, okay.

**好的，我马上来。**
Hǎo de, wǒ mǎshàng lái.
Okay, I'll be right there.

## Usage Notes

好的 is the most common way to say "okay" in response to a request, instruction, or suggestion. It is slightly warmer and more complete than a plain 好 (which can sound abrupt). The particle 的 here softens the response and confirms that the speaker has taken in the information. Compare with 是的 (shì de, yes / that's right), which confirms a factual statement rather than expressing willingness. In texting and chat, 好的 is extremely common, often written as 好哒 or 好 in very casual contexts.

## Collocations or Common Phrases

| Phrase | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| 好的，没问题 | okay, no problem |
| 好的，知道了 | okay, got it |
| 好的，马上来 | okay, coming right away |
| 好，好的 | okay, okay (agreement) |
| 是的 (compare) | yes, that's correct |

## Memory Hook

好 (good) + 的 (particle) — adding 的 turns a simple "good" into a complete, friendly "okay, got it."
