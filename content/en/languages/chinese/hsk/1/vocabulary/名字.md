## 名字 (míngzi)

A noun meaning name. It refers to a person's full name or given name. The most important phrase built around this word is 你叫什么名字 (what is your name), one of the first questions in any Chinese conversation.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Name (personal name, full name, or nickname).

## Example Sentences

**你叫什么名字？**
Nǐ jiào shénme míngzi?
What is your name?

**我的名字叫李明。**
Wǒ de míngzi jiào Lǐ Míng.
My name is Li Ming.

**这个名字的意思是什么？**
Zhège míngzi de yìsi shì shénme?
What does this name mean?

**请写下你的名字。**
Qǐng xiě xia nǐ de míngzi.
Please write down your name.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 叫什么名字 (jiào shénme míngzi) | called what name (what is your name) |
| 中文名字 (Zhōngwén míngzi) | Chinese name |
| 英文名字 (Yīngwén míngzi) | English name |
| 起名字 (qǐ míngzi) | give a name, choose a name |
| 名字的意思 (míngzi de yìsi) | the meaning of a name |

## Memory Hook

名 means "name/fame" and 字 means "character/word." Your name is the word that identifies you. The key phrase 你叫什么名字 is essential for introductions.
