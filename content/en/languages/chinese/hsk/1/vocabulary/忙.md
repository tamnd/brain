## 忙 (máng)

An adjective describing a state of being occupied with work or tasks, leaving little free time.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** Busy, occupied with work or activities.
2. **[verb]** To busy oneself with something; to rush around doing things.

## Example Sentences

**我今天很忙，没有时间吃饭。**
Wǒ jīntiān hěn máng, méiyǒu shíjiān chīfàn.
I am very busy today and have no time to eat.

**你最近忙吗？**
Nǐ zuìjìn máng ma?
Have you been busy lately?

**他忙着工作，忘了给我打电话。**
Tā mángzhe gōngzuò, wàngle gěi wǒ dǎ diànhuà.
He was busy with work and forgot to call me.

**妈妈每天都很忙。**
Māma měitiān dōu hěn máng.
Mom is very busy every day.

## Memory Hook

Think of someone running in all directions at once, arms full, no moment to stop. That frantic energy is 忙.
