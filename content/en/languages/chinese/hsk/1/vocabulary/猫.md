## 猫 (māo)

A cat. 猫 is the standard word for a domestic cat in Chinese. The pronunciation "māo" is thought to be onomatopoeic, mimicking the sound of a cat's meow.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Cat. The common domestic feline animal kept as a pet.

## Example Sentences

**我家有一只猫。**
Wǒ jiā yǒu yī zhī māo.
My family has a cat.

**这只猫很可爱。**
Zhè zhī māo hěn kě'ài.
This cat is very cute.

**猫喜欢吃鱼。**
Māo xǐhuān chī yú.
Cats like to eat fish.

**她的猫是白色的。**
Tā de māo shì báisè de.
Her cat is white.

## Memory Hook

The word 猫 sounds like "meow" said with a flat, steady tone. Say "māo" aloud and you are basically calling a cat. The pronunciation is one of the most intuitive sound connections in Chinese vocabulary.
