## 同学 (tóngxué)

A noun meaning classmate or schoolmate. It refers to someone who studies at the same school or in the same class. It can also be used as a polite way to address a student you do not know.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Classmate, schoolmate, fellow student.
2. **[address form]** Used to politely address a student you do not know.

## Example Sentences

**她是我的同学，我们在同一个班。**
Tā shì wǒ de tóngxué, wǒmen zài tóng yīgè bān.
She is my classmate, we are in the same class.

**同学们，请打开课本第五页。**
Tóngxuémen, qǐng dǎkāi kèběn dì wǔ yè.
Classmates, please open your textbooks to page five.

**我的小学同学现在住在北京。**
Wǒ de xiǎoxué tóngxué xiànzài zhù zài Běijīng.
My primary school classmate now lives in Beijing.

**同学，请问图书馆怎么走？**
Tóngxué, qǐngwèn túshūguǎn zěnme zǒu?
Excuse me (student), how do I get to the library?

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 老同学 (lǎo tóngxué) | old classmate |
| 小学同学 (xiǎoxué tóngxué) | primary school classmate |
| 大学同学 (dàxué tóngxué) | university classmate |
| 同学聚会 (tóngxué jùhuì) | class reunion |
| 同学们 (tóngxuémen) | classmates (plural address) |

## Memory Hook

同 means "same" and 学 means "study." A 同学 is someone who studies the same things as you, in the same place. Shared study makes classmates.
