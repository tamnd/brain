## 超市 (chāoshì)

A supermarket, a large self-service store selling food, household goods, and other everyday items. 超市 is short for 超级市场 (chāojí shìchǎng).

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Supermarket. A large retail store stocking a wide variety of goods including food and household products.

## Example Sentences

**我每周去超市买东西。**
Wǒ měi zhōu qù chāoshì mǎi dōngxi.
I go to the supermarket every week to buy things.

**超市里有苹果吗？**
Chāoshì lǐ yǒu píngguǒ ma?
Does the supermarket have apples?

**这家超市很大。**
Zhè jiā chāoshì hěn dà.
This supermarket is very large.

**超市几点关门？**
Chāoshì jǐ diǎn guānmén?
What time does the supermarket close?

## Memory Hook

超 means "super" or "exceed," and 市 means "market." Together they make "super market." The abbreviation perfectly mirrors the English word, making it easy to remember.
