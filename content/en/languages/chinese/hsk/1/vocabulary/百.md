## 百 (bǎi)

The word for one hundred; in compound numbers, 一 before 百 is often dropped in colloquial speech (一百 → 百, though 一百 is always correct). Gaps between non-consecutive digits require 零: 一百零五 = 105.

## Meanings

1. **[number]** Hundred, one hundred.
2. **[adjective]** All kinds of, a great many (in idioms: 百分之百, one hundred percent; 百姓, common people).

## Example Sentences

**这本书一百二十页。**
Zhè běn shū yī bǎi èrshí yè.
This book has one hundred and twenty pages.

**这件衣服两百块钱。**
Zhè jiàn yīfu liǎng bǎi kuài qián.
This piece of clothing costs two hundred yuan.

**他一百分满分考了九十八分。**
Tā yī bǎi fēn mǎnfēn kǎole jiǔshíbā fēn.
He scored 98 out of a perfect 100 marks.

**一百零五号房间在哪里？**
Yī bǎi líng wǔ hào fángjiān zài nǎlǐ?
Where is room 105?

## Memory Hook

百 has 白 (white, bái) inside it plus a horizontal stroke on top. White is the blank canvas — and one hundred is the full score, the blank that gets completely filled in.
