## 米饭 (mǐfàn)

Cooked rice, the staple food of Chinese cuisine. 米 refers to uncooked rice grains, and 饭 means cooked food or a meal. Together they specifically mean steamed or boiled rice.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Cooked rice, steamed rice. The prepared rice served as the main staple of a Chinese meal.

## Example Sentences

**我想吃米饭。**
Wǒ xiǎng chī mǐfàn.
I want to eat rice.

**你吃米饭还是面条？**
Nǐ chī mǐfàn háishì miàntiáo?
Do you eat rice or noodles?

**这里的米饭很好吃。**
Zhèlǐ de mǐfàn hěn hǎochī.
The rice here is very delicious.

**一碗米饭多少钱？**
Yī wǎn mǐfàn duōshao qián?
How much is a bowl of rice?

## Memory Hook

米 (raw rice grains) + 饭 (cooked meal) = cooked rice. The distinction between 米 (raw) and 米饭 (cooked) is important in Chinese. Think of 饭 as the transformation step that turns raw grain into a proper meal.
