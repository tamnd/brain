## 他 (tā)

The third-person singular pronoun for males (and mixed-gender groups in informal writing); 他, 她, and 它 are all pronounced tā but written differently to distinguish gender.

## Meanings

1. **[pronoun]** He (subject).
2. **[pronoun]** Him (object).
3. **[pronoun]** His (before 的: 他的).

## Example Sentences

**他是我的老师。**
Tā shì wǒ de lǎoshī.
He is my teacher.

**我认识他。**
Wǒ rènshi tā.
I know him.

**他的名字叫大卫。**
Tā de míngzi jiào Dàwèi.
His name is David.

**他今天不来了。**
Tā jīntiān bù lái le.
He is not coming today.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 他的 (tā de) | his |
| 他们 (tāmen) | they, them (masculine/mixed) |
| 他是 (tā shì) | he is |
| 他也 (tā yě) | he also |
| 他说 (tā shuō) | he says |
| 告诉他 (gàosu tā) | tell him |

## Memory Hook

他 has the "person" radical 亻on the left, plus 也 (also) on the right — he is also a person, just the one you are talking about.
