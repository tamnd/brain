## 没关系 (méi guānxi)

没关系 means "it's okay," "no problem," or "never mind." It is the standard reassuring response when someone apologizes to you.

## Meanings

1. **[phrase]** It's okay; no problem; don't worry about it; never mind (forgiving or reassuring the other person).

## Example Sentences

**A: 对不起！ B: 没关系！**
A: Duìbuqǐ! B: Méi guānxi!
A: Sorry! B: It's okay!

**没关系，我不介意。**
Méi guānxi, wǒ bù jièyì.
It's fine, I don't mind.

**你迟到了？没关系，我刚到。**
Nǐ chídào le? Méi guānxi, wǒ gāng dào.
You're late? No problem, I just got here.

**没关系，下次注意就好了。**
Méi guānxi, xià cì zhùyì jiù hǎo le.
It's okay, just be careful next time.

## Usage Notes

没关系 is primarily used as a response to 对不起 (sorry). It literally means "there is no relationship (between this incident and my being upset)" — that is, the incident has no bearing, no harm done. It can also be used proactively to reassure someone who seems worried or apologetic. Do not confuse its primary role: 没关系 goes after an apology, while 不客气 goes after a thank-you. In very casual speech, 没事 (méi shì, no big deal) is a shorter synonym.

## Collocations or Common Phrases

| Phrase | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| 没关系，没事 | it's fine, no big deal |
| 真的没关系 | it is truly fine |
| 没关系，下次 | no problem, next time |
| 没事 (synonym) | no problem; it's nothing |
| 不要紧 (synonym) | it doesn't matter |

## Memory Hook

没关系 = no (没) + relationship/connection (关系) — this incident has no connection to any problem between us, so there is nothing to worry about.
