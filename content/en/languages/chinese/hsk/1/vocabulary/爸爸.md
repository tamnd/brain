## 爸爸 (bàba)

爸爸 is the informal, affectionate word for father, equivalent to "dad" or "papa" in English. It is used in everyday speech by children and adults alike.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Dad, father (informal).

## Example Sentences

**我爸爸是老师。**
Wǒ bàba shì lǎoshī.
My dad is a teacher.

**爸爸，你在哪里？**
Bàba, nǐ zài nǎlǐ?
Dad, where are you?

**我的爸爸很高。**
Wǒ de bàba hěn gāo.
My father is very tall.

**爸爸每天早上开车上班。**
Bàba měitiān zǎoshang kāichē shàngbān.
Dad drives to work every morning.

## Memory Hook

爸 is repeated twice, mimicking the sound a baby makes: "bà bà." The doubled syllable reflects the natural way children call out to their father.
