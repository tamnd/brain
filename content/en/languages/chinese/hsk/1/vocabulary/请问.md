## 请问 (qǐngwèn)

请问 means "excuse me" or "may I ask." It is a polite phrase used at the beginning of a question, especially when addressing a stranger or in a formal context.

## Meanings

1. **[phrase]** Excuse me; may I ask; pardon me for asking (polite opener before a question).

## Example Sentences

**请问，洗手间在哪里？**
Qǐngwèn, xǐshǒujiān zài nǎlǐ?
Excuse me, where is the restroom?

**请问，你叫什么名字？**
Qǐngwèn, nǐ jiào shénme míngzì?
May I ask your name?

**请问，去火车站怎么走？**
Qǐngwèn, qù huǒchēzhàn zěnme zǒu?
Excuse me, how do I get to the train station?

**请问，这里是售票处吗？**
Qǐngwèn, zhèlǐ shì shòupiào chù ma?
May I ask, is this the ticket office?

## Usage Notes

请问 is composed of 请 (please; politely request) + 问 (to ask). It always appears at the start of a sentence and signals that what follows is a genuine, polite inquiry. It is especially useful when approaching a stranger. Unlike English "excuse me," which also draws attention or apologizes for an interruption, 请问 is specifically tied to asking a question — it is not used to get past someone physically (for that, use 借过 or 不好意思). It raises the politeness level of any question significantly.

## Collocations or Common Phrases

| Phrase | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| 请问，您贵姓？ | may I ask your surname? |
| 请问怎么走？ | excuse me, how do I get there? |
| 请问一下 | may I ask something? |
| 请问，现在几点？ | excuse me, what time is it now? |
| 请问有没有…… | excuse me, do you have...? |

## Memory Hook

请 (please) + 问 (ask) — you are politely requesting permission to ask before you even ask the question.
