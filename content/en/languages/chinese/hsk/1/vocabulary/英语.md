## 英语 (Yīngyǔ)

The English language. 英 is the phonetic approximation of "Eng-" (from "England") and also conveniently means "outstanding" or "hero" in Chinese. 语 means "language."

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** English language. The language originating from England, widely used internationally.

## Example Sentences

**你会说英语吗？**
Nǐ huì shuō Yīngyǔ ma?
Can you speak English?

**我在学英语。**
Wǒ zài xué Yīngyǔ.
I am studying English.

**他的英语说得很好。**
Tā de Yīngyǔ shuō de hěn hǎo.
He speaks English very well.

**英语和汉语都很有用。**
Yīngyǔ hé Hànyǔ dōu hěn yǒuyòng.
Both English and Chinese are very useful.

## Memory Hook

英 sounds like "Eng" from "England," and 语 means "language." So 英语 is the language of England. The pattern holds for other languages too: 法语 (French), 德语 (German), 日语 (Japanese) — all use 语 as the suffix.
