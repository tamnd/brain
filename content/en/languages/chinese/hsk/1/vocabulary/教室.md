## 教室 (jiàoshì)

A noun meaning classroom. It combines 教 (teach) and 室 (room). It specifically refers to the room where teaching and learning take place, as opposed to other rooms in a school building.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Classroom, a room for teaching.

## Example Sentences

**同学们都在教室里学习。**
Tóngxuémen dōu zài jiàoshì lǐ xuéxí.
All the classmates are studying in the classroom.

**我们的教室在三楼。**
Wǒmen de jiàoshì zài sān lóu.
Our classroom is on the third floor.

**教室里有一块大黑板。**
Jiàoshì lǐ yǒu yī kuài dà hēibǎn.
There is a large blackboard in the classroom.

**放学后，教室里只剩下一个学生。**
Fàngxué hòu, jiàoshì lǐ zhǐ shèng xià yīgè xuésheng.
After school, only one student remained in the classroom.

## Memory Hook

教 means "teach" and 室 means "room." A classroom is the teaching room. Compare with 卧室 (wòshì, bedroom: sleep-room) and 客厅 (kètīng, living room): Chinese often builds room names by describing what happens in them.
