## 人 (rén)

A fundamental noun meaning person or people. It is one of the most common characters in Chinese and forms the base of countless compound words. The character itself depicts a person in profile, with two strokes representing legs.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** A person, a human being.
2. **[noun]** People in general (no plural marker needed in Chinese).

## Example Sentences

**那个人是谁？**
Nàge rén shì shéi?
Who is that person?

**这里有很多人。**
Zhèlǐ yǒu hěn duō rén.
There are many people here.

**他是个好人。**
Tā shì gè hǎo rén.
He is a good person.

**中国有很多人。**
Zhōngguó yǒu hěn duō rén.
China has a lot of people.

## Memory Hook

The character 人 looks like a person taking a step forward, two legs spread slightly apart. It is one of the simplest characters and forms the root of dozens of words involving people.
