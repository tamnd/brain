## 大 (dà)

An adjective meaning "big," "large," or "great" in size, age, or degree. It is one of the first adjectives learners encounter and appears in countless compounds. Its antonym is 小 (xiǎo), meaning "small."

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** Big, large (in size or scale).
2. **[adjective]** Old, grown (used for age, especially of people).

## Example Sentences

**这是一个大城市。**
Zhè shì yīgè dà chéngshì.
This is a big city.

**我的包很大。**
Wǒ de bāo hěn dà.
My bag is very large.

**他比我大两岁。**
Tā bǐ wǒ dà liǎng suì.
He is two years older than me.

**大家好！**
Dàjiā hǎo!
Hello everyone! (lit. big family hello)

## Memory Hook

Antonym of 小 (xiǎo, small). The character 大 shows a person (人) with arms spread wide — stretching out to show something BIG.
