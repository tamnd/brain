## 怎么样 (zěnmeyàng)

An interrogative used to ask about the condition, quality, or state of something ("how is it?") or to solicit an opinion ("what do you think?"); unlike 怎么, which asks about the manner of an action, 怎么样 asks about a state or seeks feedback.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective/pronoun]** How is it, how are things (asking about condition or state).
2. **[phrase]** What do you think, how about it (seeking an opinion or suggestion).

## Example Sentences

**你今天怎么样？**
Nǐ jīntiān zěnmeyàng?
How are you today?

**这家餐厅怎么样？**
Zhè jiā cāntīng zěnmeyàng?
How is this restaurant?

**我们明天去爬山，怎么样？**
Wǒmen míngtiān qù páshān, zěnmeyàng?
Let's go hiking tomorrow. What do you think?

**你的新工作怎么样？**
Nǐ de xīn gōngzuò zěnmeyàng?
How is your new job?

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 你怎么样 (nǐ zěnmeyàng) | how are you |
| 感觉怎么样 (gǎnjué zěnmeyàng) | how does it feel |
| 怎么样了 (zěnmeyàng le) | what happened to it |
| 效果怎么样 (xiàoguǒ zěnmeyàng) | how is the result |
| 还怎么样 (hái zěnmeyàng) | still, so what about it |
| 样子怎么样 (yàngzi zěnmeyàng) | what does it look like |

## Memory Hook

怎么样 = 怎么 (how) + 样 (appearance/manner). Combine them to ask: "how does it appear, how does it look to you?" It is an invitation to give your impression.
