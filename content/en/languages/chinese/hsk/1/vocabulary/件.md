## 件 (jiàn)

A measure word used for individual items of clothing (typically upper-body garments) and for matters or events. 件 is one of the most important clothing classifiers.

## Meanings

1. **[measure word]** Classifier for garments, especially tops, jackets, and shirts.
2. **[measure word]** Classifier for matters, affairs, or events.

## What 件 counts

件 is used with:

- 衣服 (yīfu): clothing — 一件衣服 (one item of clothing)
- 上衣 (shàngyī): top/jacket — 一件上衣 (one top)
- 毛衣 (máoyī): sweater — 一件毛衣 (one sweater)
- 事 (shì): matter/affair — 一件事 (one matter/thing)
- 行李 (xíngli): luggage — 一件行李 (one piece of luggage)

## Example Sentences

**我想买一件衣服。**
Wǒ xiǎng mǎi yī jiàn yīfu.
I want to buy one item of clothing.

**这件衣服多少钱？**
Zhè jiàn yīfu duōshao qián?
How much does this item of clothing cost?

**他有一件事想告诉你。**
Tā yǒu yī jiàn shì xiǎng gàosu nǐ.
He has something he wants to tell you.

**我带了三件衣服。**
Wǒ dàile sān jiàn yīfu.
I brought three items of clothing.

## Common Learner Mistake

件 applies to upper-body garments and individual items of clothing. For trousers and skirts, use 条 instead. Saying 一件裤子 (pants) is incorrect. The correct form is 一条裤子.
