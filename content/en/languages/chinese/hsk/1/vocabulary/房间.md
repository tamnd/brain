## 房间 (fángjiān)

A noun meaning room, specifically a room inside a building such as a bedroom, hotel room, or living room. It combines 房 (house, building) and 间 (space between, room).

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Room, a space inside a building.

## Example Sentences

**我的房间不大，但很干净。**
Wǒ de fángjiān bù dà, dàn hěn gānjìng.
My room is not big, but it is very clean.

**请打扫一下你的房间。**
Qǐng dǎsǎo yīxià nǐ de fángjiān.
Please clean up your room.

**这家宾馆的房间很舒服。**
Zhè jiā bīnguǎn de fángjiān hěn shūfu.
The rooms at this hotel are very comfortable.

**我想要一个安静的房间。**
Wǒ xiǎng yào yīgè ānjìng de fángjiān.
I would like a quiet room.

## Memory Hook

房 is a building with a roof (the character has a roof radical at the top) and 间 is the space between two walls. A room is the space inside a building, bounded by walls.
