## 明天见 (míngtiān jiàn)

明天见 means "see you tomorrow." It is a specific, warm farewell used when you know you will meet the other person the next day.

## Meanings

1. **[phrase]** See you tomorrow (farewell when parting for the night or day, with a meeting planned for tomorrow).

## Example Sentences

**好的，明天见！**
Hǎo de, míngtiān jiàn!
All right, see you tomorrow!

**A: 我先走了。B: 好，明天见！**
A: Wǒ xiān zǒu le. B: Hǎo, míngtiān jiàn!
A: I am heading off. B: All right, see you tomorrow!

**明天见，记得带作业。**
Míngtiān jiàn, jìde dài zuòyè.
See you tomorrow, remember to bring your homework.

**不早了，明天见吧！**
Bù zǎo le, míngtiān jiàn ba!
It's getting late, see you tomorrow then!

## Usage Notes

明天见 follows the productive pattern: time word + 见. This pattern can be used flexibly to create other farewells. 周一见 means "see you Monday," 下周见 means "see you next week," 回头见 means "see you in a bit." The word 见 (jiàn) means "to see" or "to meet," and is the same character used in 再见 (goodbye). This pattern is more specific and warmer than plain 再见 because it confirms a shared future meeting.

## Collocations or Common Phrases

| Phrase | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| 再见 | goodbye |
| 后天见 | see you the day after tomorrow |
| 下周见 | see you next week |
| 周末见 | see you this weekend |
| 回头见 | see you in a bit; talk soon |

## Memory Hook

明天 (tomorrow) + 见 (see) — picture the sun rising and your friend waving at you the next morning.
