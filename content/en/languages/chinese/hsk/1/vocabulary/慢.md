## 慢 (màn)

An adjective and adverb meaning "slow" or "slowly." It describes low speed or unhurried movement. Its antonym is 快 (kuài), meaning "fast."

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** Slow, not fast.
2. **[adverb]** Slowly (describing the manner of an action).

## Example Sentences

**他说话很慢，我能听懂。**
Tā shuōhuà hěn màn, wǒ néng tīngdǒng.
He speaks very slowly, I can understand him.

**请开慢一点，路很滑。**
Qǐng kāi màn yīdiǎn, lù hěn huá.
Please drive a little slower, the road is slippery.

**乌龟走得很慢。**
Wūguī zǒu de hěn màn.
The turtle walks very slowly.

**你的动作太慢了，快一点！**
Nǐ de dòngzuò tài màn le, kuài yīdiǎn!
Your movements are too slow, speed up!

## Memory Hook

Antonym of 快 (kuài, fast). 慢 has the 心 (heart) radical — when your heart is calm and settled, you move slowly. A slow heartbeat means a slow, relaxed pace.
