## 进 (jìn)

A directional verb meaning "to enter" or "to go into" a place. It describes movement from outside to inside. Its antonym is 出 (chū), meaning "to exit" or "to go out."

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To enter, to go in, to come in.

## Example Sentences

**请进，门没有关。**
Qǐng jìn, mén méiyǒu guān.
Please come in, the door is not closed.

**他走进了教室。**
Tā zǒu jìn le jiàoshì.
He walked into the classroom.

**不要进那个房间。**
Bùyào jìn nàge fángjiān.
Do not enter that room.

**我们进去看看吧。**
Wǒmen jìnqù kànkan ba.
Let's go inside and have a look.

## Memory Hook

Antonym of 出 (chū, to exit). Think of 进 as "in" — the sound "jìn" echoes "in," the direction of movement: going in.
