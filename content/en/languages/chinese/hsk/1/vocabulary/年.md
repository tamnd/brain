## 年 (nián)

The word for year, used in dates, durations, and ages; it functions as both a noun and a time measure word, so no additional measure word is needed (三年 = three years, not 三个年).

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Year (calendar year or a period of 12 months).
2. **[noun]** Age (in the sense of years of life, though 岁 is more common for age).
3. **[noun]** New Year (in compounds: 新年, 过年).

## Example Sentences

**今年是二〇二六年。**
Jīnnián shì èr líng èr liù nián.
This year is 2026.

**我学了三年汉语了。**
Wǒ xuéle sān nián Hànyǔ le.
I have studied Chinese for three years.

**你是哪年出生的？**
Nǐ shì nǎ nián chūshēng de?
What year were you born?

**新年快乐！**
Xīnnián kuàilè!
Happy New Year!

## Memory Hook

年 contains the radical for "grain" at the top — the harvest cycle. One full harvest equals one year. Think of a farmer counting years by the number of harvests completed.
