## 结束 (jiéshù)

A word meaning "to end" or "to finish" as a verb, and "the end" as a noun. It signals the completion of an event or activity. Its antonym is 开始 (kāishǐ), meaning "to begin."

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To end, to finish, to conclude (an event or activity).
2. **[noun]** The end, the conclusion (of something).

## Example Sentences

**电影几点结束？**
Diànyǐng jǐ diǎn jiéshù?
What time does the movie end?

**工作结束后，我去买东西。**
Gōngzuò jiéshù hòu, wǒ qù mǎi dōngxi.
After work ends, I will go shopping.

**比赛结束了，大家都很高兴。**
Bǐsài jiéshù le, dàjiā dōu hěn gāoxìng.
The competition is over and everyone is happy.

**这个故事有一个好的结束。**
Zhège gùshi yǒu yīgè hǎo de jiéshù.
This story has a good ending.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |

| 圆满结束 | to end successfully |

## Memory Hook

Antonym of 开始 (kāishǐ, to begin). If 开始 is the opening of a door, 结束 is closing and tying it shut — 结 means "to tie/knot," like tying up loose ends.
