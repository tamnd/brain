## 跑 (pǎo)

A verb meaning "to run." It describes moving quickly on foot and is used for running for exercise, in a race, or to escape. The character uses the 足 (foot) radical on the left, making the meaning easy to remember.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To run, to jog, to sprint.

## Example Sentences

**我每天早上跑步。**
Wǒ měitiān zǎoshàng pǎobù.
I go running every morning.

**他跑得很快。**
Tā pǎo de hěn kuài.
He runs very fast.

**小狗跑过来了。**
Xiǎo gǒu pǎo guòlái le.
The puppy ran over.

**她跑了一个小时。**
Tā pǎo le yīgè xiǎoshí.
She ran for one hour.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 跑步 | to jog, running (exercise) |
| 跑得快 | to run fast |
| 跑过来 | to run over (toward speaker) |
| 跑过去 | to run over (away from speaker) |
| 跑步机 | treadmill |
| 快跑 | run fast, run! |

## Memory Hook

跑 has the 足 (foot) radical on the left — feet moving quickly. The right side 包 (bāo, to wrap/bag) sounds similar to "pow," like the burst of energy when you sprint off the starting line.
