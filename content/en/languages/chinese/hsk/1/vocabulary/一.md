## 一 (yī)

The number one, written as a single horizontal stroke; its tone changes depending on what follows: yí before 4th-tone syllables, yì before 1st, 2nd, or 3rd-tone syllables.

## Meanings

1. **[number]** One, the digit 1.
2. **[adverb]** Once, as soon as (in patterns like 一…就…: as soon as… then…).
3. **[determiner]** A, an (indefinite article function before measure word + noun).

## Example Sentences

**我有一个姐姐。**
Wǒ yǒu yī gè jiějie.
I have one older sister.

**他一说话，大家就安静了。**
Tā yī shuōhuà, dàjiā jiù ānjìng le.
As soon as he spoke, everyone fell quiet.

**给我一杯水，好吗？**
Gěi wǒ yī bēi shuǐ, hǎo ma?
Could you give me a glass of water?

**这件事只有一个办法。**
Zhè jiàn shì zhǐyǒu yī gè bànfǎ.
There is only one solution to this matter.

## Memory Hook

一 is literally one horizontal stroke — the simplest number in any writing system. One line, one meaning: one.
