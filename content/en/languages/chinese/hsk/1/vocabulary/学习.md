## 学习 (xuéxí)

学习 works as both a noun meaning "study" or "learning" and a verb meaning "to study" or "to learn." 学 (xué) means to learn or imitate, and 习 (xí) means to practice repeatedly. Together they capture the cycle of learning and practicing.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Study, learning.
2. **[verb]** To study, to learn (through practice and repetition).

## Example Sentences

**我每天学习两个小时的汉语。**
Wǒ měitiān xuéxí liǎng gè xiǎoshí de Hànyǔ.
I study Chinese for two hours every day.

**学习是一件很快乐的事。**
Xuéxí shì yī jiàn hěn kuàilè de shì.
Studying is a very joyful thing.

**他的学习成绩很好。**
Tā de xuéxí chéngjī hěn hǎo.
His academic results are very good.

**我们要认真学习。**
Wǒmen yào rènzhēn xuéxí.
We must study seriously.

## Memory Hook

学 shows a child under a roof with hands, learning from elders above. 习 shows a bird repeatedly flapping wings to practice flight. Learning needs both receiving knowledge and repeated practice.
