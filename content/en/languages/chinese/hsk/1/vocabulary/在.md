## 在 (zài)

在 expresses location. It functions both as a main verb meaning "to be at/in" and as a preposition meaning "at/in" placed before a location to set the scene for an action.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To be at; to be in; to be present (main verb indicating existence at a location).
2. **[preposition]** At; in; on (before a location word, indicating where an action takes place).

## Example Sentences

**我在家。**
Wǒ zài jiā.
I am at home. (verb use)

**他在北京工作。**
Tā zài Běijīng gōngzuò.
He works in Beijing. (preposition: 在 + place + verb)

**书在桌子上。**
Shū zài zhuōzi shàng.
The book is on the table. (verb use with location phrase)

**我们在教室里学习。**
Wǒmen zài jiàoshì lǐ xuéxí.
We study in the classroom. (preposition use)

## Usage Notes

When 在 is the main verb, the sentence structure is: Subject + 在 + place (我在图书馆, I am at the library). When 在 is a preposition, it comes before the place but after the subject and before the main verb: Subject + 在 + place + verb (我在图书馆看书, I read books at the library). The negative of verb 在 is 不在 (not here/not at that place). Do not confuse 在 (location) with 有 (existence/possession) — both relate to presence, but 有 focuses on whether something exists, while 在 states where it is.

## Collocations or Common Phrases

| Phrase | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| 在家 | at home |
| 在学校 | at school |
| 在哪里 | where (at what place) |
| 在……上 | on (top of) |
| 不在 | not here; not present |

## Memory Hook

在 contains 土 (earth/ground) — you are standing on the ground at a specific location.
