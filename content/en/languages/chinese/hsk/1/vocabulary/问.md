## 问 (wèn)

A verb meaning "to ask" or "to inquire." It is used when seeking information or posing a question. Its counterpart is 回答 (huídá), meaning "to answer." Together 问 and 回答 form the basic question-answer pair in conversation.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To ask, to inquire, to question.

## Example Sentences

**我想问你一个问题。**
Wǒ xiǎng wèn nǐ yīgè wèntí.
I want to ask you a question.

**请问，这里有厕所吗？**
Qǐngwèn, zhèlǐ yǒu cèsuǒ ma?
Excuse me, is there a restroom here?

**老师问我们懂了没有。**
Lǎoshī wèn wǒmen dǒng le méiyǒu.
The teacher asked us if we understood.

**你可以问他，他知道答案。**
Nǐ kěyǐ wèn tā, tā zhīdào dá'àn.
You can ask him, he knows the answer.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 问题 | question, problem |
| 请问 | excuse me, may I ask |
| 问一下 | to ask briefly |
| 问路 | to ask for directions |
| 回答问题 | to answer a question |
| 问清楚 | to ask clearly, to clarify |

## Memory Hook

问 has the 口 (mouth) radical inside 门 (door) — you open your mouth like a door to let a question out. Asking is opening the door of communication.
