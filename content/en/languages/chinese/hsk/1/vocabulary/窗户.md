## 窗户 (chuānghù)

A noun meaning window. The full form 窗户 is used in speech, while the single character 窗 (chuāng) appears more in written and compound forms. A window is a frame in a wall that lets in light and air.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Window.

## Example Sentences

**请打开窗户，空气不好。**
Qǐng dǎkāi chuānghù, kōngqì bù hǎo.
Please open the window, the air is bad.

**窗户外面有一棵大树。**
Chuānghù wàimiàn yǒu yī kē dà shù.
There is a big tree outside the window.

**他喜欢坐在窗边看书。**
Tā xǐhuān zuò zài chuāng biān kàn shū.
He likes to sit by the window and read.

**冬天太冷了，关上窗户吧。**
Dōngtiān tài lěng le, guānshàng chuānghù ba.
It is too cold in winter, let us close the window.

## Memory Hook

窗 has the character for "hole" (穴) at the top, because a window is a hole in the wall for light and air. 户 means "household unit." A window is a household hole.
