## 看 (kàn)

看 covers a wide range of visual activities. It means "to look at" (general), "to watch" (TV, a film, a performance), and "to read" (books, newspapers). Context makes the meaning clear.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To look at, to see (directing your gaze at something).
2. **[verb]** To watch (a screen, a show, a game).
3. **[verb]** To read (books, newspapers, documents).

## Example Sentences

**我晚上喜欢看电视。**
Wǒ wǎnshang xǐhuān kàn diànshì.
I like to watch TV in the evening.

**他在看一本书。**
Tā zài kàn yī běn shū.
He is reading a book.

**你看！那是什么？**
Nǐ kàn! Nà shì shénme?
Look! What is that?

**我们去看电影吧。**
Wǒmen qù kàn diànyǐng ba.
Let's go watch a movie.

## Memory Hook

看 has a hand (手) over an eye (目), like someone shading their eyes to look into the distance. You hand-over-eye look at things.
