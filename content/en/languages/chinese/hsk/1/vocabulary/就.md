## 就 (jiù)

就 is a timing adverb that conveys the sense of "right away," "as early as," or "already at that point." It emphasizes that something happens quickly, early, or with little delay.

## Meanings

1. **[adverb]** Right away; immediately; as soon as.
2. **[adverb]** Already (at an earlier-than-expected time).
3. **[adverb]** Then; in that case (in conditional sentences).

## Example Sentences

**我五点就起床了。**
Wǒ wǔ diǎn jiù qǐchuáng le.
I was up as early as five o'clock.

**她一听就笑了。**
Tā yī tīng jiù xiào le.
She laughed as soon as she heard it.

**你说，我就做。**
Nǐ shuō, wǒ jiù zuò.
You say it and I will do it right away.

**如果你累了，就休息一下。**
Rúguǒ nǐ lèi le, jiù xiūxi yīxià.
If you are tired, then rest for a bit.

## Usage Notes

就 contrasts directly with 才. 就 implies the action happens earlier or faster than expected, conveying eagerness or promptness. 才 implies the action happens later or after more difficulty than expected. Compare: 他八点就来了 (He came as early as eight o'clock, impressively early) versus 他八点才来 (He didn't come until eight, which was late). 就 also links the result clause in conditional sentences after 如果.

## Collocations or Common Phrases

| Phrase | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| 马上就来 | coming right away |
| 一……就…… | as soon as... |
| 就是这个 | it is exactly this one |
| 如果……就…… | if... then... |
| 快点儿，我们就走 | hurry up, we're leaving right now |

## Memory Hook

就 sounds a bit like "just" — just one second, I'll be right there.
