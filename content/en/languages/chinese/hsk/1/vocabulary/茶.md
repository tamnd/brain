## 茶 (chá)

Tea, the most culturally significant beverage in China. 茶 refers to both the drink and the tea leaves themselves. The word "tea" in most world languages originates from Chinese.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Tea. A hot or cold drink made by infusing dried tea leaves in water.
2. **[noun]** Tea leaves. The dried plant material used to brew the drink.

## Example Sentences

**你喝茶吗？**
Nǐ hē chá ma?
Do you drink tea?

**我每天早上喝一杯茶。**
Wǒ měitiān zǎoshang hē yī bēi chá.
I drink a cup of tea every morning.

**这杯茶很香。**
Zhè bēi chá hěn xiāng.
This cup of tea smells wonderful.

**中国人很喜欢喝茶。**
Zhōngguó rén hěn xǐhuān hē chá.
Chinese people love to drink tea.

## Memory Hook

The character 茶 has grass (草) at the top for the tea plant, a person (人) in the middle, and wood (木) at the bottom. Picture a person sitting between plants and wood, enjoying a peaceful cup of tea.
