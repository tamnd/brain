## 容易 (róngyì)

An adjective meaning easy or simple to do. It is the direct antonym of 难 (nán), which means difficult. 容易 can also mean "likely to happen" or "prone to" in some contexts.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** Easy, simple, not difficult.
2. **[adjective]** Likely, prone to (as in something that tends to happen).

## Example Sentences

**这道题很容易，我一分钟就做完了。**
Zhè dào tí hěn róngyì, wǒ yī fēnzhōng jiù zuò wán le.
This problem is very easy, I finished it in one minute.

**学说汉语不容易，但是很有意思。**
Xué shuō Hànyǔ bù róngyì, dànshì hěn yǒu yìsi.
Learning to speak Chinese is not easy, but very interesting.

**这种天气容易感冒。**
Zhè zhǒng tiānqì róngyì gǎnmào.
This kind of weather makes it easy to catch a cold.

**容易做的事情，大家都喜欢做。**
Róngyì zuò de shìqing, dàjiā dōu xǐhuān zuò.
Everyone likes to do things that are easy.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 很容易 (hěn róngyì) | very easy |
| 容易做 (róngyì zuò) | easy to do |
| 不容易 (bù róngyì) | not easy, difficult |
| 容易忘 (róngyì wàng) | easy to forget |
| 容易出错 (róngyì chū cuò) | prone to making mistakes |

## Memory Hook

Antonym: 难 (nán) means difficult. 容易 contains 容 (contain, accommodate) suggesting something that fits smoothly without resistance. That smooth fit is easiness.
