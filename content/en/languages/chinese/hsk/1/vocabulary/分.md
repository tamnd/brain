## 分 (fēn)

分 is used after a number to express minutes when telling the time. It also means "to divide" or "a portion," and appears in many compound words.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Minute (unit of time, used after a number).
2. **[verb]** To divide, to separate.
3. **[noun]** A part, a portion, a score (in exams).

## Example Sentences

**现在八点二十分。**
Xiànzài bā diǎn èrshí fēn.
It is now eight twenty.

**我们还有五分钟。**
Wǒmen hái yǒu wǔ fēnzhōng.
We still have five minutes.

**请把这个分成两份。**
Qǐng bǎ zhège fēn chéng liǎng fèn.
Please divide this into two portions.

**他考了九十分。**
Tā kǎo le jiǔshí fēn.
He scored ninety points on the exam.

## Memory Hook

分 shows a knife (刀) cutting something in two. Each minute slices an hour into smaller pieces.
