## 商店 (shāngdiàn)

A shop or store where goods are sold to customers. This word refers to any general retail establishment.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Shop, store. A place where goods are sold commercially.

## Example Sentences

**商店几点开门？**
Shāngdiàn jǐ diǎn kāimén?
What time does the shop open?

**这个商店很大。**
Zhège shāngdiàn hěn dà.
This store is very large.

**我在商店买了水。**
Wǒ zài shāngdiàn mǎile shuǐ.
I bought water at the store.

**商店里有很多东西。**
Shāngdiàn lǐ yǒu hěn duō dōngxi.
There are many things in the store.

## Memory Hook

商 means "commerce" or "trade," and 店 means "shop" or "establishment." Together they form the word for any trading shop. Think of 商 as the business side and 店 as the physical place.
