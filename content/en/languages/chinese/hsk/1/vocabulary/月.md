## 月 (yuè)

The word for both "month" and "moon"; months in Chinese are numbered (一月 = January, 十二月 = December), so knowing 月 plus numbers 1–12 gives you all twelve months automatically.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Month (calendar month: 一月 through 十二月).
2. **[noun]** Moon (the celestial body; also in compounds like 月亮, moonlight).

## Example Sentences

**我的生日是三月十五号。**
Wǒ de shēngrì shì Sān yuè shíwǔ hào.
My birthday is March 15th.

**这个月我很忙。**
Zhège yuè wǒ hěn máng.
I am very busy this month.

**月亮真漂亮！**
Yuèliang zhēn piàoliang!
The moon is so beautiful!

**你每个月存多少钱？**
Nǐ měi gè yuè cún duōshao qián?
How much money do you save each month?

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 这个月 (zhège yuè) | this month |
| 上个月 (shàng gè yuè) | last month |
| 下个月 (xià gè yuè) | next month |
| 每个月 (měi gè yuè) | every month |
| 月亮 (yuèliang) | the moon |
| 几月 (jǐ yuè) | which month |

## Memory Hook

月 looks like a crescent moon with two strokes inside — the original pictograph of the moon's curved shape. One character for both moon and month, because a month was once measured by one full moon cycle.
