## 吗 (ma)

吗 is a sentence-final particle that converts any statement into a yes/no question. It is one of the simplest and most essential particles in Mandarin.

## Meanings

1. **[particle]** Question marker for yes/no questions (always sentence-final).

## Example Sentences

**你是学生吗？**
Nǐ shì xuésheng ma?
Are you a student?

**你吃饭了吗？**
Nǐ chīfàn le ma?
Have you eaten?

**他今天来吗？**
Tā jīntiān lái ma?
Is he coming today?

**这个好吃吗？**
Zhège hǎo chī ma?
Is this delicious?

## Usage Notes

吗 always appears at the very end of the sentence. It cannot be used in the same sentence as question words like 什么 (shénme, what), 哪 (nǎ, which), or 怎么 (zěnme, how) — those question words already make the sentence a question. To answer a 吗 question, you typically echo the main verb or use 是/不是 or 对/不对. There is no single "yes" or "no" in Chinese — the answer matches the verb used in the question.

## Collocations or Common Phrases

| Phrase | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| 好吗？ | is that okay? |
| 对吗？ | is that right? |
| 你好吗？ | how are you? (are you well?) |
| 是吗？ | really? is that so? |
| 去吗？ | are you going? |

## Memory Hook

吗 has the mouth radical (口) — your mouth opens to ask, and 吗 is the sound of asking at the end.
