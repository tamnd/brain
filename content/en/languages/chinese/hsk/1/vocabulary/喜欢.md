## 喜欢 (xǐhuān)

喜欢 means to like or to enjoy. It expresses a preference or fondness and is used before nouns (things you like) and before verbs (activities you enjoy doing).

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To like, to be fond of.
2. **[verb]** To enjoy doing something (followed by another verb).

## Example Sentences

**我很喜欢中国菜。**
Wǒ hěn xǐhuān Zhōngguó cài.
I really like Chinese food.

**你喜欢做什么？**
Nǐ xǐhuān zuò shénme?
What do you like to do?

**她喜欢唱歌，也喜欢跳舞。**
Tā xǐhuān chànggē, yě xǐhuān tiàowǔ.
She likes to sing and also likes to dance.

**我不喜欢冷天气。**
Wǒ bù xǐhuān lěng tiānqì.
I do not like cold weather.

## Memory Hook

喜 shows a drum (壴) with a mouth (口) below, celebrating with drumming and cheering. 欢 means joy. To like something is to celebrate it joyfully.
