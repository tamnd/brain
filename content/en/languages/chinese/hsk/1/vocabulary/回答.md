## 回答 (huídá)

A verb and noun meaning "to answer" or "an answer." 回 (huí) means "to return" and 答 (dá) means "to respond." Together they capture the idea of sending a response back. Its counterpart is 问 (wèn), meaning "to ask."

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To answer, to reply to (a question).
2. **[noun]** An answer, a reply.

## Example Sentences

**请回答我的问题。**
Qǐng huídá wǒ de wèntí.
Please answer my question.

**他回答得很好。**
Tā huídá de hěn hǎo.
He answered very well.

**她没有回答我的问题。**
Tā méiyǒu huídá wǒ de wèntí.
She did not answer my question.

**老师等了很久，才有人回答。**
Lǎoshī děng le hěn jiǔ, cái yǒu rén huídá.
The teacher waited a long time before anyone answered.

## Memory Hook

Pair with 问 (wèn, to ask): 问 sends a question out, 回答 sends the answer back. 回 (return) + 答 (respond) — returning words to the one who asked.
