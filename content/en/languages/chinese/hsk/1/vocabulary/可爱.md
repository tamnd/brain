## 可爱 (kě'ài)

An adjective meaning cute, adorable, or lovable. It describes people, animals, or objects that inspire affection, often through their small size, round features, or innocent manner.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** Cute, adorable, lovable (for people, animals, and things).
2. **[adjective]** Charming, endearing in a sweet way.

## Example Sentences

**这只小狗太可爱了！**
Zhè zhī xiǎogǒu tài kě'ài le!
This little dog is so adorable!

**她的女儿非常可爱。**
Tā de nǚ'ér fēicháng kě'ài.
Her daughter is extremely cute.

**那个小孩子笑起来很可爱。**
Nàge xiǎo háizi xiào qǐlái hěn kě'ài.
That little child looks very cute when smiling.

**你买的这个玩具真可爱！**
Nǐ mǎi de zhège wánjù zhēn kě'ài!
The toy you bought is really adorable!

## Memory Hook

可 means "can/able" and 爱 means "love." Put them together: something you cannot help but love. That irresistible cuteness is 可爱.
