## 公司 (gōngsī)

A noun meaning company, corporation, or firm. It refers to any business organization. The word combines 公 (public, collective) and 司 (manage, oversee).

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Company, corporation, business firm.

## Example Sentences

**他在一家大公司工作。**
Tā zài yī jiā dà gōngsī gōngzuò.
He works at a large company.

**这家公司有五百名员工。**
Zhè jiā gōngsī yǒu wǔbǎi míng yuángōng.
This company has five hundred employees.

**她想开一家自己的公司。**
Tā xiǎng kāi yī jiā zìjǐ de gōngsī.
She wants to start her own company.

**公司今天开会，我不能去喝咖啡了。**
Gōngsī jīntiān kāihuì, wǒ bù néng qù hē kāfēi le.
The company has a meeting today, I cannot go for coffee.

## Memory Hook

The measure word for companies is 家 (jiā), the same measure word used for families and households. A company is like a family or household unit in Chinese conceptual terms.
