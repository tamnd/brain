## 日 (rì)

The word for both "day" and "sun"; in dates, 日 is the formal written form for a day of the month (especially in official documents), while 号 (hào) is the colloquial spoken equivalent. Both mean the same thing in dates.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Day (of the month, formal: 三月十日 = March 10th).
2. **[noun]** Sun (the celestial body; in compounds: 日出 = sunrise, 日落 = sunset).
3. **[noun]** Day, daytime (in compounds: 今日 = today [formal], 日常 = daily).

## Example Sentences

**今天是五月三十日。**
Jīntiān shì Wǔ yuè sānshí rì.
Today is May 30th.

**日出的景色非常美。**
Rìchū de jǐngsè fēicháng měi.
The scenery of sunrise is extremely beautiful.

**他每日坚持锻炼。**
Tā měi rì jiānchí duànliàn.
He insists on exercising every day.

**这是我们公司日常的工作。**
Zhè shì wǒmen gōngsī rìcháng de gōngzuò.
This is the daily work of our company.

## Memory Hook

日 is a square with a line through the middle — a pictograph of the sun with its central light. The sun rises and sets to mark each day: one 日, one day.
