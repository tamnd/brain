## 女人 (nǚrén)

A noun meaning woman or adult female. It combines 女 (female) and 人 (person). In everyday speech, 女人 refers to adult women, while 女孩 (nǚhái) refers to girls.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Woman, adult female.
2. **[noun]** Women in general (as a group or category).

## Example Sentences

**那个女人是我妈妈的朋友。**
Nàge nǚrén shì wǒ māma de péngyou.
That woman is my mother's friend.

**她是一个聪明的女人。**
Tā shì yīgè cōngmíng de nǚrén.
She is an intelligent woman.

**教室里有三个女人和两个男人。**
Jiàoshì lǐ yǒu sān gè nǚrén hé liǎng gè nánrén.
There are three women and two men in the classroom.

**这个女人每天早上六点起床。**
Zhège nǚrén měitiān zǎoshang liù diǎn qǐchuáng.
This woman gets up at six o'clock every morning.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 女人 vs 男人 (nǚrén vs nánrén) | woman vs. man |
| 女性 (nǚxìng) | female (more formal) |
| 女孩 (nǚhái) | girl |
| 老女人 (lǎo nǚrén) | old woman (informal) |
| 年轻女人 (niánqīng nǚrén) | young woman |

## Memory Hook

The character 女 depicts a person in a seated, graceful pose. It is the foundational character for all words related to women and femininity in Chinese. Add 人 (person) and you get woman: 女人.
