## 杯 (bēi)

A measure word used for cups, glasses, and the drinks contained in them. 杯 also functions as a noun meaning "cup" or "glass" itself.

## Meanings

1. **[measure word]** Classifier for a cup or glass of a beverage.
2. **[noun]** Cup, glass, mug. The vessel used for drinking.

## What 杯 counts

杯 is used with:

- 茶 (chá): tea — 一杯茶 (a cup of tea)
- 水 (shuǐ): water — 一杯水 (a glass of water)
- 咖啡 (kāfēi): coffee — 一杯咖啡 (a cup of coffee)
- 果汁 (guǒzhī): juice — 一杯果汁 (a glass of juice)
- 酒 (jiǔ): alcohol — 一杯酒 (a glass of wine/beer)

## Example Sentences

**请给我一杯茶。**
Qǐng gěi wǒ yī bēi chá.
Please give me a cup of tea.

**我每天喝两杯水。**
Wǒ měitiān hē liǎng bēi shuǐ.
I drink two glasses of water every day.

**来一杯咖啡吧。**
Lái yī bēi kāfēi ba.
Let's have a cup of coffee.

**这杯茶太热了。**
Zhè bēi chá tài rè le.
This cup of tea is too hot.

## Common Learner Mistake

杯 refers to the container and its content together. Saying 一杯水 means "one glass of water" (the water in a glass). Do not confuse this with 一瓶水, which means "one bottle of water." 杯 is for cup-sized portions; 瓶 is for bottled drinks.
