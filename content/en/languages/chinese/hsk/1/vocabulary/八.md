## 八 (bā)

The number eight, considered the luckiest number in Chinese culture because bā sounds similar to 发 (fā, to prosper/get rich); license plates, phone numbers, and addresses containing 八 command premium prices.

## Meanings

1. **[number]** Eight, the digit 8.

## Example Sentences

**我每天睡八个小时。**
Wǒ měitiān shuì bā gè xiǎoshí.
I sleep eight hours every day.

**他用八万块钱买了一辆车。**
Tā yòng bā wàn kuài qián mǎile yī liàng chē.
He bought a car for 80,000 yuan.

**八月天气很热，我想去游泳。**
Bā yuè tiānqì hěn rè, wǒ xiǎng qù yóuyǒng.
The weather in August is very hot; I want to go swimming.

**这场比赛八点开始。**
Zhè chǎng bǐsài bā diǎn kāishǐ.
This match starts at eight o'clock.

## Memory Hook

八 (bā) rhymes with the English word "baa" (a sheep sound), and sheep are associated with wealth in some cultures — but more directly, just remember: eight is the GREAT number, the one everyone in China wants in their phone number.
