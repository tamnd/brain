## 给 (gěi)

给 means "to give" as a main verb and "for" or "to" as a preposition indicating the recipient or beneficiary of an action.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To give; to hand over (something to someone).
2. **[preposition]** For; to (indicating the recipient before the main verb).

## Example Sentences

**我给你一本书。**
Wǒ gěi nǐ yī běn shū.
I give you a book.

**妈妈给我做了饭。**
Māma gěi wǒ zuò le fàn.
Mom made food for me.

**你可以给我打电话吗？**
Nǐ kěyǐ gěi wǒ dǎ diànhuà ma?
Can you give me a call?

**请把这个给他。**
Qǐng bǎ zhège gěi tā.
Please give this to him.

## Usage Notes

As a main verb, 给 follows the standard Subject + Verb + Indirect Object + Direct Object pattern: 我给你钱 (I give you money). As a preposition, 给 comes after the subject and before the main verb to indicate who benefits: 我给你买水果 (I will buy fruit for you). In many sentences the two uses look similar — the key is whether 给 is the only verb or whether a second verb follows it.

## Collocations or Common Phrases

| Phrase | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| 给我 | give me; for me |
| 给你 | for you; here you go |
| 给……打电话 | to call (someone) |
| 给……发消息 | to send a message to |
| 送给 | to give as a gift |

## Memory Hook

给 contains 糸 (silk thread) — imagine passing a thread from your hands to someone else's, a direct handoff.
