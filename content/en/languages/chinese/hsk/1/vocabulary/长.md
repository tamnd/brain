## 长 (cháng)

An adjective meaning "long," referring to physical length (a long road) or duration (a long time). Its antonym is 短 (duǎn), meaning "short." Note: 长 has a second reading, zhǎng, meaning "to grow" or "elder," but the HSK 1 meaning is cháng (long).

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** Long (in physical length or time duration).

## Example Sentences

**这条路很长。**
Zhè tiáo lù hěn cháng.
This road is very long.

**她的头发很长。**
Tā de tóufa hěn cháng.
Her hair is very long.

**等了很长时间。**
Děng le hěn cháng shíjiān.
Waited for a very long time.

**这条河有多长？**
Zhè tiáo hé yǒu duō cháng?
How long is this river?

## Memory Hook

Antonym of 短 (duǎn, short). The character 长 shows flowing strokes extending outward — like long hair spreading out or a road stretching to the horizon.
