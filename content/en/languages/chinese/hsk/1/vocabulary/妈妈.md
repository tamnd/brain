## 妈妈 (māma)

妈妈 is the informal, affectionate word for mother, equivalent to "mom" or "mama." It is among the first words children learn in any language, and Chinese is no exception.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Mom, mother (informal).

## Example Sentences

**妈妈在厨房做饭。**
Māma zài chúfáng zuòfàn.
Mom is cooking in the kitchen.

**我妈妈很漂亮。**
Wǒ māma hěn piàoliang.
My mom is very beautiful.

**妈妈，我饿了。**
Māma, wǒ è le.
Mom, I am hungry.

**她的妈妈是医生。**
Tā de māma shì yīshēng.
Her mother is a doctor.

## Memory Hook

妈 has the woman radical (女) on the left and "horse" (马) for the sound. A mother gallops forward to protect her family.
