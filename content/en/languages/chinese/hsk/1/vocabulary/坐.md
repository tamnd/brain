## 坐 (zuò)

A verb with two common uses: "to sit" (to take a seated position) and "to take/ride" a vehicle. When talking about transportation, 坐 is used with buses, trains, planes, and taxis.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To sit, to be seated.
2. **[verb]** To take, to ride (a vehicle such as a bus, train, or plane).

## Example Sentences

**请坐，喝点茶。**
Qǐng zuò, hē diǎn chá.
Please sit, have some tea.

**我每天坐公共汽车上学。**
Wǒ měitiān zuò gōnggòng qìchē shàngxué.
I take the bus to school every day.

**他坐在窗户旁边看书。**
Tā zuò zài chuānghù pángbiān kànshū.
He is sitting by the window reading.

**我们坐飞机去北京。**
Wǒmen zuò fēijī qù Běijīng.
We are taking a plane to Beijing.

## Memory Hook

The character 坐 shows two people (人人) sitting on the ground (土) — two people seated on earth. Picture them waiting at a bus stop before they ride.
