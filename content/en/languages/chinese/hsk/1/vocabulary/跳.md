## 跳 (tiào)

A verb meaning "to jump" or "to leap." It also appears in the phrase 跳舞 (tiàowǔ), meaning "to dance," because dancing involves rhythmic jumping and stepping movements. The 足 (foot) radical reinforces the physical, foot-related action.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To jump, to leap, to hop.
2. **[verb]** Component of 跳舞 (to dance).

## Example Sentences

**猫跳上了桌子。**
Māo tiào shàng le zhuōzi.
The cat jumped onto the table.

**她喜欢跳舞。**
Tā xǐhuān tiàowǔ.
She likes to dance.

**孩子们在跳绳。**
Háizimen zài tiàoshéng.
The children are jumping rope.

**他高兴地跳了起来。**
Tā gāoxìng de tiào le qǐlái.
He jumped up happily.

## Memory Hook

跳 has the 足 (foot) radical — you need your feet to jump. The right side 兆 (zhào) sounds slightly like "tiao," helping with pronunciation. Think: feet going "tiao!" as they spring off the ground.
