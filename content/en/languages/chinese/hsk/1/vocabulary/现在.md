## 现在 (xiànzài)

现在 means now or currently, referring to the present moment. It is placed at the beginning of a sentence or directly before the verb.

## Meanings

1. **[adverb/noun]** Now, at present, currently.

## Example Sentences

**现在几点了？**
Xiànzài jǐ diǎn le?
What time is it now?

**我现在很忙。**
Wǒ xiànzài hěn máng.
I am very busy right now.

**现在我们开始上课。**
Xiànzài wǒmen kāishǐ shàngkè.
Now let's start class.

**你现在在哪里？**
Nǐ xiànzài zài nǎlǐ?
Where are you right now?

## Memory Hook

现 has the "now/present" meaning and 在 means "to be at." Together they say "being at the present moment."
