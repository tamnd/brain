## 昨天 (zuótiān)

昨天 means yesterday, the day before today. It is placed at the beginning of a sentence or before the verb as a time expression.

## Meanings

1. **[noun/adverb]** Yesterday, the previous day.

## Example Sentences

**昨天我去了图书馆。**
Zuótiān wǒ qù le túshūguǎn.
Yesterday I went to the library.

**她昨天没有来上课。**
Tā zuótiān méiyǒu lái shàngkè.
She did not come to class yesterday.

**你昨天晚上做什么了？**
Nǐ zuótiān wǎnshang zuò shénme le?
What did you do last night?

**昨天的考试很难。**
Zuótiān de kǎoshì hěn nán.
Yesterday's exam was very difficult.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |

| 昨天早上 | yesterday morning |
| 昨天下午 | yesterday afternoon |

| 昨天的新闻 | yesterday's news |

## Memory Hook

昨 contains the character for "work/make" on the left and a time radical. Think of it as the day you already "made" your effort, now passed.
