## 喝 (hē)

喝 is the verb for drinking any kind of liquid. It is the counterpart to 吃 (chī, to eat) in everyday conversation about food and beverages.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To drink.

## Example Sentences

**你喝茶还是喝咖啡？**
Nǐ hē chá háishì hē kāfēi?
Do you drink tea or coffee?

**我每天早上喝一杯水。**
Wǒ měitiān zǎoshang hē yī bēi shuǐ.
I drink a glass of water every morning.

**天气很热，多喝水。**
Tiānqì hěn rè, duō hē shuǐ.
The weather is very hot. Drink more water.

**他喜欢喝牛奶。**
Tā xǐhuān hē niúnǎi.
He likes to drink milk.

## Memory Hook

喝 has the mouth radical (口) on the left. Your mouth is open and something is flowing in. The right side (曷) contains elements suggesting an open throat.
