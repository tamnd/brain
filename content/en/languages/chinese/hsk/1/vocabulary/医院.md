## 医院 (yīyuàn)

A hospital or medical facility where doctors treat patients. It is one of the most common public places discussed in everyday Chinese.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Hospital. A building or institution providing medical and surgical treatment.

## Example Sentences

**我去医院看病。**
Wǒ qù yīyuàn kàn bìng.
I am going to the hospital to see a doctor.

**医院在哪里？**
Yīyuàn zài nǎlǐ?
Where is the hospital?

**她在医院工作。**
Tā zài yīyuàn gōngzuò.
She works at the hospital.

**我们去医院看他。**
Wǒmen qù yīyuàn kàn tā.
We are going to the hospital to visit him.

## Memory Hook

Picture a building with a giant red cross and a sign reading "医院." The character 医 shows a person being treated with medicine, and 院 means a courtyard or institution.
