## 便宜 (piányí)

An adjective meaning "cheap" or "inexpensive." It describes a price that is low or affordable. Note: in this meaning, 便 is read as pián, not biàn. Its antonym is 贵 (guì), meaning "expensive."

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** Cheap, inexpensive, affordable (in price).

## Example Sentences

**这家店的东西很便宜。**
Zhè jiā diàn de dōngxi hěn piányí.
The things in this shop are very cheap.

**这条裤子比那条便宜多了。**
Zhè tiáo kùzi bǐ nà tiáo piányí duō le.
These pants are much cheaper than those.

**能便宜一点吗？**
Néng piányí yīdiǎn ma?
Can you make it a little cheaper?

**在市场上买东西比超市便宜。**
Zài shìchǎng shàng mǎi dōngxi bǐ chāoshì piányí.
Buying things at the market is cheaper than at the supermarket.

## Memory Hook

Antonym of 贵 (guì, expensive). The word 便宜 has an unusual reading (pián instead of biàn) — remember it as "piano easy": a piano that's easy to afford is a bargain.
