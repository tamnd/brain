## 短 (duǎn)

An adjective meaning "short" in length or duration. It is used for physical dimensions (a short rope) and time (a short period). Its antonym is 长 (cháng), meaning "long." Do not confuse with 矮 (ǎi), which means short in height (of a person).

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** Short (in physical length or time duration).

## Example Sentences

**这条裙子太短了。**
Zhè tiáo qúnzi tài duǎn le.
This skirt is too short.

**他的头发很短。**
Tā de tóufa hěn duǎn.
His hair is very short.

**这篇文章太短了，能写长一点吗？**
Zhè piān wénzhāng tài duǎn le, néng xiě cháng yīdiǎn ma?
This article is too short, can you write it a bit longer?

**我们相识的时间很短。**
Wǒmen xiāngshí de shíjiān hěn duǎn.
The time we have known each other is very short.

## Memory Hook

Antonym of 长 (cháng, long). 短 has the 矢 (arrow) radical — an arrow is compact and short compared to a long spear. Short and sharp.
