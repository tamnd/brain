## 中午 (zhōngwǔ)

中午 means noon or midday, around 12:00 p.m. 中 means "middle" and 午 means "noon," forming a word that pinpoints the exact middle of the day.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Noon, midday (approximately 11:30 to 13:00).

## Example Sentences

**中午我们一起吃饭吧。**
Zhōngwǔ wǒmen yīqǐ chīfàn ba.
Let's eat together at noon.

**他中午不回家吃饭。**
Tā zhōngwǔ bù huí jiā chīfàn.
He does not go home for lunch at noon.

**中午十二点我有空。**
Zhōngwǔ shí'èr diǎn wǒ yǒu kòng.
I am free at twelve noon.

**今天中午天气很热。**
Jīntiān zhōngwǔ tiānqì hěn rè.
The weather at noon today is very hot.
