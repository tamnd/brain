## 她们 (tāmen)

The third-person plural pronoun for an exclusively female group; it is spoken identically to 他们 (tāmen) and 它们 (tāmen) — the distinction only appears in writing.

## Meanings

1. **[pronoun]** They (subject, all-female group).
2. **[pronoun]** Them (object, all-female group).
3. **[pronoun]** Their (before 的: 她们的).

## Example Sentences

**她们是我的同学。**
Tāmen shì wǒ de tóngxué.
They are my classmates.

**我很喜欢她们。**
Wǒ hěn xǐhuan tāmen.
I like them very much.

**她们的歌唱得很好听。**
Tāmen de gē chàng de hěn hǎotīng.
They sing beautifully.

**她们今天一起去购物了。**
Tāmen jīntiān yīqǐ qù gòuwù le.
They went shopping together today.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 她们的 (tāmen de) | their, theirs (all female) |
| 她们都 (tāmen dōu) | they all (female) |
| 告诉她们 (gàosu tāmen) | tell them (female) |
| 她们是 (tāmen shì) | they are (female) |
| 帮助她们 (bāngzhù tāmen) | help them (female) |
| 和她们 (hé tāmen) | with them (female) |

## Memory Hook

她们 is 她 (she) plus 们. The 女 radical on the left signals the group is entirely female. When you see 女 in a pronoun, picture a group of women.
