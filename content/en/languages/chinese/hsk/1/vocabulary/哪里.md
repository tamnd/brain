## 哪里 (nǎlǐ)

The standard written form and southern-preferred spoken form of "where," fully interchangeable with 哪儿 (nǎr) in meaning; 哪里 is more common in formal writing, the south, and Taiwan Mandarin.

## Meanings

1. **[pronoun]** Where (asking about location or destination).
2. **[pronoun]** Wherever (open or conditional sense).
3. **[phrase]** "Not at all / you're too kind" (set phrase in response to a compliment).

## Example Sentences

**你想去哪里？**
Nǐ xiǎng qù nǎlǐ?
Where do you want to go?

**图书馆在哪里？**
Túshūguǎn zài nǎlǐ?
Where is the library?

**你是从哪里来的？**
Nǐ shì cóng nǎlǐ lái de?
Where are you from?

**A：你的汉语说得真好！B：哪里哪里。**
A: Nǐ de Hànyǔ shuō de zhēn hǎo! B: Nǎlǐ nǎlǐ.
A: Your Chinese is really good! B: Not at all.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 去哪里 (qù nǎlǐ) | going where |
| 在哪里 (zài nǎlǐ) | located where |
| 从哪里 (cóng nǎlǐ) | from where |
| 哪里都 (nǎlǐ dōu) | everywhere, anywhere |
| 哪里哪里 (nǎlǐ nǎlǐ) | not at all (humble reply) |
| 到哪里 (dào nǎlǐ) | to where |

## Memory Hook

哪里 is literally "which place" (哪 = which, 里 = inside/place). Picture looking around a map and pointing: "which place is it?"
