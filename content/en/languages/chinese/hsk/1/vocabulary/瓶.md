## 瓶 (píng)

A measure word used for bottles and their contents. 瓶 also functions as a noun meaning "bottle." It is used whenever you count or refer to something contained in a bottle.

## Meanings

1. **[measure word]** Classifier for bottles and bottled contents.
2. **[noun]** Bottle. A narrow-necked container for liquids.

## What 瓶 counts

瓶 is used with:

- 水 (shuǐ): water — 一瓶水 (one bottle of water)
- 酒 (jiǔ): alcohol — 一瓶酒 (one bottle of wine/spirits)
- 果汁 (guǒzhī): juice — 一瓶果汁 (one bottle of juice)
- 牛奶 (niúnǎi): milk — 一瓶牛奶 (one bottle of milk)
- 饮料 (yǐnliào): beverage — 一瓶饮料 (one bottle of drink)

## Example Sentences

**请给我一瓶水。**
Qǐng gěi wǒ yī píng shuǐ.
Please give me a bottle of water.

**超市里有很多瓶果汁。**
Chāoshì lǐ yǒu hěn duō píng guǒzhī.
There are many bottles of juice in the supermarket.

**他买了两瓶牛奶。**
Tā mǎile liǎng píng niúnǎi.
He bought two bottles of milk.

**这瓶水是你的吗？**
Zhè píng shuǐ shì nǐ de ma?
Is this bottle of water yours?

## Common Learner Mistake

Do not confuse 瓶 with 杯. 一瓶水 is a whole bottle of water, while 一杯水 is a glass of water. 瓶 is for sealed or bottled containers; 杯 is for open cups or glasses. Using the wrong one changes the quantity significantly.
