## 水 (shuǐ)

Water, the most essential liquid. 水 is one of the most fundamental characters in Chinese, appearing in hundreds of compound words related to liquids, rivers, and flow.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Water. The clear liquid essential for life, drinking, and cooking.
2. **[noun]** Any liquid in general, in some compound words.

## Example Sentences

**我想喝水。**
Wǒ xiǎng hē shuǐ.
I want to drink water.

**请给我一杯水。**
Qǐng gěi wǒ yī bēi shuǐ.
Please give me a glass of water.

**这里的水很干净。**
Zhèlǐ de shuǐ hěn gānjìng.
The water here is very clean.

**你喝水还是喝茶？**
Nǐ hē shuǐ háishì hē chá?
Do you drink water or tea?

## Memory Hook

The character 水 looks like a flowing river with waves. It is one of the oldest Chinese pictographic characters, originally drawn to resemble water flowing in a stream. Trace the strokes and see the flow.
