## 第一 (dìyī)

The ordinal number "first," formed by placing the prefix 第 (dì) before any cardinal number; this pattern applies to all ordinals (第二 = second, 第三 = third, etc.) and is used in sequences, rankings, and lists.

## Meanings

1. **[ordinal number]** First, number one in a sequence or ranking.
2. **[adjective]** Primary, most important (第一重要 = of primary importance).

## Example Sentences

**她是我们班第一名。**
Tā shì wǒmen bān dìyī míng.
She is first in our class.

**这是我第一次来中国。**
Zhè shì wǒ dìyī cì lái Zhōngguó.
This is my first time coming to China.

**安全是第一位的。**
Ānquán shì dìyī wèi de.
Safety comes first.

**第一章介绍了中国的历史。**
Dìyī zhāng jièshàole Zhōngguó de lìshǐ.
The first chapter introduces Chinese history.

## Memory Hook

第 (dì) is the ordinal prefix — think of it as a badge you pin on a number to turn it from a count into a rank. 第一 = badge + one = "the one wearing the first-place badge."
