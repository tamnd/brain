## 朋友 (péngyou)

A noun meaning friend. It is one of the first and most important social words to learn in Chinese. Note that 友 on its own also means friend or friendly, but 朋友 is the standard word used in speech.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Friend, companion.
2. **[noun]** (Informal) Boyfriend or girlfriend, when the context is a romantic relationship.

## Example Sentences

**他是我最好的朋友。**
Tā shì wǒ zuì hǎo de péngyou.
He is my best friend.

**我在中国有很多朋友。**
Wǒ zài Zhōngguó yǒu hěn duō péngyou.
I have many friends in China.

**你的朋友都是学生吗？**
Nǐ de péngyou dōu shì xuésheng ma?
Are all your friends students?

**她带着男朋友来参加晚会。**
Tā dàizhe nán péngyou lái cānjiā wǎnhuì.
She brought her boyfriend to the party.

## Memory Hook

朋 originally depicted two strings of shells side by side, suggesting equals walking together. 友 shows two hands clasped. Together they paint a picture of equals with hands joined: friends.
