## 小 (xiǎo)

An adjective meaning "small," "little," or "young." It is the antonym of 大 (dà, big). Beyond size, 小 is also used to indicate youth (小孩, small child) and appears as an affectionate prefix for names (小明, Little Ming).

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** Small, little (in size).
2. **[adjective]** Young (in age).

## Example Sentences

**这只猫很小。**
Zhè zhī māo hěn xiǎo.
This cat is very small.

**她的房间比我的小。**
Tā de fángjiān bǐ wǒ de xiǎo.
Her room is smaller than mine.

**他还很小，才五岁。**
Tā hái hěn xiǎo, cái wǔ suì.
He is still very young, only five years old.

**小朋友们在花园里玩。**
Xiǎo péngyoumen zài huāyuán lǐ wán.
The little children are playing in the garden.

## Memory Hook

Antonym of 大 (dà, big). The character 小 shows a central stroke with two small dots on the side — three tiny marks, representing something small and spread out delicately.
