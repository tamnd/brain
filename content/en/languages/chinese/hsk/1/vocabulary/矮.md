## 矮 (ǎi)

An adjective meaning "short" in height, used to describe people or objects that are not tall. It refers specifically to vertical height, not length. Its antonym is 高 (gāo), meaning "tall." Do not confuse with 短 (duǎn, short in length).

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** Short (in height), not tall (for people, trees, buildings, or objects).

## Example Sentences

**她比她姐姐矮一点。**
Tā bǐ tā jiějie ǎi yīdiǎn.
She is a little shorter than her older sister.

**那棵树很矮。**
Nà kē shù hěn ǎi.
That tree is very short.

**这张桌子有点矮。**
Zhè zhāng zhuōzi yǒudiǎn ǎi.
This table is a little short.

**他虽然矮，但是跑得很快。**
Tā suīrán ǎi, dànshì pǎo de hěn kuài.
Although he is short, he runs very fast.

## Memory Hook

Antonym of 高 (gāo, tall). 矮 has the 矢 (arrow) component — imagine an arrow shot downward, keeping things low and short rather than high.
