## 这 (zhè)

A demonstrative pronoun and determiner meaning "this," pointing to people or things near the speaker; its counterpart for distant things is 那 (nà, that).

## Meanings

1. **[pronoun]** This (used alone as a subject or object).
2. **[determiner]** This (before a measure word and noun: 这 + measure word + noun).

## Example Sentences

**这是我的书。**
Zhè shì wǒ de shū.
This is my book.

**这个苹果很甜。**
Zhège píngguǒ hěn tián.
This apple is very sweet.

**这里有很多人。**
Zhèlǐ yǒu hěn duō rén.
There are many people here.

**我喜欢这件衣服。**
Wǒ xǐhuan zhè jiàn yīfu.
I like this piece of clothing.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 这个 (zhège) | this one (with general measure word 个) |
| 这里 (zhèlǐ) | here |
| 这儿 (zhèr) | here (colloquial) |
| 这些 (zhèxiē) | these (plural) |
| 这次 (zhè cì) | this time |
| 这样 (zhèyàng) | like this, in this way |

## Memory Hook

这 has the movement radical 辶 (chuò) at the bottom, as if you are stepping forward toward the thing — it is right here, close enough to touch.
