## 电视 (diànshì)

A noun meaning television or TV. It combines 电 (electricity) and 视 (see/vision). The word refers to both the television set and to television as a medium.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Television, TV set.
2. **[noun]** Television as a medium (watching TV in general).

## Example Sentences

**你喜欢看电视吗？**
Nǐ xǐhuān kàn diànshì ma?
Do you like watching TV?

**这台电视很大。**
Zhè tái diànshì hěn dà.
This television set is very big.

**他每天晚上看两个小时的电视。**
Tā měitiān wǎnshang kàn liǎng gè xiǎoshí de diànshì.
He watches two hours of television every evening.

**电视上在播一个很好看的节目。**
Diànshì shàng zài bō yīgè hěn hǎokàn de jiémù.
A very interesting program is playing on TV.

## Memory Hook

电 (electricity) plus 视 (vision/see). Television is electricity you watch. The pattern 电 + something appears in many technology words: 电脑 (computer), 电话 (phone), 电视 (TV).
