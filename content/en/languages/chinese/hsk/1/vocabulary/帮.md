## 帮 (bāng)

A common verb meaning "to help" or "to assist." It is used when one person does something for another's benefit. The fuller form 帮助 (bāngzhù) is also common, but 帮 alone is standard in everyday speech.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To help, to assist, to do something for someone.

## Example Sentences

**你能帮我吗？**
Nǐ néng bāng wǒ ma?
Can you help me?

**妈妈帮我做作业。**
Māma bāng wǒ zuò zuòyè.
Mom helps me with my homework.

**请帮我拿一下那本书。**
Qǐng bāng wǒ ná yīxià nà běn shū.
Please help me get that book.

**他总是帮朋友搬东西。**
Tā zǒng shì bāng péngyou bān dōngxi.
He always helps his friends move things.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 帮我 | help me |
| 帮助 | help, assistance (noun/verb) |
| 帮忙 | to lend a hand, to help out |
| 帮朋友 | to help a friend |
| 请帮我…… | please help me... |
| 互相帮助 | to help each other |

## Memory Hook

Picture a friend putting their arm around your shoulder — 帮 sounds like "bang," as in giving someone a helping "bang" of support from the side.
