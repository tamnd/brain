## 用 (yòng)

A common verb meaning "to use" something. It also functions as a preposition meaning "with" or "by means of" (using a tool or method). It is one of the most frequent words in daily Chinese.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To use, to employ, to make use of.
2. **[preposition]** With, by means of (using a tool or method).

## Example Sentences

**你用什么写字？**
Nǐ yòng shénme xiězì?
What do you use to write?

**我用筷子吃饭。**
Wǒ yòng kuàizi chīfàn.
I eat with chopsticks.

**这个手机很好用。**
Zhège shǒujī hěn hǎo yòng.
This phone is very easy to use.

**请不要用手机上课。**
Qǐng bùyào yòng shǒujī shàngkè.
Please do not use your phone during class.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 用……做…… | to use something to do something |
| 好用 | easy to use, useful |
| 不用 | no need to, don't use |
| 用功 | to work hard, to be studious |
| 用处 | use, purpose |
| 使用 | to use, to employ (formal) |

## Memory Hook

用 looks like a bucket or container being put to work — something you reach into and draw from to accomplish a task. You use what you have.
