## 新 (xīn)

An adjective meaning "new," "fresh," or "modern." It describes things that are recently made, acquired, or encountered. Its antonym is 旧 (jiù), meaning "old" (for objects, not people). For people, the antonym is 老 (lǎo).

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** New, fresh, recently made or acquired.

## Example Sentences

**他买了一辆新车。**
Tā mǎi le yī liàng xīn chē.
He bought a new car.

**我认识了一个新朋友。**
Wǒ rènshi le yīgè xīn péngyou.
I made a new friend.

**这本书是新的。**
Zhè běn shū shì xīn de.
This book is new.

**新年快乐！**
Xīnnián kuàilè!
Happy New Year!

## Memory Hook

Antonym of 旧 (jiù, old/worn). 新 has the 斤 (axe) radical and 木 (wood/tree) — a freshly cut tree is new timber. Something just made is 新.
