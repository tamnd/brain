## 做 (zuò)

做 is a general-purpose verb meaning to do, to make, or to perform an activity. It is one of the most versatile verbs in Chinese and appears in countless everyday phrases.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To do (an activity or task).
2. **[verb]** To make (a physical object or food).
3. **[verb]** To work as, to be (in a profession): 做老师 (be a teacher).

## Example Sentences

**你在做什么？**
Nǐ zài zuò shénme?
What are you doing?

**妈妈在做饭。**
Māma zài zuòfàn.
Mom is cooking (making a meal).

**他做了一个很好的决定。**
Tā zuò le yī gè hěn hǎo de juédìng.
He made a very good decision.

**她做老师已经十年了。**
Tā zuò lǎoshī yǐjīng shí nián le.
She has been a teacher for ten years already.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 做饭 | cook a meal |
| 做作业 | do homework |
| 做事 | do things, handle matters |
| 做梦 | dream (have a dream) |
| 做到 | accomplish, manage to do |
| 做生意 | do business |

## Memory Hook

做 has the person radical (亻) on the left, and a complex right side. A person performs an action: that is doing or making.
