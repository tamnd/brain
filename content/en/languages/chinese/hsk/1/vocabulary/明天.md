## 明天 (míngtiān)

明天 refers to tomorrow, the day after today. It functions as both a time noun and a time adverb, placed before the main verb or at the start of a sentence.

## Meanings

1. **[noun/adverb]** Tomorrow, the next day.

## Example Sentences

**明天我有一个重要的会议。**
Míngtiān wǒ yǒu yī gè zhòngyào de huìyì.
Tomorrow I have an important meeting.

**你明天去哪里？**
Nǐ míngtiān qù nǎlǐ?
Where are you going tomorrow?

**明天天气怎么样？**
Míngtiān tiānqì zěnmeyàng?
How is the weather tomorrow?

**我们明天见！**
Wǒmen míngtiān jiàn!
See you tomorrow!

## Memory Hook

明 means "bright" and 天 means "sky/day." The next day is the bright new sky ahead of you.
