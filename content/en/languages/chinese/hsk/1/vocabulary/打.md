## 打 (dǎ)

A highly productive verb whose core meaning is "to strike" or "to hit." From this core sense, 打 extends to many everyday actions: making a phone call (打电话), playing ball sports (打球), fighting (打人), and more. Whenever an action involves a striking, hitting, or forceful contact motion, 打 is often the right verb.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To hit, to strike, to beat.
2. **[verb]** To play (ball sports that involve striking: basketball, tennis, etc.).
3. **[verb]** Component of many verb phrases: 打电话 (to call), 打车 (to take a taxi), 打针 (to give/get an injection).

## Example Sentences

**不可以打人，那是不对的。**
Bù kěyǐ dǎ rén, nà shì bù duì de.
You must not hit people, that is wrong.

**他喜欢打篮球。**
Tā xǐhuān dǎ lánqiú.
He likes to play basketball.

**我要给朋友打电话。**
Wǒ yào gěi péngyou dǎ diànhuà.
I am going to call my friend.

**我们打车去吧，走路太远了。**
Wǒmen dǎchē qù ba, zǒulù tài yuǎn le.
Let's take a taxi, walking is too far.

## Memory Hook

打 is the "Swiss Army knife" of action verbs. The 手 (hand) radical on the left tells you the action involves hands — hitting, dialing, playing, grabbing.
