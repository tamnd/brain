## 站 (zhàn)

A word used both as a verb meaning "to stand" and as a noun meaning a bus stop or train station. In everyday speech, both uses are frequent.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To stand, to be standing.
2. **[noun]** A stop, a station (for buses, trains, metro).

## Example Sentences

**老师让我们站起来。**
Lǎoshī ràng wǒmen zhàn qǐlái.
The teacher asked us to stand up.

**下一站是火车站。**
Xià yī zhàn shì huǒchēzhàn.
The next stop is the train station.

**他站在门口等我。**
Tā zhàn zài ménkǒu děng wǒ.
He is standing at the door waiting for me.

**公共汽车站在哪里？**
Gōnggòng qìchēzhàn zài nǎlǐ?
Where is the bus stop?

## Memory Hook

站 sounds like "jan" — imagine a soldier standing at attention at a station: straight, upright, waiting.
