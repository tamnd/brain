## 它 (tā)

The third-person singular pronoun for animals, objects, and abstract things; it sounds identical to 他 (he) and 她 (she) — all pronounced tā — but is written distinctly for clarity in text.

## Meanings

1. **[pronoun]** It (subject or object, referring to non-human entities).
2. **[pronoun]** Its (before 的: 它的).

## Example Sentences

**这只猫很可爱，它叫小白。**
Zhè zhī māo hěn kě'ài, tā jiào Xiǎo Bái.
This cat is very cute; it is called Xiao Bai.

**我有一辆自行车，它是红色的。**
Wǒ yǒu yī liàng zìxíngchē, tā shì hóngsè de.
I have a bicycle; it is red.

**这个问题很难，你怎么看它？**
Zhège wèntí hěn nán, nǐ zěnme kàn tā?
This problem is very difficult. How do you see it?

**它的颜色很漂亮。**
Tā de yánsè hěn piàoliang.
Its color is very beautiful.

## Memory Hook

它 has the "roof" radical 宀 on top and 匕 below, looking like something sitting under a roof — an object in a house. Non-human, so it gets its own character.
