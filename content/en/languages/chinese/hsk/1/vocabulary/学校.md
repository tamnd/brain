## 学校 (xuéxiào)

A noun meaning school or educational institution. It is a general term that covers primary schools, middle schools, high schools, and universities. It combines 学 (learn/study) and 校 (school, institution).

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** School, educational institution (general term).

## Example Sentences

**我的学校离家很近。**
Wǒ de xuéxiào lí jiā hěn jìn.
My school is very close to home.

**她在一所大学校工作。**
Tā zài yī suǒ dà xuéxiào gōngzuò.
She works at a large school.

**学校九月开学。**
Xuéxiào jiǔ yuè kāixué.
School starts in September.

**放学以后，孩子们都去操场玩。**
Fàngxué yǐhòu, háizimen dōu qù cāochǎng wán.
After school, all the children go to the playground.

## Memory Hook

学 (study) plus 校 (institution). A school is an institution where studying happens. The measure word for schools is 所 (suǒ): 一所学校 (one school).
