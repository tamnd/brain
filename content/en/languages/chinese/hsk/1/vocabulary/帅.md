## 帅 (shuài)

An adjective meaning handsome or cool, most commonly used to describe men. In casual modern Chinese, it is also used to express that something looks stylish or impressive.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** Handsome, good-looking (typically for men).
2. **[adjective]** Cool, stylish, impressive (informal, modern usage).

## Example Sentences

**那个男生真帅！**
Nàge nánshēng zhēn shuài!
That guy is really handsome!

**你今天穿这件衣服很帅。**
Nǐ jīntiān chuān zhè jiàn yīfu hěn shuài.
You look very stylish in that outfit today.

**他的动作太帅了！**
Tā de dòngzuò tài shuài le!
His moves are so cool!

**大家都说他很帅，但他自己不觉得。**
Dàjiā dōu shuō tā hěn shuài, dàn tā zìjǐ bù juéde.
Everyone says he is handsome, but he does not think so himself.
