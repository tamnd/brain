## 上午 (shàngwǔ)

上午 refers to the morning or forenoon, generally the time from around 8 a.m. to noon. 上 means "above/upper" and 午 means "noon," so it literally is "before noon."

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Morning, forenoon (approximately 8:00 to 12:00).

## Example Sentences

**上午我在家学习。**
Shàngwǔ wǒ zài jiā xuéxí.
In the morning I study at home.

**他上午九点有课。**
Tā shàngwǔ jiǔ diǎn yǒu kè.
He has class at nine in the morning.

**今天上午天气很好。**
Jīntiān shàngwǔ tiānqì hěn hǎo.
The weather this morning is very nice.

**我们上午十点出发。**
Wǒmen shàngwǔ shí diǎn chūfā.
We set off at ten in the morning.

## Memory Hook

上 (above) + 午 (noon): the time that sits above noon, before it arrives.
