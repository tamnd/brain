## 想 (xiǎng)

想 covers three related meanings depending on context. Before another verb it means "want to" or "would like to." Alone or with a topic it means "to think" or "to believe." Before a person or place it means "to miss" (to long for someone or somewhere).

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To want to, would like to (before another verb).
2. **[verb]** To think, to consider, to have a thought.
3. **[verb]** To miss, to long for (someone or somewhere).

## Example Sentences

**我想喝一杯热茶。**
Wǒ xiǎng hē yī bēi rè chá.
I would like to drink a cup of hot tea.

**我想，这个方法很好。**
Wǒ xiǎng, zhège fāngfǎ hěn hǎo.
I think this method is very good.

**我很想我的妈妈。**
Wǒ hěn xiǎng wǒ de māma.
I really miss my mother.

**你在想什么？**
Nǐ zài xiǎng shénme?
What are you thinking about?

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 想要 | want to have/do |
| 想到 | think of, come to mind |
| 想起 | recall, remember |
| 想一想 | think about it |
| 想念 | miss (someone), long for |
| 不想 | do not want to |

## Memory Hook

想 has heart (心) at the bottom and "tree/appearance" above. Longing and thinking both come from the heart looking toward something out of reach.
