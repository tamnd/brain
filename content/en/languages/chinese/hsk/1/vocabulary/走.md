## 走 (zǒu)

A common verb meaning "to walk" when describing the manner of movement, and "to leave" or "to go" when describing departure. Context determines which meaning is intended.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To walk, to go on foot.
2. **[verb]** To leave, to depart, to go away.

## Example Sentences

**我每天走路上学。**
Wǒ měitiān zǒulù shàngxué.
I walk to school every day.

**他走了，你来晚了。**
Tā zǒu le, nǐ lái wǎn le.
He has left, you came too late.

**我们走吧，时间不多了。**
Wǒmen zǒu ba, shíjiān bù duō le.
Let's go, there isn't much time.

**她慢慢地走进了图书馆。**
Tā mànmàn de zǒu jìn le túshūguǎn.
She walked slowly into the library.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|

## Memory Hook

走 looks like a person with legs in motion. The top stroke is a person leaning forward, legs striding — always moving, never still.
