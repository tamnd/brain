## 吃 (chī)

吃 is the standard verb for eating. It takes food as its object and appears in some of the most common daily phrases in Chinese.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To eat, to consume food.

## Example Sentences

**你吃早饭了吗？**
Nǐ chī zǎofàn le ma?
Have you eaten breakfast?

**我想吃饺子。**
Wǒ xiǎng chī jiǎozi.
I want to eat dumplings.

**他每天中午在公司吃饭。**
Tā měitiān zhōngwǔ zài gōngsī chīfàn.
He eats lunch at the company every day at noon.

**我们去吃中国菜吧。**
Wǒmen qù chī Zhōngguó cài ba.
Let's go eat Chinese food.

## Memory Hook

吃 has the mouth radical (口) on the left, because you use your mouth to eat. The right side (乞) sounds like the word and reinforces the action.
