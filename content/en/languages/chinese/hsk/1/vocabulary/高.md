## 高 (gāo)

An adjective meaning "tall" or "high." It describes both physical height (of a person or building) and elevated level or degree (high score, high price). Its antonym is 矮 (ǎi), meaning "short" (in height).

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** Tall, high (in physical height).
2. **[adjective]** High (in level, score, degree, or quality).

## Example Sentences

**他很高，有一米八。**
Tā hěn gāo, yǒu yī mǐ bā.
He is very tall, 1.8 meters.

**这座楼很高。**
Zhè zuò lóu hěn gāo.
This building is very tall.

**今天气温很高。**
Jīntiān qìwēn hěn gāo.
The temperature today is very high.

**她的成绩非常高。**
Tā de chéngjì fēicháng gāo.
Her grades are very high.

Antonym of 矮 (ǎi, short). The character 高 looks like a tall tower or pagoda with layers stacked up — rising high above the ground.
