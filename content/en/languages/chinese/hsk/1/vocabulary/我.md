## 我 (wǒ)

The first-person singular pronoun meaning "I" as a subject and "me" as an object; it is one of the most frequent words in Mandarin.

## Meanings

1. **[pronoun]** I (subject).
2. **[pronoun]** Me (object).
3. **[pronoun]** My (before 的, meaning possession: 我的).

## Example Sentences

**我是学生。**
Wǒ shì xuésheng.
I am a student.

**你认识我吗？**
Nǐ rènshi wǒ ma?
Do you know me?

**我的书在桌子上。**
Wǒ de shū zài zhuōzi shàng.
My book is on the table.

**我今天很高兴。**
Wǒ jīntiān hěn gāoxìng.
I am very happy today.

## Collocations

## Memory Hook

The character 我 shows a hand holding a halberd weapon, as if pointing to yourself and saying "this is mine — I did this."
