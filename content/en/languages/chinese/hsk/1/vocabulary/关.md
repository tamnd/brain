## 关 (guān)

A verb meaning "to close," "to shut," or "to turn off." It is the action that stops or seals something that was previously open or active. Its antonym is 开 (kāi), meaning "to open" or "to turn on."

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To close, to shut (a door, window).
2. **[verb]** To turn off (a light, TV, computer).

## Example Sentences

**请关门，外面很冷。**
Qǐng guān mén, wàimiàn hěn lěng.
Please close the door, it is very cold outside.

**睡觉前关灯。**
Shuìjiào qián guān dēng.
Turn off the light before sleeping.

**你关电视了吗？**
Nǐ guān diànshì le ma?
Did you turn off the TV?

**把窗户关上，要下雨了。**
Bǎ chuānghù guān shàng, yào xià yǔ le.
Close the window, it is going to rain.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 关门 | to close the door |
| 关灯 | to turn off the light |
| 关掉 | to shut off, to close completely |
| 关上 | to close (result: door is now shut) |
| 关心 | to care about, to show concern |
| 关系 | relationship, connection |

## Memory Hook

Antonym of 开 (kāi, to open/turn on). When the day ends, you 关灯 (turn off the light) and 关门 (close the door). 关 shuts everything down.
