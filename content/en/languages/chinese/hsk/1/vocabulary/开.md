## 开 (kāi)

A versatile verb meaning "to open," "to turn on" (a device or light), and "to drive" (a vehicle). It covers the general idea of activating or opening something. Its antonym is 关 (guān), meaning "to close" or "to turn off."

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To open (a door, window, a book).
2. **[verb]** To turn on (a light, TV, appliance).
3. **[verb]** To drive (a car, vehicle).

## Example Sentences

**请开门。**
Qǐng kāi mén.
Please open the door.

**把电视开一下。**
Bǎ diànshì kāi yīxià.
Turn on the TV for a moment.

**他会开车。**
Tā huì kāichē.
He knows how to drive.

**请把窗户开开，空气不好。**
Qǐng bǎ chuānghù kāi kāi, kōngqì bù hǎo.
Please open the window, the air is bad in here.

## Memory Hook

Antonym of 关 (guān, to close/turn off). Think: 开 = "key" — you use a key to open something. Open the door, turn on the lights, start the engine — all with 开.
