## 读 (dú)

读 primarily means to read aloud or to read text, and also means to study or attend school. It differs from 看 (kàn), which means to look at or read silently. 读书 can mean either "read a book" or "go to school/study."

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To read (aloud or with attention).
2. **[verb]** To study, to attend school (in 读书 or 读大学).

## Example Sentences

**请跟我读这个句子。**
Qǐng gēn wǒ dú zhège jùzi.
Please read this sentence after me.

**他在读大学。**
Tā zài dú dàxué.
He is studying at university.

**我每天读一段英语文章。**
Wǒ měitiān dú yī duàn Yīngyǔ wénzhāng.
I read an English passage every day.

**这个字怎么读？**
Zhège zì zěnme dú?
How do you read/pronounce this character?

## Memory Hook

读 has the speech radical (讠) on the left, because reading aloud is producing speech from written text.
