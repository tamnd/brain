## 唱 (chàng)

A verb meaning "to sing." It describes using the voice to produce musical sound, whether in a song, at karaoke, or in a performance. In Chinese culture, karaoke (K歌) is hugely popular, making 唱歌 an everyday phrase.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To sing (a song, a melody).

## Example Sentences

**她唱歌唱得很好听。**
Tā chànggē chàng de hěn hǎotīng.
She sings beautifully.

**我们一起去唱歌吧！**
Wǒmen yīqǐ qù chànggē ba!
Let's go sing together!

**孩子在唱一首儿歌。**
Háizi zài chàng yī shǒu érgē.
The child is singing a nursery rhyme.

**你会唱这首歌吗？**
Nǐ huì chàng zhè shǒu gē ma?
Can you sing this song?

## Memory Hook

唱 has the 口 (mouth) radical on the left — you need your mouth to sing. The right part 昌 (chāng) means prosperous and also sounds close, giving you the pronunciation cue.
