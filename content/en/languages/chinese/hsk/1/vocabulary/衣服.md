## 衣服 (yīfu)

Clothes or clothing in general. 衣 means "garment" and 服 also relates to clothing or attire. Together they form the general word for any type of clothing.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Clothes, clothing. Any garments or items worn to cover the body.

## Example Sentences

**我想买一件衣服。**
Wǒ xiǎng mǎi yī jiàn yīfu.
I want to buy a piece of clothing.

**这件衣服很好看。**
Zhè jiàn yīfu hěn hǎokàn.
This outfit looks very nice.

**你的衣服在哪里？**
Nǐ de yīfu zài nǎlǐ?
Where are your clothes?

**今天天气冷，多穿点衣服。**
Jīntiān tiānqì lěng, duō chuān diǎn yīfu.
The weather is cold today, wear more clothes.

## Memory Hook

衣 is an ancient pictographic character that looks like a garment with a collar and sleeves. 服 means "to serve" or "clothing." Together they reinforce the concept of garments worn for function and presentation. The character 衣 appears as a radical in many clothing-related words.
