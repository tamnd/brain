## 弟弟 (dìdi)

弟弟 means younger brother. It refers specifically to a male sibling who is younger than the speaker.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Younger brother, little brother.

## Example Sentences

**我弟弟今年八岁。**
Wǒ dìdi jīnnián bā suì.
My younger brother is eight years old this year.

**弟弟喜欢玩游戏。**
Dìdi xǐhuān wán yóuxì.
My younger brother likes to play games.

**你有弟弟吗？**
Nǐ yǒu dìdi ma?
Do you have a younger brother?

**我要照顾好我的弟弟。**
Wǒ yào zhàogù hǎo wǒ de dìdi.
I need to take good care of my younger brother.
