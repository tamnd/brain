## 叫 (jiào)

叫 is most commonly used to introduce a name: 我叫... (My name is...). It also means to call out or shout, and to tell or ask someone to do something.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To be called, to have the name of (我叫 wǒ jiào = my name is).
2. **[verb]** To call out, to shout, to cry out.
3. **[verb]** To ask or tell (someone to do something).

## Example Sentences

**我叫李明，你叫什么名字？**
Wǒ jiào Lǐ Míng, nǐ jiào shénme míngzi?
My name is Li Ming. What is your name?

**她在外面叫你。**
Tā zài wàimiàn jiào nǐ.
She is calling for you outside.

**老师叫我们打开书。**
Lǎoshī jiào wǒmen dǎkāi shū.
The teacher told us to open our books.

**那只狗一直在叫。**
Nà zhī gǒu yīzhí zài jiào.
That dog keeps barking (calling out).

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 叫什么名字 | what is (your/his/her) name |
| 叫做 | to be called, known as |
| 大叫 | shout loudly |
| 叫出租车 | call a taxi |
| 叫人 | call someone over |
| 被叫 | be called upon |

## Memory Hook

叫 has the mouth radical (口) on the left, because calling out and naming both happen through the mouth.
