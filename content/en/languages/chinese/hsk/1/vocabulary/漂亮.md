## 漂亮 (piàoliang)

An adjective meaning beautiful or pretty, most naturally used to describe people, especially women. Compare with 好看 (hǎokàn), which means "pleasant to look at" and applies more broadly to objects and scenery. 漂亮 carries a stronger sense of striking beauty and is more personal.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** Beautiful, pretty (primarily for people and their appearance).
2. **[adjective]** Excellent, impressive (for actions or results, informal).

## Example Sentences

**她长得很漂亮。**
Tā zhǎng de hěn piàoliang.
She is very beautiful.

**你今天穿的裙子真漂亮！**
Nǐ jīntiān chuān de qúnzi zhēn piàoliang!
The skirt you are wearing today is really pretty!

**这个汉字写得很漂亮。**
Zhège hànzì xiě de hěn piàoliang.
This Chinese character is written beautifully.

**他打篮球打得很漂亮。**
Tā dǎ lánqiú dǎ de hěn piàoliang.
He plays basketball brilliantly.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |

## Memory Hook

Imagine a spotlight on a stunning person on stage. The whole room turns to look. That kind of striking, personal beauty is 漂亮.
