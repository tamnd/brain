## 她 (tā)

The third-person singular pronoun for females; it is pronounced identically to 他 (he) and 它 (it) — all are tā — so gender is only visible in writing.

## Meanings

1. **[pronoun]** She (subject).
2. **[pronoun]** Her (object).
3. **[pronoun]** Her (before 的: 她的).

## Example Sentences

**她是我的朋友。**
Tā shì wǒ de péngyou.
She is my friend.

**我爱她。**
Wǒ ài tā.
I love her.

**她的中文说得很好。**
Tā de Zhōngwén shuō de hěn hǎo.
She speaks Chinese very well.

**她今天穿了一件红色的衣服。**
Tā jīntiān chuānle yī jiàn hóngsè de yīfu.
She wore a red outfit today.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 她的 (tā de) | her, hers |
| 她们 (tāmen) | they, them (all female) |
| 她是 (tā shì) | she is |
| 她也 (tā yě) | she also |
| 她说 (tā shuō) | she says |
| 告诉她 (gàosu tā) | tell her |

## Memory Hook

她 has the "woman" radical 女 on the left — the same radical you see in 妈 (mom) and 姐 (older sister). Spot the 女 and you know the pronoun is female.
