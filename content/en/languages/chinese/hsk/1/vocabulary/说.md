## 说 (shuō)

说 is the primary verb for speaking, saying, or telling. It is used for talking in general, speaking a language, and introducing direct or indirect speech.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To speak, to say.
2. **[verb]** To tell, to explain.
3. **[verb]** To speak (a language), used with 语/话.

## Example Sentences

**她说中文说得很好。**
Tā shuō Zhōngwén shuō de hěn hǎo.
She speaks Chinese very well.

**他说他不来了。**
Tā shuō tā bù lái le.
He said he is not coming.

**你刚才说什么？**
Nǐ gāngcái shuō shénme?
What did you just say?

**老师说明天有考试。**
Lǎoshī shuō míngtiān yǒu kǎoshì.
The teacher said there is an exam tomorrow.

## Memory Hook

说 has the speech radical (讠) on the left, the universal marker for speaking and language-related verbs in Chinese.
