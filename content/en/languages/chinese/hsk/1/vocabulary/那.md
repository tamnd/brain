## 那 (nà)

A demonstrative pronoun and determiner meaning "that," pointing to people or things away from the speaker; its near counterpart is 这 (zhè, this). Before measure words it is sometimes pronounced nèi in fast speech.

## Meanings

1. **[pronoun]** That (used alone as a subject or object).
2. **[determiner]** That (before a measure word and noun: 那 + measure word + noun).
3. **[conjunction]** Then, in that case (introducing a conclusion: 那就…).

## Example Sentences

**那是谁的车？**
Nà shì shuí de chē?
Whose car is that?

**那个人是我的老师。**
Nàge rén shì wǒ de lǎoshī.
That person is my teacher.

**你不想去，那我自己去。**
Nǐ bù xiǎng qù, nà wǒ zìjǐ qù.
If you don't want to go, then I'll go by myself.

**那些书都是我的。**
Nàxiē shū dōu shì wǒ de.
All those books are mine.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 那个 (nàge) | that one |
| 那里 (nàlǐ) | there |
| 那儿 (nàr) | there (colloquial) |
| 那些 (nàxiē) | those (plural) |
| 那么 (nàme) | then, in that case; so |
| 那次 (nà cì) | that time |

## Memory Hook

那 sounds like "nah" — picture pointing at something far away and saying "nah, not this one, THAT one over there."
