## 老师 (lǎoshī)

A noun meaning teacher. It is also used as a respectful form of address, spoken directly to the teacher just as you might say "Teacher" in English. The word combines 老 (old, experienced) and 师 (master, teacher).

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Teacher, instructor, educator.
2. **[address form]** Used to address a teacher directly (e.g., 王老师, Teacher Wang).

## Example Sentences

**我的汉语老师是中国人。**
Wǒ de Hànyǔ lǎoshī shì Zhōngguó rén.
My Chinese teacher is Chinese.

**老师，我有一个问题。**
Lǎoshī, wǒ yǒu yīgè wèntí.
Teacher, I have a question.

**她想以后当一名老师。**
Tā xiǎng yǐhòu dāng yī míng lǎoshī.
She wants to become a teacher in the future.

**王老师对学生非常好。**
Wáng lǎoshī duì xuésheng fēicháng hǎo.
Teacher Wang treats students very well.

## Memory Hook

老 means old and experienced; 师 means master. A teacher is an experienced master who passes knowledge on. In Chinese culture, teachers are addressed with this respectful title directly by name.
