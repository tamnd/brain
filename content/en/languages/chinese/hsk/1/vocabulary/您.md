## 您 (nín)

The formal, polite form of "you," used when addressing elders, teachers, customers, or anyone deserving extra respect; it always stays singular and has no plural form.

## Meanings

1. **[pronoun]** You (formal/respectful singular).

## Example Sentences

**您好，请问您贵姓？**
Nín hǎo, qǐngwèn nín guìxìng?
Hello, may I ask your surname?

**老师，您辛苦了。**
Lǎoshī, nín xīnkǔ le.
Teacher, you have worked hard.

**您请坐。**
Nín qǐng zuò.
Please have a seat.

**感谢您的帮助。**
Gǎnxiè nín de bāngzhù.
Thank you for your help.

## Memory Hook

您 is 你 with a 心 (heart) beneath it. Treating someone with heart means you respect them — that is when you switch from 你 to 您.
