## 买 (mǎi)

买 means to buy or to purchase. It is the opposite of 卖 (mài, to sell). The two characters look similar: 买 has a cross on top and no person radical below, while 卖 has an extra component on top.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To buy, to purchase.

## Example Sentences

**我要去超市买水果。**
Wǒ yào qù chāoshì mǎi shuǐguǒ.
I want to go to the supermarket to buy fruit.

**她买了一件新衣服。**
Tā mǎi le yī jiàn xīn yīfu.
She bought a new piece of clothing.

**这本书在哪里买？**
Zhè běn shū zài nǎlǐ mǎi?
Where can I buy this book?

**我用手机买了火车票。**
Wǒ yòng shǒujī mǎi le huǒchēpiào.
I bought a train ticket with my phone.

## Memory Hook

买 has a net (罒) over a shell (贝, an ancient currency symbol). You cast a net over money to catch the thing you want to buy.
