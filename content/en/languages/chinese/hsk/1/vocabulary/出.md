## 出 (chū)

A directional verb meaning "to exit" or "to go out" of a place. It describes movement from inside to outside. Its antonym is 进 (jìn), meaning "to enter" or "to go in."

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To exit, to go out, to come out, to leave (a place).

## Example Sentences

**他出去买东西了。**
Tā chūqù mǎi dōngxi le.
He went out to buy things.

**请从这个门出去。**
Qǐng cóng zhège mén chūqù.
Please exit through this door.

**太阳出来了。**
Tàiyáng chūlái le.
The sun has come out.

**她刚出学校就下雨了。**
Tā gāng chū xuéxiào jiù xià yǔ le.
As soon as she left school it started raining.

## Memory Hook

Antonym of 进 (jìn, to enter). The character 出 looks like two mountains stacked — something rising up and coming out of the ground.
