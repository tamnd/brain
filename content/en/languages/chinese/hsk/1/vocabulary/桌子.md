## 桌子 (zhuōzi)

A noun meaning table or desk. The 子 (zi) suffix is a common neutral-tone suffix added to many household nouns. 桌子 can refer to a dining table, a desk, or any flat-surface table.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Table, desk.

## Example Sentences

**书放在桌子上。**
Shū fàng zài zhuōzi shàng.
The book is on the table.

**这张桌子是木头做的。**
Zhè zhāng zhuōzi shì mùtou zuò de.
This table is made of wood.

**请把桌子擦干净。**
Qǐng bǎ zhuōzi cā gānjìng.
Please wipe the table clean.

**教室里有二十张桌子和二十把椅子。**
Jiàoshì lǐ yǒu èrshí zhāng zhuōzi hé èrshí bǎ yǐzi.
There are twenty tables and twenty chairs in the classroom.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |

## Memory Hook

The measure word for tables (and other flat surfaces) is 张 (zhāng). Practice saying 一张桌子 (one table) to lock in both the word and its measure word together.
