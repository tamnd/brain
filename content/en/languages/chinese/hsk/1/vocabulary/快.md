## 快 (kuài)

An adjective and adverb meaning "fast," "quick," or "soon." As an adjective, it describes speed. As an adverb before a verb, it means "quickly" or "hurry up." Its antonym is 慢 (màn), meaning "slow."

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** Fast, quick, speedy.
2. **[adverb]** Quickly, soon (describing how something happens or urging speed).

## Example Sentences

**他跑得很快。**
Tā pǎo de hěn kuài.
He runs very fast.

**快点，要迟到了！**
Kuài diǎn, yào chídào le!
Hurry up, we are going to be late!

**这辆车开得很快。**
Zhè liàng chē kāi de hěn kuài.
This car drives very fast.

**快到了，还有两站。**
Kuài dào le, hái yǒu liǎng zhàn.
Almost there, just two more stops.

## Collocations

## Memory Hook

Antonym of 慢 (màn, slow). 快 has the 心 (heart) radical — when your heart races, you move fast. A racing heart means urgency and speed.
