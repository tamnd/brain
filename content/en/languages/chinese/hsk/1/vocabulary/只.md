## 只 (zhī)

A measure word used for animals and for one of a pair of objects. 只 is pronounced zhī when used as a measure word. It is the standard classifier for most animals, including pets.

## Meanings

1. **[measure word]** Classifier for animals (most types).
2. **[measure word]** Classifier for one of a pair (one hand, one ear, one shoe).

## What 只 counts

只 is used with:

- 猫 (māo): cat — 一只猫 (one cat)
- 狗 (gǒu): dog — 一只狗 (one dog)
- 鸟 (niǎo): bird — 一只鸟 (one bird)
- 手 (shǒu): hand — 一只手 (one hand)
- 鞋 (xié): shoe — 一只鞋 (one shoe, from a pair)

## Example Sentences

**我家有一只猫。**
Wǒ jiā yǒu yī zhī māo.
My family has one cat.

**公园里有几只狗。**
Gōngyuán lǐ yǒu jǐ zhī gǒu.
There are several dogs in the park.

**树上有一只鸟。**
Shù shàng yǒu yī zhī niǎo.
There is a bird in the tree.

**她养了两只猫。**
Tā yǎngle liǎng zhī māo.
She keeps two cats.

## Common Learner Mistake

Do not use 个 for animals. Saying 一个猫 is incorrect. Use 只 for all common animals. Also note that large animals like horses and cows sometimes use 匹 (pǐ) and 头 (tóu) respectively, but 只 works for most everyday animals including cats, dogs, birds, and fish.
