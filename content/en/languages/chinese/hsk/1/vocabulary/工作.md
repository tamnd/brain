## 工作 (gōngzuò)

工作 functions as both a noun meaning "work" or "job" and a verb meaning "to work." As a noun it can be modified and measured; as a verb it describes the act of working.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Work, job, employment.
2. **[verb]** To work, to be employed.

## Example Sentences

**我的工作很有意思。**
Wǒ de gōngzuò hěn yǒu yìsi.
My work is very interesting.

**她在一家银行工作。**
Tā zài yī jiā yínháng gōngzuò.
She works at a bank.

**你找到工作了吗？**
Nǐ zhǎodào gōngzuò le ma?
Have you found a job?

**他每天工作八个小时。**
Tā měitiān gōngzuò bā gè xiǎoshí.
He works eight hours every day.

工 (work/craft) + 作 (make/do): making things through craft is exactly what work is.
