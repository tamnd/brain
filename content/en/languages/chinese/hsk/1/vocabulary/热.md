## 热 (rè)

An adjective meaning "hot" in temperature, whether describing weather, food, beverages, or how a person feels. Its antonym is 冷 (lěng), meaning "cold."

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** Hot (in temperature, for weather, objects, or feelings of heat).

## Example Sentences

**今天天气很热。**
Jīntiān tiānqì hěn rè.
The weather today is very hot.

**这碗汤很热，小心别烫到。**
Zhè wǎn tāng hěn rè, xiǎoxīn bié tàng dào.
This bowl of soup is very hot, be careful not to burn yourself.

**我很热，能开一下空调吗？**
Wǒ hěn rè, néng kāi yīxià kōngtiáo ma?
I am very hot, can you turn on the air conditioning?

**夏天北京非常热。**
Xiàtiān Běijīng fēicháng rè.
Beijing is extremely hot in summer.

## Memory Hook

Antonym of 冷 (lěng, cold). 热 contains the 火 (fire) radical at the bottom — fire makes things hot. When you see 热, picture flames underneath.
