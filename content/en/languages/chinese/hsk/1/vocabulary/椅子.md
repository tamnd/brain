## 椅子 (yǐzi)

A noun meaning chair or seat. Like 桌子 (table), it takes the common 子 (zi) suffix. 椅子 refers to any standard chair with a back. The measure word is 把 (bǎ).

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Chair, seat with a back.

## Example Sentences

**请坐，这把椅子是你的。**
Qǐng zuò, zhè bǎ yǐzi shì nǐ de.
Please sit down, this chair is yours.

**她把椅子搬到窗边。**
Tā bǎ yǐzi bān dào chuāng biān.
She moved the chair to the window.

**这把椅子坐起来很舒服。**
Zhè bǎ yǐzi zuò qǐlái hěn shūfu.
This chair is very comfortable to sit in.

**教室里有多少把椅子？**
Jiàoshì lǐ yǒu duōshao bǎ yǐzi?
How many chairs are in the classroom?

## Memory Hook

The measure word for chairs is 把 (bǎ), the same measure word used for things with handles. A chair has a back you grip, so 把 fits naturally. Say 一把椅子 to remember both chair and its measure word.
