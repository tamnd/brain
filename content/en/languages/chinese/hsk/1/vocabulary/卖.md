## 卖 (mài)

卖 means to sell. It is the opposite of 买 (mǎi, to buy). The two characters look alike. 卖 has an extra element on top of 买, representing the additional layer of "sending goods out" to a buyer.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To sell, to offer for sale.

## Example Sentences

**这家店卖很多种水果。**
Zhè jiā diàn mài hěn duō zhǒng shuǐguǒ.
This store sells many kinds of fruit.

**他想卖掉他的旧车。**
Tā xiǎng mài diào tā de jiù chē.
He wants to sell his old car.

**这里卖不卖咖啡？**
Zhèlǐ mài bù mài kāfēi?
Do they sell coffee here?

**她在网上卖手工艺品。**
Tā zài wǎng shàng mài shǒugōngyìpǐn.
She sells handicrafts online.

## Memory Hook

卖 has an extra cap on top of 买 (buy). The seller is "capping" the buyer: they add a price before handing goods over.
