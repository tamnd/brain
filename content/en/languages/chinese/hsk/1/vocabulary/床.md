## 床 (chuáng)

A noun meaning bed. It is a single-character word with no suffix, one of the few basic furniture words that does not take 子. The measure word for beds is 张 (zhāng), the same used for tables.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Bed, sleeping surface.

## Example Sentences

**我的床很舒服。**
Wǒ de chuáng hěn shūfu.
My bed is very comfortable.

**他累了，去床上躺一躺。**
Tā lèi le, qù chuáng shàng tǎng yi tǎng.
He was tired and went to lie down on the bed.

**床下面有一只猫。**
Chuáng xiàmiàn yǒu yī zhī māo.
There is a cat under the bed.

**请整理一下你的床。**
Qǐng zhěnglǐ yīxià nǐ de chuáng.
Please make your bed.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 床上 (chuáng shàng) | on the bed |
| 上床 (shàng chuáng) | get into bed |
| 下床 (xià chuáng) | get out of bed |
| 一张床 (yī zhāng chuáng) | one bed (张 is the measure word) |
| 床单 (chuángdān) | bed sheet |
| 双人床 (shuāngrén chuáng) | double bed |

## Memory Hook

上床 (get into bed) and 下床 (get out of bed) are the two most natural phrases with 床. Picture the bed as a raised platform you climb up onto and come down from.
