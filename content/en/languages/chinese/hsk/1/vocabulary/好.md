## 好 (hǎo)

One of the most important and versatile words in Mandarin. As an adjective, 好 means "good" or "fine." As an adverb before a verb or adjective, it means "well" or "very." As a standalone response, 好 means "OK," "sure," or "alright." It also appears in many fixed phrases and compounds.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** Good, fine, well (describing things or states).
2. **[adverb]** Well (describing how something is done); very (intensifier before adjectives).
3. **[interjection]** OK, sure, alright (response expressing agreement).

## Example Sentences

**这本书很好。**
Zhè běn shū hěn hǎo.
This book is very good.

**他说汉语说得很好。**
Tā shuō Hànyǔ shuō de hěn hǎo.
He speaks Chinese very well.

**好，我明白了。**
Hǎo, wǒ míngbái le.
OK, I understand now.

**你好吗？我很好，谢谢。**
Nǐ hǎo ma? Wǒ hěn hǎo, xièxiè.
How are you? I am fine, thank you.

## Memory Hook

好 is made of 女 (woman) and 子 (child) — a mother and child together, the picture of something good and harmonious. Everything is good when family is together.
