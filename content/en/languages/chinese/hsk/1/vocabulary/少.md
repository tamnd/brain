## 少 (shǎo)

An adjective meaning "few," "a little," or "not many." It describes small quantities. Its antonym is 多 (duō), meaning "many." Note: 少 (shǎo, few) is different from 小 (xiǎo, small in size).

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** Few, a small number of (for countable things).
2. **[adjective]** A little, a small amount of (for uncountable things).

## Example Sentences

**今天来的人很少。**
Jīntiān lái de rén hěn shǎo.
Very few people came today.

**我只有一点点时间，时间很少。**
Wǒ zhǐ yǒu yīdiǎndiǎn shíjiān, shíjiān hěn shǎo.
I only have a little time, there is very little time.

**你吃得太少了，多吃一点。**
Nǐ chī de tài shǎo le, duō chī yīdiǎn.
You ate too little, eat a bit more.

**这里的雨水很少。**
Zhèlǐ de yǔshuǐ hěn shǎo.
There is very little rainfall here.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 很少 | very few, rarely |
| 少一点 | a little less |
| 多少 | how many, how much |
| 至少 | at least |
| 减少 | to reduce, to decrease |
| 不少 | quite a few, not a few |

## Memory Hook

Antonym of 多 (duō, many). 少 looks like 小 (xiǎo, small) with one stroke removed — even smaller than small means there is very little.
