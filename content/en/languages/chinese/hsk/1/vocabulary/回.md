## 回 (huí)

A verb meaning "to return" or "to go back" to a place you have been before. It always implies returning to a familiar or original location. It also appears in 回答 (huídá, to answer) and 回来 (huílái, to come back).

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To return, to go back, to come back to a place.

## Example Sentences

**我想回家。**
Wǒ xiǎng huí jiā.
I want to go home.

**他昨天回来了。**
Tā zuótiān huílái le.
He came back yesterday.

**你什么时候回学校？**
Nǐ shénme shíhou huí xuéxiào?
When will you go back to school?

**她每天六点回到家。**
Tā měitiān liù diǎn huídào jiā.
She gets home every day at six o'clock.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 回家 | to go home |
| 回来 | to come back |
| 回去 | to go back |
| 回到 | to return to (a place) |
| 回答 | to answer, to reply |
| 回国 | to return to one's home country |

## Memory Hook

The character 回 looks like a square inside a square, like a path that loops back to where it started — just like returning home.
