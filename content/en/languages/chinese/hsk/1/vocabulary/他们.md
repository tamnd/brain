## 他们 (tāmen)

The third-person plural pronoun used for groups of males or mixed-gender groups; for all-female groups, 她们 is preferred in writing, though both are spoken identically as tāmen.

## Meanings

1. **[pronoun]** They (subject, masculine or mixed group).
2. **[pronoun]** Them (object).
3. **[pronoun]** Their (before 的: 他们的).

## Example Sentences

**他们都是我的朋友。**
Tāmen dōu shì wǒ de péngyou.
They are all my friends.

**我不认识他们。**
Wǒ bú rènshi tāmen.
I do not know them.

**他们的学校在哪里？**
Tāmen de xuéxiào zài nǎlǐ?
Where is their school?

**他们昨天去看电影了。**
Tāmen zuótiān qù kàn diànyǐng le.
They went to see a movie yesterday.

## Collocations

## Memory Hook

他们 is just 他 (he) with the crowd suffix 们. Whenever you see 们 attached, multiply that single person into a group of people.
