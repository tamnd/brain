These 15 lessons walk you through every topic tested in HSK 1, the entry-level Chinese proficiency exam. HSK 1 recognises 150 vocabulary words and a handful of core grammar patterns. At this level you are building the foundation: pronouncing tones, reading basic characters, and stringing together simple sentences about everyday life.

Each lesson introduces 12-15 vocabulary items, two or three grammar patterns with worked examples, a realistic dialogue, and exercises you can do with a pen and paper. Work through them in order the first time, then revisit individual lessons when you want to drill a specific pattern.
