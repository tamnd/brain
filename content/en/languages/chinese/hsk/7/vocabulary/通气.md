## 通气 (tōngqì)

通气 in clinical medicine refers to ventilation — the movement of air or respiratory gases into and out of the lungs, whether spontaneous or mechanically assisted. At the C2 level, it encompasses a spectrum from basic bag-mask ventilation to advanced mechanical ventilation strategies in ICU settings. It is a cornerstone concept in respiratory medicine, critical care, anesthesia, and emergency medicine.

## Meanings
1. **verb** to ventilate; to support or facilitate respiratory gas exchange in a patient

## Example Sentences
**急性呼吸窘迫综合征患者须接受肺保护性机械通气策略。** Jíxìng hūxī jǒnpò zōnghé zhèng huànzhě xū jiēshòu fèi bǎohùxìng jīxiè tōngqì cèlüè. Patients with acute respiratory distress syndrome must receive lung-protective mechanical ventilation strategies.

**无创正压通气是慢性阻塞性肺病急性加重期的一线呼吸支持手段。** Wúchuāng zhèng yā tōngqì shì mànxìng zǔsèxìng fèibìng jíxìng jiāzhòng qī de yīxiàn hūxī zhīchí shǒuduàn. Non-invasive positive pressure ventilation is the first-line respiratory support measure during acute exacerbations of COPD.

**通气血流比值失调是导致低氧血症最常见的病理生理机制。** Tōngqì xuèliú bǐzhí shītiáo shì dǎozhì dī yǎng xuèzhèng zuì chángjiàn de bìnglǐ shēnglǐ jīzhì. Ventilation-perfusion mismatch is the most common pathophysiological mechanism leading to hypoxemia.

**俯卧位通气可显著改善重度急性呼吸窘迫综合征患者的氧合状态。** Fǔwòwèi tōngqì kě xiǎnzhù gǎishàn zhòngdù jíxìng hūxī jǒnpò zōnghé zhèng huànzhě de yǎnghé zhuàngtài. Prone positioning ventilation can significantly improve oxygenation in patients with severe ARDS.

**撤机时机的评估需综合考量患者的通气力学和自主呼吸能力。** Chèjī shíjī de pínggū xū zōnghé kǎoliàng huànzhě de tōngqì lìxué hé zìzhǔ hūxī nénglì. Assessment of weaning timing requires comprehensive consideration of the patient's ventilatory mechanics and spontaneous breathing capacity.

**高频振荡通气是常规机械通气失败后的挽救性呼吸支持选择。** Gāopín zhèndàng tōngqì shì chángguī jīxiè tōngqì shībài hòu de wǎnjiù xìng hūxī zhīchí xuǎnzé. High-frequency oscillatory ventilation is a rescue respiratory support option when conventional mechanical ventilation fails.

## Usage Notes
通气 has both a general meaning (airflow/ventilation in buildings) and the precise clinical meaning in respiratory medicine. In clinical contexts it exclusively refers to pulmonary ventilation. Respiratory physiology uses the ventilation-perfusion ratio (通气血流比值, V/Q ratio) as a key index of lung efficiency, and its disruption is the basis for most causes of hypoxemia.

In ICU medicine, 机械通气 (mechanical ventilation) management is a highly specialized skill, and the parameters — tidal volume (潮气量), respiratory rate (呼吸频率), PEEP (呼气末正压) — must be meticulously titrated. Ventilator-induced lung injury (呼吸机相关肺损伤) is a recognized complication requiring lung-protective strategies.

## Memory Hook
通 (to pass through/open) + 气 (air/breath) — "make air pass through": ensuring that air can flow freely through the airways and lungs.
