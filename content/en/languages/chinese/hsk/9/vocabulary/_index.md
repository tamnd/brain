HSK 9 represents mastery-level command of both classical and contemporary Chinese vocabulary. This tier encompasses archaic literary forms, wenyan constructions, and highly nuanced contemporary usage that distinguishes educated native writers. Learners at this level are expected to produce and analyze text with the precision of a native scholar.
