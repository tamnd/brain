## 号召 (hàozhào)

号召 describes the act of publicly calling on people to take action, often for a collective or social purpose.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To call on, to appeal to, to rally, to mobilize.
2. **[noun]** A call, an appeal, a rallying cry.

## Example Sentences

**政府号召全体市民积极参与垃圾分类，共同保护环境。**
Zhèngfǔ hàozhào quántǐ shìmín jījí cānyù lājī fēnlèi, gòngtóng bǎohù huánjìng.
The government called on all citizens to actively participate in waste sorting and protect the environment together.

**这位领导人的号召得到了广大民众的热烈响应。**
Zhè wèi lǐngdǎo rén de hàozhào dédào le guǎngdà mínzhòng de rèliè xiǎngyìng.
This leader's call received an enthusiastic response from the general public.

**组织号召志愿者在节假日期间为独居老人提供陪伴服务。**
Zǔzhī hàozhào zhìyuànzhě zài jiéjiàrì qījiān wèi dújū lǎorén tígōng péibàn fúwù.
The organization called on volunteers to provide companionship services for elderly people living alone during holidays.

**响应号召，数千名工人放下工具，参加了这次示威活动。**
Xiǎngyìng hàozhào, shù qiān míng gōngrén fàngxià gōngjù, cānjiā le zhè cì shìwēi huódòng.
Responding to the call, thousands of workers put down their tools and joined the demonstration.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 发出号召 | to issue a call |
| 响应号召 | to respond to the call |
| 号召力 | rallying power, appeal |
| 积极号召 | to actively call on |
| 广泛号召 | to broadly appeal |

## Usage Notes

号召 is typically used for public, collective action rather than personal requests. The noun 号召力 (rallying power) describes a leader's or cause's ability to motivate people. Compare with 呼吁 (hūyù — to appeal/urge), which is slightly more formal and often used in written or journalistic contexts for earnest appeals on important issues.

## Memory Hook

号 means "to shout a signal/order" and 召 means "to summon" — shouting an order to summon people to your cause is a 号召.
