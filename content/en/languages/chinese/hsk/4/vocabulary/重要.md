## 重要 (zhòngyào)

重要 describes something that carries great weight, consequence, or significance.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** Important; significant; crucial.

## Example Sentences

**健康是最重要的事情。**
Jiànkāng shì zuì zhòngyào de shìqíng.
Health is the most important thing.

**这次会议非常重要，请不要缺席。**
Zhè cì huìyì fēicháng zhòngyào, qǐng bùyào quēxí.
This meeting is very important; please do not be absent.

**学好外语对未来很重要。**
Xué hǎo wàiyǔ duì wèilái hěn zhòngyào.
Learning a foreign language well is very important for the future.

**他在公司里扮演着重要的角色。**
Tā zài gōngsī lǐ bànyǎn zhe zhòngyào de juésè.
He plays an important role in the company.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 重要性 | importance; significance |
| 重要角色 | important role |
| 非常重要 | very important |
| 重要决定 | important decision |
| 起重要作用 | to play an important role |

## Usage Notes

重要 is one of the most versatile adjectives in Chinese. The noun form 重要性 (zhòngyàoxìng) means "importance." The pattern 对...很重要 ("important to/for ...") is extremely common at this level.

## Memory Hook

重 means "heavy/weighty" and 要 means "necessary/want" — something so weighty that everyone needs it is truly important.
