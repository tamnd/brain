## 处理 (chǔlǐ)

处理 covers the full range of managing, resolving, and processing — from everyday problems to technical operations.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To handle, to deal with, to manage, to take care of (a situation or problem).
2. **[verb]** To process, to treat (data, materials, or cases).

## Example Sentences

**她每天都要处理大量的工作邮件，效率非常高。**
Tā měitiān dōu yào chǔlǐ dàliàng de gōngzuò yóujiàn, xiàolǜ fēicháng gāo.
She has to handle a large volume of work emails every day, and her efficiency is very high.

**这起纠纷必须按照法律程序妥善处理，不能私下解决。**
Zhè qǐ jiūfēn bìxū ànzhào fǎlǜ chéngxù tuǒshàn chǔlǐ, bù néng sīxià jiějué.
This dispute must be handled properly through legal procedures and cannot be resolved privately.

**系统正在处理您的请求，请稍候。**
Xìtǒng zhèngzài chǔlǐ nín de qǐngqiú, qǐng shāohòu.
The system is processing your request; please wait a moment.

**管理者处理突发事件的方式，往往能反映出他的领导能力。**
Guǎnlǐzhě chǔlǐ tūfā shìjiàn de fāngshì, wǎngwǎng néng fǎnyìng chū tā de lǐngdǎo nénglì.
The way a manager handles unexpected events often reflects their leadership ability.

## Usage Notes

处理 is versatile and appears in both formal and informal contexts. In technical fields it means "to process" (处理数据); in interpersonal or legal contexts it means "to resolve" or "to handle." The adverb 妥善 (properly/appropriately) is a very common companion: 妥善处理. Avoid using 处理 for simple physical actions — you would not say 处理苹果 to mean "peel an apple."

## Memory Hook

处 means "place/deal with" and 理 means "to manage/order" — putting things in their proper place and ordering them is exactly what 处理 does.
