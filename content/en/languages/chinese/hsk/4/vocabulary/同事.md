## 同事 (tóngshì)

同事 refers to a person who works at the same organization or company as you.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** colleague; coworker; workmate

## Example Sentences

**我的同事们都非常友好。** Wǒ de tóngshìmen dōu fēicháng yǒuhǎo. My colleagues are all very friendly.

**她和同事一起完成了这个项目。** Tā hé tóngshì yīqǐ wánchéngle zhège xiàngmù. She completed this project together with her colleagues.

**我跟我的同事关系很好，经常一起吃午饭。** Wǒ gēn wǒ de tóngshì guānxi hěn hǎo, jīngcháng yīqǐ chī wǔfàn. I have a good relationship with my colleagues and often have lunch together.

**他被同事推荐为今年的优秀员工。** Tā bèi tóngshì tuījiàn wéi jīnnián de yōuxiù yuángōng. He was recommended by his colleagues as this year's outstanding employee.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 老同事 | old colleague (long-time coworker) |
| 同事关系 | relationship with colleagues |
| 前同事 | former colleague |
| 同事之间 | among colleagues |

## Usage Notes

同事 refers specifically to coworkers. It should not be confused with 同学 (classmate) or 朋友 (friend), although colleagues can also become friends. The plural is formed by adding 们: 同事们.

## Memory Hook

同 means "same" and 事 means "work/matter" -- 同事 are people who deal with the same work, i.e., colleagues.
