## 程序 (chéngxù)

程序 has two main senses: in computing, it means a program or piece of software; in general usage, it means a procedure, process, or set of steps to follow. Both meanings are common in formal and everyday Chinese.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** program; a set of instructions for a computer
2. **[noun]** procedure; a sequence of steps or formal process

## Example Sentences

**这个程序运行速度很快。** Zhège chéngxù yùnxíng sùdù hěn kuài. This program runs very fast.

**办理手续需要按照正确的程序来。** Bànlǐ shǒuxù xūyào ànzhào zhèngquè de chéngxù lái. Handling formalities requires following the correct procedure.

**他正在编写一个新的程序。** Tā zhèngzài biānxiě yī gè xīn de chéngxù. He is writing a new program.

**申请签证的程序比较复杂。** Shēnqǐng qiānzhèng de chéngxù bǐjiào fùzá. The procedure for applying for a visa is quite complex.

## Usage Notes

程序 is a versatile word. In tech contexts it typically means "program" (计算机程序). In bureaucratic or formal contexts it means "procedure" or "process" (办事程序, "administrative procedure"). The compound 应用程序 specifically means "application/app." 程序员 (chéngxùyuán) means "programmer," a very common job title.

## Memory Hook

程 (journey/process) + 序 (order/sequence): an ordered journey of steps — a procedure or program.
