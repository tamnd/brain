## 挑战 (tiǎozhàn)

挑战 means a challenge, used both as a noun (a difficult test or task) and as a verb (to challenge someone or something), requiring effort and courage to overcome.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** a challenge; a difficult test; a daunting task
2. **[verb]** to challenge; to confront; to defy

## Example Sentences

**学习一门新语言是一个很大的挑战。** Xuéxí yī mén xīn yǔyán shì yīgè hěn dà de tiǎozhàn. Learning a new language is a great challenge.

**他决定挑战自己，参加马拉松比赛。** Tā juédìng tiǎozhàn zìjǐ, cānjiā mǎlāsōng bǐsài. He decided to challenge himself and participate in a marathon.

**面对挑战，我们要有积极的心态。** Miànduì tiǎozhàn, wǒmen yào yǒu jījí de xīntài. When facing challenges, we need to have a positive mindset.

**这位年轻选手挑战了卫冕冠军。** Zhè wèi niánqīng xuǎnshǒu tiǎozhànle wèimiǎn guànjūn. This young competitor challenged the defending champion.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 迎接挑战 | to meet/embrace a challenge |
| 面临挑战 | to face challenges |
| 挑战自我 | to challenge oneself |
| 严峻挑战 | severe challenges |

## Usage Notes

挑战 can be used positively (welcoming a challenge) or to describe a serious obstacle. The phrase 迎接挑战 (to embrace/meet a challenge) is very positive in tone. As a verb, 挑战 can take a person or a situation as its object. It is common in motivational, sports, and business contexts.

## Memory Hook

挑 means "to pick up/carry" and 战 means "battle." So 挑战 is to pick up a battle: like picking up a gauntlet that has been thrown at your feet.
