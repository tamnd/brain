## 创新 (chuàngxīn)

创新 means to innovate or bring about innovation, referring to the creation of new ideas, methods, or products that improve on what exists.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to innovate, to create new approaches or ideas
2. **[noun]** innovation, a new idea or method that represents an improvement

## Example Sentences

**创新是推动社会进步的重要力量。** Chuàngxīn shì tuīdòng shèhuì jìnbù de zhòngyào lìliàng. Innovation is an important force driving social progress.

**这家公司鼓励员工不断创新。** Zhè jiā gōngsī gǔlì yuángōng búduàn chuàngxīn. This company encourages employees to continuously innovate.

**技术创新改变了我们的日常生活。** Jìshù chuàngxīn gǎibiàn le wǒmen de rìcháng shēnghuó. Technological innovation has changed our daily life.

**没有创新精神，企业很难在竞争中生存。** Méiyǒu chuàngxīn jīngshén, qǐyè hěn nán zài jìngzhēng zhōng shēngcún. Without an innovative spirit, companies find it hard to survive in competition.

## Usage Notes

创新 is a high-frequency word in modern Chinese, especially in business, education, and policy discourse. It is often used as a noun in phrases like 技术创新 (technological innovation) or 创新精神 (spirit of innovation). The word carries a strongly positive connotation in Chinese society and media.

## Memory Hook

创 means "to create" and 新 means "new," so 创新 is simply "creating something new," the essence of innovation.
