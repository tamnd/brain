## 评估 (pínggū)

评估 means to evaluate or assess something, or refers to an evaluation or assessment process.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to evaluate; to assess; to appraise
2. **[noun]** evaluation; assessment; appraisal

## Example Sentences

**公司每年对员工进行一次评估。** Gōngsī měi nián duì yuángōng jìnxíng yīcì pínggū. The company conducts an annual evaluation of employees.

**我们需要认真评估这个计划的风险。** Wǒmen xūyào rènzhēn pínggū zhège jìhuà de fēngxiǎn. We need to carefully evaluate the risks of this plan.

**这次项目评估的结果非常理想。** Zhè cì xiàngmù pínggū de jiéguǒ fēicháng lǐxiǎng. The results of this project evaluation were very satisfactory.

**医生对病人的健康状况做了全面评估。** Yīshēng duì bìngrén de jiànkāng zhuàngkuàng zuò le quánmiàn pínggū. The doctor conducted a comprehensive assessment of the patient's health condition.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 进行评估 (jìnxíng pínggū) | to conduct an evaluation |
| 风险评估 (fēngxiǎn pínggū) | risk assessment |
| 绩效评估 (jìxiào pínggū) | performance evaluation |
| 评估结果 (pínggū jiéguǒ) | evaluation results |

## Usage Notes

评估 is a formal term used in professional, academic, and medical contexts. It is more systematic and comprehensive than 评价 (píngjià), which means "to comment on" or "to evaluate" in a more general sense. 评估 often implies a structured process with measurable criteria.

## Memory Hook

评 (to comment/judge) + 估 (to estimate/appraise) — to comment and estimate the value of something is to evaluate it.
