## 选择 (xuǎnzé)

选择 means "to choose," "to select," or "choice" and refers to the act of picking from available options, or the option itself.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to choose; to select; to pick
2. **[noun]** choice; option; selection

## Example Sentences

**你有权利选择自己的生活方式。** Nǐ yǒu quánlì xuǎnzé zìjǐ de shēnghuó fāngshì. You have the right to choose your own way of life.

**这道题有四个选择，只能选一个。** Zhè dào tí yǒu sì gè xuǎnzé, zhǐ néng xuǎn yīgè. This question has four choices; you can only choose one.

**他选择了继续深造而不是工作。** Tā xuǎnzé le jìxù shēnzào ér bùshì gōngzuò. He chose to continue his studies rather than work.

**面对困难，我们要做出正确的选择。** Miànduì kùnnán, wǒmen yào zuòchū zhèngquè de xuǎnzé. When facing difficulties, we must make the right choices.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 做出选择 | to make a choice |
| 自由选择 | free choice |
| 多种选择 | multiple options |
| 正确选择 | right choice |

## Usage Notes

选择 is used both as a verb (to choose) and a noun (a choice or option). It is more deliberate and thoughtful than 挑 (tiāo, to pick casually). When making decisions in formal contexts, 做出选择 (to make a choice) is the natural expression. In test-taking contexts, 选择题 (xuǎnzé tí) means "multiple-choice question."

## Memory Hook

选 (xuǎn) means "to select or elect" and 择 (zé) also means "to choose or pick": both characters reinforce the meaning of choosing, making it easy to remember.
