## 总体 (zǒngtǐ)

总体 means overall or in general, used to describe something from a comprehensive, whole-picture perspective rather than focusing on individual parts.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** overall; general; comprehensive
2. **[adverb]** on the whole; in general; overall

## Example Sentences

**总体来说，这次活动非常成功。** Zǒngtǐ lái shuō, zhè cì huódòng fēicháng chénggōng. Overall, this event was very successful.

**公司总体表现不错，但还有改进的空间。** Gōngsī zǒngtǐ biǎoxiàn bùcuò, dàn hái yǒu gǎijìn de kōngjiān. The company's overall performance is good, but there is still room for improvement.

**这个计划的总体方向是正确的。** Zhège jìhuà de zǒngtǐ fāngxiàng shì zhèngquè de. The overall direction of this plan is correct.

**总体上，今年的经济情况比去年好。** Zǒngtǐ shàng, jīnnián de jīngjì qíngkuàng bǐ qùnián hǎo. On the whole, this year's economic situation is better than last year's.

## Usage Notes

总体 is often used at the beginning of a sentence in the phrases 总体来说 or 总体上 to summarize or give a general assessment. It is slightly more formal than 总的来说 and is common in reports, presentations, and formal writing. It focuses on the whole picture rather than details.

## Memory Hook

总 means "overall/total" and 体 means "body/entity." So 总体 is the "total body": looking at everything as one complete whole.
