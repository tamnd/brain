## 拥有 (yōngyǒu)

拥有 describes the possession of something — typically something valuable, significant, or abstract — and carries more weight than simply 有 (to have).

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To possess, to own, to have (especially something of value or significance).

## Example Sentences

**这座城市拥有数百年的历史，保存着大量珍贵的文化遗址。**
Zhè zuò chéngshì yōngyǒu shù bǎi nián de lìshǐ, bǎocún zhe dàliàng zhēnguì de wénhuà yízhǐ.
This city possesses hundreds of years of history and preserves numerous precious cultural sites.

**她凭借出色的能力，在三十岁之前就拥有了自己的企业。**
Tā píngjiè chūsè de nénglì, zài sānshí suì zhīqián jiù yōngyǒu le zìjǐ de qǐyè.
Drawing on her outstanding ability, she owned her own business before the age of thirty.

**拥有良好的人际关系，往往比拥有大量财富更能带给人幸福感。**
Yōngyǒu liánghǎo de rénjì guānxi, wǎngwǎng bǐ yōngyǒu dàliàng cáifù gèng néng dài gěi rén xìngfúgǎn.
Possessing good interpersonal relationships often brings a greater sense of happiness than owning a great deal of wealth.

**这家科技公司拥有超过三千项核心技术专利，在行业内具有领先地位。**
Zhè jiā kējì gōngsī yōngyǒu chāoguò sān qiān xiàng héxīn jìshù zhuānlì, zài hángyè nèi jùyǒu lǐngxiān dìwèi.
This technology company holds over three thousand core technology patents and occupies a leading position in the industry.

## Usage Notes

拥有 is more emphatic and literary than 有. You would say 我有一本书 (I have a book) casually, but 他拥有丰富的经验 (he possesses rich experience) for more weight. 拥有 is particularly suited to abstract or valuable things: talent, land, wealth, technology. It appears frequently in news writing, formal descriptions, and inspiring speeches.

## Memory Hook

拥 means "to embrace/hold close" and 有 means "to have" — holding something close in your arms is the most intimate form of possession.
