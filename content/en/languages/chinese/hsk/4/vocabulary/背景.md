## 背景 (bèijǐng)

背景 means "background" or "context" and can refer to the visual background of a scene, the historical or social context of an event, or a person's personal background.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** background; backdrop (visual)
2. **[noun]** context; background (of an event or situation)
3. **[noun]** personal background; connections; backing

## Example Sentences

**这张照片的背景非常漂亮。** Zhè zhāng zhàopiàn de bèijǐng fēicháng piàoliang. The background of this photo is very beautiful.

**了解历史背景有助于理解这部电影。** Liǎojiě lìshǐ bèijǐng yǒuzhù yú lǐjiě zhè bù diànyǐng. Understanding the historical background helps to understand this film.

**她有很好的教育背景。** Tā yǒu hěn hǎo de jiàoyù bèijǐng. She has a very good educational background.

**在这个社会背景下，这个问题更加复杂。** Zài zhège shèhuì bèijǐng xià, zhège wèntí gèngjiā fùzá. In this social context, the issue is even more complex.

## Usage Notes

背景 is versatile and used across many domains. In photography and design it refers to a visual backdrop. In history or literature, it sets the context. In HR or social settings it refers to a person's background or connections. The third meaning (personal connections or "backing") can carry a slightly informal or political tone.

## Memory Hook

背 (bèi) means "back" and 景 (jǐng) means "scenery": the "scenery at the back" is the background.
