## 节省 (jiéshěng)

节省 means "to save" or "to economize" and refers to using resources such as money, time, or energy carefully to avoid waste.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to save; to economize; to be frugal with
2. **[adjective]** economical; thrifty

## Example Sentences

**坐地铁可以节省很多时间。** Zuò dìtiě kěyǐ jiéshěng hěn duō shíjiān. Taking the subway can save a lot of time.

**他养成了节省的好习惯。** Tā yǎngchéng le jiéshěng de hǎo xíguàn. He developed the good habit of being economical.

**我们应该节省用水，保护环境。** Wǒmen yīnggāi jiéshěng yòng shuǐ, bǎohù huánjìng. We should save water and protect the environment.

**这个方法可以节省很多费用。** Zhège fāngfǎ kěyǐ jiéshěng hěn duō fèiyòng. This method can save a lot of cost.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 节省时间 | to save time |
| 节省费用 | to save costs |
| 节省资源 | to save resources |
| 节省能源 | to conserve energy |

## Usage Notes

节省 focuses on using less of something to avoid waste, and can be applied to time, money, energy, or resources. It is slightly more formal than 省 alone. The related word 节约 (jiéyuē) is nearly synonymous but is more common in official or environmental contexts.

## Memory Hook

节 (jié) means "to conserve or restrain" and 省 (shěng) means "to save": together they reinforce the idea of saving by restraint.
