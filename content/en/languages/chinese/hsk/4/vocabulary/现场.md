## 现场 (xiànchǎng)

现场 refers to the actual scene or site where an event is happening or has happened, often meaning "on the spot" or "live."

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** site; scene; the actual location where something is occurring or occurred

## Example Sentences

**警察赶到了案件现场进行调查。** Jǐngchá gǎn dào le ànjiàn xiànchǎng jìnxíng diàochá. The police rushed to the crime scene to investigate.

**现场观众对演出反应非常热烈。** Xiànchǎng guānzhòng duì yǎnchū fǎnyìng fēicháng rèliè. The live audience responded very enthusiastically to the performance.

**我们需要到工作现场了解实际情况。** Wǒmen xūyào dào gōngzuò xiànchǎng liǎojiě shíjì qíngkuàng. We need to go to the work site to understand the actual situation.

**这次活动提供现场直播服务。** Zhè cì huódòng tígōng xiànchǎng zhíbō fúwù. This event offers a live broadcast service.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 现场直播 | live broadcast |
| 犯罪现场 | crime scene |
| 现场观众 | live audience |
| 到现场 | to go to the scene/site |

## Usage Notes

现场 emphasizes that you are physically at the actual location of an event, whether a crime scene, a construction site, or a concert venue. The phrase 现场直播 (live broadcast) is very common in media contexts. It differs from 地点 (location) by stressing the immediacy and physical presence.

## Memory Hook

现 means "present/current" and 场 means "place/venue": 现场 is the place where things are currently happening, the live scene.
