## 标准 (biāozhǔn)

标准 refers to a standard, criterion, or benchmark used to evaluate whether something meets a required level. As an adjective it means "standard" or "up to standard." It is used in quality control, language learning, and all situations where norms need to be established.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Standard; criterion; benchmark; norm.
2. **[adjective]** Standard; up to standard; correct.

## Example Sentences

**这家工厂生产的产品完全符合国家质量标准。**
Zhè jiā gōngchǎng shēngchǎn de chǎnpǐn wánquán fúhé guójiā zhìliàng biāozhǔn.
The products made by this factory fully comply with national quality standards.

**她的普通话发音非常标准，听起来像专业播音员。**
Tā de pǔtōnghuà fāyīn fēicháng biāozhǔn, tīng qǐlái xiàng zhuānyè bōyīn yuán.
Her Mandarin pronunciation is very standard; she sounds like a professional broadcaster.

**每个人对成功的标准都不一样，这是非常正常的。**
Měi gèrén duì chénggōng de biāozhǔn dōu bù yīyàng, zhè shì fēicháng zhèngcháng de.
Everyone has a different standard for success, and that is completely normal.

**申请这个职位，候选人需要达到一定的学历和经验标准。**
Shēnqǐng zhège zhíwèi, hòuxuǎn rén xūyào dádào yīdìng de xuélì hé jīngyàn biāozhǔn.
To apply for this position, candidates need to meet certain educational and experience standards.

## Usage Notes

标准 can function as both a noun and an attributive adjective: 标准答案 (standard answer), 标准时间 (standard time), 标准普通话 (standard Mandarin). When used as a predicate adjective, it usually takes 非常/很: 她的发音很标准 (Her pronunciation is very standard). In language learning, 标准 pronunciation is aspirational — it refers to the neutral, prestige form of Mandarin Chinese.

## Memory Hook

标 means "a marking pole/sign" and 准 means "accurate/on target." A standard is a marking pole you shoot at to know if your aim is accurate — the benchmark everything is measured against.
