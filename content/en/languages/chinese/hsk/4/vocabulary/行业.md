## 行业 (hángyè)

行业 refers to a specific sector, trade, or field of economic activity.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Industry; sector; trade; line of work.

## Example Sentences

**互联网行业发展得非常快。**
Hùliánwǎng hángyè fāzhǎn de fēicháng kuài.
The internet industry is developing very rapidly.

**他在金融行业工作了很多年。**
Tā zài jīnróng hángyè gōngzuòle hěn duō nián.
He has worked in the financial industry for many years.

**进入这个行业需要专业的技术。**
Jìnrù zhège hángyè xūyào zhuānyè de jìshù.
Entering this industry requires professional skills.

**各行业都面临着数字化转型的挑战。**
Gè hángyè dōu miànlín zhe shùzìhuà zhuǎnxíng de tiǎozhàn.
Every industry faces the challenges of digital transformation.

## Usage Notes

行业 is a broad term for any economic sector or professional field. The common phrase 各行各业 (all walks of life) covers every type of work. Note the pronunciation: 行 in this word is háng (not xíng), which refers to a trade or profession.

## Memory Hook

行 (háng) means "a row/trade" and 业 means "industry/profession" — the row of businesses in a trade makes up an industry.
