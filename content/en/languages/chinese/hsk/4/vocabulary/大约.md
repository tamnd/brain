## 大约 (dàyuē)

大约 introduces an approximate number or time, signaling that the figure given is an estimate, not an exact amount.

## Meanings

1. **[adverb]** Approximately; about; roughly (indicates an estimate, not an exact figure).

## Example Sentences

**从这里到车站大约需要二十分钟。**
Cóng zhèlǐ dào chēzhàn dàyuē xūyào èrshí fēnzhōng.
It takes approximately twenty minutes from here to the station.

**这本书大约有三百页。**
Zhè běn shū dàyuē yǒu sānbǎi yè.
This book has about three hundred pages.

**他大约下午五点到家。**
Tā dàyuē xiàwǔ wǔ diǎn dào jiā.
He arrives home at roughly five in the afternoon.

**这个工程大约要花两年时间才能完成。**
Zhège gōngchéng dàyuē yào huā liǎng nián shíjiān cái néng wánchéng.
This project will take approximately two years to complete.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 大约需要 | approximately requires |
| 大约有 | there are approximately |
| 大约在 | at approximately (a time or place) |
| 大约花了 | spent approximately |

## Usage Notes

大约 and 大概 are often interchangeable when expressing approximation, but 大概 can also express probability (大概是真的 = probably true), while 大约 is almost always used only for quantity and time estimates.

## Memory Hook

大 means "big/general" and 约 means "approximately/to agree"; 大约 gives you the big-picture agreed-upon estimate, not the precise detail.
