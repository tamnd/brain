## 开展 (kāizhǎn)

开展 means to carry out, launch, or develop an activity, project, or campaign, especially one that is organized and purposeful.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to carry out; to launch; to develop; to unfold (activities)

## Example Sentences

**学校开展了一系列环保活动。** Xuéxiào kāizhǎnle yī xìliè huánbǎo huódòng. The school launched a series of environmental protection activities.

**公司正在开展新的培训项目。** Gōngsī zhèngzài kāizhǎn xīn de péixùn xiàngmù. The company is carrying out new training programs.

**政府开展了一场反腐败运动。** Zhèngfǔ kāizhǎnle yī chǎng fǎn fǔbài yùndòng. The government launched an anti-corruption campaign.

**工作开展得很顺利，没有遇到大问题。** Gōngzuò kāizhǎn de hěn shùnlì, méiyǒu yùdào dà wèntí. The work is progressing smoothly without major problems.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 开展活动 | to carry out activities |
| 开展工作 | to carry out work |

| 顺利开展 | to proceed smoothly |

## Usage Notes

开展 is commonly used in formal and official contexts to describe launching or conducting organized activities, campaigns, or projects. It implies that the activity is structured and intentional. It is often used with activities, campaigns, programs, or research as its object.

## Memory Hook

开 means "to open" and 展 means "to spread out/develop." So 开展 is to open something up and let it spread: launching an activity and letting it unfold.
