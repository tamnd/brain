## 速度 (sùdù)

速度 refers to the rate of movement or change, covering physical speed as well as the pace of development or progress.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Speed; pace; velocity; rate.

## Example Sentences

**这列高铁的速度超过三百公里每小时。**
Zhè liè gāotiě de sùdù chāoguò sānbǎi gōnglǐ měi xiǎoshí.
The speed of this high-speed train exceeds 300 kilometers per hour.

**请说话慢一点，你的速度太快了。**
Qǐng shuōhuà màn yīdiǎn, nǐ de sùdù tài kuài le.
Please speak more slowly; your pace is too fast.

**中国经济增长的速度令世界惊叹。**
Zhōngguó jīngjì zēngzhǎng de sùdù lìng shìjiè jīngtàn.
The speed of China's economic growth has amazed the world.

**提高阅读速度是备考的重要方法。**
Tígāo yuèdú sùdù shì bèikǎo de zhòngyào fāngfǎ.
Improving reading speed is an important method for exam preparation.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 加快速度 | to speed up |
| 降低速度 | to slow down |
| 发展速度 | pace of development |
| 速度很快 | very fast speed |
| 以...的速度 | at the speed of ... |

## Usage Notes

速度 is used both for physical speed (of vehicles, objects) and metaphorical pace (of learning, development, economic growth). The pattern 以...的速度 (at the speed/rate of ...) is a versatile structure. For everyday "fast/slow," Chinese often just uses 快 or 慢, but 速度 is needed when quantifying or discussing rate more formally.

## Memory Hook

速 means "swift/fast" and 度 means "degree/measurement" — the degree of swiftness is your speed.
