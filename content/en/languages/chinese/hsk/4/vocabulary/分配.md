## 分配 (fēnpèi)

分配 means to allocate, distribute, or assign something — such as tasks, resources, time, or roles — among people or categories.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to allocate, distribute, assign, apportion
2. **[noun]** allocation, distribution, assignment

## Example Sentences

**老师把任务分配给每个学生。** Lǎoshī bǎ rènwù fēnpèi gěi měi gè xuésheng. The teacher assigned tasks to each student.

**合理分配时间对学习非常重要。** Hélǐ fēnpèi shíjiān duì xuéxí fēicháng zhòngyào. Reasonably allocating time is very important for studying.

**公司把资源分配到最重要的项目上。** Gōngsī bǎ zīyuán fēnpèi dào zuì zhòngyào de xiàngmù shàng. The company allocated resources to the most important projects.

**财富的不公平分配是一个严重的社会问题。** Cáifù de bù gōngpíng fēnpèi shì yīgè yánzhòng de shèhuì wèntí. The unequal distribution of wealth is a serious social problem.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |

| 合理分配 | allocate reasonably / fair distribution |

## Usage Notes

分配 can be used as both a verb and a noun. As a verb, it is commonly used with 把…分配给… (assign/allocate … to …). It appears frequently in workplace, economic, and academic contexts. It differs from 分发 (to hand out/distribute physical items) in that 分配 emphasizes systematic planning and assignment.

## Memory Hook

分 (divide/split) + 配 (match/pair) = "divide and match each part to its place" = allocate/distribute.
