## 互联网 (hùliánwǎng)

互联网 is the Chinese term for the internet, referring to the global network that connects computers and devices worldwide.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** the internet, the worldwide computer network

## Example Sentences

**互联网改变了我们的生活方式。** Hùliánwǎng gǎibiàn le wǒmen de shēnghuó fāngshì. The internet has changed our way of life.

**现在很多人通过互联网购物。** Xiànzài hěn duō rén tōngguò hùliánwǎng gòuwù. Many people now shop through the internet.

**互联网让全世界的人可以互相交流。** Hùliánwǎng ràng quán shìjiè de rén kěyǐ hùxiāng jiāoliú. The internet allows people all over the world to communicate with each other.

**没有互联网，现代商业很难运转。** Méiyǒu hùliánwǎng, xiàndài shāngyè hěn nán yùnzhuǎn. Without the internet, modern business would be very difficult to operate.

## Usage Notes

互联网 is the formal term for the internet. In casual speech, people also say 网络 (wǎngluò, network/internet) or simply 网 (wǎng, net/web). The term 互联网+ (Internet Plus) became a major government initiative in China, referring to the integration of the internet with traditional industries.

## Memory Hook

互 means "mutual," 联 means "connect," and 网 means "net/network," so 互联网 is literally a "mutually connected net," describing the internet perfectly.
