## 互相 (hùxiāng)

互相 is an adverb indicating that an action is reciprocal — each party both performs and receives the action in relation to the other.

## Meanings

1. **[adverb]** Mutually, each other, one another (indicating a reciprocal relationship).

## Example Sentences

**两国之间需要互相尊重，才能建立起长久稳定的合作关系。**
Liǎng guó zhī jiān xūyào hùxiāng zūnzhòng, cái néng jiànlì qǐ chángjiǔ wěndìng de hézuò guānxi.
The two countries need to respect each other before they can establish a long-term, stable cooperative relationship.

**团队成员之间互相支持、互相鼓励，是取得成功的重要因素。**
Tuánduì chéngyuán zhī jiān hùxiāng zhīchí, hùxiāng gǔlì, shì qǔdé chénggōng de zhòngyào yīnsù.
Team members supporting and encouraging each other is an important factor in achieving success.

**在陌生的城市里，互相帮助的旅行者往往能结下深厚的友谊。**
Zài mòshēng de chéngshì lǐ, hùxiāng bāngzhù de lǚxíngzhě wǎngwǎng néng jié xià shēnhòu de yǒuyì.
Travelers who help one another in unfamiliar cities often form deep friendships.

**我们虽然意见不同，但仍然可以互相理解，寻找共同点。**
Wǒmen suīrán yìjiàn bùtóng, dàn réngrán kěyǐ hùxiāng lǐjiě, xúnzhǎo gòngtóngdiǎn.
Although we have different opinions, we can still understand each other and find common ground.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 互相尊重 | to respect each other |
| 互相帮助 | to help each other |
| 互相理解 | to understand each other |
| 互相学习 | to learn from each other |
| 互相依赖 | to depend on each other |

## Usage Notes

互相 always modifies a verb and requires a plural or bilateral subject — you cannot say 我互相帮助 (I mutually help) because one person cannot be mutual. It is often used in pairs for emphasis: 互相帮助、互相鼓励. Compare with 彼此 (bǐcǐ — each other), which is slightly more literary and can also function as a pronoun (我们彼此了解 — we understand each other).

## Memory Hook

互 depicts two interlocked hands, and 相 means "facing/toward each other" — two parties facing each other with interlocked action is the very picture of reciprocity.
