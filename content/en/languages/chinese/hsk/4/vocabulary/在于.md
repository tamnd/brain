## 在于 (zàiyú)

在于 expresses that the key factor or essence of something lies in a particular cause or condition, similar to "the point is" or "it all depends on."

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To lie in; to consist in; to depend on (indicates where the key factor is located).

## Example Sentences

**成功的关键在于坚持不懈。**
Chénggōng de guānjiàn zàiyú jiānchí bùxiè.
The key to success lies in perseverance.

**问题的根源在于缺乏沟通。**
Wèntí de gēnyuán zàiyú quēfá gōutōng.
The root of the problem lies in a lack of communication.

**学习的价值在于理解，而不是记忆。**
Xuéxí de jiàzhí zàiyú lǐjiě, ér bùshì jìyì.
The value of learning lies in understanding, not in memorization.

**生活的意义在于不断追求进步。**
Shēnghuó de yìyì zàiyú bùduàn zhuīqiú jìnbù.
The meaning of life lies in the constant pursuit of progress.

## Usage Notes

在于 is typically used in formal or analytical writing. The subject before 在于 is usually an abstract noun such as 问题, 关键, 价值, or 原因. The complement after 在于 explains the essential factor.

## Memory Hook

在 means "to be at/in" and 于 marks location; 在于 asks "where does it live?" and answers with the core reason or essence.
