## 对于 (duìyú)

对于 is a preposition that introduces the topic or target of a statement, meaning "regarding," "with respect to," or "as for."

## Meanings

1. **[preposition]** Regarding; with respect to; as for (marks the subject matter of a comment or attitude).

## Example Sentences

**对于这个问题，我有不同的看法。**
Duìyú zhège wèntí, wǒ yǒu bùtóng de kànfǎ.
Regarding this problem, I have a different opinion.

**对于学生来说，时间管理非常重要。**
Duìyú xuésheng lái shuō, shíjiān guǎnlǐ fēicháng zhòngyào.
For students, time management is very important.

**她对于新工作非常兴奋。**
Tā duìyú xīn gōngzuò fēicháng xīngfèn.
She is very excited about her new job.

**对于这份报告，经理提出了很多建议。**
Duìyú zhè fèn bàogào, jīnglǐ tíchū le hěn duō jiànyì.
Regarding this report, the manager raised many suggestions.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 对于…来说 | as far as ... is concerned |
| 对于…而言 | with respect to ...; for ... |
| 对于此事 | regarding this matter |
| 对于问题 | regarding the issue |

## Usage Notes

对于 and 关于 are often confused. 关于 means "about; concerning" and introduces a topic broadly, while 对于 focuses on a specific target or the reaction toward it. 对于 cannot be replaced by 关于 when expressing attitudes or feelings.

## Memory Hook

对 means "facing" and 于 marks direction; together they point you directly at a topic: "facing toward" that subject matter.
