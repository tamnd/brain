## 上传 (shàngchuán)

上传 means to upload, describing the action of sending files, photos, or data from a local device to the internet or a remote server. It is the opposite of 下载 (to download).

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to upload; to send data from a local device to a server or the internet

## Example Sentences

**她把照片上传到社交媒体上了。** Tā bǎ zhàopiàn shàngchuán dào shèjiāo méitǐ shàng le. She uploaded the photos to social media.

**请把作业文件上传到系统里。** Qǐng bǎ zuòyè wénjiàn shàngchuán dào xìtǒng lǐ. Please upload the homework file to the system.

**上传视频需要良好的网络信号。** Shàngchuán shìpín xūyào liánghǎo de wǎngluò xìnhào. Uploading a video requires a good internet signal.

**他每天都上传新的内容给粉丝。** Tā měitiān dōu shàngchuán xīn de nèiróng gěi fěnsī. He uploads new content for his fans every day.

## Usage Notes

上传 and 下载 are direct antonyms. 上 (up) reflects data going up to a server, and 传 (to transmit) is the action. In formal or professional contexts, 提交 (tíjiāo, to submit) may be used instead when uploading official documents. The 把 structure is commonly used with 上传 to indicate what is being uploaded and where.

## Memory Hook

上 (up) + 传 (to transmit/pass): transmitting your data upward to the internet — uploading.
