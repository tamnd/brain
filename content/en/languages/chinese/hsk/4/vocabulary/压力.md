## 压力 (yālì)

压力 refers to physical pressure exerted on an object or, more commonly in everyday Chinese, the psychological stress or burden a person feels.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Pressure (physical).
2. **[noun]** Stress; pressure; burden (psychological or social).

## Example Sentences

**工作压力太大让他很难入睡。**
Gōngzuò yālì tài dà ràng tā hěn nán rùshuì.
The work pressure is so great that he finds it hard to fall asleep.

**现代年轻人面临很大的生活压力。**
Xiàndài niánqīngrén miànlín hěn dà de shēnghuó yālì.
Modern young people face enormous life pressure.

**她学会了在压力下保持冷静。**
Tā xuéhuìle zài yālì xià bǎochí lěngjìng.
She has learned to stay calm under pressure.

**来自家庭的压力让他感到疲惫。**
Lái zì jiātíng de yālì ràng tā gǎndào píbèi.
Pressure from his family left him feeling exhausted.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 工作压力 | work stress |

| 压力很大 | under great pressure |
| 在压力下 | under pressure |

## Usage Notes

压力 is one of the most frequently used words in modern Chinese lifestyle discussions. It collocates with many types of stress: 工作压力 (work), 学业压力 (academic), 生活压力 (life). The pattern 感到压力 (to feel pressure) and 承受压力 (to withstand pressure) are both common.

## Memory Hook

压 means "to press down/crush" and 力 means "force/strength" — the force pressing down on you is pressure or stress.
