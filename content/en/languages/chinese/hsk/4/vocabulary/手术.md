## 手术 (shǒushù)

手术 refers to a surgical operation performed by a doctor to treat injury or disease. It is a common medical term used in everyday conversations about health and hospitals.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** surgery; a medical operation involving incision or other physical intervention

## Example Sentences

**医生说他需要做手术才能康复。** Yīshēng shuō tā xūyào zuò shǒushù cái néng kāngfù. The doctor said he needs surgery in order to recover.

**手术很成功，病人已经醒了。** Shǒushù hěn chénggōng, bìngrén yǐjīng xǐng le. The surgery was very successful and the patient has already woken up.

**她明天早上要做心脏手术。** Tā míngtiān zǎoshang yào zuò xīnzàng shǒushù. She is having heart surgery tomorrow morning.

**手术之前，医生解释了所有的风险。** Shǒushù zhīqián, yīshēng jiěshì le suǒyǒu de fēngxiǎn. Before the surgery, the doctor explained all the risks.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 做手术 | to have / perform surgery |
| 手术室 | operating room |
| 心脏手术 | heart surgery |
| 手术成功 | successful surgery |

## Usage Notes

手术 is always used as a noun. To express "to undergo surgery," Chinese uses the verb-object structure 做手术 (zuò shǒushù). You can also say 动手术 (dòng shǒushù), which has the same meaning and is equally common in spoken Chinese. The word 手 (hand) + 术 (technique/art) literally means "hand technique," reflecting the skilled manual nature of surgery.

## Memory Hook

Think of 手 (hand) + 术 (technique) — surgery is literally a "hand technique" performed by a skilled doctor.
