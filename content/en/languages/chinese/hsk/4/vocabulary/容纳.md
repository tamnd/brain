## 容纳 (róngnà)

容纳 describes the capacity to hold or accommodate a certain number of people or a certain volume, used for physical spaces or metaphorically for acceptance.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To hold; to accommodate; to contain (to have the capacity for a number of people or things).

## Example Sentences

**这个体育馆能容纳五万名观众。**
Zhège tǐyùguǎn néng róngnà wǔ wàn míng guānzhòng.
This stadium can accommodate fifty thousand spectators.

**新建的图书馆可以容纳更多的书籍。**
Xīn jiàn de túshūguǎn kěyǐ róngnà gèng duō de shūjí.
The newly built library can hold more books.

**这间教室最多容纳三十名学生。**
Zhè jiān jiàoshì zuìduō róngnà sānshí míng xuésheng.
This classroom can accommodate at most thirty students.

**她宽广的心胸能容纳各种不同的意见。**
Tā kuānguǎng de xīnxiōng néng róngnà gèzhǒng bùtóng de yìjiàn.
Her broad-minded nature can accommodate all kinds of different opinions.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 容纳多少人 | how many people can it hold |
| 最多容纳 | can accommodate at most |
| 容纳不下 | cannot accommodate; no room for |
| 容纳能力 | capacity; ability to accommodate |

## Usage Notes

容纳 is mainly used for physical spaces and their capacity. It can also be used metaphorically to describe a person's ability to accept different ideas or people (e.g., 心胸能容纳). The related noun 容量 refers to the volume or capacity itself.

## Memory Hook

容 means "to contain/tolerate" and 纳 means "to receive/take in"; 容纳 opens up space to receive and hold whatever comes in.
