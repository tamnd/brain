## 理解 (lǐjiě)

理解 means to understand or comprehend something at a deeper level, going beyond surface recognition to genuine comprehension.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to understand; to comprehend; to grasp the meaning or significance of something
2. **[noun]** understanding; comprehension

## Example Sentences

**我完全理解你的感受。** Wǒ wánquán lǐjiě nǐ de gǎnshòu. I completely understand your feelings.

**这道数学题我还没理解。** Zhè dào shùxué tí wǒ hái méi lǐjiě. I still haven't understood this math problem.

**我们需要互相理解，才能更好地合作。** Wǒmen xūyào hùxiāng lǐjiě, cái néng gèng hǎo de hézuò. We need to understand each other in order to cooperate better.

**他对这个概念有深刻的理解。** Tā duì zhège gàiniàn yǒu shēnkè de lǐjiě. He has a deep understanding of this concept.

## Usage Notes

理解 implies a thorough, internalized understanding, deeper than 明白 (to understand/get it) or 懂 (to understand). It is used when you grasp not just the facts but the significance or nuance of something. In interpersonal contexts it often implies empathy and appreciation of another's perspective.

## Memory Hook

理 means "to sort out/reason" and 解 means "to untie/explain": 理解 is sorting out and untying the knots, fully unlocking meaning.
