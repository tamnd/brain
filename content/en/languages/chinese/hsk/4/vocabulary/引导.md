## 引导 (yǐndǎo)

引导 means to guide, lead, or direct someone toward a goal or in the right direction, often used in educational, social, or leadership contexts.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to guide; to lead; to direct

## Example Sentences

**老师引导学生思考问题。** Lǎoshī yǐndǎo xuéshēng sīkǎo wèntí. The teacher guided students to think about the problem.

**父母应该引导孩子养成好习惯。** Fùmǔ yīnggāi yǐndǎo háizi yǎngchéng hǎo xíguàn. Parents should guide children to develop good habits.

**他引导我们找到了出口。** Tā yǐndǎo wǒmen zhǎodàole chūkǒu. He led us to find the exit.

**媒体引导公众关注环境问题。** Méitǐ yǐndǎo gōngzhòng guānzhù huánjìng wèntí. The media guided the public to pay attention to environmental issues.

## Usage Notes

引导 implies gentle, positive direction rather than forceful command. It is commonly used in education, parenting, management, and media contexts. The subject is typically a person in authority or a guiding force, and the object is the person being guided or the process being directed.

## Memory Hook

YIN (引 = pull/lead) + DAO (导 = guide): pull someone along the path and guide them -- that is 引导.
