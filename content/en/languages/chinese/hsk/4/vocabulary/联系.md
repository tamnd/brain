## 联系 (liánxì)

联系 covers both the act of contacting someone and the abstract idea of a connection or relationship between things.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To contact, to get in touch with, to reach out to.
2. **[noun]** Contact, connection, link, relationship.

## Example Sentences

**毕业后我们虽然各奔东西，但始终保持着密切的联系。**
Bìyè hòu wǒmen suīrán gè bèn dōngxī, dàn shǐzhōng bǎochí zhe mìqiē de liánxì.
After graduation we all went our separate ways, but we have always maintained close contact.

**如果您有任何疑问，请随时与我们的客服团队联系。**
Rúguǒ nín yǒu rènhé yíwèn, qǐng suíshí yǔ wǒmen de kèfú tuánduì liánxì.
If you have any questions, please feel free to contact our customer service team at any time.

**研究人员发现，久坐与心脏病之间存在直接的联系。**
Yánjiū rényuán fāxiàn, jiǔ zuò yǔ xīnzàngbìng zhī jiān cúnzài zhíjiē de liánxì.
Researchers found a direct connection between prolonged sitting and heart disease.

**这两件事表面上看起来毫无关联，但实际上有内在的联系。**
Zhè liǎng jiàn shì biǎomiàn shàng kàn qǐlái háowú guānlián, dàn shíjì shàng yǒu nèizài de liánxì.
These two matters appear to have no connection on the surface, but in fact there is an intrinsic link.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 保持联系 | to keep in touch |
| 取得联系 | to get in contact |
| 失去联系 | to lose contact |
| 联系方式 | contact information |
| 有联系 | to have a connection |

## Usage Notes

联系 works for both people (联系朋友 — contact a friend) and abstract things (事物之间的联系 — connections between things). 联系方式 (contact information) is an extremely useful phrase. Compare with 联络 (liánluò), a near-synonym that is slightly more formal and often used in official or military communication contexts.

## Memory Hook

联 means "to link/join" and 系 means "to tie/bind" — linking and tying things or people together creates a 联系.
