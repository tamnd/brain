## 交流 (jiāoliú)

交流 describes the two-way sharing of ideas, information, or experiences, emphasizing the mutual and interactive nature of the exchange.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To exchange, to communicate, to share (ideas, experiences, views).
2. **[noun]** An exchange, communication, interaction.

## Example Sentences

**这次国际学术会议为来自不同国家的研究人员提供了交流的平台。**
Zhè cì guójì xuéshù huìyì wèi láizì bùtóng guójiā de yánjiū rényuán tígōng le jiāoliú de píngtái.
This international academic conference provided a platform for researchers from different countries to exchange ideas.

**良好的沟通和交流是解决大多数职场矛盾的关键。**
Liánghǎo de gōutōng hé jiāoliú shì jiějué dàduōshù zhíchǎng máodùn de guānjiàn.
Good communication and exchange are key to resolving most workplace conflicts.

**他通过与母语者交流，大大提升了自己的口语表达能力。**
Tā tōngguò yǔ mǔyǔ zhě jiāoliú, dàdà tíshēng le zìjǐ de kǒuyǔ biǎodá nénglì.
Through communicating with native speakers, he greatly improved his spoken expression.

**两校之间建立了长期的文化交流项目，每年互派留学生。**
Liǎng xiào zhī jiān jiànlì le chángqī de wénhuà jiāoliú xiàngmù, měi nián hù pài liúxuéshēng.
A long-term cultural exchange program was established between the two schools, with exchange students sent each year.

## Usage Notes

交流 implies a two-way, balanced interaction, unlike 传达 (to convey, one-way) or 通知 (to notify). It is commonly used in educational, diplomatic, and business contexts. The pattern 通过交流 (through exchange/communication) is very common. Compare with 沟通 (gōutōng — to communicate), which often focuses on clearing up misunderstandings or reaching understanding, while 交流 emphasizes the sharing process itself.

## Memory Hook

交 means "to hand over/exchange" and 流 means "to flow" — ideas and information flowing back and forth between people is exactly what communication is.
