## 保持 (bǎochí)

保持 describes the act of keeping something in its current state, preventing it from changing or deteriorating over time.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To maintain, to keep, to preserve, to sustain (a state or condition).

## Example Sentences

**无论多忙，她都坚持保持每天锻炼的习惯。**
Wúlùn duō máng, tā dōu jiānchí bǎochí měitiān duànliàn de xíguàn.
No matter how busy she is, she insists on maintaining the habit of exercising every day.

**两国之间一直保持着友好的外交关系。**
Liǎng guó zhī jiān yīzhí bǎochí zhe yǒuhǎo de wàijiāo guānxi.
The two countries have always maintained friendly diplomatic relations.

**在高压的工作环境中，保持冷静非常关键。**
Zài gāoyā de gōngzuò huánjìng zhōng, bǎochí lěngjìng fēicháng guānjiàn.
In a high-pressure work environment, maintaining composure is extremely important.

**这座古建筑经过修复后，依然保持了原有的风貌。**
Zhè zuò gǔ jiànzhù jīngguò xiūfù hòu, yīrán bǎochí le yuányǒu de fēngmào.
After restoration, this ancient building still preserved its original appearance.

## Usage Notes

保持 is always followed by the state being maintained, not an action. You say 保持安静 (maintain silence) but not 保持说话. It pairs naturally with abstract states: 保持耐心 (maintain patience), 保持距离 (keep a distance). Compare with 维持, which is closer in meaning but often used in more formal or structural contexts (维持秩序 — maintain order).

## Memory Hook

保 means "to protect" and 持 means "to hold" — you protect by holding on to the current state without letting it slip away.
