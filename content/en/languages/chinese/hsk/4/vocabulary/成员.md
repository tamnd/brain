## 成员 (chéngyuán)

成员 refers to a person who belongs to and is part of a group, team, organization, or family.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Member; member of a group, team, organization, or family.

## Example Sentences

**我们团队有五名成员。**
Wǒmen tuánduì yǒu wǔ míng chéngyuán.
Our team has five members.

**他是这个组织的正式成员。**
Tā shì zhège zǔzhī de zhèngshì chéngyuán.
He is a formal member of this organization.

**每位家庭成员都有各自的责任。**
Měi wèi jiātíng chéngyuán dōu yǒu gèzì de zérèn.
Every family member has their own responsibilities.

**新成员需要参加入职培训。**
Xīn chéngyuán xūyào cānjiā rùzhí péixùn.
New members need to attend onboarding training.

## Usage Notes

成员 is a neutral, formal term used for members of groups ranging from families to international organizations. The measure word is 名 (míng) for people or 个 (gè) informally. It differs from 会员 (huìyuán), which specifically means a fee-paying or registered club/association member.

## Memory Hook

成 means "to become/form" and 员 means "person/personnel" — a person who has become part of the group is a member.
