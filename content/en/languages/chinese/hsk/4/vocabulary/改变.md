## 改变 (gǎibiàn)

改变 refers to a meaningful shift or transformation in something — a state, a plan, an attitude, or a situation — and works as both verb and noun.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To change, to alter, to transform.
2. **[noun]** A change, an alteration, a transformation.

## Example Sentences

**出国留学的经历从根本上改变了他看待世界的方式。**
Chūguó liúxué de jīnglì cóng gēnběn shàng gǎibiàn le tā kàndài shìjiè de fāngshì.
The experience of studying abroad fundamentally changed the way he sees the world.

**面对困境，她没有抱怨，而是努力改变现状。**
Miàn duì kùnjìng, tā méiyǒu bàoyuàn, ér shì nǔlì gǎibiàn xiànzhuàng.
Faced with adversity, she did not complain but worked hard to change the situation.

**这座城市在过去二十年里发生了翻天覆地的改变。**
Zhè zuò chéngshì zài guòqù èrshí nián lǐ fāshēng le fāntiānfùdì de gǎibiàn.
This city has undergone earth-shattering changes over the past twenty years.

**科技的进步正在以惊人的速度改变我们的生活方式。**
Kējì de jìnbù zhèngzài yǐ jīngrén de sùdù gǎibiàn wǒmen de shēnghuó fāngshì.
Advances in technology are changing our way of life at an astonishing pace.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 改变主意 | to change one's mind |
| 改变现状 | to change the status quo |
| 发生改变 | to undergo a change |
| 巨大的改变 | a huge change |
| 无法改变 | unable to change |

## Usage Notes

改变 implies a substantive transformation, not just a minor adjustment (for which 调整 is more appropriate). As a noun, it often appears after 发生 (to occur): 发生了很大的改变. 改变主意 is a fixed phrase meaning to change one's mind. Compare with 变化 (biànhuà), which is also "change" but more often used as a noun describing natural or gradual shifts.

## Memory Hook

改 means "to correct/reform" and 变 means "to change" — reforming and changing together give you a complete transformation.
