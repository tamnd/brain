## 报告 (bàogào)

报告 means a report (noun) or to report (verb), referring to the formal presentation of information, findings, or updates to an audience or authority.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** a report; a presentation; a formal account
2. **[verb]** to report; to present findings; to inform

## Example Sentences

**他向经理提交了一份销售报告。** Tā xiàng jīnglǐ tíjiāole yī fèn xiāoshòu bàogào. He submitted a sales report to the manager.

**她在会议上作了一个重要的工作报告。** Tā zài huìyì shàng zuòle yīgè zhòngyào de gōngzuò bàogào. She gave an important work report at the meeting.

**学生需要写一份关于环境问题的报告。** Xuéshēng xūyào xiě yī fèn guānyú huánjìng wèntí de bàogào. Students need to write a report on environmental issues.

**他报告说工厂的产量本月提高了百分之二十。** Tā bàogào shuō gōngchǎng de chǎnliàng běn yuè tígāole bǎifēnzhī èrshí. He reported that the factory's output increased by twenty percent this month.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 提交报告 | to submit a report |
| 工作报告 | work report |
| 研究报告 | research report |
| 作报告 | to give a report/presentation |

## Usage Notes

报告 can be both a written document and an oral presentation. In formal contexts, making a speech or presentation is called 作报告. It is used in academic, business, and governmental settings. The phrase 向……报告 (to report to someone) is used when informing a superior or authority.

## Memory Hook

报 means "to report/announce" and 告 means "to tell/inform." Together, 报告 is to report and inform: delivering information to those who need it.
