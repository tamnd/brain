## 风险 (fēngxiǎn)

风险 refers to risk or the possibility of something going wrong, particularly in business, health, or decision-making.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** risk; danger; hazard; potential loss

## Example Sentences

**投资有风险，决策需谨慎。** Tóuzī yǒu fēngxiǎn, juécè xū jǐnshèn. Investment involves risks; decisions must be made carefully.

**医生提醒病人手术存在一定的风险。** Yīshēng tíxǐng bìngrén shǒushù cúnzài yīdìng de fēngxiǎn. The doctor reminded the patient that surgery carries certain risks.

**创业者要学会承担风险。** Chuàngyèzhě yào xuéhuì chéngdān fēngxiǎn. Entrepreneurs need to learn how to bear risks.

**我们需要在风险和收益之间找到平衡。** Wǒmen xūyào zài fēngxiǎn hé shōuyì zhījiān zhǎodào pínghéng. We need to find a balance between risk and return.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 承担风险 (chéngdān fēngxiǎn) | to bear/take on risk |
| 降低风险 (jiàngdī fēngxiǎn) | to reduce risk |
| 风险评估 (fēngxiǎn pínggū) | risk assessment |
| 高风险 (gāo fēngxiǎn) | high risk |

## Usage Notes

风险 is widely used in business, financial, medical, and daily life contexts. It often appears in the structure 有风险 (there is a risk) or 存在风险 (a risk exists). The related phrase 冒风险 (mào fēngxiǎn) means "to take a risk," similar to "to risk it" in English.

## Memory Hook

风 (wind) + 险 (danger/steep) — a dangerous wind that can blow you off course; that unpredictable wind is the risk you face.
