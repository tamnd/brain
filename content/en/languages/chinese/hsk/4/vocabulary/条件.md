## 条件 (tiáojiàn)

条件 refers to the requirements, prerequisites, or terms that must be met for something to happen or be accepted.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Condition; requirement; terms (a prerequisite or stated stipulation).

## Example Sentences

**申请这份工作需要满足一定的条件。**
Shēnqǐng zhè fèn gōngzuò xūyào mǎnzú yīdìng de tiáojiàn.
Applying for this job requires meeting certain conditions.

**在这样的条件下工作真的很辛苦。**
Zài zhèyàng de tiáojiàn xià gōngzuò zhēn de hěn xīnkǔ.
Working under such conditions is really hard.

**他们提出了三个合作条件。**
Tāmen tíchū le sān gè hézuò tiáojiàn.
They put forward three terms for cooperation.

**自然条件的不同影响了当地的农业发展。**
Zìrán tiáojiàn de bùtóng yǐngxiǎng le dāngdì de nóngyè fāzhǎn.
Differences in natural conditions have affected local agricultural development.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |

## Usage Notes

条件 can refer to external circumstances (living conditions, natural conditions) or stated requirements (terms of a contract, application requirements). The phrase 在…条件下 (under the conditions of...) is a useful structure for describing circumstances.

## Memory Hook

条 means "strip/item" and 件 means "piece/item"; 条件 is each individual item on the checklist of requirements that must be ticked off.
