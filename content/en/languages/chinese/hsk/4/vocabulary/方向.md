## 方向 (fāngxiàng)

方向 refers to a physical direction such as north or left, or a figurative direction such as the path of a career or project.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Direction; orientation (a physical bearing or figurative course of action).

## Example Sentences

**请问火车站在哪个方向？**
Qǐngwèn huǒchēzhàn zài nǎge fāngxiàng?
Excuse me, in which direction is the train station?

**我们需要重新考虑公司的发展方向。**
Wǒmen xūyào chóngxīn kǎolǜ gōngsī de fāzhǎn fāngxiàng.
We need to reconsider the direction of the company's development.

**他在人生的方向上感到迷茫。**
Tā zài rénshēng de fāngxiàng shàng gǎndào mímáng.
He feels lost about the direction of his life.

**这辆车朝错误的方向行驶了。**
Zhè liàng chē cháo cuòwù de fāngxiàng xíngshǐ le.
This car was traveling in the wrong direction.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 发展方向 | direction of development |
| 朝…方向 | toward the direction of ... |
| 努力方向 | direction of effort |
| 改变方向 | to change direction |
| 明确方向 | to clarify the direction |

## Usage Notes

方向 works for both literal physical directions (east, west, left, right) and abstract ones (life direction, research direction, policy direction). The verb 朝 (toward) is often paired with 方向: 朝正确的方向走 (go in the right direction).

## Memory Hook

方 means "square/side" and 向 means "facing/toward"; 方向 is the side you are facing, pointing you to where you should go.
