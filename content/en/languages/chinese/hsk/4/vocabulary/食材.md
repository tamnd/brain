## 食材 (shícái)

食材 refers to the raw ingredients or foodstuffs used in preparing a meal. It is a common word in everyday cooking contexts.

## Meanings
1. **noun** ingredient, foodstuff (raw material used in cooking)

## Example Sentences
**新鲜的食材是做好菜的关键。** Xīnxiān de shícái shì zuò hǎo cài de guānjiàn. Fresh ingredients are the key to cooking a good dish.

**他去市场买了很多食材。** Tā qù shìchǎng mǎile hěn duō shícái. He went to the market and bought many ingredients.

**这道菜的食材很简单，但味道很好。** Zhè dào cài de shícái hěn jiǎndān, dàn wèidào hěn hǎo. The ingredients for this dish are simple, but it tastes great.

**有机食材越来越受欢迎。** Yǒujī shícái yuè lái yuè shòu huānyíng. Organic ingredients are becoming more and more popular.

## Usage Notes
食材 is a neutral, everyday noun used whenever discussing the components that go into a dish. It can refer to a single ingredient or a collection of ingredients, and it appears frequently in recipes, cooking shows, and restaurant menus.

## Memory Hook
Think of 食 (shí, eat/food) plus 材 (cái, material) — the "eating materials" you need before cooking.
