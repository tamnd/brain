## 保护 (bǎohù)

保护 means to actively shield a person, animal, object, or environment from damage, harm, or loss.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To protect, to safeguard, to preserve from harm.

## Example Sentences

**政府出台了新的法律来保护濒危野生动物。**
Zhèngfǔ chūtái le xīn de fǎlǜ lái bǎohù bīndāng yěshēng dòngwù.
The government introduced new laws to protect endangered wildlife.

**戴口罩是保护自己和他人不受病毒感染的有效方式。**
Dài kǒuzhào shì bǎohù zìjǐ hé tārén bù shòu bìngdú gǎnrǎn de yǒuxiào fāngshì.
Wearing a mask is an effective way to protect yourself and others from viral infection.

**这片森林受到国家的严格保护，禁止任何商业开发。**
Zhè piàn sēnlín shòudào guójiā de yángé bǎohù, jìnzhǐ rènhé shāngyè kāifā.
This forest is under strict national protection; any commercial development is prohibited.

**她一直努力保护孩子们免受网络上有害内容的影响。**
Tā yīzhí nǔlì bǎohù háizimen miǎn shòu wǎngluò shàng yǒuhài nèiróng de yǐngxiǎng.
She has consistently worked to protect the children from harmful content online.

## Usage Notes

保护 can take a direct object (保护环境) or appear in a passive structure (受到保护). When used with 不受...影响/伤害, it conveys shielding from a specific threat. Compare with 保留, which means to retain or preserve something for future use, not to protect it from harm.

## Memory Hook

保 means "to protect" and 护 also means "to guard/nurse" — doubling up the protective meaning makes this word unmistakably about keeping something safe.
