## 预测 (yùcè)

预测 means "to predict" or "to forecast" and refers to making an informed judgment about what will happen in the future, often based on data or analysis.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to predict; to forecast; to project
2. **[noun]** prediction; forecast; projection

## Example Sentences

**天气预报预测明天会下雨。** Tiānqì yùbào yùcè míngtiān huì xià yǔ. The weather forecast predicts rain tomorrow.

**经济学家预测今年的经济增长率为5%。** Jīngjìxuéjiā yùcè jīnnián de jīngjì zēngzhǎnglǜ wéi 5%. Economists forecast an economic growth rate of 5% this year.

**这种趋势是很难预测的。** Zhè zhǒng qūshì shì hěn nán yùcè de. This kind of trend is very difficult to predict.

**他们的预测非常准确。** Tāmen de yùcè fēicháng zhǔnquè. Their predictions were very accurate.

## Usage Notes

预测 is used in contexts that involve data, analysis, or expertise, such as weather, economics, and science. It is more analytical than 猜 (cāi, to guess). The noun 预测 refers to the forecast or prediction itself. It is often followed by a clause expressing the predicted outcome, or it can take a noun as its object (预测结果, predicted result).

## Memory Hook

预 (yù) means "in advance" and 测 (cè) means "to measure or assess": 预测 is assessing or measuring what will happen in advance.
