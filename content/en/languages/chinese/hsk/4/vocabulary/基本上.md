## 基本上 (jīběnshàng)

基本上 is an adverb meaning "basically" or "essentially," used to indicate that something is mostly or largely the case, though not necessarily 100% perfect.

## Meanings

1. **[adverb]** basically; for the most part; largely

## Example Sentences

**这个项目基本上完成了。** Zhège xiàngmù jīběnshàng wánchéngle. This project is basically finished.

**我基本上同意你的看法。** Wǒ jīběnshàng tóngyì nǐ de kànfǎ. I basically agree with your view.

**新员工基本上已经适应了工作环境。** Xīn yuángōng jīběnshàng yǐjīng shìyìngle gōngzuò huánjìng. The new employees have essentially adapted to the work environment.

**这两个计划基本上是一样的。** Zhè liǎng gè jìhuà jīběnshàng shì yīyàng de. These two plans are basically the same.

## Collocations

## Usage Notes

基本上 is slightly softer than 完全 (completely). It leaves room for minor exceptions or imperfections. It is commonly used in spoken and written Chinese at a B1 level to soften statements or indicate approximate completeness.

## Memory Hook

基本 means "basic/fundamental" and 上 adds the sense of "on that basis," so 基本上 means you are standing on the fundamental truth of something: basically true.
