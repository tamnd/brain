## 怀疑 (huáiyí)

怀疑 means to doubt or suspect something or someone, and can also function as a noun meaning doubt or suspicion.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to doubt; to suspect; to question
2. **[noun]** doubt; suspicion

## Example Sentences

**我怀疑他说的不是真话。** Wǒ huáiyí tā shuō de bù shì zhēnhuà. I suspect what he said is not true.

**她对这个计划有很多怀疑。** Tā duì zhège jìhuà yǒu hěn duō huáiyí. She has many doubts about this plan.

**别怀疑自己的能力。** Bié huáiyí zìjǐ de nénglì. Don't doubt your own abilities.

**警察怀疑他是嫌疑人。** Jǐngchá huáiyí tā shì xiányírén. The police suspect he is a suspect.

## Usage Notes

怀疑 can express either genuine uncertainty (I'm not sure) or active suspicion (I think something is wrong). Context determines which meaning applies. When followed by a clause, it often means "suspect that..." When used as a noun after 有, it means "have doubts." It is more formal than 不相信 (to not believe).

## Memory Hook

HUAI (怀 = hold in the heart) + YI (疑 = uncertainty): holding uncertainty in your heart -- that is doubt.
