## 通过 (tōngguò)

通过 can mean "to pass through" a place, "to pass" an exam or vote, or "by means of" as a preposition indicating a method or channel.

## Meanings

1. **[preposition]** Through; by means of; via (indicates the method or channel used).
2. **[verb]** To pass (an exam, a law, a review); to go through.

## Example Sentences

**他通过努力学习，终于考上了大学。**
Tā tōngguò nǔlì xuéxí, zhōngyú kǎo shàng le dàxué.
By studying hard, he finally got into university.

**这项法律已经通过了投票。**
Zhè xiàng fǎlǜ yǐjīng tōngguò le tóupiào.
This law has already been passed by vote.

**通过这次旅行，我了解了很多新文化。**
Tōngguò zhè cì lǚxíng, wǒ liǎojiě le hěn duō xīn wénhuà.
Through this trip, I learned about many new cultures.

**他顺利通过了面试。**
Tā shùnlì tōngguò le miànshì.
He successfully passed the interview.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 通过考试 | to pass an exam |
| 通过努力 | through hard work |
| 通过投票 | to pass by vote |
| 通过学习 | through studying |
| 通过网络 | via the internet |

## Usage Notes

When 通过 is a preposition, it comes before the method noun and precedes the main verb. When it is a verb, it takes an object such as an exam, law, or review. The context usually makes the role clear.

## Memory Hook

通 means "to go through/connect" and 过 means "to cross/pass"; together they describe something traveling all the way through to the other side.
