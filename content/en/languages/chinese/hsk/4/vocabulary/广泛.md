## 广泛 (guǎngfàn)

广泛 describes something that covers a wide range, affects many areas, or is spread across a large scope. It is used to modify nouns or to describe how extensively something is done. It carries a neutral to positive connotation of breadth and reach.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** Widespread; extensive; broad; wide-ranging.

## Example Sentences

**这项技术在医疗、教育和工业领域都得到了广泛应用。**
Zhè xiàng jìshù zài yīliáo, jiàoyù hé gōngyè lǐngyù dōu dédào le guǎngfàn yìngyòng.
This technology has been widely applied in the fields of medicine, education, and industry.

**她阅读范围十分广泛，从历史小说到科学论文都有涉猎。**
Tā yuèdú fànwéi shífēn guǎngfàn, cóng lìshǐ xiǎoshuō dào kēxué lùnwén dōu yǒu shèliè.
Her reading is very extensive, ranging from historical fiction to scientific papers.

**这一话题引起了社会各界的广泛关注和讨论。**
Zhè yī huàtí yǐnqǐ le shèhuì gèjiè de guǎngfàn guānzhù hé tǎolùn.
This topic has attracted widespread attention and discussion from all sectors of society.

**他在工作中积累了广泛的专业人脉，对职业发展帮助很大。**
Tā zài gōngzuò zhōng jīlěi le guǎngfàn de zhuānyè rénmài, duì zhíyè fāzhǎn bāngzhù hěn dà.
He accumulated an extensive professional network at work, which is very helpful for career development.

## Usage Notes

广泛 is typically used as an attributive adjective before a noun (广泛的影响, 广泛的合作) or as an adverb before a verb (广泛应用, 广泛参与). It focuses on the scope or range of coverage, whereas 普遍 (pǔbiàn, universal/common) emphasizes that something is true or common across all cases. A useful contrast: 广泛应用 (applied across many fields) vs. 普遍存在 (existing in virtually all cases).

## Memory Hook

广 means "vast/wide" and 泛 means "to spread across/overflow." 广泛 paints a picture of water spreading wide in all directions, covering everything within reach.
