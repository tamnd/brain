## 菜单 (càidān)

菜单 is the menu at a restaurant or eatery — the list of available dishes and their prices. It is an essential word for dining out in China.

## Meanings
1. **noun** menu (a list of available dishes in a restaurant)

## Example Sentences
**请给我一份菜单。** Qǐng gěi wǒ yī fèn càidān. Please give me a menu.

**这家餐厅的菜单上有很多素食选项。** Zhè jiā cāntīng de càidān shàng yǒu hěn duō sùshí xuǎnxiàng. This restaurant's menu has many vegetarian options.

**服务员，这道菜在菜单上找不到。** Fúwùyuán, zhè dào cài zài càidān shàng zhǎo bù dào. Waiter, I cannot find this dish on the menu.

**他们的菜单每个季节都会更换。** Tāmen de càidān měi gè jìjié dōu huì gēnghuàn. Their menu changes every season.

## Collocations

| 更换菜单 (gēnghuàn càidān) | change the menu |

## Usage Notes
菜单 specifically refers to a restaurant menu. In digital contexts, 电子菜单 is used for QR-code or tablet-based menus. Do not confuse 菜单 with 清单 (qīngdān), which is a general list or checklist.

## Memory Hook
菜 (cài) means dish or vegetable, and 单 (dān) means a slip of paper or list. A "dish list" is exactly what a menu is.
