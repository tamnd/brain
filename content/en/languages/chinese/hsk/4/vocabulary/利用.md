## 利用 (lìyòng)

利用 means to make use of or utilize something, taking advantage of available resources, opportunities, or situations to achieve a goal.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to use, make use of, or utilize something for a specific purpose

## Example Sentences

**我们应该充分利用有限的资源。** Wǒmen yīnggāi chōngfèn lìyòng yǒuxiàn de zīyuán. We should make full use of limited resources.

**他利用空闲时间学习外语。** Tā lìyòng kòngxián shíjiān xuéxí wàiyǔ. He uses his free time to study foreign languages.

**公司利用新技术提高了生产效率。** Gōngsī lìyòng xīn jìshù tígāo le shēngchǎn xiàolǜ. The company used new technology to improve production efficiency.

**我们要善于利用互联网获取信息。** Wǒmen yào shànyú lìyòng hùliánwǎng huòqǔ xìnxī. We should be good at using the internet to obtain information.

## Usage Notes

利用 can sometimes carry a slightly negative connotation when a person "uses" another person for personal gain (e.g., 他利用了她 "he used her"). In neutral contexts with non-personal objects like time, resources, or technology, it simply means to make use of. Pay attention to context to determine if the meaning is neutral or negative.

## Memory Hook

利 means "benefit or profit" and 用 means "to use," so 利用 is to use something to gain benefit.
