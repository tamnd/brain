## 志愿者 (zhìyuànzhě)

志愿者 refers to a volunteer, a person who freely offers their time and effort to help others without expecting payment.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** a volunteer; someone who helps willingly without payment

## Example Sentences

**他是一名社区志愿者，每周帮助老人。** Tā shì yī míng shèqū zhìyuànzhě, měi zhōu bāngzhù lǎorén. He is a community volunteer who helps elderly people every week.

**这次活动需要招募五十名志愿者。** Zhè cì huódòng xūyào zhāomù wǔshí míng zhìyuànzhě. This event needs to recruit fifty volunteers.

**很多大学生利用假期做志愿者服务。** Hěn duō dàxuéshēng lìyòng jiàqī zuò zhìyuànzhě fúwù. Many university students use their holidays to do volunteer service.

**她在灾区做了三个月的志愿者工作。** Tā zài zāiqū zuòle sān gè yuè de zhìyuànzhě gōngzuò. She did three months of volunteer work in the disaster area.

## Usage Notes

志愿者 comes from 志愿 (aspiration/voluntary) plus 者 (person suffix). It is the standard modern term for "volunteer" in mainland China. The related word 义工 is also used, especially in Hong Kong and Taiwan. 志愿者 has a positive social connotation and is associated with civic responsibility.

## Memory Hook

志 means "aspiration/will," 愿 means "wish/willing," and 者 means "person." So 志愿者 is a "person of willing aspiration": someone who volunteers from the heart.
