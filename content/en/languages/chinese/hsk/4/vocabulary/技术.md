## 技术 (jìshù)

技术 refers to technology, technical skill, or a specialized technique used in a particular field.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Technology; technique; technical skill.

## Example Sentences

**这家公司在技术方面领先全球。**
Zhè jiā gōngsī zài jìshù fāngmiàn lǐngxiān quánqiú.
This company leads the world in technology.

**他的技术非常熟练。**
Tā de jìshù fēicháng shúliàn.
His technical skills are very proficient.

**新技术的应用改变了我们的生活方式。**
Xīn jìshù de yìngyòng gǎibiàn le wǒmen de shēnghuó fāngshì.
The application of new technology has changed our way of life.

**学习一门技术可以让你找到好工作。**
Xuéxí yī mén jìshù kěyǐ ràng nǐ zhǎodào hǎo gōngzuò.
Learning a technical skill can help you find a good job.

## Usage Notes

技术 can refer to technology as a broad concept or to a specific skill or technique. The measure word for a specific skill is 门 (mén), as in 一门技术 (a skill/trade). 技术人员 means technical staff, and 技术支持 (technical support) is widely used in IT contexts.

## Memory Hook

技 means "skill/craft" (with the hand radical) and 术 means "method/art" — the skill of a craft combined with its method creates technology.
