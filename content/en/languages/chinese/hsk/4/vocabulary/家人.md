## 家人 (jiārén)

家人 refers to the members of one's family, used collectively or individually.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** family member; family; one's family (collectively)

## Example Sentences

**她每天都给家人打电话。** Tā měitiān dōu gěi jiārén dǎ diànhuà. She calls her family every day.

**节日里，家人聚在一起吃饭，非常热闹。** Jiérì lǐ, jiārén jù zài yīqǐ chīfàn, fēicháng rènào. During the holidays, the family gathers together for meals; it's very lively.

**他的成功离不开家人的支持。** Tā de chénggōng lí bù kāi jiārén de zhīchí. His success cannot be separated from the support of his family.

**出门在外，最想念的还是家人。** Chūmén zài wài, zuì xiǎngniàn de háishì jiārén. When away from home, the people you miss most are still your family.

## Usage Notes

家人 refers collectively to one's family members and is commonly used to talk about the family as a group. It is slightly warmer and more intimate than 家庭成员 (family members, a more formal term). 家人 can also be used to refer to a single family member in context, e.g., 我的一个家人 (one of my family members).

## Memory Hook

家 means "home/family" and 人 means "person/people" -- 家人 are simply the people of your home, i.e., your family.
