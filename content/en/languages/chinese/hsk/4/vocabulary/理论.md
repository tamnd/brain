## 理论 (lǐlùn)

理论 means a theory or theoretical framework, referring to a set of ideas that explains how something works.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** theory; a systematic set of ideas that explains phenomena or guides practice

## Example Sentences

**这个理论已经被很多实验证明了。** Zhège lǐlùn yǐjīng bèi hěn duō shíyàn zhèngmíng le. This theory has been proven by many experiments.

**理论和实践同样重要。** Lǐlùn hé shíjiàn tóngyàng zhòngyào. Theory and practice are equally important.

**他在理论研究方面有很深的造诣。** Tā zài lǐlùn yánjiū fāngmiàn yǒu hěn shēn de zàoyì. He has deep attainment in theoretical research.

**从理论上说，这个计划是可行的。** Cóng lǐlùn shàng shuō, zhège jìhuà shì kěxíng de. In theory, this plan is feasible.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 理论研究 | theoretical research |
| 理论基础 | theoretical foundation |
| 从理论上 | in theory; theoretically |
| 理论联系实际 | linking theory with practice |

## Usage Notes

理论 is contrasted with 实践 (practice) in Chinese discourse, and the phrase 理论联系实际 (linking theory to practice) is a very common expression. 从理论上说 or 理论上 means "theoretically" or "in theory" and is useful for expressing something that works conceptually but may not in reality.

## Memory Hook

理 means "to reason/principles" and 论 means "to discuss/discourse": a 理论 is a discourse built on reasoned principles, a theory.
