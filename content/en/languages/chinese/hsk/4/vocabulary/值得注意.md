## 值得注意 (zhídé zhùyì)

值得注意 is a common phrase meaning that something is worthy of attention or noteworthy — it signals the reader or listener to pay special attention.

## Meanings

1. **[phrase]** worth noting, noteworthy, deserving attention, it should be noted that

## Example Sentences

**值得注意的是，这项研究还没有得到证实。** Zhídé zhùyì de shì, zhè xiàng yánjiū hái méiyǒu dédào zhèngshí. It is worth noting that this research has not yet been confirmed.

**有几个值得注意的问题需要讨论。** Yǒu jǐ gè zhídé zhùyì de wèntí xūyào tǎolùn. There are several noteworthy issues that need to be discussed.

**这种现象值得注意，我们不能忽视它。** Zhè zhǒng xiànxiàng zhídé zhùyì, wǒmen bù néng hūshì tā. This phenomenon is worth noting; we cannot ignore it.

**值得注意的是，儿童使用手机的时间越来越长。** Zhídé zhùyì de shì, értóng shǐyòng shǒujī de shíjiān yuè lái yuè cháng. It is worth noting that children are spending more and more time on their phones.

## Usage Notes

值得注意 is often used to introduce important information or warnings in essays, reports, and formal speech. The structure 值得 + verb phrase means "worth doing (verb)." Other common examples: 值得学习 (worth learning), 值得鼓励 (worth encouraging).

## Memory Hook

值得 (worth it) + 注意 (pay attention) = "worth paying attention to" = noteworthy.
