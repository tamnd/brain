## 方案 (fāng'àn)

方案 is a detailed, thought-out plan or proposal designed to achieve a specific goal or solve a problem.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Plan; scheme; program; proposal; solution.

## Example Sentences

**我们提出了一个新的发展方案。**
Wǒmen tíchūle yīgè xīn de fāzhǎn fāng'àn.
We put forward a new development plan.

**这个方案需要进一步讨论。**
Zhège fāng'àn xūyào jìnyībù tǎolùn.
This proposal needs further discussion.

**请在明天前提交你的方案。**
Qǐng zài míngtiān qián tíjiāo nǐ de fāng'àn.
Please submit your plan before tomorrow.

**经理批准了这个项目方案。**
Jīnglǐ pīzhǔnle zhège xiàngmù fāng'àn.
The manager approved this project plan.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 制定方案 | to formulate a plan |
| 提出方案 | to put forward a proposal |
| 实施方案 | to implement a plan |
| 项目方案 | project plan |
| 解决方案 | solution plan |

## Usage Notes

方案 is more formal and structured than 计划 (jìhuà, plan) and implies a detailed written proposal rather than just an intention. 解决方案 (jiějué fāng'àn) is a very common compound meaning "solution" (to a problem). The measure word is 个 (gè) or 套 (tào) for a set of plans.

## Memory Hook

方 means "direction/method" and 案 means "case/document on a desk" — a plan is a method written down as a document on your desk.
