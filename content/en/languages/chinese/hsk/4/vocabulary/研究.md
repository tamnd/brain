## 研究 (yánjiū)

研究 means to research or study something carefully, or refers to the research or study itself as a noun.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to research; to study; to investigate carefully
2. **[noun]** research; a study; careful investigation of a subject

## Example Sentences

**科学家正在研究这种新病毒。** Kēxuéjiā zhèngzài yánjiū zhè zhǒng xīn bìngdú. Scientists are researching this new virus.

**她的研究方向是人工智能。** Tā de yánjiū fāngxiàng shì réngōng zhìnéng. Her research direction is artificial intelligence.

**这项研究结果对医学有重要意义。** Zhè xiàng yánjiū jiéguǒ duì yīxué yǒu zhòngyào yìyì. The results of this research have important implications for medicine.

**我正在研究如何提高工作效率。** Wǒ zhèngzài yánjiū rúhé tígāo gōngzuò xiàolǜ. I am studying how to improve work efficiency.

## Usage Notes

研究 is one of the most versatile and frequent words in academic Chinese. As a verb it can mean anything from casually looking into something to conducting formal scientific investigation. The degree of rigor is determined by context. 研究生 (research student) means a graduate student or postgraduate.

## Memory Hook

研 means "to grind/study carefully" and 究 means "to investigate thoroughly": 研究 is grinding away with thorough investigation until you reach deep knowledge.
