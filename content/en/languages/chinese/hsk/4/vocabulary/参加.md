## 参加 (cānjiā)

参加 means to take part in or join an activity, event, or organization.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to participate in; to join; to attend; to take part in

## Example Sentences

**我想参加这次国际会议。** Wǒ xiǎng cānjiā zhè cì guójì huìyì. I want to participate in this international conference.

**他参加了学校的篮球队。** Tā cānjiāle xuéxiào de lánqiú duì. He joined the school basketball team.

**请问，我可以参加你们的活动吗？** Qǐngwèn, wǒ kěyǐ cānjiā nǐmen de huódòng ma? Excuse me, may I join your activity?

**她参加工作已经有五年了。** Tā cānjiā gōngzuò yǐjīng yǒu wǔ nián le. She has been working for five years already.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 参加比赛 | to participate in a competition |
| 参加会议 | to attend a meeting |
| 参加活动 | to join an activity |
| 参加工作 | to start working (join the workforce) |

## Usage Notes

参加 is one of the most common verbs in Chinese and is used across a wide range of contexts. The phrase 参加工作 specifically means to start one's career (entering the workforce) and is a set expression commonly used in official documents or resumes.

## Memory Hook

参 looks like a person putting up three arms in excitement, eager to 加 (add themselves) to an activity -- that enthusiasm is what 参加 (to join/participate) is all about.
