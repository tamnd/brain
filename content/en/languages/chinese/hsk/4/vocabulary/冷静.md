## 冷静 (lěngjìng)

冷静 describes a state of being calm, composed, and rational — especially when under pressure or in a difficult situation.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** calm, cool-headed, composed, level-headed

## Example Sentences

**遇到紧急情况时，一定要保持冷静。** Yùdào jǐnjí qíngkuàng shí, yīdìng yào bǎochí lěngjìng. When you encounter an emergency, you must stay calm.

**他在压力下依然很冷静，处理问题很有条理。** Tā zài yālì xià yīrán hěn lěngjìng, chǔlǐ wèntí hěn yǒu tiáolǐ. He remained very calm under pressure and handled problems in an organized way.

**先冷静一下，再做决定。** Xiān lěngjìng yīxià, zài zuò juédìng. Calm down first, then make a decision.

**她是一个冷静的领导者，从不轻易激动。** Tā shì yīgè lěngjìng de lǐngdǎozhě, cóng bù qīngyì jīdòng. She is a calm leader who never gets easily excited.

## Usage Notes

冷静 is often used in the imperative to advise someone to calm down: 冷静一下 (calm down a bit). It describes rational calmness rather than mere silence or stillness. Contrast with 平静 (peaceful/tranquil), which often refers to a peaceful environment or emotional state, whereas 冷静 implies mental clarity and rational composure.

## Memory Hook

冷 (cold) + 静 (still/quiet) = "cold and still" — like cold, still water that is not easily disturbed — perfectly composed.
