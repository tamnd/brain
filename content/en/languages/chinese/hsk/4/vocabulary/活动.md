## 活动 (huódòng)

活动 can mean an activity, event, or campaign as a noun, or to move about and be active as a verb.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** activity; event; campaign; an organized or social happening
2. **[verb]** to move; to exercise; to be active

## Example Sentences

**学校组织了很多课外活动。** Xuéxiào zǔzhī le hěn duō kèwài huódòng. The school organized many extracurricular activities.

**这次促销活动吸引了大量顾客。** Zhè cì cùxiāo huódòng xīyǐn le dàliàng gùkè. This promotional campaign attracted a large number of customers.

**早上起来活动一下身体很重要。** Zǎoshang qǐlái huódòng yīxià shēntǐ hěn zhòngyào. It is important to move your body in the morning after getting up.

**周末有很多文化活动可以参加。** Zhōumò yǒu hěn duō wénhuà huódòng kěyǐ cānjiā. There are many cultural events to attend on the weekend.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 课外活动 | extracurricular activity |
| 促销活动 | promotional campaign |
| 文化活动 | cultural event |
| 参加活动 | to participate in an activity |

## Usage Notes

As a noun 活动 covers a wide range of organized events from school activities to marketing campaigns. As a verb it means to move or exercise lightly, as in stretching or warming up. The noun usage is far more common at B1 level.

## Memory Hook

活 means "alive/living" and 动 means "to move": an activity is living movement, things that keep you alive and moving.
