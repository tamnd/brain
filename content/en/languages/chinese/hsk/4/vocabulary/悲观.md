## 悲观 (bēiguān)

悲观 describes a pessimistic outlook -- tending to see the worst in situations or expect negative outcomes.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** pessimistic
2. **[noun]** pessimism

## Example Sentences

**他对未来很悲观。** Tā duì wèilái hěn bēiguān. He is very pessimistic about the future.

**不要悲观，要相信自己。** Bùyào bēiguān, yào xiāngxìn zìjǐ. Don't be pessimistic; believe in yourself.

**她的悲观态度影响了整个团队。** Tā de bēiguān tàidù yǐngxiǎngle zhěnggè tuánduì. Her pessimistic attitude affected the whole team.

**悲观的人很难感到幸福。** Bēiguān de rén hěn nán gǎndào xìngfú. Pessimistic people find it hard to feel happy.

## Usage Notes

悲观 is the direct antonym of 乐观 (lèguān = optimistic). Both follow the same grammatical pattern: 对...悲观/乐观 (pessimistic/optimistic about...). 悲观 can serve as both an adjective (悲观的人 = pessimistic person) and a predicate (他很悲观 = he is pessimistic).

## Memory Hook

BEI (悲 = sad/grief) + GUAN (观 = view/perspective): having a sad view of everything -- that is being pessimistic.
