## 记录 (jìlù)

记录 means "to record" or "to document" as a verb, and as a noun it refers to a record, log, or written account of something.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to record; to document; to write down
2. **[noun]** record; log; documentation; (also: a sports record)

## Example Sentences

**请把会议内容记录下来。** Qǐng bǎ huìyì nèiróng jìlù xiàlái. Please record the content of the meeting.

**他打破了世界纪录。** Tā dǎpò le shìjiè jìlù. He broke the world record.

**这本日记记录了她的成长过程。** Zhè běn rìjì jìlù le tā de chéngzhǎng guòchéng. This diary documents her growth process.

**会议记录需要在明天之前发给大家。** Huìyì jìlù xūyào zài míngtiān zhīqián fā gěi dàjiā. The meeting minutes need to be sent to everyone by tomorrow.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 会议记录 | meeting minutes |
| 打破记录 | to break a record |
| 详细记录 | detailed record |
| 历史记录 | historical record |

## Usage Notes

记录 has two roles: as a verb (to record something) and as a noun (a record or log). When referring to sports achievements, 记录 and 纪录 are both used, though 纪录 (jìlù with a different character 纪) is more standard for athletic records. In everyday contexts, 记录 covers meeting notes, diary entries, and data logs.

## Memory Hook

记 (jì) means "to remember or note down" and 录 (lù) means "to write or record": 记录 is the act of writing something down to remember it.
