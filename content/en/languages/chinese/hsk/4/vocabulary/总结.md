## 总结 (zǒngjié)

总结 means to summarize or wrap up, used both as a verb (to summarize) and a noun (a summary or conclusion), especially at the end of an activity or period.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to summarize; to wrap up; to draw conclusions
2. **[noun]** a summary; a conclusion; a review

## Example Sentences

**老师在课末总结了今天的重点内容。** Lǎoshī zài kè mò zǒngjiéle jīntiān de zhòngdiǎn nèiróng. The teacher summarized the key content of today's lesson at the end of class.

**他写了一份年终总结，回顾了全年的工作。** Tā xiěle yī fèn nián zhōng zǒngjié, huígùle quánnián de gōngzuò. He wrote a year-end summary, reviewing the work of the whole year.

**总结经验教训，有助于我们未来做得更好。** Zǒngjié jīngyàn jiàoxun, yǒuzhù yú wǒmen wèilái zuò de gèng hǎo. Summarizing lessons learned helps us do better in the future.

**请你总结一下今天讨论的主要结论。** Qǐng nǐ zǒngjié yīxià jīntiān tǎolùn de zhǔyào jiélùn. Please summarize the main conclusions from today's discussion.

## Usage Notes

总结 implies a thorough review and drawing of conclusions, not just a brief recap. It is widely used in Chinese educational and professional settings. At the end of projects, semesters, or years, writing a 总结 is standard practice. It differs from 概括 (to generalize) in that it focuses more on lessons and conclusions.

## Memory Hook

总 means "to gather/total" and 结 means "to tie/conclude." So 总结 is to gather everything together and tie it into a neat conclusion: the perfect summary.
