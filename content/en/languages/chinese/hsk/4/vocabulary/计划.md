## 计划 (jìhuà)

计划 means a plan or schedule, and as a verb means to plan or intend to do something.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** plan; program; schedule
2. **[verb]** to plan; to intend; to schedule

## Example Sentences

**我们需要制定一个详细的计划。** Wǒmen xūyào zhìdìng yīgè xiángxì de jìhuà. We need to make a detailed plan.

**她计划明年去留学。** Tā jìhuà míngnián qù liúxué. She plans to study abroad next year.

**这个项目的计划已经完成了。** Zhège xiàngmù de jìhuà yǐjīng wánchéng le. The plan for this project has already been completed.

**按照计划，我们明天出发。** Ànzhào jìhuà, wǒmen míngtiān chūfā. According to the plan, we set off tomorrow.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 制定计划 (zhìdìng jìhuà) | to make a plan |
| 按计划 (àn jìhuà) | according to plan |
| 工作计划 (gōngzuò jìhuà) | work plan |
| 计划经济 (jìhuà jīngjì) | planned economy |

## Usage Notes

计划 as a verb is followed directly by a verb phrase: 计划去 (plan to go), 计划做 (plan to do). It is slightly more formal than 打算 (dǎsuàn), which also means "to plan/intend" but is more conversational. In business and academic settings, 计划 is strongly preferred.

## Memory Hook

计 (to calculate/count) + 划 (to draw/mark out) — to plan is to calculate and draw out every step before you begin.
