## 直接 (zhíjiē)

直接 means direct or straightforward, describing something done without any intermediary step or in a frank, unambiguous manner.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** direct; without intermediary; straightforward
2. **[adverb]** directly; straight; without going through an intermediate step

## Example Sentences

**有问题请直接告诉我。** Yǒu wèntí qǐng zhíjiē gàosù wǒ. If you have a problem, please tell me directly.

**这件事与你有直接关系。** Zhè jiàn shì yǔ nǐ yǒu zhíjiē guānxi. This matter is directly related to you.

**你可以直接联系负责人。** Nǐ kěyǐ zhíjiē liánxi fùzérén. You can contact the person in charge directly.

**他说话很直接，从不拐弯抹角。** Tā shuōhuà hěn zhíjiē, cóng bù guǎiwān mòjiǎo. He speaks very directly and never beats around the bush.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 直接联系 | to contact directly |
| 直接影响 | direct influence |
| 直接原因 | direct cause |
| 直接了当 | straightforward; to the point |

## Usage Notes

直接 can modify both verbs (直接说 means "say it directly") and nouns (直接原因 means "direct cause"). As an adverb it often implies skipping intermediate steps. The four-character phrase 直截了当 (zhíjié liǎodàng) means to be completely straightforward and is a common idiom.

## Memory Hook

直 means "straight" and 接 means "to connect/reach": 直接 is connecting straight, going from A to B without any detour.
