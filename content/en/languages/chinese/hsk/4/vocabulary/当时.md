## 当时 (dāngshí)

当时 refers to a specific moment in the past that has already been mentioned or is understood from context, meaning "at that time" or "back then."

## Meanings

1. **[adverb]** At that time; then; at the time (refers to a past moment in context).
2. **[noun]** That time; that moment (used as a time noun).

## Example Sentences

**我当时不知道他已经离开了。**
Wǒ dāngshí bù zhīdào tā yǐjīng líkāi le.
At that time, I did not know he had already left.

**当时的情况非常复杂。**
Dāngshí de qíngkuàng fēicháng fùzá.
The situation at that time was very complicated.

**他当时才二十岁，但已经很有经验了。**
Tā dāngshí cái èrshí suì, dàn yǐjīng hěn yǒu jīngyàn le.
At the time, he was only twenty years old, but already very experienced.

**我们当时都很高兴，没想到后来发生了那些事。**
Wǒmen dāngshí dōu hěn gāoxìng, méi xiǎngdào hòulái fāshēng le nàxiē shì.
We were all very happy at the time; we had no idea what would happen later.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 当时的情况 | the situation at that time |
| 当时决定 | decided at that time |
| 回想当时 | looking back at that time |
| 就在当时 | right at that moment |

## Usage Notes

当时 always refers to a past time already established in the conversation or text. It cannot refer to the present or future. For the present moment, use 此时 or 现在; for a future moment, use 到时候 or 那时.

## Memory Hook

当 means "at the time of" and 时 means "time"; 当时 points directly to a specific moment in the past, like a finger on a timeline.
