## 预计 (yùjì)

预计 means "to expect," "to estimate," or "to anticipate" and is used when making a formal or calculated estimate about a future event or outcome.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to expect; to estimate; to anticipate; to project

## Example Sentences

**预计明年公司的利润会增加。** Yùjì míngnián gōngsī de lìrùn huì zēngjiā. It is expected that the company's profits will increase next year.

**这项工程预计需要三年时间完成。** Zhè xiàng gōngchéng yùjì xūyào sān nián shíjiān wánchéng. This project is estimated to take three years to complete.

**预计到达时间是下午两点。** Yùjì dàodá shíjiān shì xiàwǔ liǎng diǎn. The estimated arrival time is 2 p.m.

**与预计相比，实际结果更好。** Yǔ yùjì xiāng bǐ, shíjì jiéguǒ gèng hǎo. Compared to expectations, the actual results were better.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 预计时间 | estimated time |
| 预计完成 | expected to complete |
| 与预计相比 | compared to expectations |
| 超出预计 | to exceed expectations |

## Usage Notes

预计 is often used in reports, announcements, and planning documents to state expected outcomes. It tends to be more formal and data-driven than 估计 (gūjì, to estimate roughly). A common pattern is 预计 + time phrase + result, as in 预计三年后完成 (expected to be completed in three years). It can also be used impersonally at the start of a sentence.

## Memory Hook

预 (yù) means "in advance" and 计 (jì) means "to plan or calculate": 预计 is planning or calculating ahead, forming an expectation.
