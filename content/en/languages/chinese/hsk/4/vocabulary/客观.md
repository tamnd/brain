## 客观 (kèguān)

客观 describes something that is objective or impartial, based on facts and evidence rather than personal opinions or emotions.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** objective; impartial; unbiased; based on facts

## Example Sentences

**我们需要客观地看待这个问题。** Wǒmen xūyào kèguān de kàndài zhège wèntí. We need to look at this problem objectively.

**他的评价非常客观，没有偏见。** Tā de píngjià fēicháng kèguān, méiyǒu piānjiàn. His evaluation was very objective, with no bias.

**做决定时，要尽量保持客观。** Zuò juédìng shí, yào jǐnliàng bǎochí kèguān. When making decisions, try to stay objective.

**这份报告提供了客观的数据分析。** Zhè fèn bàogào tígōngle kèguān de shùjù fēnxī. This report provided an objective analysis of the data.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |

| 客观评价 | objective evaluation |

## Usage Notes

客观 is the opposite of 主观 (subjective). At B1 level, it often appears in academic, journalistic, and business writing. It can modify nouns (客观分析) or serve as a predicate adjective (很客观). The adverb form 客观地 is common before verbs.

## Memory Hook

客 means "guest" (an outsider) and 观 means "to observe." An outside observer has no personal stake, so they see things objectively. A guest watching from outside is perfectly objective.
