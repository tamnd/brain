## 简洁 (jiǎnjié)

简洁 means concise, brief, and clear, describing language, design, or style that is free of unnecessary elements.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** concise; brief; succinct; clean and clear without unnecessary elements

## Example Sentences

**他的演讲风格简洁有力。** Tā de yǎnjiǎng fēnggé jiǎnjié yǒulì. His speaking style is concise and powerful.

**好的设计应该简洁美观。** Hǎo de shèjì yīnggāi jiǎnjié měiguān. Good design should be concise and attractive.

**请用简洁的语言表达你的想法。** Qǐng yòng jiǎnjié de yǔyán biǎodá nǐ de xiǎngfǎ. Please express your ideas in concise language.

**这份报告写得简洁清晰，很容易理解。** Zhè fèn bàogào xiě de jiǎnjié qīngxī, hěn róngyì lǐjiě. This report is written concisely and clearly, making it easy to understand.

## Usage Notes

简洁 refers to brevity combined with clarity and elegance. It differs from 简单 (simple), which may imply something easy or plain without necessarily being well-crafted. 简洁 implies quality through reduction: everything needed is there, and nothing extra is added.

## Memory Hook

简 means "simple/brief" and 洁 means "clean/pure": 简洁 is simple and clean, stripped down to its pure, essential form.
