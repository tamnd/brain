## 经济 (jīngjì)

经济 refers to the economy of a country or region, the field of economics, or colloquially something that is economical or cost-effective.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Economy; economics; financial situation.
2. **[adjective]** Economical; cost-effective; thrifty.

## Example Sentences

**中国的经济近年来发展迅速。**
Zhōngguó de jīngjì jìnnián lái fāzhǎn xùnsù.
China's economy has developed rapidly in recent years.

**全球经济正面临很多不确定因素。**
Quánqiú jīngjì zhèng miànlín hěn duō bù quèdìng yīnsù.
The global economy is currently facing many uncertainties.

**这条路线更经济，能省不少油。**
Zhè tiáo lùxiàn gèng jīngjì, néng shěng bùshǎo yóu.
This route is more economical and can save quite a bit of fuel.

**他在大学学的是经济学。**
Tā zài dàxué xué de shì jīngjìxué.
He studied economics at university.

## Usage Notes

经济 most commonly appears as a noun referring to the economy ("国家经济," national economy). As an adjective it means "economical/cost-saving," often used to describe travel classes or budget options. 经济学 is the academic discipline of economics.

## Memory Hook

经 means "to pass through/manage" and 济 means "to help/benefit" — managing resources to benefit everyone is the essence of the economy.
