## 领导 (lǐngdǎo)

领导 means "leader," "leadership," or "to lead" and refers to guiding or being in charge of a group, organization, or effort.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to lead; to guide; to head
2. **[noun]** leader; leadership; (a person in authority)

## Example Sentences

**她被选为团队的领导。** Tā bèi xuǎn wéi tuánduì de lǐngdǎo. She was selected as the team leader.

**好的领导要能够激励团队成员。** Hǎo de lǐngdǎo yào nénggòu jīlì tuánduì chéngyuán. A good leader must be able to motivate team members.

**他领导这家公司走向了成功。** Tā lǐngdǎo zhè jiā gōngsī zǒuxiàng le chénggōng. He led this company toward success.

**部门领导要求大家按时完成任务。** Bùmén lǐngdǎo yāoqiú dàjiā ànshí wánchéng rènwù. The department leader asked everyone to complete tasks on time.

## Usage Notes

领导 is both a verb and a noun. As a noun, it can refer to a specific leader or to the concept of leadership. In Chinese professional culture, 领导 is a respectful term for someone in authority, and addressing or referring to superiors as 领导 is common. 领导力 (lǐngdǎolì) is the more abstract term for "leadership" as a quality or skill.

## Memory Hook

领 (lǐng) means "to lead or guide" and 导 (dǎo) also means "to guide or direct": both characters mean guiding, reinforcing the concept of leadership through double emphasis.
