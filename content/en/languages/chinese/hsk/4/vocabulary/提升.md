## 提升 (tíshēng)

提升 means to improve, upgrade, or promote, describing the act of raising something to a higher level, whether in quality, rank, or capability.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to improve; to upgrade; to promote; to enhance; to elevate

## Example Sentences

**他努力提升自己的工作能力。** Tā nǔlì tíshēng zìjǐ de gōngzuò nénglì. He works hard to improve his professional abilities.

**公司决定提升她为部门经理。** Gōngsī juédìng tíshēng tā wéi bùmén jīnglǐ. The company decided to promote her to department manager.

**这次培训有效地提升了员工的技能水平。** Zhè cì péixùn yǒuxiào de tíshēngle yuángōng de jìnéng shuǐpíng. This training effectively improved employees' skill levels.

**多读书可以提升你的思维能力。** Duō dúshū kěyǐ tíshēng nǐ de sīwéi nénglì. Reading more can enhance your thinking ability.

## Usage Notes

提升 implies moving upward to a higher level. It is used for both personal improvement (skills, abilities) and organizational changes (promotions, upgrades). It is slightly more formal and dynamic than 提高 (to raise/improve), though the two are often interchangeable. 提升 tends to emphasize the positive upward movement more strongly.

## Memory Hook

提 means "to lift" and 升 means "to rise/ascend." So 提升 is to lift something so it rises: elevating quality, rank, or ability to a higher position.
