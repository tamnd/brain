## 学生 (xuéshēng)

学生 is the general term for a student or pupil at any educational level.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** student; pupil; learner

## Example Sentences

**这所学校有三千名学生。** Zhè suǒ xuéxiào yǒu sānqiān míng xuéshēng. This school has three thousand students.

**老师鼓励学生积极发言。** Lǎoshī gǔlì xuéshēng jījí fāyán. The teacher encourages students to actively speak up.

**他是一名非常认真的学生，从不缺课。** Tā shì yī míng fēicháng rènzhēn de xuéshēng, cóng bù quē kè. He is a very conscientious student who never misses class.

**大学生和中学生都可以申请这个奖学金。** Dàxuéshēng hé zhōngxuéshēng dōu kěyǐ shēnqǐng zhège jiǎngxuéjīn. Both university students and high school students can apply for this scholarship.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 大学生 | university/college student |
| 中学生 | middle/high school student |
| 留学生 | international/overseas student |
| 优秀学生 | outstanding student |

## Usage Notes

学生 is one of the most basic and common nouns in Chinese. It can refer to students at any level. When combined with 大学 (university), 中学 (middle/high school), or 小学 (elementary school), it specifies the level. 留学生 specifically refers to international students studying abroad.

## Memory Hook

学 means "to study" and 生 means "person/life" -- a 学生 is a person whose life is defined by studying, a student.
