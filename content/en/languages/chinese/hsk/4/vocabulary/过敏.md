## 过敏 (guòmǐn)

过敏 describes an allergic reaction or the state of being allergic to something. It is commonly used in medical consultations and everyday health conversations.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** allergy; an allergic condition
2. **[verb]** to be allergic; to have an allergic reaction

## Example Sentences

**我对花粉过敏，每年春天都很难受。** Wǒ duì huāfěn guòmǐn, měi nián chūntiān dōu hěn nánshòu. I am allergic to pollen, and every spring is very uncomfortable for me.

**他吃了海鲜之后出现了过敏反应。** Tā chī le hǎixiān zhīhòu chūxiàn le guòmǐn fǎnyìng. He had an allergic reaction after eating seafood.

**去看医生之前，告诉他你对哪些药过敏。** Qù kàn yīshēng zhīqián, gàosù tā nǐ duì nǎxiē yào guòmǐn. Before seeing the doctor, tell him which medicines you are allergic to.

**皮肤过敏的时候不要抓，要去看皮肤科。** Pífū guòmǐn de shíhòu bùyào zhuā, yào qù kàn pífū kē. When you have a skin allergy, don't scratch — go see a dermatologist.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 对...过敏 | to be allergic to... |
| 过敏反应 | allergic reaction |
| 过敏症状 | allergy symptoms |
| 食物过敏 | food allergy |

## Usage Notes

过敏 is used as both a verb and a noun. As a verb, it takes the pattern 对...过敏 (duì...guòmǐn), meaning "to be allergic to something." For example: 我对青霉素过敏 (I am allergic to penicillin). As a noun, it often appears in compounds like 过敏反应 (allergic reaction) and 过敏症状 (allergy symptoms). 过 here means "excessively" and 敏 means "sensitive."

## Memory Hook

过 (excessively) + 敏 (sensitive) — an allergy is simply being "excessively sensitive" to something harmless.
