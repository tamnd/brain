## 转变 (zhuǎnbiàn)

转变 means "to transform," "to change," or "transition" and describes a significant shift in nature, form, attitude, or direction.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to transform; to change; to shift; to convert
2. **[noun]** transformation; change; transition

## Example Sentences

**他的态度发生了很大的转变。** Tā de tàidù fāshēng le hěn dà de zhuǎnbiàn. His attitude underwent a great transformation.

**这个城市正在从工业城市向服务业城市转变。** Zhège chéngshì zhèngzài cóng gōngyè chéngshì xiàng fúwùyè chéngshì zhuǎnbiàn. This city is transforming from an industrial city to a service-oriented city.

**我们需要转变思路，寻找新的解决方案。** Wǒmen xūyào zhuǎnbiàn sīlù, xúnzhǎo xīn de jiějué fāng'àn. We need to change our thinking and find new solutions.

**经济的转变带来了新的发展机遇。** Jīngjì de zhuǎnbiàn dàilái le xīn de fāzhǎn jīyù. The economic transformation has brought new development opportunities.

## Collocations

| 发生转变 | to undergo a transformation |

## Usage Notes

转变 implies a more fundamental or directional shift than 变化 (biànhuà, change), which can describe any kind of change. 转变 often suggests moving from one clearly defined state to another, such as from a negative to a positive attitude, or from one industry to another. It is commonly used in formal speech and writing.

## Memory Hook

转 (zhuǎn) means "to turn or rotate" and 变 (biàn) means "to change": 转变 is turning around to become something different.
