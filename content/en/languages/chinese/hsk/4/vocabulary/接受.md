## 接受 (jiēshòu)

接受 means to accept or receive, implying a willingness to take something offered, whether an object, idea, criticism, or invitation.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to accept; to receive; to take; to agree to

## Example Sentences

**她接受了公司的录用通知。** Tā jiēshòule gōngsī de lùyòng tōngzhī. She accepted the company's job offer.

**他很难接受失败，总是想着再试一次。** Tā hěn nán jiēshòu shībài, zǒngshì xiǎngzhe zài shì yī cì. He has difficulty accepting failure and always thinks about trying again.

**请接受我真诚的道歉。** Qǐng jiēshòu wǒ zhēnchéng de dàoqiàn. Please accept my sincere apology.

**我们应该接受不同的观点和看法。** Wǒmen yīnggāi jiēshòu bùtóng de guāndiǎn hé kànfǎ. We should accept different viewpoints and perspectives.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 接受批评 | to accept criticism |
| 接受邀请 | to accept an invitation |
| 接受教育 | to receive education |
| 难以接受 | difficult to accept |

## Usage Notes

接受 is active and implies a degree of willingness or consent. It is different from 收到 (to receive a physical item), as 接受 has a psychological element of agreeing or acknowledging. Common collocations include accepting criticism (接受批评), accepting an invitation (接受邀请), and receiving education (接受教育).

## Memory Hook

接 means "to connect/receive" and 受 means "to receive/undergo." Both characters relate to receiving, creating a strong sense of taking something in and making it yours: full acceptance.
