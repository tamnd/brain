## 预防 (yùfáng)

预防 means to prevent or take precautionary measures against something undesirable, especially illness or disaster. It is widely used in public health and safety contexts.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to prevent; to take precautions against
2. **[noun]** prevention; precautionary measures

## Example Sentences

**洗手是预防传染病最有效的方法之一。** Xǐshǒu shì yùfáng chuánrǎnbìng zuì yǒuxiào de fāngfǎ zhī yī. Washing hands is one of the most effective methods of preventing infectious diseases.

**政府采取了多种措施来预防流感的传播。** Zhèngfǔ cǎiqǔ le duō zhǒng cuòshī lái yùfáng liúgǎn de chuánbō. The government took various measures to prevent the spread of influenza.

**预防疾病比治疗疾病更经济、更有效。** Yùfáng jíbìng bǐ zhìliáo jíbìng gèng jīngjì, gèng yǒuxiào. Preventing disease is more economical and effective than treating it.

**打疫苗是预防很多传染病的好方法。** Dǎ yìmiáo shì yùfáng hěn duō chuánrǎnbìng de hǎo fāngfǎ. Getting vaccinated is a good way to prevent many infectious diseases.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 预防疾病 | to prevent disease |
| 预防措施 | preventive measures |
| 预防为主 | prevention first |
| 预防传染 | to prevent infection |

## Usage Notes

预防 functions as both a verb and a noun. As a verb, it is typically followed by the thing being prevented: 预防感冒 (prevent a cold), 预防事故 (prevent accidents). The phrase 预防为主 (prevention first) is a key principle in Chinese public health policy. 预防 is more formal than 防止 (fángzhǐ), though both mean "to prevent" — 防止 is more common in everyday speech.

## Memory Hook

预 (in advance) + 防 (to defend/guard) — prevention means "defending in advance" before a problem arises.
