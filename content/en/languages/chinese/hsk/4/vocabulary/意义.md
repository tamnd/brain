## 意义 (yìyì)

意义 refers to the deeper meaning, significance, or value of something, going beyond simple dictionary definitions to encompass purpose and importance.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Meaning; significance; purpose (the deeper value or importance of something).

## Example Sentences

**这项工作对社会有重要的意义。**
Zhè xiàng gōngzuò duì shèhuì yǒu zhòngyào de yìyì.
This work has important significance for society.

**他认为旅行的意义在于了解不同文化。**
Tā rènwéi lǚxíng de yìyì zàiyú liǎojiě bùtóng wénhuà.
He believes the meaning of travel lies in understanding different cultures.

**这件事对我来说没有任何意义。**
Zhè jiàn shì duì wǒ lái shuō méiyǒu rènhé yìyì.
This matter has no meaning for me whatsoever.

**纪念这一天是有深远意义的。**
Jìniàn zhè yī tiān shì yǒu shēnyuǎn yìyì de.
Commemorating this day has far-reaching significance.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 有意义 | to be meaningful; significant |

| 历史意义 | historical significance |

## Usage Notes

有意义 (meaningful) and 没有意义 (meaningless) are very common phrases. 意义 emphasizes the intrinsic value or deeper purpose of something, while 意思 (yìsi) usually refers to the literal meaning of a word or the point of what someone is saying.

## Memory Hook

意 means "meaning/intent" and 义 means "righteousness/principle"; 意义 is the principled intent behind something, its deeper purpose.
