## 稳定 (wěndìng)

稳定 means stable or steady as an adjective, or to stabilize something as a verb, describing a state of balance and consistency.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** stable; steady; consistent; not subject to sudden change
2. **[verb]** to stabilize; to make steady or secure

## Example Sentences

**他有一份稳定的工作和收入。** Tā yǒu yī fèn wěndìng de gōngzuò hé shōurù. He has a stable job and income.

**政府采取措施稳定市场价格。** Zhèngfǔ cǎiqǔ cuòshī wěndìng shìchǎng jiàgé. The government took measures to stabilize market prices.

**她的情绪很稳定，从不轻易发火。** Tā de qíngxù hěn wěndìng, cóng bù qīngyì fāhuǒ. Her emotions are very stable; she never loses her temper easily.

**经济稳定对社会发展非常重要。** Jīngjì wěndìng duì shèhuì fāzhǎn fēicháng zhòngyào. Economic stability is very important for social development.

## Usage Notes

稳定 is widely used in economics, politics, and personal life contexts. The verb usage (to stabilize) is common with institutions as subjects: 政府稳定市场 (the government stabilizes the market). Its antonym is 动荡 (turbulent, unstable).

## Memory Hook

稳 means "steady/firm" and 定 means "fixed/settled": 稳定 is being firmly settled, anchored in place without swaying.
