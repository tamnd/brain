## 同时 (tóngshí)

同时 means at the same time or simultaneously, and is also used as a conjunction to add supplementary information or a parallel point.

## Meanings

1. **[adverb]** at the same time, simultaneously
2. **[conjunction]** at the same time, moreover, in addition (linking two simultaneous facts)

## Example Sentences

**他同时做好几件事，效率非常高。** Tā tóngshí zuò hǎo jǐ jiàn shì, xiàolǜ fēicháng gāo. He does several things at the same time with very high efficiency.

**这个方案既节省时间，同时也降低了成本。** Zhège fāng'àn jì jiéshěng shíjiān, tóngshí yě jiàngdī le chéngběn. This plan saves time while simultaneously reducing costs.

**我们同时收到了来自多个客户的反馈。** Wǒmen tóngshí shōudào le láizì duō gè kèhù de fǎnkuì. We received feedback from multiple clients at the same time.

**同时，我们也需要注意风险管理。** Tóngshí, wǒmen yě xūyào zhùyì fēngxiǎn guǎnlǐ. At the same time, we also need to pay attention to risk management.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 同时进行 | proceed simultaneously |
| 同时也 | at the same time, also |
| 同时兼顾 | balance at the same time |
| 同时发生 | occur at the same time |

## Usage Notes

同时 serves both as a time adverb (happening simultaneously) and a discourse connector (similar to "meanwhile" or "at the same time, it should be noted that"). When used at the start of a sentence, it introduces an additional relevant point. It is a useful connective in formal writing and speeches.

## Memory Hook

同 means "same" and 时 means "time," so 同时 is simply "same time," at the same moment.
