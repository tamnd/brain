## 曾经 (céngjīng)

曾经 indicates that something happened at some point in the past, with the implication that the situation may have since changed, equivalent to "once," "used to," or "formerly."

## Meanings

1. **[adverb]** Once; formerly; at some point in the past (an action or state that occurred before, now possibly ended).

## Example Sentences

**我曾经在北京住过三年。**
Wǒ céngjīng zài Běijīng zhù guò sān nián.
I once lived in Beijing for three years.

**他曾经是一名职业运动员。**
Tā céngjīng shì yī míng zhíyè yùndòngyuán.
He was once a professional athlete.

**她曾经梦想成为一名医生。**
Tā céngjīng mèngxiǎng chéngwéi yī míng yīshēng.
She once dreamed of becoming a doctor.

**这里曾经是一片森林，现在已经变成了城市。**
Zhèlǐ céngjīng shì yī piàn sēnlín, xiànzài yǐjīng biànchéng le chéngshì.
This place was once a forest; it has now become a city.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 曾经是 | was once; used to be |
| 曾经有过 | once had; there was once |
| 曾经去过 | has been to (at some point) |
| 曾经经历 | once experienced |

## Usage Notes

曾经 is often followed by 过 at the end of the verb phrase to reinforce the completed past experience: 曾经去过 (has been there once). It always refers to the past and cannot be used for present or future contexts.

## Memory Hook

曾 relates to "a past era" and 经 means "to pass through/experience"; 曾经 marks an experience that was passed through at some point in history.
