## 另外 (lìngwài)

另外 is used to introduce additional information or a separate item, similar to "in addition," "besides," or "additionally" in English.

## Meanings

1. **[adverb/conjunction]** in addition, besides, additionally, or another/separate one

## Example Sentences

**我买了一本书，另外还买了一支笔。** Wǒ mǎi le yī běn shū, lìngwài hái mǎi le yī zhī bǐ. I bought a book and also bought a pen in addition.

**另外，我还有一个问题想问你。** Lìngwài, wǒ hái yǒu yīgè wèntí xiǎng wèn nǐ. In addition, I have one more question to ask you.

**这个问题很复杂，另外还涉及法律问题。** Zhège wèntí hěn fùzá, lìngwài hái shèjí fǎlǜ wèntí. This issue is complex; in addition, it also involves legal matters.

**我们需要两个人来完成这项工作，另外还需要一些工具。** Wǒmen xūyào liǎng gè rén lái wánchéng zhè xiàng gōngzuò, lìngwài hái xūyào yīxiē gōngjù. We need two people to complete this work and additionally some tools.

## Usage Notes

另外 can appear at the beginning of a sentence to introduce a new, supplementary point (like "furthermore"), or within a sentence to indicate "another" item. It differs from 此外 (cǐwài, moreover), which is slightly more formal. 另外 is very natural in both written and spoken Chinese at the B1 level.

## Memory Hook

另 means "another" and 外 means "outside," so 另外 is "another thing outside" what was already mentioned, something additional.
