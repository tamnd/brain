## 上升 (shàngshēng)

上升 describes movement upward in a physical, numerical, or abstract sense, commonly used when talking about prices, temperatures, or rankings rising.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to rise, go up, or ascend in position, level, or quantity

## Example Sentences

**气温上升了五度。** Qìwēn shàngshēng le wǔ dù. The temperature rose by five degrees.

**公司的销售额每年都在上升。** Gōngsī de xiāoshòu'é měi nián dōu zài shàngshēng. The company's sales increase every year.

**他的成绩不断上升。** Tā de chéngjì búduàn shàngshēng. His grades keep going up.

**物价上升让很多人感到压力。** Wùjià shàngshēng ràng hěn duō rén gǎndào yālì. Rising prices put a lot of pressure on many people.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 价格上升 | prices rise |
| 温度上升 | temperature rises |
| 不断上升 | continuously rising |
| 迅速上升 | rise rapidly |

## Usage Notes

上升 is typically used for gradual or measurable increases in data, temperature, rankings, or status. It pairs well with adverbs like 不断 (continuously) and 迅速 (rapidly). For a more sudden leap upward, 上涨 is sometimes preferred for prices specifically, while 上升 covers broader contexts.

## Memory Hook

Imagine a sun (上 means "above") slowly rising (升 means "to rise") above the horizon each morning.
