## 合理 (hélǐ)

合理 describes something that conforms to reason, logic, fairness, or established principles. It is used to evaluate whether a decision, plan, price, request, or arrangement makes sense and can be justified.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** Reasonable; rational; sensible; justified; fair.

## Example Sentences

**这个价格是合理的，符合市场行情。**
Zhège jiàgé shì hélǐ de, fúhé shìchǎng hángqíng.
This price is reasonable and consistent with market rates.

**他的要求非常合理，公司应该予以考虑。**
Tā de yāoqiú fēicháng hélǐ, gōngsī yīnggāi yǔyǐ kǎolǜ.
His request is very reasonable, and the company should give it consideration.

**在制定工作计划时，要合理分配时间和资源。**
Zài zhìdìng gōngzuò jìhuà shí, yào hélǐ fēnpèi shíjiān hé zīyuán.
When drawing up a work plan, you should allocate time and resources reasonably.

**这项政策的设计不够合理，有待进一步完善。**
Zhè xiàng zhèngcè de shèjì bùgòu hélǐ, yǒudài jìnyībù wánshàn.
The design of this policy is not sufficiently rational and needs further improvement.

## Usage Notes

合理 is the opposite of 不合理 (unreasonable) and is close in meaning to 合适 (héshì, suitable/appropriate), but 合理 emphasizes logical or principled justification, while 合适 focuses on fitness for a particular situation. 合理化 (hélǐhuà) means "to rationalize" or "to make more rational/efficient" and is common in business restructuring discussions.

## Memory Hook

合 means "to fit/combine" and 理 means "principle/reason." Something 合理 fits neatly with reason and principle — like a key that fits perfectly in its lock.
