## 安慰 (ānwèi)

安慰 means to comfort or console someone who is sad, worried, or distressed.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to comfort; to console; to soothe
2. **[noun]** comfort; consolation; solace

## Example Sentences

**朋友的话给了她很大的安慰。** Péngyǒu de huà gěile tā hěn dà de ānwèi. Her friend's words gave her great comfort.

**她失败后，家人纷纷赶来安慰她。** Tā shībài hòu, jiārén fēnfēn gǎn lái ānwèi tā. After she failed, her family all rushed over to comfort her.

**有时候，一个拥抱胜过千言万语的安慰。** Yǒu shíhòu, yī gè yǒngbào shèngguò qiānyán wànyǔ de ānwèi. Sometimes a hug is better than a thousand words of comfort.

**你不用担心，我只是想来安慰你一下。** Nǐ bù yòng dānxīn, wǒ zhǐshì xiǎng lái ānwèi nǐ yīxià. Don't worry; I just came to comfort you a little.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 给予安慰 | to offer comfort |
| 得到安慰 | to receive comfort |
| 精神安慰 | emotional/spiritual comfort |
| 安慰奖 | consolation prize |

## Usage Notes

安慰 is used when someone is emotionally distressed and needs support. As a verb, it takes a person as its object. As a noun, it is often preceded by 给 (to give) or 得到 (to receive). The compound 安慰奖 (consolation prize) is a commonly used fixed expression in competitions.

## Memory Hook

安 means "peaceful/calm" and 慰 means "to soothe/relieve" -- 安慰 is making someone's heart peaceful and soothed, i.e., comforting them.
