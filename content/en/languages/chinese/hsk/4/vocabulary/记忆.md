## 记忆 (jìyì)

记忆 means "memory" as a noun and "to memorize" or "to remember" as a verb, referring to the mental faculty of retaining and recalling information or past experiences.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** memory; recollection; remembrance
2. **[verb]** to memorize; to commit to memory

## Example Sentences

**那段童年的记忆至今难忘。** Nà duàn tóngnián de jìyì zhìjīn nánwàng. Those childhood memories are still unforgettable to this day.

**她的记忆力非常好，什么都记得住。** Tā de jìyìlì fēicháng hǎo, shénme dōu jìde zhù. Her memory is very good; she can remember everything.

**这首歌唤起了他的美好记忆。** Zhè shǒu gē huànqǐ le tā de měihǎo jìyì. This song evoked his beautiful memories.

**学习新单词需要反复记忆。** Xuéxí xīn dāncí xūyào fǎnfù jìyì. Learning new vocabulary requires repeated memorization.

## Usage Notes

记忆 as a noun refers to both the faculty of memory (记忆力, memory ability) and specific memories (一段记忆, a memory). As a verb, it is more formal than 记住 (jìzhù), which means to successfully remember something. 记忆 as a verb is common in educational or psychological contexts.

## Memory Hook

记 (jì) means "to note down" and 忆 (yì) means "to recall or reminisce": 记忆 is the cycle of recording and recalling that forms a memory.
