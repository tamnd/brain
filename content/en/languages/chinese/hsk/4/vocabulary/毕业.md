## 毕业 (bìyè)

毕业 means to graduate or finish a course of study, marking the completion of formal education at a school or university.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to graduate; to complete a course of study
2. **[noun]** graduation; the act of completing one's studies

## Example Sentences

**她去年从大学毕业了。** Tā qùnián cóng dàxué bìyè le. She graduated from university last year.

**毕业以后，他找到了一份好工作。** Bìyè yǐhòu, tā zhǎodào le yī fèn hǎo gōngzuò. After graduating, he found a good job.

**毕业典礼定在六月举行。** Bìyè diǎnlǐ dìng zài liùyuè jǔxíng. The graduation ceremony is scheduled to be held in June.

**我的毕业论文还没写完。** Wǒ de bìyè lùnwén hái méi xiě wán. My graduation thesis is not finished yet.

## Usage Notes

毕业 is a verb-object compound. It is followed by 于 or 从 to indicate the school: 毕业于北京大学 (graduated from Peking University). The related noun 毕业生 means a graduate or graduating student and is very common in job-related discussions.

## Memory Hook

毕 means "to finish/complete" and 业 means "studies/career": 毕业 is completing your studies, the end of your academic career chapter.
