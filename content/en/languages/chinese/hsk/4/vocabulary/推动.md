## 推动 (tuīdòng)

推动 means to cause something to advance, develop, or progress, either physically or figuratively, equivalent to "to push forward," "to promote," or "to drive."

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To push forward; to promote; to drive; to propel (to cause advancement or development).

## Example Sentences

**科技的发展推动了社会的进步。**
Kējì de fāzhǎn tuīdòng le shèhuì de jìnbù.
The development of technology has driven social progress.

**政府出台了新政策来推动经济发展。**
Zhèngfǔ chūtái le xīn zhèngcè lái tuīdòng jīngjì fāzhǎn.
The government introduced new policies to promote economic development.

**他的热情推动了整个团队前进。**
Tā de rèqíng tuīdòng le zhěnggè tuánduì qiánjìn.
His enthusiasm pushed the entire team forward.

**这次会议的目标是推动双方合作。**
Zhè cì huìyì de mùbiāo shì tuīdòng shuāngfāng hézuò.
The goal of this meeting is to promote cooperation between both parties.

## Usage Notes

推动 is most often used with abstract objects such as development (发展), progress (进步), and reform (改革). The related noun 推动力 means "driving force" or "impetus." 促进 is a near-synonym but tends to be used for smaller-scale encouragement.

## Memory Hook

推 means "to push" and 动 means "to move"; 推动 is the act of pushing something until it starts moving forward on its own.
