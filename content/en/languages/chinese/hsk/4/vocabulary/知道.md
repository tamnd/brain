## 知道 (zhīdào)

知道 is one of the most common verbs in Chinese, meaning to know or be aware of a fact, situation, or piece of information.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to know, to be aware of, to realize

## Example Sentences

**你知道他住在哪里吗？** Nǐ zhīdào tā zhù zài nǎlǐ ma? Do you know where he lives?

**我不知道这件事。** Wǒ bù zhīdào zhè jiàn shì. I don't know about this matter.

**她知道自己做错了。** Tā zhīdào zìjǐ zuò cuò le. She knows she made a mistake.

**我刚刚知道这个好消息。** Wǒ gānggāng zhīdào zhège hǎo xiāoxi. I just found out about this good news.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 不知道 (bù zhīdào) | don't know |
| 知道了 (zhīdào le) | got it, I see, understood |
| 让……知道 (ràng...zhīdào) | to let someone know |
| 早就知道 (zǎo jiù zhīdào) | knew long ago |

## Usage Notes

知道 is used for factual knowledge (knowing that something is the case), while 认识 (rènshi) means to know a person or recognize something through familiarity. You cannot use 知道 for people: say 我认识他 (I know him), not 我知道他. The common response 知道了 (zhīdào le) means "understood" or "got it" in everyday conversation.

## Memory Hook

知 means "know" and 道 means "way/path" — you have found the "path of knowing" when you 知道 something.
