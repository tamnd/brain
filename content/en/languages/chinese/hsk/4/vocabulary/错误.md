## 错误 (cuòwù)

错误 refers to a mistake or error, or describes something as wrong or incorrect.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** mistake; error; fault
2. **[adjective]** wrong; incorrect; mistaken

## Example Sentences

**我犯了一个很严重的错误。** Wǒ fàn le yīgè hěn yánzhòng de cuòwù. I made a very serious mistake.

**承认错误是改正的第一步。** Chéngrèn cuòwù shì gǎizhèng de dì yī bù. Admitting a mistake is the first step to correcting it.

**这个决定是完全错误的。** Zhège juédìng shì wánquán cuòwù de. This decision is completely wrong.

**我们要从错误中吸取教训。** Wǒmen yào cóng cuòwù zhōng xīqǔ jiàoxun. We need to learn lessons from our mistakes.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 犯错误 (fàn cuòwù) | to make a mistake |
| 承认错误 (chéngrèn cuòwù) | to admit a mistake |
| 纠正错误 (jiūzhèng cuòwù) | to correct a mistake |
| 严重错误 (yánzhòng cuòwù) | serious mistake |

## Usage Notes

错误 is the formal noun for "mistake" or "error." In informal speech, 错 (cuò) alone is often used predicatively: 你错了 (you are wrong). The phrase 犯错误 (to commit an error) is very common in both spoken and written Chinese. 错误 contrasts with 正确 (zhèngquè, correct) as its direct antonym.

## Memory Hook

错 (wrong/crossed) + 误 (to mislead/miss) — when things get crossed and you miss the mark, that is an error.
