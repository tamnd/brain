## 作用 (zuòyòng)

作用 refers to the function, effect, or role that something has — what it does or how it influences something else.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** function, effect, role, action (what something does or contributes)

## Example Sentences

**睡眠对健康有很重要的作用。** Shuìmián duì jiànkāng yǒu hěn zhòngyào de zuòyòng. Sleep plays a very important role in health.

**这种药对感冒有很好的作用。** Zhè zhǒng yào duì gǎnmào yǒu hěn hǎo de zuòyòng. This medicine has a good effect on the common cold.

**团队合作发挥了很大的作用。** Tuánduì hézuò fāhuī le hěn dà de zuòyòng. Teamwork played a big role.

**锻炼对减肥有直接的作用。** Duànliàn duì jiǎnféi yǒu zhíjiē de zuòyòng. Exercise has a direct effect on weight loss.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 发挥作用 | play a role; bring into effect |
| 起到作用 | have an effect; work |
| 重要作用 | important role |
| 副作用 | side effect |

## Usage Notes

作用 is commonly used in the pattern 对…有作用 (have an effect on) or 发挥作用 (play a role / bring into play). It is a neutral noun and does not inherently imply a positive or negative effect — context determines that. Compare with 效果 (result/effect), which focuses more on the outcome.

## Memory Hook

作 (make/do) + 用 (use/function) = "making use" or "what it does" = function/effect.
