## 屏幕 (píngmù)

屏幕 refers to the screen or display of any electronic device, including smartphones, computers, tablets, and televisions. It is used in both technical descriptions and casual speech.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** screen; the display surface of an electronic device

## Example Sentences

**手机屏幕碎了，需要换一块。** Shǒujī píngmù suì le, xūyào huàn yī kuài. The phone screen is cracked; it needs to be replaced.

**这台电视的屏幕非常清晰。** Zhè tái diànshì de píngmù fēicháng qīngxī. The screen of this TV is very clear.

**长时间盯着屏幕对眼睛不好。** Cháng shíjiān dīng zhe píngmù duì yǎnjing bù hǎo. Staring at a screen for a long time is bad for your eyes.

**他调暗了屏幕亮度来省电。** Tā tiáo àn le píngmù liàngdù lái shěng diàn. He dimmed the screen brightness to save battery.

## Usage Notes

屏幕 is the general word for any display screen. 屏 (píng) also appears in 屏风 (screen/room divider), showing its original meaning of a flat panel. For the specific type of touchscreen, people say 触摸屏 (chùmō píng). 幕 means curtain or backdrop. Note: 银幕 (yínmù) specifically refers to a movie screen.

## Memory Hook

屏 (flat panel/screen) + 幕 (curtain/backdrop): a flat curtain that displays images — a screen.
