## 接触 (jiēchù)

接触 means to come into contact with someone or something, whether physically, socially, or through exposure.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to come into contact with; to touch; to get in touch with; to be exposed to

## Example Sentences

**她从小就接触音乐，有很好的音感。** Tā cóng xiǎo jiù jiēchù yīnyuè, yǒu hěn hǎo de yīngǎn. She was exposed to music from a young age and has a great sense of tone.

**在工作中，他经常接触来自不同文化的人。** Zài gōngzuò zhōng, tā jīngcháng jiēchù láizì bùtóng wénhuà de rén. In his work, he frequently comes into contact with people from different cultures.

**请不要接触那些有毒的化学品。** Qǐng bú yào jiēchù nàxiē yǒudú de huàxuépǐn. Please do not come into contact with those toxic chemicals.

**通过旅行，我接触到了很多新的事物和想法。** Tōngguò lǚxíng, wǒ jiēchù dào le hěn duō xīn de shìwù hé xiǎngfǎ. Through travel, I have been exposed to many new things and ideas.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 接触新事物 | to be exposed to new things |
| 接触社会 | to get in touch with society |
| 避免接触 | to avoid contact with |
| 直接接触 | direct contact |

## Usage Notes

接触 covers both physical contact and non-physical exposure or interaction. It is commonly used to talk about being introduced to or having experience with something (e.g., a hobby, culture, or field of knowledge). Compare with 联系 (to get in touch/contact), which is more about communication.

## Memory Hook

接 means "to receive/connect" and 触 means "to touch/feel" -- 接触 is receiving and touching something, i.e., coming into contact with it.
