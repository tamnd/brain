## 教授 (jiàoshòu)

教授 refers to a university professor, or as a verb, the act of teaching and imparting knowledge.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** professor (academic title)
2. **[verb]** to teach; to instruct; to impart (knowledge or skills)

## Example Sentences

**这位教授在大学里教了三十年的历史。** Zhè wèi jiàoshòu zài dàxué lǐ jiāole sānshí nián de lìshǐ. This professor has taught history at the university for thirty years.

**他是一位著名的经济学教授。** Tā shì yī wèi zhùmíng de jīngjìxué jiàoshòu. He is a famous professor of economics.

**师傅教授徒弟各种传统技艺。** Shīfù jiàoshòu túdì gèzhǒng chuántǒng jìyì. The master teaches his apprentice various traditional crafts.

**张教授，您好！我是您的学生李明。** Zhāng jiàoshòu, nín hǎo! Wǒ shì nín de xuéshēng Lǐ Míng. Professor Zhang, hello! I am your student Li Ming.

## Usage Notes

As a noun, 教授 is both the title and the word for professor. When addressing a professor, Chinese speakers use 姓 + 教授 (e.g., 王教授, Professor Wang). As a verb, 教授 is more formal than 教 (to teach) and implies the transmission of expertise or a system of knowledge.

## Memory Hook

教 means "to teach" and 授 means "to bestow/confer" -- a 教授 is someone who bestows knowledge through teaching, a professor.
