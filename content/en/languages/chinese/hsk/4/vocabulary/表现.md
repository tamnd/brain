## 表现 (biǎoxiàn)

表现 means to show or display behavior, or refers to one's performance or conduct in a given context.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to show; to display; to perform; to express
2. **[noun]** performance; behavior; manifestation

## Example Sentences

**他在比赛中的表现非常出色。** Tā zài bǐsài zhōng de biǎoxiàn fēicháng chūsè. His performance in the competition was outstanding.

**这个学生在课堂上表现很积极。** Zhège xuéshēng zài kètáng shàng biǎoxiàn hěn jījí. This student performs very actively in class.

**她的表现让所有人都印象深刻。** Tā de biǎoxiàn ràng suǒyǒu rén dōu yìnxiàng shēnkè. Her performance left a deep impression on everyone.

**这些症状是身体不健康的表现。** Zhèxiē zhèngzhuàng shì shēntǐ bù jiànkāng de biǎoxiàn. These symptoms are a manifestation of poor health.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 好的表现 (hǎo de biǎoxiàn) | good performance |
| 工作表现 (gōngzuò biǎoxiàn) | work performance |
| 表现出色 (biǎoxiàn chūsè) | to perform outstandingly |
| 行为表现 (xíngwéi biǎoxiàn) | behavioral performance |

## Usage Notes

表现 is a versatile word used in academic, professional, and everyday contexts. As a verb, it often appears in 表现出 (biǎoxiàn chū) meaning "to demonstrate/show." As a noun, it is used to evaluate someone's conduct or performance. It differs from 演出 (yǎnchū), which refers specifically to a stage performance or show.

## Memory Hook

表 (surface/to show) + 现 (to appear/present) — what you show on the surface that appears to others is your performance.
