## 管理 (guǎnlǐ)

管理 means to manage or administer, whether it is a team of people, a project, an organization, or one's own time and resources. It implies systematic oversight and the authority to direct. As a noun it refers to management or administration.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To manage; to administer; to oversee; to supervise.
2. **[noun]** Management; administration.

## Example Sentences

**这家公司的管理非常规范，员工满意度很高。**
Zhè jiā gōngsī de guǎnlǐ fēicháng guīfàn, yuángōng mǎnyì dù hěn gāo.
This company's management is very well-regulated, and employee satisfaction is high.

**他被任命为项目经理，负责管理整个开发团队。**
Tā bèi rènmìng wéi xiàngmù jīnglǐ, fùzé guǎnlǐ zhěnggè kāifā tuánduì.
He was appointed project manager, responsible for managing the entire development team.

**有效的时间管理可以大幅提高工作效率。**
Yǒuxiào de shíjiān guǎnlǐ kěyǐ dàfú tígāo gōngzuò xiàolǜ.
Effective time management can significantly improve work efficiency.

**政府加强了对食品安全的管理和监督。**
Zhèngfǔ jiāqiáng le duì shípǐn ānquán de guǎnlǐ hé jiāndū.
The government strengthened its management and supervision of food safety.

## Usage Notes

管理 can also be used reflexively with 自己: 管理自己的情绪 (to manage one's own emotions). In modern Chinese business vocabulary, 管理层 (management tier/executives) and 管理者 (manager/administrator) are commonly used nouns derived from 管理. It is more formal than 管 alone, which is colloquial and means "to be in charge of/to care about."

## Memory Hook

管 means "tube/pipe" — picture a pipe channeling water in the right direction. 理 means "to put in order." Management is channeling people and resources in the right direction and keeping everything in order.
