## 进程 (jìnchéng)

进程 means "progress," "process," or "course" and refers to the forward movement or development of an ongoing situation, often at a larger or more formal scale.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** progress; course; development; process (often large-scale)

## Example Sentences

**历史的进程不可阻挡。** Lìshǐ de jìnchéng bùkě zǔdǎng. The course of history cannot be stopped.

**改革的进程比预期的要慢。** Gǎigé de jìnchéng bǐ yùqī de yào màn. The progress of reform is slower than expected.

**科技的进程加速了社会变化。** Kējì de jìnchéng jiāsù le shèhuì biànhuà. The progress of technology has accelerated social change.

**工程的进程受到了天气的影响。** Gōngchéng de jìnchéng shòudào le tiānqì de yǐngxiǎng. The progress of the project was affected by the weather.

## Collocations

## Usage Notes

进程 is similar to 过程 (guòchéng) but has a more dynamic, forward-moving sense. While 过程 focuses on the steps or stages of a process, 进程 emphasizes the forward momentum or rate of advancement. It is more commonly used for large-scale historical, social, or technological developments rather than personal activities.

## Memory Hook

进 (jìn) means "to advance or move forward" and 程 (chéng) means "a journey or course": 进程 is the "advancing course" of something moving forward.
