## 治疗师 (zhìliáoshī)

治疗师 refers to a therapist, a trained professional who provides treatment for physical or psychological conditions. It covers a range of specialists from physical therapists to psychotherapists.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** therapist; a professional who provides therapeutic treatment

## Example Sentences

**她每周去看治疗师，帮助自己处理焦虑问题。** Tā měi zhōu qù kàn zhìliáoshī, bāngzhù zìjǐ chǔlǐ jiāolǜ wèntí. She sees a therapist every week to help manage her anxiety.

**治疗师建议他进行呼吸练习来减轻压力。** Zhìliáoshī jiànyì tā jìnxíng hūxī liànxí lái jiǎnqīng yālì. The therapist recommended that he do breathing exercises to reduce stress.

**找一位好的治疗师可以帮助你更快地康复。** Zhǎo yī wèi hǎo de zhìliáoshī kěyǐ bāngzhù nǐ gèng kuài de kāngfù. Finding a good therapist can help you recover more quickly.

**心理治疗师需要接受多年的专业培训。** Xīnlǐ zhìliáoshī xūyào jiēshòu duō nián de zhuānyè péixùn. Psychotherapists need to receive years of professional training.

## Usage Notes

治疗师 is built from 治疗 (zhìliáo, treatment/therapy) + 师 (shī, teacher/professional). The suffix 师 is commonly added to professional titles in Chinese: 医师 (physician), 律师 (lawyer), 教师 (teacher). To specify the type of therapist, a modifier is placed before 治疗师: 心理治疗师 (psychotherapist), 物理治疗师 (physical therapist), 职业治疗师 (occupational therapist).

## Memory Hook

治疗 (treatment) + 师 (professional/master) — a therapist is a "treatment professional," someone who has mastered the art of healing.
