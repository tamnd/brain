## 处于 (chǔyú)

处于 means to be in or find oneself in a particular state, condition, or position.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to be in (a state/condition/situation); to find oneself at (a position)

## Example Sentences

**公司目前处于快速发展阶段。** Gōngsī mùqián chǔyú kuàisù fāzhǎn jiēduàn. The company is currently in a stage of rapid development.

**他处于一种很矛盾的状态，不知道该怎么选择。** Tā chǔyú yī zhǒng hěn máodùn de zhuàngtài, bù zhīdào gāi zěnme xuǎnzé. He is in a very conflicted state and doesn't know how to choose.

**这个地区处于两国边界上，情况比较复杂。** Zhège dìqū chǔyú liǎng guó biānjiè shàng, qíngkuàng bǐjiào fùzá. This area is situated on the border between two countries, making the situation quite complex.

**谈判仍处于初级阶段，还有很多问题需要解决。** Tánpàn réng chǔyú chūjí jiēduàn, hái yǒu hěn duō wèntí xūyào jiějué. The negotiations are still at an early stage; there are still many issues to resolve.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 处于劣势 | to be at a disadvantage |
| 处于危险中 | to be in danger |
| 处于发展阶段 | to be in a development stage |
| 处于领先位置 | to be in a leading position |

## Usage Notes

处于 is always followed by a noun phrase that describes a state, condition, or location. It is more formal than 在 and specifically emphasizes being in a particular condition or phase rather than simple physical location. It is commonly used in written and formal spoken Chinese.

## Memory Hook

处 means "to be located/to deal with" and 于 is a formal preposition meaning "at/in" -- 处于 means being located in a certain condition, i.e., to be in a state.
