## 讨论 (tǎolùn)

讨论 means to discuss a topic by exchanging views among two or more people. It implies an open, interactive process aimed at exploring ideas, solving problems, or reaching a conclusion. Both the verb and noun forms are widely used in academic, professional, and everyday settings.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To discuss; to deliberate; to talk over.
2. **[noun]** Discussion; deliberation.

## Example Sentences

**同学们就这个话题进行了热烈的讨论。**
Tóngxuémen jiù zhège huàtí jìnxíng le rèliè de tǎolùn.
The students had a lively discussion on this topic.

**在做出最终决定之前，我们需要认真讨论各种方案。**
Zài zuòchū zuìzhōng juédìng zhīqián, wǒmen xūyào rènzhēn tǎolùn gège fāng'àn.
Before making a final decision, we need to seriously discuss all the options.

**这个问题值得深入讨论，不能草率处理。**
Zhège wèntí zhídé shēnrù tǎolùn, bù néng cǎoshuài chǔlǐ.
This issue deserves thorough discussion and should not be handled carelessly.

**经过多次讨论，团队终于达成了共识。**
Jīngguò duō cì tǎolùn, tuánduì zhōngyú dáchéng le gòngshí.
After multiple discussions, the team finally reached a consensus.

## Usage Notes

讨论 implies a two-way exchange of ideas, while 研究 (yánjiū) implies more individual or scholarly investigation. A common formal pattern is 就 X 进行讨论 (to hold a discussion on X). In academic papers, 讨论 often appears as a section heading (Discussion), contrasting with 结论 (Conclusion) and 方法 (Methods).

## Memory Hook

讨 means "to seek/demand" and 论 means "to discuss/argue." Discussing is seeking meaning through argument — both characters pull you toward productive inquiry.
