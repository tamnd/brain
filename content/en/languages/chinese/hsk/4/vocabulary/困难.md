## 困难 (kùnnán)

困难 describes something that is difficult or refers to a difficulty or hardship that one faces.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** difficult, hard, or challenging
2. **[noun]** a difficulty, problem, or hardship

## Example Sentences

**学习一门新语言是困难的，但很有价值。** Xuéxí yī mén xīn yǔyán shì kùnnán de, dàn hěn yǒu jiàzhí. Learning a new language is difficult but very worthwhile.

**面对困难，我们不能轻易放弃。** Miàn duì kùnnán, wǒmen bù néng qīngyì fàngqì. When facing difficulties, we should not easily give up.

**他克服了很多困难，终于成功了。** Tā kèfú le hěn duō kùnnán, zhōngyú chénggōng le. He overcame many difficulties and finally succeeded.

**经济困难使很多家庭感到压力。** Jīngjì kùnnán shǐ hěn duō jiātíng gǎndào yālì. Economic hardship puts pressure on many families.

## Usage Notes

困难 functions as both an adjective and a noun in Chinese. As an adjective, it describes tasks or situations: 这很困难 (this is very difficult). As a noun, it refers to a specific problem or hardship: 遇到困难 (to encounter a difficulty). The related word 困 alone means "to be stuck or trapped," adding to the sense of struggle the word conveys.

## Memory Hook

困 means "trapped" (like a tree trapped inside a box) and 难 means "hard or difficult," so 困难 is being trapped in something hard, a genuine difficulty.
