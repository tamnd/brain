## 原则 (yuánzé)

原则 refers to a fundamental principle, rule, or guiding belief that directs one's behavior or approach to decisions.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** a principle, rule, or fundamental tenet that guides behavior or decisions

## Example Sentences

**诚实是他做人的基本原则。** Chénshí shì tā zuòrén de jīběn yuánzé. Honesty is his basic principle in life.

**我们必须坚持公平公正的原则。** Wǒmen bìxū jiānchí gōngpíng gōngzhèng de yuánzé. We must adhere to the principle of fairness and justice.

**这个决定违反了公司的核心原则。** Zhège juédìng wéifǎn le gōngsī de héxīn yuánzé. This decision violated the company's core principles.

**无论遇到什么情况，他都坚守自己的原则。** Wúlùn yùdào shénme qíngkuàng, tā dōu jiānshǒu zìjǐ de yuánzé. No matter what situation he encounters, he always upholds his principles.

## Usage Notes

原则 refers to a higher-level guiding belief rather than a specific rule or regulation. Saying someone 有原则 (has principles) is a compliment, meaning they act with integrity. 没有原则 (has no principles) is a criticism. The phrase 坚持原则 (to uphold principles) is very commonly used in Chinese.

## Memory Hook

原 means "original or fundamental" and 则 means "rule or standard," so 原则 is a fundamental rule, a principle one lives by.
