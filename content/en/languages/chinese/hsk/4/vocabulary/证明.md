## 证明 (zhèngmíng)

证明 means to prove or verify something using evidence or reasoning. As a noun it refers to a proof, a piece of evidence, or an official certificate (such as an employment certificate or medical certificate).

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To prove; to demonstrate; to verify.
2. **[noun]** Proof; evidence; certificate; certification.

## Example Sentences

**科学家用实验证明了这个理论的正确性。**
Kēxuéjiā yòng shíyàn zhèngmíng le zhège lǐlùn de zhèngquè xìng.
Scientists used experiments to prove the correctness of this theory.

**你需要提供一份工作证明才能办理这项业务。**
Nǐ xūyào tígōng yī fèn gōngzuò zhèngmíng cái néng bànlǐ zhè xiàng yèwù.
You need to provide an employment certificate to handle this matter.

**他的行动证明他是一个值得信赖的人。**
Tā de xíngdòng zhèngmíng tā shì yīgè zhídé xìnlài de rén.
His actions prove that he is a trustworthy person.

**请出示居住证明才可以登记。**
Qǐng chūshì jūzhù zhèngmíng cái kěyǐ dēngjì.
Please present proof of residence before you can register.

## Usage Notes

As a noun, 证明 is very commonly used in bureaucratic and official contexts in China where people must submit various certificates: 收入证明 (income certificate), 无犯罪记录证明 (no-criminal-record certificate). As a verb, it often follows the pattern X 证明 Y, meaning "X proves Y," which is useful for both logical arguments and factual statements.

## Memory Hook

证 contains the speech radical 讠 and the character for "straight" (正), meaning a straight, official statement. 明 means clear. To 证明 is to make an official, clear statement of truth backed by evidence.
