## 代表 (dàibiǎo)

代表 can mean the act of representing (as a verb), a person who represents (as a noun), or a delegation or body of representatives.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To represent, to stand for, to act on behalf of.
2. **[noun]** A representative, a delegate.
3. **[noun]** A delegation; a group of representatives.

## Example Sentences

**她作为公司代表出席了本次国际贸易峰会。**
Tā zuòwéi gōngsī dàibiǎo chūxí le běn cì guójì màoyì fēnghuì.
She attended this international trade summit as the company's representative.

**这枚奖牌不仅代表个人荣誉，更代表整个团队的努力。**
Zhè méi jiǎngpái bù jǐn dàibiǎo gèrén róngyù, gèng dàibiǎo zhěnggè tuánduì de nǔlì.
This medal represents not only personal honor but also the efforts of the entire team.

**学生代表在毕业典礼上发表了一篇感人的演讲。**
Xuésheng dàibiǎo zài bìyè diǎnlǐ shàng fābiǎo le yī piān gǎnrén de yǎnjiǎng.
A student representative delivered a moving speech at the graduation ceremony.

**这些数据不能完全代表整个群体的实际情况。**
Zhèxiē shùjù bù néng wánquán dàibiǎo zhěnggè qúntǐ de shíjì qíngkuàng.
This data cannot fully represent the actual situation of the entire group.

## Usage Notes

代表 is frequently used in political and organizational contexts. The compound 代表团 refers to an official delegation. In everyday speech, 这不代表... is used to mean "this doesn't mean..." — a subtle logical use of the word. When 代表 acts as a verb, it can also mean "to symbolize" (红色代表热情 — red symbolizes passion).

## Memory Hook

代 means "to substitute/stand in for" and 表 means "to show/express" — a representative stands in for others and shows their position.
