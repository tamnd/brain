## 粗略 (cūlüè)

粗略 describes something done or given in a rough, approximate, or cursory manner, without attention to detail or precision.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** rough, approximate, cursory, not thorough

## Example Sentences

**我只是粗略地看了一下这份报告。** Wǒ zhǐshì cūlüè de kàn le yīxià zhè fèn bàogào. I only skimmed through this report roughly.

**他做了一个粗略的估计，认为需要三天完成。** Tā zuò le yīgè cūlüè de gūjì, rènwéi xūyào sān tiān wánchéng. He made a rough estimate that it would take three days to complete.

**这只是一个粗略的计划，还需要进一步完善。** Zhè zhǐshì yīgè cūlüè de jìhuà, hái xūyào jìnyībù wánshàn. This is just a rough plan that still needs to be further refined.

**粗略地统计，大约有五百人参加了活动。** Cūlüè de tǒngjì, dàyuē yǒu wǔbǎi rén cānjiā le huódòng. Roughly speaking, about five hundred people attended the event.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 粗略估计 (cūlüè gūjì) | rough estimate |
| 粗略了解 (cūlüè liǎojiě) | cursory understanding |
| 粗略地看 (cūlüè de kàn) | to glance through |
| 粗略介绍 (cūlüè jièshào) | brief/rough introduction |

## Usage Notes

粗略 is always an adjective or adverb and is not used to describe people's character (for that, use 粗心 cūxīn "careless" or 粗鲁 cūlǔ "rude"). It often appears as an adverb with 地: 粗略地 (cūlüè de). It contrasts with 精确 (jīngquè, precise) and 详细 (xiángxì, detailed).

## Memory Hook

粗 means "coarse/rough" and 略 means "brief/outline" — 粗略 is a "coarse outline," just the rough shape without fine details.
