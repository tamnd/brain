## 勤奋 (qínfèn)

勤奋 describes a person who works hard and persistently without laziness.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** diligent; hardworking; industrious

## Example Sentences

**她非常勤奋，每天学习十个小时。** Tā fēicháng qínfèn, měitiān xuéxí shí gè xiǎoshí. She is very diligent and studies ten hours every day.

**勤奋的人往往比聪明的人更成功。** Qínfèn de rén wǎngwǎng bǐ cōngmíng de rén gèng chénggōng. Diligent people are often more successful than clever ones.

**他以勤奋著称，从不迟到早退。** Tā yǐ qínfèn zhùchēng, cóng bù chídào zǎotuì. He is known for his diligence and never arrives late or leaves early.

**勤奋学习是取得好成绩的关键。** Qínfèn xuéxí shì qǔdé hǎo chéngjì de guānjiàn. Studying diligently is the key to achieving good results.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 勤奋学习 | to study diligently |
| 勤奋工作 | to work diligently |
| 非常勤奋 | very diligent |
| 勤奋努力 | hardworking and dedicated |

## Usage Notes

勤奋 describes a sustained quality of character rather than a single act of effort. It is commonly used as a predicate adjective or as an attributive modifier before a noun. It differs from 努力 (to make an effort), which focuses more on the act of trying hard in a specific moment.

## Memory Hook

Think of 勤 as "frequent/regular" and 奋 as "exerting energy" -- someone who regularly exerts energy is 勤奋, diligent.
