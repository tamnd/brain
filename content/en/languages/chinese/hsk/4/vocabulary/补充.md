## 补充 (bǔchōng)

补充 means "to supplement," "to add to," or "to complement" and describes filling in gaps or adding more to something that is incomplete.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to supplement; to add; to replenish
2. **[noun]** supplement; addition; complement

## Example Sentences

**老师让同学们补充自己的答案。** Lǎoshī ràng tóngxuémen bǔchōng zìjǐ de dá'àn. The teacher asked students to supplement their answers.

**我想补充一点重要信息。** Wǒ xiǎng bǔchōng yīdiǎn zhòngyào xìnxī. I would like to add one important piece of information.

**运动后需要补充水分。** Yùndòng hòu xūyào bǔchōng shuǐfèn. You need to replenish fluids after exercise.

**这本书是对教材的补充。** Zhè běn shū shì duì jiàocái de bǔchōng. This book is a supplement to the textbook.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 补充说明 | to add an explanation |
| 补充资料 | supplementary materials |
| 补充水分 | to replenish fluids |
| 互相补充 | to complement each other |

## Usage Notes

补充 is used in both spoken and written Chinese. In meetings or discussions, 我补充一下 (wǒ bǔchōng yīxià) is a polite way to add something to what has been said. As a noun, it describes extra content provided alongside a main source.

## Memory Hook

补 (bǔ) means "to patch or fill" and 充 (chōng) means "to fill up": 补充 fills up the gaps to make something complete.
