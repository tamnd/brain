## 锻炼 (duànliàn)

锻炼 means physical exercise or training. It is one of the most common words in health and fitness discussions in Chinese, used both as a verb and as a noun.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to exercise; to work out; to train physically
2. **[noun]** exercise; physical training; workout

## Example Sentences

**我每天早上都去公园锻炼。** Wǒ měitiān zǎoshang dōu qù gōngyuán duànliàn. I go to the park to exercise every morning.

**医生建议他多锻炼，少吃油腻食物。** Yīshēng jiànyì tā duō duànliàn, shǎo chī yóunì shíwù. The doctor advised him to exercise more and eat less greasy food.

**坚持锻炼对身体非常有好处。** Jiānchí duànliàn duì shēntǐ fēicháng yǒu hǎochù. Sticking to regular exercise is very beneficial for the body.

**她通过锻炼成功减轻了体重。** Tā tōngguò duànliàn chénggōng jiǎnqīng le tǐzhòng. She successfully lost weight through exercise.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 坚持锻炼 | to stick to exercising |
| 锻炼身体 | to exercise / work out (lit. train the body) |
| 体育锻炼 | physical exercise |
| 锻炼意志 | to train one's willpower |

## Usage Notes

锻炼 is versatile — it can describe physical exercise (锻炼身体) but also metaphorical "training" of character or willpower (锻炼意志). As a verb, it is often followed by a complement or duration: 锻炼了一个小时 (exercised for an hour). It can also follow 坚持 (to persist) to express the idea of maintaining a regular exercise habit.

## Memory Hook

锻 (to forge metal) + 练 (to practice/drill) — just as a blacksmith forges metal through repeated hammering, you forge a strong body through repeated exercise.
