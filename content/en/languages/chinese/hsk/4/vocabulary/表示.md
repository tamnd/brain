## 表示 (biǎoshì)

表示 means to express, indicate, or show something through words, actions, or symbols. It is used when conveying attitudes, emotions, opinions, or when one thing represents or symbolizes another.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To indicate; to express; to show; to mean; to signify.

## Example Sentences

**她微笑着表示对这个决定的支持。**
Tā wēixiào zhe biǎoshì duì zhège juédìng de zhīchí.
She smiled to express her support for this decision.

**红灯表示禁止通行，绿灯表示可以通行。**
Hóngdēng biǎoshì jìnzhǐ tōngxíng, lǜdēng biǎoshì kěyǐ tōngxíng.
A red light indicates no entry; a green light indicates you may proceed.

**政府表示将采取措施改善交通状况。**
Zhèngfǔ biǎoshì jiāng cǎiqǔ cuòshī gǎishàn jiāotōng zhuàngkuàng.
The government indicated that it would take measures to improve traffic conditions.

**他向老师表示感谢，感激老师多年来的指导。**
Tā xiàng lǎoshī biǎoshì gǎnxiè, gǎnjī lǎoshī duōnián lái de zhǐdǎo.
He expressed gratitude to his teacher, appreciating years of guidance.

## Usage Notes

表示 is often followed by a noun or a verb phrase indicating what is being expressed: 表示 + 感谢/同意/反对/理解. When used with an abstract subject (like a symbol or data), it means "to represent/signify": 这个符号表示加法 (This symbol represents addition). It is more formal and explicit than 说 (to say) because it emphasizes the communicative intent behind an act.

## Memory Hook

表 means "surface/outside" and 示 means "to show/demonstrate." 表示 is literally to show something on the outside, making an inner feeling or meaning visible to others.
