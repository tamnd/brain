## 知识 (zhīshi)

知识 refers to knowledge gained through study, experience, or education, encompassing facts, skills, and understanding.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** knowledge, learning, education

## Example Sentences

**读书可以增长知识。** Dúshū kěyǐ zēngzhǎng zhīshi. Reading books can increase your knowledge.

**他的专业知识非常丰富。** Tā de zhuānyè zhīshi fēicháng fēngfù. His professional knowledge is very extensive.

**学生应该把课堂上的知识用于实践。** Xuéshēng yīnggāi bǎ kètáng shàng de zhīshi yòng yú shíjiàn. Students should apply the knowledge learned in class to practice.

**缺乏必要的知识会让工作变得困难。** Quēfá bìyào de zhīshi huì ràng gōngzuò biàn de kùnnán. Lacking the necessary knowledge makes work difficult.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 专业知识 (zhuānyè zhīshi) | professional / specialized knowledge |
| 增长知识 (zēngzhǎng zhīshi) | to expand knowledge |
| 知识渊博 (zhīshi yuānbó) | knowledgeable, well-learned |
| 基础知识 (jīchǔ zhīshi) | basic / foundational knowledge |

## Usage Notes

知识 is an uncountable noun in Chinese and is not pluralized. When referring to a specific domain, a modifier is placed before it: 医学知识 (yīxué zhīshi, medical knowledge). Note that 知识 (knowledge as a concept) differs from 知道 (zhīdào, to know a fact).

## Memory Hook

知 means "to know" and 识 means "to recognize" — 知识 is what you know and recognize from learning.
