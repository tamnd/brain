## 正常 (zhèngcháng)

正常 describes a state, condition, or behavior that falls within the expected, standard, or healthy range.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** Normal; regular; usual; within the standard range.

## Example Sentences

**他的体温很正常。**
Tā de tǐwēn hěn zhèngcháng.
His body temperature is quite normal.

**今天一切都很正常。**
Jīntiān yīqiè dōu hěn zhèngcháng.
Everything is completely normal today.

**这种担心是正常的。**
Zhè zhǒng dānyōu shì zhèngcháng de.
This kind of worry is normal.

**火车运行正常，没有延误。**
Huǒchē yùnxíng zhèngcháng, méiyǒu yánwù.
The train is running normally with no delays.

## Usage Notes

正常 can function as a predicate adjective ("很正常") or as an attributive before a noun ("正常情况"). Its opposite is 不正常 or 异常 (abnormal), which carries a stronger sense of irregularity.

## Memory Hook

正 means "upright/correct" and 常 means "usual/constant" — something that is constantly correct is perfectly normal.
