## 活跃 (huóyuè)

活跃 describes someone or something that is active, lively, and dynamic, or it can be used as a verb meaning to enliven or animate.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** active; lively; dynamic; full of energy and participation
2. **[verb]** to make lively; to animate; to enliven

## Example Sentences

**他是班里最活跃的学生。** Tā shì bān lǐ zuì huóyuè de xuéshēng. He is the most active student in the class.

**这个市场最近非常活跃。** Zhège shìchǎng zuìjìn fēicháng huóyuè. This market has been very active recently.

**她的到来活跃了整个气氛。** Tā de dào lái huóyuè le zhěnggè qìfēn. Her arrival enlivened the whole atmosphere.

**课堂讨论让学习气氛变得更活跃。** Kètáng tǎolùn ràng xuéxí qìfēn biàn de gèng huóyuè. Classroom discussions make the learning atmosphere more dynamic.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|

| 活跃气氛 | to liven up the atmosphere |

| 性格活跃 | lively personality |

## Usage Notes

活跃 as an adjective describes high levels of participation, energy, or market activity. As a verb it means to make a situation or atmosphere more lively. It is more energetic than 积极 (positive, active in attitude) and focuses on visible, outward liveliness.

## Memory Hook

活 means "alive" and 跃 means "to leap": 活跃 is leaping with life, bursting with energy and movement.
