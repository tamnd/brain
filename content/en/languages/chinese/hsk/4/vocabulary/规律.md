## 规律 (guīlǜ)

规律 refers to a consistent natural law, pattern, or regularity that governs the way things happen.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Law; rule; regularity; pattern (of nature or behavior).

## Example Sentences

**科学家努力发现自然界的规律。**
Kēxuéjiā nǔlì fāxiàn zìránjiè de guīlǜ.
Scientists work hard to discover the laws of nature.

**生活要有规律，按时吃饭和睡觉。**
Shēnghuó yào yǒu guīlǜ, ànshí chīfàn hé shuìjiào.
Life should follow a regular pattern, eating and sleeping on time.

**找到学习的规律可以让你事半功倍。**
Zhǎodào xuéxí de guīlǜ kěyǐ ràng nǐ shì bàn gōng bèi.
Finding the patterns in learning can help you achieve twice the result with half the effort.

**这个经济现象有一定的规律可循。**
Zhège jīngjì xiànxiàng yǒu yīdìng de guīlǜ kě xún.
This economic phenomenon has certain patterns that can be followed.

## Usage Notes

规律 focuses on inherent, often discoverable patterns in nature or behavior. It differs from 规则 (guīzé, rule/regulation), which refers to man-made rules. The adjective form 有规律 (regular, following a pattern) and its opposite 没有规律/不规律 (irregular) are very useful in daily conversation.

## Memory Hook

规 means "compass/standard" and 律 means "law/rhythm" — the compass and the rhythm together create the regular law of the universe.
