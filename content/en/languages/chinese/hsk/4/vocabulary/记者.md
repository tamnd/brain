## 记者 (jìzhě)

记者 refers to a journalist or reporter who gathers and writes news for media outlets.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** journalist; reporter; news correspondent

## Example Sentences

**这位记者报道了很多重要新闻。** Zhè wèi jìzhě bàodào le hěn duō zhòngyào xīnwén. This journalist has reported many important news stories.

**记者在现场采访了事故目击者。** Jìzhě zài xiànchǎng cǎifǎng le shìgù mùjīzhě. The reporter interviewed eyewitnesses at the scene of the accident.

**她从大学毕业后就成为了一名记者。** Tā cóng dàxué bìyè hòu jiù chéngwéi le yī míng jìzhě. She became a journalist right after graduating from university.

**记者的工作需要强烈的责任感。** Jìzhě de gōngzuò xūyào qiángliè de zérèngǎn. A journalist's work requires a strong sense of responsibility.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 新闻记者 (xīnwén jìzhě) | news journalist |
| 记者采访 (jìzhě cǎifǎng) | journalist interview |
| 驻外记者 (zhùwài jìzhě) | foreign correspondent |
| 记者招待会 (jìzhě zhāodàihuì) | press conference |

## Usage Notes

记者 is the standard term for a journalist or reporter. The suffix 者 indicates a person who performs the action described by the verb. So 记 (to record/write) + 者 (person) literally means "one who records." The compound 记者招待会 (press conference) is a useful colocation to know at this level.

## Memory Hook

记 (to record) + 者 (person who does something) — a journalist is literally "the one who records," capturing events and reporting them to the public.
