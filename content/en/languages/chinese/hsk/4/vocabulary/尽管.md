## 尽管 (jǐnguǎn)

尽管 is a conjunction meaning "although" or "even though," used to introduce a clause that acknowledges a fact while the main clause states a contrasting or unexpected result.

## Meanings

1. **[conjunction]** although; even though; despite the fact that

## Example Sentences

**尽管很累，他还是坚持完成了工作。** Jǐnguǎn hěn lèi, tā háishì jiānchí wánchéngle gōngzuò. Although he was very tired, he still persisted and finished the work.

**尽管困难重重，我们没有放弃。** Jǐnguǎn kùnnán chóngchóng, wǒmen méiyǒu fàngqì. Even though there were many difficulties, we did not give up.

**尽管她不同意，也没有说出来。** Jǐnguǎn tā bù tóngyì, yě méiyǒu shuō chūlái. Although she disagreed, she did not say anything.

**尽管外面下雨，他们还是出发了。** Jǐnguǎn wàimiàn xià yǔ, tāmen háishì chūfāle. Even though it was raining outside, they still set off.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 尽管如此 | even so; despite this |
| 尽管……但是…… | although... but... |
| 尽管……还是…… | even though... still... |
| 尽管……也…… | although... also... |

## Usage Notes

尽管 is typically used at the beginning of a sentence and is usually paired with 但是, 还是, or 也 in the second clause to show contrast. It is slightly more formal than 虽然 but they are often interchangeable. The key difference is that 尽管 often emphasizes a stronger contrast.

## Memory Hook

尽管 literally contains 尽 (to the full extent) and 管 (to manage/control): even when something is fully in control or fully known, the main clause still surprises you. The contrast is maximized.
