## 决定 (juédìng)

决定 means to decide or make a decision, and can also be used as a noun referring to the decision itself.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to decide, to determine, or to resolve on a course of action
2. **[noun]** a decision or resolution that has been made

## Example Sentences

**我决定明年去中国留学。** Wǒ juédìng míngnián qù Zhōngguó liúxué. I have decided to study in China next year.

**这个决定对公司的未来非常重要。** Zhège juédìng duì gōngsī de wèilái fēicháng zhòngyào. This decision is very important for the company's future.

**他花了很长时间才做出这个决定。** Tā huā le hěn cháng shíjiān cái zuòchū zhège juédìng. He took a long time to make this decision.

**努力工作可以决定你的成功。** Nǔlì gōngzuò kěyǐ juédìng nǐ de chénggōng. Hard work can determine your success.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 做出决定 | make a decision |
| 重要决定 | important decision |
| 最终决定 | final decision |
| 共同决定 | decide together |

## Usage Notes

决定 is one of the most common verbs at B1 level. As a verb, it is followed by the content of the decision (e.g., 决定去 to decide to go). As a noun, it appears in patterns like 做出决定 (make a decision) or 这个决定 (this decision). It can also mean "to determine" in the sense of "success is determined by effort."

## Memory Hook

决 means "to determine" (with a sense of cutting off other options) and 定 means "to settle or fix," so 决定 is to cut off alternatives and settle on one choice.
