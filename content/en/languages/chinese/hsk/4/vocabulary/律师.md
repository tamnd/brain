## 律师 (lǜshī)

律师 is the standard word for a lawyer or attorney -- a professional who provides legal advice and represents clients in legal matters.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** lawyer; attorney

## Example Sentences

**她是一位有名的律师。** Tā shì yī wèi yǒumíng de lǜshī. She is a well-known lawyer.

**我需要请一位律师。** Wǒ xūyào qǐng yī wèi lǜshī. I need to hire a lawyer.

**律师帮他赢了官司。** Lǜshī bāng tā yíngle guānsī. The lawyer helped him win the lawsuit.

**他毕业后成为了一名律师。** Tā bìyè hòu chéngwéile yī míng lǜshī. After graduating, he became a lawyer.

## Usage Notes

律师 is always a noun and takes the classifier 位 (wèi) or 名 (míng). The character 律 (lǜ) means law or rule, and 师 (shī) means master or professional, so 律师 is literally a "master of law." It is a neutral, formal term used in both spoken and written Chinese.

## Memory Hook

LV (律 = law) + SHI (师 = master): a master of the law -- that is a lawyer.
