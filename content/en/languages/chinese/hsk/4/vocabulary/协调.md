## 协调 (xiétiáo)

协调 means to coordinate different parts or people so they work together harmoniously, or describes a state of harmony and balance.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to coordinate; to harmonize; to reconcile
2. **[adjective]** coordinated; harmonious; well-balanced

## Example Sentences

**项目经理负责协调各部门的工作。** Xiàngmù jīnglǐ fùzé xiétiáo gè bùmén de gōngzuò. The project manager is responsible for coordinating the work of each department.

**这两种颜色搭配得非常协调。** Zhè liǎng zhǒng yánsè dāpèi de fēicháng xiétiáo. These two colors are paired together very harmoniously.

**我们需要协调各方意见，达成一致。** Wǒmen xūyào xiétiáo gèfāng yìjiàn, dáchéng yīzhì. We need to coordinate different parties' opinions to reach consensus.

**她的动作协调优美，很适合学舞蹈。** Tā de dòngzuò xiétiáo yōuměi, hěn shìhé xué wǔdǎo. Her movements are graceful and coordinated, making her very suited to learning dance.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 协调工作 | to coordinate work |

| 协调关系 | to coordinate relationships |

## Usage Notes

协调 can function as both a verb (to coordinate) and an adjective (harmonious/balanced). As a verb, it often takes an object that refers to people, departments, or opinions. As an adjective, it describes visual or physical balance, such as in colors, movements, or proportions.

## Memory Hook

协 contains the character for "together" and 调 means "to adjust" -- 协调 is adjusting things together so everything works in sync.
