## 否定 (fǒudìng)

否定 means to deny, negate, or reject something, or describes something that is negative in character.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to negate; to deny; to reject; to nullify
2. **[adjective]** negative (as opposed to affirmative)

## Example Sentences

**他否定了所有的指控。** Tā fǒudìngle suǒyǒu de zhǐkòng. He denied all the accusations.

**这种做法完全否定了之前的努力。** Zhè zhǒng zuòfǎ wánquán fǒudìngle zhīqián de nǔlì. This approach completely negates all previous efforts.

**在语法上，"不"和"没"用来构成否定句。** Zài yǔfǎ shàng, "bù" hé "méi" yòng lái gòuchéng fǒudìng jù. In grammar, "不" and "没" are used to form negative sentences.

**你不应该全盘否定他的建议，其中有些是有价值的。** Nǐ bù yīnggāi quánpán fǒudìng tā de jiànyì, qízhōng yǒuxiē shì yǒu jiàzhí de. You should not completely reject his suggestions; some of them are valuable.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 全盘否定 | to completely reject/negate |
| 否定句 | negative sentence (grammar) |
| 否定回答 | negative answer |
| 自我否定 | self-negation; to deny oneself |

## Usage Notes

否定 is the antonym of 肯定 (to affirm/confirm). It is more formal than simply saying 不 (no) and is used in contexts such as logic, grammar, and debate. The phrase 全盘否定 (to reject entirely) carries a strong sense of wholesale dismissal and is frequently used in discussions or arguments.

## Memory Hook

否 means "to negate/no" and 定 means "to decide/determine" -- 否定 is deciding to say no, i.e., to negate or deny.
