## 期间 (qījiān)

期间 refers to a span of time or a period during which something takes place, often used after a noun to indicate "during X."

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** period; duration; the time during which something happens

## Example Sentences

**假期期间，很多人选择去旅游。** Jiàqī qījiān, hěn duō rén xuǎnzé qù lǚyóu. During the holiday period, many people choose to travel.

**活动期间请保持安静。** Huódòng qījiān qǐng bǎochí ānjìng. Please keep quiet during the event.

**工作期间不能使用手机。** Gōngzuò qījiān bù néng shǐyòng shǒujī. You cannot use your phone during working hours.

**这段期间他学到了很多东西。** Zhè duàn qījiān tā xué dào le hěn duō dōngxi. During this period he learned a great deal.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 假期期间 | during the holiday |
| 工作期间 | during working hours |
| 培训期间 | during training |
| 这段期间 | during this period |

## Usage Notes

期间 is typically preceded by a noun phrase that defines the time frame, such as 假期, 会议, or 活动. It is more formal than 的时候 and is common in written Chinese. It cannot stand alone as a subject or object without a modifying phrase.

## Memory Hook

期 means "period of time" and 间 means "in between": 期间 is literally the time in between, a period sandwiched between two moments.
