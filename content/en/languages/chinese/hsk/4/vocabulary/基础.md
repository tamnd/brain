## 基础 (jīchǔ)

基础 refers to the foundational knowledge, skills, or physical structure upon which something more advanced is built.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Foundation; basis; groundwork; base; fundamentals.

## Example Sentences

**学好数学需要打好基础。**
Xué hǎo shùxué xūyào dǎ hǎo jīchǔ.
Learning mathematics well requires building a solid foundation.

**这栋楼的基础非常牢固。**
Zhè dòng lóu de jīchǔ fēicháng láogù.
The foundation of this building is very solid.

**没有良好的语言基础很难阅读专业文章。**
Méiyǒu liánghǎo de yǔyán jīchǔ hěn nán yuèdú zhuānyè wénzhāng.
Without a good language foundation it is difficult to read professional articles.

**两国关系建立在互信的基础上。**
Liǎng guó guānxi jiànlì zài hùxìn de jīchǔ shàng.
The relationship between the two countries is built on a foundation of mutual trust.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 打好基础 | to build a solid foundation |
| 基础知识 | foundational knowledge |
| 在...基础上 | on the basis of... |
| 基础教育 | basic/fundamental education |
| 扎实基础 | solid foundation |

## Usage Notes

基础 often appears in the pattern 在...的基础上 (on the basis of ...), which is extremely common in formal writing. 打基础 or 打好基础 (to lay a foundation) is common in educational contexts. The adjective 基础性 (foundational/basic) derives from this word.

## Memory Hook

基 means "foundation/base" (showing a building's base) and 础 contains the stone radical, reinforcing the idea of a stone base — together they mean a solid stone foundation.
