## 仔细 (zǐxì)

仔细 means to be careful, attentive, and thorough — paying close attention to details when doing something.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** careful, meticulous, attentive
2. **[adverb]** carefully, attentively (modifying a verb)

## Example Sentences

**请仔细阅读说明书，再开始操作。** Qǐng zǐxì yuèdú shuōmíngshū, zài kāishǐ cāozuò. Please read the instructions carefully before starting to operate.

**她是一个非常仔细的人，工作很少出错。** Tā shì yīgè fēicháng zǐxì de rén, gōngzuò hěn shǎo chūcuò. She is a very careful person; she rarely makes mistakes at work.

**你要仔细检查每一份合同。** Nǐ yào zǐxì jiǎnchá měi yī fèn hétong. You need to carefully check every contract.

**仔细听老师说话，不要分心。** Zǐxì tīng lǎoshī shuōhuà, bùyào fēnxīn. Listen carefully to what the teacher says; don't get distracted.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 仔细检查 | check carefully |
| 仔细阅读 | read carefully |
| 仔细观察 | observe carefully |
| 不够仔细 | not careful enough |

## Usage Notes

仔细 can modify verbs as an adverb (仔细看) or describe a person's character as an adjective (他很仔细). It emphasizes thoroughness and attention to detail. Do not confuse it with 小心 (careful in the sense of cautious/watching out for danger) — 仔细 focuses on precision and thoroughness.

## Memory Hook

仔 contains the character for "son/child" (子) — imagine a parent checking every detail of their child's homework with great care.
