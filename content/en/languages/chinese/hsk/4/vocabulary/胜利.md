## 胜利 (shènglì)

胜利 expresses the idea of winning or achieving a triumphant outcome. It functions as both a noun ("victory") and a verb ("to win, to triumph"), and carries a more formal or heroic tone than the simple verb 赢 (yíng).

## Meanings
1. **n** victory, triumph, win
2. **v** to win, to triumph

## Example Sentences
**球队赢得了比赛的胜利。** Qiúduì yíngdé le bǐsài de shènglì. The team won victory in the match.

**他们经过艰苦努力终于取得了胜利。** Tāmen jīngguò jiānkǔ nǔlì zhōngyú qǔdé le shènglì. After hard work they finally achieved victory.

**这场胜利让整个国家欢欣鼓舞。** Zhè chǎng shènglì ràng zhěnggè guójiā huānxīn gǔwǔ. This victory filled the whole nation with joy.

**胜利属于坚持到底的人。** Shènglì shǔyú jiānchí dào dǐ de rén. Victory belongs to those who persevere to the end.

## Collocations
| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 取得胜利 (qǔdé shènglì) | to achieve victory |
| 走向胜利 (zǒuxiàng shènglì) | to march toward victory |
| 最终胜利 (zuìzhōng shènglì) | final victory |
| 胜利的喜悦 (shènglì de xǐyuè) | the joy of victory |

## Usage Notes
胜利 often appears in formal, motivational, or military contexts. In everyday speech about games, 赢 (yíng) is more common. 胜利 can be modified by 取得, 赢得, or 获得 to mean "achieve/win a victory."

## Memory Hook
胜 contains the radical for "strength" — use your strength (胜) to gain profit/benefit (利) and you have victory.
