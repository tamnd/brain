## 无论 (wúlùn)

无论 introduces a conditional clause meaning "no matter (what/who/how)," and the main clause typically contains 都 or 也 to complete the pattern.

## Meanings

1. **[conjunction]** No matter; regardless of (introduces a condition that does not affect the outcome).

## Example Sentences

**无论天气怎么样，我都会来。**
Wúlùn tiānqì zěnme yàng, wǒ dōu huì lái.
No matter what the weather is like, I will come.

**无论你走到哪里，家人都会支持你。**
Wúlùn nǐ zǒu dào nǎlǐ, jiārén dōu huì zhīchí nǐ.
No matter where you go, your family will support you.

**无论多难，他都不放弃。**
Wúlùn duō nán, tā dōu bù fàngqì.
No matter how difficult it is, he never gives up.

**无论发生什么事，保持冷静最重要。**
Wúlùn fāshēng shénme shì, bǎochí lěngjìng zuì zhòngyào.
No matter what happens, staying calm is most important.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 无论如何 | no matter what; in any case |
| 无论怎样 | no matter how |
| 无论何时 | no matter when |
| 无论何地 | no matter where |
| 无论是…还是… | whether ... or ... |

## Usage Notes

无论 always pairs with 都 or 也 in the result clause. The question word inside the 无论 clause (什么, 哪里, 怎么, etc.) makes the condition open-ended. 不管 is a colloquial synonym used more often in spoken Chinese.

## Memory Hook

无 means "without" and 论 means "to discuss/consider"; 无论 says "without even discussing the condition" — the result stays the same no matter what.
