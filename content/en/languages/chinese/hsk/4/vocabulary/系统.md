## 系统 (xìtǒng)

系统 refers to an organized, interconnected set of parts working together, or describes an approach that is thorough and methodical.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** System; network; organized structure.
2. **[adjective]** Systematic; thorough; methodical.

## Example Sentences

**这套教育系统需要改革。**
Zhè tào jiàoyù xìtǒng xūyào gǎigé.
This education system needs reform.

**电脑系统突然崩溃了。**
Diànnǎo xìtǒng tūrán bēngkuì le.
The computer system suddenly crashed.

**我们需要系统地解决这个问题。**
Wǒmen xūyào xìtǒng de jiějué zhège wèntí.
We need to solve this problem systematically.

**他对这个领域进行了系统的研究。**
Tā duì zhège lǐngyù jìnxíngle xìtǒng de yánjiū.
He conducted systematic research in this field.

## Usage Notes

系统 is used both as a technical noun (computer system, education system) and as an adjective/adverb meaning "systematic." The adverb form 系统地 (systematically) is common in academic and professional writing. 操作系统 (computer operating system) is a very common compound in tech contexts.

## Memory Hook

系 means "to connect/tie" and 统 means "to unify/govern" — tying things together under one governing structure creates a system.
