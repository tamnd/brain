## 相关 (xiāngguān)

相关 means related to or connected with a particular subject, person, or issue. It is widely used in formal and academic writing to indicate that something is pertinent or relevant, and also in everyday speech to mean "related."

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** Related; relevant; associated; correlated; pertaining to.

## Example Sentences

**请提供与申请职位相关的工作经历和证书。**
Qǐng tígōng yǔ shēnqǐng zhíwèi xiāngguān de gōngzuò jīnglì hé zhèngshū.
Please provide work experience and certificates relevant to the position you are applying for.

**研究人员发现，睡眠质量与心理健康密切相关。**
Yánjiū rényuán fāxiàn, shuìmián zhìliàng yǔ xīnlǐ jiànkāng mìqiè xiāngguān.
Researchers found that sleep quality is closely related to mental health.

**所有相关部门必须在规定时间内提交进度报告。**
Suǒyǒu xiāngguān bùmén bìxū zài guīdìng shíjiān nèi tíjiāo jìndù bàogào.
All relevant departments must submit progress reports within the specified time.

**在阅读这篇文章之前，建议先了解一些相关背景知识。**
Zài yuèdú zhè piān wénzhāng zhīqián, jiànyì xiān liǎojiě yīxiē xiāngguān bèijǐng zhīshi.
Before reading this article, it is recommended to first learn some relevant background knowledge.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 相关部门 | relevant department |
| 相关信息 | related information |
| 密切相关 | closely related |
| 相关政策 | relevant policies |
| 与…相关 | related to… |

## Usage Notes

相关 is very common in formal Chinese as a pre-modifier: 相关部门 (relevant departments), 相关规定 (relevant regulations), 相关研究 (related research). It can also be used predicatively: X 与 Y 相关 (X is related to Y) or X 与 Y 密切相关 (X is closely related to Y). In statistics, 相关性 (xiāngguān xìng) means "correlation."

## Memory Hook

相 means "mutually" and 关 means "to connect/close." Something 相关 is mutually connected to something else — two things that are tied together like two doors that share the same hinge.
