## 表明 (biǎomíng)

表明 means "to indicate," "to make clear," or "to show" and is used when someone or something clearly demonstrates or states a position, attitude, or fact.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to indicate; to show clearly
2. **[verb]** to make clear; to express; to state explicitly

## Example Sentences

**他的行动表明他非常认真。** Tā de xíngdòng biǎomíng tā fēicháng rènzhēn. His actions show that he is very serious.

**调查结果表明这个方法有效。** Diàochá jiéguǒ biǎomíng zhège fāngfǎ yǒuxiào. The survey results indicate that this method is effective.

**她表明了自己的立场。** Tā biǎomíng le zìjǐ de lìchǎng. She made her position clear.

**这些数据表明情况正在改善。** Zhèxiē shùjù biǎomíng qíngkuàng zhèngzài gǎishàn. These data indicate that the situation is improving.

## Usage Notes

表明 is more formal than 说明 (shuōmíng) and is often used in academic, journalistic, or official contexts. It typically takes a clause as its object. A common pattern is: subject + 表明 + that-clause, meaning the subject demonstrates or reveals something.

## Memory Hook

表 (biǎo) means "to express outwardly" and 明 (míng) means "clear": 表明 means to express something so clearly it becomes obvious.
