## 态度 (tàidu)

态度 refers to a person's attitude, stance, or manner, including their mental approach, emotional response, or behavioral disposition toward a person, thing, or situation.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** attitude; stance; manner; approach

## Example Sentences

**他对工作的态度非常认真。** Tā duì gōngzuò de tàidu fēicháng rènzhēn. His attitude toward work is very serious.

**面对挑战，要保持积极的态度。** Miànduì tiǎozhàn, yào bǎochí jījí de tàidu. When facing challenges, you should maintain a positive attitude.

**她对客户的态度总是很礼貌。** Tā duì kèhù de tàidu zǒng shì hěn lǐmào. Her attitude toward customers is always very polite.

**他的学习态度让老师非常满意。** Tā de xuéxí tàidu ràng lǎoshī fēicháng mǎnyì. His attitude toward studying made the teacher very satisfied.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 积极态度 | positive attitude |

| 学习态度 | attitude toward learning |
| 表明态度 | to express one's stance |

## Usage Notes

态度 often appears in the structure 对……的态度 (attitude toward something). It can describe both emotional stances (positive, negative) and behavioral manners (polite, serious). In Chinese culture, having a good 态度 (attitude) is highly valued in academic, professional, and social contexts.

## Memory Hook

态 (state/appearance) and 度 (degree/measure). So 态度 is the "degree of one's state": how you measure and present your inner state to the world.
