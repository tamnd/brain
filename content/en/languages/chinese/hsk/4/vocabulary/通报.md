## 通报 (tōngbào)

通报 means "to notify," "to brief," or "to inform" and refers to officially communicating information to a group, often in a formal or organizational context.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to notify; to inform officially; to brief
2. **[noun]** official notice; bulletin; circular

## Example Sentences

**领导向全体员工通报了公司的最新情况。** Lǐngdǎo xiàng quántǐ yuángōng tōngbào le gōngsī de zuìxīn qíngkuàng. The leader briefed all employees on the latest company situation.

**学校发了通报，说明新的规定。** Xuéxiào fā le tōngbào, shuōmíng xīn de guīdìng. The school issued a notice explaining the new regulations.

**政府对此事进行了公开通报。** Zhèngfǔ duì cǐ shì jìnxíng le gōngkāi tōngbào. The government made a public notification about this matter.

**请及时通报最新进展。** Qǐng jíshí tōngbào zuìxīn jìnzhǎn. Please promptly report the latest developments.

## Collocations

## Usage Notes

通报 is formal and typically used in organizational, governmental, or institutional contexts. It implies sharing information with a defined group through an official channel. As a noun, it refers to the written or spoken notification itself. It differs from 通知 (tōngzhī, notice) in that 通报 often implies sharing a report or briefing on a situation, while 通知 is more about announcing a decision or requirement.

## Memory Hook

通 (tōng) means "to communicate or pass through" and 报 (bào) means "to report": 通报 is communicating a report to those who need to know.
