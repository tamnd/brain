## 下载 (xiàzài)

下载 means to download, referring to transferring files, apps, or data from the internet or a server to a local device. It is one of the most commonly used tech verbs in everyday Chinese.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to download; to transfer data from a remote source to a local device

## Example Sentences

**我需要下载这个应用程序。** Wǒ xūyào xiàzài zhège yìngyòng chéngxù. I need to download this application.

**文件太大，下载需要一点时间。** Wénjiàn tài dà, xiàzài xūyào yīdiǎn shíjiān. The file is too large; downloading will take a little time.

**你可以免费下载这首歌。** Nǐ kěyǐ miǎnfèi xiàzài zhè shǒu gē. You can download this song for free.

**网速太慢，下载了半天还没完成。** Wǎngsù tài màn, xiàzài le bàntiān hái méi wánchéng. The internet is too slow; I've been downloading for a long time and it's still not done.

## Usage Notes

下载 pairs with 上传 (shàngchuán, to upload) as natural opposites. 下 (down) reflects data coming down to your device, while 上 (up) in 上传 reflects sending data upward to a server. 下载 can be used as both a verb and a noun (e.g., 这个下载很快, "this download is fast").

## Memory Hook

下 (down) + 载 (to carry/load): carrying data down from the internet to your device — downloading.
