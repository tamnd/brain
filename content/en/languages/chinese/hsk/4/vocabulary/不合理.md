## 不合理 (bù hélǐ)

不合理 describes something that does not conform to reason, logic, or fairness — the opposite of 合理 (reasonable).

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** unreasonable, irrational, unfair, illogical

## Example Sentences

**这个规定非常不合理，大家都不满意。** Zhège guīdìng fēicháng bù hélǐ, dàjiā dōu bù mǎnyì. This rule is very unreasonable, and everyone is dissatisfied.

**他的要求太不合理了，我没办法答应。** Tā de yāoqiú tài bù hélǐ le, wǒ méi bànfǎ dāying. His demands are too unreasonable; I have no way to agree.

**这种不合理的现象必须改变。** Zhè zhǒng bù hélǐ de xiànxiàng bìxū gǎibiàn. This kind of unreasonable phenomenon must change.

**价格这么高，真的很不合理。** Jiàgé zhème gāo, zhēn de hěn bù hélǐ. Such a high price is truly unreasonable.

## Usage Notes

不合理 is the negation of 合理 (reasonable/rational). It is often used to criticize rules, prices, behavior, or systems. At B1 level, it frequently appears in opinion essays or discussions about social issues.

## Memory Hook

Break it down: 不 (not) + 合 (fit/conform) + 理 (reason/logic) = "does not fit reason" = unreasonable.
