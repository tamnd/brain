## 想象 (xiǎngxiàng)

想象 means to imagine or picture something in the mind, and as a noun it means imagination -- the creative faculty of forming mental images.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to imagine; to picture
2. **[noun]** imagination

## Example Sentences

**你能想象未来是什么样子吗？** Nǐ néng xiǎngxiàng wèilái shì shénme yàngzi ma? Can you imagine what the future will look like?

**他有丰富的想象力。** Tā yǒu fēngfù de xiǎngxiànglì. He has a rich imagination.

**这个故事超出了我的想象。** Zhège gùshi chāochūle wǒ de xiǎngxiàng. This story went beyond my imagination.

**小孩子喜欢想象各种奇怪的事情。** Xiǎo háizi xǐhuān xiǎngxiàng gèzhǒng qíguài de shìqing. Young children love to imagine all kinds of strange things.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 想象力 | imagination (ability) |
| 发挥想象 | to let imagination run |
| 超出想象 | beyond imagination |
| 难以想象 | hard to imagine |

## Usage Notes

想象 is used as a verb (想象+clause/noun) and as a noun (我的想象 = my imagination). The compound 想象力 (xiǎngxiànglì) specifically means the capacity for imagination, like a skill or talent. The phrase 难以想象 (hard to imagine) is very commonly used to express disbelief or amazement.

## Memory Hook

XIANG (想 = think/want) + XIANG (象 = image/elephant): thinking in images -- that is imagination.
