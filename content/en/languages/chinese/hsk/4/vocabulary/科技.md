## 科技 (kējì)

科技 is a shorthand combining 科学 (science) and 技术 (technology), referring to the field of science and technology together.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** technology; science and technology; the combined field of scientific knowledge and its technical applications

## Example Sentences

**现代科技改变了人们的生活方式。** Xiàndài kējì gǎibiàn le rénmen de shēnghuó fāngshì. Modern technology has changed people's way of life.

**这家公司专注于高科技产品的研发。** Zhè jiā gōngsī zhuānzhù yú gāo kējì chǎnpǐn de yánfā. This company focuses on the research and development of high-tech products.

**科技进步使医疗水平大大提高。** Kējì jìnbù shǐ yīliáo shuǐpíng dàdà tígāo. Technological progress has greatly improved the level of medical care.

**我们要善于利用科技手段解决问题。** Wǒmen yào shànyú lìyòng kējì shǒuduàn jiějué wèntí. We should be good at using technological means to solve problems.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 高科技 | high technology; hi-tech |
| 科技发展 | technological development |
| 科技进步 | technological progress |
| 科技创新 | technological innovation |

## Usage Notes

科技 is an abbreviation of 科学技术 and is used when referring to science and technology as a unified field. It is extremely common in news, policy documents, and everyday discussion about modern progress. 高科技 (high-tech) is a particularly frequent collocation.

## Memory Hook

科技 is simply 科学 (science) plus 技术 (technology) compressed into two characters: the science-tech shorthand for the modern world's engine.
