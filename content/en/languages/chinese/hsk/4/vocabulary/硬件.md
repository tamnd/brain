## 硬件 (yìngjiàn)

硬件 refers to the physical components of a computer or electronic system, such as the processor, memory, and screen. It is the counterpart of 软件 (software) and is used in both technical and general conversation.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** hardware; the physical parts and components of a computer or device

## Example Sentences

**这台电脑的硬件配置很高。** Zhè tái diànnǎo de yìngjiàn pèizhì hěn gāo. This computer has high hardware specifications.

**硬件损坏了，需要维修。** Yìngjiàn sǔnhuài le, xūyào wéixiū. The hardware is damaged and needs repair.

**新手机的硬件比旧款好多了。** Xīn shǒujī de yìngjiàn bǐ jiù kuǎn hǎo duō le. The new phone's hardware is much better than the old model.

**软件问题好解决，硬件问题更麻烦。** Ruǎnjiàn wèntí hǎo jiějué, yìngjiàn wèntí gèng máfan. Software problems are easy to fix; hardware problems are more troublesome.

## Usage Notes

硬件 directly contrasts with 软件 (software). 硬 means "hard/rigid" and 件 means "component/item." In everyday speech, people might say 硬件不够好 (hardware is not good enough) when their device runs slowly due to physical limitations. The word also appears in broader non-tech contexts meaning physical facilities or infrastructure.

## Memory Hook

硬 (hard/rigid) + 件 (item/component): the hard, physical items inside your device — hardware.
