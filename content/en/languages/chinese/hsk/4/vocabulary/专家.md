## 专家 (zhuānjiā)

专家 refers to a person who has deep expertise or mastery in a particular field, equivalent to "expert" or "specialist" in English.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** an expert or specialist with advanced knowledge in a field

## Example Sentences

**我们请了一位医学专家来给我们讲课。** Wǒmen qǐng le yī wèi yīxué zhuānjiā lái gěi wǒmen jiǎngkè. We invited a medical expert to give us a lecture.

**这位专家在环境保护方面很有经验。** Zhè wèi zhuānjiā zài huánjìng bǎohù fāngmiàn hěn yǒu jīngyàn. This expert has a lot of experience in environmental protection.

**专家建议我们改变饮食习惯。** Zhuānjiā jiànyì wǒmen gǎibiàn yǐnshí xíguàn. Experts suggest we change our eating habits.

**政府邀请了多位专家讨论这个问题。** Zhèngfǔ yāoqǐng le duō wèi zhuānjiā tǎolùn zhège wèntí. The government invited several experts to discuss this issue.

## Usage Notes

专家 is a respectful title in Chinese, commonly seen in formal, academic, or news contexts. It is often modified by a field noun before it, such as 经济专家 (economic expert) or 法律专家 (legal expert). Compare with 专业 (zhuānyè), which refers to a field, while 专家 refers to the person.

## Memory Hook

专 means "specialized" and 家 means "person at home in something," so a 专家 is someone who is truly "at home" in their specialty.
