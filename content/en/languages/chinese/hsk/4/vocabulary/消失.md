## 消失 (xiāoshī)

消失 means to disappear or vanish, referring to something that ceases to be visible or no longer exists.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to disappear; to vanish; to cease to exist or be seen

## Example Sentences

**他的身影慢慢消失在人群中。** Tā de shēnyǐng mànmàn xiāoshī zài rénqún zhōng. His figure slowly disappeared into the crowd.

**这种珍稀动物正在慢慢消失。** Zhè zhǒng zhēnxī dòngwù zhèngzài mànmàn xiāoshī. This rare animal is gradually disappearing.

**疼痛感在吃了药之后消失了。** Téngtòng gǎn zài chī le yào zhīhòu xiāoshī le. The pain disappeared after taking the medicine.

**很多传统文化在现代社会中消失了。** Hěn duō chuántǒng wénhuà zài xiàndài shèhuì zhōng xiāoshī le. Many traditional cultures have disappeared in modern society.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 慢慢消失 | to gradually disappear |
| 完全消失 | to completely vanish |
| 消失不见 | to disappear from sight |
| 从视野中消失 | to disappear from view |

## Usage Notes

消失 is intransitive and cannot take a direct object. It describes a natural or gradual process of disappearing. It differs from 消除 (to eliminate, to remove), which is transitive and implies actively getting rid of something.

## Memory Hook

消 means "to fade/diminish" and 失 means "to lose": 消失 is fading until lost, gradually vanishing from existence.
