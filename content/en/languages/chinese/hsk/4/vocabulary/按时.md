## 按时 (ànshí)

按时 means on time or punctually, describing the act of doing something at the scheduled, agreed-upon, or required time without being late.

## Meanings

1. **[adverb]** on time; punctually; at the appointed time

## Example Sentences

**请按时提交作业，不要迟交。** Qǐng ànshí tíjiāo zuòyè, bùyào chí jiāo. Please submit your homework on time; do not hand it in late.

**他每天按时去上班，从不迟到。** Tā měitiān ànshí qù shàngbān, cóng bù chídào. He goes to work on time every day and is never late.

**病人需要按时服药才能痊愈。** Bìngrén xūyào ànshí fúyào cái néng quányù. Patients need to take their medicine on time to recover.

**我们必须按时完成这个项目。** Wǒmen bìxū ànshí wánchéng zhège xiàngmù. We must complete this project on time.

## Usage Notes

按时 is an adverb placed before the verb it modifies. It emphasizes adherence to a specific scheduled time. It differs from 及时 (in time/promptly), which focuses more on acting before it is too late. 按时 is about following a set schedule.

## Memory Hook

按 means "according to/following" and 时 means "time." So 按时 means "following the time": sticking exactly to the schedule.
