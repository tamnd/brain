## 软件 (ruǎnjiàn)

软件 is the standard Chinese term for software, covering everything from operating systems to mobile apps. It contrasts directly with 硬件 (hardware) and is used in both technical and everyday contexts.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** software; computer programs, applications, and operating systems

## Example Sentences

**这款软件使用起来非常简单。** Zhè kuǎn ruǎnjiàn shǐyòng qǐlái fēicháng jiǎndān. This software is very easy to use.

**公司开发了一款新的管理软件。** Gōngsī kāifā le yī kuǎn xīn de guǎnlǐ ruǎnjiàn. The company developed a new management software.

**请把软件更新到最新版本。** Qǐng bǎ ruǎnjiàn gēngxīn dào zuìxīn bǎnběn. Please update the software to the latest version.

**这台电脑缺少必要的办公软件。** Zhè tái diànnǎo quēshǎo bìyào de bàngōng ruǎnjiàn. This computer lacks the necessary office software.

## Usage Notes

软件 literally means "soft item," contrasting with 硬件 (yìngjiàn, hardware). The measure word for software is typically 款 (kuǎn) or 个 (gè). In tech contexts, 软件工程师 (software engineer) and 软件公司 (software company) are common compounds. Do not confuse with 应用程序, which specifically refers to individual apps.

## Memory Hook

软 (soft) + 件 (item/component): the "soft" component of a computer system, as opposed to the hard physical parts.
