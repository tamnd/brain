## 演讲 (yǎnjiǎng)

演讲 refers to a formal speech or lecture given to an audience, or the act of giving such a speech.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** speech; lecture; a formal address given to an audience
2. **[verb]** to give a speech; to deliver a lecture

## Example Sentences

**他在大会上做了一次精彩的演讲。** Tā zài dàhuì shàng zuò le yī cì jīngcǎi de yǎnjiǎng. He gave a wonderful speech at the conference.

**我需要为明天的演讲做充分准备。** Wǒ xūyào wèi míngtiān de yǎnjiǎng zuò chōngfèn zhǔnbèi. I need to prepare thoroughly for tomorrow's speech.

**演讲比赛将于下周举行。** Yǎnjiǎng bǐsài jiāng yú xià zhōu jǔxíng. The speech competition will be held next week.

**他在台上演讲时非常自信。** Tā zài tái shàng yǎnjiǎng shí fēicháng zìxìn. He was very confident when giving his speech on stage.

## Usage Notes

演讲 implies a prepared, formal speech delivered to an audience. It is more formal than 讲话 (to speak/address) and more structured than 发言 (to make a remark). 演讲稿 means the written draft of a speech. Common in academic, political, and competitive contexts.

## Memory Hook

演 means "to perform/act" and 讲 means "to speak": an 演讲 is a performance of speaking, a polished act of public address.
