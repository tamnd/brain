## 否认 (fǒurèn)

否认 means to deny or refuse to acknowledge something, asserting that a claim or accusation is not true.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to deny, negate, or refuse to acknowledge a fact or accusation

## Example Sentences

**他否认了所有的指控。** Tā fǒurèn le suǒyǒu de zhǐkòng. He denied all the accusations.

**你不能否认这件事情的重要性。** Nǐ bù néng fǒurèn zhè jiàn shìqing de zhòngyào xìng. You cannot deny the importance of this matter.

**她否认自己曾经说过那句话。** Tā fǒurèn zìjǐ céngjīng shuō guò nà jù huà. She denied that she had ever said those words.

**事实摆在眼前，无法否认。** Shìshí bǎi zài yǎnqián, wúfǎ fǒurèn. The facts are right in front of us and cannot be denied.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 否认指控 | deny an accusation |
| 坚决否认 | firmly deny |
| 无法否认 | cannot deny |
| 否认事实 | deny the facts |

## Usage Notes

否认 requires a specific object describing what is being denied, such as a statement, accusation, or fact. It contrasts with 承认 (chéngrèn, to admit or acknowledge). In news and legal contexts, 否认 is very common. The phrase 无法否认 (cannot deny) is used to emphasize undeniable facts.

## Memory Hook

否 means "no or negative" and 认 means "to recognize or acknowledge," so 否认 is to say "no" to recognizing something, to deny it.
