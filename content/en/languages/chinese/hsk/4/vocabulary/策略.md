## 策略 (cèlüè)

策略 refers to a strategy, tactic, or planned approach used to achieve a specific goal or handle a situation effectively.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** strategy; tactic; approach; a carefully planned method for achieving a goal

## Example Sentences

**公司需要制定新的市场策略。** Gōngsī xūyào zhìdìng xīn de shìchǎng cèlüè. The company needs to develop a new marketing strategy.

**在谈判中，掌握正确的策略非常重要。** Zài tánpàn zhōng, zhǎngwò zhèngquè de cèlüè fēicháng zhòngyào. In negotiations, mastering the right tactics is very important.

**他改变了策略，最终赢得了比赛。** Tā gǎibiàn le cèlüè, zuìzhōng yíngdé le bǐsài. He changed his strategy and ultimately won the competition.

**这个问题需要用灵活的策略来处理。** Zhège wèntí xūyào yòng línghuó de cèlüè lái chǔlǐ. This problem needs to be handled with flexible tactics.

## Usage Notes

策略 emphasizes the tactical and planned nature of an approach, often with a shorter-term or more flexible focus than 战略 (zhànlüè), which implies grand, long-term strategy. At B1 level, 策略 appears in business, sports, and problem-solving contexts.

## Memory Hook

策 means "plan/scheme" and 略 means "outline/brief plan": a 策略 is a sketched-out plan, a tactical outline for getting things done.
