## 独特 (dútè)

独特 describes something that is unique or distinctive, unlike anything else. It carries a positive connotation, often expressing admiration for an original style, approach, or quality that sets someone or something apart from the rest.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** Unique; distinctive; one-of-a-kind; original.

## Example Sentences

**这位设计师以其独特的风格在业界享有很高的声誉。**
Zhè wèi shèjìshī yǐ qí dútè de fēnggé zài yèjiè xiǎngyǒu hěn gāo de shēngyù.
This designer enjoys a high reputation in the industry for her distinctive style.

**每个人都有独特的才能，关键是找到适合自己的发展道路。**
Měi gèrén dōu yǒu dútè de cáinéng, guānjiàn shì zhǎodào shìhé zìjǐ de fāzhǎn dàolù.
Every person has unique talents; the key is to find a development path that suits oneself.

**这个城市独特的建筑风格融合了东西方文化的精华。**
Zhège chéngshì dútè de jiànzhú fēnggé rónghé le dōng xīfāng wénhuà de jīnghuá.
The city's distinctive architectural style blends the essence of Eastern and Western cultures.

**她处理问题的方式非常独特，总能找到别人想不到的解决方案。**
Tā chǔlǐ wèntí de fāngshì fēicháng dútè, zǒng néng zhǎodào biérén xiǎng bù dào de jiějué fāng'àn.
Her way of dealing with problems is very unique; she always finds solutions that others cannot think of.

## Usage Notes

独特 always functions as an attributive or predicative adjective. It is more emphatic than 特别 (tèbié, special) and implies a quality that is truly one-of-a-kind. A common construction is 以其独特的 X 著称/闻名 (to be known/famous for its unique X). 独特 pairs naturally with aesthetic and intellectual qualities: style, perspective, charm, approach, flavor.

## Memory Hook

独 means "alone/solitary" and 特 means "special/exceptional." Something 独特 stands alone in its specialness — so distinct that it occupies a category of its own.
