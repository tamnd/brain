## 技能 (jìnéng)

技能 refers to a practical skill or technical ability -- something learned and developed through training and practice.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** skill; technical ability; competence

## Example Sentences

**沟通技能对工作很重要。** Gōutōng jìnéng duì gōngzuò hěn zhòngyào. Communication skills are very important for work.

**他有很多实用的技能。** Tā yǒu hěn duō shíyòng de jìnéng. He has many practical skills.

**学习新技能可以提升竞争力。** Xuéxí xīn jìnéng kěyǐ tíshēng jìngzhēnglì. Learning new skills can boost competitiveness.

**这份工作需要计算机技能。** Zhè fèn gōngzuò xūyào jìsuànjī jìnéng. This job requires computer skills.

## Usage Notes

技能 emphasizes practical, hands-on ability rather than theoretical knowledge. It is commonly used in career and education contexts. Compare with 能力 (nénglì = ability/capacity), which is broader, and 技术 (jìshù = technology/technique), which leans more toward technical knowledge.

## Memory Hook

JI (技 = technique/craft) + NENG (能 = ability/can): technique + ability = skill.
