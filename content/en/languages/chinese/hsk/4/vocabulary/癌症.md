## 癌症 (áizhèng)

癌症 is the general term for cancer in Chinese. It refers to diseases characterized by the uncontrolled growth of abnormal cells and is one of the most discussed serious medical conditions.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** cancer; a malignant disease caused by uncontrolled growth of abnormal cells

## Example Sentences

**早期发现癌症可以大大提高治愈率。** Zǎoqī fāxiàn áizhèng kěyǐ dàdà tígāo zhìyù lǜ. Early detection of cancer can greatly improve the cure rate.

**他的父亲去年被诊断出患有肺癌症。** Tā de fùqīn qùnián bèi zhěnduàn chū huànyǒu fèi áizhèng. His father was diagnosed with lung cancer last year.

**吸烟是导致癌症的重要原因之一。** Xīyān shì dǎozhì áizhèng de zhòngyào yuányīn zhī yī. Smoking is one of the important causes of cancer.

**随着医学的进步，许多癌症已经可以治疗。** Suízhe yīxué de jìnbù, xǔduō áizhèng yǐjīng kěyǐ zhìliáo. With advances in medicine, many cancers can now be treated.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 患癌症 | to have / be diagnosed with cancer |
| 预防癌症 | to prevent cancer |
| 治疗癌症 | to treat cancer |
| 肺癌 / 胃癌 | lung cancer / stomach cancer |

## Usage Notes

癌症 is the general term for cancer. Specific cancers drop the 症 and add the organ: 肺癌 (lung cancer), 胃癌 (stomach cancer), 乳腺癌 (breast cancer), etc. The character 癌 (ái) on its own also means cancer/malignant tumor. 症 (zhèng) means "condition" or "symptom," making 癌症 literally "cancer condition."

## Memory Hook

癌 contains 品 (products/goods) inside a 疒 (illness radical) — imagine unhealthy "products" growing uncontrollably inside the body.
