## 观点 (guāndiǎn)

观点 refers to a person's point of view, perspective, or standpoint on a topic, especially in discussion or debate.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Viewpoint; perspective; standpoint (a personal position on a topic).

## Example Sentences

**我们应该尊重不同人的观点。**
Wǒmen yīnggāi zūnzhòng bùtóng rén de guāndiǎn.
We should respect the viewpoints of different people.

**从我的观点来看，这个决定是正确的。**
Cóng wǒ de guāndiǎn lái kàn, zhège juédìng shì zhèngquè de.
From my perspective, this decision is correct.

**他的观点很有意思，值得认真考虑。**
Tā de guāndiǎn hěn yǒu yìsi, zhídé rènzhēn kǎolǜ.
His viewpoint is very interesting and worth serious consideration.

**两位专家的观点完全相反。**
Liǎng wèi zhuānjiā de guāndiǎn wánquán xiāngfǎn.
The two experts' viewpoints are completely opposite.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 表达观点 | to express a viewpoint |

| 支持观点 | to support a viewpoint |
| 个人观点 | personal opinion |

## Usage Notes

观点 is more formal and structured than 想法 (idea/thought) or 意见 (opinion/suggestion). It often appears in academic writing, debates, and formal discussions. When presenting your viewpoint, the phrase 从我的观点来看 or 在我看来 both work well.

## Memory Hook

观 means "to observe/look at" and 点 means "point/dot"; 观点 is the specific point from which you observe, your unique vantage point.
