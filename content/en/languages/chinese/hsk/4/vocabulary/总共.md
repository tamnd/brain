## 总共 (zǒnggòng)

总共 indicates the total sum of something when all parts are counted together, meaning "in total," "altogether," or "in all."

## Meanings

1. **[adverb]** In total; altogether; in all (states the combined count or amount).

## Example Sentences

**这次旅行总共花了三千元。**
Zhè cì lǚxíng zǒnggòng huā le sānqiān yuán.
This trip cost three thousand yuan in total.

**班上总共有三十名学生。**
Bān shàng zǒnggòng yǒu sānshí míng xuésheng.
There are thirty students in the class altogether.

**他总共买了五本书。**
Tā zǒnggòng mǎi le wǔ běn shū.
He bought five books in all.

**这个项目总共需要六个月才能完成。**
Zhège xiàngmù zǒnggòng xūyào liù gè yuè cái néng wánchéng.
This project needs six months in total to be completed.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |

| 总共加起来 | adding up to a total of |

## Usage Notes

总共 is very close in meaning to 共 and 一共, all meaning "in total." 总共 is slightly more emphatic and often preferred in slightly more formal contexts. 一共 is the most common in everyday conversation.

## Memory Hook

总 means "general/overall" and 共 means "together/shared"; 总共 counts everything together under one roof to give you the grand total.
