## 培训 (péixùn)

培训 refers to organized training or instruction provided to develop skills or knowledge for a job or task.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to train; to provide training for
2. **[noun]** training; instruction program

## Example Sentences

**公司每年都会培训新员工。** Gōngsī měi nián dōu huì péixùn xīn yuángōng. The company trains new employees every year.

**她参加了一个为期两周的职业培训。** Tā cānjiāle yī gè wéiqī liǎng zhōu de zhíyè péixùn. She attended a two-week vocational training program.

**这次培训对提高我们的工作效率很有帮助。** Zhè cì péixùn duì tígāo wǒmen de gōngzuò xiàolǜ hěn yǒu bāngzhù. This training was very helpful in improving our work efficiency.

**他正在接受专业的销售技巧培训。** Tā zhèngzài jiēshòu zhuānyè de xiāoshòu jìqiǎo péixùn. He is currently receiving professional sales skills training.

## Usage Notes

培训 is used specifically for organized, skill-focused instruction, especially in professional or vocational contexts. It is different from 教育 (education, which is broader) and 教学 (teaching/instruction in a classroom). 培训班 (training class) is a very common compound noun.

## Memory Hook

培 means "to cultivate/nurture" and 训 means "to instruct/drill" -- 培训 is cultivating people by drilling them with instruction, i.e., training.
