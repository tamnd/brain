## 粗鲁 (cūlǔ)

粗鲁 describes behavior or speech that is rude, rough, or lacking in consideration for others, often carrying a strongly negative judgment.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** rude, crude, rough, impolite, boorish

## Example Sentences

**他说话很粗鲁，让大家都很不舒服。** Tā shuōhuà hěn cūlǔ, ràng dàjiā dōu hěn bù shūfu. He speaks very rudely, making everyone feel uncomfortable.

**粗鲁的行为会让人失去朋友。** Cūlǔ de xíngwéi huì ràng rén shīqù péngyou. Rude behavior will cause people to lose friends.

**不要对长辈说话粗鲁。** Bùyào duì zhǎngbèi shuōhuà cūlǔ. Do not speak rudely to elders.

**他意识到自己刚才的行为很粗鲁，于是道歉了。** Tā yìshí dào zìjǐ gāngcái de xíngwéi hěn cūlǔ, yúshì dàoqiàn le. He realized his earlier behavior was rude and so apologized.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 粗鲁的行为 (cūlǔ de xíngwéi) | rude behavior |
| 说话粗鲁 (shuōhuà cūlǔ) | to speak rudely |
| 态度粗鲁 (tàidu cūlǔ) | rude attitude |
| 粗鲁无礼 (cūlǔ wúlǐ) | rude and disrespectful |

## Usage Notes

粗鲁 specifically refers to rudeness in manner or speech toward people, making it different from 粗略 (cūlüè, rough/approximate). Its opposite is 礼貌 (lǐmào, polite) or 文雅 (wényǎ, refined/elegant). 粗鲁 is often used predicatively: 他很粗鲁 (tā hěn cūlǔ, "he is very rude").

## Memory Hook

粗 means "coarse/rough" and 鲁 evokes roughness — imagine a coarse, rough person who bulldozes through social niceties: that is 粗鲁 behavior.
