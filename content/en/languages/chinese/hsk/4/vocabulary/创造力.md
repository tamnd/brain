## 创造力 (chuàngzàolì)

创造力 refers to one's creative ability or power, the capacity to produce original ideas, solutions, or works.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** creativity, creative power, or the ability to invent and imagine

## Example Sentences

**孩子们的创造力令人惊叹。** Háizimen de chuàngzàolì lìng rén jīngtàn. Children's creativity is astonishing.

**这份工作需要很强的创造力。** Zhè fèn gōngzuò xūyào hěn qiáng de chuàngzàolì. This job requires strong creativity.

**良好的教育应该培养学生的创造力。** Liánghǎo de jiàoyù yīnggāi péiyǎng xuéshēng de chuàngzàolì. Good education should develop students' creativity.

**他用独特的创造力设计出了这件作品。** Tā yòng dútè de chuàngzàolì shèjì chū le zhè jiàn zuòpǐn. He used his unique creativity to design this work.

## Usage Notes

创造力 is formed from 创造 (to create) and the suffix 力 (power/ability). This pattern is common in Chinese: 能力 (capability), 竞争力 (competitiveness), 影响力 (influence). At B1 level, recognizing and using this suffix productively is a valuable skill. 创造力 is highly valued in educational and professional contexts.

## Memory Hook

创造 means "to create" and 力 means "power," so 创造力 is the "power to create," the very definition of creativity.
