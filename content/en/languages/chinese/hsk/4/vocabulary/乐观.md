## 乐观 (lèguān)

乐观 describes a positive, hopeful attitude toward life or a situation — the Chinese equivalent of "optimistic."

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** optimistic, hopeful, positive (in outlook)

## Example Sentences

**他总是很乐观，即使遇到困难也不放弃。** Tā zǒngshì hěn lèguān, jíshǐ yùdào kùnnan yě bù fàngqì. He is always very optimistic; even when he faces difficulties, he does not give up.

**我们应该对未来保持乐观的态度。** Wǒmen yīnggāi duì wèilái bǎochí lèguān de tàidu. We should maintain an optimistic attitude toward the future.

**乐观的人往往比悲观的人更健康。** Lèguān de rén wǎngwǎng bǐ bēiguān de rén gèng jiànkāng. Optimistic people are often healthier than pessimistic people.

**尽管情况不好，她依然保持乐观。** Jǐnguǎn qíngkuàng bù hǎo, tā yīrán bǎochí lèguān. Despite the bad situation, she remained optimistic.

## Usage Notes

乐观 is the antonym of 悲观 (pessimistic). It can function as a predicate adjective (他很乐观) or as an attributive modifier before a noun (乐观的态度). It can also be used as a noun in compounds like 乐观主义 (optimism).

## Memory Hook

乐 means "joy/happiness" and 观 means "view/perspective." So 乐观 literally means "to view things with joy" — an optimistic outlook.
