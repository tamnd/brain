## 合作 (hézuò)

合作 means to cooperate or collaborate, combining the efforts of two or more parties to achieve a common goal. It is widely used in professional, academic, and interpersonal contexts and carries a positive connotation of productive partnership.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To cooperate; to collaborate; to work together.
2. **[noun]** Cooperation; collaboration; partnership.

## Example Sentences

**两家公司决定展开长期合作，共同开发新产品。**
Liǎng jiā gōngsī juédìng zhǎnkāi cháng qī hézuò, gòng tóng kāifā xīn chǎnpǐn.
The two companies decided to enter into long-term cooperation to jointly develop new products.

**团队合作精神是这个项目成功的关键因素之一。**
Tuánduì hézuò jīngshén shì zhège xiàngmù chénggōng de guānjiàn yīnsù zhī yī.
The spirit of teamwork is one of the key factors in the success of this project.

**中美两国在气候变化问题上有广泛的合作空间。**
Zhōng Měi liǎng guó zài qìhòu biànhuà wèntí shàng yǒu guǎngfàn de hézuò kōngjiān.
China and the United States have extensive room for cooperation on the issue of climate change.

**请大家积极配合，共同完成这次合作任务。**
Qǐng dàjiā jījí pèihé, gòng tóng wánchéng zhè cì hézuò rènwù.
Please cooperate actively so we can complete this collaborative task together.

## Usage Notes

合作 often appears in the phrase 合作愉快 (hézuò yúkuài), a polite expression meaning "it has been a pleasure working together," similar to the English sign-off "a pleasure doing business with you." As a verb, it commonly takes the pattern 和/与 X 合作 (to cooperate with X) or 在 X 方面合作 (to cooperate in the area of X).

## Memory Hook

合 means "to combine/fit together" and 作 means "to do/make." Cooperation is combining what you make with what others make — fitting your efforts together perfectly.
