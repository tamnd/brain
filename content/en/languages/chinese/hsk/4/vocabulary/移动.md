## 移动 (yídòng)

移动 means to move or shift from one place to another, or describes something as mobile or portable.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to move; to shift; to change position
2. **[adjective]** mobile; portable; designed to be moved

## Example Sentences

**请把椅子移动到窗边。** Qǐng bǎ yǐzi yídòng dào chuāng biān. Please move the chair to the window.

**移动互联网改变了人们的沟通方式。** Yídòng hùliánwǎng gǎibiàn le rénmen de gōutōng fāngshì. Mobile internet has changed the way people communicate.

**这个架子可以自由移动，非常方便。** Zhège jiàzi kěyǐ zìyóu yídòng, fēicháng fāngbiàn. This shelf can move freely, which is very convenient.

**他快速移动，躲开了障碍物。** Tā kuàisù yídòng, duǒkāi le zhàng'àiwù. He moved quickly and dodged the obstacle.

## Usage Notes

移动 can describe physical movement of objects or people, or it can be used as a modifier meaning "mobile" in technology contexts (移动互联网, 移动支付). China Mobile, the telecom company, is called 中国移动 (Zhōngguó Yídòng). This dual usage makes it very common in both everyday and tech-related Chinese.

## Memory Hook

移 means "to shift/transfer" and 动 means "to move": 移动 is shifting and moving, changing your position in space.
