## 模式 (móshì)

模式 refers to a pattern, mode, or established way of doing something, often describing a recurring structure or operational approach.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** model; pattern; mode; a standard or recurring way of operating or structuring something

## Example Sentences

**这种工作模式越来越受欢迎。** Zhè zhǒng gōngzuò móshì yuè lái yuè shòu huānyíng. This work pattern is becoming increasingly popular.

**传统的教育模式正在发生变化。** Chuántǒng de jiàoyù móshì zhèngzài fāshēng biànhuà. The traditional model of education is changing.

**他们采用了全新的商业模式。** Tāmen cǎiyòng le quánxīn de shāngyè móshì. They adopted a completely new business model.

**睡眠模式对健康有很大影响。** Shuìmián móshì duì jiànkāng yǒu hěn dà yǐngxiǎng. Sleep patterns have a great impact on health.

## Usage Notes

模式 differs from 模型 in that 模式 is more abstract, referring to patterns or modes of behavior and organization, while 模型 is more often a concrete physical or mathematical model. 模式 is very common in business and social sciences discourse.

## Memory Hook

模 means "template" and 式 means "style/form": a 模式 is a template style, the recurring pattern in how things are done.
