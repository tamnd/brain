## 反对 (fǎnduì)

反对 means to oppose or object to something, expressing disagreement or resistance.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to oppose; to object to; to be against; to disagree with

## Example Sentences

**他坚决反对这个计划。** Tā jiānjué fǎnduì zhège jìhuà. He firmly opposes this plan.

**很多人反对在这里建工厂。** Hěn duō rén fǎnduì zài zhèlǐ jiàn gōngchǎng. Many people oppose building a factory here.

**她反对浪费食物，每次都吃得干干净净。** Tā fǎnduì làngfèi shíwù, měi cì dōu chī de gāngānjìngjìng. She is against wasting food and always finishes everything on her plate.

**我不反对你的意见，只是有些不同看法。** Wǒ bù fǎnduì nǐ de yìjiàn, zhǐshì yǒuxiē bùtóng kànfǎ. I don't oppose your opinion; I just have some different views.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 坚决反对 | to firmly oppose |
| 反对意见 | opposing opinion; objection |
| 强烈反对 | to strongly oppose |
| 反对票 | opposing vote; "no" vote |

## Usage Notes

反对 is the antonym of 支持 (to support). It is a transitive verb and must be followed by an object. It can be negated with 不 to mean "not oppose/have no objection to." In formal settings, 反对意见 (objection) is a common noun form.

## Memory Hook

反 means "to reverse/go against" and 对 means "correct/to face" -- 反对 means turning against what is in front of you, i.e., opposing it.
