## 掌握 (zhǎngwò)

掌握 means to master or have thorough command of something, whether a skill, language, knowledge, or situation, implying confident control and deep understanding.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to master; to grasp; to command; to have control of

## Example Sentences

**他掌握了三门外语，工作非常顺利。** Tā zhǎngwòle sān mén wàiyǔ, gōngzuò fēicháng shùnlì. He has mastered three foreign languages and his work goes very smoothly.

**要掌握一项技能，需要大量的练习。** Yào zhǎngwò yī xiàng jìnéng, xūyào dàliàng de liànxí. To master a skill requires a great deal of practice.

**她掌握了谈判的主动权。** Tā zhǎngwòle tánpàn de zhǔdòngquán. She took command of the initiative in the negotiation.

**学生需要掌握基本的数学概念。** Xuéshēng xūyào zhǎngwò jīběn de shùxué gàiniàn. Students need to master basic mathematical concepts.

## Usage Notes

掌握 implies a high level of proficiency and confident control, going beyond just knowing something superficially. It is often used to describe mastery of a language, skill, or situation. It can also refer to having control of a situation (掌握主动权). The image is of something firmly held in the palm of your hand.

## Memory Hook

掌 means "palm of the hand" and 握 means "to grip/hold." So 掌握 is to hold something in the palm of your hand: having something so well mastered that you hold it completely.
