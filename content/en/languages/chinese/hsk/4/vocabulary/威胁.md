## 威胁 (wēixié)

威胁 means to threaten someone or something, or refers to a threat that puts someone at risk.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to threaten; to intimidate; to pose a threat to
2. **[noun]** threat; menace

## Example Sentences

**污染正在严重威胁这里的生态环境。** Wūrǎn zhèngzài yánzhòng wēixié zhèlǐ de shēngtài huánjìng. Pollution is seriously threatening the ecological environment here.

**他受到了恐怖分子的威胁，不敢出门。** Tā shòudào le kǒngbù fènzǐ de wēixié, bù gǎn chūmén. He was threatened by terrorists and didn't dare to go out.

**气候变化对粮食安全构成了严重威胁。** Qìhòu biànhuà duì liángshí ānquán gòuchéngle yánzhòng wēixié. Climate change poses a serious threat to food security.

**她威胁说如果不还钱就报警。** Tā wēixié shuō rúguǒ bù huán qián jiù bàojǐng. She threatened to call the police if the money wasn't returned.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 构成威胁 | to pose a threat |
| 受到威胁 | to be under threat |
| 严重威胁 | serious threat |
| 安全威胁 | security threat |

## Usage Notes

威胁 can be used as both a verb and a noun. As a verb, it takes an object that is being threatened. As a noun, it often appears in the structures 构成威胁 (to constitute a threat) or 受到威胁 (to come under threat). It carries a negative connotation and is used in formal and informal contexts alike.

## Memory Hook

威 means "might/authority" and 胁 means "to coerce/rib area" -- 威胁 is using your might to pressure someone's vulnerable side, i.e., to threaten.
