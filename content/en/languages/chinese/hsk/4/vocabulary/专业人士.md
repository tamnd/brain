## 专业人士 (zhuānyè rénshì)

专业人士 refers to a person who has specialized training or expertise in a particular field — a professional or expert.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** professional, expert, specialist (in a given field)

## Example Sentences

**遇到法律问题，最好咨询专业人士。** Yùdào fǎlǜ wèntí, zuìhǎo zīxún zhuānyè rénshì. When you encounter legal issues, it is best to consult a professional.

**这个项目需要很多不同领域的专业人士参与。** Zhège xiàngmù xūyào hěn duō bùtóng lǐngyù de zhuānyè rénshì cānyù. This project requires professionals from many different fields to participate.

**她是医疗领域的专业人士，有二十年经验。** Tā shì yīliáo lǐngyù de zhuānyè rénshì, yǒu èrshí nián jīngyàn. She is a professional in the medical field with twenty years of experience.

**企业应该聘请专业人士来管理财务。** Qǐyè yīnggāi pìnqǐng zhuānyè rénshì lái guǎnlǐ cáiwù. Companies should hire professionals to manage their finances.

## Usage Notes

专业人士 is a formal compound noun. 专业 means "professional/specialized" and 人士 means "person of status or expertise." It is slightly more formal than 专家 (expert) and is often used in written or official contexts. It can refer to any profession depending on context.

## Memory Hook

专业 (specialized field) + 人士 (person of distinction) = a distinguished person in a specialized field = professional.
