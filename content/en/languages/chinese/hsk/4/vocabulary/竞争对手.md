## 竞争对手 (jìngzhēng duìshǒu)

竞争对手 means a competitor or rival, referring to an individual or organization that competes against you in the same field.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** competitor; rival; an entity that competes with you for the same goal or market

## Example Sentences

**我们要了解竞争对手的优势和弱点。** Wǒmen yào liǎojiě jìngzhēng duìshǒu de yōushì hé ruòdiǎn. We need to understand the strengths and weaknesses of our competitors.

**这两家公司是直接竞争对手。** Zhè liǎng jiā gōngsī shì zhíjiē jìngzhēng duìshǒu. These two companies are direct competitors.

**有了竞争对手，才能促进自己不断进步。** Yǒu le jìngzhēng duìshǒu, cái néng cùjìn zìjǐ bùduàn jìnbù. Having competitors motivates you to keep improving.

**他把昔日的竞争对手变成了合作伙伴。** Tā bǎ xīrì de jìngzhēng duìshǒu biàn chéng le hézuò huǒbàn. He turned his former competitor into a business partner.

## Usage Notes

竞争对手 combines 竞争 (competition) and 对手 (opponent/counterpart). It is used in business, sports, and academic contexts. Note that 对手 alone can mean opponent in sports or debates. The full compound 竞争对手 is specifically for competitive rivals in a shared arena.

## Memory Hook

竞争 means "competition" and 对手 means "opponent/the hand facing you": your 竞争对手 is the competing hand reaching for the same prize.
