## 市场 (shìchǎng)

市场 refers to a market in both the physical sense (a marketplace) and the economic sense (a market for goods, services, or ideas).

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** a market; a marketplace (physical or economic)
2. **[noun]** the market (economic context, such as demand and supply)

## Example Sentences

**这家公司正在开拓海外市场。** Zhè jiā gōngsī zhèngzài kāituò hǎiwài shìchǎng. This company is expanding into overseas markets.

**市场竞争越来越激烈。** Shìchǎng jìngzhēng yuèlái yuè jīliè. Market competition is becoming increasingly fierce.

**这种产品在年轻人中很有市场。** Zhè zhǒng chǎnpǐn zài niánqīngrén zhōng hěn yǒu shìchǎng. This type of product has a strong market among young people.

**他每天早上去菜市场买新鲜蔬菜。** Tā měitiān zǎoshang qù cài shìchǎng mǎi xīnxiān shūcài. He goes to the vegetable market every morning to buy fresh vegetables.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 市场竞争 | market competition |
| 市场需求 | market demand |
| 开拓市场 | to expand/open up a market |
| 股票市场 | stock market |

## Usage Notes

市场 is widely used in both everyday and business Chinese. In everyday speech it can mean a physical market (like a food market), while in business and economics it refers to the broader market system. The phrase 有市场 (to have a market) means something is in demand or popular.

## Memory Hook

市 means "city/trade" and 场 means "place/field." A 市场 is a "city trade place," which is exactly what a market is: a place where city trading happens.
