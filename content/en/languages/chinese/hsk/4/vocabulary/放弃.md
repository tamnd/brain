## 放弃 (fàngqì)

放弃 means to give up or abandon, referring to the act of voluntarily stopping an effort, relinquishing a right, or letting go of a goal or possession.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to give up; to abandon; to relinquish; to let go of

## Example Sentences

**不管多难，我都不会放弃。** Bùguǎn duō nán, wǒ dōu bù huì fàngqì. No matter how difficult, I will not give up.

**她放弃了高薪工作，去追求自己的梦想。** Tā fàngqìle gāo xīn gōngzuò, qù zhuīqiú zìjǐ de mèngxiǎng. She gave up a high-paying job to pursue her own dreams.

**我们不应该轻易放弃自己的机会。** Wǒmen bù yīnggāi qīngyì fàngqì zìjǐ de jīhuì. We should not easily give up our opportunities.

**他放弃了参加比赛的资格。** Tā fàngqìle cānjiā bǐsài de zīgé. He gave up his qualification to participate in the competition.

## Usage Notes

放弃 can apply to tangible things (rights, opportunities, positions) and intangible ones (dreams, hopes, plans). It is a conscious and voluntary act. In Chinese motivational culture, the emphasis is often on NOT 放弃 (not giving up), so this word frequently appears in contexts of perseverance and resilience.

## Memory Hook

放 means "to release/let go" and 弃 means "to discard/abandon." Together they create a double sense of releasing and discarding: letting something go completely.
