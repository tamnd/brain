## 授权 (shòuquán)

授权 means to authorize or grant authority, referring to the act of giving someone official permission or delegating power to act on one's behalf.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to authorize; to grant permission; to delegate authority
2. **[noun]** authorization; granted permission

## Example Sentences

**经理授权他处理这个紧急项目。** Jīnglǐ shòuquán tā chǔlǐ zhège jǐnjí xiàngmù. The manager authorized him to handle this urgent project.

**没有授权，不能访问这些文件。** Méiyǒu shòuquán, bù néng fǎngwèn zhèxiē wénjiàn. Without authorization, these files cannot be accessed.

**公司授权当地代理商销售产品。** Gōngsī shòuquán dāngdì dàilǐ shāng xiāoshòu chǎnpǐn. The company authorized the local agent to sell products.

**他收到了上级的授权，可以独立签合同。** Tā shōudàole shàngjí de shòuquán, kěyǐ dúlì qiān hétong. He received authorization from his superior to sign contracts independently.

## Usage Notes

授权 is formal and commonly used in legal, business, and digital security contexts. In technology, it often refers to access control (authorizing a user). The structure 授权某人做某事 (to authorize someone to do something) is a common pattern. Its opposite is 未经授权 (unauthorized).

## Memory Hook

授 means "to grant/bestow" and 权 means "authority/rights." So 授权 is to bestow authority: handing over the keys of power to someone.
