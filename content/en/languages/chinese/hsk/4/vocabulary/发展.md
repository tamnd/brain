## 发展 (fāzhǎn)

发展 means to develop or describes development, covering growth and progress of economies, skills, relationships, or ideas.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to develop, grow, or expand
2. **[noun]** development, growth, or progress

## Example Sentences

**中国的经济发展速度令世界惊叹。** Zhōngguó de jīngjì fāzhǎn sùdù lìng shìjiè jīngtàn. China's speed of economic development is astonishing to the world.

**他的中文水平发展得很快。** Tā de zhōngwén shuǐpíng fāzhǎn de hěn kuài. His Chinese level is developing very quickly.

**这个城市的基础设施发展得非常好。** Zhège chéngshì de jīchǔ shèshī fāzhǎn de fēicháng hǎo. This city's infrastructure has developed very well.

**教育是推动社会发展的重要因素。** Jiàoyù shì tuīdòng shèhuì fāzhǎn de zhòngyào yīnsù. Education is an important factor in driving social development.

## Usage Notes

发展 is one of the most important and frequently used words in modern Chinese, appearing in news, politics, business, and everyday conversation. As a noun, it pairs with many adjectives and domain words. As a verb, it describes growth over time. The concept of 发展 is central to China's national narrative, with phrases like 科学发展观 (Scientific Outlook on Development) being common in public discourse.

## Memory Hook

发 means "to launch or send out" and 展 means "to spread or unfold," so 发展 is to launch something and let it unfold and spread, to develop.
