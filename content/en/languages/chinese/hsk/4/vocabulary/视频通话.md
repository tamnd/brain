## 视频通话 (shìpín tōnghuà)

视频通话 refers to a video call, where participants can see and hear each other in real time over the internet. It is widely used through apps like WeChat (微信) and has become a common way to stay in touch with family and friends.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** video call; a real-time call that transmits both audio and video

## Example Sentences

**我每周都和父母进行视频通话。** Wǒ měi zhōu dōu hé fùmǔ jìnxíng shìpín tōnghuà. I have a video call with my parents every week.

**视频通话让远距离的家人感觉更近了。** Shìpín tōnghuà ràng yuǎn jùlí de jiārén gǎnjué gèng jìn le. Video calls make faraway family feel closer.

**我们用微信打视频通话吧。** Wǒmen yòng Wēixìn dǎ shìpín tōnghuà ba. Let's make a video call using WeChat.

**网络不好，视频通话一直断线。** Wǎngluò bù hǎo, shìpín tōnghuà yīzhí duànxiàn. The internet is bad; the video call keeps disconnecting.

## Usage Notes

视频通话 is the standard formal term. Colloquially, people often say 视频 alone as a verb (我们视频吧, "let's video call") or 打视频 (dǎ shìpín). 通话 alone means "phone call." The combination 视频 + 通话 specifies that it is a call with video. Compare with 语音通话 (yǔyīn tōnghuà, voice call only).

## Memory Hook

视频 (video/visual) + 通话 (call/communicate): a call where you communicate visually — a video call.
