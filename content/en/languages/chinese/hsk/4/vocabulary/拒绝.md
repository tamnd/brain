## 拒绝 (jùjué)

拒绝 means to refuse, reject, or decline a request, offer, or suggestion.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to refuse; to reject; to decline; to turn down

## Example Sentences

**他拒绝了那家公司的工作邀请。** Tā jùjuéle nà jiā gōngsī de gōngzuò yāoqǐng. He turned down the job offer from that company.

**她礼貌地拒绝了他的求婚。** Tā lǐmào de jùjuéle tā de qiúhūn. She politely rejected his marriage proposal.

**你有权利拒绝任何让你感到不舒服的要求。** Nǐ yǒu quánlì jùjué rènhé ràng nǐ gǎndào bù shūfu de yāoqiú. You have the right to refuse any request that makes you feel uncomfortable.

**会议室已经拒绝了新的预约，因为已经满了。** Huìyìshì yǐjīng jùjuéle xīn de yùyuē, yīnwèi yǐjīng mǎn le. The meeting room has already declined new reservations because it is fully booked.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 拒绝请求 | to refuse a request |
| 断然拒绝 | to flatly refuse |
| 礼貌拒绝 | to politely decline |
| 拒绝诱惑 | to resist temptation |

## Usage Notes

拒绝 is a transitive verb that must be followed by an object. It is stronger and more definitive than 不想 (don't want to) or 不愿意 (unwilling to). The adverb 断然 (flatly/decisively) is often combined with 拒绝 to emphasize a firm refusal. 拒绝诱惑 (to resist temptation) is a common idiomatic phrase.

## Memory Hook

拒 means "to resist/ward off" and 绝 means "absolutely/to cut off" -- 拒绝 is absolutely cutting off and resisting something, i.e., to refuse completely.
