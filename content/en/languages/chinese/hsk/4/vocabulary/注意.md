## 注意 (zhùyì)

注意 means to pay attention, to take note of, or to be careful about something.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to pay attention to; to be mindful of; to take note
2. **[noun]** attention; notice

## Example Sentences

**请注意安全，小心地滑。** Qǐng zhùyì ānquán, xiǎoxīn dì huá. Please pay attention to safety; watch out for slippery ground.

**上课时要注意听老师讲课。** Shàngkè shí yào zhùyì tīng lǎoshī jiǎngkè. During class you should pay attention to the teacher's lecture.

**你有没有注意到他的变化？** Nǐ yǒu méiyǒu zhùyì dào tā de biànhuà? Did you notice the change in him?

**注意身体，不要太累了。** Zhùyì shēntǐ, bùyào tài lèi le. Take care of your health; don't get too tired.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 注意安全 | pay attention to safety |

## Usage Notes

注意 can be used as a verb (注意某事: to pay attention to something) or in the fixed phrase 引起注意 (to attract attention). As an imperative, 注意! alone means "Watch out!" or "Attention!" It is softer than 小心 (be careful) but similar in everyday use.

## Memory Hook

注 means "to pour/focus" and 意 means "mind/intention": 注意 is pouring your mind onto something, directing your full attention.
