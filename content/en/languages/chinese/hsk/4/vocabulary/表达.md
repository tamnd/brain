## 表达 (biǎodá)

表达 refers to the act of making internal thoughts, emotions, or intentions known through words, actions, or other means of communication.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To express, to convey, to communicate (feelings, ideas, opinions).

## Example Sentences

**他不擅长用语言表达自己内心的感受。**
Tā bù shàncháng yòng yǔyán biǎodá zìjǐ nèixīn de gǎnshòu.
He is not good at expressing his inner feelings through words.

**这幅画以独特的方式表达了艺术家对自由的渴望。**
Zhè fú huà yǐ dútè de fāngshì biǎodá le yìshùjiā duì zìyóu de kěwàng.
This painting expresses the artist's yearning for freedom in a unique way.

**在正式场合，我们需要注意如何得体地表达自己的观点。**
Zài zhèngshì chǎnghé, wǒmen xūyào zhùyì rúhé détǐ de biǎodá zìjǐ de guāndiǎn.
In formal settings, we need to pay attention to how to express our views appropriately.

**她用一束鲜花表达了对老师多年来辛勤付出的感激之情。**
Tā yòng yī shù xiānhuā biǎodá le duì lǎoshī duō nián lái xīnqín fùchū de gǎnjī zhī qíng.
She used a bouquet of flowers to convey her gratitude for the teacher's years of dedicated effort.

## Usage Notes

表达 focuses on outward communication of inner states and is more formal than 说 (to say). It often appears with abstract objects: 表达情感, 表达立场, 表达不满. The related noun form 表达能力 (expressive ability) is widely used in education and workplace contexts. 表达 can use various means, not just speech: 用音乐表达, 用画表达.

## Memory Hook

表 means "outside/surface" and 达 means "to reach" — your inner thoughts reach the outside surface through expression.
