## 顺利 (shùnlì)

顺利 describes a process or event that goes smoothly, without unexpected problems or obstacles.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** Smooth; without a hitch; going well; successful.

## Example Sentences

**祝你旅途顺利！**
Zhù nǐ lǚtú shùnlì!
Wishing you a smooth journey!

**会议进行得很顺利。**
Huìyì jìnxíng de hěn shùnlì.
The meeting went very smoothly.

**他顺利地通过了面试。**
Tā shùnlì de tōngguòle miànshì.
He passed the interview without a hitch.

**希望你的手术顺利。**
Xīwàng nǐ de shǒushù shùnlì.
I hope your surgery goes smoothly.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |

| 顺利完成 | to complete smoothly |

| 一切顺利 | everything goes well |
| 祝...顺利 | wish ... success/smooth going |

## Usage Notes

顺利 is very commonly used in wishes and greetings such as "祝你一切顺利" (wishing you all the best). It can function as a predicate ("进行得很顺利") or as an adverb ("顺利地完成"). Its opposite is 不顺利 or 遇到困难 (to encounter difficulties).

## Memory Hook

顺 means "to go along with the flow" and 利 means "sharp/beneficial" — going with the current and cutting through cleanly means everything is smooth and easy.
