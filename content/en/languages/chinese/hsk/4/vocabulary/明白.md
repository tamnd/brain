## 明白 (míngbai)

明白 means to understand something clearly, or to describe something as clear and obvious.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to understand; to realize
2. **[adjective]** clear; obvious; plain

## Example Sentences

**你明白我的意思吗？** Nǐ míngbai wǒ de yìsi ma? Do you understand what I mean?

**我现在终于明白了。** Wǒ xiànzài zhōngyú míngbaile. I finally understand now.

**他把问题说得很明白。** Tā bǎ wèntí shuō de hěn míngbai. He explained the problem very clearly.

**这件事情你要明白。** Zhè jiàn shìqíng nǐ yào míngbai. You need to be clear about this matter.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 明白了 (míngbaile) | understood; I see |
| 说明白 (shuō míngbai) | to explain clearly |
| 搞明白 (gǎo míngbai) | to figure out |
| 明白道理 (míngbai dàolǐ) | to understand the reasoning |

## Usage Notes

明白 is frequently used as a response to show you have understood, similar to saying "I see" or "got it" in English. As an adjective it describes clarity of expression or thought. It is more informal than 理解 (lǐjiě) and suits everyday conversation.

## Memory Hook

When something becomes bright (明) and white (白), it is crystal clear — you truly understand it.
