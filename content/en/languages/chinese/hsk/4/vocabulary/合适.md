## 合适 (héshì)

合适 means suitable, appropriate, or fitting, describing something that is the right match for a given situation, purpose, or person.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** suitable, appropriate, fitting, or just right for a particular situation

## Example Sentences

**这件衣服的颜色很合适，非常适合你。** Zhè jiàn yīfú de yánsè hěn héshì, fēicháng shìhé nǐ. The color of this outfit is very suitable; it suits you perfectly.

**这个价格很合适，我就买这个吧。** Zhège jiàgé hěn héshì, wǒ jiù mǎi zhège ba. This price is very appropriate; I will buy this one.

**他是这个职位最合适的人选。** Tā shì zhège zhíwèi zuì héshì de rénxuǎn. He is the most suitable candidate for this position.

**你需要找一个合适的时间和他谈谈。** Nǐ xūyào zhǎo yīgè héshì de shíjiān hé tā tán tán. You need to find a suitable time to talk to him.

## Usage Notes

合适 is a broadly applicable adjective for describing suitability in terms of people, prices, timing, sizes, or situations. It is slightly more colloquial than 适合 (shìhé), which functions as a verb (这件衣服适合你, this outfit suits you). 合适 is typically used predicatively (价格很合适) or before nouns (合适的候选人).

## Memory Hook

合 means "to fit or match" and 适 means "appropriate," so 合适 is something that fits and is appropriate, a perfect match.
