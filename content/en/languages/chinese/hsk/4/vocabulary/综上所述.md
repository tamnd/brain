## 综上所述 (zōng shàng suǒ shù)

综上所述 is a formal written phrase used to signal the beginning of a summary or final conclusion, equivalent to "in summary," "to sum up," or "as stated above."

## Meanings

1. **[phrase]** in summary, to sum up, as stated above, in conclusion

## Example Sentences

**综上所述，我们可以得出这个方法是有效的。** Zōng shàng suǒ shù, wǒmen kěyǐ dé chū zhège fāngfǎ shì yǒuxiào de. In summary, we can conclude that this method is effective.

**综上所述，这个项目具有很高的研究价值。** Zōng shàng suǒ shù, zhège xiàngmù jùyǒu hěn gāo de yánjiū jiàzhí. To sum up, this project has great research value.

**综上所述，改善环境需要所有人的共同努力。** Zōng shàng suǒ shù, gǎishàn huánjìng xūyào suǒyǒu rén de gòngtóng nǔlì. In summary, improving the environment requires the collective effort of everyone.

**综上所述，我们的建议是增加投资。** Zōng shàng suǒ shù, wǒmen de jiànyì shì zēngjiā tóuzī. To conclude, our recommendation is to increase investment.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 综上所述，…… (zōng shàng suǒ shù,...) | in summary, ... |
| 综上所述，可以得出 (zōng shàng suǒ shù, kěyǐ dé chū) | from the above, one can conclude |
| 综上所述，我们认为 (zōng shàng suǒ shù, wǒmen rènwéi) | in summary, we believe |
| 综上所述，建议 (zōng shàng suǒ shù, jiànyì) | in summary, it is recommended |

## Usage Notes

综上所述 is exclusively a written and formal spoken phrase, found in academic papers, reports, and formal presentations. It is almost never used in casual conversation. It always appears at the beginning of a sentence, introducing the concluding paragraph or statement. The equivalent in casual speech is 总的来说 (zǒng de lái shuō, "generally speaking/to sum up").

## Memory Hook

综 (synthesize) + 上 (above) + 所述 (what was stated) = "synthesizing everything stated above" — a perfect image of gathering all your points into one final statement.
