## 关系 (guānxi)

关系 can mean a relationship or connection between people or things as a noun, and as a verb it means "to matter" or "to be related to."

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Relationship; connection; tie (between people, organizations, or concepts).
2. **[verb]** To matter; to be relevant; to affect.

## Example Sentences

**他们两个人的关系非常好。**
Tāmen liǎng gè rén de guānxi fēicháng hǎo.
The relationship between the two of them is very good.

**这件事和我没有关系。**
Zhè jiàn shì hé wǒ méiyǒu guānxi.
This matter has nothing to do with me.

**没关系，我不介意。**
Méi guānxi, wǒ bù jièyì.
It does not matter; I do not mind.

**国家之间的外交关系很重要。**
Guójiā zhījiān de wàijiāo guānxi hěn zhòngyào.
Diplomatic relationships between countries are very important.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 没关系 | it does not matter; never mind |
| 有关系 | to be related; to matter |
| 人际关系 | interpersonal relationships |
| 建立关系 | to establish a relationship |
| 维持关系 | to maintain a relationship |

## Usage Notes

没关系 is one of the most common phrases in daily Chinese, used to say "never mind" or "it is fine." When 关系 is used as a verb meaning "to matter," it often appears in negative sentences: 这和我没关系 (this has nothing to do with me).

## Memory Hook

关 means "to close/connect" and 系 means "to tie/link"; 关系 is the tie that closes the gap between two people or things, connecting them.
