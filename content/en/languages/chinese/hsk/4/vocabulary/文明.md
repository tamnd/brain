## 文明 (wénmíng)

文明 refers to civilization as a noun, or to civilized and cultured behavior as an adjective.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** civilization; a stage of human social development with advanced culture and society
2. **[adjective]** civilized; cultured; well-mannered

## Example Sentences

**中国有着悠久的文明历史。** Zhōngguó yǒuzhe yōujiǔ de wénmíng lìshǐ. China has a long history of civilization.

**请文明使用公共设施。** Qǐng wénmíng shǐyòng gōnggòng shèshī. Please use public facilities in a civilized manner.

**古代文明对现代社会影响很大。** Gǔdài wénmíng duì xiàndài shèhuì yǐngxiǎng hěn dà. Ancient civilizations have a great influence on modern society.

**我们应该做文明的市民。** Wǒmen yīnggāi zuò wénmíng de shìmín. We should be civilized citizens.

## Usage Notes

文明 can function as both a noun and an adjective. As a noun it refers to a whole civilization or cultural era. As an adjective it describes polite, cultured behavior in everyday contexts. At B1 level it appears in discussions of culture, history, and public etiquette.

## Memory Hook

Think of "wen" (writing/culture) plus "ming" (bright): a civilization is where culture shines brightly.
