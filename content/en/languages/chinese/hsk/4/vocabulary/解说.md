## 解说 (jiěshuō)

解说 means "to explain," "to comment," or "to narrate" and is often used for providing spoken commentary or interpretation of events, exhibits, or broadcasts.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to explain; to interpret; to provide commentary
2. **[noun]** commentary; narration; explanation

## Example Sentences

**导游正在解说这座古建筑的历史。** Dǎoyóu zhèngzài jiěshuō zhè zuò gǔ jiànzhú de lìshǐ. The tour guide is explaining the history of this ancient building.

**比赛时有专业解说员为观众讲解。** Bǐsài shí yǒu zhuānyè jiěshuō yuán wèi guānzhòng jiǎngjiě. During the competition, a professional commentator explained things to the audience.

**博物馆提供语音解说服务。** Bówùguǎn tígōng yǔyīn jiěshuō fúwù. The museum provides audio commentary services.

**这部纪录片的解说词写得很好。** Zhè bù jìlùpiàn de jiěshuō cí xiě de hěn hǎo. The narration of this documentary is very well written.

## Usage Notes

解说 is commonly associated with sports broadcasting (解说员 as a sports commentator), museum audio guides, and documentary narration. It differs from 解释 (jiěshì, to explain) in that 解说 usually implies a performance or broadcast context with an audience, while 解释 is more conversational.

## Memory Hook

解 (jiě) means "to explain" and 说 (shuō) means "to speak": 解说 is explaining by speaking, like a commentator who speaks to make things clear.
