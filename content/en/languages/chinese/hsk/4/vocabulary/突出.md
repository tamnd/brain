## 突出 (tūchū)

突出 means to be noticeably prominent or excellent, or physically to protrude and stand out beyond a surface.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** Prominent; outstanding; remarkable; conspicuous.
2. **[verb]** To stick out; to protrude; to highlight; to emphasize.

## Example Sentences

**她在班里的表现非常突出。**
Tā zài bān lǐ de biǎoxiàn fēicháng tūchū.
Her performance in class is very outstanding.

**这个设计的突出特点是简洁。**
Zhège shèjì de tūchū tèdiǎn shì jiǎnjié.
The outstanding feature of this design is its simplicity.

**报告应该突出重点，不要太罗嗦。**
Bàogào yīnggāi tūchū zhòngdiǎn, bùyào tài luōsuo.
The report should highlight the key points and not be too wordy.

**山顶上有一块突出的大岩石。**
Shāndǐng shàng yǒu yī kuài tūchū de dà yánshí.
There is a large protruding rock at the top of the mountain.

## Usage Notes

As an adjective, 突出 praises someone or something for standing above the rest. As a verb, it means to bring something forward or emphasize it deliberately, as in 突出重点 (highlight key points). Do not confuse with 突然 (tūrán, suddenly).

## Memory Hook

突 shows a dog (犬) bursting out of a hole (穴) — something that suddenly pops out is literally sticking out and standing apart.
