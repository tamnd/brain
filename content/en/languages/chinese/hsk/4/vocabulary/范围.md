## 范围 (fànwéi)

范围 means "range," "scope," or "extent" and is used to describe the boundaries or limits within which something operates or applies.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** range; scope; extent; boundary

## Example Sentences

**这次考试的范围很广。** Zhè cì kǎoshì de fànwéi hěn guǎng. The scope of this exam is very wide.

**请在规定范围内完成任务。** Qǐng zài guīdìng fànwéi nèi wánchéng rènwù. Please complete the task within the specified scope.

**这个问题超出了我们的工作范围。** Zhège wèntí chāochū le wǒmen de gōngzuò fànwéi. This issue is beyond the scope of our work.

**调查的范围需要进一步扩大。** Diàochá de fànwéi xūyào jìnyībù kuòdà. The scope of the investigation needs to be further expanded.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 工作范围 | scope of work |
| 研究范围 | research scope |
| 在范围内 | within the scope |
| 超出范围 | to exceed the scope |

## Usage Notes

范围 sets limits or defines boundaries, and is widely used in academic, professional, and everyday contexts. It often pairs with verbs like 扩大 (to expand), 缩小 (to narrow), 超出 (to exceed), or 在...内 (within). It is a key word for describing the limits of a study, project, or responsibility.

## Memory Hook

范 (fàn) means "model or pattern" and 围 (wéi) means "to surround or encircle": the 范围 is the circle that defines the model boundary.
