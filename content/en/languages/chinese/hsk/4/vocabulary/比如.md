## 比如 (bǐrú)

比如 is used to introduce an example or illustration of a general statement.

## Meanings

1. **[conjunction]** for example; for instance; such as

## Example Sentences

**很多水果对身体好，比如苹果和橙子。** Hěn duō shuǐguǒ duì shēntǐ hǎo, bǐrú píngguǒ hé chéngzi. Many fruits are good for your health, for example apples and oranges.

**你可以用多种方式学习，比如看书或者看视频。** Nǐ kěyǐ yòng duō zhǒng fāngshì xuéxí, bǐrú kànshū huòzhě kàn shìpín. You can learn in many ways, for example by reading books or watching videos.

**比如说，如果天气不好，我们就改变计划。** Bǐrú shuō, rúguǒ tiānqì bù hǎo, wǒmen jiù gǎibiàn jìhuà. For example, if the weather is bad, we will change the plan.

**有些工作需要特别的技能，比如编程。** Yǒuxiē gōngzuò xūyào tèbié de jìnéng, bǐrú biānchéng. Some jobs require special skills, such as programming.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 比如说 (bǐrú shuō) | for example (spoken) |
| 就比如 (jiù bǐrú) | just like; take for example |
| 再比如 (zài bǐrú) | another example being |
| 比如以下 (bǐrú yǐxià) | for example, the following |

## Usage Notes

比如 and 例如 (lìrú) both mean "for example," but 比如 is more conversational while 例如 is more formal and often appears in written Chinese. 比如说 adds 说 to make it sound more natural in speech. It typically appears mid-sentence after making a general claim.

## Memory Hook

比 (compare) + 如 (like) — to illustrate a point, you compare it to something like a concrete example.
