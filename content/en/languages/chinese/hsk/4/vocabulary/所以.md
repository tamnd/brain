## 所以 (suǒyǐ)

所以 is a conjunction meaning "therefore," "so," or "as a result" -- it introduces a conclusion or consequence based on a reason given earlier.

## Meanings

1. **[conjunction]** therefore; so; as a result; that is why

## Example Sentences

**他生病了，所以没来上班。** Tā shēngbìng le, suǒyǐ méi lái shàngbān. He was sick, so he didn't come to work.

**因为下雨，所以我们取消了比赛。** Yīnwèi xià yǔ, suǒyǐ wǒmen qǔxiāole bǐsài. Because it was raining, we cancelled the match.

**我很努力学习，所以考试考得很好。** Wǒ hěn nǔlì xuéxí, suǒyǐ kǎoshì kǎo de hěn hǎo. I studied hard, so I did well on the exam.

**他不喜欢吃辣，所以点了清淡的菜。** Tā bù xǐhuān chī là, suǒyǐ diǎnle qīngdàn de cài. He doesn't like spicy food, so he ordered mild dishes.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 因为...所以... | because... therefore... |
| 所以说 | therefore (emphatic) |
| 正因为...所以... | precisely because... so... |
| 这就是所以 | that is why |

## Usage Notes

所以 most commonly pairs with 因为 (yīnwèi = because) in the pattern 因为...所以... to form a cause-and-effect sentence. However, 所以 can also appear alone when the cause is already understood from context. In English, "so" at the start of a sentence is informal, but 所以 is neutral and appropriate in both spoken and written Chinese.

## Memory Hook

SUO (所 = place/that which) + YI (以 = by means of): "by means of what came before" -- therefore, so.
