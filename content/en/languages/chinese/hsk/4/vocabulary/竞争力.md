## 竞争力 (jìngzhēnglì)

竞争力 refers to competitiveness, the ability or strength to compete effectively in a market, workplace, or other competitive environment.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** competitiveness; competitive strength; the capacity to compete effectively

## Example Sentences

**提高产品质量是增强竞争力的关键。** Tígāo chǎnpǐn zhìliàng shì zēngqiáng jìngzhēnglì de guānjiàn. Improving product quality is the key to enhancing competitiveness.

**他不断学习新技能，以保持竞争力。** Tā bùduàn xuéxí xīn jìnéng, yǐ bǎochí jìngzhēnglì. He continually learns new skills in order to maintain his competitiveness.

**这家公司的国际竞争力越来越强。** Zhè jiā gōngsī de guójì jìngzhēnglì yuè lái yuè qiáng. This company's international competitiveness is growing stronger.

**教育水平的提高有助于增强国家的整体竞争力。** Jiàoyù shuǐpíng de tígāo yǒu zhù yú zēngqiáng guójiā de zhěngtǐ jìngzhēnglì. Improving educational levels helps enhance the country's overall competitiveness.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 增强竞争力 | to enhance competitiveness |
| 核心竞争力 | core competitiveness |
| 国际竞争力 | international competitiveness |
| 失去竞争力 | to lose competitiveness |

## Usage Notes

竞争力 is built from 竞争 (competition) plus 力 (power/strength). It is used in business, economics, and personal development contexts. The phrase 核心竞争力 (core competitiveness or core competency) is a key term in business strategy.

## Memory Hook

竞争 means "competition" and 力 means "power/strength": 竞争力 is the power to compete, your competitive muscle.
