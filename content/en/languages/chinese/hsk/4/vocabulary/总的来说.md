## 总的来说 (zǒng de lái shuō)

总的来说 is a set phrase used to introduce a general conclusion or overall assessment, equivalent to "generally speaking," "overall," or "on the whole."

## Meanings

1. **[phrase]** generally speaking; on the whole; overall

## Example Sentences

**总的来说，这次旅行很愉快。** Zǒng de lái shuō, zhè cì lǚxíng hěn yúkuài. Generally speaking, this trip was very enjoyable.

**总的来说，他的表现还不错。** Zǒng de lái shuō, tā de biǎoxiàn hái bùcuò. Overall, his performance was not bad.

**总的来说，这个方案是可行的。** Zǒng de lái shuō, zhège fāng'àn shì kěxíng de. On the whole, this plan is feasible.

**总的来说，学中文需要时间和耐心。** Zǒng de lái shuō, xué Zhōngwén xūyào shíjiān hé nàixīn. Generally speaking, learning Chinese requires time and patience.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 总的来说还好 | overall it is fine |
| 总的来说问题不大 | overall not a big problem |
| 总的来说还不错 | on the whole not bad |
| 总的来说可以 | generally speaking acceptable |

## Usage Notes

总的来说 is slightly softer and more analytical than 总之 (zǒngzhī). While both introduce summaries, 总的来说 frames a balanced overall view, often used in evaluations, reviews, or reports. It is a fixed phrase and is always used at the start of a sentence or clause.

## Memory Hook

ZONG (总 = total/overall) + DE LAI SHUO (的来说 = speaking from): "speaking from a total view" -- that is the overall picture.
