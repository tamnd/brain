## 战略 (zhànlüè)

战略 means strategy in a broad, high-level sense -- a long-term plan or approach to achieving a major goal, originally military but now widely used in business and politics.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** strategy; strategic plan
2. **[adjective]** strategic

## Example Sentences

**公司制定了新的发展战略。** Gōngsī zhìdìngle xīn de fāzhǎn zhànlüè. The company formulated a new development strategy.

**这是一个重要的战略决定。** Zhè shì yīgè zhòngyào de zhànlüè juédìng. This is an important strategic decision.

**他们的战略非常成功。** Tāmen de zhànlüè fēicháng chénggōng. Their strategy was very successful.

**国家需要长期的经济战略。** Guójiā xūyào chángqī de jīngjì zhànlüè. The country needs a long-term economic strategy.

## Usage Notes

战略 implies large-scale, long-term planning, in contrast to 战术 (zhànshù = tactics), which refers to short-term methods. In modern Chinese, 战略 is used extensively in business, politics, and international relations. The adjective use (战略性 or simply 战略+noun) is common in formal texts.

## Memory Hook

ZHAN (战 = battle/war) + LUE (略 = plan/brief): the big battle plan -- that is strategy.
