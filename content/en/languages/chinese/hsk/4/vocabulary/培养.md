## 培养 (péiyǎng)

培养 means to cultivate, train, or develop a person or quality over time through deliberate effort and care.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to cultivate or nurture (talent, ability, habits)
2. **[verb]** to train or develop (a person, skill, or interest)

## Example Sentences

**学校要培养学生的创造力。** Xuéxiào yào péiyǎng xuéshēng de chuàngzàolì. The school aims to cultivate students' creativity.

**父母应该培养孩子的好习惯。** Fùmǔ yīnggāi péiyǎng háizi de hǎo xíguàn. Parents should develop good habits in their children.

**这家公司花了很多时间培养新员工。** Zhè jiā gōngsī huāle hěn duō shíjiān péiyǎng xīn yuángōng. This company spends a lot of time training new employees.

**他从小培养了对音乐的兴趣。** Tā cóng xiǎo péiyǎngle duì yīnyuè de xìngqù. He developed an interest in music from a young age.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 培养人才 | to cultivate talent |
| 培养习惯 | to develop habits |
| 培养兴趣 | to foster interest |
| 培养能力 | to develop ability |

## Usage Notes

培养 implies a gradual, intentional process of nurturing over time. It is often used in educational or professional contexts when talking about developing skills, talent, or good qualities. It carries a positive connotation and suggests patience and investment.

## Memory Hook

Think of 培 (to cultivate soil) and 养 (to raise/nourish): you prepare the ground and nourish the plant until it grows strong, just like training a person.
