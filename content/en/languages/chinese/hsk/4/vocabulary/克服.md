## 克服 (kèfú)

克服 means to overcome or conquer a difficulty, fear, or obstacle through effort and determination.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to overcome, conquer, or surmount a challenge, difficulty, or obstacle

## Example Sentences

**他克服了对公开演讲的恐惧。** Tā kèfú le duì gōngkāi yǎnjiǎng de kǒngjù. He overcame his fear of public speaking.

**成功需要克服许多困难和挑战。** Chénggōng xūyào kèfú xǔduō kùnnán hé tiǎozhàn. Success requires overcoming many difficulties and challenges.

**她凭着坚强的意志克服了重病。** Tā píng zhe jiānqiáng de yìzhì kèfú le zhòngbìng. She overcame a serious illness through sheer determination.

**我们必须克服语言障碍才能更好地沟通。** Wǒmen bìxū kèfú yǔyán zhàng'ài cái néng gèng hǎo de gōutōng. We must overcome the language barrier to communicate better.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 克服困难 | overcome difficulties |
| 克服障碍 | overcome obstacles |
| 克服恐惧 | overcome fear |
| 努力克服 | strive to overcome |

## Usage Notes

克服 always takes an object that represents a challenge, fear, or difficulty. It cannot be used without one. Common objects include 困难 (difficulties), 恐惧 (fear), 障碍 (obstacles), and 缺点 (shortcomings). The word carries a sense of triumph through personal effort.

## Memory Hook

克 means "to conquer" and 服 means "to submit," so 克服 is to conquer something until it submits, to overcome it completely.
