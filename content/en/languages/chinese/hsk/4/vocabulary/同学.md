## 同学 (tóngxué)

同学 refers to someone who studies or studied at the same school or in the same class as you.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** classmate; schoolmate; fellow student

## Example Sentences

**我和我的同学一起复习功课。** Wǒ hé wǒ de tóngxué yīqǐ fùxí gōngkè. My classmates and I review our lessons together.

**她是我大学时最好的同学。** Tā shì wǒ dàxué shí zuì hǎo de tóngxué. She was my best classmate during university.

**同学们，请翻开教材第三十页。** Tóngxuémen, qǐng fān kāi jiàocái dìsānshí yè. Classmates, please turn to page thirty of your textbook.

**我和老同学叙旧，聊了很久。** Wǒ hé lǎo tóngxué xùjiù, liáole hěn jiǔ. I caught up with an old classmate and chatted for a long time.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 老同学 | old classmate (from the past) |
| 同学聚会 | class reunion |
| 同学关系 | relationship with classmates |
| 大学同学 | university classmate |

## Usage Notes

同学 is also used as a polite form of address when speaking to students, similar to saying "student" or "young person" in English. Teachers often address their class as 同学们 (classmates/students). It is distinct from 同事 (colleague) which refers to coworkers.

## Memory Hook

同 means "same" and 学 means "study/learn" -- 同学 are people who study the same things at the same place, i.e., classmates.
