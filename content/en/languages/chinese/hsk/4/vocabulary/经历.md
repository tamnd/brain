## 经历 (jīnglì)

经历 means both the lived experiences one accumulates and the act of going through an event or period, functioning as a noun and a verb.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Experience; personal history (events one has gone through).
2. **[verb]** To experience; to go through; to live through.

## Example Sentences

**这次旅行是我一生中最难忘的经历。**
Zhè cì lǚxíng shì wǒ yīshēng zhōng zuì nánwàng de jīnglì.
This trip is the most unforgettable experience of my life.

**他经历了很多困难，最终成功了。**
Tā jīnglì le hěn duō kùnnan, zuìzhōng chénggōng le.
He went through many difficulties and eventually succeeded.

**丰富的工作经历对找工作很有帮助。**
Fēngfù de gōngzuò jīnglì duì zhǎo gōngzuò hěn yǒu bāngzhù.
Rich work experience is very helpful for finding a job.

**她经历了失去家人的痛苦，但还是坚强地生活着。**
Tā jīnglì le shīqù jiārén de tòngkǔ, dànshì háishi jiānqiáng de shēnghuó zhe.
She experienced the pain of losing a family member but continued to live with strength.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 工作经历 | work experience |
| 丰富的经历 | rich experience |
| 亲身经历 | personal firsthand experience |
| 经历困难 | to go through difficulties |
| 难忘的经历 | an unforgettable experience |

## Usage Notes

经历 emphasizes personally living through something, often over a period of time. It differs from 经验 (jīngyàn), which refers to accumulated knowledge or skill gained from experience. 经历 is more about events; 经验 is more about know-how.

## Memory Hook

经 means "to pass through" and 历 means "to go through/calendar of events"; 经历 is your personal timeline of everything you have passed through.
