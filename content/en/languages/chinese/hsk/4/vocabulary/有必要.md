## 有必要 (yǒu bìyào)

有必要 expresses that something is necessary or that there is a need to do something.

## Meanings

1. **[phrase]** it is necessary; there is a need (to do something)

## Example Sentences

**有必要提前准备。** Yǒu bìyào tíqián zhǔnbèi. It is necessary to prepare in advance.

**我觉得有必要解释一下。** Wǒ juéde yǒu bìyào jiěshì yīxià. I think it is necessary to give an explanation.

**有没有必要开这个会议？** Yǒu méiyǒu bìyào kāi zhège huìyì? Is it necessary to hold this meeting?

**你有必要了解这些规则。** Nǐ yǒu bìyào liǎojiě zhèxiē guīzé. It is necessary for you to understand these rules.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 有必要做 (yǒu bìyào zuò) | it is necessary to do |
| 有没有必要 (yǒu méiyǒu bìyào) | is it necessary or not |
| 有必要说明 (yǒu bìyào shuōmíng) | it is necessary to explain |
| 有必要考虑 (yǒu bìyào kǎolǜ) | it is necessary to consider |

## Usage Notes

有必要 is typically followed by a verb phrase. It can be negated as 没有必要 or shortened to 没必要 to mean "there is no need." In formal contexts it often appears at the beginning of a sentence to frame an argument or recommendation.

## Memory Hook

有 (have) + 必要 (necessity) — if you "have necessity," then the action must happen.
