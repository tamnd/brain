## 进步 (jìnbù)

进步 means to make progress or improve, and as a noun refers to progress or advancement.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to make progress; to improve; to advance
2. **[noun]** progress; improvement; advancement

## Example Sentences

**你的中文进步很快！** Nǐ de Zhōngwén jìnbù hěn kuài! Your Chinese is improving very quickly!

**只要坚持努力，就一定会有进步。** Zhǐyào jiānchí nǔlì, jiù yīdìng huì yǒu jìnbù. As long as you keep working hard, you will definitely make progress.

**科技的进步改变了我们的生活。** Kējì de jìnbù gǎibiàn le wǒmen de shēnghuó. Advances in technology have changed our lives.

**她在这次考试中取得了很大的进步。** Tā zài zhè cì kǎoshì zhōng qǔdé le hěn dà de jìnbù. She made great progress in this exam.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 取得进步 (qǔdé jìnbù) | to achieve progress |
| 不断进步 (bùduàn jìnbù) | to continuously improve |
| 技术进步 (jìshù jìnbù) | technological progress |
| 进步很快 (jìnbù hěn kuài) | to improve quickly |

## Usage Notes

进步 is both a verb and a noun, and its usage is very flexible. It is frequently used in encouragement and feedback contexts. 你进步了 (you have improved) is a common compliment. It differs from 发展 (fāzhǎn), which implies broader development or growth at a system level, while 进步 is more personal or specific.

## Memory Hook

进 (to advance/move forward) + 步 (step) — progress is made one step forward at a time.
