## 帮助 (bāngzhù)

帮助 means to help or assist someone, and can also function as a noun meaning help or assistance.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to help; to assist
2. **[noun]** help; assistance

## Example Sentences

**谢谢你帮助我。** Xièxiè nǐ bāngzhù wǒ. Thank you for helping me.

**他的帮助对我很重要。** Tā de bāngzhù duì wǒ hěn zhòngyào. His help is very important to me.

**我需要你的帮助。** Wǒ xūyào nǐ de bāngzhù. I need your help.

**她帮助了很多人找到工作。** Tā bāngzhùle hěn duō rén zhǎodào gōngzuò. She helped many people find jobs.

## Collocations

## Usage Notes

帮助 is more formal and complete than the shorter 帮 (bāng), which is more colloquial. In spoken Chinese, 帮 alone is common, while 帮助 appears frequently in writing, speeches, and polite contexts. As a noun, 帮助 is uncountable and does not take measure words.

## Memory Hook

BANG (帮) sounds like "bang on the door" calling for help, and ZHU (助) means assist -- bang on the door to assist someone.
