## 考虑 (kǎolǜ)

考虑 means "to consider" or "to think over" and is used when someone is carefully weighing options or thinking about a situation before making a decision.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to consider; to think about carefully
2. **[verb]** to take into account; to bear in mind

## Example Sentences

**你考虑清楚了再做决定。** Nǐ kǎolǜ qīngchǔ le zài zuò juédìng. Think it through carefully before making a decision.

**我们需要考虑所有可能性。** Wǒmen xūyào kǎolǜ suǒyǒu kěnéngxìng. We need to consider all possibilities.

**他正在考虑换工作。** Tā zhèngzài kǎolǜ huàn gōngzuò. He is considering changing jobs.

**请考虑一下我们的建议。** Qǐng kǎolǜ yīxià wǒmen de jiànyì. Please consider our suggestion.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 认真考虑 | to consider carefully |
| 充分考虑 | to fully consider |
| 考虑到 | to take into account |
| 重新考虑 | to reconsider |

## Usage Notes

考虑 implies a deliberate and thoughtful process. It is often followed by 一下 to soften a request or suggestion. The phrase 考虑到 (kǎolǜ dào) means "taking into account" and is very common in formal speech and writing.

## Memory Hook

考 (kǎo) is related to "examining" (as in exams), and 虑 (lǜ) means "to worry or ponder": together they describe the careful mental examination of a problem.
