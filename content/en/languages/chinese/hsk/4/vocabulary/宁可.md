## 宁可 (nìngkě)

宁可 expresses a preference for one choice even if it involves some cost or sacrifice, equivalent to "would rather" or "prefer to" in English.

## Meanings

1. **[adverb]** Would rather; prefer to (chooses one option, often the harder one, over another).

## Example Sentences

**我宁可走路，也不想坐那辆车。**
Wǒ nìngkě zǒulù, yě bù xiǎng zuò nà liàng chē.
I would rather walk than ride in that car.

**她宁可少睡觉，也要完成作业。**
Tā nìngkě shǎo shuìjiào, yě yào wánchéng zuòyè.
She would rather sleep less than leave her homework unfinished.

**他宁可一个人住，也不愿意搬回家。**
Tā nìngkě yī gè rén zhù, yě bù yuànyì bān huí jiā.
He would rather live alone than move back home.

**宁可相信它是真的，也不要冒险。**
Nìngkě xiāngxìn tā shì zhēn de, yě bùyào màoxiǎn.
It is better to believe it is true than to take the risk.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 宁可…也不… | would rather ... than not ... |
| 宁可…也要… | would rather ... in order to ... |
| 宁可如此 | better to be like this |
| 宁可少 | would rather have less |

## Usage Notes

宁可 typically pairs with 也不 or 也要 in the second clause to complete the contrast. It emphasizes a willing sacrifice or trade-off. 宁愿 is a near-synonym; both can be used in this pattern, though 宁可 is slightly more formal.

## Memory Hook

宁 suggests peace and contentment, 可 means "acceptable"; 宁可 says "I find peace in choosing this, even if it costs me."
