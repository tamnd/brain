## 员工 (yuángōng)

员工 refers to an employee or staff member of an organization, company, or institution.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** employee, staff member, or worker at an organization

## Example Sentences

**公司有五百名员工。** Gōngsī yǒu wǔbǎi míng yuángōng. The company has five hundred employees.

**管理者应该尊重每一位员工。** Guǎnlǐzhě yīnggāi zūnzhòng měi yī wèi yuángōng. Managers should respect every employee.

**公司为员工提供了很好的福利待遇。** Gōngsī wèi yuángōng tígōng le hěn hǎo de fúlì dàiyù. The company provides employees with excellent benefits.

**员工满意度对公司的发展至关重要。** Yuángōng mǎnyì dù duì gōngsī de fāzhǎn zhìguān zhòngyào. Employee satisfaction is crucial for the company's development.

## Usage Notes

员工 is the standard neutral term for "employee" in a corporate or organizational setting. Compare with 工人 (gōngrén, factory worker or laborer), which implies manual or blue-collar work. 员工 is appropriate for any level of staff including white-collar workers, managers, and professionals.

## Memory Hook

员 means "member" (as in a member of a group) and 工 means "work," so an 员工 is a "working member," an employee.
