## 场所 (chǎngsuǒ)

场所 refers to a specific place, venue, or premises designated for a particular activity or purpose.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** a place, venue, or premises for a specific activity or purpose

## Example Sentences

**图书馆是学习的好场所。** Túshūguǎn shì xuéxí de hǎo chǎngsuǒ. The library is a good place for studying.

**这些公共场所必须保持整洁。** Zhèxiē gōnggòng chǎngsuǒ bìxū bǎochí zhěngjié. These public places must be kept clean and tidy.

**会议在一个专业的场所举行。** Huìyì zài yīgè zhuānyè de chǎngsuǒ jǔxíng. The meeting was held at a professional venue.

**禁止在公共场所大声喧哗。** Jìnzhǐ zài gōnggòng chǎngsuǒ dàshēng xuānhuá. Making loud noise in public places is prohibited.

## Usage Notes

场所 emphasizes the purposeful nature of a space rather than just its physical location. It is often used in official notices, regulations, and formal writing. 公共场所 (public places) is a very common phrase in Chinese public signage and social norms discussions. Compare with 地方 (dìfāng), which is a more casual word for "place."

## Memory Hook

场 means "field or venue" and 所 means "place or location," so 场所 is a venue-place, a specific space designated for something.
