## 心理健康 (xīnlǐ jiànkāng)

心理健康 refers to mental and emotional wellbeing. As awareness of mental health grows in Chinese society, this term has become increasingly important in both everyday and formal contexts.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** mental health; psychological wellbeing; emotional health

## Example Sentences

**我们要同样重视身体健康和心理健康。** Wǒmen yào tóngyàng zhòngshì shēntǐ jiànkāng hé xīnlǐ jiànkāng. We should pay equal attention to physical health and mental health.

**长期的压力会影响一个人的心理健康。** Chángqī de yālì huì yǐngxiǎng yī gè rén de xīnlǐ jiànkāng. Long-term stress can affect a person's mental health.

**学校开始为学生提供心理健康方面的支持。** Xuéxiào kāishǐ wèi xuéshēng tígōng xīnlǐ jiànkāng fāngmiàn de zhīchí. The school has started providing mental health support for students.

**保持良好的心理健康需要定期休息和社交。** Bǎochí liánghǎo de xīnlǐ jiànkāng xūyào dìngqī xiūxi hé shèjiāo. Maintaining good mental health requires regular rest and social interaction.

## Usage Notes

心理健康 is a compound noun: 心理 (psychology/mindset) + 健康 (health). While 心理 alone means "psychology" or "mental/psychological," 健康 means "health" or "healthy." Together they form "mental health." This term is increasingly used in Chinese media and education as awareness of mental wellbeing grows. The opposite concept is 心理问题 (psychological problems) or 心理障碍 (psychological disorder).

## Memory Hook

心理 (mind/psychology) + 健康 (health) — mental health is simply the "health of your mind."
