## 声明 (shēngmíng)

声明 refers to a formal declaration or statement, used both as a verb (to declare) and a noun (a declaration or announcement).

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to declare; to state formally; to announce
2. **[noun]** a declaration; a formal statement

## Example Sentences

**政府发表了一份正式声明。** Zhèngfǔ fābiǎole yī fèn zhèngshì shēngmíng. The government issued a formal declaration.

**他声明自己对这件事一无所知。** Tā shēngmíng zìjǐ duì zhè jiàn shì yī wú suǒ zhī. He declared that he knew nothing about this matter.

**公司发表声明，否认了相关报道。** Gōngsī fābiǎo shēngmíng, fǒurènle xiāngguān bàodào. The company issued a statement denying the related reports.

**她在会议上声明支持这项政策。** Tā zài huìyì shàng shēngmíng zhīchí zhè xiàng zhèngcè. She declared her support for this policy at the meeting.

## Collocations

## Usage Notes

声明 is more formal than 说 (to say) and is typically used in official, political, or legal contexts. As a noun it often appears with 发表 (to publish/issue). At B1 level, it is common in news articles and formal writing.

## Memory Hook

声 means "sound/voice" and 明 means "clear/bright," so 声明 is making your voice clearly heard: a clear public declaration.
