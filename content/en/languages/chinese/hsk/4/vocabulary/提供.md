## 提供 (tígōng)

提供 means to provide, supply, or offer something to someone. It emphasizes the act of making a resource, service, or information available to someone who needs it. It is used in formal and informal contexts alike.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To provide; to supply; to offer; to furnish.

## Example Sentences

**公司为员工提供免费的健康体检服务。**
Gōngsī wèi yuángōng tígōng miǎnfèi de jiànkāng tǐjiǎn fúwù.
The company provides free health check-up services for its employees.

**这所大学为留学生提供各种奖学金机会。**
Zhè suǒ dàxué wèi liúxuéshēng tígōng gèzhǒng jiǎngxuéjīn jīhuì.
This university provides various scholarship opportunities for international students.

**请提供相关证明文件，以便我们尽快处理您的申请。**
Qǐng tígōng xiāngguān zhèngmíng wénjiàn, yǐbiàn wǒmen jǐnkuài chǔlǐ nín de shēnqǐng.
Please provide the relevant supporting documents so that we can process your application as soon as possible.

**这个平台提供了大量的学习资源，帮助用户提高语言能力。**
Zhège píngtái tígōng le dàliàng de xuéxí zīyuán, bāngzhù yònghù tígāo yǔyán nénglì.
This platform provides a large number of learning resources to help users improve their language skills.

## Usage Notes

提供 always takes a beneficiary: 为 X 提供 Y (to provide Y for X) or 向 X 提供 Y (to provide Y to X). It is more formal than 给 (gěi, to give), which is used in everyday speech. 提供 emphasizes the function of making something available for use, so it pairs naturally with services, information, funding, and resources rather than physical objects passed directly from person to person.

## Memory Hook

提 means "to carry/lift up" and 供 means "to supply/offer." To 提供 is to lift up and hold out something for someone to take — a generous gesture of handing over what is needed.
