## 变化 (biànhuà)

变化 refers to any change or transformation in state, condition, or situation.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** change; transformation; variation
2. **[verb]** to change; to vary; to transform

## Example Sentences

**这座城市十年间发生了巨大变化。** Zhè zuò chéngshì shí nián jiān fāshēngle jùdà biànhuà. This city has undergone enormous changes over ten years.

**天气变化很快，出门要带伞。** Tiānqì biànhuà hěn kuài, chūmén yào dài sǎn. The weather changes quickly; bring an umbrella when you go out.

**科技的发展给生活带来了很多变化。** Kējì de fāzhǎn gěi shēnghuó dàiláile hěn duō biànhuà. The development of technology has brought many changes to life.

**她的态度有了很大变化，变得更加开朗了。** Tā de tàidu yǒule hěn dà biànhuà, biàn de gèngjiā kāilǎng le. Her attitude has changed greatly; she has become much more cheerful.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 发生变化 | to undergo a change |
| 巨大变化 | enormous change |
| 变化多端 | constantly changing |
| 气候变化 | climate change |

## Usage Notes

变化 is very commonly used in spoken and written Chinese. As a noun, it often appears in the structure 发生变化 (a change occurs). As a verb, it describes how something shifts over time. The phrase 变化多端 is an idiom meaning "constantly and unpredictably changing."

## Memory Hook

变 means "to change" and 化 means "to transform" -- 变化 combines both ideas, describing change and transformation together.
