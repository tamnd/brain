## 完整 (wánzhěng)

完整 means complete or intact, indicating that all necessary parts are present and nothing is missing, broken, or fragmented. It is used for information, documents, systems, structures, and arguments to indicate they are whole and undamaged.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** Complete; intact; whole; undamaged; comprehensive.

## Example Sentences

**请提交完整的申请材料，缺少任何文件都会影响审核进度。**
Qǐng tíjiāo wánzhěng de shēnqǐng cáiliào, quēshǎo rènhé wénjiàn dōu huì yǐngxiǎng shěnhé jìndù.
Please submit complete application materials; missing any document will affect the review progress.

**这套古代瓷器保存完整，是极为珍贵的文化遗产。**
Zhè tào gǔdài cíqì bǎocún wánzhěng, shì jí wéi zhēnguì de wénhuà yíchǎn.
This set of ancient porcelain is preserved intact and is an extremely precious cultural heritage.

**要理解一段历史，必须掌握完整的背景信息。**
Yào lǐjiě yī duàn lìshǐ, bìxū zhǎngwò wánzhěng de bèijǐng xìnxī.
To understand a period of history, you must have a complete background of information.

**他的论文结构完整，论点清晰，得到了评委的高度评价。**
Tā de lùnwén jiégòu wánzhěng, lùndiǎn qīngxī, dédào le pínwěi de gāodù píngjià.
His paper has a complete structure and clear arguments, and received high praise from the judges.

## Usage Notes

完整 emphasizes that nothing is missing from a whole, while 完全 (wánquán, completely/entirely) is an adverb emphasizing degree. Compare: 资料完整 (the data is complete, nothing is missing) vs. 完全正确 (completely correct). 完整性 (wánzhěng xìng) means "completeness" or "integrity" and is used in data science, law, and archival work.

## Memory Hook

完 means "to finish/complete" and 整 means "orderly/whole." Something 完整 is fully finished and in perfect order, with every piece in its rightful place.
