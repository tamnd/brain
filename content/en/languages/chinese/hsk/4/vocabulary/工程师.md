## 工程师 (gōngchéngshī)

工程师 refers to a professional engineer -- someone trained to design, build, or maintain systems and structures.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** engineer (professional title)

## Example Sentences

**他是一名软件工程师。** Tā shì yī míng ruǎnjiàn gōngchéngshī. He is a software engineer.

**工程师们正在设计新桥梁。** Gōngchéngshīmen zhèngzài shèjì xīn qiáoliáng. The engineers are designing a new bridge.

**我想成为一名工程师。** Wǒ xiǎng chéngwéi yī míng gōngchéngshī. I want to become an engineer.

**这位工程师解决了很多难题。** Zhè wèi gōngchéngshī jiějuéle hěn duō nántí. This engineer solved many difficult problems.

## Usage Notes

工程师 is a professional title and is typically preceded by a classifier 一名 or a modifier like 软件 (software) or 机械 (mechanical). It can also be used as a form of address or job title on business cards and resumes.

## Memory Hook

GONG (工 = work) + CHENG (程 = procedure/program) + SHI (师 = master/teacher): a master of work procedures -- that is an engineer.
