## 维护 (wéihù)

维护 means "to maintain," "to uphold," or "to defend" and is commonly used to describe protecting rights, interests, or relationships.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to maintain; to keep in good condition
2. **[verb]** to uphold; to defend; to protect (rights, interests, order)

## Example Sentences

**我们要维护社会的稳定。** Wǒmen yào wéihù shèhuì de wěndìng. We must maintain social stability.

**他努力维护自己的权利。** Tā nǔlì wéihù zìjǐ de quánlì. He works hard to defend his own rights.

**这台机器需要定期维护。** Zhè tái jīqì xūyào dìngqī wéihù. This machine needs regular maintenance.

**维护良好的人际关系非常重要。** Wéihù liánghǎo de rénjì guānxi fēicháng zhòngyào. Maintaining good interpersonal relationships is very important.

## Usage Notes

维护 can refer to both physical maintenance (of machines, systems) and abstract protection (of rights, dignity, peace). It carries a sense of active effort and responsibility. In formal contexts it often appears in phrases about upholding laws, social order, or national interests.

## Memory Hook

维 (wéi) means to hold together, and 护 (hù) means to protect: put them together and you are "holding things together by protecting them."
