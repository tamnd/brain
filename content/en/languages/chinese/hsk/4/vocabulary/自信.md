## 自信 (zìxìn)

自信 means having confidence in oneself, or the quality of self-confidence.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** confident; self-confident
2. **[noun]** self-confidence; confidence

## Example Sentences

**她在舞台上表现得非常自信。** Tā zài wǔtái shàng biǎoxiàn de fēicháng zìxìn. She performed very confidently on stage.

**你要对自己有自信。** Nǐ yào duì zìjǐ yǒu zìxìn. You need to have confidence in yourself.

**成功能给人带来自信。** Chénggōng néng gěi rén dài lái zìxìn. Success can give people confidence.

**他缺乏自信，不敢开口说话。** Tā quēfá zìxìn, bù gǎn kāikǒu shuōhuà. He lacks confidence and does not dare to speak up.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 有自信 (yǒu zìxìn) | to be confident; to have confidence |
| 充满自信 (chōngmǎn zìxìn) | full of confidence |
| 缺乏自信 (quēfá zìxìn) | to lack confidence |
| 自信心 (zìxìnxīn) | sense of self-confidence |

## Usage Notes

自信 can be used predicatively (他很自信) or as a noun (他有自信). The compound 自信心 emphasizes the inner sense of confidence. Be aware that 骄傲 (jiāo'ào) means "arrogant" or "proud," which is different from 自信. True 自信 is seen as a positive trait, while excessive pride crosses into 骄傲.

## Memory Hook

自 (self) + 信 (to believe/trust) — to be confident is simply to believe in yourself.
