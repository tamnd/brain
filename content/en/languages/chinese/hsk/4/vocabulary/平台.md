## 平台 (píngtái)

平台 refers to a physical raised flat surface or, more commonly in modern usage, a digital or organizational platform that supports activity.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Platform; stage; terrace (physical).
2. **[noun]** Platform; base (for online services, business, or opportunities).

## Example Sentences

**这个电商平台拥有数百万用户。**
Zhège diànshāng píngtái yōngyǒu shù bǎi wàn yònghù.
This e-commerce platform has millions of users.

**公司为员工提供了一个展示才华的平台。**
Gōngsī wèi yuángōng tígōngle yīgè zhǎnshì cáihuá de píngtái.
The company provided employees with a platform to showcase their talents.

**这个学习平台上有很多免费课程。**
Zhège xuéxí píngtái shàng yǒu hěn duō miǎnfèi kèchéng.
There are many free courses on this learning platform.

**火车站的站台就是一个平台。**
Huǒchēzhàn de zhàntái jiù shì yīgè píngtái.
The platform at the train station is a type of platform.

## Usage Notes

平台 has expanded greatly in the digital age to mean any online platform or service ecosystem. The physical sense (a raised flat area) is less common in everyday speech. It is now extremely frequent in business and tech contexts.

## Memory Hook

平 means "flat/level" and 台 means "raised stage/terrace" — a flat raised stage is a platform where things can be displayed or built upon.
