## 统计 (tǒngjì)

统计 refers to statistics as a body of data, and also means to compile or tally data systematically.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** statistics, statistical data
2. **[verb]** to compile statistics, to count up, to tally

## Example Sentences

**根据最新统计，这个城市有一千万人口。** Gēnjù zuìxīn tǒngjì, zhège chéngshì yǒu yīqiān wàn rénkǒu. According to the latest statistics, this city has a population of ten million.

**我们需要统计一下参加会议的人数。** Wǒmen xūyào tǒngjì yīxià cānjiā huìyì de rénshù. We need to tally up the number of people attending the meeting.

**统计数据显示，越来越多的人选择网上购物。** Tǒngjì shùjù xiǎnshì, yuèlái yuè duō de rén xuǎnzé wǎng shàng gòuwù. Statistical data shows that more and more people are choosing to shop online.

**这项统计工作需要细心和耐心。** Zhè xiàng tǒngjì gōngzuò xūyào xìxīn hé nàixīn. This statistical work requires care and patience.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |

| 统计结果 (tǒngjì jiéguǒ) | statistical results |
| 人口统计 (rénkǒu tǒngjì) | population statistics, demographics |

## Usage Notes

统计 functions as both a noun and a verb. As a noun it often combines with 数据 (shùjù, data) or 结果 (jiéguǒ, results). As a verb it means to systematically count or compile data. In academic or formal writing, 根据统计 ("according to statistics") is a standard phrase for citing quantitative evidence.

## Memory Hook

统 means "to unite/consolidate" and 计 means "to count/plan" — 统计 is "consolidating counts" from many sources into one set of figures.
