## 网站 (wǎngzhàn)

网站 means "website" and refers to a collection of web pages accessible on the internet under a single domain.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** website; web site; online platform

## Example Sentences

**这个网站提供很多免费资料。** Zhège wǎngzhàn tígōng hěn duō miǎnfèi zīliào. This website provides a lot of free materials.

**我们公司有一个官方网站。** Wǒmen gōngsī yǒu yīgè guānfāng wǎngzhàn. Our company has an official website.

**你可以在这个网站上学习中文。** Nǐ kěyǐ zài zhège wǎngzhàn shàng xuéxí Zhōngwén. You can learn Chinese on this website.

**那个购物网站经常有打折活动。** Nàgè gòuwù wǎngzhàn jīngcháng yǒu dǎzhé huódòng. That shopping website often has discount events.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 官方网站 | official website |

| 建立网站 | to build a website |
| 访问网站 | to visit a website |

## Usage Notes

网站 (website) should not be confused with 网页 (wǎngyè, web page), which refers to a single page. 网站 refers to the entire site. In modern usage, 网站 can also refer to mobile apps or online platforms in casual speech.

## Memory Hook

网 (wǎng) means "net" or "internet," and 站 (zhàn) means "station" or "stop": a website is your "station on the internet."
