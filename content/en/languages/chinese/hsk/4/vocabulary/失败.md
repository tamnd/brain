## 失败 (shībài)

失败 describes the outcome of not achieving a goal, or the act of failing. It is used both as a noun meaning "failure" and as a verb meaning "to fail" or "to be defeated."

## Meanings
1. **n** failure, defeat
2. **v** to fail, to be defeated

## Example Sentences
**失败是成功之母。** Shībài shì chénggōng zhī mǔ. Failure is the mother of success.

**他们在比赛中失败了，但没有放弃。** Tāmen zài bǐsài zhōng shībài le, dàn méiyǒu fàngqì. They failed in the competition but did not give up.

**这次实验的失败让科学家们获得了宝贵的经验。** Zhè cì shíyàn de shībài ràng kēxuéjiāmen huòdé le bǎoguì de jīngyàn. The failure of this experiment gave scientists valuable experience.

**我们要从失败中学习，不断成长。** Wǒmen yào cóng shībài zhōng xuéxí, bùduàn chéngzhǎng. We must learn from failure and keep growing.

## Collocations
| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 走向失败 (zǒuxiàng shībài) | to head toward failure |
| 失败的经验 (shībài de jīngyàn) | experience of failure |
| 彻底失败 (chèdǐ shībài) | to fail completely |
| 不怕失败 (bù pà shībài) | not afraid of failure |

## Usage Notes
失败 is the antonym of 成功 (chénggōng, success). When used as a verb it does not take an object; say 他失败了 (he failed) rather than 他失败了比赛. To express defeat in a contest, 输 (shū) is more natural.

## Memory Hook
失 means "to lose" and 败 means "to be beaten" — double loss equals total failure.
