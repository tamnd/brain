## 参考 (cānkǎo)

参考 means to look at or consult something as a source of information or guidance, without necessarily copying it directly.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To refer to, to consult, to take as a reference.
2. **[noun]** Reference, source material.

## Example Sentences

**写报告之前，她参考了大量国内外的相关文献。**
Xiě bàogào zhīqián, tā cānkǎo le dàliàng guónèi wài de xiāngguān wénxiàn.
Before writing the report, she consulted a large number of relevant domestic and foreign documents.

**这份数据仅供参考，不能作为最终决策的依据。**
Zhè fèn shùjù jǐn gōng cānkǎo, bù néng zuòwéi zuìzhōng juécè de yījù.
This data is for reference only and cannot serve as the basis for a final decision.

**设计师参考了传统建筑风格，创作出这座兼具现代感的建筑。**
Shèjìshī cānkǎo le chuántǒng jiànzhù fēnggé, chuàngzuò chū zhè zuò jiānjù xiàndàigǎn de jiànzhù.
The architect referred to traditional architectural styles to create this building with a modern sensibility.

**老师建议学生多参考原著，而不只是看摘要。**
Lǎoshī jiànyì xuésheng duō cānkǎo yuánzhù, ér bù zhǐ shì kàn zhāiyào.
The teacher advised students to consult the original works more, rather than just reading summaries.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 仅供参考 | for reference only |
| 参考资料 | reference material |
| 参考书 | reference book |
| 参考意见 | reference opinion / input |
| 以...为参考 | to use ... as reference |

## Usage Notes

参考 implies selective use of external material, not copying. The phrase 仅供参考 (for reference only) is a very common disclaimer in official documents and presentations. As a noun, 参考书 specifically refers to study reference books used alongside textbooks in Chinese schools. Avoid confusing 参考 with 参加 (to participate) — the characters look similar but mean very different things.

## Memory Hook

参 means "to participate/visit" and 考 means "to examine" — you visit a source to examine it before drawing your own conclusions.
