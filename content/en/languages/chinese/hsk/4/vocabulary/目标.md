## 目标 (mùbiāo)

目标 refers to a goal, target, or objective that a person or group is working to achieve or reach.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Goal; target; objective (an aim or destination one works toward).

## Example Sentences

**我的目标是在一年内学会日语。**
Wǒ de mùbiāo shì zài yī nián nèi xuéhuì Rìyǔ.
My goal is to learn Japanese within one year.

**他们定下了今年的销售目标。**
Tāmen dìng xià le jīnnián de xiāoshòu mùbiāo.
They set the sales target for this year.

**要实现目标，需要坚持努力。**
Yào shíxiàn mùbiāo, xūyào jiānchí nǔlì.
To achieve your goals, you need to keep working hard.

**这个队伍的目标是赢得比赛冠军。**
Zhège duìwu de mùbiāo shì yíngdé bǐsài guànjūn.
This team's objective is to win the championship.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 实现目标 | to achieve a goal |
| 设定目标 | to set a goal |
| 达到目标 | to reach a target |
| 明确目标 | to clarify the objective |
| 长期目标 | long-term goal |

## Usage Notes

目标 can refer to both abstract goals (life goals, career goals) and concrete targets (a bullseye, a sales figure). In business and academic contexts, 目标 is very frequently paired with 实现 (to achieve) or 达到 (to reach).

## Memory Hook

目 means "eye" and 标 means "mark/sign"; 目标 is the mark your eye is fixed on, the thing you are aiming at.
