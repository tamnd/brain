## 流行病 (liúxíngbìng)

流行病 refers to an epidemic or pandemic — a disease that spreads rapidly and widely among a population. It became especially prominent in global vocabulary after recent health crises.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** epidemic; pandemic; a widely spreading infectious disease

## Example Sentences

**这次流行病在全世界范围内造成了很大的影响。** Zhè cì liúxíngbìng zài quán shìjiè fànwéi nèi zàochéng le hěn dà de yǐngxiǎng. This epidemic had a huge impact across the world.

**历史上出现过许多严重的流行病。** Lìshǐ shàng chūxiàn guò xǔduō yánzhòng de liúxíngbìng. Many serious epidemics have appeared throughout history.

**科学家们正在努力研究如何控制流行病。** Kēxuéjiāmen zhèngzài nǔlì yánjiū rúhé kòngzhì liúxíngbìng. Scientists are working hard to research how to control epidemics.

**流行病期间，大家应该减少外出，戴好口罩。** Liúxíngbìng qījiān, dàjiā yīnggāi jiǎnshǎo wàichū, dài hǎo kǒuzhào. During an epidemic, everyone should reduce going out and wear masks properly.

## Usage Notes

流行病 combines 流行 (liúxíng, popular/widespread) + 病 (bìng, disease). 流行 alone means "trendy" or "popular" in everyday contexts, but in the medical compound 流行病 it means "spreading widely." A related term is 传染病 (chuánrǎnbìng, infectious disease), which is broader. 流行病学 (epidemiology) is the scientific study of how diseases spread through populations.

## Memory Hook

流行 (spreading widely/popular) + 病 (disease) — an epidemic is a disease that has "gone viral" through a population.
