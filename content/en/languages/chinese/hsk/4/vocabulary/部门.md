## 部门 (bùmén)

部门 refers to a distinct unit or division within an organization, government, or company.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Department; section; division; unit (within an organization).

## Example Sentences

**你在哪个部门工作？**
Nǐ zài nǎge bùmén gōngzuò?
Which department do you work in?

**这件事需要各个部门合作完成。**
Zhè jiàn shì xūyào gège bùmén hézuò wánchéng.
This matter requires cooperation from every department to complete.

**销售部门本季度超额完成了任务。**
Xiāoshòu bùmén běn jìdù chāo'é wánchéngle rènwù.
The sales department exceeded its target this quarter.

**政府相关部门正在调查此事。**
Zhèngfǔ xiāngguān bùmén zhèngzài diàochá cǐ shì.
The relevant government departments are currently investigating this matter.

## Usage Notes

部门 is a general term used across government, companies, and institutions. In a company context, specific departments are often named with their function plus 部门 or simply 部 (e.g., 销售部, 技术部). The word 部 alone can sometimes replace 部门 in compound names.

## Memory Hook

部 means "section/part" and 门 means "door/gate" — each department is like a separate door leading into a part of the organization.
