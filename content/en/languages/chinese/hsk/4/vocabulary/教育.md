## 教育 (jiàoyù)

教育 refers to the broad system and process of educating people, or the act of teaching someone proper values and knowledge.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** education; schooling; upbringing
2. **[verb]** to educate; to instruct; to teach (values or discipline)

## Example Sentences

**教育是国家发展的基础。** Jiàoyù shì guójiā fāzhǎn de jīchǔ. Education is the foundation of a country's development.

**父母要重视对孩子的早期教育。** Fùmǔ yào zhòngshì duì háizi de zǎoqī jiàoyù. Parents should pay attention to their children's early education.

**老师耐心地教育学生要诚实守信。** Lǎoshī nàixīn de jiàoyù xuéshēng yào chéngshí shǒuxìn. The teacher patiently educated students to be honest and trustworthy.

**她接受过良好的高等教育，毕业于名校。** Tā jiēshòuguò liánghǎo de gāoděng jiàoyù, bìyè yú míngxiào. She received a good higher education and graduated from a prestigious school.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 高等教育 | higher education |

| 教育制度 | education system |

## Usage Notes

教育 as a noun refers to the entire education system or one's overall educational background. As a verb, it often implies teaching someone correct values or behavior, and can carry a slight sense of discipline or moral guidance. 义务教育 (compulsory education) is a fixed term used in official contexts in China, referring to nine years of required schooling.

## Memory Hook

教 means "to teach" and 育 means "to nurture/raise" -- 教育 is teaching and nurturing, the two pillars of education.
