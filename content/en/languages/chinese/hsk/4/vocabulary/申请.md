## 申请 (shēnqǐng)

申请 means to formally apply for something such as a job, visa, or admission, or it refers to the application itself as a noun.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to apply for; to formally request something through an official process
2. **[noun]** application; a formal request submitted to an authority

## Example Sentences

**我申请了一所美国大学的研究生项目。** Wǒ shēnqǐng le yī suǒ měiguó dàxué de yánjiūshēng xiàngmù. I applied to a graduate program at an American university.

**你的签证申请已经被批准了。** Nǐ de qiānzhèng shēnqǐng yǐjīng bèi pīzhǔn le. Your visa application has been approved.

**请填写申请表并提交所需材料。** Qǐng tiánxiě shēnqǐng biǎo bìng tíjiāo suǒ xū cáiliào. Please fill out the application form and submit the required materials.

**他向银行申请了一笔贷款。** Tā xiàng yínháng shēnqǐng le yī bǐ dàikuǎn. He applied to the bank for a loan.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 申请表 | application form |
| 签证申请 | visa application |
| 提交申请 | to submit an application |
| 申请入学 | to apply for admission |

## Usage Notes

申请 is used in formal contexts for official requests. It requires an institution or authority as the recipient, often introduced by 向 (to/toward). The phrase 提出申请 means to put forward an application, while 批准申请 means to approve an application.

## Memory Hook

申 means "to state/express" and 请 means "to request": 申请 is formally stating your request, putting your case forward officially.
