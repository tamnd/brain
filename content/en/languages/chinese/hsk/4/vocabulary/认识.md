## 认识 (rènshi)

认识 means to know or be acquainted with a person, or to recognize and understand something.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to know (a person); to be acquainted with; to recognize
2. **[noun]** understanding; cognition; knowledge

## Example Sentences

**我认识他已经三年了。** Wǒ rènshi tā yǐjīng sān nián le. I have known him for three years already.

**很高兴认识你！** Hěn gāoxìng rènshi nǐ! Very pleased to meet you!

**她对这个问题有深刻的认识。** Tā duì zhège wèntí yǒu shēnkè de rènshi. She has a deep understanding of this issue.

**通过这次经历，我认识到了自己的不足。** Tōngguò zhè cì jīnglì, wǒ rènshi dào le zìjǐ de bùzú. Through this experience, I came to recognize my own shortcomings.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 认识到 (rènshi dào) | to come to realize; to recognize |
| 互相认识 (hùxiāng rènshi) | to know each other |
| 深刻认识 (shēnkè rènshi) | deep understanding |
| 正确认识 (zhèngquè rènshi) | correct understanding |

## Usage Notes

认识 is used for knowing people or having an understanding of something. It is different from 知道 (zhīdào), which means "to know a fact." You 认识 a person but you 知道 information. The phrase 认识到 means "to come to realize" and is often used in reflective or formal writing.

## Memory Hook

认 (to recognize) + 识 (to know/understand) — to recognize someone's face and know who they are, that is 认识.
