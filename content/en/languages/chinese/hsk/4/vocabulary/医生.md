## 医生 (yīshēng)

医生 is the general term for a medical doctor or physician in Chinese.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** doctor; physician; medical practitioner

## Example Sentences

**医生建议我多休息，少工作。** Yīshēng jiànyì wǒ duō xiūxi, shǎo gōngzuò. The doctor advised me to rest more and work less.

**她从小就想成为一名医生。** Tā cóng xiǎo jiù xiǎng chéngwéi yī míng yīshēng. She has wanted to become a doctor since she was young.

**请问，这里有没有会说英语的医生？** Qǐngwèn, zhèlǐ yǒu méiyǒu huì shuō Yīngyǔ de yīshēng? Excuse me, is there a doctor here who can speak English?

**医生检查后说我的身体很健康。** Yīshēng jiǎnchá hòu shuō wǒ de shēntǐ hěn jiànkāng. After the examination, the doctor said my body is very healthy.

## Usage Notes

医生 is the most common and neutral term for a doctor. The more formal term 大夫 (dàifu) is also used in northern China. When visiting a hospital, Chinese speakers typically say 看医生 (to see a doctor) or 看病 (to see a doctor about an illness).

## Memory Hook

医 means "medicine/to heal" and 生 means "person/student" -- a 医生 is literally "a person of medicine," a doctor.
