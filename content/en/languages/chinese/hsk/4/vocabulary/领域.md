## 领域 (lǐngyù)

领域 means "field," "domain," or "realm" and refers to a specific area of knowledge, activity, or expertise.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** field; domain; area; realm

## Example Sentences

**他在医学领域做出了重大贡献。** Tā zài yīxué lǐngyù zuòchū le zhòngdà gòngxiàn. He made major contributions in the field of medicine.

**人工智能是一个发展迅速的领域。** Réngōng zhìnéng shì yīgè fāzhǎn xùnsù de lǐngyù. Artificial intelligence is a rapidly developing field.

**她在艺术领域非常有才华。** Tā zài yìshù lǐngyù fēicháng yǒu cáihuá. She is very talented in the field of art.

**不同领域的专家聚集在一起讨论这个问题。** Bùtóng lǐngyù de zhuānjiā jùjí zài yīqǐ tǎolùn zhège wèntí. Experts from different fields gathered together to discuss this issue.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 专业领域 | specialized field |
| 各个领域 | various fields |
| 领域专家 | domain expert |
| 在...领域 | in the field of... |

## Usage Notes

领域 is used broadly across academic, scientific, professional, and artistic contexts. It is more abstract than 行业 (hángyè, industry or trade), which refers to economic sectors. A useful construction is 在...领域 (in the field of...) followed by an activity or achievement.

## Memory Hook

领 (lǐng) means "territory or to lead" and 域 (yù) means "region or boundary": a 领域 is the "territory" of a particular area of knowledge or activity.
