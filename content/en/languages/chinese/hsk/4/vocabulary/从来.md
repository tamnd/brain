## 从来 (cónglái)

从来 indicates that something has consistently been true from the past up to now. In positive sentences it means "always"; when negated with 不 or 没 it means "never."

## Meanings

1. **[adverb]** Always; at all times (consistently true from the past to now).
2. **[adverb]** Never (when used with negation 不 or 没).

## Example Sentences

**她从来不迟到，总是准时到达。**
Tā cónglái bù chídào, zǒngshì zhǔnshí dàodá.
She never arrives late; she is always on time.

**我从来没有去过日本，很想有机会去看看。**
Wǒ cónglái méiyǒu qù guò Rìběn, hěn xiǎng yǒu jīhuì qù kàn kan.
I have never been to Japan; I really want to have the chance to go.

**他从来都很认真，做事一丝不苟。**
Tā cónglái dōu hěn rènzhēn, zuòshì yīsī bùgǒu.
He has always been very serious and meticulous in everything he does.

**这种情况从来没有发生过。**
Zhè zhǒng qíngkuàng cónglái méiyǒu fāshēng guò.
This kind of situation has never happened before.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 从来不 | never (habitual negation) |
| 从来没有 | have never (experience) |
| 从来都 | has always been; invariably |
| 从来如此 | it has always been like this |

## Usage Notes

从来 nearly always appears with negation (从来不 or 从来没有) in everyday speech. Positive 从来都 is less common but used for emphasis. 从来不 expresses a habitual behavior (never does X), while 从来没有 expresses a completed experience (has never done X).

## Memory Hook

从 means "from" and 来 means "to come"; 从来 traces all the way from the beginning up to now, covering the entire timeline.
