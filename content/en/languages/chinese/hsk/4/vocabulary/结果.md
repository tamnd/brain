## 结果 (jiéguǒ)

结果 refers to the final outcome or result of a process, and it also functions as a connective meaning "as a result" or "in the end."

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Result; outcome; conclusion.
2. **[conjunction]** As a result; consequently; in the end.

## Example Sentences

**考试结果明天才能知道。**
Kǎoshì jiéguǒ míngtiān cái néng zhīdào.
We won't know the exam results until tomorrow.

**他努力工作，结果获得了晋升。**
Tā nǔlì gōngzuò, jiéguǒ huòdéle jìnshēng.
He worked hard, and as a result he received a promotion.

**这次实验的结果令科学家们很满意。**
Zhè cì shíyàn de jiéguǒ lìng kēxuéjiāmen hěn mǎnyì.
The results of this experiment satisfied the scientists.

**他没有认真复习，结果没通过考试。**
Tā méiyǒu rènzhēn fùxí, jiéguǒ méi tōngguò kǎoshì.
He did not review seriously, and as a result he failed the exam.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 最终结果 | final result |
| 产生结果 | to produce a result |
| 取得结果 | to obtain a result |
| 结果表明 | the results indicate |
| 导致结果 | to lead to a result |

## Usage Notes

结果 functions both as a noun ("exam results") and as a discourse connective ("as a result, in the end"). When used as a connective, it often introduces an unexpected or consequential outcome. It is more final-sounding than 效果 (effect/impact), which focuses on the quality of the impact rather than the definitive end state.

## Memory Hook

结 means "to knot/conclude" and 果 means "fruit/outcome" — the fruit that appears when something concludes is your result.
