## 会议 (huìyì)

会议 refers to a formal meeting, conference, or session where people gather to discuss issues and make decisions.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** a meeting, conference, or formal session for discussion

## Example Sentences

**公司每周都会召开一次会议。** Gōngsī měi zhōu dōu huì zhàokāi yī cì huìyì. The company holds a meeting every week.

**这次国际会议吸引了来自各国的专家。** Zhè cì guójì huìyì xīyǐn le láizì gè guó de zhuānjiā. This international conference attracted experts from various countries.

**会议室已经为明天的会议做好了准备。** Huìyì shì yǐjīng wèi míngtiān de huìyì zuò hǎo le zhǔnbèi. The conference room has been prepared for tomorrow's meeting.

**我下午有一个重要的会议，不能离开。** Wǒ xiàwǔ yǒu yīgè zhòngyào de huìyì, bù néng líkāi. I have an important meeting this afternoon and cannot leave.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |

## Usage Notes

会议 is more formal than 会 (meeting) alone and is used in professional, academic, and official contexts. 召开会议 (to convene a meeting) is the standard phrase for organizing one. Compare with 会面 (a personal meeting with someone) which is less formal and implies a one-on-one encounter.

## Memory Hook

会 means "to meet" and 议 means "to discuss," so 会议 is a gathering to meet and discuss, a meeting.
