## 扩大 (kuòdà)

扩大 means to expand, enlarge, or extend something, making its scope, size, or scale greater than before.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to expand; to enlarge; to extend; to broaden

## Example Sentences

**公司计划扩大在亚洲的市场。** Gōngsī jìhuà kuòdà zài yàzhōu de shìchǎng. The company plans to expand its market in Asia.

**我们需要扩大交流与合作的范围。** Wǒmen xūyào kuòdà jiāoliú yǔ hézuò de fànwéi. We need to expand the scope of exchange and cooperation.

**这次活动扩大了他的社交圈。** Zhè cì huódòng kuòdàle tā de shèjiāo quān. This activity expanded his social circle.

**经过改革，工厂的生产规模大幅扩大。** Jīngguò gǎigé, gōngchǎng de shēngchǎn guīmó dàfú kuòdà. After the reforms, the factory's production scale expanded significantly.

## Usage Notes

扩大 can be used with abstract objects (influence, scope, cooperation) as well as concrete ones (factory, market, area). It implies a deliberate act of making something bigger. The opposite would be 缩小 (to reduce, shrink). It is common in business, political, and academic contexts.

## Memory Hook

扩 means "to expand/widen" and 大 means "big." So 扩大 is to expand into something big: straightforwardly to make larger.
