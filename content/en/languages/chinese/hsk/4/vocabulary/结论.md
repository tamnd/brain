## 结论 (jiélùn)

结论 is the conclusion or final judgment reached at the end of a process of reasoning, research, or discussion.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** conclusion, final judgment, finding

## Example Sentences

**研究人员得出了一个重要的结论。** Yánjiū rényuán dé chū le yīgè zhòngyào de jiélùn. The researchers reached an important conclusion.

**在没有足够证据之前，不要轻易下结论。** Zài méiyǒu zúgòu zhèngjù zhīqián, bùyào qīngyì xià jiélùn. Do not jump to conclusions before there is enough evidence.

**这篇文章的结论很清晰，让人容易理解。** Zhè piān wénzhāng de jiélùn hěn qīngxī, ràng rén róngyì lǐjiě. The conclusion of this article is very clear and easy to understand.

**经过讨论，大家最终达成了一致的结论。** Jīngguò tǎolùn, dàjiā zuìzhōng dáchéng le yīzhì de jiélùn. After discussion, everyone finally reached a unanimous conclusion.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 得出结论 (dé chū jiélùn) | to reach a conclusion |
| 下结论 (xià jiélùn) | to draw a conclusion |
| 最终结论 (zuìzhōng jiélùn) | final conclusion |
| 错误结论 (cuòwù jiélùn) | wrong conclusion |

## Usage Notes

结论 is used for a reasoned or researched conclusion. The common verb collocations are 得出结论 (to arrive at a conclusion) and 下结论 (to make a conclusion, often used in caution: 不要轻易下结论, "don't jump to conclusions"). 结论 appears most often in academic, argumentative, or analytical contexts.

## Memory Hook

结 means "to tie/conclude" and 论 means "argument/theory" — a 结论 "ties up" all the arguments into one final point.
