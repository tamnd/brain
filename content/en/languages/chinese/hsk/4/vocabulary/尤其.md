## 尤其 (yóuqí)

尤其 highlights one item or situation as standing out above others, equivalent to "especially" or "particularly" in English.

## Meanings

1. **[adverb]** Especially; particularly; in particular (singles out one thing as most notable).

## Example Sentences

**我喜欢中国菜，尤其是四川菜。**
Wǒ xǐhuān Zhōngguó cài, yóuqí shì Sìchuān cài.
I like Chinese food, especially Sichuan cuisine.

**这里夏天很热，尤其是七月和八月。**
Zhèlǐ xiàtiān hěn rè, yóuqí shì qīyuè hé bāyuè.
It is very hot here in summer, particularly in July and August.

**他在语言学习上很有天赋，尤其是发音方面。**
Tā zài yǔyán xuéxí shàng hěn yǒu tiānfù, yóuqí shì fāyīn fāngmiàn.
He has great talent in language learning, especially in pronunciation.

**这本书对初学者很有帮助，尤其是第一章。**
Zhè běn shū duì chūxuézhě hěn yǒu bāngzhù, yóuqí shì dì yī zhāng.
This book is very helpful for beginners, in particular the first chapter.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 尤其是 | especially; in particular (with noun) |
| 尤其在 | especially in/at |
| 尤其当 | especially when |
| 更尤其 | even more especially (emphatic) |

## Usage Notes

尤其 is often followed by 是 (尤其是) when introducing a specific noun. It can also precede a verb phrase or clause. 特别 is a near-synonym; both are very common, but 尤其 is slightly more formal and more often used in writing.

## Memory Hook

尤 means "outstanding/surpassing" and 其 means "that particular one"; 尤其 points to the one that surpasses all others in the group.
