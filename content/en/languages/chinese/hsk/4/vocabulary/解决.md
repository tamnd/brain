## 解决 (jiějué)

解决 means "to solve," "to resolve," or "to settle" and is used when a problem, conflict, or issue is successfully dealt with.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to solve; to resolve; to settle (a problem or conflict)
2. **[verb]** to deal with; to handle; to take care of

## Example Sentences

**这个问题很难解决。** Zhège wèntí hěn nán jiějué. This problem is very difficult to solve.

**我们一起找方法解决这个困难。** Wǒmen yīqǐ zhǎo fāngfǎ jiějué zhège kùnnán. Let's find a way together to resolve this difficulty.

**谈判最终解决了两国之间的争端。** Tánpàn zuìzhōng jiějué le liǎng guó zhījiān de zhēngduān. Negotiations ultimately resolved the dispute between the two countries.

**这个软件可以帮你解决很多问题。** Zhège ruǎnjiàn kěyǐ bāng nǐ jiějué hěn duō wèntí. This software can help you solve many problems.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 解决问题 | to solve a problem |
| 解决矛盾 | to resolve a conflict |
| 彻底解决 | to completely resolve |
| 解决方案 | solution; resolution |

## Usage Notes

解决 is a very common and versatile verb. 解决方案 (jiějué fāng'àn) means "solution" as a noun phrase. In formal contexts, it often implies that a resolution has been reached after effort or negotiation. It is frequently paired with 问题 (problem), 矛盾 (conflict), or 困难 (difficulty).

## Memory Hook

解 (jiě) means "to untie or unravel" and 决 (jué) means "to decide or determine": solving a problem is like untying a knot and making a decision.
