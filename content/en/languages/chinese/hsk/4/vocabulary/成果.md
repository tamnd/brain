## 成果 (chéngguǒ)

成果 refers to the positive results or achievements gained through hard work, effort, or research, often described as the "fruits" of one's labor.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** achievement; result; fruit of effort; accomplishment

## Example Sentences

**科学家发布了最新的研究成果。** Kēxuéjiā fābùle zuìxīn de yánjiū chéngguǒ. The scientists released their latest research findings.

**多年的努力终于取得了丰硕的成果。** Duō nián de nǔlì zhōngyú qǔdéle fēngshuò de chéngguǒ. Years of effort finally yielded fruitful results.

**这个团队的工作成果令人印象深刻。** Zhège tuánduì de gōngzuò chéngguǒ lìng rén yìnxiàng shēnkè. The team's work results were very impressive.

**我们要保护和推广这些文化成果。** Wǒmen yào bǎohù hé tuīguǎng zhèxiē wénhuà chéngguǒ. We should protect and promote these cultural achievements.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 研究成果 | research results/findings |
| 取得成果 | to achieve results |
| 丰硕成果 | fruitful results |
| 工作成果 | work achievements |

## Usage Notes

成果 always has a positive connotation, referring to achievements or results that are valued and worth noting. It is different from 结果 (result), which is neutral and can be good or bad. 成果 specifically implies that effort and hard work led to something worthwhile.

## Memory Hook

成 means "to accomplish" and 果 means "fruit." So 成果 is "the fruit of accomplishment": the sweet reward you pick after cultivating something with effort.
