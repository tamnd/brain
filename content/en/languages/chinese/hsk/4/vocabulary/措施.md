## 措施 (cuòshī)

措施 refers to a concrete action, step, or measure deliberately taken to address a problem or achieve an objective.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Measure; step; action; countermeasure.

## Example Sentences

**政府采取了有效的措施控制污染。**
Zhèngfǔ cǎiqǔle yǒuxiào de cuòshī kòngzhì wūrǎn.
The government took effective measures to control pollution.

**我们需要立即采取行动，否则问题会更严重。**
Wǒmen xūyào lìjí cǎiqǔ cuòshī, fǒuzé wèntí huì gèng yánzhòng.
We need to take measures immediately, or the problem will get worse.

**学校制定了一系列安全措施。**
Xuéxiào zhìdìngle yī xìliè ānquán cuòshī.
The school has formulated a series of safety measures.

**这些措施取得了明显的效果。**
Zhèxiē cuòshī qǔdéle míngxiǎn de xiàoguǒ.
These measures have achieved noticeable results.

## Collocations

## Usage Notes

措施 is almost always used with 采取 (to take/adopt), forming the highly frequent phrase 采取措施 (to take measures). It is a formal noun used in administrative, political, and business contexts. Its near-synonym 办法 (bànfǎ, method/way) is more colloquial.

## Memory Hook

措 contains the hand radical, suggesting placing something in order, and 施 means "to implement" — placing and implementing actions are the two steps of any measure.
