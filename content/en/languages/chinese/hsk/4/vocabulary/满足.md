## 满足 (mǎnzú)

满足 means either to fulfill or meet a need or standard (as a verb), or to feel contentment and satisfaction with what one has (as an adjective).

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To satisfy, to meet, to fulfill (a need, condition, or requirement).
2. **[adjective]** Satisfied, content, fulfilled.

## Example Sentences

**这家公司努力满足客户不断变化的需求，赢得了良好的口碑。**
Zhè jiā gōngsī nǔlì mǎnzú kèhù bùduàn biànhuà de xūqiú, yíngdé le liánghǎo de kǒubēi.
This company works hard to meet customers' constantly changing needs, earning a good reputation.

**他对目前的工作感到非常满足，暂时不打算换公司。**
Tā duì mùqián de gōngzuò gǎndào fēicháng mǎnzú, zànshí bù dǎsuàn huàn gōngsī.
He is very satisfied with his current job and has no plans to change companies for now.

**仅仅满足于现状是不够的，我们必须不断进步，应对未来的挑战。**
Jǐnjǐn mǎnzú yú xiànzhuàng shì bùgòu de, wǒmen bìxū bùduàn jìnbù, yìngduì wèilái de tiǎozhàn.
Simply being content with the status quo is not enough; we must keep improving to face future challenges.

**申请人必须满足以下条件，否则将不予受理。**
Shēnqǐng rén bìxū mǎnzú yǐxià tiáojiàn, fǒuzé jiāng bù yǔ shòulǐ.
Applicants must meet the following conditions; otherwise the application will not be accepted.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 满足需求 | to meet needs |
| 满足条件 | to meet conditions |
| 感到满足 | to feel satisfied |
| 满足于 | to be content with |
| 不满足 | dissatisfied / to not satisfy |

## Usage Notes

满足 as a verb always has an object (满足需求, 满足条件). As an adjective it often appears in 感到满足 or 满足于. The subtle distinction: 满意 (mǎnyì) means "pleased/satisfied with how something turned out" and is more evaluative, while 满足 as an adjective reflects a deeper sense of fulfillment. 满足于现状 (content with the status quo) often carries a slightly negative connotation of complacency.

## Memory Hook

满 means "full" and 足 means "enough/foot" — when your cup is full and you have enough under your feet to stand on, you feel satisfied.
