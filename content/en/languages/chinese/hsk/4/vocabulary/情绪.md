## 情绪 (qíngxù)

情绪 refers to a person's current emotional state, mood, or the general emotional atmosphere of a group.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** Mood; emotion; sentiment; feeling; morale.

## Example Sentences

**他今天情绪很好，一直在笑。**
Tā jīntiān qíngxù hěn hǎo, yīzhí zài xiào.
He is in a very good mood today and has been smiling all along.

**她的情绪受到了坏消息的影响。**
Tā de qíngxù shòudào le huài xiāoxi de yǐngxiǎng.
Her mood was affected by the bad news.

**学会控制情绪是一种重要能力。**
Xuéhuì kòngzhì qíngxù shì yī zhǒng zhòngyào nénglì.
Learning to control one's emotions is an important ability.

**员工的情绪对工作效率影响很大。**
Yuángōng de qíngxù duì gōngzuò xiàolǜ yǐngxiǎng hěn dà.
Employee morale has a major impact on work efficiency.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 控制情绪 | to control one's emotions |
| 情绪好/差 | in a good/bad mood |
| 情绪稳定 | emotionally stable |
| 调节情绪 | to regulate emotions |
| 受情绪影响 | to be affected by emotions |

## Usage Notes

情绪 often refers to one's mood or emotional state at a given moment. It can be modified by adjectives: 情绪好 (good mood), 情绪低落 (low mood/depressed). The verb collocations 控制情绪 (control emotions) and 调节情绪 (regulate emotions) are very common in discussions of mental health and personal development.

## Memory Hook

情 means "feeling/emotion" and 绪 means "thread/beginning" — emotions are like threads that, once pulled, unravel your whole mood.
