## 充分 (chōngfèn)

充分 describes a quantity or degree that is fully adequate, leaving nothing lacking.

## Meanings

1. **[adjective]** Sufficient; full; ample; thorough.

## Example Sentences

**你需要充分的准备才能通过考试。**
Nǐ xūyào chōngfèn de zhǔnbèi cái néng tōngguò kǎoshì.
You need ample preparation to pass the exam.

**我们应该充分利用时间。**
Wǒmen yīnggāi chōngfèn lìyòng shíjiān.
We should make full use of our time.

**会议前大家进行了充分的讨论。**
Huìyì qián dàjiā jìnxíngle chōngfèn de tǎolùn.
Before the meeting, everyone had a thorough discussion.

**这个理由不够充分。**
Zhège lǐyóu bùgòu chōngfèn.
This reason is not sufficiently convincing.

## Usage Notes

充分 is often used before verbs as an adverb meaning "fully/thoroughly," as in 充分利用 (make full use of) or 充分发挥 (bring into full play). It emphasizes completeness of degree rather than just quantity.

## Memory Hook

充 means "to fill up" and 分 means "portion" — when every portion is filled up, you have more than enough.
