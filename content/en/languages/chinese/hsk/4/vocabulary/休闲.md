## 休闲 (xiūxián)

休闲 describes leisure time and recreational activities, as well as a casual, relaxed style or atmosphere. It is used both as a noun and as an adjective modifying clothing, venues, and lifestyles.

## Meanings
1. **n** leisure, recreation, free time
2. **adj** casual, leisure (as in clothing style)

## Example Sentences
**周末我喜欢做一些休闲活动，比如看电影。** Zhōumò wǒ xǐhuān zuò yīxiē xiūxián huódòng, bǐrú kàn diànyǐng. On weekends I like to do leisure activities, such as watching movies.

**他穿着休闲服装去参加聚会。** Tā chuānzhe xiūxián fúzhuāng qù cānjiā jùhuì. He wore casual clothing to attend the gathering.

**这个城市有很多休闲娱乐场所。** Zhège chéngshì yǒu hěn duō xiūxián yúlè chǎngsuǒ. This city has many leisure and entertainment venues.

**现代人越来越重视休闲时间的质量。** Xiàndài rén yuèlái yuè zhòngshì xiūxián shíjiān de zhìliàng. Modern people increasingly value the quality of their leisure time.

## Collocations
| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 休闲活动 (xiūxián huódòng) | leisure activities |
| 休闲服装 (xiūxián fúzhuāng) | casual clothing |
| 休闲时间 (xiūxián shíjiān) | free time, leisure time |
| 休闲娱乐 (xiūxián yúlè) | leisure and entertainment |

## Usage Notes
休闲 combines 休 (rest) and 闲 (idle/free). As an adjective before clothing it means "casual" rather than "formal." It is often paired with 娱乐 to form the compound 休闲娱乐, referring to the leisure and entertainment industry.

## Memory Hook
休 shows a person (人) leaning against a tree (木) — resting freely (闲) is the perfect picture of leisure.
