## 模型 (móxíng)

模型 refers to a model or template, either a physical small-scale replica or a conceptual framework used as a reference.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** model; template; a physical replica or conceptual framework serving as a reference or example

## Example Sentences

**他做了一个飞机模型送给儿子。** Tā zuò le yī gè fēijī móxíng sòng gěi érzi. He made a model airplane to give to his son.

**这个经济模型可以预测市场趋势。** Zhège jīngjì móxíng kěyǐ yùcè shìchǎng qūshì. This economic model can predict market trends.

**工程师根据模型设计了新产品。** Gōngchéngshī gēnjù móxíng shèjì le xīn chǎnpǐn. The engineer designed the new product based on the model.

**这种教育模型在很多国家都有使用。** Zhè zhǒng jiàoyù móxíng zài hěn duō guójiā dōu yǒu shǐyòng. This educational model is used in many countries.

## Usage Notes

模型 can refer to a physical scale model (like a toy airplane or architectural mockup) or an abstract conceptual model (like an economic or mathematical framework). In everyday speech the physical sense is more common; in academic or professional contexts the abstract sense dominates.

## Memory Hook

模 means "mold/template" and 型 means "form/type": a model is a mold that defines the form of something.
