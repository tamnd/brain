## 机制 (jīzhì)

机制 refers to a mechanism or system, describing how something is organized or how it operates internally.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** mechanism; system; the structured way in which something functions or is organized

## Example Sentences

**公司需要建立有效的管理机制。** Gōngsī xūyào jiànlì yǒuxiào de guǎnlǐ jīzhì. The company needs to establish an effective management mechanism.

**市场机制决定了商品的价格。** Shìchǎng jīzhì juédìng le shāngpǐn de jiàgé. The market mechanism determines the price of goods.

**我们要完善监督机制。** Wǒmen yào wánshàn jiāndū jīzhì. We need to improve the supervision mechanism.

**这种激励机制能提高员工积极性。** Zhè zhǒng jīlì jīzhì néng tígāo yuángōng jījíxìng. This incentive mechanism can improve employee motivation.

## Usage Notes

机制 is a formal term common in economics, management, and policy discussions. It describes an underlying system or set of processes, rather than just a single rule. At B1 level it frequently appears in texts about organizations and society.

## Memory Hook

机 means "machine" and 制 means "system/control": a mechanism is the machine-like system that controls how something works.
