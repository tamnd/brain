## 尊重 (zūnzhòng)

尊重 means to respect or show esteem toward someone or something, and is used both as a verb and a noun in everyday Chinese.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** to respect; to show respect for
2. **[noun]** respect; esteem

## Example Sentences

**我们应该尊重老人。** Wǒmen yīnggāi zūnzhòng lǎorén. We should respect the elderly.

**尊重别人的意见很重要。** Zūnzhòng biérén de yìjiàn hěn zhòngyào. Respecting others' opinions is very important.

**他赢得了大家的尊重。** Tā yíngdéle dàjiā de zūnzhòng. He earned everyone's respect.

**请尊重我的决定。** Qǐng zūnzhòng wǒ de juédìng. Please respect my decision.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 尊重他人 | to respect others |
| 赢得尊重 | to earn respect |
| 互相尊重 | to respect each other |
| 尊重意见 | to respect opinions |

## Usage Notes

尊重 is a formal yet everyday word. As a verb it takes a direct object (尊重+person/thing). As a noun it often appears in structures like 赢得尊重 (earn respect) or 表示尊重 (show respect). It carries a stronger sense of deliberate esteem than simply being polite.
