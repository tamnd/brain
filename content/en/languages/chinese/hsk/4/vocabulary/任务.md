## 任务 (rènwù)

任务 refers to a task, assignment, or mission given to someone to accomplish, used in both everyday and professional contexts.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** task, mission, or assigned duty that must be completed

## Example Sentences

**我们今天的任务是完成这份报告。** Wǒmen jīntiān de rènwù shì wánchéng zhè fèn bàogào. Our task today is to complete this report.

**他顺利完成了这次任务。** Tā shùnlì wánchéng le zhè cì rènwù. He successfully completed this mission.

**这项任务需要团队合作才能完成。** Zhè xiàng rènwù xūyào tuánduì hézuò cái néng wánchéng. This task requires teamwork to complete.

**老师给学生布置了一项特别的任务。** Lǎoshī gěi xuéshēng bùzhì le yī xiàng tèbié de rènwù. The teacher assigned a special task to the students.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 完成任务 | complete a task |
| 布置任务 | assign a task |
| 执行任务 | carry out a mission |
| 重要任务 | important mission |

## Usage Notes

任务 is used widely in academic, professional, and military contexts. 完成任务 (to complete the task) is one of the most common collocations. Unlike 工作 (work/job), 任务 implies a specific, defined assignment with a goal to be achieved. It can refer to a simple homework assignment or a complex organizational mission.

## Memory Hook

任 means "to appoint or take responsibility" and 务 means "affair or duty," so a 任务 is an affair you have been appointed to handle.
