## 专业 (zhuānyè)

专业 refers to a specific field of study or work, and can describe something as professional or specialized in nature.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** a field of study, major, or profession
2. **[adjective]** professional, specialized, or expert-level

## Example Sentences

**他学的专业是计算机科学。** Tā xué de zhuānyè shì jìsuànjī kēxué. His major is computer science.

**我们需要专业的意见来解决这个问题。** Wǒmen xūyào zhuānyè de yìjiàn lái jiějué zhège wèntí. We need professional advice to solve this problem.

**她的专业技能让公司非常满意。** Tā de zhuānyè jìnéng ràng gōngsī fēicháng mǎnyì. Her professional skills made the company very satisfied.

**这项工作需要非常专业的知识。** Zhè xiàng gōngzuò xūyào fēicháng zhuānyè de zhīshi. This job requires very specialized knowledge.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| 专业知识 | specialized knowledge |

| 专业人士 | professional person |
| 专业课程 | specialized courses |

## Usage Notes

专业 is highly versatile in Chinese: as a noun, it refers to one's academic major or area of specialization; as an adjective, it means professional or skilled. University students frequently say 我的专业是... (my major is...). At B1 level, understanding both uses is important.

## Memory Hook

专 means "focused on one thing" and 业 means "profession," so 专业 is literally "focused on one profession," a true specialist.
