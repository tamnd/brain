## 了解 (liǎojiě)

了解 means to understand something by actively learning about it or gathering information. It implies a deeper familiarity than simply knowing a fact (知道 zhīdào), suggesting that you have taken steps to inquire, observe, or research in order to grasp the full picture.

## Meanings

1. **[verb]** To understand; to find out; to learn about; to get to know.
2. **[noun]** Understanding; knowledge; familiarity.

## Example Sentences

**我想进一步了解这家公司的文化和发展方向。**
Wǒ xiǎng jìnyībù liǎojiě zhè jiā gōngsī de wénhuà hé fāzhǎn fāngxiàng.
I would like to learn more about this company's culture and direction of development.

**通过多次交流，他们对彼此有了更深的了解。**
Tōngguò duō cì jiāoliú, tāmen duì bǐcǐ yǒu le gèng shēn de liǎojiě.
Through multiple exchanges, they developed a deeper understanding of each other.

**在做决策之前，你需要充分了解所有相关信息。**
Zài zuò juécè zhīqián, nǐ xūyào chōngfèn liǎojiě suǒyǒu xiāngguān xìnxī.
Before making a decision, you need to fully understand all the relevant information.

**医生需要了解患者的病史才能做出准确的诊断。**
Yīshēng xūyào liǎojiě huànzhě de bìngshǐ cái néng zuòchū zhǔnquè de zhěnduàn.
The doctor needs to understand the patient's medical history to make an accurate diagnosis.

## Usage Notes

了解 and 知道 are both translated as "to know/understand," but they differ in depth. 知道 (zhīdào) is used for simple factual knowledge ("I know your name"), while 了解 implies a more thorough, experiential understanding ("I understand your background"). 了解 often takes the preposition 对 (duì): 他对这个领域非常了解 (He is very knowledgeable about this field).

## Memory Hook

了 means "to complete/finish" and 解 means "to untie/solve." To 了解 something is to completely untie all its knots until nothing is hidden from you.
