## 资金 (zījīn)

资金 means "funds," "capital," or "funding" and refers to money available or allocated for a specific purpose such as investment, a project, or running an organization.

## Meanings

1. **[noun]** funds; capital; financial resources; funding

## Example Sentences

**公司正在寻求新的投资资金。** Gōngsī zhèngzài xúnqiú xīn de tóuzī zījīn. The company is seeking new investment funds.

**这个项目需要大量资金支持。** Zhège xiàngmù xūyào dàliàng zījīn zhīchí. This project needs substantial funding support.

**政府为小企业提供了专项资金。** Zhèngfǔ wèi xiǎo qǐyè tígōng le zhuānxiàng zījīn. The government provided special funds for small businesses.

**由于资金不足，计划被推迟了。** Yóuyú zījīn bùzú, jìhuà bèi tuīchí le. Due to insufficient funds, the plan was postponed.

## Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |

## Usage Notes

资金 refers to money in the context of finance, business, or project management, and is more formal than 钱 (qián, money). It often appears in discussions of investment, budgeting, and economic policy. 筹集资金 (to raise funds) is a common verb phrase for fundraising or mobilizing capital.

## Memory Hook

资 (zī) means "resources or assets" and 金 (jīn) means "gold or money": 资金 is the "golden resource" that finances activities.
