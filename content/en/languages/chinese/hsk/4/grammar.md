HSK 4 grammar covers approximately 80 core patterns for confident upper-intermediate communication. This level focuses on complex comparisons, formal registers, advanced conditional structures, topic-comment sentences, and nuanced aspect usage.

## 1. Advanced Comparisons

### 1.1 比 with Specific Difference

| Pattern | Example | English |
|---------|---------|---------|
| A + 比 + B + Adj + 一点儿 | 他比我高一点儿。| He is a little taller than me. |
| A + 比 + B + Adj + 得多 | 她比他聪明得多。| She is much smarter than him. |
| A + 比 + B + Adj + 多了 | 今天比昨天热多了。| Today is much hotter than yesterday. |
| A + 比 + B + 多/少 + V + Number | 他比我多学了两年。| He studied two more years than me. |

### 1.2 不如 (bùrú) — Not As Good As

| Pattern | Example | English |
|---------|---------|---------|
| A + 不如 + B (+ Adj) | 我不如他跑得快。| I don't run as fast as him. |
| A + 不如 + B | 这个方案不如那个。| This plan is not as good as that one. |

### 1.3 是…的 Structure — Emphasizing Circumstances

Used to emphasize the time, place, manner, or agent of a **past** action.

| Pattern | Example | English |
|---------|---------|---------|
| 是 + [Emphasis] + V + 的 | 他是昨天来的。| He came (just) yesterday. (emphasis on time) |
| 是 + [Emphasis] + V + 的 | 我是在北京学的中文。| I learned Chinese (specifically) in Beijing. |
| 是 + [Emphasis] + V + 的 | 这是谁写的？| Who wrote this? (whose writing is this?) |

## 2. Complex Conjunctions

### 2.1 不但…而且 — Not Only … But Also

| Pattern | Example | English |
|---------|---------|---------|
| 不但 + A，而且 + B | 他不但会中文，而且会日语。| He not only knows Chinese but also Japanese. |
| 不仅…还 | 她不仅聪明，还很勤奋。| She is not only smart but also hardworking. |

### 2.2 虽然…却/但是 — Although … Yet

| Pattern | Example | English |
|---------|---------|---------|
| 虽然 + Concession, 却 + Contrast | 虽然累，却很开心。| Although tired, (I am) still happy. |
| 尽管…仍然 | 尽管下雨，仍然来了。| Even though it rained, (I) still came. |

### 2.3 既…又 — Both … And

| Pattern | Example | English |
|---------|---------|---------|
| 既 + A + 又 + B | 这道菜既好吃又便宜。| This dish is both delicious and cheap. |
| 又…又 | 他又高又帅。| He is both tall and handsome. |

### 2.4 宁可/宁愿…也 — Would Rather … Than

| Pattern | Example | English |
|---------|---------|---------|
| 宁可 + A + 也 + B | 宁可早到，也不迟到。| Would rather arrive early than be late. |
| 宁愿 + A + 也不 + B | 宁愿一个人，也不将就。| Would rather be alone than settle. |

## 3. Topic-Comment Sentences

The topic is placed first to establish what is being discussed, followed by a comment about it.

### 3.1 Basic Topic-Comment

| Pattern | Example | English |
|---------|---------|---------|
| Topic + Comment | 那本书，我看完了。| That book — I've finished reading it. |
| Topic + Comment | 中文，他学了三年了。| Chinese — he has studied it for three years. |
| Topic + Comment | 这件事，我不太清楚。| This matter — I'm not very clear about it. |

### 3.2 Double-Subject Sentences

| Pattern | Example | English |
|---------|---------|---------|
| S1 + S2 + VP | 那栋楼，门坏了。| That building — the door is broken. |
| S1 + S2 + Adj | 大象，鼻子很长。| An elephant — its trunk is very long. |

## 4. The 越…越 Pattern

### 4.1 越来越 — More and More

| Pattern | Example | English |
|---------|---------|---------|
| 越来越 + Adj | 天气越来越冷。| The weather is getting colder and colder. |
| 越来越 + V | 他越来越不想上班。| He wants to go to work less and less. |

### 4.2 越…越 — The More … The More

| Pattern | Example | English |
|---------|---------|---------|
| 越 + V + 越 + Adj | 越学越有意思。| The more you study, the more interesting it becomes. |
| 越 + Adj + 越 + V | 越贵越不想买。| The more expensive, the less I want to buy. |

## 5. Formal Negation Patterns

### 5.1 不…也不 — Neither … Nor

| Pattern | Example | English |
|---------|---------|---------|
| 不 A + 也不 B | 他不吃也不喝。| He neither eats nor drinks. |

### 5.2 非…不可 — Must; Cannot Help But

| Pattern | Example | English |
|---------|---------|---------|
| 非 + V + 不可 | 这件事非做不可。| This matter must be done (no getting around it). |
| 非要 + V | 他非要去。| He insists on going. |

### 5.3 无论/不管…都/也 — No Matter … Still

| Pattern | Example | English |
|---------|---------|---------|
| 无论 + Condition + 都 | 无论多忙，都要吃饭。| No matter how busy, one must eat. |
| 不管 + Condition + 也 | 不管他说什么，我也不信。| No matter what he says, I won't believe it. |
| 无论…还是… + 都 | 无论刮风还是下雨，都要来。| Whether it's windy or rainy, (you) must come. |

## 6. Expressing Regret and Wishful Thinking

### 6.1 要是/如果…就好了 — If Only

| Pattern | Example | English |
|---------|---------|---------|
| 要是 + Condition + 就好了 | 要是我有钱就好了。| If only I had money. |
| 如果…多好啊 | 如果你在就多好啊。| How great it would be if you were here. |

### 6.2 早知道…就 — If I Had Known Earlier

| Pattern | Example | English |
|---------|---------|---------|
| 早知道 + Condition + 就 + Result | 早知道这么难，就不来了。| If I had known it was so hard, I wouldn't have come. |

## 7. Purpose and Goal Expressions

### 7.1 为了 — In Order To / For the Purpose of

| Pattern | Example | English |
|---------|---------|---------|
| 为了 + Goal + V | 为了减肥，她每天跑步。| In order to lose weight, she runs every day. |
| 为了 + Person/Thing | 为了家人，他努力工作。| For his family, he works hard. |

### 7.2 以便 — So As To / For the Convenience of

| Pattern | Example | English |
|---------|---------|---------|
| V + 以便 + Goal | 请早点通知我，以便我安排时间。| Please notify me early so that I can arrange my schedule. |

## 8. Concession with 倒是/是

### 8.1 倒是 — Admittedly / True, But…

| Pattern | Example | English |
|---------|---------|---------|
| Adj + 倒是 + Adj, 但是… | 便宜倒是便宜，但质量不好。| It is cheap, but the quality is poor. |
| Statement + 倒是 | 他倒是来了，可没说话。| He did come, but didn't say anything. |

## 9. Pivot and Causative Sentences

### 9.1 让/叫/使 + Person + V — Causatives

| Verb | Register | Example | English |
|------|----------|---------|---------|
| 让 | neutral | 老师让我重写。| The teacher made me rewrite. |
| 叫 | colloquial | 妈妈叫我回家。| Mom told me to come home. |
| 使 | formal | 这使我很感动。| This moved me deeply. |
| 令 | formal/written | 这令人惊讶。| This is surprising. |

## 11. Time Expressions and Tense

### 11.1 已经…了 — Already

| Pattern | Example | English |
|---------|---------|---------|
| 已经 + V + 了 | 他已经离开了。| He has already left. |
| 都 + 已经 + V + 了 | 都已经十点了。| It's already 10 o'clock. |

### 11.2 快/快要/将要…了 — About To

| Pattern | Example | English |
|---------|---------|---------|
| 快 + V + 了 | 快下课了。| Class is about to end. |
| 快要 + V + 了 | 我快要毕业了。| I am about to graduate. |
| 将要 + V | 他将要出发。| He is about to depart. (formal) |

### 11.3 曾经 — Once (in the past)

| Pattern | Example | English |
|---------|---------|---------|
| 曾经 + V + 过 | 我曾经去过那里。| I once went there. |

## 12. Emphasis Structures

### 12.1 连…都/也 — Even … (Still/Also)

| Pattern | Example | English |
|---------|---------|---------|
| 连 + N + 都 + V | 连孩子都知道。| Even children know (it). |
| 连 + V + 都 + V-Neg | 连吃饭都没时间。| No time even to eat. |

### 12.2 就 for Emphasis / Restriction

| Usage | Example | English |
|-------|---------|---------|
| Restriction | 就这一个。| Just this one. |
| Emphasis on condition | 我就是不去。| I simply refuse to go. |

## 13. Sentence-Final Particles

| Particle | Tone | Function | Example |
|----------|------|----------|---------|
| 了 | — | change of state | 下雨了。It's raining now. |
| 吧 | — | suggestion / assumption | 走吧。Let's go. / 你是老师吧？|
| 呢 | — | ongoing state / mild question | 他呢？Where is he? / 还在呢。Still here. |
| 嘛 | — | "obviously" / explanation | 他不来嘛，你知道的。Obviously he won't come. |
| 啊 | — | surprise, exclamation, softening | 真漂亮啊！So beautiful! |
