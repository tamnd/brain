Mandarin Chinese is spoken by ~1.1 billion native speakers. This guide covers everything from zero to C2 — HSK vocabulary, grammar patterns, all six skills (listening, speaking, reading, writing, tones, characters), and the best apps, textbooks, and resources.

## Quick Start (Week 1)

1. Install **[Pleco](https://www.pleco.com)** — the essential Chinese dictionary app (free, iOS/Android)
2. Learn **[Pinyin](skills/pinyin/)** — the romanization system; 2–4 weeks of focused study
3. Master **[the 4 tones](skills/tones/)** — non-negotiable from day 1
4. Start **HelloChinese** or **Integrated Chinese** for structured beginner lessons
5. Set up **Anki** with an HSK vocabulary deck — 15 min/day builds long-term retention

## The Two HSK Standards

| | HSK 2.0 (Old) | HSK 3.0 (New) |
|---|---|---|
| **Levels** | 1–6 | 1–9 |
| **Valid until** | June 30, 2026 | July 1, 2026 onwards |
| **Total vocab** | ~5,000 words | ~11,092 words |
| **Grammar points** | — | 572 points |
| **Characters (recog.)** | ~2,500 | 3,088 |
| **Highest CEFR** | C1–C2 | C2 |

## Time to Each Level

| HSK Level | Hours | CEFR | Description |
|-----------|-------|------|-------------|
| HSK 1 | 75–150 | A1 | Basic greetings, 299 words |
| HSK 2 | 150–300 | A1+ | Daily conversations, 498 words |
| HSK 3 | 300–600 | A2 | Familiar topics, 997 words |
| HSK 4 | 600–1,200 | B1 | Wide discussion range, 1,997 words |
| HSK 5 | 1,200–1,800 | B2 | Newspapers, professional contexts, 3,597 words |
| HSK 6 | 1,800–2,500 | C1 | Complex discourse, 5,397 words |
| HSK 7–9 | 3,000–5,000+ | C2 | Near-native, 10,877 words |

*Hours assume adult learner, English speaker, 10 hrs/week study.*

## Core Tool Stack

The minimum viable toolkit for any level:

- **Pleco** — dictionary, OCR camera lookup, flashcards
- **Anki** — spaced repetition for vocabulary (HSK 3.0 deck)
- **Chinese Grammar Wiki** — [resources.allsetlearning.com](https://resources.allsetlearning.com/chinese/grammar/) — free reference for all 572 grammar points
- **HelloChinese** or **Integrated Chinese** — structured course
- **Mandarin Companion** or **Du Chinese** — graded reading
- **Comprehensible Chinese** (YouTube) — input-based listening
