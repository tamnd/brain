Example sentences are the most effective way to learn both grammar and vocabulary simultaneously. A single well-chosen sentence teaches word meaning, grammatical context, tonal environment, and natural usage all at once. Grammar rules are generalizations — sentences are the data.

**For Anki mining.** Any sentence here can become an Anki card. Copy the Chinese sentence to the front; add pinyin + translation to the back.

## Sentences by Grammar Pattern

### 把 sentences (disposal construction)

把 (bǎ) moves the object before the verb to indicate how the object is "disposed of" — acted upon, changed, moved, or completed.

Structure: **Subject + 把 + Object + Verb + Complement**

| Chinese | Pinyin | English |
|---------|--------|---------|
| 我把书放在桌子上。 | Wǒ bǎ shū fàng zài zhuōzi shàng. | I put the book on the table. |
| 请把门关上。 | Qǐng bǎ mén guān shàng. | Please close the door. |
| 他把饭吃完了。 | Tā bǎ fàn chī wán le. | He finished eating the food. |
| 我把作业做完了。 | Wǒ bǎ zuòyè zuò wán le. | I finished doing the homework. |
| 她把手机落在家里了。 | Tā bǎ shǒujī là zài jiā lǐ le. | She left her phone at home. |

---

### 被 sentences (passive construction)

被 (bèi) marks the passive voice — the subject receives the action.

Structure: **Subject + 被 + (Agent) + Verb + Complement**

| Chinese | Pinyin | English |
|---------|--------|---------|
| 书被他拿走了。 | Shū bèi tā ná zǒu le. | The book was taken away by him. |
| 他被老师表扬了。 | Tā bèi lǎoshī biǎoyáng le. | He was praised by the teacher. |
| 自行车被偷了。 | Zìxíngchē bèi tōu le. | The bicycle was stolen. |
| 这个消息被大家知道了。 | Zhège xiāoxi bèi dàjiā zhīdào le. | This news became known to everyone. |
| 他被蚊子咬了。 | Tā bèi wénzi yǎo le. | He was bitten by a mosquito. |

---

### 比 sentences (comparison)

比 (bǐ) is used for direct comparisons.

Structure: **A + 比 + B + Adjective** (A is more [adjective] than B)

| Chinese | Pinyin | English |
|---------|--------|---------|
| 他比我高。 | Tā bǐ wǒ gāo. | He is taller than me. |
| 今天比昨天冷。 | Jīntiān bǐ zuótiān lěng. | Today is colder than yesterday. |
| 这里比那里贵多了。 | Zhèlǐ bǐ nàlǐ guì duō le. | This place is much more expensive than that one. |
| 苹果比香蕉甜。 | Píngguǒ bǐ xiāngjiāo tián. | Apples are sweeter than bananas. |
| 坐地铁比开车快。 | Zuò dìtiě bǐ kāi chē kuài. | Taking the subway is faster than driving. |

---

### 连...都/也 (even...also/even)

Emphasizes that something unexpected or extreme applies.

Structure: **连 + [thing] + 都/也 + [verb phrase]**

| Chinese | Pinyin | English |
|---------|--------|---------|
| 连小孩都知道。 | Lián xiǎohái dōu zhīdào. | Even children know this. |
| 他连饭都没吃。 | Tā lián fàn dōu méi chī. | He didn't even eat. |
| 连他也来了。 | Lián tā yě lái le. | Even he came. |
| 我连他的名字都不知道。 | Wǒ lián tā de míngzì dōu bù zhīdào. | I don't even know his name. |
| 连一点时间都没有。 | Lián yīdiǎn shíjiān dōu méiyǒu. | There isn't even a little time. |

---

### 越来越 (more and more)

Expresses a continuous increase or intensification.

Structure: **越来越 + Adjective/Verb**

| Chinese | Pinyin | English |
|---------|--------|---------|
| 天气越来越热。 | Tiānqì yuèlái yuè rè. | The weather is getting hotter and hotter. |
| 他的汉语越来越好了。 | Tā de hànyǔ yuèlái yuè hǎo le. | His Chinese is getting better and better. |
| 我越来越喜欢中国菜。 | Wǒ yuèlái yuè xǐhuān zhōngguó cài. | I like Chinese food more and more. |
| 城市越来越大。 | Chéngshì yuèlái yuè dà. | Cities are getting bigger and bigger. |
| 问题越来越复杂。 | Wèntí yuèlái yuè fùzá. | The problem is getting more and more complex. |

---

### 虽然...但是 (although...but)

Concessive structure: acknowledges one fact while asserting a contrasting one.

| Chinese | Pinyin | English |
|---------|--------|---------|
| 虽然很累，但是他还是去了。 | Suīrán hěn lèi, dànshì tā háishi qù le. | Although he was tired, he still went. |
| 虽然下雨，但是我们还是出发了。 | Suīrán xià yǔ, dànshì wǒmen háishi chūfā le. | Although it was raining, we still set off. |
| 虽然贵，但是质量很好。 | Suīrán guì, dànshì zhìliàng hěn hǎo. | Although it's expensive, the quality is very good. |
| 虽然我不喜欢，但是我还是吃了。 | Suīrán wǒ bù xǐhuān, dànshì wǒ háishi chī le. | Although I don't like it, I still ate it. |

---

### 因为...所以 (because...therefore)

Causal structure: states reason then result.

| Chinese | Pinyin | English |
|---------|--------|---------|
| 因为下雨，所以我带了伞。 | Yīnwèi xià yǔ, suǒyǐ wǒ dài le sǎn. | Because it was raining, I brought an umbrella. |
| 因为太贵，所以我没买。 | Yīnwèi tài guì, suǒyǐ wǒ méi mǎi. | Because it was too expensive, I didn't buy it. |
| 因为他努力学习，所以成绩很好。 | Yīnwèi tā nǔlì xuéxí, suǒyǐ chéngjì hěn hǎo. | Because he studied hard, his grades are excellent. |
| 因为堵车，所以迟到了。 | Yīnwèi dǔchē, suǒyǐ chídào le. | Because of traffic, I was late. |

---

### 如果...就 (if...then)

Conditional structure.

| Chinese | Pinyin | English |
|---------|--------|---------|
| 如果你来，我就很高兴。 | Rúguǒ nǐ lái, wǒ jiù hěn gāoxìng. | If you come, I'll be very happy. |
| 如果下雨，就不去了。 | Rúguǒ xià yǔ, jiù bù qù le. | If it rains, we won't go. |
| 如果你有问题，就告诉我。 | Rúguǒ nǐ yǒu wèntí, jiù gàosu wǒ. | If you have questions, tell me. |
| 如果明天有时间，我就去看你。 | Rúguǒ míngtiān yǒu shíjiān, wǒ jiù qù kàn nǐ. | If I have time tomorrow, I'll come see you. |
