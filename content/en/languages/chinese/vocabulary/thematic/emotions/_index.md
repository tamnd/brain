## Expressing Emotional States (表达情绪 biǎodá qíngxù)

### Degree Modifiers

| Chinese | Pinyin | English | Usage |
|---------|--------|---------|-------|
| 很 | hěn | very | 我很高兴。Very happy. |
| 非常 | fēicháng | extremely | 我非常感动。Extremely moved. |
| 有点儿 | yǒudiǎnr | a little / somewhat | 我有点儿担心。A little worried. |
| 有些 | yǒuxiē | somewhat / rather | 我有些失望。Somewhat disappointed. |
| 特别 | tèbié | especially / particularly | 我特别开心！Particularly happy! |
| 太...了 | tài...le | too / way too | 太好了！Too great! |

### Common Patterns

| Pattern | Example | English |
|---------|---------|---------|
| 我很 + emotion | 我很高兴。| I'm very happy. |
| 我感到 + emotion | 我感到很孤独。| I feel very lonely. |
| 让我感到 + emotion | 这让我感到感动。| This makes me feel touched. |
| 我有点儿 + emotion | 我有点儿担心。| I'm a little worried. |
| 为...感到 + emotion | 我为你感到高兴。| I'm happy for you. |
