## Clock Time (时间表达 shíjiān biǎodá)

| Chinese | Pinyin | English | Example |
|---------|--------|---------|---------|
| 点 | diǎn | o'clock | 三点 = 3 o'clock |
| 分 | fēn | minute | 三点二十分 = 3:20 |
| 秒 | miǎo | second | 三十秒 = 30 seconds |
| 半 | bàn | half | 三点半 = 3:30 |
| 差 | chà | less than (used before time) | 差五分三点 = 2:55 (5 minutes to 3) |
| 上午 | shàngwǔ | morning / AM (before noon) | 上午九点开会。Meeting at 9am. |
| 下午 | xiàwǔ | afternoon / PM | 下午两点见。See you at 2pm. |
| 早上 | zǎoshang | morning (early) | 早上好！Good morning! |
| 中午 | zhōngwǔ | noon / midday | 中午我去吃午饭。I'll go for lunch at noon. |
| 晚上 | wǎnshang | evening / night | 晚上你有空吗？Are you free tonight? |
| 凌晨 | língchén | in the early morning (midnight–5am) | 凌晨三点到的。Arrived at 3am. |

**Time structure:** 上午/下午 + hour + 点 + minutes + 分
- 上午八点三十分 = 8:30 AM
- 下午三点一刻 = 3:15 PM (一刻 = quarter hour)

## Key Time Grammar Patterns

**Asking time:**
- 现在几点了？(Xiànzài jǐ diǎn le?) — What time is it now?
- 几点开始/结束？(Jǐ diǎn kāishǐ/jiéshù?) — What time does it start/end?

**Time words go before the verb:**
- 我明天去。✓ (I'm going tomorrow.)
- ~~我去明天。~~✗

**Duration comes after the verb:**
- 我学了两年中文。(I've studied Chinese for 2 years.)
- 他睡了八个小时。(He slept for 8 hours.)

**Date format:** Year + Month + Day (大→小)
- 2026年5月30日 = May 30, 2026
- 二零二六年五月三十日
