## Useful Phrases (实用句子 shíyòng jùzi)

| Phrase | Pinyin | English |
|--------|--------|---------|
| 我在...公司工作。| Wǒ zài... gōngsī gōngzuò. | I work at... company. |
| 我是...（职位）。| Wǒ shì... | I am a... (job title). |
| 我们什么时候开会？| Wǒmen shénme shíhou kāihuì? | When are we having the meeting? |
| 请把报告发给我。| Qǐng bǎ bàogào fā gěi wǒ. | Please send me the report. |
| 我需要请假。| Wǒ xūyào qǐngjià. | I need to take leave. |
| 有什么我可以帮到你的？| Yǒu shénme wǒ kěyǐ bāng dào nǐ de? | Is there anything I can help you with? |
| 这个项目什么时候截止？| Zhège xiàngmù shénme shíhou jiézhǐ? | What's the deadline for this project? |
