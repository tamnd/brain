## Key Frequency Facts

- Top **1,000 characters** cover ~90% of common written Chinese text
- Top **500 words** cover ~75% of basic conversation
- Functional literacy (newspaper): ~2,000–3,000 characters
- HSK 6 (~5,400 words) ≈ educated adult reading level for most modern Chinese

## Frequency Sources

- **SUBTLEX-CH** (Cai & Brysbaert 2010): frequency from ~33M word subtitle corpus
- **Jun Da**: character frequency from ~3M character news/literature corpus
- **CC-CEDICT**: 120,000+ dictionary entries (basis for word lists)
