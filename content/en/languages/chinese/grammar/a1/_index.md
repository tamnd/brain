40 grammar patterns at HSK 1–2 / CEFR A1 level.

| # | Pattern | Structure | Example ZH | Pinyin | English | Note |
|---|---------|-----------|-----------|--------|---------|------|
| 1 | **是 copula** | S + 是 + N | 我是学生。 | Wǒ shì xuésheng. | I am a student. | Only before nouns, NOT adjectives |
| 2 | **Adjective predicate** | S + 很/太/真 + Adj | 她很高。 | Tā hěn gāo. | She is tall. | No 是 needed; bare adj implies comparison |
| 3 | **有 possession** | S + 有 + Object | 我有一本书。 | Wǒ yǒu yī běn shū. | I have a book. | Negated with 没有, never 不有 |
| 4 | **有 existence** | Place + 有 + Thing | 桌子上有书。 | Zhuōzi shàng yǒu shū. | There is a book on the table. | Place first, then 有, then thing |
| 5 | **不 negation** | S + 不 + V/Adj | 我不是老师。 | Wǒ bú shì lǎoshī. | I am not a teacher. | 不 → bú before 4th-tone syllable |
| 6 | **没有 negation** | S + 没有 + Object/V | 我没有钱。 | Wǒ méiyǒu qián. | I don't have money. | Negates 有 and past actions |
| 7 | **吗 yes/no question** | Statement + 吗？ | 你是学生吗？ | Nǐ shì xuésheng ma? | Are you a student? | Add 吗 to statement; no word-order change |
| 8 | **呢 follow-up question** | Noun/Pronoun + 呢？ | 我很好，你呢？ | Wǒ hěn hǎo, nǐ ne? | I'm fine, and you? | Continues conversation topic |
| 9 | **Basic SVO** | S + V + O | 我喜欢中文。 | Wǒ xǐhuān Zhōngwén. | I like Chinese. | Default word order |
| 10 | **什么 (what)** | 什么 + N / V + 什么 | 你叫什么名字？ | Nǐ jiào shénme míngzi? | What is your name? | Question word stays in place |
| 11 | **哪里/哪儿 (where)** | 在 + 哪里/哪儿？ | 你在哪里？ | Nǐ zài nǎlǐ? | Where are you? | 哪儿 is more colloquial/Beijing |
| 12 | **谁 (who)** | 谁 + V / S + 是 + 谁 | 他是谁？ | Tā shì shéi? | Who is he? | 谁 in same position as the answer |
| 13 | **怎么 (how)** | 怎么 + V | 你怎么去？ | Nǐ zěnme qù? | How are you going? | Asks about manner/method |
| 14 | **多少/几 (how many)** | 几/多少 + MW + N | 你有几本书？ | Nǐ yǒu jǐ běn shū? | How many books do you have? | 几 < ~10; 多少 for any amount |
| 15 | **的 possessive** | Pronoun/N + 的 + N | 我的书 | wǒ de shū | my book | 的 can be omitted with close relations: 我妈 |
| 16 | **Measure words** | Num + MW + N | 一个人；两本书 | yī gè rén; liǎng běn shū | one person; two books | 两 (not 二) before MW for quantity |
| 17 | **Time before verb** | S + Time + V + O | 我明天去北京。 | Wǒ míngtiān qù Běijīng. | I'm going to Beijing tomorrow. | Time before place before verb |
| 18 | **Place with 在** | S + 在 + Place | 我在学校。 | Wǒ zài xuéxiào. | I am at school. | 在 as main verb = "be at" |
| 19 | **Location before verb** | S + 在 + Place + V | 我在图书馆学习。 | Wǒ zài túshūguǎn xuéxí. | I study at the library. | 在-phrase as adverbial before verb |
| 20 | **了 completion** | S + V + 了 (+ O) | 我吃了。 | Wǒ chī le. | I ate. | Negated with 没 (drop 了); not strictly past tense |
| 21 | **要 want/will** | S + 要 + V | 我要喝水。 | Wǒ yào hē shuǐ. | I want to / will drink water. | Context clarifies want vs future |
| 22 | **想 would like** | S + 想 + V | 我想去中国。 | Wǒ xiǎng qù Zhōngguó. | I would like to go to China. | Softer than 要; expresses wish |
| 23 | **可以 can/may** | S + 可以 + V + 吗？ | 我可以进来吗？ | Wǒ kěyǐ jìnlái ma? | May I come in? | Permission or possibility |
| 24 | **会 know how/will** | S + 会 + V | 我会说中文。 | Wǒ huì shuō Zhōngwén. | I can speak Chinese. | Learned skill; also future inference |
| 25 | **也 also/too** | S + 也 + V/Adj | 我也是学生。 | Wǒ yě shì xuésheng. | I am also a student. | Must come before verb/adj, not end |
| 26 | **都 all/both** | S(plural) + 都 + V | 他们都是学生。 | Tāmen dōu shì xuésheng. | They are all students. | Comes before verb; subject must be plural |
| 27 | **很 very** | S + 很 + Adj | 这本书很有意思。 | Zhè běn shū hěn yǒu yìsi. | This book is very interesting. | Bare adj without 很 implies comparison |
| 28 | **太…了 too** | 太 + Adj + 了 | 太贵了！ | Tài guì le! | Too expensive! | 了 required for "too" meaning |
| 29 | **还是 or (questions)** | A + 还是 + B？ | 你喝茶还是咖啡？ | Nǐ hē chá háishì kāfēi? | Tea or coffee? | Choice question; answer selects one |
| 30 | **或者 or (statements)** | A + 或者 + B | 你可以坐公共汽车或者地铁。 | Nǐ kěyǐ zuò gōnggòng qìchē huòzhě dìtiě. | You can take a bus or subway. | In statements/suggestions, not questions |
| 31 | **A不A question** | V + 不 + V / Adj + 不 + Adj | 你是不是学生？ | Nǐ shì bu shì xuésheng? | Are you a student or not? | Informal yes/no question; no 吗 needed |
| 32 | **Numbers + measure words** | Num + MW + N | 三个人 | sān gè rén | three people | 个 is default MW when unsure |
| 33 | **Ordinal 第** | 第 + Num (+ MW) + N | 第一名 | dì yī míng | first place | 第 prefix makes ordinal |
| 34 | **这/那 demonstratives** | 这/那 (+ MW) + N | 这是什么？ | Zhè shì shénme? | What is this? | 这 near speaker; 那 further away |
| 35 | **极了 extremely** | Adj + 极了 | 好极了！ | Hǎo jí le! | Excellent! | Stronger than 太…了; always positive feel |
| 36 | **Subject omission** | (S omitted) + V | 去。 | Qù. | (I'm) going. | Subject dropped when clear from context |
| 37 | **Double object** | S + V + IO + DO | 我给他一本书。 | Wǒ gěi tā yī běn shū. | I gave him a book. | Indirect object before direct object |
| 38 | **Direction complements** | V + 来/去 | 进来；出去 | jìnlái; chūqù | come in; go out | 来 toward speaker; 去 away from speaker |
| 39 | **Duration** | S + V + 了 + Duration | 我学了两年。 | Wǒ xué le liǎng nián. | I studied for two years. | Duration after verb (with 了) |
| 40 | **Age expressions** | S + Num + 岁 | 我二十岁。 | Wǒ èrshí suì. | I am 20 years old. | No 是 or 有 needed; 岁 follows number |
