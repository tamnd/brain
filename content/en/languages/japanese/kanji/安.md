## 安

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Meaning | cheap, peaceful |
| Strokes | 6 |
| JLPT Level | N5 |
| On-reading (音読み) | アン |
| Kun-reading (訓読み) | やす-い |
| Radical | 宀 (roof / kanmuri) [U+5B80] |

## Stroke Order

The stroke order for 安 follows the standard rule of top-to-bottom and left-to-right.

1. **Stroke 1**: The dot at the top center.
2. **Stroke 2**: The horizontal line starting from the left, turning into a downward stroke on the right (the roof).
3. **Stroke 3**: The dot on the left side inside the roof.
4. **Stroke 4**: The horizontal line inside the roof.
5. **Stroke 5**: The vertical line descending from the center of the horizontal line.
6. **Stroke 6**: The long horizontal base line at the bottom.

## Radical Analysis

The radical is **宀 (u-kanmuri)**, which represents a **roof**. 
The bottom component is **女 (onna)**, meaning **woman**. 
**Mnemonic logic**: The image is of a woman sitting peacefully under a roof. When a woman is safe and settled under a roof, she is at peace. Furthermore, if a household is peaceful and stable, life is considered "cheap" or affordable because there is no conflict or chaos to pay for.

## Mnemonic

*   **The "Peaceful Home" Mnemonic**: Imagine a woman (女) sitting calmly under a roof (宀). She is completely at peace. Because she is at peace, she doesn't need to buy expensive things to feel happy; she is content with "cheap" (安) and simple living.

## Common Confusion Pairs

| Kanji | Reading | Meaning | How to distinguish |
|-------|---------|---------|-------------------|
| **安** | アン / やす | Cheap/Peace | Has the 女 (woman) radical at the bottom. |
| **宴** | エン | Banquet | Has the 宀 (roof) but a different bottom (日 + 女). It means a party/feast. |
| **案** | アン | Plan/Idea | Has the 木 (tree) radical at the bottom. It means a proposal or draft. |

## Usage Frequency Note

**安** is an extremely high-frequency kanji. It is one of the first kanji learned by students (N5 level) and appears daily in news, advertisements, and casual conversation. Whether you are talking about the price of goods (安い) or the state of one's mind (安心), this kanji is essential for basic communication in Japanese.
