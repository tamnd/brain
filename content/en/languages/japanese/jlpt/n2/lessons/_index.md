N2 corresponds to B2 level — approximately 1,200–2,200 study hours from zero. At this level you can read newspaper articles, follow formal speech, and handle most real-world written Japanese. These lessons focus on the grammar patterns, conjunctions, and reading strategies that distinguish N2 from N3.

## How to Use

Work through lessons in order — later lessons build on vocabulary and patterns introduced earlier. Each lesson includes example sentences, pattern drills, and a short quiz. Aim for one lesson per week alongside regular reading practice.
