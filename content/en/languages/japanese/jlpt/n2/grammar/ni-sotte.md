## Pattern

**〜に沿って / 〜に沿った**
(ni sotte / ni sotta)

## Structure

- **Noun + に沿って** (followed by verb)
- **Noun + に沿った + Noun** (attributive — modifying a noun)

## Formality Level

**Formal to neutral** — used in business planning, policy documents, academic discourse. Also used in everyday formal speech.

## Meaning

"In line with X," "along X," "following the course of X." Expresses that an action proceeds consistently with a pre-defined direction, plan, policy, or guideline. Can also mean physically following a path.

The key nuance is **alignment with a pre-established direction** — the focus is on staying on course or consistent with something, rather than obeying it as a rule (〜に従って).

## Examples

| Japanese | Reading | Romaji | English |
|----------|---------|--------|---------|
| 計画に沿って、プロジェクトを進めています。 | けいかくにそって、プロジェクトをすすめています。 | keikaku ni sotte, purojekuto o susumete imasu. | We are proceeding with the project in line with the plan. |
| 新しいガイドラインに沿った対応をお願いします。 | あたらしいガイドラインにそったたいおうをおねがいします。 | atarashii gaidorain ni sotta taiō o onegai shimasu. | Please respond in accordance with the new guidelines. |
| 方針に沿って業務を進めることが重要です。 | ほうしんにそってぎょうむをすすめることがじゅうようです。 | hōshin ni sotte gyōmu o susumeru koto ga jūyō desu. | It is important to carry out work in line with policy. |
| 川に沿って歩いて行きました。 | かわにそってあるいていきました。 | kawa ni sotte aruite ikimashita. | I walked along the river. |
| 顧客のニーズに沿った製品開発が求められる。 | こきゃくのニーズにそったせいひんかいはつがもとめられる。 | kokyaku no nīzu ni sotta seihin kaihatsu ga motomerareru. | Product development in line with customer needs is required. |

## Comparison Table: The Four "Based on / Following" Patterns

| Pattern | Core Meaning | Basis Type | Test Question |
|---------|-------------|-----------|---------------|
| 〜に基づいて | Derived from objective foundation | Evidence, law, data | "Is the basis authoritative/factual?" |
| 〜に従って | Compliance with authority | Rules, orders, instructions | "Is there an authority being obeyed?" |
| 〜に沿って | Alignment with direction | Plans, policies, paths, guidelines | "Is this staying on a pre-set course?" |
| 〜をもとに | Using as raw material | Ideas, events, data → new creation | "Is something new being created from it?" |

## Common Mistakes

- ❌ 規則に沿って行動する → More natural: 規則に従って行動する (rules are obeyed, not just "followed as a path")
- ❌ 実績をもとに評価する vs 実績に基づいて評価する — both acceptable; 実績をもとに is more creative/interpretive, 実績に基づいて is more objective/systematic
- ✓ 方針に沿った判断 (judgment that stays aligned with policy) — correct use
