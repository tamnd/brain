## Stroke Order

1. Short horizontal-diagonal stroke (hand, ナ-like). 2-3. ナ completion. 4-5. 口 (mouth) at bottom.

## Mnemonic

A hand holding food to the mouth (口) — the right hand feeds you.

## Common Mistakes

右 (right) vs 左 (left) — 右 has 口 (mouth) at bottom; 左 has 工 (work).
