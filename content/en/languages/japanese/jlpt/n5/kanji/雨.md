## Stroke Order

1-2. Top horizontal and left side of frame. 3. Right side. 4. Bottom horizontal (closing box). 5-8. Four dots inside (raindrops).

## Mnemonic

A cloud frame (box on top) with raindrops (dots) falling inside — rain.

## Common Mistakes

雨 (rain) vs 雲 (cloud) — 雲 has 雨 on top with 云 (say/cloud) below it.
