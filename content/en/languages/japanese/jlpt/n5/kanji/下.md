## Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| On (音) | カ/ゲ | ka/ge | Chinese-origin (音読み) |
| Kun (訓) | した/しも/もと/さ-がる/くだ-る/おろ-す | shita/shimo/moto/sa-garu/kuda-ru/oro-su | below; lower; to descend |

## Meanings

1. below
2. lower
3. down
4. descend

## Stroke Order

1. Longer horizontal stroke (base). 2. Short horizontal stroke above base. 3. Short vertical hanging down.

## Mnemonic

A line hanging below a baseline — indicating something below.

## Common Mistakes

下 (below) vs 上 (above) — mirror concept; 下 has the dangling stroke going downward.
