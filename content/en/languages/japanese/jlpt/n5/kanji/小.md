## Readings

## Meanings

1. small
2. little

## Stroke Order

1. Central vertical with downward hook. 2. Left dot. 3. Right dot.

## Mnemonic

A small thing with tiny dots on each side — smallness, tiny elements.

## Common Mistakes

小 (small) vs 少 (few/little) — 少 has a diagonal added; 小 is the pure small-dots form.
