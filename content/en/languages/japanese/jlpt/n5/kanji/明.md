## Character Info

| Kanji | Strokes | JLPT | Radical | Radical Meaning |
|-------|---------|------|---------|-----------------|
| 明 | 8 | N5 | 日 | sun/day |

## Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| On (音) | メイ/ミョウ | mei/myou | Chinese-origin (音読み) |
| Kun (訓) | あか-るい/あ-かり/あ-く | aka-rui/a-kari/a-ku | bright; light up |

## Meanings

1. bright
2. clear
3. tomorrow
4. dawn

## Stroke Order

Left: 日 (sun, 4 strokes). Right: 月 (moon, 4 strokes).

## Mnemonic

Sun (日) and moon (月) together — both celestial lights make brightness.

## Common Mistakes

明日 has two readings: あした (casual) and あす (literary); both are correct at N5.
