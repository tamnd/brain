## Stroke Order

Left: 辶 (movement radical, 3 strokes). Right: 周 (circumference, 8 strokes) — top part then 用.

## Mnemonic

Moving (辶) around (周) in a cycle — a week is a regular circuit of seven days.

## Common Mistakes

週 vs 週 written with wrong inner components — the 周 part contains 吉 on top and 用 below.
