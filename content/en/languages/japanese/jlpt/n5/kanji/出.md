## Character Info

| Kanji | Strokes | JLPT | Radical | Radical Meaning |
|-------|---------|------|---------|-----------------|
| 出 | 5 | N5 | 凵 | container |

## Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| On (音) | シュツ/スイ | shutsu/sui | Chinese-origin (音読み) |
| Kun (訓) | で-る/だ-す | de-ru/da-su | to exit; to take out |

## Meanings

1. exit
2. go out
3. come out
4. take out

## Stroke Order

1. Left side of lower box. 2. Bottom of lower box. 3. Right of lower box. 4-5. Upper mountain/peak shape (山-like) on top.

## Mnemonic

Two mountains (山 stacked) — coming up and out like peaks emerging from a container.

## Common Mistakes

出 (exit) vs 山 (mountain) — 出 has two stacked mountain shapes; 山 has only one set of peaks.
