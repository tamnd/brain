## Meanings

1. now
2. present

## Stroke Order

1. Top diagonal 'roof' strokes (亼). 2-4. Lower 今-specific strokes.

## Mnemonic

A person under a roof — present tense, here and now.

## Common Mistakes

今 vs 令 (command/order) — both have similar tops; 今 is simpler with a distinct bottom hook.
