## Stroke Order

Left: 亲 (parent/close, 9 strokes). Right: 斤 (axe, 4 strokes).

## Mnemonic

An axe (斤) cutting a tree (亲-like plant) — creating something new from raw material.

## Common Mistakes

新 (new) vs 親 (parent/intimate) — 親 adds 見 (see) to the left component; 新 has 斤 on the right.
