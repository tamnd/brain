## Readings

| Kun (訓) | き/こ | ki/ko | tree; wood |

## Meanings

1. tree
2. wood
3. Thursday

## Stroke Order

1. Vertical stroke (trunk). 2. Horizontal stroke (branches). 3-4. Left and right lower strokes (roots).

## Mnemonic

A tree with a trunk, branches spreading horizontally, and roots below.

## Common Mistakes

木 (tree) vs 本 (root/book) — 本 has an extra line at the bottom marking the root; 末 has a line at the top (tip).
