## Stroke Order

1. Short diagonal stroke left. 2. Long horizontal stroke. 3. Long vertical stroke through the horizontal.

## Mnemonic

A person (人) standing on a horizontal line — 1,000 people on the horizon.

## Common Mistakes

千 (1,000) vs 干 (dry); 千 has a diagonal on top left, 干 does not.
