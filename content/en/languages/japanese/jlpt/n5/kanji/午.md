## Character Info

| Kanji | Strokes | JLPT | Radical | Radical Meaning |
|-------|---------|------|---------|-----------------|
| 午 | 4 | N5 | 十 | ten |

## Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| On (音) | ゴ | go | Chinese-origin (音読み) |

## Meanings

1. noon
2. horse (zodiac, 7th)

## Stroke Order

1. Short diagonal left top. 2. Long horizontal. 3. Short left stroke below. 4. Long vertical through middle.

## Mnemonic

Like the character 干 (dry) with an extra stroke — the sun at its peak, noon.

## Common Mistakes

午 vs 牛 (cow/ox) — 牛 has a short horizontal at the very top; 午 has a diagonal.
