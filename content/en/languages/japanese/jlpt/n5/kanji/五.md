## Stroke Order

1. Top horizontal. 2. Middle horizontal. 3. Left vertical down. 4. Bottom curve right.

## Mnemonic

The kanji resembles a hook or cane — think of a five-shaped numeral rotated.

## Common Mistakes

Do not confuse with 王 (king) which has a similar structure but different proportions.
