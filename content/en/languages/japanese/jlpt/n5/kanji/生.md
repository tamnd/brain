## Character Info

| Kanji | Strokes | JLPT | Radical | Radical Meaning |
|-------|---------|------|---------|-----------------|
| 生 | 5 | N5 | 生 | life/birth |

## Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| On (音) | セイ/ショウ | sei/shou | Chinese-origin (音読み) |
| Kun (訓) | い-きる/う-まれる/は-える/なま | i-kiru/u-mareru/ha-eru/nama | to live; to be born; raw |

## Meanings

1. life
2. birth
3. raw
4. student

## Stroke Order

1. Left slanted stroke. 2. Short horizontal above center. 3. Long horizontal middle. 4. Short horizontal lower. 5. Vertical stroke.

## Mnemonic

A plant growing upward — life springing from the earth.

## Common Mistakes

生 (life) vs 正 (correct) — similar appearance; 正 has a foot (止) at the bottom, 生 tapers differently.
