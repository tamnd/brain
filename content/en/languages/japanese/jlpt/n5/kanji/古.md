## Character Info

| 古 | 5 | N5 | 口 | mouth |

## Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| On (音) | コ | ko | Chinese-origin (音読み) |
| Kun (訓) | ふる-い/ふる-す | furu-i/furu-su | old (things) |

## Meanings

1. old
2. ancient

## Stroke Order

Top: 十 (ten, 2 strokes). Bottom: 口 (mouth, 3 strokes).

## Mnemonic

Ten (十) mouths (口) — stories passed down through ten generations, making something ancient.

## Common Mistakes

古 (old things) is used for inanimate objects; 老 (old/aged) is used for living beings.
