## Character Info

| Kanji | Strokes | JLPT | Radical | Radical Meaning |
|-------|---------|------|---------|-----------------|
| 安 | 6 | N5 | 宀 | roof |

## Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| On (音) | アン | an | Chinese-origin (音読み) |
| Kun (訓) | やす-い/やす-らか | yasu-i/yasu-raka | cheap; peaceful; safe |

## Meanings

1. cheap
2. safe
3. peaceful
4. easy

## Stroke Order

Top: 宀 (roof, 3 strokes). Bottom: 女 (woman, 3 strokes).

## Mnemonic

A woman (女) under a roof (宀) — safety and peace at home.

## Common Mistakes

安 (cheap/safe) vs 案 (idea/proposal) — 案 adds 木 (tree) at the bottom; 安 only has 女.
