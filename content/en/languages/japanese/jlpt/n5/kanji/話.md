## Character Info

| Kanji | Strokes | JLPT | Radical | Radical Meaning |
|-------|---------|------|---------|-----------------|
| 話 | 13 | N5 | 言 | speech/word |

## Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| On (音) | ワ | wa | Chinese-origin (音読み) |
| Kun (訓) | はな-す/はなし | hana-su/hanashi | to talk; talk/story |

## Meanings

1. talk
2. speak
3. story
4. conversation

## Stroke Order

Left: 言 (speech, 7 strokes). Right: 舌 (tongue, 6 strokes).

## Mnemonic

Words (言) from the tongue (舌) — talking, using language.

## Common Mistakes

話 (talk) vs 語 (language) — both have 言 on the left; 語 has 吾 (I/self) on the right.
