## Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |

| Kun (訓) | まる-い | maru-i | round; circle |

## Meanings

1. yen (currency)
2. circle
3. round

## Stroke Order

1. Left vertical. 2. Top horizontal. 3. Right vertical with small feet. 4. Inner horizontal close.

## Mnemonic

A simplified box — the traditional character 圓 was simplified to this box shape representing a coin.

## Common Mistakes

円 (yen/circle) vs 日 (sun/day) — 円 is taller and more rectangular than square-ish 日.
