## 南

**JLPT Level:** N5 | **Strokes:** 9 | **Radical:** 十 (ten)

### Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| On-yomi | ナン、ナ | nan, na | nan = south standard; na in some compounds |
| Kun-yomi | みなみ | minami | south |

### Meanings
1. South
2. Southern

### Vocabulary

### Stroke Order
1. Top horizontal stroke with vertical
2-5. 冂-like enclosure with inner strokes
6-9. Bottom components with crossing strokes

### Memory Aid
南 has a complex shape — imagine a compass needle pointing DOWN (south) with a complex rooftop structure above. The southern direction is warm (like the tropics), and the character feels "full" and warm.

### Common Confusion
南 (south) vs 楠 (camphor tree) — the kanji 南 appears as a component in some tree-related characters. Memorize 南 as "south" by its compass association.
