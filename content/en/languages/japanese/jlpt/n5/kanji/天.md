## Character Info

| Kanji | Strokes | JLPT | Radical | Radical Meaning |
|-------|---------|------|---------|-----------------|
| 天 | 4 | N5 | 大 | big |

## Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| On (音) | テン | ten | Chinese-origin (音読み) |
| Kun (訓) | あめ/あま | ame/ama | sky; heaven |

## Meanings

1. heaven
2. sky
3. celestial
4. nature

## Stroke Order

1. Short horizontal top stroke. 2. Long horizontal stroke below. 3-4. Two leg strokes (like 大 with a hat).

## Mnemonic

A person (大, big) with a line above their head — heaven, above humanity.

## Common Mistakes

天 (heaven) vs 大 (big) — 天 has an extra horizontal stroke above; 大 does not.
