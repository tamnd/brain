## Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| On (音) | ショク/ジキ | shoku/jiki | Chinese-origin (音読み) |
| Kun (訓) | た-べる/く-う | ta-beru/ku-u | to eat |

## Meanings

1. eat
2. food

## Stroke Order

Top: 亼 (roof/cover, 3 strokes). Middle: 一 (1 stroke). Bottom: 良-like component (5 strokes).

## Mnemonic

A roof over a person eating — shelter for the act of eating.

## Common Mistakes

食 (eat/food) vs 飲 (drink) — both are in the same semantic category; 飲 adds 欠 (yawn/lacking) on the right.
