## Character Info

| 空 | 8 | N5 | 穴 | hole/cave |

## Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| On (音) | クウ | kuu | Chinese-origin (音読み) |
| Kun (訓) | そら/から/す-く | sora/kara/su-ku | sky; empty; void |

## Meanings

1. sky
2. empty
3. air
4. void

## Stroke Order

Top: 穴 (hole/cave, 5 strokes). Bottom: 工 (work, 3 strokes).

## Mnemonic

A cave (穴) with work (工) done inside — empty space worked through, like sky.

## Common Mistakes

空 (sky/empty) vs 穴 (hole) — 空 has 工 at the bottom; 穴 alone does not.
