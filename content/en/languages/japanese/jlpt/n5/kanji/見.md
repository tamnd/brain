## Character Info

| Kanji | Strokes | JLPT | Radical | Radical Meaning |
|-------|---------|------|---------|-----------------|
| 見 | 7 | N5 | 見 | see |

## Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| On (音) | ケン | ken | Chinese-origin (音読み) |
| Kun (訓) | み-る/み-える/み-せる | mi-ru/mi-eru/mi-seru | to see; to look |

## Meanings

1. see
2. look
3. show

## Stroke Order

Top: 目 (eye, 5 strokes). Bottom: 儿 (human legs, 2 strokes).

## Mnemonic

An eye (目) on human legs (儿) — a person walking around looking at things.

## Common Mistakes

見 (see) vs 貝 (shellfish) — both have 目 on top, but 見 has 儿 (legs) underneath, 貝 has 八.
