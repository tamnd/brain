## Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| On (音) | コウ | kou | Chinese-origin (音読み) |
| Kun (訓) | たか-い/たか | taka-i/taka | tall; high; expensive |

## Meanings

1. tall
2. high
3. expensive

## Stroke Order

Top: 亠 (lid, 2 strokes). 口 (mouth). Then 冂 (enclosure). Then 口 again. Bottom cross strokes (5-10).

## Mnemonic

A tall tower with windows and a roof — elevated, high structure.

## Common Mistakes

高 (tall/expensive) vs 商 (commerce) — both have similar vertical tower structures; check the bottom components.
