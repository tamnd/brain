## Stroke Order

1. Top dot. 2. Horizontal stroke. 3. Left diagonal down. 4. Right diagonal down.

## Mnemonic

A hat on top of two legs — like the number six standing up.

## Common Mistakes

Confusion with 穴 (hole) is possible; 六 has a dot and simpler structure.
