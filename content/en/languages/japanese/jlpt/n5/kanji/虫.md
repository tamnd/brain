## 虫

**JLPT Level:** N5 | **Strokes:** 6 | **Radical:** 虫 (insect)

### Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| On-yomi | チュウ | chuu | insect in compounds |
| Kun-yomi | むし | mushi | insect, bug, worm |

### Meanings
1. Insect, bug
2. Worm, creepy-crawly
3. (Broadly) any small creature

### Stroke Order
1. Top mouth-like shape: horizontal and vertical strokes
2-4. Middle: box structure (口-like)
5. Short horizontal stroke below
6. Bottom dot stroke

### Memory Aid
虫 looks like a coiled snake or worm — the squiggly body of an insect or worm curled up. The original character depicted a coiled snake, and the meaning expanded to include all insects and small creatures.

### Common Confusion
虫 (insect) is the radical for insect-related kanji: 蝶 (butterfly), 蟻 (ant), 蚊 (mosquito). Recognizing 虫 helps with many nature vocabulary items at higher levels.
