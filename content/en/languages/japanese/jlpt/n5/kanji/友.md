1. friend
2. friendship

## Stroke Order

1-2. Two diverging strokes (like 𠂇 or ノ+oblique). 3-4. 又 (hand/again) at bottom right.

## Mnemonic

Two hands (又) reaching toward each other — friends reaching out.

## Common Mistakes

友 (friend) vs 反 (anti/oppose) — 反 has a different top stroke; 友 is rounder in origin.
