1. England
2. hero
3. brilliant
4. superior

## Stroke Order

Top: 艹 (grass radical, 3 strokes). Middle: 𠙶 shape (2 strokes). Bottom: 央 (center, 5 strokes).

## Mnemonic

A flowering (艹) central (央) plant — something brilliant that blooms in the center.

## Common Mistakes

英 (England/brilliant) is typically only used in 英語 (English) and 英国 (England) at N5; other uses are N4+.
