## 花

**JLPT Level:** N5 | **Strokes:** 7 | **Radical:** 艹 (grass/plant)

### Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| On-yomi | カ | ka | flower in compounds |
| Kun-yomi | はな | hana | flower, blossom |

### Meanings
1. Flower, blossom
2. Beautiful (figurative)
3. Fireworks (花火)

### Vocabulary

| Word | Reading | Romaji | English | JLPT |

### Stroke Order
1-2. Top: 艹 (grass radical) — two short strokes
3-7. Bottom: 化 (change/transform) component — person strokes on left and right

### Memory Aid
花 = 艹 (grass/plants) + 化 (change/transform). A plant that transforms — a bud that opens and blooms into a flower. The transformation is the blooming process.

### Common Confusion
花 (flower) vs 鼻 (nose) — both read as はな (hana). Context always makes it clear: 花 has the plant radical; 鼻 has a completely different shape.
