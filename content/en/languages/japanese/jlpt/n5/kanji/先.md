## Character Info

| Kanji | Strokes | JLPT | Radical | Radical Meaning |
|-------|---------|------|---------|-----------------|
| 先 | 6 | N5 | 儿 | human legs |

## Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| On (音) | セン | sen | Chinese-origin (音読み) |
| Kun (訓) | さき | saki | ahead; first; tip |

## Meanings

1. before
2. ahead
3. first
4. previous

## Stroke Order

Top: 土 or ⼟-like component (3 strokes). Bottom: 儿 (human legs, 2 strokes). Then 先 additional.

## Mnemonic

A person (儿) going ahead — the first to walk forward.

## Common Mistakes

先 (first/before) vs 失 (lose) — 先 has 儿 at the bottom; 失 has a different structure.
