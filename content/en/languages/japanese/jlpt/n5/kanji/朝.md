## Character Info

| 朝 | 12 | N5 | 月 | moon |

## Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |

| Kun (訓) | あさ | asa | morning |

## Meanings

1. morning
2. dynasty

## Stroke Order

Left: 十 + 日 + 十 (forming a sunrise image). Right: 月 (moon, 4 strokes).

## Mnemonic

The moon (月) fading as the sun rises through the reeds — dawn, morning.

## Common Mistakes

朝 has 12 strokes and is complex; ensure the left radical (sunrise image with reeds) is drawn correctly.
