## Stroke Order

1. Central vertical stroke. 2. Left diagonal down. 3-4. Two outer dots/strokes spreading out.

## Mnemonic

Flames leaping upward with sparks on both sides — the shape of fire.

## Common Mistakes

火 (fire) vs 犬 (dog) — 犬 has a dot on top right; 火 has spreading bottom strokes.
