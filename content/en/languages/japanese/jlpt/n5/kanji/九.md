## Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |

| Kun (訓) | ここの-つ | kokono-tsu | nine (native count) |

## Meanings

1. nine

## Stroke Order

1. Horizontal stroke angling down-right. 2. Long hook curving down and left.

## Mnemonic

A hook that curves — like a fishing hook, nine catches the last single digit.

## Common Mistakes

Confusion with 力 (power/strength); 九 has a longer hook curving more dramatically to the left.
