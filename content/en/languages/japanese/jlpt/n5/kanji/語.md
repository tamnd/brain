## Stroke Order

Left: 言 (speech, 7 strokes). Right: 吾 (I/self — 五+口, 7 strokes).

## Mnemonic

Speaking (言) from the self (吾) — personal language and expression.

## Common Mistakes

語 vs 話 — both have 言; 語 uses 吾 (self) on right; 話 uses 舌 (tongue).
