## Character Info

| 土 | 3 | N5 | 土 | earth/soil |

## Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| On (音) | ド/ト | do/to | Chinese-origin (音読み) |
| Kun (訓) | つち | tsuchi | earth; soil; ground |

## Meanings

1. earth
2. soil
3. ground
4. Saturday

## Stroke Order

1. Short horizontal top stroke. 2. Long horizontal stroke below. 3. Vertical stroke connecting them.

## Mnemonic

A cross planted in the ground — a marker stuck into the earth.

## Common Mistakes

土 (earth) vs 士 (samurai/scholar) — 土 has a longer bottom stroke; 士 has a longer top stroke.
