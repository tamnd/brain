## Meanings

1. long
2. chief
3. leader
4. senior

## Stroke Order

1. Horizontal top stroke. 2. Left diagonal. 3. Vertical connecting stroke. 4-8. Lower components forming flowing hair.

## Mnemonic

Long flowing hair — the character depicts long flowing locks, symbolizing length.

## Common Mistakes

長 (long) vs 髪 (hair) — 髪 contains 長 but is much more complex; at N5, just remember 長 for 'long.'
