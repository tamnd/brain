## Stroke Order

1. Short horizontal-diagonal stroke (hand, ナ-like). 2-3. ナ completion. 4-5. 工 (work) at bottom.

## Mnemonic

A hand holding a work tool — the left hand that assists with craft.

## Common Mistakes

左 (left) vs 右 (right) — both start similarly; 左 has 工 at the bottom, 右 has 口 (mouth).
