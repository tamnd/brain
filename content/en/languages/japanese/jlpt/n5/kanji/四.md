## Stroke Order

1. Vertical stroke left side. 2. Top horizontal. 3. Right vertical. 4. Bottom horizontal closing box. 5. Two inner vertical strokes.

## Mnemonic

A box with two legs inside — imagine four legs of a table packed into a square.

## Common Mistakes

When counting time/months, use よ (yo) not し (shi) before じ and がつ; し can sound like 死 (death).
