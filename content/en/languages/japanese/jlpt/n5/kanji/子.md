## Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| On (音) | シ/ス | shi/su | Chinese-origin (音読み) |
| Kun (訓) | こ/ね | ko/ne | child; small thing |

## Meanings

1. child
2. small
3. offspring

## Stroke Order

1. Top curved stroke (head). 2. Crossing horizontal (arms). 3. Curved bottom stroke.

## Mnemonic

A swaddled baby with arms out — the shape of an infant being carried.

## Common Mistakes

子 (child) vs 了 (finish/complete) — 了 lacks the arms; 子 has a horizontal stroke for arms.
