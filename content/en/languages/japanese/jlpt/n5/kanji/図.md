## Character Info

| Kanji | Strokes | JLPT | Radical | Radical Meaning |
|-------|---------|------|---------|-----------------|
| 図 | 7 | N5 | 囗 | enclosure |

## Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| On (音) | ズ/ト | zu/to | Chinese-origin (音読み) |
| Kun (訓) | はか-る | haka-ru | to plan; to scheme |

## Meanings

1. map
2. diagram
3. figure
4. plan

## Stroke Order

Outer: 囗 (enclosure, 3 strokes). Inner: 図 simplified — 乂 (cross/cut, 2 strokes).

## Mnemonic

Lines drawn inside a frame — a plan or map enclosed in a box.

## Common Mistakes

図 is the simplified form of 圖; the traditional form is more complex inside the enclosure.
