## Character Info

| Kanji | Strokes | JLPT | Radical | Radical Meaning |
|-------|---------|------|---------|-----------------|
| 気 | 6 | N5 | 气 | steam/air |

## Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| On (音) | キ/ケ | ki/ke | Chinese-origin (音読み) |

## Meanings

1. spirit
2. energy
3. feeling
4. air
5. atmosphere

## Stroke Order

Outer: 气 (steam radical, 3 strokes). Inner: 米 (rice, 3 strokes — simplified).

## Mnemonic

Steam (气) rising from rice (米) — the vital energy/spirit of life.

## Common Mistakes

気 is the simplified (shinjitai) form of 氣; the traditional form has more strokes inside the steam radical.
