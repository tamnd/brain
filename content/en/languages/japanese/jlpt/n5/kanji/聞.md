## Character Info

| Kanji | Strokes | JLPT | Radical | Radical Meaning |
|-------|---------|------|---------|-----------------|
| 聞 | 14 | N5 | 耳 | ear |

## Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| On (音) | ブン/モン | bun/mon | Chinese-origin (音読み) |
| Kun (訓) | き-く/き-こえる | ki-ku/ki-koeru | to hear; to listen; to ask |

## Meanings

1. hear
2. listen
3. ask

## Stroke Order

Outer: 門 (gate, 8 strokes). Inner: 耳 (ear, 6 strokes).

## Mnemonic

An ear (耳) inside the gate (門) — listening through the gate, straining to hear.

## Common Mistakes

聞 (hear) vs 間 (between/time) — 聞 has 耳 (ear) inside; 間 has 日 (sun) inside.
