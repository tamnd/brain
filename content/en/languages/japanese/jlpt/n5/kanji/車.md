## Stroke Order

1. Top horizontal. 2. Box middle section. 3. Closing strokes. 4. Vertical through center. 5-7. Bottom cross strokes.

## Mnemonic

A wheel viewed from above with a central axle — the character looks like a wheel's cross-section.

## Common Mistakes

車 (vehicle) vs 東 (east) — both have similar structure; 東 has no central vertical dividing a wheel shape.
