## Stroke Order

1. Left-to-right sweeping stroke (arms). 2. Crossing vertical stroke. 3. Bottom horizontal.

## Mnemonic

A person kneeling with arms crossed — the traditional depiction of a woman.

## Common Mistakes

女 (woman) vs 母 (mother) — 母 is derived from 女 but has additional dots representing breasts.
