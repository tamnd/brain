## Character Info

| Kanji | Strokes | JLPT | Radical | Radical Meaning |
|-------|---------|------|---------|-----------------|
| 言 | 7 | N5 | 言 | speech/word |

## Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| On (音) | ゲン/ゴン | gen/gon | Chinese-origin (音読み) |
| Kun (訓) | い-う/こと | i-u/koto | to say; word |

## Meanings

1. say
2. speak
3. word

## Stroke Order

Top: 亠 (lid, 2 strokes) + 口-like (1 stroke). Then: 三 horizontal lines (3 strokes). Bottom: 口 (mouth, 1 stroke).

## Mnemonic

A mouth (口) with sound waves above — the character for speech and language.

## Common Mistakes

言 (say/word) is the standalone form; as a radical it becomes 訁 (7-stroke form) on the left of compound kanji.
