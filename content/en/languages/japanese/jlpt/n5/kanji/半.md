## Character Info

| 半 | 5 | N5 | 十 | ten |

## Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| On (音) | ハン | han | Chinese-origin (音読み) |
| Kun (訓) | なか-ば | naka-ba | half; middle |

## Meanings

1. half
2. semi-

## Stroke Order

1. Short left diagonal. 2. Short right diagonal. 3. Long horizontal. 4-5. Vertical stroke, short horizontal.

## Mnemonic

A cross with a dividing line — cutting something in half.

## Common Mistakes

半 (half) vs 手 (hand) — 手 has three horizontal strokes and a different bottom; 半 has two diagonals at top.
