2. day
3. Japan

## Stroke Order

1. Left vertical. 2. Top horizontal. 3. Right vertical with foot. 4. Inner horizontal dividing box. 5. Bottom horizontal.

## Mnemonic

A square with a line across the middle — the sun framed as a glowing disk.

## Common Mistakes

日 (sun/day) has two horizontal inner lines dividing it; 目 (eye) also has two, but 目 is taller.
