## Stroke Order

Top: 土 (earth, 3 strokes). Middle: 人/亦-like (1 stroke). Bottom: 灬 or 赤 foot (3 strokes).

## Mnemonic

Earth (土) with fire — the reddish color of embers and earth.

## Common Mistakes

赤 (red) vs 変 (change) — different structures; 赤 has a fire-like bottom; memorize by color association.
