## Character Info

| Kanji | Strokes | JLPT | Radical | Radical Meaning |
|-------|---------|------|---------|-----------------|
| 昨 | 9 | N5 | 日 | sun/day |

## Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| On (音) | サク | saku | Chinese-origin (音読み) |

## Meanings

1. yesterday
2. previous

## Stroke Order

Left: 日 (sun, 4 strokes). Right: 乍 (suddenly/make, 5 strokes).

## Mnemonic

The sun (日) of a sudden past moment (乍) — yesterday, that passed day.

## Common Mistakes

昨日 is read きのう in everyday speech, not さくじつ — the on-reading さくじつ is formal/written only.
