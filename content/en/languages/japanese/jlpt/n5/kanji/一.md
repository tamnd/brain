## Meanings

1. one
2. first

## Stroke Order

One horizontal stroke from left to right.

## Mnemonic

A single horizontal stroke — the simplest kanji, representing the number one.

## Common Mistakes

Learners sometimes confuse 一 (one) with 二 (two, two strokes) in handwriting when written hastily.
