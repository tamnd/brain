## Meanings

1. ten thousand
2. myriad

## Stroke Order

1. Horizontal stroke at top. 2. Diagonal left stroke. 3. Long sweeping right stroke with bend.

## Mnemonic

A sprawling shape suggesting vastness —万 means ten thousand, or 'countless.'

## Common Mistakes

万 vs 力 (power) — 万 has a top horizontal; 力 does not.
