## Stroke Order

Left: 木 (tree, 4 strokes). Right: 交 (intersect/mix, 6 strokes).

## Mnemonic

A wooden (木) place where ideas cross (交) — a school where knowledge intersects.

## Common Mistakes

校 (school) vs 校 written with 父 instead of 交 — ensure the right component is 交 (交差 = intersection).
