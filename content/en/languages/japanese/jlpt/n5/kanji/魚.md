## 魚

**JLPT Level:** N5 | **Strokes:** 11 | **Radical:** 魚 (fish)

### Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| On-yomi | ギョ | gyo | fish in compounds |
| Kun-yomi | さかな、うお | sakana, uo | sakana = fish (common); uo = fish (in compounds/poetic) |

### Meanings
1. Fish
2. (Used as a food category)

### Stroke Order
1-3. Top: 刀-like head and strokes
4-8. Middle: 田 (rice field) — box with inner cross
9-11. Bottom: four short strokes (tail fins)

### Memory Aid
魚 is a pictograph of a fish: the top part is the head/body, the middle 田 is the body/scales, and the four strokes at the bottom are the tail fin spreading out. You can actually see the fish shape!

### Common Confusion
魚 (fish) is used as a radical in many fish-related kanji at higher levels (鯨 whale, 鮭 salmon, etc.). Recognizing 魚 unlocks many seafood vocabulary items.
