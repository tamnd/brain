## Character Info

| 後 | 9 | N5 | 彳 | step |

## Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| On (音) | ゴ/コウ | go/kou | Chinese-origin (音読み) |
| Kun (訓) | あと/うし-ろ/のち | ato/ushi-ro/nochi | after; behind; later |

## Meanings

1. behind
2. after
3. later
4. back

## Stroke Order

Left: 彳 (step, 3 strokes). Middle-right: 幺 (small, 3 strokes). Bottom right: 夂 (walk, 3 strokes).

## Mnemonic

Stepping (彳) back behind small (幺) things — what comes after, behind you.

## Common Mistakes

後 (behind/after) vs 待 (wait) — similar left radical 彳; 待 has 寺 on the right instead.
