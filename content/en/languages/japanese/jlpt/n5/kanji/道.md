## Meanings

1. road
2. way
3. path
4. method
5. doctrine

## Stroke Order

Left/bottom: 辶 (movement radical, 3 strokes). Inner: 首 (neck/head, 9 strokes).

## Mnemonic

Walking (辶) with your head (首) — following a road, a path you choose.

## Common Mistakes

道 (road) vs 遺 (bequeath) — both use 辶; remember 道 has 首 inside.
