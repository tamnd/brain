## 北

**JLPT Level:** N5 | **Strokes:** 5 | **Radical:** 匕 (spoon/change)

### Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| On-yomi | ホク | hoku | north in compounds |
| Kun-yomi | きた | kita | north |

### Meanings
1. North
2. Northern

### Stroke Order
1. Left component: stroke going left-up then down
2. Left component: short horizontal stroke
3. Right component: short stroke
4. Right component: vertical stroke
5. Right component: horizontal base stroke

### Memory Aid
北 looks like two people standing back-to-back, turning away from each other — in the northern cold, people huddle together but face away from the bitter wind. Cold north = people turning their backs.

### Common Confusion
北 (north) vs 比 (compare) — they look similar. 北 has slightly different stroke arrangement. Associate 北 with "cold north = backs turned away from cold."
