## Character Info

| Kanji | Strokes | JLPT | Radical | Radical Meaning |
|-------|---------|------|---------|-----------------|
| 夜 | 8 | N5 | 夕 | evening |

## Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| On (音) | ヤ | ya | Chinese-origin (音読み) |
| Kun (訓) | よる/よ | yoru/yo | night |

## Meanings

1. night
2. evening

## Stroke Order

1. Top cover stroke. 2-3. 亦 (also/moreover) upper part. 4-8. Lower components finishing the character.

## Mnemonic

A person (亻-like) under cover at night — the shelter of darkness.

## Common Mistakes

夜 (night) vs 家 (house/home) — both have a 'roof' element; remember 夜 has 夕 (evening) in its etymology.
