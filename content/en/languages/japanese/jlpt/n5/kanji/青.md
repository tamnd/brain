## Readings

## Meanings

1. blue
2. green
3. young
4. unripe

## Stroke Order

Top: 生 (life, first 4 strokes). Bottom: 月 (moon variant/blue, 4 strokes).

## Mnemonic

Life (生) under the moon (月) — the blue-green of nature at night.

## Common Mistakes

青 covers both blue and green in Japanese (e.g., 青信号 is a green traffic light, not blue).
