## Stroke Order

1. Left stroke going up-right then down. 2. Tall central vertical. 3. Right stroke going up then down.

## Mnemonic

Three peaks — a mountain range with a tallest central peak.

## Common Mistakes

山 (mountain) vs 出 (exit/come out) — 出 has two mountain shapes stacked; remember 出 has two humps.
