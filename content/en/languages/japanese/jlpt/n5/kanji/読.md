## Stroke Order

Left: 言 (speech, 7 strokes). Right: 売 (sell, 7 strokes).

## Mnemonic

Words (言) being sold/exchanged (売) — reading as receiving the author's words.

## Common Mistakes

読 (read) vs 続 (continue) — both have 言 on the left; 続 has 売 topped by different element.
