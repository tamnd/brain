## Character Info

| 前 | 9 | N5 | 刀 | knife |

## Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| On (音) | ゼン | zen | Chinese-origin (音読み) |
| Kun (訓) | まえ | mae | before; front; ago |

## Meanings

1. before
2. front
3. previous
4. ago

## Stroke Order

Top: 羊-like component (first 6 strokes). Bottom: 刀 (knife) component (strokes 7-9).

## Mnemonic

A knife (刀) used before something — preparing in advance.

## Common Mistakes

前 (before/front) vs 後 (behind/after) — remember: 前 is the direction you face (forward).
