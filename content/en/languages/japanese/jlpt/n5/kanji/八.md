## Stroke Order

1. Left diagonal stroke going down-left. 2. Right diagonal stroke going down-right, slightly longer.

## Mnemonic

Two diverging strokes — like legs spreading apart, representing abundance (eight was lucky in old China).

## Common Mistakes

八 looks like ハ (katakana ha) — remember 八 is wider and the strokes diverge more.
