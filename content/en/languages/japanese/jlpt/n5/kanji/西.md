## 西

**JLPT Level:** N5 | **Strokes:** 6 | **Radical:** 西 (west)

### Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| On-yomi | セイ、サイ | sei, sai | sei = west standard; sai = in some words |
| Kun-yomi | にし | nishi | west |

### Meanings
1. West
2. Western

### Vocabulary

| Word | Reading | Romaji | English | JLPT |

| 西口 | にしぐち | nishiguchi | west exit | N5 |

### Stroke Order
1. Top horizontal stroke
2. Left vertical stroke (curved)
3. Right vertical stroke (curved)
4. Inner left diagonal stroke
5. Inner right diagonal stroke
6. Bottom horizontal stroke

### Memory Aid
西 looks like a bird's nest (a box with inner crossings) — birds return to their nests in the WEST as the sun sets. Sunset = west = going home to the nest.

### Common Confusion
西 (west) vs 四 (four) — both have a box-like shape. 西 is taller with inner diagonal strokes; 四 has a more regular box with two inner horizontals.
