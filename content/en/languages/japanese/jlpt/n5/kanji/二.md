| 二 | 2 | N5 | 二 | two |

## Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| On (音) | ニ | ni | Chinese-origin (音読み) |
| Kun (訓) | ふた-つ | futa-tsu | two (native count) |

## Meanings

1. two
2. second

## Stroke Order

Two horizontal strokes: shorter one on top, longer one below.

## Mnemonic

Two horizontal lines — counting one above the other.

## Common Mistakes

The upper stroke of 二 is shorter than the lower; reversing them is a common writing error.
