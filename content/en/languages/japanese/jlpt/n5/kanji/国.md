## Meanings

1. country
2. nation
3. land

## Stroke Order

Outer: 囗 (enclosure, 3 strokes). Inner: 玉 (jewel/king, 5 strokes).

## Mnemonic

A jewel (玉) protected inside a border (囗) — a nation guards its treasure.

## Common Mistakes

国 (country) vs 囲 (surround) — 囲 has a different inner component (井); 国 has 玉.
