## Stroke Order

Top: 雨 (rain, 8 strokes). Bottom: 申 simplified (5 strokes).

## Mnemonic

Lightning (like a bolt through rain/雨) — electricity from the sky.

## Common Mistakes

電 (electricity) vs 雲 (cloud) — both start with 雨; 電 has a lightning-bolt component below.
