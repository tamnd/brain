## Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |

| Kun (訓) | く-る/きた-る/きた-す | ku-ru/kita-ru/kita-su | to come |

## Meanings

1. come
2. future
3. next

## Stroke Order

1. Top horizontal. 2. Central vertical. 3. Left upper diagonal. 4. Right upper diagonal. 5-7. Left, middle, right lower strokes.

## Mnemonic

A grain plant with seeds spreading — abundance coming, like something arriving.

## Common Mistakes

来 (come) vs 未 (not yet) — 未 has a longer upper horizontal; 来 has equal horizontals with diagonals.
