## Character Info

| Kanji | Strokes | JLPT | Radical | Radical Meaning |
|-------|---------|------|---------|-----------------|
| 晩 | 12 | N5 | 日 | sun/day |

## Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| On (音) | バン | ban | Chinese-origin (音読み) |

## Meanings

1. evening
2. night
3. late

## Stroke Order

Left: 日 (sun, 4 strokes). Right: 免 (escape/exemption, 8 strokes).

## Mnemonic

The sun (日) escaping (免) below the horizon — evening, the sun getting away.

## Common Mistakes

晩 (evening) vs 番 (number/turn) — similar right components; 晩 has 日 on the left.
