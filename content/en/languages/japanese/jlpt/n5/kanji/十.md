## Stroke Order

1. Horizontal stroke. 2. Vertical stroke crossing it in the middle.

## Mnemonic

A cross — two strokes crossing, like a plus sign, representing the completion of single digits.

## Common Mistakes

十 (ten) vs 土 (earth/soil) — 土 has a shorter top line; 十 has equal-length arms of the cross.
