## Character Info

| Kanji | Strokes | JLPT | Radical | Radical Meaning |
|-------|---------|------|---------|-----------------|
| 大 | 3 | N5 | 大 | big |

## Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| On (音) | ダイ/タイ | dai/tai | Chinese-origin (音読み) |
| Kun (訓) | おお-きい | oo-kii | big; large |

## Meanings

1. big
2. large
3. great

## Stroke Order

1. Left diagonal stroke. 2. Right diagonal stroke. 3. Short horizontal stroke (arms).

## Mnemonic

A person standing with arms spread wide — expressing bigness.

## Common Mistakes

大 (big) vs 太 (fat/thick) — 太 has a dot added; 犬 (dog) has a dot on top right.
