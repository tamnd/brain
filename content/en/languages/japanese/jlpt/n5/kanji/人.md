## Meanings

1. person
2. people
3. human being

## Stroke Order

1. Left-leaning diagonal stroke. 2. Right-leaning stroke crossing the first.

## Mnemonic

Two legs walking — a person in profile, walking forward.

## Common Mistakes

人 (person) vs 入 (enter) — 入 has the second stroke starting higher and going further right; 人 is more balanced.
