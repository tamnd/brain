## Stroke Order

1. Left vertical with slight curve. 2. Middle vertical (straight). 3. Right vertical with slight outward curve.

## Mnemonic

Three parallel strokes like flowing water — the shape of a river's current.

## Common Mistakes

川 (river) vs 三 (three) — 川 is vertical strokes; 三 is horizontal strokes.
