## Character Info

| Kanji | Strokes | JLPT | Radical | Radical Meaning |
|-------|---------|------|---------|-----------------|
| 地 | 6 | N5 | 土 | earth/soil |

## Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| On (音) | チ/ジ | chi/ji | Chinese-origin (音読み) |

## Meanings

1. ground
2. earth
3. place

## Stroke Order

Left: 土 (earth, 3 strokes). Right: 也 (also/it is, 3 strokes).

## Mnemonic

Earth (土) that simply is (也) — the ground beneath our feet.

## Common Mistakes

地 (ground/place) vs 池 (pond) — 池 has 氵 (water radical) on the left; 地 has 土 (earth).
