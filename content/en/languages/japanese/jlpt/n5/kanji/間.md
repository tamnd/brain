## Character Info

| Kanji | Strokes | JLPT | Radical | Radical Meaning |
|-------|---------|------|---------|-----------------|
| 間 | 12 | N5 | 門 | gate |

## Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| On (音) | カン/ケン | kan/ken | Chinese-origin (音読み) |
| Kun (訓) | あいだ/ま | aida/ma | between; interval; space |

## Meanings

1. interval
2. between
3. space
4. time

## Stroke Order

Outer: 門 (gate, 8 strokes). Inner: 日 (sun, 4 strokes).

## Mnemonic

The sun (日) shining through the gate (門) — light filtering through the space in between.

## Common Mistakes

間 (interval/between) vs 問 (question) — 問 has 口 (mouth) inside the gate, not 日.
