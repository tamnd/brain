## Character Info

| Kanji | Strokes | JLPT | Radical | Radical Meaning |
|-------|---------|------|---------|-----------------|
| 七 | 2 | N5 | 一 | one |

## Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| On (音) | シチ | shichi | Chinese-origin (音読み) |
| Kun (訓) | なな-つ | nana-tsu | seven (native count) |

## Meanings

1. seven

## Stroke Order

1. Horizontal stroke (left to right). 2. Vertical stroke crossing and hooking left at bottom.

## Mnemonic

Like the numeral 7 rotated — a horizontal bar with a downward hook.

## Common Mistakes

Both シチ (shichi) and なな (nana) are acceptable; use なな when confusion with 一 (ichi) is possible in speech.
