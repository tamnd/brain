## Stroke Order

1. Short diagonal left. 2. Horizontal stroke. 3. Vertical through center. 4-6. Three strokes forming lower half.

## Mnemonic

A person (人-like shape) walking through time — each year a step forward.

## Common Mistakes

年 (year) vs 牛 (cow) — similar shape but 年 has more strokes and a different radical.
