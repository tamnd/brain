## Stroke Order

Left: 食 minus bottom (6 strokes, food radical 飠). Right: 欠 (yawn/lacking, 4 strokes).

## Mnemonic

Food/nourishment (食) taken in with an open mouth/yawn (欠) — drinking.

## Common Mistakes

飲 vs 食 — 飲 has 欠 on the right side; both share the food radical but 飲 is specifically for liquids.
