## Character Info

|-------|---------|------|---------|-----------------|
| 入 | 2 | N5 | 入 | enter |

## Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| On (音) | ニュウ/ジュ | nyuu/ju | Chinese-origin (音読み) |
| Kun (訓) | い-る/い-れる/はい-る | i-ru/i-reru/hai-ru | to enter; to put in |

## Meanings

1. enter
2. put in
3. income

## Stroke Order

1. Left diagonal stroke going down-left. 2. Right diagonal stroke going down-right and longer.

## Mnemonic

An arrow pointing downward into a space — entering, going in.

## Common Mistakes

入 (enter) vs 人 (person) — 入 has the right stroke longer and more prominent; 人 has balanced strokes.
