## Character Info

| Kanji | Strokes | JLPT | Radical | Radical Meaning |
|-------|---------|------|---------|-----------------|
| 分 | 4 | N5 | 刀 | knife |

## Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| On (音) | フン/ブン/ブ | fun/bun/bu | Chinese-origin (音読み) |
| Kun (訓) | わか-る/わ-ける | waka-ru/wa-keru | to understand; to divide |

## Meanings

1. minute
2. part
3. understand
4. divide

## Stroke Order

1-2. 八 (eight shape, two diverging strokes at top). 3-4. 刀 (knife shape, right side).

## Mnemonic

A knife (刀) dividing things (八) — dividing time into minutes.

## Common Mistakes

The reading changes: ふん after 2,5,7,9; ぷん after 1,3,4,6,8,10 — this must be memorized.
