## Character Info

| Kanji | Strokes | JLPT | Radical | Radical Meaning |
|-------|---------|------|---------|-----------------|
| 時 | 10 | N5 | 日 | sun/day |

## Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| On (音) | ジ | ji | Chinese-origin (音読み) |
| Kun (訓) | とき | toki | time; when |

## Meanings

1. time
2. o'clock
3. hour

## Stroke Order

Left: 日 (sun, 4 strokes). Right: 寺 (temple, 6 strokes) —土 then 寸.

## Mnemonic

The sun (日) at the temple (寺) marks the time — monks used temple bells to mark hours.

## Common Mistakes

時 is often confused with 待 (to wait); 待 uses 彳 (step radical) on the left instead of 日.
