## Stroke Order

Left: 馬 (horse, 10 strokes). Right: 尺 (measure, 4 strokes).

## Mnemonic

Horses (馬) measured (尺) out — a relay station where horses were exchanged in old Japan.

## Common Mistakes

駅 is complex (14 strokes); the horse radical 馬 on the left has a distinctive 4-dot bottom.
