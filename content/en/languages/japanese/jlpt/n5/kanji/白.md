## Character Info

| 白 | 5 | N5 | 白 | white |

## Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| On (音) | ハク/ビャク | haku/byaku | Chinese-origin (音読み) |
| Kun (訓) | しろ/しろ-い | shiro/shiro-i | white |

## Meanings

1. white
2. blank
3. clear

## Stroke Order

1. Short horizontal top stroke. 2. Left vertical. 3. Top of internal box horizontal. 4. Right vertical. 5. Bottom horizontal.

## Mnemonic

A white candle flame above a box — the sun rising, bringing white light.

## Common Mistakes

白 (white) vs 自 (self) — 自 starts with a different top stroke; 白 has a dot/short line at top.
