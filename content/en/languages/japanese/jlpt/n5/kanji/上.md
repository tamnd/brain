## Character Info

| Kanji | Strokes | JLPT | Radical | Radical Meaning |
|-------|---------|------|---------|-----------------|
| 上 | 3 | N5 | 一 | one |

## Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| On (音) | ジョウ/ショウ | jou/shou | Chinese-origin (音読み) |
| Kun (訓) | うえ/うわ/かみ/のぼ-る/あ-がる | ue/uwa/kami/nobo-ru/a-garu | above; upper; to go up |

## Meanings

1. above
2. upper
3. up
4. ascend

## Stroke Order

1. Short vertical stroke (the thing above). 2. Short horizontal stroke. 3. Longer horizontal stroke (base).

## Mnemonic

A line rising above a baseline — indicating something above.

## Common Mistakes

上 (above) vs 下 (below) — 上 has the short stroke on top; 下 has the hanging stroke below.
