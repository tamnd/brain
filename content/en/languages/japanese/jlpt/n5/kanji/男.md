## Stroke Order

Top: 田 (rice field, 5 strokes). Bottom: 力 (strength/power, 2 strokes).

## Mnemonic

A rice field (田) with strength (力) — a man working in the fields.

## Common Mistakes

男 (man) vs 異 (different) — both have 田 on top; 男 has 力 (strength) underneath.
