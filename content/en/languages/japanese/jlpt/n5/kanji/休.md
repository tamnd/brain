## Character Info

| Kanji | Strokes | JLPT | Radical | Radical Meaning |
|-------|---------|------|---------|-----------------|
| 休 | 6 | N5 | 人 | person |

## Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| On (音) | キュウ | kyuu | Chinese-origin (音読み) |
| Kun (訓) | やす-む/やす-まる/やす-める | yasu-mu/yasu-maru/yasu-meru | to rest; to be absent |

## Meanings

1. rest
2. holiday
3. absent

## Stroke Order

Left: 亻 (person radical, 2 strokes). Right: 木 (tree, 4 strokes).

## Mnemonic

A person (亻) leaning against a tree (木) — resting in the shade.

## Common Mistakes

休 (rest) vs 体 (body) — 体 has 本 on the right (with an extra mark); 休 has 木 (plain tree).
