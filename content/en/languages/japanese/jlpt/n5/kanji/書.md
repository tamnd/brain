## Meanings

1. write
2. book
3. document

## Stroke Order

Top: 聿 (brush, 6 strokes). Bottom: 曰 (say/speak, 4 strokes).

## Mnemonic

A brush (聿) making words (曰) — the act of writing.

## Common Mistakes

書 (write) vs 昔 (old times) — both have similar top components; 書 has 曰 at bottom, 昔 has 日.
