## Character Info

| 月 | 4 | N5 | 月 | moon |

## Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| On (音) | ゲツ/ガツ | getsu/gatsu | Chinese-origin (音読み) |
| Kun (訓) | つき | tsuki | moon; month |

## Meanings

1. moon
2. month

## Stroke Order

1. Left vertical stroke. 2. Horizontal top closing right. 3. Right vertical with feet. 4. Internal horizontal stroke.

## Mnemonic

A crescent moon — the shape suggests the moon in its crescent phase.

## Common Mistakes

月 vs 日 — 月 has an internal horizontal; 日 has two internal horizontals closing off a box.
