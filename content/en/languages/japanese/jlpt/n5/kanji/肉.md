## 肉

**JLPT Level:** N5 | **Strokes:** 6 | **Radical:** 肉 (flesh/meat)

### Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| On-yomi | ニク | niku | standard reading |
| Kun-yomi | — | — | no common kun-yomi |

### Meanings
1. Meat, flesh
2. Body (in some compounds)
3. Pulp (of fruit)

### Vocabulary

### Stroke Order
1. Left vertical stroke
2. Right vertical stroke
3. Top horizontal connecting stroke
4. Inner left stroke
5. Inner right stroke
6. Bottom horizontal stroke

### Memory Aid
肉 looks like a piece of meat wrapped in a package — the outer strokes form the wrapper, and the inner strokes are the cuts/marbling of the meat inside. The radical 月 (moon-shaped) is used for body-part compounds.

### Common Confusion
肉 (meat) vs 月 (moon) — the body radical is actually written like 月 when appearing as a side component (e.g., 腕/arm, 胃/stomach). Standalone 肉 has a slightly different structure than 月.
