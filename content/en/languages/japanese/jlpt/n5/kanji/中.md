## Character Info

| 中 | 4 | N5 | 囗 | enclosure |

## Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| On (音) | チュウ | chuu | Chinese-origin (音読み) |
| Kun (訓) | なか | naka | inside; middle; center |

## Meanings

1. inside
2. middle
3. center
4. China

## Stroke Order

1. Vertical stroke (through center). 2. Left half of box. 3. Right half of box. 4. Bottom completing box.

## Mnemonic

A vertical line hitting the center of a box — the middle point.

## Common Mistakes

中 (inside/middle) vs 口 (mouth) — 中 has a vertical line through 口; 口 alone has no center stroke.
