## Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |

| Kun (訓) | まな-ぶ | mana-bu | to learn; to study |

## Meanings

1. study
2. learning
3. science

## Stroke Order

Top: 爻 (lines, 4 strokes) + 冖 (cover, 2 strokes). Bottom: 子 (child, 3 strokes).

## Mnemonic

A child (子) under a covered space with patterns — a child in school, learning.

## Common Mistakes

学 (study) vs 覚 (remember/wake) — similar top components; 覚 has 見 (see) at the bottom instead of 子.
