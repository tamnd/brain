## Stroke Order

Top: 里 (village/li, 7 strokes). Bottom: 灬 (fire dots, 4 strokes).

## Mnemonic

A village (里) on fire (灬) — smoke and fire make things black.

## Common Mistakes

黒 (black) has 11 strokes; the 灬 at the bottom are four dots representing fire, not individual strokes written singly.
