## 東

**JLPT Level:** N5 | **Strokes:** 8 | **Radical:** 木 (tree)

### Readings

| Type | Reading | Romaji | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| On-yomi | トウ | too | east in compounds |
| Kun-yomi | ひがし | higashi | east |

### Meanings
1. East
2. Eastern

### Stroke Order
1-4. 木 (tree) component: horizontal, vertical, left diagonal, right diagonal
5-8. Additional strokes forming the sun rising through the tree

### Memory Aid
東 = sun rising through a tree. The sun rises in the east — when you see the sun (日) caught in a tree (木), you're facing east. A perfect mnemonic pictograph.

### Common Confusion
東 (east) vs 束 (bundle) — 東 has an extra horizontal stroke; 束 does not. Remember: East = where the sun rises through the full tree.
