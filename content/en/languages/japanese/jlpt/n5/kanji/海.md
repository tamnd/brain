1. sea

## Stroke Order

Left: 氵(water radical, 3 strokes). Right: 每 (every, 6 strokes).

## Mnemonic

Water (氵) everywhere (每) — the vast ocean where water is everywhere.

## Common Mistakes

海 (sea) vs 毎 (every) — 海 has the water radical 氵 on the left; 毎 does not.
