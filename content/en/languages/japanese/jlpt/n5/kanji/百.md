## Stroke Order

1. Horizontal stroke at top. 2. Vertical stroke down through center. 3-6. The white (白) component below: horizontal, box sides, bottom close, internal horizontal.

## Mnemonic

The number 1 over the character 白 (white) — one hundred pure things.

## Common Mistakes

百 has a short horizontal at top followed by the 白 shape; don't confuse with 白 alone.
