3. metal

## Stroke Order

Top: 人 shape (2 strokes). 2 more above. Middle: 土 (earth, 3 strokes). Bottom: 2 dots.

## Mnemonic

Gold nuggets (dots at bottom) buried in the earth (土) — precious metal mined from the ground.

## Common Mistakes

金 (gold/money) vs 全 (complete/all) — 全 has similar top structure but different bottom components.
