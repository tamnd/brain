## Pattern: 〜とともに

**Structure:** Noun + とともに / Verb (dictionary form) + とともに

**Formality:** Formal / Standard

**Meaning:** "together with ~, along with ~, as ~ also ~" (simultaneous co-occurrence or parallel change)

## Explanation

〜とともに has two uses:

**Meaning 1 — Accompaniment/simultaneity:** Doing something together with another person or thing. 「彼女とともに働く」(work together with her).

**Meaning 2 — Parallel development:** As one development occurs, another occurs along with it. This is the more important N3 use. Unlike 〜につれて (which emphasizes *gradual* co-change), 〜とともに emphasizes *parallel occurrence* — two things happening at the same time, which may or may not be gradual.

〜とともに is more formal than 〜と一緒に (accompaniment) and appears frequently in formal speeches, essays, and news.

## Example Sentences

| Japanese | Reading | Romaji | English |
|----------|---------|--------|---------|
| 経済の発展とともに、生活が豊かになった。 | けいざいのはってんとともに、せいかつがゆたかになった。 | Keizai no hatten to tomo ni, seikatsu ga yutaka ni natta. | Along with economic development, life became richer. |
| 時代の変化とともに、言葉も変わる。 | じだいのへんかとともに、ことばもかわる。 | Jidai no henka to tomo ni, kotoba mo kawaru. | Along with changes in the times, language also changes. |
| 成功するとともに、責任も増えた。 | せいこうするとともに、せきにんもふえた。 | Seikō suru to tomo ni, sekinin mo fueta. | Along with success, responsibility also increased. |
| 春の訪れとともに、桜が咲き始めた。 | はるのおとずれとともに、さくらがさきはじめた。 | Haru no otozure to tomo ni, sakura ga saki hajimeta. | With the arrival of spring, cherry blossoms began to bloom. |
| 彼女の説明とともに、全員が状況を理解した。 | かのじょのせつめいとともに、ぜんいんがじょうきょうをりかいした。 | Kanojo no setsumei to tomo ni, zenin ga jōkyō o rikai shita. | Along with her explanation, everyone understood the situation. |

## Common Mistakes

❌ 友達とともに遊んだ。(too formal for casual activity)  
✓ 友達と一緒に遊んだ。— Use 〜と一緒に for casual accompaniment.

## Comparison with Similar Patterns

| Pattern | Nuance | Formality |
|---------|--------|-----------|
| 〜とともに | Parallel co-occurrence (formal) | Formal |
| 〜と一緒に | Simple accompaniment | Casual/neutral |
| 〜につれて | Gradual proportional co-change | Standard/formal |
| 〜に従って | Proportional (compliance nuance) | Formal |
