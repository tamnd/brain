N3 marks the B1 milestone — mid-level competence reached after roughly 600–1200 hours of study. You can follow everyday conversation, read simple newspaper articles, and handle most situations while travelling. These ten lessons build the grammar and register awareness needed to pass.

## Lessons

| # | Lesson | Focus |
|---|--------|-------|
| 1 | [Formal vs. Informal Register](01-formal-vs-informal-register.md) | Keigo basics, plain vs. polite switching |
| 2 | [Causal Expressions Deep Dive](02-causal-expressions-deep-dive.md) | ので、から、ため、せいで |
| 3 | [Complex Conjunctions I](03-complex-conjunctions-i-location-and-stance.md) | Location and stance conjunctions |
| 4 | [Complex Conjunctions II](04-complex-conjunctions-ii-change-and-proportion.md) | Change and proportion conjunctions |
| 5 | [Advanced Conditionals and Timing](05-advanced-conditionals-and-timing.md) | たら、なら、と、ば — nuance and timing |
| 6 | [Expressing Change, Tendency, and Difficulty](06-expressing-change-tendency-difficulty.md) | てくる、ていく、がち、にくい、やすい |
| 7 | [Limits, Scope, and Contrast](07-limits-scope-and-contrast.md) | だけ、しか、ばかり、のに、くせに |
| 8 | [Workplace and Formal Japanese](08-workplace-and-formal-japanese.md) | Business phrases, humble/respectful forms |
| 9 | [Reading Newspaper Japanese](09-reading-newspaper-japanese.md) | Headlines, passive constructions, kanji patterns |
| 10 | [N3 Capstone — Integration and Review](10-n3-capstone.md) | Mixed practice across all N3 grammar points |

## How to Use

Work through the lessons in order — each builds on the previous one. For each lesson: read the grammar notes, study the example sentences, then do the exercises before checking answers. Revisit weak points using the lesson tags.
