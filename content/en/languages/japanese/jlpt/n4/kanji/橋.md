## Character Info

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Kanji | 橋 |
| Strokes | 16 |
| Radical | 木 (tree/wood) |
| JLPT Level | N4 |

## Readings

|------|----------|--------|
| On-yomi | キョウ | kyō |
| Kun-yomi | はし | hashi |

## Vocabulary

| 橋 | はし | hashi | bridge |

| 橋本 | はしもと | Hashimoto | (common surname) |
| 鉄橋 | てっきょう | tekkyō | iron bridge |
| 歩道橋 | ほどうきょう | hodōkyō | pedestrian overpass |

## Stroke Order

1-4. Left radical 木 (wood/tree). 5-16. Right side 喬 (tall, lofty).

## Mnemonic

A **wooden** (木) structure that is **tall** (喬) — a **bridge** towering over the water below.

## Common Confusion Pairs

| 矯 (correct/straighten) | 矯 has 矢 on left; 橋 has 木 |
| 喬 (tall) | 喬 alone; 橋 adds 木 on left |
