## Stroke Order

1-3. Left 弓 (bow/archery). 4-11. Right 虽 (insect, used here for phonetic).

## Mnemonic

A **bow** (弓) that requires **insect-like persistence** (虽) to pull — you need to be **strong** to draw a powerful bow.

## Common Confusion Pairs

| 弾 (bullet/play) | 弾 has 単 on right; 強 has 虽 |
| 弓 (bow) | 弓 is just the left part of 強 |
