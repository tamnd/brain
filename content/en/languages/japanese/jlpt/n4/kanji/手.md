## Stroke Order

1. Top horizontal. 2. Second horizontal. 3. Third horizontal. 4. Vertical stroke through all three.

## Mnemonic

Three **fingers** (horizontal lines) spread wide, connected by a **palm line** — it looks exactly like an open **hand**.

## Common Confusion Pairs

| 毛 (hair/fur) | 毛 has the bottom stroke curving out; 手 has even horizontal strokes |
| 才 (talent) | 才 has fewer strokes |
