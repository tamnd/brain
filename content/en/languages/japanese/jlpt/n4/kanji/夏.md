## Mnemonic

1-7. Top/middle portion (a face with elaborate headdress). 8-10. Bottom 夂 (legs walking).

## Common Confusion Pairs

A person wearing an elaborate **summer headdress** performing a **dance** (夂 = walking/dancing feet) — a **summer** festival.
