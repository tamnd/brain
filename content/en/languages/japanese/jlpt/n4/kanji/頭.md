## Character Info

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Kanji | 頭 |
| Strokes | 16 |
| Radical | 頁 (page/head) |
| JLPT Level | N4 |

## Readings

| Type | Japanese | Romaji |
|------|----------|--------|
| On-yomi | トウ、ズ | tō, zu |
| Kun-yomi | あたま、かしら | atama, kashira |

## Vocabulary

| 頭 | あたま | atama | head |
| 先頭 | せんとう | sentō | lead, front |
| 頭脳 | ずのう | zunō | brain, intellect |
| 口頭 | こうとう | kōtō | oral, verbal |
| 頭痛 | ずつう | zutsū | headache |

## Stroke Order

1-7. Left side 豆 (bean). 8-16. Right radical 頁 (head/page).

## Mnemonic

A **bean**-shaped thing with the **head radical** — your **head** looks round like a bean on your neck.

## Common Confusion Pairs

| 頑 (stubborn) | 頑 has 元 on left; 頭 has 豆 |
| 顔 (face) | 顔 has 彦 on left; 頭 has 豆 |
