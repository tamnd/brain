## Character Info

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Kanji | 路 |
| Strokes | 13 |
| Radical | 足 (foot) |
| JLPT Level | N4 |

## Readings

| Type | Japanese | Romaji |
|------|----------|--------|
| On-yomi | ロ | ro |
| Kun-yomi | じ | ji |

## Vocabulary

| 道路 | どうろ | dōro | road, highway |
| 路線 | ろせん | rosen | route, line |
| 路地 | ろじ | roji | alley, lane |
| 進路 | しんろ | shinro | course, direction |
| 通路 | つうろ | tsūro | passageway, aisle |

## Stroke Order

1-6. Left radical 足/⻊ (foot). 7-13. Right side 各 (each).

## Mnemonic

Your **foot** (⻊) going to **each** (各) place — a **route** your feet take.

## Common Confusion Pairs

| 跳 (jump) | 跳 has 兆 on right; 路 has 各 |
| 距 (distance) | 距 has 巨 on right; 路 has 各 |
