## Stroke Order

1. Left radical 亻(person). 2-9. Right side 更 (renew/change).

## Mnemonic

A **person** (亻) who **changes/updates** (更) things — making things **convenient** or delivering **mail** as a message.

## Common Confusion Pairs

| 使 (use/envoy) | 使 has 吏 on right; 便 has 更 |
| 復 (return) | 復 has 彳on left; 便 has 亻 |
