## Stroke Order

1-9. Inner portion 首 (head/neck). 10-12. Outer movement radical 辶(movement).

## Mnemonic

A **head** (首) moving along a **road** (辶) — using your head to navigate the **way** or **path**.

## Common Confusion Pairs

| 導 (guide) | 導 has 道 on top and 寸 below |
| 遺 (bequeath) | 遺 has 貴 inside; 道 has 首 |
