## Character Info

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Kanji | 指 |
| Strokes | 9 |
| Radical | 手/扌(hand) |
| JLPT Level | N4 |

## Readings

| Type | Japanese | Romaji |
|------|----------|--------|
| On-yomi | シ | shi |
| Kun-yomi | ゆび、さ・す | yubi, sa-su |

## Vocabulary

| 指 | ゆび | yubi | finger |
| 指定 | してい | shitei | designation, appointment |
| 指導 | しどう | shidō | guidance, instruction |
| 薬指 | くすりゆび | kusuriyubi | ring finger |
| 指輪 | ゆびわ | yubiwa | ring (jewelry) |

## Stroke Order

1-3. Left radical 扌(hand). 4-9. Right side 旨 (delicious/purpose).

## Mnemonic

Your **hand** (扌) pointing toward something **delicious** (旨) — your **finger** points at what you want.

## Common Confusion Pairs

| 持 (hold) | 持 has 寺 on right; 指 has 旨 |
| 拾 (pick up) | 拾 has 合 on right; 指 has 旨 |
