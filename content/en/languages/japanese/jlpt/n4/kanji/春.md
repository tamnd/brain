## Character Info

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Kanji | 春 |
| Strokes | 9 |
| Radical | 日 (sun) |
| JLPT Level | N4 |

## Readings

| Type | Japanese | Romaji |
|------|----------|--------|
| On-yomi | シュン | shun |
| Kun-yomi | はる | haru |

## Vocabulary

| 春 | はる | haru | spring |
| 春休み | はるやすみ | haruyasumi | spring vacation |
| 青春 | せいしゅん | seishun | youth, adolescence |
| 春分 | しゅんぶん | shunbun | spring equinox |
| 春夏秋冬 | しゅんかしゅうとう | shunkashūtō | the four seasons |

## Stroke Order

1-5. Top 夫 (person with arms spread). 6-9. Bottom 日 (sun).

## Mnemonic

A **person** (夫) spreading their arms wide above the **sun** (日) — **spring** is when the sun rises and people celebrate outdoors.

## Common Confusion Pairs

| 泰 (peaceful) | 泰 has 水 at bottom; 春 has 日 |
| 奏 (play music) | 奏 has 天 on top; 春 has 夫 |
