## Stroke Order

1. 口 at top. 2-7. 貝 (shell/money) below.

## Mnemonic

A **mouth** (口) on top of a **shell** (貝) — a **member** who gets paid (shell = ancient currency) and has a voice.

## Common Confusion Pairs

| Confused With | How to Distinguish |
|---------------|-------------------|
| 貝 (shell) | 員 has 口 on top; 貝 is just the shell character alone |
| 買 (buy) | 買 has 罒 (net) on top; 員 has 口 |
