## Stroke Order

1-2. Top 八 (divide/eight). 3-4. Bottom 厶 (private).

## Mnemonic

**Dividing** (八) what was **private** (厶) — making something **public** by sharing it with all.

## Common Confusion Pairs

| 私 (private/I) | 私 has 禾 on left; 公 is standalone |
