## Character Info

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Kanji | 事 |
| Strokes | 8 |
| Radical | 亅 (hook) |
| JLPT Level | N4 |

## Readings

| Type | Japanese | Romaji |
|------|----------|--------|
| On-yomi | ジ | ji |
| Kun-yomi | こと | koto |

## Vocabulary

| Word | Reading | Romaji | English |
|------|---------|--------|---------|
| 仕事 | しごと | shigoto | work, job |
| 大事 | だいじ | daiji | important, precious |
| 食事 | しょくじ | shokuji | meal |
| 用事 | ようじ | yōji | errand, business |
| 無事 | ぶじ | buji | safe, without incident |

## Stroke Order

1-4. Top portion (like a flag on a pole). 5-8. Bottom strokes forming support.

## Mnemonic

A flag being waved at an **event** — something happening, a **matter** or **thing**.

## Common Confusion Pairs

| Confused With | How to Distinguish |
|---------------|-------------------|
| 書 (write) | 事 lacks the 者 portion; 書 has 日 at bottom |
| 実 (reality) | 実 has 宀 on top; 事 has the hook radical |
