## Stroke Order

1-3. Top 巛 (hair flowing). 4-9. Bottom 自 (self/nose).

## Mnemonic

**Hair** (巛) flowing above a **nose** (自/自 face area) — the **neck** connecting head to body.

## Common Confusion Pairs

| 道 (road) | 道 has 辶 at bottom; 首 does not |
| 自 (self) | 自 is just the lower portion of 首 |
