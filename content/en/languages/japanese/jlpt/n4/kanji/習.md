## Character Info

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Kanji | 習 |
| Strokes | 11 |
| Radical | 羽 (feathers) |
| JLPT Level | N4 |

## Readings

| Type | Japanese | Romaji |
|------|----------|--------|
| On-yomi | シュウ | shū |
| Kun-yomi | なら・う | nara-u |

## Vocabulary

| 習う | ならう | narau | to learn, to take lessons |
| 練習 | れんしゅう | renshū | practice |
| 学習 | がくしゅう | gakushū | learning, study |
| 習慣 | しゅうかん | shūkan | custom, habit |
| 自習 | じしゅう | jishū | self-study |

## Stroke Order

1-6. Top 羽 (feathers/wings). 7-11. Bottom 白 (white) + one more stroke.

## Mnemonic

A young bird with **white** (白) and **feathered** (羽) wings **practicing** flying — the bird **learns** by repeatedly flapping.

## Common Confusion Pairs

| 翌 (next day) | 翌 has 立 above 羽; 習 has 羽 on top |
| 翼 (wing) | 翼 has 異 inside 羽; 習 has 白 below |
