## Character Info

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Kanji | 薬 |
| Strokes | 16 |
| Radical | 艹 (grass) |
| JLPT Level | N4 |

|------|----------|--------|
| On-yomi | ヤク | yaku |

| 薬 | くすり | kusuri | medicine, drug |

## Stroke Order

1-3. Top radical 艹 (grass/plants). 4-16. Middle 楽 (comfortable/pleasant).

## Mnemonic

**Plants** (艹) that make you feel **comfortable** (楽) — herbal **medicine** from nature.

## Common Confusion Pairs

| 楽 (comfortable, music) | 薬 has 艹 on top; 楽 does not |
| 約 (promise) | 約 has 糸 on left; 薬 has 艹 on top |
