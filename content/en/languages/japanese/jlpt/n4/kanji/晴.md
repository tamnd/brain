## Character Info

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Kanji | 晴 |
| Strokes | 12 |
| Radical | 日 (sun) |
| JLPT Level | N4 |

## Readings

| Type | Japanese | Romaji |
|------|----------|--------|
| On-yomi | セイ | sei |
| Kun-yomi | は・れる | ha-reru |

## Vocabulary

| 晴れる | はれる | hareru | to clear up (weather) |
| 晴れ | はれ | hare | clear/fine weather |
| 晴天 | せいてん | seiten | clear sky |
| 晴れ間 | はれま | harema | break in the clouds |
| 快晴 | かいせい | kaisei | clear sky |

## Stroke Order

1-4. Left radical 日 (sun). 5-12. Right side 青 (blue/green).

## Mnemonic

The **sun** (日) and **blue** (青) sky — a **clear, sunny** day.

## Common Confusion Pairs

| 清 (pure/clean) | 清 has 氵(water) on left; 晴 has 日 |
| 請 (request) | 請 has 言 on left; 晴 has 日 |
