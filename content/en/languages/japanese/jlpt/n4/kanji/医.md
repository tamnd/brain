## Character Info

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Kanji | 医 |
| Strokes | 7 |
| Radical | 匸 (hiding) |
| JLPT Level | N4 |

## Readings

| Type | Japanese | Romaji |
|------|----------|--------|
| On-yomi | イ | i |
| Kun-yomi | — (no common kun) | — |

## Stroke Order

1. Outer 匸 (box/hiding). 2-5. Inner 矢 (arrow). 6-7. 又 (hand) at bottom right.

## Mnemonic

A **hand** removing an **arrow** from a **box** (a quiver/wound) — the original meaning of **medicine**: healing arrow wounds.

## Common Confusion Pairs

| Confused With | How to Distinguish |
|---------------|-------------------|
| 区 (district) | 医 has 矢 inside; 区 has a different interior |
| 匹 (counter for animals) | 匹 has different internal strokes |
