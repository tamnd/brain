## Character Info

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Kanji | 冬 |
| Strokes | 5 |
| Radical | 夂 (walk slowly) |
| JLPT Level | N4 |

## Readings

| Type | Japanese | Romaji |
|------|----------|--------|
| On-yomi | トウ | tō |
| Kun-yomi | ふゆ | fuyu |

## Vocabulary

| 冬 | ふゆ | fuyu | winter |
| 冬休み | ふゆやすみ | fuyuyasumi | winter vacation |
| 冬至 | とうじ | tōji | winter solstice |
| 真冬 | まふゆ | mafuyu | midwinter |
| 越冬 | えっとう | ettō | wintering, overwintering |

## Stroke Order

1-2. Top 夂 (slow movement). 3-5. Bottom two ice dots (冫).

## Mnemonic

Slow **footsteps** (夂) through the **ice** (冫) — moving carefully through **winter** cold.

## Common Confusion Pairs

| 各 (each) | 各 has 口 at bottom; 冬 has ice dots |
| 外 (outside) | 外 has 夕 on left; 冬 is standalone |
