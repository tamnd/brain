## Character Info

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Kanji | 行 |
| Strokes | 6 |
| Radical | 行 (itself) |
| JLPT Level | N4 |

## Readings

| Type | Japanese | Romaji |
|------|----------|--------|
| On-yomi | コウ、ギョウ | kō, gyō |
| Kun-yomi | い・く、おこな・う | i-ku, okonau |

## Vocabulary

| 銀行 | ぎんこう | ginkō | bank |
| 行く | いく | iku | to go |
| 旅行 | りょこう | ryokō | travel |
| 行動 | こうどう | kōdō | action, behavior |
| 歩行 | ほこう | hokō | walking |

## Stroke Order

1-3. Left side of the walking radical. 4-6. Right side.

## Mnemonic

Two **legs** (the two halves of 行) moving in the same direction — **going** down a **row**.

## Common Confusion Pairs

| 術 (art/skill) | 術 has 朮 inside; 行 is the outer shell |
| 衡 (balance) | 衡 has additional strokes inside 行 |
