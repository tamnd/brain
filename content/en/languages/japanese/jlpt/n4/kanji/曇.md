## Character Info

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Kanji | 曇 |
| Strokes | 16 |
| Radical | 日 (sun) |
| JLPT Level | N4 |

## Readings

| Type | Japanese | Romaji |
|------|----------|--------|
| On-yomi | ドン | don |
| Kun-yomi | くも・る | kumo-ru |

## Vocabulary

| 曇る | くもる | kumoru | to become cloudy |
| 曇り | くもり | kumori | cloudy weather |
| 曇天 | どんてん | donten | overcast sky |
| 薄曇り | うすぐもり | usugumori | slightly cloudy |
| 曇りがち | くもりがち | kumorigachi | tending to be cloudy |

## Stroke Order

1-8. Top 雲 (cloud). 9-16. Bottom 日 (sun).

## Mnemonic

A **cloud** (雲) blocking the **sun** (日) — **cloudy** skies.

## Common Confusion Pairs

| 雲 (cloud) | 雲 lacks the 日 at bottom; 曇 has 日 below 雲 |
| 暗 (dark) | 暗 has 音 on right; 曇 has cloud above sun |
