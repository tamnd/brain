## Character Info

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Kanji | 園 |
| Strokes | 13 |
| Radical | 囗 (enclosure) |
| JLPT Level | N4 |

## Readings

| Type | Japanese | Romaji |
|------|----------|--------|
| On-yomi | エン | en |
| Kun-yomi | その | sono |

## Vocabulary

| 公園 | こうえん | kōen | park |
| 動物園 | どうぶつえん | dōbutsuen | zoo |
| 庭園 | ていえん | teien | garden |
| 幼稚園 | ようちえん | yōchien | kindergarten |
| 植物園 | しょくぶつえん | shokubutsuen | botanical garden |

## Stroke Order

1-3. Outer frame 囗 (enclosure). 4-13. Inner 袁 (a person in robes).

## Mnemonic

An **enclosed** (囗) space where a person in robes (袁) walks — a formal **garden** or **park**.

## Common Confusion Pairs

| 遠 (far) | 遠 has 辶 on bottom-right; 園 has 囗 enclosure |
| 圏 (sphere/area) | 圏 has 卷 inside; 園 has 袁 |
