## Stroke Order

1-4. Outer 戸 (door). 5-8. Inner 月/肉 (flesh).

## Mnemonic

A **door** (戸) protecting the **flesh** (月) — the **shoulder** is like a protective door for your arm.

## Common Confusion Pairs

| 局 (bureau) | 局 has 口 inside; 肩 has 月/肉 |
| 扇 (fan) | 扇 has 羽 inside; 肩 has 月 |
