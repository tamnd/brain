## Vocabulary

| 秋 | あき | aki | autumn, fall |
| 秋分 | しゅうぶん | shūbun | autumn equinox |
| 秋祭り | あきまつり | akimatsuri | autumn festival |

## Stroke Order

1-5. Left side 禾 (grain/rice plant). 6-9. Right side 火 (fire).

## Mnemonic

**Grain** (禾) that needs to be dried by **fire** (火) — in **autumn**, the harvest is dried and processed.

## Common Confusion Pairs

| 利 (profit) | 利 has 刀 on right; 秋 has 火 |
| 移 (move) | 移 has 多 on right; 秋 has 火 |
