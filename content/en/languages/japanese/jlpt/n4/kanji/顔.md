## Character Info

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Kanji | 顔 |
| Strokes | 18 |
| Radical | 頁 (head) |
| JLPT Level | N4 |

## Readings

| Type | Japanese | Romaji |
|------|----------|--------|
| On-yomi | ガン | gan |
| Kun-yomi | かお | kao |

## Vocabulary

| 顔 | かお | kao | face |
| 顔色 | かおいろ | kaoiro | complexion, facial expression |
| 笑顔 | えがお | egao | smiling face |
| 顔見知り | かおみしり | kaomishiri | acquaintance |
| 素顔 | すがお | sugao | bare face, no makeup |

## Stroke Order

1-9. Left side 彦 (accomplished person). 10-18. Right radical 頁 (head).

## Mnemonic

An **accomplished person's** (彦) **head** (頁) — showing their **face** to the world.

## Common Confusion Pairs

| 頭 (head) | 頭 has 豆 on left; 顔 has 彦 |
| 頑 (stubborn) | 頑 has 元 on left; 顔 has 彦 |
