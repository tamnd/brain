## Character Info

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Kanji | 寒 |
| Strokes | 12 |
| Radical | 宀 (roof) |
| JLPT Level | N4 |

## Readings

| Type | Japanese | Romaji |
|------|----------|--------|
| On-yomi | カン | kan |
| Kun-yomi | さむ・い | samu-i |

## Vocabulary

| 寒い | さむい | samui | cold |
| 寒さ | さむさ | samusa | coldness |
| 寒気 | さむけ | samuke | chills; cold air |
| 極寒 | ごっかん | gokkan | extreme cold |
| 寒暖差 | かんだんさ | kandansa | temperature difference |

## Stroke Order

1-3. Top 宀 (roof). 4-12. Middle/bottom 茻 and 冫(ice).

## Mnemonic

Under a **roof** (宀) with **frost/ice** (冫) — you stay inside because it's **cold** outside.

## Common Confusion Pairs

| 実 (reality/fruit) | 実 has 木 at bottom; 寒 has ice dots |
| 審 (examine) | 審 has 番 inside; 寒 has different interior |
