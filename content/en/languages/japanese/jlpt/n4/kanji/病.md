## Character Info

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Kanji | 病 |
| Strokes | 10 |
| Radical | 疒 (sickness radical) |
| JLPT Level | N4 |

## Readings

| Type | Japanese | Romaji |
|------|----------|--------|
| On-yomi | ビョウ、ヘイ | byō, hei |
| Kun-yomi | や・む、やまい | ya-mu, yamai |

## Vocabulary

| Word | Reading | Romaji | English |
|------|---------|--------|---------|
| 病院 | びょういん | byōin | hospital |
| 病気 | びょうき | byōki | illness, sickness |
| 病人 | びょうにん | byōnin | sick person |
| 看病 | かんびょう | kanbyō | nursing (sick person) |
| 疾病 | しっぺい | shippei | disease (formal) |

## Stroke Order

1-5. Outer sickness radical 疒. 6-10. Inner 丙 (third, bright).

## Mnemonic

The **sickness** radical (疒, a person on a bed) with 丙 inside — you're **laid up in bed** with an **illness**.

## Common Confusion Pairs

| Confused With | How to Distinguish |
|---------------|-------------------|
| 疲 (fatigue) | Both have 疒 radical; 病 has 丙 inside; 疲 has 皮 |
| 痛 (pain) | 痛 has 甬 inside; 病 has 丙 |
