## Stroke Order

1-7. Top 聿 (writing brush). 8-10. Bottom 日 (sun/surface to write on).

## Mnemonic

A **writing brush** (聿) moving over a **surface** (日) — to **write** or a **book**.

## Common Confusion Pairs

| 昼 (daytime) | 昼 has 尺 on top; 書 has 聿 |
| 者 (person) | 者 has different top; 書 has 聿 |
