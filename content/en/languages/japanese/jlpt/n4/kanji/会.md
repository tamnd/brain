## Character Info

|----------|-------|

| Strokes | 6 |
| Radical | 人 (person) |

## Readings

| Type | Japanese | Romaji |
|------|----------|--------|
| On-yomi | カイ、エ | kai, e |
| Kun-yomi | あ・う、あわせる | a-u, awa-seru |

## Stroke Order

1. Top horizontal line (short)
2. Left-leaning stroke (like ノ)
3. Short vertical stroke through center
4. Left downstroke
5. Short bottom left
6. Bottom right stroke

## Mnemonic

A person (人) holding a **lid** on top — two people coming **together** under one roof to **meet**.

## Common Confusion Pairs

| Confused With | How to Distinguish |
|---------------|-------------------|
| 合 (gō — combine) | 会 = people meeting; 合 = fitting/combining things together |
| 全 (zen — all/complete) | 全 has 入 on top; 会 has the two-stroke top |
