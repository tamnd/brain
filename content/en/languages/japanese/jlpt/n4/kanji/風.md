## Character Info

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Kanji | 風 |
| Strokes | 9 |
| Radical | 风 (wind) |
| JLPT Level | N4 |

## Readings

| Type | Japanese | Romaji |
|------|----------|--------|
| On-yomi | フウ、フ | fū, fu |
| Kun-yomi | かぜ、かざ | kaze, kaza |

## Vocabulary

| 風 | かぜ | kaze | wind |
| 台風 | たいふう | taifū | typhoon |
| 風景 | ふうけい | fūkei | scenery, landscape |
| 風邪 | かぜ | kaze | cold (illness) |
| 風俗 | ふうぞく | fūzoku | customs, manners |

## Stroke Order

1-4. Outer frame 几 (table/wind frame). 5-9. Inner 虫 (insect).

## Mnemonic

An **insect** (虫) trapped inside a **whirling frame** — like a bug caught in the **wind**.

## Common Confusion Pairs

| 凤 (phoenix, traditional) | Similar but different composition |
| 嵐 (storm) | 嵐 has 山 on top; 風 has the 几 frame |
