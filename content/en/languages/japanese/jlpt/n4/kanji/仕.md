## Character Info

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Kanji | 仕 |
| Strokes | 5 |
| Radical | 亻 (person) |
| JLPT Level | N4 |

## Readings

| Type | Japanese | Romaji |
|------|----------|--------|
| On-yomi | シ、ジ | shi, ji |
| Kun-yomi | つか・える | tsuka-eru |

## Vocabulary

| Word | Reading | Romaji | English |
|------|---------|--------|---------|
| 仕事 | しごと | shigoto | work, job |
| 仕方 | しかた | shikata | way, method |
| 仕える | つかえる | tsukaeru | to serve |
| 仕上げ | しあげ | shiage | finish, completion |
| 仕組み | しくみ | shikumi | structure, mechanism |

## Stroke Order

1. Left radical 亻(person). 2-5. Right side 士 (samurai/person).

## Mnemonic

A **person** (亻) who is a **samurai-servant** (士) — someone who **serves** or **works** for others.

## Common Confusion Pairs

| Confused With | How to Distinguish |
|---------------|-------------------|
| 士 (samurai, person) | 仕 has person radical 亻; 士 stands alone |
| 任 (responsibility) | 任 has 壬 on right; 仕 has 士 |
