## Character Info

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Kanji | 番 |
| Strokes | 12 |
| Radical | 田 (field) |
| JLPT Level | N4 |

## Readings

| Type | Japanese | Romaji |
|------|----------|--------|
| On-yomi | バン | ban |
| Kun-yomi | — (no kun) | — |

## Vocabulary

| 交番 | こうばん | kōban | police box |
| 番号 | ばんごう | bangō | number |
| 番組 | ばんぐみ | bangumi | TV/radio program |
| 一番 | いちばん | ichiban | number one, most |
| 当番 | とうばん | tōban | one's turn, duty |

## Stroke Order

1-5. Top 釆 (distinguish/sort). 6-12. Bottom 田 (field/rice field).

## Mnemonic

**Sorting** (釆) crops in a **field** (田) — keeping **numbered** rows in order, taking **turns** at the work.

## Common Confusion Pairs

| 審 (examine) | 審 has 宀 on top; 番 has 釆 |
| 播 (sow) | 播 has 手 on left; 番 is standalone |
