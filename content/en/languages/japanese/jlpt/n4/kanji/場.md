## Character Info

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Kanji | 場 |
| Strokes | 12 |
| Radical | 土 (earth) |
| JLPT Level | N4 |

## Readings

| Type | Japanese | Romaji |
|------|----------|--------|
| On-yomi | ジョウ | jō |
| Kun-yomi | ば | ba |

## Stroke Order

1. Left radical 土 (earth). 2-12. Right side 昜 (sun rising, light).

## Mnemonic

**Earth** (土) where the **sun** (日) shines brightly — an open **place** or **field**.

## Common Confusion Pairs

| Confused With | How to Distinguish |
|---------------|-------------------|
| 揚 (raise, fry) | 場 has 土 left; 揚 has 手 (hand) left |
| 湯 (hot water) | 湯 has 氵(water) left; 場 has 土 |
