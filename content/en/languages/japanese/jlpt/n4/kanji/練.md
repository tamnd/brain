## Character Info

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Kanji | 練 |
| Strokes | 14 |
| Radical | 糸 (thread) |
| JLPT Level | N4 |

## Readings

| Type | Japanese | Romaji |
|------|----------|--------|
| On-yomi | レン | ren |
| Kun-yomi | ね・る | ne-ru |

## Vocabulary

| 練習 | れんしゅう | renshū | practice |
| 訓練 | くんれん | kunren | training |
| 練り歩く | ねりあるく | neriaruku | to parade, to march |
| 鍛錬 | たんれん | tanren | tempering, training |
| 熟練 | じゅくれん | jukuren | skill, proficiency |

## Stroke Order

1-6. Left radical 糸 (thread). 7-14. Right side 東 (east).

## Mnemonic

**Threads** (糸) pulled toward the **east** (東) direction — repeatedly pulling and twisting = **practicing** or **kneading**.

## Common Confusion Pairs

| 緑 (green) | 緑 has 彔 on right; 練 has 東 |
| 繰 (reel/spin) | 繰 has 票 on right; 練 has 東 |
