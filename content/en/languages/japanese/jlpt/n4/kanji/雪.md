## Stroke Order

1-8. Top radical 雨 (rain). 9-11. Bottom 彐/ヨ (a broom/hand).

## Mnemonic

**Rain** (雨) that a **hand** (彐) sweeps away — **snow** is frozen rain you have to sweep from your door.

## Common Confusion Pairs

| 雲 (cloud) | 雲 has 云 below; 雪 has 彐 below |
| 霜 (frost) | 霜 has 相 below 雨; 雪 has 彐 |
