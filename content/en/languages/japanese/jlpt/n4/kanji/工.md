## Stroke Order

1. Top horizontal line. 2. Vertical line (center). 3. Bottom horizontal line.

## Mnemonic

Looks like a **carpenter's I-beam** or a **work tool** — perfectly captures **craftsmanship and construction**.

## Common Confusion Pairs

| Confused With | How to Distinguish |
|---------------|-------------------|
| 王 (king) | 王 has middle horizontal slightly up; 工 has even spacing |
| 土 (earth) | 土 has short top and long bottom; 工 has equal lines |
