## Character Info

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Kanji | 交 |
| Strokes | 6 |
| Radical | 亠 (top) |
| JLPT Level | N4 |

## Readings

| Type | Japanese | Romaji |
|------|----------|--------|
| On-yomi | コウ | kō |
| Kun-yomi | ま・じる、まじわる | ma-jiru, majiwaru |

## Vocabulary

| 交番 | こうばん | kōban | police box |
| 交通 | こうつう | kōtsū | traffic, transportation |
| 交換 | こうかん | kōkan | exchange |
| 外交 | がいこう | gaikō | diplomacy |
| 交差点 | こうさてん | kōsaten | intersection |

## Stroke Order

1-2. Top 亠 (lid/top). 3-6. Crossed strokes below (like crossing paths).

## Mnemonic

Two paths that **cross** — **mixing**, **exchanging**, or **intersecting** like roads at a crossing.

## Common Confusion Pairs

| 文 (writing) | 文 has 乂 crossing at bottom; 交 has similar but different top |
| 父 (father) | 父 has 乂 at bottom; 交 has abutting top stroke |
