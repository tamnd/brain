## Character Info

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Kanji | 暑 |
| Strokes | 12 |
| Radical | 日 (sun) |
| JLPT Level | N4 |

## Readings

| Type | Japanese | Romaji |
|------|----------|--------|
| On-yomi | ショ | sho |
| Kun-yomi | あつ・い | atsu-i |

## Vocabulary

| 暑い | あつい | atsui | hot (weather) |
| 暑さ | あつさ | atsusa | heat |
| 真夏日 | まなつび | manatsubi | summer day over 30°C |
| 猛暑 | もうしょ | mōsho | intense heat |
| 暑中 | しょちゅう | shochū | midsummer |

## Stroke Order

1-4. Top 日 (sun). 5-12. Bottom 者 (person).

## Mnemonic

The **sun** (日) beating down on a **person** (者) — they feel the **summer heat**.

## Common Confusion Pairs

| 熱 (hot/heat/fever) | 熱 has 灬(fire) at bottom; 暑 has 者 |
| 者 (person) | 者 alone; 暑 has 日 on top of 者 |
