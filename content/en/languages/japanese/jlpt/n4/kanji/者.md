## Character Info

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Kanji | 者 |
| Strokes | 8 |
| Radical | 耂 (old) |
| JLPT Level | N4 |

## Readings

| Type | Japanese | Romaji |
|------|----------|--------|
| On-yomi | シャ | sha |
| Kun-yomi | もの | mono |

## Vocabulary

| Word | Reading | Romaji | English |
|------|---------|--------|---------|
| 医者 | いしゃ | isha | doctor |
| 記者 | きしゃ | kisha | journalist |
| 作者 | さくしゃ | sakusha | author, creator |
| 患者 | かんじゃ | kanja | patient (medical) |
| 若者 | わかもの | wakamono | young person |

## Stroke Order

1-5. Top portion 土 and 日 combined. 6-8. Bottom 日 with additional strokes.

## Mnemonic

An **old person** (耂) looking at the **sun** (日) — a wise **person** who has seen many days.

## Common Confusion Pairs

| Confused With | How to Distinguish |
|---------------|-------------------|
| 都 (capital city) | 者 alone; 都 has 阝on the right |
| 著 (write, note) | 著 has 艹 (grass) on top; 者 does not |
