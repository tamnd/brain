## Character Info

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Kanji | 館 |
| Strokes | 16 |
| Radical | 食 (eat) |
| JLPT Level | N4 |

## Readings

| Type | Japanese | Romaji |
|------|----------|--------|
| On-yomi | カン | kan |
| Kun-yomi | やかた | yakata |

## Vocabulary

| 図書館 | としょかん | toshokan | library |

## Stroke Order

1-8. Left 食 (eat/food). 9-16. Right 官 (official).

## Mnemonic

A place where officials **eat** (食) — a grand **building** or **hall** where formal gatherings happen.

## Common Confusion Pairs

| 飯 (cooked rice) | 飯 has 反 on right; 館 has 官 |
| 飲 (drink) | 飲 has 欠 on right; 館 has 官 |
