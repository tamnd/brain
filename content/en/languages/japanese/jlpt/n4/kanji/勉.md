## Character Info

| Property | Value |

## Stroke Order

1-8. Left/top 免 (avoid, excuse). 9-10. Right 力 (power/strength).

## Mnemonic

Using all your **strength** (力) to overcome **excuses** (免) — making a real **effort** to study despite wanting to avoid it.

## Common Confusion Pairs

| 免 (excuse/avoid) | 免 alone; 勉 adds 力 on the right |
| 晩 (evening) | 晩 has 日 and 免; 勉 has 力 |
