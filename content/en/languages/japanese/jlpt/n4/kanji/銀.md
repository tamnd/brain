## Stroke Order

1-8. Left radical 金 (metal/gold). 9-14. Right side 艮 (stubborn, limit).

## Mnemonic

A **metal** (金) that has a **limit/boundary** (艮) — **silver** is valuable but less so than gold.

## Common Confusion Pairs

| 金 (gold/money) | 金 stands alone; 銀 has 艮 on right |
| 鉄 (iron) | 鉄 has 失 on right; 銀 has 艮 |
