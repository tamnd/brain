## Character Info

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Kanji | 局 |
| Strokes | 7 |
| Radical | 尸 (corpse/roof) |
| JLPT Level | N4 |

|------|----------|--------|
| On-yomi | キョク | kyoku |
| Kun-yomi | — (no common kun) | — |

## Vocabulary

| 郵便局 | ゆうびんきょく | yūbinkyoku | post office |

| 薬局 | やっきょく | yakkyoku | pharmacy |

## Stroke Order

1-3. Outer 尸 (roof/shelter). 4-7. Inner 句 (phrase).

## Mnemonic

A **sheltered building** (尸) with a **phrase/counter** (句) — a **bureau** or **office** where official business happens.

## Common Confusion Pairs

| 居 (reside) | 居 has 古 inside; 局 has 句 |
| 屋 (room/shop) | 屋 has 至 inside; 局 has 句 |
