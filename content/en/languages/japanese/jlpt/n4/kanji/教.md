## Stroke Order

1-7. Left 孝 (filial piety/filial child with elder). 8-11. Right 攵 (strike/action).

## Mnemonic

A **child** being guided by an **elder** (孝) through a **gentle action** (攵) — an elder **teaching** through patient guidance.

## Common Confusion Pairs

| 攻 (attack) | 攻 has 工 on left; 教 has 孝 |
| 敦 (sincere) | 敦 has 享 on left; 教 has 孝 |
