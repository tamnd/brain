## Character Info

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Kanji | 社 |
| Strokes | 7 |
| Radical | 示 (show/altar) |
| JLPT Level | N4 |

## Readings

| Type | Japanese | Romaji |
|------|----------|--------|
| On-yomi | シャ | sha |
| Kun-yomi | やしろ | yashiro |

## Vocabulary

| Word | Reading | Romaji | English |
|------|---------|--------|---------|
| 会社 | かいしゃ | kaisha | company |
| 社会 | しゃかい | shakai | society |
| 神社 | じんじゃ | jinja | Shinto shrine |
| 社員 | しゃいん | shain | company employee |
| 商社 | しょうしゃ | shōsha | trading company |

## Stroke Order

1. Left radical 示 (altar). 2-7. Right portion 土 (earth) and 乙.

## Mnemonic

An **altar** (示) on **earth** (土) — where a company gathers, like an ancient shrine.

## Common Confusion Pairs

| Confused With | How to Distinguish |
|---------------|-------------------|
| 祖 (ancestor) | 社 has 土 on the right; 祖 has 且 on the right |
| 初 (first) | 初 has 刀 on the right; 社 has 土 |
