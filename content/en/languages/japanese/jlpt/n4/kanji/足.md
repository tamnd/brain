## Character Info

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Kanji | 足 |
| Strokes | 7 |
| Radical | 足 (itself) |
| JLPT Level | N4 |

## Readings

| Type | Japanese | Romaji |
|------|----------|--------|
| On-yomi | ソク | soku |
| Kun-yomi | あし、た・りる | ashi, ta-riru |

## Vocabulary

| 足 | あし | ashi | foot, leg |
| 足りる | たりる | tariru | to be enough |
| 満足 | まんぞく | manzoku | satisfaction |
| 遠足 | えんそく | ensoku | excursion, field trip |
| 不足 | ふそく | fusoku | shortage, lack |

## Stroke Order

1-3. Top 口 (mouth) then short stroke. 4-7. Bottom strokes forming the 'foot' shape.

## Mnemonic

A **knee** bent above a **foot** — the character visually resembles a leg and foot in profile.

## Common Confusion Pairs

| 走 (run) | 走 has 夭 on top and 止 below; 足 is distinct |
| 促 (urge) | 促 has 亻 on left; 足 stands alone |
