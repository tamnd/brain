| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|

| Strokes | 7 |

| JLPT Level | N4 |

## Readings

|------|----------|--------|

## Vocabulary

| 体 | からだ | karada | body |
| 体育 | たいいく | taiiku | physical education |

| 全体 | ぜんたい | zentai | whole, entirety |

## Stroke Order

1. Left radical 亻(person). 2-7. Right side 本 (origin, root).

## Mnemonic

A **person** (亻) with their **roots** (本) — the **body** is the root of a person.

## Common Confusion Pairs

| 休 (rest) | 休 has 木 (tree) right; 体 has 本 |
| 伸 (stretch) | 伸 has 申 on right; 体 has 本 |
