| 退院 | たいいん | taiin | discharge from hospital |

## Stroke Order

1. Left 阝(hill/mound radical). 2-10. Right 完 (complete).

## Mnemonic

A large **mound** (阝) that is **complete** (完) — a big **institution** or **building** where things are completed (hospitals, schools).

## Common Confusion Pairs

| 完 (complete) | 院 has 阝 left radical; 完 stands alone |
| 陸 (land) | 陸 has 坴 on right; 院 has 完 |
