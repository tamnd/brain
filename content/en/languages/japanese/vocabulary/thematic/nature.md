Nature vocabulary is rich in Japanese culture, deeply tied to the four seasons, haiku traditions, and appreciation of natural beauty (mono no aware). Many nature words appear in N5–N3 JLPT material.

## Example Sentences

- 今日は天気がいいですね。Kyou wa tenki ga ii desu ne. — The weather is nice today, isn't it?
- 桜がきれいに咲いています。Sakura ga kirei ni saite imasu. — The cherry blossoms are blooming beautifully.
- 日本の秋は紅葉が有名です。Nihon no aki wa kouyou ga yuumei desu. — Japan is famous for autumn leaves.
- 富士山は日本で一番高い山です。Fujisan wa Nihon de ichiban takai yama desu. — Mt. Fuji is the tallest mountain in Japan.
- 川の近くでカエルの声が聞こえます。Kawa no chikaku de kaeru no koe ga kikoemasu. — I can hear frogs near the river.
- 台風が来るそうです。Taifuu ga kuru sou desu. — I hear a typhoon is coming.
