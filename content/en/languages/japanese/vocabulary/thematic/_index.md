Thematic vocabulary lists help you learn words in context-relevant groups, making them easier to remember and use in real conversations. Each topic covers the most important words from N5 to N3 level.

## Topics

| Topic | Link | Words |
|-------|------|-------|

| [Food](food/) | Foods, drinks, cooking, restaurants | ~80 words |
| [Numbers and Counters](numbers/) | Numbers 1–10,000 + key counters | ~60 items |
| [Time](time/) | Days, months, seasons, time expressions | ~60 words |
| [Body](body/) | Body parts, health, medical | ~50 words |
| [Travel](travel/) | Transport, accommodation, directions | ~70 words |
| [Work](work/) | Jobs, office, business, professions | ~60 words |
| [Nature](nature/) | Weather, animals, plants, environment | ~70 words |

## How to Use These Lists

1. **Don't try to memorize all at once**: Pick one topic relevant to an upcoming conversation or situation
2. **Add to Anki**: Copy words you want to learn into an Anki deck for SRS reinforcement
3. **Find them in the wild**: After learning a topic, actively look for these words in NHK Easy, manga, or anime
4. **Learn in sentences**: Whenever possible, learn a word with an example sentence attached
