## Technology Vocabulary — JLPT N1

At the N1 level, technology vocabulary shifts from basic hardware to abstract concepts, infrastructure, cybersecurity, and the societal impact of digitalization. Mastering these terms is essential for reading professional news, understanding technical reports, and engaging in high-level discussions about the future of AI and digital transformation (DX).

## Key Phrases

1. **システムの脆弱性を突く** | しすてむのぜいじゃくせいをつく (shisutemu no zeijakusei o tsuku) | To exploit a system vulnerability.
2. **デジタル化を推進する** | でじたるかをすいしんする (dejitaruka o suishin suru) | To promote digitalization.
3. **互換性を確保する** | ごかんせいをかくほする (gokansei o kakuho suru) | To ensure compatibility.
4. **負荷を分散させる** | ふかをぶんさんさせる (fuka o bunsan saseru) | To distribute the load.
5. **個人情報の流出を防ぐ** | こじんじょうほうのりゅうしゅつをふせぐ (kojin jouhou no ryuushutsu o fusegu) | To prevent the leakage of personal information.
6. **最適化を図る** | さいてきかをはかる (saitekika o hakaru) | To aim for optimization.
7. **技術革新を促す** | ぎじゅつかくしんをうながす (gijutsu kakushin o unagasu) | To encourage technological innovation.
8. **セキュリティ対策を講じる** | せきゅりてぃたいさくをこうじる (sekyuriti taisaku o koujiru) | To take security measures.
9. **クラウド環境へ移行する** | くらうどかんきょうへいこうする (kuraudo kankyou e ikou suru) | To migrate to a cloud environment.
10. **倫理的な課題に直面する** | りんりてきなかだいにちょくめんする (rinriteki na kadai ni chokumen suru) | To face ethical challenges.

## Practice Dialogue

**A:** 最近、社内のシステム刷新プロジェクトで忙しいよ。  
(さいきん、しゃないのしすてむさっしんぷろじぇくとでいそがしいよ。 / Saikin, shanai no shisutemu sasshin purojekuto de isogashii yo.)  
*I've been busy with the in-house system renewal project lately.*

**B:** DX推進の一環？セキュリティ対策は万全なの？  
(でぃーえっくすすいしんのいっかん？せきゅりてぃたいさくはばんぜんなの？ / DX suishin no ikkan? Sekyuriti taisaku wa banzen na no?)  
*Is it part of the DX promotion? Are the security measures foolproof?*

**A:** 脆弱性診断を徹底しているから大丈夫だと思うけど、負荷分散が課題だね。  
(ぜいじゃくせいしんだんをてっていしているからだいじょうぶだとおもうけど、ふかぶんさんがかだいだね。 / Zeijakusei shindan o tettei shite iru kara daijoubu da to omou kedo, fuka bunsan ga kadai da ne.)  
*We are being thorough with vulnerability assessments, so I think it's fine, but load balancing is the challenge.*

**B:** 確かに。クラウド移行後のパフォーマンス低下は避けたいよね。  
(たしかに。くらうどいこうごのぱふぉーまんすていかはさけたいよね。 / Tashika ni. Kuraudo ikougo no pafōmansu teika wa saketai yo ne.)  
*True. We definitely want to avoid performance degradation after the cloud migration.*

## Self-Study Tips

1. **Read Tech News:** Subscribe to Japanese tech news outlets like *ITmedia* or *Nikkei CrossTech*. N1 learners should focus on the editorial sections to see how these abstract nouns are used in context.
2. **Contextual Flashcards:** Don't just memorize the word "脆弱性" (vulnerability). Memorize the collocation "脆弱性を突く" (to exploit a vulnerability) to understand how the word functions in a sentence.
3. **Summarization:** After reading a tech article, try to summarize the main point in three sentences using at least five of the vocabulary words from the table above.
4. **Active Usage:** If you work in a tech-related field, try to use these terms in your next Japanese email or meeting to solidify your professional vocabulary.
