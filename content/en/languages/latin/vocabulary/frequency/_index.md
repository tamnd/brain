The 200 most frequent Latin words (DCC Core Band 1) are the foundation of Latin vocabulary. Learn these first — they appear on every page of any Latin text.

**Source**: DCC Latin Core Vocabulary, Band 1 (words 1–200). Full list at [dcc.dickinson.edu/latin-core-list1](https://dcc.dickinson.edu/latin-core-list1).

## Learning These 200 Words

The full 200-word list is available at the DCC website and as an Anki deck. These 200 words alone cover the highest-frequency slot in nearly every Latin sentence.

**Target**: Learn all 200 within the first 3–4 months of study. With Anki at 15 min/day (10 new cards/day), you'll cover all 200 in 20 days of new cards — then spend 2–3 months solidifying them.
